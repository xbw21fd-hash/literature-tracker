## Abstract
arXiv:2506.17548v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: This review exhaustively evaluates the role of nanomaterials across the synthesis, characterization and application stages of biofuel systems. Common types of nanomaterials that are used for biofuel applications include metal oxides, carbon-based structures, and hybrids, which are evaluated for their effectiveness in efficient biofuel production. The properties of such nanomaterials are being utilized as an aid to produce biofuels through improved catalysis, enzyme immobilization and thermal stability. Common synthesis methods, such as sol-gel, coprecipitation, and green synthesis, are compared, alongside characterization tools, such as TEM, SEM, FTIR, and BET. This study focuses on transesterification, biomass pretreatment, and fermentation processes, where nanomaterials significantly improve yield and reusability. There are several challenges, despite the merits of using nanomaterials, and the trade-offs include cost, scalability, and environmental impact, which further expand into evaluating the life cycle of such materials. This review outlines the practical potential of nanomaterials in enabling efficient and sustainable biofuel production.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.17548v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：这篇综述详尽地评估了纳米材料在生物燃料系统的合成、表征和应用阶段的作用。用于生物燃料应用的常见纳米材料类型包括金属氧化物、碳基结构和混合材料，这些材料在高效生物燃料生产中的有效性受到评估。通过改进催化作用、酶固定化和热稳定性，此类纳米材料的特性被用来辅助生产生物燃料。对溶胶-凝胶、共沉淀和绿色合成等常见合成方法以及 TEM、SEM、FTIR 和 BET 等表征工具进行了比较。本研究重点关注酯交换、生物质预处理和发酵过程，其中纳米材料可显着提高产量和可重复使用性。尽管使用纳米材料有诸多优点，但仍存在一些挑战，并且需要权衡成本、可扩展性和环境影响，这些因素进一步扩展到评估此类材料的生命周期。本综述概述了纳米材料在实现高效和可持续生物燃料生产方面的实际潜力。
