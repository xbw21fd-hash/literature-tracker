# Exceptionally High Carrier Mobility in Hexagonal Diamond

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15076v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Hexagonal diamond (h-diamond), or Lonsdaleite, has been reported to be a wide-bandgap semiconductor with high thermal conductivity and hardness. Our $ab initio$ calculations show that h-diamond has exceptionally high carrier mobility. Along $xy$ and $z$ directions, the hole mobilities at 300 K are 5631 and 5552 cm$^2$V$^{-1}$s$^{-1}$, and the room-temperature electron mobilities are 11462 and 28464 cm$^2$V$^{-1}$s$^{-1}$, respectively. These values are superior to the mobility of most known semiconductors including cubic diamond (c-diamond). The small effective masses in h-diamond, comparable to those in c-diamond, cannot explain the mobility difference between the two phases. For holes, scattering induced by transverse acoustic phonons is the predominant mechanism near room temperature in c-diamond, whereas considerably suppressed in d-diamond by selection rules. The high electron mobility in h-diamond can be attributed to the wavefunction at the conduction band minimum, which is extended and distributed primarily in the lattice interstitial, leading to weak coupling with scattering potentials. The temperature dependence of h-diamond is investigated as well, which deviates from the power-law relationship due to the significantly increased occupation of optical modes at elevated temperatures. Consequently, our findings reveal h-diamond as a promising high-mobility semiconductor, and elucidate the microscopic origin in terms of the carrier-phonon scattering mechanisms beyond conventional understandings based on simple parameters such as effective mass.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15076v1 公告类型：新 摘要：六方金刚石（h-金刚石）或 Lonsdaleite 已被报道为一种具有高导热率和硬度的宽带隙半导体。我们的$ab initio$计算表明h-金刚石具有极高的载流子迁移率。沿着$xy$和$z$方向，300 K时的空穴迁移率分别为5631和5552 cm$^2$V$^{-1}$s$^{-1}$，室温电子迁移率分别为11462和28464 cm$^2$V$^{-1}$s$^{-1}$。这些值优于大多数已知半导体（包括立方金刚石（c-金刚石））的迁移率。与 c 金刚石相比，h 金刚石中的有效质量较小，无法解释两相之间的迁移率差异。对于空穴，横向声子引起的散射是 c 金刚石中室温附近的主要机制，而在 d 金刚石中则受到选择规则的显着抑制。 h-金刚石中的高电子迁移率可归因于导带最小值处的波函数，该波函数主要延伸并分布在晶格间隙中，导致与散射势的弱耦合。还研究了 h 金刚石的温度依赖性，由于高温下光学模式的占用显着增加，因此它偏离了幂律关系。因此，我们的研究结果揭示了氢金刚石是一种有前途的高迁移率半导体，并阐明了载流子-声子散射机制的微观起源，超出了基于有效质量等简单参数的传统理解。
