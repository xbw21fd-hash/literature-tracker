## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04898v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Phase-field (PF) modeling is a powerful tool for simulating microstructure evolution. To overcome the high computational cost of PF in solving complex PDEs, machine learning methods such as PINNs, convLSTM have been used to predict PF evolution. However, current methods still face shortages of low flexibility, poor generalization and short predicting time length. In this work, we present a joint framework coupling voxel-flow network (VFN) with PF simulations in an alternating manner for long-horizon temporal prediction of microstructure evolution. The VFN iteratively predicts future evolution by learning the flow of pixels from past snapshots, with periodic boundaries preserved in the process. Periodical PF simulations suppresses nonphysical artifacts, reduces accumulated error, and extends reliable prediction time length. The VFN is about 1,000 times faster than PF simulation on GPU. In validation using grain growth and spinodal decomposition, MSE and SSIM remain 6.76% and 0.911 when predicted 18 frames from only 2 input frames, outperforming similar predicting methods. For an ultra-long grain growth prediction for 82 frames from 2 input frames, grain number decreases from 600 to 29 with NMSE of average grain area remaining 1.64%. This joint framework enables rapid, generalized, flexible and physically consistent microstructure forecasting from image-based data for ultra-long time scales.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04898v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：相场（PF）建模是模拟微观结构演化的强大工具。为了克服 PF 在求解复杂偏微分方程时的高计算成本，机器学习方法如 PINN、convLSTM 已被用来预测 PF 演化。然而，现有方法仍面临灵活性低、泛化性差、预测时间短等缺点。在这项工作中，我们提出了一种以交替方式将体素流网络（VFN）与 PF 模拟耦合的联合框架，用于微观结构演化的长视野时间预测。 VFN 通过学习过去快照中的像素流来迭代预测未来的演化，并在此过程中保留周期性边界。定期 PF 模拟可抑制非物理伪影、减少累积误差并延长可靠的预测时间长度。 VFN 比 GPU 上的 PF 模拟快约 1,000 倍。在使用晶粒生长和旋节线分解的验证中，当仅从 2 个输入帧预测 18 帧时，MSE 和 SSIM 保持为 6.76% 和 0.911，优于类似的预测方法。对于来自 2 个输入帧的 82 帧的超长晶粒生长预测，晶粒数量从 600 减少到 29，平均晶粒面积的 NMSE 仍为 1.64%。该联合框架能够根据超长时间尺度的基于图像的数据进行快速、通用、灵活和物理一致的微观结构预测。
