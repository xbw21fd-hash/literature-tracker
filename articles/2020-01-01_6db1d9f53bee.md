## Abstract
TLDR: It is found that the sample contains only the low-polarization phase and that domains of one polarization orientation are strained, whereas domains of the opposite polarization direction are fully relaxed, which produces polarization maps and layer spacing which are compared to macroscopic X-ray measurements.
