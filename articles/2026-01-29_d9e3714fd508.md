## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20786v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a wall model for large-eddy simulation that incorporates surface-roughness effects and is applicable across low- and high-speed flows, for both transitional and fully rough conditions. The model, implemented using an artificial neural network, is trained on a direct numerical simulation database of compressible turbulent channel flows over rough walls. The dataset contains 372 cases spanning a wide range of irregular roughness topographies, including Gaussian and Weibull distributions, Mach numbers 0~3.3, and friction Reynolds numbers 180~2000. We employ an information-theoretic, dimensionless learning method to identify the inputs with the highest predictive power for the dimensionless wall friction and wall heat flux. Predictions are accompanied by a confidence score derived from a spectrally normalized neural Gaussian process, which quantifies uncertainty in regions that deviate from the training dataset. The model performance is first evaluated a-priori on 110 turbulent channel flow cases, yielding prediction errors below 4%. The model is assessed a-posteriori in wall-modeled large-eddy simulations across diverse test cases. These include over 160 subsonic and supersonic turbulent channel flows with rough walls, a transonic high-pressure turbine (HPT) blade with Gaussian roughness, a high-speed compression ramp with sandpaper roughness, and three hypersonic blunt bodies with sand-grain roughness. Results show that the proposed wall model typically achieves a-posteriori predictive accuracy within 10% for wall shear stress and within 15% for wall heat flux, with high confidence in the channel flows and HPT blade cases. In the rough-wall compression ramp and hypersonic blunt bodies, the model captures the heating augmentation with errors ranging 0%~20%. In the cases with the highest errors, the reduced performance is correctly detected by a drop in the confidence score.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20786v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种用于大涡模拟的壁模型，该模型结合了表面粗糙度效应，适用于低速和高速流动、过渡条件和完全粗糙条件。该模型使用人工神经网络实现，并在粗糙壁上可压缩湍流通道流的直接数值模拟数据库上进行训练。该数据集包含 372 个案例，涵盖各种不规则粗糙度地形，包括高斯分布和威布尔分布、马赫数 0~3.3 和摩擦雷诺数 180~2000。我们采用信息论、无量纲学习方法来识别对无量纲壁摩擦和壁热通量具有最高预测能力的输入。预测伴随着从谱归一化神经高斯过程得出的置信度得分，该过程量化了偏离训练数据集的区域的不确定性。首先在 110 个湍流河道流情况下对模型性能进行先验评估，得出的预测误差低于 4%。该模型在不同测试用例的壁建模大涡模拟中进行事后评估。其中包括超过 160 个具有粗糙壁的亚音速和超音速湍流通道流、具有高斯粗糙度的跨音速高压涡轮 (HPT) 叶片、具有砂纸粗糙度的高速压缩坡道以及三个具有沙粒粗糙度的高超音速钝体。结果表明，所提出的壁面模型通常可以实现壁面剪应力的后验预测精度在 10% 以内，壁面热通量的后验预测精度在 15% 以内，并且对通道流动和 HPT 叶片情况具有很高的置信度。在粗壁压缩坡道和高超声速钝体中，模型捕获了加热增强，误差范围为 0%~20%。在错误最高的情况下，性能下降可以通过置信度分数的下降来正确检测到。
