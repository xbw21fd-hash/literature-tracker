## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01056v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Microbial swarming on mucosal surfaces reshapes microbial communities and influences mucosal healing and antibiotic tolerance. Yet even with time-lapse microscopy and deep learning, analyses of swarming colonies remain descriptive and cannot forecast how their fronts reorganize in time. This limitation is significant because the advancing edge determines access to nutrients, host tissue and competing microbes. We recast the expansion of Enterobacter sp. SM3 swarms as a problem of morphological forecasting, and assemble SwarmEvo, a time-lapse dataset represented as boundary-resolved segmentations. TexPol--Net, a texture- and geometry-aware segmentation model, sharpens diffuse edges and preserves fingered fronts, creating a stable substrate for dynamics. On this representation, we develop Morpher, an autoregressive forecasting network with a ``Morphon'' memory that links local curvature to long-range temporal dependencies. Morpher outperforms leading video-prediction models in maintaining front localization and anisotropic branching, and modest segmentation improvements yield noticeably more stable forecasts. Ablations across sequence models, inference strategies and observation ratios show that attention-based architectures with structural memory best preserve dense-finger propagation. By uniting geometry-aware segmentation with morphology-level forecasting, this framework turns swarming expansion into a predictive dynamical system, enabling quantitative interrogation and potential control of microbial collectives during mucosal repair and gut ecosystem engineering.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01056v1 公告类型：新 摘要：粘膜表面的微生物群落重塑微生物群落并影响粘膜愈合和抗生素耐受性。然而，即使有了延时显微镜和深度学习，对蜂拥而至的菌落的分析仍然是描述性的，无法预测它们的前沿如何及时重组。这种限制很重要，因为前进的边缘决定了营养物质、宿主组织和竞争微生物的获取。我们重新构建了肠杆菌属的扩展。 SM3 群作为形态预测问题，并组装了 SwarmEvo，一个表示为边界解析分割的延时数据集。 TexPol--Net 是一种纹理和几何感知分割模型，可锐化漫反射边缘并保留指状前沿，为动态创建稳定的基底。在这种表示上，我们开发了 Morpher，一种自回归预测网络，具有“Morphon”内存，可将局部曲率与长期时间依赖性联系起来。 Morpher 在保持前端定位和各向异性分支方面优于领先的视频预测模型，并且适度的分割改进可产生明显更稳定的预测。序列模型、推理策略和观察比率的消融表明，具有结构记忆的基于注意力的架构最好地保留密集指传播。通过将几何感知分割与形态水平预测相结合，该框架将群体扩张转变为预测动力系统，从而在粘膜修复和肠道生态系统工程过程中实现对微生物群体的定量询问和潜在控制。
