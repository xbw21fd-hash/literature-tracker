## Abstract
arXiv:2510.21170v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Inhomogeneous mixing and the consequent mesoscopic layered structure have been observed in many physical systems, including magnetically confined fusion plasmas. Especially, in plasmas, mixing can be enhanced through turbulence spreading by intermittent coherent structures (blobs/voids), or suppressed due to the formation of transport barriers (sheared zonal flows). Interestingly, blobs/voids and zonal flows are not independent, and they can co-exist in a state of inhomogeneous mixing, often called the E x B staircase. In this paper, we first introduce recent experimental progress on the physics of blobs/voids: how turbulence spreading by blobs/voids occurs, the consequences of enhanced turbulence spreading for the scrape-off layer (SOL) power decay length, and the interaction between blobs/voids and zonal flows. Then, we provide a brief review of experimental results on staircases, or more generally layered mesoscopic transport barriers. Staircases are often elusive in experiments, requiring integrating multi-diagnostic data, utilizing high-dimensional diagnostics, or extracting hidden information from signals for their identification. Our understanding is still incomplete. This paper serves as an initial step toward applying insights gained from inhomogeneous mixing due to blobs/voids to the understanding of a staircase.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.21170v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在许多物理系统中都观察到不均匀混合和随之而来的介观层状结构，包括磁约束聚变等离子体。特别是，在等离子体中，混合可以通过间歇性相干结构（斑点/空隙）的湍流扩散来增强，或者由于传输障碍（剪切层流）的形成而被抑制。有趣的是，斑点/空隙和区域流并不是独立的，它们可以以不均匀混合的状态共存，通常称为 E x B 阶梯。在本文中，我们首先介绍了斑点/空隙物理学的最新实验进展：斑点/空隙的湍流传播是如何发生的，增强的湍流传播对刮掉层（SOL）功率衰减长度的影响，以及斑点/空隙与纬向流之间的相互作用。然后，我们简要回顾了楼梯或更普遍的分层介观运输障碍的实验结果。楼梯在实验中通常难以捉摸，需要集成多诊断数据、利用高维诊断或从信号中提取隐藏信息进行识别。我们的理解仍然不完整。本文是将从斑点/空隙导致的不均匀混合中获得的见解应用于理解楼梯的第一步。
