## Abstract
Author(s): Filippo Radicchi, Filipi N. Silva, Alessandro Flammini, Santo Fortunato, and Sadamori Kojaku Community detection is one of the most debated issues in network theory. Here, the authors study the detectability limits in the case of undirected weighted networks. The results indicate that large variability in edge weights can make communities less detectable. #WellStructured #ClearMotivation #TechnicalAdvancement [Phys. Rev.
