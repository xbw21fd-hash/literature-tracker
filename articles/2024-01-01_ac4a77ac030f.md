## Abstract
TLDR: A machine-learning surrogate model is harnessed to qualitatively explore the structural landscape of neutral point defects and predicts favourable reconstructions for unseen defects in unseen compositions for 90% of cases, thereby reducing the number of first-principles calculations by 73%.
