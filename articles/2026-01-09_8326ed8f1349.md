## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04472v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Optical-electrical-optical (OEO) converters are key primitives for low-latency, energy-efficient photonic computing because they enable nonlinear activation and optical signal regeneration on chip. We report two monolithically integrated silicon-photonic OEO converters-load-resistor (high-speed variant) and current-injection (high-gain variant) types-fabricated at a silicon photonics foundry. Each device combines a germanium photodetector with a micro-ring modulator (MRM). The converters exhibit reconfigurable nonlinear transfer functions and measurable on-chip RF OEO gain. The RF OEO gain scales linearly with the MRM bias power, with slopes of 0.10 mW^-1 (load-resistor of 10 k{\Omega}) and 1.4 mW^-1 (current-injection), enabling a gain > 1 region at practical bias powers (~10 mW and ~1 mW, respectively). Eye diagrams confirm clear openings up to 4 Gb/s for a high-speed load-resistor variant with a 500-{\Omega} load. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first experimental demonstration of a monolithically integrated, foundry-fabricated silicon-photonic load-resistor type OEO converter exhibiting reconfigurable nonlinear transfer and on-chip RF OEO gain. In the carrier-injection device, the activation slope exceeds unity, yielding 3.9 dB extinction-ratio regeneration. Short-pulse measurements yield 3-dB bandwidths of 1.49 GHz, 160 MHz (load-resistor of 500 {\Omega} and 10 k{\Omega}), and 76 MHz (current-injection), consistent with the RF data. Energy analysis shows an energy-bandwidth trade-off (RC-limited for load-resistor vs. lifetime-limited for injection) and outline routes to sub-pJ/bit operation via reduced capacitance and improved EO efficiency. These results establish silicon-photonic OEO converters as compact, foundry-compatible building blocks for scalable optoelectronic computing and optical neural networks.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04472v1 公告类型：新 摘要：光电光 (OEO) 转换器是低延迟、节能光子计算的关键原语，因为它们能够在芯片上实现非线性激活和光信号再生。我们报告了两种在硅光子代工厂制造的单片集成硅光子 OEO 转换器——负载电阻（高速变体）和电流注入（高增益变体）类型。每个器件都结合了锗光电探测器和微环调制器 (MRM)。该转换器具有可重新配置的非线性传递函数和可测量的片上 RF OEO 增益。 RF OEO 增益与 MRM 偏置功率呈线性关系，斜率分别为 0.10 mW^-1（负载电阻为 10 k{\Omega}）和 1.4 mW^-1（电流注入），从而在实际偏置功率下实现增益 > 1 的区域（分别为约 10 mW 和约 1 mW）。眼图确认了具有 500Ω 负载的高速负载电阻变体高达 4 Gb/s 的清晰开口。据我们所知，这是单片集成、代工厂制造的硅光子负载电阻型 OEO 转换器的首次实验演示，展示了可重新配置的非线性传输和片上 RF OEO 增益。在载流子注入装置中，激活斜率超过 1，产生 3.9 dB 消光比再生。短脉冲测量产生 1.49 GHz、160 MHz（负载电阻为 500 {\Omega} 和 10 k{\Omega}）和 76 MHz（电流注入）的 3 dB 带宽，与 RF 数据一致。能量分析显示了能量带宽权衡（负载电阻器的 RC 限制与注入的寿命限制），并概述了通过降低电容和提高 EO 效率实现亚 pJ/位操作的路线。这些结果将硅光子 OEO 转换器确立为紧凑型、兼容代工的构建模块，用于可扩展的光电计算和光神经网络。
