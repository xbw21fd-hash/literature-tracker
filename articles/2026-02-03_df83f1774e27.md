## Abstract
arXiv:2509.14053v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Music is a structured and perceptually rich sequence of sounds in time with well-defined symbolic features, whose perception is shaped by the interplay of expectation and uncertainty. Network science offers a powerful framework for studying its structural organization and communication efficiency. However, it remains unclear how feature selection affects the properties of reconstructed networks and perceptual alignment. Here, we systematically compare eight encodings of musical sequences, ranging from single-feature descriptions to richer multi-feature combinations. We show that representational choices fundamentally shape network topology, the distribution of uncertainty, and the estimated communication efficiency under perceptual constraints. Single-feature representations compress sequences into dense transition structures that support efficient communication, yielding high entropy rates with low modeled perceptual error, but they discard structural richness. By contrast, multi-feature representations preserve descriptive detail and structural specificity, expanding the state space and producing sharper transition profiles and lower entropy rates, which leads to higher modeled perceptual error. Across representations, we found that uncertainty increasingly concentrates in nodes with higher diffusion-based centrality while their perceptual error remains low, unveiling an interplay between predictable structure and localized surprise. Together, these results show that feature choice directly shapes music network representation, describing trade-offs between descriptive richness and communication efficiency and suggesting structural conditions that may support efficient learning and prediction.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.14053v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：音乐是一种结构化且感知丰富的声音序列，具有明确的符号特征，其感知是由期望和不确定性的相互作用形成的。网络科学为研究其结构组织和通信效率提供了强大的框架。然而，目前尚不清楚特征选择如何影响重建网络和感知对齐的属性。在这里，我们系统地比较了音乐序列的八种编码，从单一特征描述到更丰富的多特征组合。我们表明，表征选择从根本上塑造了网络拓扑、不确定性的分布以及感知约束下的估计通信效率。单特征表示将序列压缩成支持高效通信的密集转换结构，产生高熵率和低建模感知误差，但它们放弃了结构丰富性。相比之下，多特征表示保留了描述细节和结构特异性，扩展了状态空间并产生更清晰的转换轮廓和更低的熵率，从而导致更高的建模感知误差。在各种表示中，我们发现不确定性越来越集中在具有较高基于扩散的中心性的节点中，而它们的感知误差仍然很低，揭示了可预测结构和局部惊喜之间的相互作用。总之，这些结果表明，特征选择直接塑造音乐网络表示，描述描述丰富性和通信效率之间的权衡，并提出可能支持高效学习和预测的结构条件。
