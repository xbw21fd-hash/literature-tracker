## Abstract
arXiv:2511.15876v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Let $A_q$ be the alternating central extension of the q-Onsager algebra, a comodule algebra over the quantum loop algebra of $sl_2$. We classify one-dimensional representations of $A_q$, and show that spin-j K-operators constructed in arXiv:2301.00781 act as K-matrices previously obtained in the literature. Using these K-operators and K-matrices, we construct universal spin-j transfer matrices generating commutative subalgebras in $A_q$. Within a technical conjecture, we derive their fusion hierarchy, the so-called universal TT-relations. On spin-chain representations of $A_q$, we show how the universal transfer matrices evaluate to spin-chain transfer matrices, and as a result we get explicit TT-relations for all values of spins for auxiliary and quantum spaces, any inhomogeneities, and general integrable boundary conditions. In particular, we derive previously conjectured TT-relations. Using the TT-relations, we show that n-th local conserved quantities of the spin-j chains of length N are polynomials of total degree 4Njn in two non-local operators of the q-Onsager algebra. As a result, we give an algorithm of explicit calculation of all local conserved quantities in terms of spin operators. Furthermore, using the universal TT-relations we derive exchange relations between spin-j Hamiltonians and the two non-local operators showing non-trivial symmetries for special boundary conditions, that they commute with all Hamiltonian densities. As another application of our universal TT-relations we propose universal T-system, Y-system and universal TQ-relations for $A_q$, and as a result, universal TQ for the q-Onsager algebra. For diagonal boundary conditions, we also obtain universal TT- and TQ-relations for a degenerate version of $A_q$ known as centrally extended augmented q-Onsager algebra. We finally discuss implications of our results for generalized Gibbs ensemble construction.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.15876v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：令 $A_q$ 为 q-Onsager 代数的交替中心扩展，q-Onsager 代数是 $sl_2$ 量子环代数上的共模代数。我们对 $A_q$ 的一维表示进行分类，并表明在 arXiv:2301.00781 中构造的 spin-j K 运算符充当先前在文献中获得的 K 矩阵。使用这些 K 运算符和 K 矩阵，我们构造通用自旋 j 传递矩阵，在 $A_q$ 中生成可交换子代数。在技​​术猜想中，我们得出了它们的融合层次结构，即所谓的通用 TT 关系。在 $A_q$ 的自旋链表示上，我们展示了通用转移矩阵如何评估自旋链转移矩阵，因此我们得到了辅助空间和量子空间的所有自旋值、任何不均匀性以及一般可积边界条件的显式 TT 关系。特别是，我们推导了之前推测的 TT 关系。使用 TT 关系，我们证明长度为 N 的自旋 j 链的第 n 个局部守恒量是 q-Onsager 代数的两个非局部算子中总次数为 4Njn 的多项式。因此，我们给出了一种用自旋算子显式计算所有局部守恒量的算法。此外，使用通用 TT 关系，我们推导出自旋 j 哈密顿量和两个非局部算子之间的交换关系，这两个非局部算子在特殊边界条件下表现出非平凡对称性，即它们与所有哈密顿密度可交换。作为通用 TT 关系的另一个应用，我们提出了 $A_q$ 的通用 T 系统、Y 系统和通用 TQ 关系，并因此提出了 q-Onsager 代数的通用 TQ。对于对角线边界条件，我们还获得了 $A_q$ 的简并版本（称为中心扩展增广 q-Onsager 代数）的通用 TT 和 TQ 关系。最后，我们讨论了我们的结果对广义吉布斯系综构建的影响。
