## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04981v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Chiral-induced spin selectivity (CISS) enables spin selectivity of charge carriers in chiral molecular systems without magnetic materials. While spin selectivity has been widely investigated, its quantum coherence has not yet been explored. Here, we investigate spin-dependent photoluminescence (PL) dynamics in multilayer quantum-dot (QD) assemblies coupled by chiral linkers. Using circularly polarized excitation in the presence of an external magnetic field, we observe a pronounced modulation of the PL lifetime that depends on the magnetic field magnitude and geometry. The lifetime difference between left- and right-circularly polarized excitations exhibits a field-angle dependence, consistent with spin precession driven by the transverse magnetic-field component relative to the chiral axis. A model incorporating coupled spin precession and decay processes reproduces the experimental trends. These results establish chiral QD assemblies as a room-temperature platform for probing quantum coherent manifestations of the CISS effect, with implications for spintronic and quantum technologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04981v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：手性诱导自旋选择性（CISS）能够在没有磁性材料的情况下实现手性分子系统中电荷载流子的自旋选择性。虽然自旋选择性已被广泛研究，但其量子相干性尚未得到探索。在这里，我们研究了由手性连接体耦合的多层量子点（QD）组件中的自旋相关光致发光（PL）动力学。在存在外部磁场的情况下使用圆偏振激励，我们观察到 PL 寿命的明显调制，这取决于磁场强度和几何形状。左旋和右旋圆偏振激发之间的寿命差异表现出场角依赖性，与相对于手性轴的横向磁场分量驱动的自旋进动一致。结合了耦合自旋进动和衰变过程的模型再现了实验趋势。这些结果将手性量子点组件建立为室温平台，用于探测 CISS 效应的量子相干表现，对自旋电子和量子技术具有影响。
