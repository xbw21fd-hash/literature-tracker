## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24186v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Topological spin structures such as magnetic skyrmions are of fundamental interest and promising for various types of applications in spintronics. Skyrmions have been predicted to emerge also in antiferromagnetic materials where they exhibit superior transport properties. They were experimentally revealed in synthetic antiferromagnets, however, still remain elusive in intrinsic antiferromagnets. Here, we demonstrate the stabilization of topological spin structures in an antiferromagnetic monolayer. Using spin-polarized scanning tunneling microscopy, we observe an antiferromagnetic spin spiral in the Mn monolayer and a collinear antiferromagnetic state in the Mn double-layer on Ta(110). Near the boundary to the double-layer half-skyrmions form in the monolayer as revealed in combination with first-principles calculations and micromagnetic simulations. Our work shows how the topological state in antiferromagnetic material systems can be controlled by the configuration within a lateral heterostructure, resulting in trivial non-coplanar states or antiferromagnetic skyrmions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24186v1 公告类型：新 摘要：诸如磁性斯格明子之类的拓扑自旋结构具有根本意义，并且在自旋电子学中的各种类型的应用中具有广阔的前景。据预测，斯格明子也会出现在反铁磁材料中，它们表现出优异的传输特性。它们在合成反铁磁体中通过实验得到揭示，然而，在本征反铁磁体中仍然难以捉摸。在这里，我们证明了反铁磁单层中拓扑自旋结构的稳定性。使用自旋偏振扫描隧道显微镜，我们观察到Ta(110)上Mn单层中的反铁磁自旋螺旋和Mn双层中的共线反铁磁状态。结合第一原理计算和微磁模拟揭示了单层中双层半斯格明子边界附近的形成。我们的工作展示了如何通过横向异质结构内的配置来控制反铁磁材料系统中的拓扑状态，从而产生微不足道的非共面状态或反铁磁斯格明子。
