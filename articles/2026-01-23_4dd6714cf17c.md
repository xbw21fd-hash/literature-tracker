## Abstract
Chlorine–sulfur oxides (Cl 2 SO n , n = 1–4) are key intermediates in the oxidative and photochemical cycles that couple sulfur and chlorine chemistry in the atmosphere of Venus. These compounds are thought to participate in catalytic processes converting SO 2 to SO 3 and, ultimately, to H 2 SO 4 , thereby contributing to the formation and maintenance of the planet’s characteristic cloud system. While thionyl and sulfuryl chloride have been experimentally characterized, the higher oxidation members of the series remain largely unobserved and their structures and spectroscopic signatures are essentially unknown. In this work, high-accuracy equilibrium geometries, rotational constants, and anharmonic vibrational frequencies are determined for the Cl 2 SO n series using explicitly correlated ab initio methods combined with second-order vibrational perturbation theory. This approach enables a balanced description of electron correlation and anharmonic effects, providing spectroscopic parameters that closely reproduce available experimental data and extend predictive accuracy to the uncharacterized higher oxides. The resulting dataset furnishes the first consistent reference for these reactive species, facilitating their potential identification in laboratory spectra and planetary observations. The predicted rotational and vibrational features are expected to assist in the interpretation of forthcoming high-resolution measurements from ESA’s EnVision and NASA’s VERITAS missions, offering new insight into the complex chlorine–sulfur–oxygen chemistry shaping the Venusian atmosphere.

## 摘要（中文）
氯-硫氧化物（Cl 2 SO n ，n = 1-4）是氧化和光化学循环中的关键中间体，将金星大气中的硫和氯化学结合起来。这些化合物被认为参与将 SO 2 转化为 SO 3 并最终转化为 H 2 SO 4 的催化过程，从而有助于地球特征云系统的形成和维持。虽然亚硫酰和硫酰氯已通过实验进行了表征，但该系列的较高氧化成员在很大程度上仍未被观察到，并且它们的结构和光谱特征基本上是未知的。在这项工作中，使用明确相关的从头计算方法结合二阶振动摄动理论，确定了 Cl 2 SO n 系列的高精度平衡几何形状、旋转常数和非简谐振动频率。这种方法能够平衡地描述电子相关性和非谐波效应，提供能够准确再现可用实验数据的光谱参数，并将预测准确性扩展到未表征的高级氧化物。由此产生的数据集为这些活性物质提供了第一个一致的参考，促进了它们在实验室光谱和行星观测中的潜在识别。预测的旋转和振动特征预计将有助于解释欧洲航天局的远景和美国宇航局的VERITAS任务即将进行的高分辨率测量，为塑造金星大气的复杂的氯-硫-氧化学提供新的见解。
