## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10447v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The origin of cosmic magnetic fields is widely attributed to the amplification of weak seed fields by turbulent dynamos. However, a critical understanding gap remains between the microscopic generation of these seeds and the macroscopic onset of the dynamo. Current kinetic models, often constrained to electron scales, predict premature saturation via magnetic trapping, leaving the generated fields potentially too weak and small-scale to effectively prime magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) processes. Here, using high-resolution kinetic simulations with a realistic mass ratio, we reveal the physics of this unexplored ion-kinetic regime. Under generalized continuous shear driving, used to simulate ubiquitous macroscopic flows, we demonstrate that the saturation of electron instabilities is not the endpoint but a precursor to a distinct, ion-dominated evolution. Massive ions, sustaining the velocity shear, trigger a subsequent filamentation instability that accesses the vast ion kinetic energy reservoir. This mechanism amplifies the magnetic energy by orders of magnitude beyond the electron-saturation limit, expanding the field coherence to ion scales. Our results establish ion kinetics as the essential ''missing link'' that bridges the divide between microscopic plasma instabilities and macroscopic cosmic dynamos.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10447v1 公告类型：新 摘要：宇宙磁场的起源被广泛归因于湍流发电机对弱种子场的放大。然而，这些种子的微观产生和发电机的宏观开始之间仍然存在重要的理解差距。当前的动力学模型通常受限于电子尺度，通过磁捕获预测过早饱和，从而导致生成的磁场可能太弱且规模太小，无法有效启动磁流体动力学（MHD）过程。在这里，我们使用具有真实质量比的高分辨率动力学模拟，揭示了这种未经探索的离子动力学体系的物理原理。在用于模拟普遍存在的宏观流动的广义连续剪切驱动下，我们证明电子不稳定性的饱和不是终点，而是独特的、离子主导的演化的前兆。维持速度剪切的大量离子会引发随后的丝状不稳定性，从而进入巨大的离子动能库。这种机制将磁能放大了几个数量级，超出了电子饱和极限，将场相干性扩展到离子尺度。我们的结果确立了离子动力学作为弥合微观等离子体不稳定性和宏观宇宙发电机之间鸿沟的重要“缺失环节”。
