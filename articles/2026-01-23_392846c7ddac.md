## Abstract
arXiv:2505.16885v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Modern quantum engineering techniques allow for synthesizing quantum systems in exotic lattice geometries, from self-similar fractal networks to negatively curved hyperbolic graphs. We demonstrate that these structures profoundly reshape Bose-Einstein condensation. Fractal lattices dramatically lower the condensation temperature and enhance condensation fluctuations. In a Sierpi\'nski carpet, quasi-degeneracies in the tight-binding spectrum fragment the condensate. Hyperbolic lattices, on the other hand, exhibit condensation features similar to regular three-dimensional lattices, despite their embedding in only two dimensions: The critical temperature increases as the system grows, and the temperature-dependence of the condensate fraction follows the same power-law as for cubic lattices. We explain these similarities through the similarity of the densities of state at low energies. When strong repulsive interactions are included, the gas enters a Mott insulating state. Using a multi-site Gutzwiller approach as well as a simple strong-coupling expansion, for the Sierpi\'nski triangle we find a smooth interpolation between the characteristic insulating lobes of one-dimensional and two-dimensional systems. Our findings establish lattice geometry as a powerful tuning knob for quantum phase phenomena and pave the way for experimental exploration in photonic waveguide arrays and Rydberg-atom tweezer arrays.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.16885v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：现代量子工程技术允许合成奇异晶格几何形状的量子系统，从自相似分形网络到负弯曲双曲图。我们证明这些结构深刻地重塑了玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚。分形晶格显着降低了凝结温度并增强了凝结波动。在谢尔宾斯基毯中，紧束缚光谱中的准简并态使凝聚态碎片化。另一方面，双曲晶格表现出与常规三维晶格类似的凝聚特征，尽管它们仅嵌入二维：临界温度随着系统的生长而增加，并且凝聚分数的温度依赖性遵循与立方晶格相同的幂律。我们通过低能量状态密度的相似性来解释这些相似性。当包含强排斥相互作用时，气体进入莫特绝缘状态。使用多点 Gutzwiller 方法以及简单的强耦合展开，对于 Sierpi\'nski 三角形，我们在一维和二维系统的特征绝缘瓣之间找到了平滑插值。我们的研究结果将晶格几何结构确立为量子相现象的强大调谐旋钮，并为光子波导阵列和里德伯原子镊子阵列的实验探索铺平了道路。
