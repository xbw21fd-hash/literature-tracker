## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00331v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Explainable AI (XAI) is essential for understanding machine learning (ML) decision-making and ensuring model trustworthiness in scientific applications. Prototype-based XAI methods offer an intrinsically interpretable alternative to post-hoc approaches which often yield inconsistent explanations. Prototype-based XAI methods make predictions based on the similarity between inputs and learned prototypes that represent typical characteristics of target classes. However, existing prototype-based models are primarily designed for standard RGB image data and are not optimized for the distinct, variable-specific channels commonly found in geoscientific image and raster datasets. In this study, we develop a prototype-based XAI approach tailored for multi-channel geospatial data, where each channel represents a distinct physical environmental variable or spectral channel. Our approach enables the model to identify separate, channel-specific prototypical characteristics sourced from multiple distinct training examples that inform how these features individually and in combination influence model prediction while achieving comparable performance to standard neural networks. We demonstrate this method through two geoscientific case studies: (1) classification of Madden Julian Oscillation phases using multi-variable climate data and (2) land-use classification from multispectral satellite imagery. This approach produces both local (instance-level) and global (model-level) explanations for providing insights into feature-relevance across channels. By explicitly incorporating channel-prototypes into the prediction process, we discuss how this approach enhances the transparency and trustworthiness of ML models for geoscientific learning tasks.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00331v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：可解释的人工智能 (XAI) 对于理解机器学习 (ML) 决策和确保科学应用中模型的可信度至关重要。基于原型的 XAI 方法为事后方法提供了本质上可解释的替代方案，而事后方法通常会产生不一致的解释。基于原型的 XAI 方法根据输入和代表目标类典型特征的学习原型之间的相似性进行预测。然而，现有的基于原型的模型主要是针对标准 RGB 图像数据而设计的，并未针对地球科学图像和栅格数据集中常见的独特的、特定于变量的通道进行优化。在本研究中，我们开发了一种针对多通道地理空间数据的基于原型的 XAI 方法，其中每个通道代表一个不同的物理环境变量或光谱通道。我们的方法使模型能够识别来自多个不同训练示例的单独的、特定于通道的原型特征，这些训练示例告知这些特征如何单独和组合影响模型预测，同时实现与标准神经网络相当的性能。我们通过两个地球科学案例研究演示了这种方法：（1）使用多变量气候数据对马登朱利安振荡阶段进行分类，以及（2）根据多光谱卫星图像进行土地利用分类。这种方法产生本地（实例级）和全局（模型级）解释，以提供跨渠道特征相关性的见解。通过将通道原型明确地纳入预测过程，我们讨论了这种方法如何增强地球科学学习任务的 ML 模型的透明度和可信度。
