## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20271v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Breaking the memory wall in advanced computing architectures will require complex 3D integration of emerging memory materials such as ferroelectrics-either within the back-end-of-line (BEOL) of CMOS front-end processes or through advanced 3D packaging technologies. Achieving this integration demands that memory materials exhibit high thermal resilience, with the capability to operate reliably at elevated temperatures such as 125C, due to the substantial heat generated by front-end transistors. However, silicon-compatible HfO2-based ferroelectrics tend to exhibit antiferroelectric-like behavior in this temperature range, accompanied by a more pronounced wake-up effect, posing significant challenges to their thermal reliability. Here, we report that by introducing a thin tungsten oxide (WO3-x) layer-known as an oxygen reservoir-and carefully tuning its oxygen content, ultra-thin Hf0.5Zr0.5O2 (5 nm) films can be made robust against the ferroelectric-to-antiferroelectric transition at elevated temperatures. This approach not only minimizes polarization loss in the pristine state but also effectively suppresses the wake-up effect, reducing the required wake-up cycles from 105 to only 10 at 125C- a qualifying temperature for back-end memory integrated with front-end logic, as defined by the JEDEC standard. First-principles density functional theory calculations reveal that WO3 enhances the stability of the ferroelectric orthorhombic phase at elevated temperatures by increasing the tetragonal-to-orthorhombic phase energy gap, and promoting favorable phonon mode evolution, thereby supporting o-phase formation under both thermodynamic and kinetic constraints.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20271v1 公告类型：新 摘要：打破先进计算架构中的内存壁垒将需要对铁电体等新兴内存材料进行复杂的 3D 集成，无论是在 CMOS 前端工艺的后端 (BEOL) 内，还是通过先进的 3D 封装技术。实现这种集成要求存储材料具有高热弹性，并且由于前端晶体管产生大量热量，能够在 125°C 等高温下可靠运行。然而，硅相容的 HfO2 基铁电体在该温度范围内往往表现出类似反铁电的行为，并伴有更明显的唤醒效应，对其热可靠性提出了重大挑战。在这里，我们报告说，通过引入薄氧化钨（WO3-x）层（称为氧储层）并仔细调整其氧含量，超薄 Hf0.5Zr0.5O2 (5 nm) 薄膜可以在高温下抵抗铁电到反铁电转变。这种方法不仅最大限度地减少了原始状态下的极化损耗，而且还有效地抑制了唤醒效应，将所需的唤醒周期从 105 次减少到 125C（JEDEC 标准定义的后端存储器与前端逻辑集成的合格温度）下的 10 次。第一性原理密度泛函理论计算表明，WO3通过增加四方相到斜方相的能隙，促进有利的声子模式演化，从而增强铁电斜方相在高温下的稳定性，从而支持热力学和动力学约束下o相的形成。
