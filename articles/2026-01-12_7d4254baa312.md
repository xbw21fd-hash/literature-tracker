## Abstract
arXiv:2505.21294v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: A network's community structure commonly impacts its functions. For instance, networks seeking synchronisation will see this process follow the topology's hierarchical and community structuring. Herein, the interplay of network adjacency and Laplacian eigenspectra is shown to uncover hierarchical influence and community structure. Ring networks embedded with hubs of high connectivity are first analysed to characterise the differing insights of the adjacency and Laplacian eigenspectra. This understanding is then transferred to the case study of satellite networks composed of intersecting rings. From these scenarios, it is found that where the adjacency reflects a network's structure, the Laplacian detects nodes' influence profiles. The adjacency identifies the number and relative sizes of salient network structures, for example hubs of high connectivity. In contrast, the Laplacian decomposes these structures in sets of nodes with equivalent influence profiles, where influences emerges due to variations in nodes' connectivity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.21294v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：网络的社区结构通常会影响其功能。例如，寻求同步的网络将看到此过程遵循拓扑的层次结构和社区结构。在此，网络邻接和拉普拉斯特征谱的相互作用揭示了层次影响和社区结构。首先分析嵌入有高连接性集线器的环形网络，以表征邻接和拉普拉斯特征谱的不同见解。然后将这种理解转移到由相交环组成的卫星网络的案例研究中。从这些场景中可以发现，在邻接反映网络结构的情况下，拉普拉斯算子检测节点的影响力概况。邻接标识了显着网络结构的数量和相对大小，例如高连接性的集线器。相反，拉普拉斯算子将这些结构分解为具有等效影响剖面的节点组，其中影响是由于节点连接性的变化而出现的。
