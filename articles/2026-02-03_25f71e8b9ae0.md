## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01732v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: BaAl$_2$O$_4$ is a ferroelectric material that exhibits structural quantum criticality through chemical composition tuning. Although theoretical calculations and several diffraction experiments have suggested the involvement of a soft mode in its ferroelectric structural phase transition, direct experimental verification is still lacking. In this study, we successfully observed two soft modes of BaAl$_2$O$_4$ using x-ray inelastic scattering, providing direct experimental evidence for their role in the structural phase transition. Furthermore, we reveal that the soft modes at the M and K points are nearly degenerate in energy, indicating a delicate balance in which either mode could potentially freeze. The K-point mode simultaneously softens toward the transition temperature ($T_{\rm C}$) in a manner nearly identical to the M-point mode. However, the phase transition condenses only at the M point, with the M-point mode stabilizing as an acoustic mode in the low-temperature structure and the K-point mode hardening as temperature decreases.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01732v1 公告类型：新 摘要：BaAl$_2$O$_4$ 是一种铁电材料，通过化学成分调整表现出结构量子临界性。尽管理论计算和一些衍射实验表明软模式参与了其铁电结构相变，但仍然缺乏直接的实验验证。在这项研究中，我们利用x射线非弹性散射成功观察到了BaAl$_2$O$_4$的两种软模式，为它们在结构相变中的作用提供了直接的实验证据。此外，我们发现 M 点和 K 点的软模式在能量上几乎是简并的，这表明任何一种模式都可能冻结的微妙平衡。 K 点模式同时软化到转变温度 ($T_{\rm C}$)，其方式与 M 点模式几乎相同。然而，相变仅在M点处凝结，M点模式在低温结构中稳定为声学模式，而K点模式随着温度降低而硬化。
