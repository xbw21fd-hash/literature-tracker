## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21250v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Photons are among the most important carriers of quantum information owing to their rich degrees of freedom (DoFs), including various spatiotemporal structures. The ability to characterize these DoFs, as well as the hidden correlations among them, directly determines whether they can be exploited for quantum tasks. While various methods have been developed for measuring the spatiotemporal structure of classical light fields, owing to the technical challenges posed by weak photon flux, there have so far been no reports of observing such structures in their quantum counterparts, except for a few studies limited to correlations within individual DoFs. Here, we propose and experimentally demonstrate a self-referenced, high-efficiency, and all-optical method, termed 3D imaging of photonic wave packets, for comprehensive characterization of the spatiotemporal structure of a quantum light field, i.e., the biphoton spatiotemporal wave packet. Benefiting from this developed method, we successfully observe the spatial-spatial, spectral-spectral, and spatiotemporal correlations of biphotons generated via spontaneous parametric down-conversion, revealing rich local and nonlocal spatiotemporal structure in quantum light fields. This method will further advance the understanding of the dynamics in nonlinear quantum optics and expand the potential of photons for applications in quantum communication and quantum computing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21250v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：光子因其丰富的自由度（DoF）（包括各种时空结构）而成为量子信息最重要的载体之一。表征这些自由度的能力以及它们之间隐藏的相关性直接决定它们是否可以用于量子任务。虽然已经开发了各种方法来测量经典光场的时空结构，但由于弱光子通量带来的技术挑战，迄今为止还没有在量子对应物中观察到这种结构的报道，除了一些仅限于单个自由度内的相关性的研究。在这里，我们提出并实验证明了一种自参考、高效、全光学的方法，称为光子波包3D成像，用于全面表征量子光场的时空结构，即双光子时空波包。受益于这种开发的方法，我们成功地观察到通过自发参量下转换产生的双光子的空间-空间、光谱-光谱和时空相关性，揭示了量子光场中丰富的局域和非局域时空结构。该方法将进一步增进对非线性量子光学动力学的理解，并扩大光子在量子通信和量子计算中应用的潜力。
