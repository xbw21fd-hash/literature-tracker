## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19796v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Transition metal di-iodides such as FeI2, NiI2 and CoI2 are an emerging class of 2D magnets exhibiting rich and diverse magnetic behaviour, but their study at the monolayer limit has been severely hindered by fabrication challenges due to their air-sensitivity. Here, we introduce a polymer-free method for clean, rapid, and high-yield assembly of hermetically encapsulated suspended samples of air-sensitive monolayers. Applying it to di-iodides enables atomic resolution characterisation of thin samples - down to the monolayer limit - for the first time. Our imaging, combined with complementary first-principles calculations, reveals an unusually small energy barrier between alternate stable stacking polytypes in few-layer films, enabling extrinsic control of the stacking phase. We also observe stable isolated iodine vacancies that do not aggregate to form extended structures, and identify and verify the stability of the various edge configurations of thin samples. These results establish the unique structural characteristics of these materials in the thin limit, and more broadly demonstrate the utility of our transfer platform for creating atomically clean suspended vdW heterostructures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19796v1 公告类型：新 摘要：过渡金属二碘化物（例如 FeI2、NiI2 和 CoI2）是一类新兴的二维磁体，表现出丰富多样的磁行为，但由于其空气敏感性，它们在单层极限上的研究受到制造挑战的严重阻碍。在这里，我们介绍了一种无聚合物方法，用于清洁、快速和高产地组装空气敏感单层的密封悬浮样品。将其应用于二碘化物，首次能够对薄样品进行原子分辨率表征（低至单层极限）。我们的成像与互补的第一原理计算相结合，揭示了少层薄膜中交替稳定堆叠多型体之间异常小的能垒，从而实现了堆叠相的外在控制。我们还观察到稳定的孤立碘空位，这些空位不会聚集形成延伸结构，并识别和验证薄样品各种边缘配置的稳定性。这些结果确立了这些材料在薄极限下的独特结构特征，并更广泛地证明了我们的转移平台在创建原子级清洁的悬浮 vdW 异质结构方面的实用性。
