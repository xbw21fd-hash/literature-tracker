# Intrinsic ductility enhancement in Mg alloys elucidated via large-scale ab-initio calculations

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12202v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnesium is the lightest structural alloy, yet its practical use is limited by its low ductility. Recent studies suggest ductility enhancement in dilute Mg alloys may stem from favorable solute modification of pyramidal I/II screw dislocation core energy difference, activating slip via a double cross-slip mechanism. This work conducts large-scale DFT calculations, reaching ~6,000 atoms, of dislocation energetics in Mg and Mg-Y/Zn alloys. We find that relative solute strengthening effects on pyramidal I and II screw dislocation glide are crucial for cross-slip enhancement in Mg-Y, in contrast to prior investigations, that find solute-mediated dislocation-core energy modification as the main driver. Our predictions align with single- and poly-crystal experimental results and also capture the transition from pyramidal II to I preferred slip in Mg-Y.

arXiv:2601.12202v1 公告类型：新 摘要：镁是最轻的结构合金，但其低延展性限制了其实际应用。最近的研究表明，稀镁合金延展性的增强可能源于锥体 I/II 螺旋位错核心能量差异的有利溶质改性，通过双交叉滑移机制激活滑移。这项工作对 Mg 和 Mg-Y/Zn 合金中的位错能量进行了大规模 DFT 计算，计算范围达到约 6,000 个原子。我们发现，相对溶质强化对锥体 I 和 II 螺旋位错滑移的影响对于 Mg-Y 中的交叉滑移增强至关重要，与之前的研究相反，之前的研究发现溶质介导的位错核心能量修改是主要驱动力。我们的预测与单晶和多晶实验结果一致，并且还捕获了 Mg-Y 中从金字塔 II 到 I 首选滑移的转变。
