## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18215v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Over the past decades, research on two-dimensional melting has established that both first-order and continuous hexatic-liquid transitions can occur, influenced by various factors in the potential energy and system details. The fundamental thermodynamic origins of this sensitivity remains elusive. Here, by decomposing the Helmholtz free energy across three representative repulsive systems, we reveal a universal competition between energy and entropy that dictates the melting pathway. The energetic contribution consistently imparts convexity to the free energy, whereas entropy imparts concavity. A first-order transition occurs when concave entropy dominates; otherwise, the transition is continuous. Further decomposition shows that vibrational entropy drives the concave total entropic curvature, while the configurational entropy's curvature switches from convex (first-order) to concave (continuous), mirroring defect proliferation measured by Shannon entropy. The convexity of the energy is dominated by the inherent potential, with minimal vibrational influence. Finally, we predict and verify that the first-order transition becomes continuous at zero temperature, where entropic effects vanish. Our work establishes the curvature of different thermodynamic quantities as a fundamental principle for understanding the nature of two-dimensional melting.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18215v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在过去的几十年中，二维熔化的研究已经证实，受势能和系统细节的各种因素的影响，可以发生一阶和连续的六方相液体转变。这种敏感性的基本热力学起源仍然难以捉摸。在这里，通过分解三个代表性排斥系统的亥姆霍兹自由能，我们揭示了能量和熵之间的普遍竞争，它决定了熔化路径。能量贡献始终赋予自由能凸性，而熵则赋予凹性。当凹熵占主导地位时，会发生一阶跃迁；否则，过渡是连续的。进一步分解表明，振动熵驱动凹形总熵曲率，而构型熵的曲率从凸形（一阶）切换到凹形（连续），反映了香农熵测量的缺陷扩散。能量的凸度由固有势能主导，振动影响最小。最后，我们预测并验证了一阶转变在零温度下变得连续，此时熵效应消失。我们的工作建立了不同热力学量的曲率作为理解二维熔化本质的基本原理。
