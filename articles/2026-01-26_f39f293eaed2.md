## Abstract
arXiv:2510.02621v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In this paper, we study two years of access card validation records from Bogot\'a's multimodal public transport system, comprising over 2.3 billion trips across bus rapid transit, feeder buses, dual-service buses, and an aerial cable network. From user trajectories constructed exclusively from access records, we derive motifs that reveal recurrent mobility patterns extending beyond simple two-location visits. This approach enables the construction of an integrated origin-destination (OD) matrix covering 2,828 urban zones. Similarity analysis using the Jensen-Shannon divergence confirms the temporal stability of mobility structures across semesters, despite infrastructure changes and fare policy adjustments. From the obtained OD matrices, we derive transition probabilities between zones and uncover a robust power-law relationship with geographical distance, consistent with L\'evy flight dynamics. We validate our model using Monte Carlo simulations showing that reproduces both local and long-range displacements, with similar scaling exponents across time. These findings demonstrate that Bogot\'a's public transport mobility can be effectively modeled through L\'evy processes, providing a novel framework for analyzing complex transportation systems based solely on user access records.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.02621v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：在本文中，我们研究了波哥大多式联运公共交通系统两年来的门禁卡验证记录，其中包括超过 23 亿次的快速公交、支线公交车、双业务公交车和空中电缆网络的行程。从仅根据访问记录构建的用户轨迹中，我们得出了揭示超出简单的两个地点访问范围的循环移动模式的主题。这种方法能够构建覆盖 2,828 个城市区域的综合出发地-目的地 (OD) 矩阵。使用詹森-香农散度的相似性分析证实了跨学期流动结构的时间稳定性，尽管基础设施发生了变化和票价政策调整。从获得的 OD 矩阵中，我们推导出区域之间的转移概率，并揭示了与地理距离的稳健幂律关系，与 L'evy 飞行动力学一致。我们使用蒙特卡罗模拟验证了我们的模型，显示该模型可以再现局部和远程位移，并且随着时间的推移具有相似的缩放指数。这些发现表明，波哥大的公共交通流动性可以通过 L'evy 过程进行有效建模，为仅基于用户访问记录来分析复杂交通系统提供了一个新颖的框架。
