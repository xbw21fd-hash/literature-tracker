## Abstract
arXiv:2510.10752v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Random telegraph signal (RTS) analysis is increasingly important for characterizing meaningful temporal fluctuations in physical, chemical, and biological systems. The simplest RTS arises from discrete stochastic switching events between two binary states, quantified by their transition amplitude and dwell times in each state. Quantitative analysis of RTSs provides valuable insights into microscopic processes such as charge trapping in semiconductors. However, analyzing RTS becomes considerably complex when signals exhibit multi-level structures or are corrupted by background white or pink noise. To address these challenges and support high-throughput RTS characterization, we propose a modular, training-free signal processing pipeline that integrates adaptive dual-tree complex wavelet transform (DTCWT) denoising with a lightweight Bayesian digitization strategy. The adaptive DTCWT denoiser incorporates autonomous parameter selection rules for its decomposition level and thresholds, optimizing white noise suppression without manual tuning. Our Bayesian digitizer formulates RTS level assignment as a probabilistic latent-state inference problem incorporating temporal regularization without iterative optimization, effectively resolving binary trap states even under residual notorious background pink noise. Quantitative benchmarking on large synthetic datasets with known ground truth demonstrates improved RTS reconstruction accuracy, trap-state resolution, and dwell-time estimation across diverse noise regimes and multi-trap scenarios, while achieving up to 83x speedups over classical and neural baselines. Qualitative validation on experimental RTS data when no ground truth is available illustrates practical usability and flexibility for real-time or large-scale analysis in real measurement settings.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.10752v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：随机电报信号 (RTS) 分析对于表征物理、化学和生物系统中有意义的时间波动越来越重要。最简单的 RTS 源于两个二元状态之间的离散随机切换事件，通过它们的转变幅度和每个状态的停留时间来量化。 RTS 的定量分析为半导体中的电荷捕获等微观过程提供了宝贵的见解。然而，当信号呈现多级结构或被背景白噪声或粉噪声破坏时，分析 RTS 就会变得相当复杂。为了应对这些挑战并支持高通量 RTS 表征，我们提出了一种模块化、免训练的信号处理管道，它将自适应双树复小波变换 (DTCWT) 去噪与轻量级贝叶斯数字化策略集成在一起。自适应 DTCWT 降噪器结合了分解级别和阈值的自主参数选择规则，无需手动调整即可优化白噪声抑制。我们的贝叶斯数字化仪将 RTS 级别分配制定为概率潜在状态推理问题，结合时间正则化而无需迭代优化，即使在残留的臭名昭著的背景粉红噪声下也能有效解决二进制陷阱状态。对具有已知地面事实的大型合成数据集进行的定量基准测试表明，在不同的噪声状况和多陷阱场景中，RTS 重建精度、陷阱状态分辨率和驻留时间估计得到了改善，同时比经典和神经基线实现了高达 83 倍的加速。在没有真实数据的情况下对实验 RTS 数据进行定性验证，说明了在实际测量设置中进行实时或大规模分析的实际可用性和灵活性。
