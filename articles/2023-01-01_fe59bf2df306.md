## Abstract
TLDR: The scheme builds on the power of equivariant neural network potentials in combination with parallelized enhanced sampling and on-the-fly training to simultaneously explore and learn the phase space in an iterative manner to construct accurate and data-efficient machine learning potentials for MOFs.
