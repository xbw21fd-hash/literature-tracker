## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19152v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We investigate the post-quench dynamics of the quantum geometric tensor (QGT) of 1D periodic systems with a suddenly changed Hamiltonian. The diagonal component with respect to the crystal momentum gives a metric corresponding to the variance of the time-evolved position, and its coefficient of the quadratic term in time is the group-velocity variance, signaling ballistic wavepacket dispersion. The other diagonal QGT component with respect to time reveals the energy variance. The off-diagonal QGT component features a real part as a covariance and an imaginary part representing a quench-induced curvature. Using the Su-Schrieffer-Heeger (SSH) model as an example, our numerical results of different quenches confirm that the post-quench QGT is governed by physical quantities and local geometric objects from the initial state and post-quench bands, such as the Berry connection, group velocities, and energy variance. Furthermore, the connections between the QGT and physical observables suggest the QGT as a comprehensive probe for nonequilibrium phenomena.

arXiv:2601.19152v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们研究了具有突然变化的哈密顿量的一维周期系统的量子几何张量（QGT）的淬火后动力学。相对于晶体动量的对角分量给出了与时间演化位置的方差相对应的度量，其时间二次项的系数是群速度方差，表示弹道波包色散。相对于时间的另一个对角 QGT 分量揭示了能量方差。非对角线 QGT 分量具有作为协方差的实部和表示淬火引起的曲率的虚部。以 Su-Schrieffer-Heeger (SSH) 模型为例，我们对不同淬火的数值结果证实，淬火后 QGT 受到初始状态和淬火后能带的物理量和局部几何对象的控制，例如 Berry 连接、群速度和能量方差。此外，QGT 和物理可观测量之间的联系表明 QGT 是对非平衡现象的综合探测。
