## Abstract
In our paper on “Developments and Further Applications of Ephemeral Data Derived Potentials” 1 we state in the first paragraph of Sec. V C that LaH 10 has a “high hydrogen diffusion coefficient of 1.7 × 10 4 cm 2 /s at 170 GPa and 1500 K.” This is a typographical error. The original Ref. 2 states clearly that the diffusion coefficient is 1.7 × 10 − 4 cm 2 /s. This error does not affect any of the conclusions of our paper.
