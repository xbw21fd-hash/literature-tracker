## Abstract
Nature Machine Intelligence, Published online: 12 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s42256-025-01154-z Xia et al. introduce GerNA-Bind, a geometric deep learning framework designed to predict RNA–ligand binding specificity by integrating multistate RNA–ligand interactions.
