## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10931v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: For precision atomic magnetometry, inert buffer gas is included in alkali-metal vapor cells to significantly broaden hyperfine transitions, which facilitates optical pumping and reduces diffusive relaxation, while also providing non-radiative excited state quenching. We show low-buffer gas pressure (below 50 Torr) alkali vapor cells with resolved hyperfine manifolds can also yield high-performance magnetometers. For high polarization in $^{87}$Rb, we optically pump $F=2$ states with narrow linewidth $\sigma_+$ light, while tuning a probe beam to depopulate $F=1$ states ($\Delta\nu = 6.8$ GHz from $F=2$). The probe tuning then also provides $F=2$ detection with high optical rotation and low probe broadening; we demonstrate top-bottom gradiometry, within a single 25 Torr, 0.5 cc cell, that yields an Earth's field free-precession magnetometer sensitivity of 18 fT/$\sqrt{\text{Hz}}$ with a 1 kHz bandwidth, as well as RF magnetometer sensitivity of 12 fT/$\sqrt{\text{Hz}}$ in a small band about 110 kHz.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10931v1 公告类型：新 摘要：对于精密原子磁力测量，碱金属蒸气池中包含惰性缓冲气体，可显着拓宽超精细跃迁，从而促进光泵浦并减少扩散弛豫，同时还提供非辐射激发态淬灭。我们展示了具有解析超精细歧管的低缓冲气体压力（低于 50 Torr）碱蒸气池也可以产生高性能磁力计。对于 $^{87}$Rb 中的高偏振，我们用窄线宽 $\sigma_+$ 光光学泵浦 $F=2$ 态，同时调整探测光束以减少 $F=1$ 态（$\Delta\nu = 6.8$ GHz，来自 $F=2$）。然后，探针调谐还提供具有高旋光性和低探针展宽的$F=2$检测；我们演示了在单个 25 Torr、0.5 cc 单元内的上下梯度测量，可在 1 kHz 带宽下产生 18 fT/$\sqrt{\text{Hz}}$ 的地球场自由进动磁力计灵敏度，以及在约 110 kHz 的小频段内产生 12 fT/$\sqrt{\text{Hz}}$ 的射频磁力计灵敏度。
