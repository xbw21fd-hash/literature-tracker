## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16979v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Understanding the curvature evolution of the loss landscape is fundamental to analyzing the training dynamics of neural networks. The most commonly studied measure, Hessian sharpness ($\lambda_{\max}^H$) -- the largest eigenvalue of the loss Hessian -- determines local training stability and interacts with the learning rate throughout training. Despite its significance in analyzing training dynamics, direct measurement of Hessian sharpness remains prohibitive for Large Language Models (LLMs) due to high computational cost. We analyze $\textit{critical sharpness}$ ($\lambda_c$), a computationally efficient measure requiring fewer than $10$ forward passes given the update direction $\Delta \mathbf{\theta}$. Critically, this measure captures well-documented Hessian sharpness phenomena, including progressive sharpening and Edge of Stability. Using this measure, we provide the first demonstration of these sharpness phenomena at scale, up to $7$B parameters, spanning both pre-training and mid-training of OLMo-2 models. We further introduce $\textit{relative critical sharpness}$ ($\lambda_c^{1\to 2}$), which quantifies the curvature of one loss landscape while optimizing another, to analyze the transition from pre-training to fine-tuning and guide data mixing strategies. Critical sharpness provides practitioners with a practical tool for diagnosing curvature dynamics and informing data composition choices at scale. More broadly, our work shows that scalable curvature measures can provide actionable insights for large-scale training.

arXiv:2601.16979v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：理解损失景观的曲率演化是分析神经网络训练动态的基础。最常研究的指标 Hessian 锐度 ($\lambda_{\max}^H$)——损失 Hessian 的最大特征值——决定了局部训练的稳定性，并与整个训练过程中的学习率相互作用。尽管 Hessian 清晰度在分析训练动态方面具有重要意义，但由于计算成本较高，直接测量 Hessian 清晰度对于大型语言模型 (LLM) 来说仍然是令人望而却步的。我们分析$\textit{临界锐度}$ ($\lambda_c$)，这是一种计算有效的度量，在给定更新方向$\Delta \mathbf{\theta}$的情况下，需要不到$10$的前向传递。至关重要的是，该测量捕捉了有据可查的 Hessian 锐度现象，包括渐进锐化和稳定边缘。使用这种方法，我们首次大规模演示了这些锐度现象，参数高达 $7$B，涵盖 OLMo-2 模型的预训练和训练中期。我们进一步引入$\textit{相对临界锐度}$ ($\lambda_c^{1\to 2}$)，它量化一个损失景观的曲率，同时优化另一个损失景观，以分析从预训练到微调的过渡并指导数据混合策略。临界清晰度为从业者提供了一种实用工具，用于诊断曲率动力学并为大规模数据组合选择提供信息。更广泛地说，我们的工作表明，可扩展的曲率测量可以为大规模训练提供可行的见解。
