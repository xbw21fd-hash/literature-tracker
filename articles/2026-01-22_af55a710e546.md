## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15087v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The multicritical generalizations of the Lee-Yang universality class arise as renormalization-group fixed points of scalar field theories with complex $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ interaction, $n\in\mathbb{N}$, just below their upper critical dimension. It has been recently conjectured that their continuation to two dimensions corresponds to the non-unitary conformal minimal models $\mathcal{M}(2,2n+3)$. Motivated by that, we revisit the functional renormalization group approach to complex $\mathcal{P}\mathcal{T}$-symmetric scalar field theories in the Local Potential Approximation, without or with wavefunction renormalization (LPA and LPA' respectively), aiming to explore the fate of the $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ theories from their upper critical dimension to two dimensions. The $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ fixed points are identified using a perturbative expansion of the functional fixed-point equation near their upper critical dimensions, and they are followed to lower dimensions by numerical integration of the full equation. A peculiar feature of the complex $\mathcal{P}\mathcal{T}$-symmetric potentials is that the fixed points are characterized by real but negative anomalous dimensions $\eta$, and in low dimension $d$, this can lead to a change of sign of the scaling dimensions $\Delta=(d-2+\eta)/2$, thus requiring a novel analysis of the analytical properties of the functional fixed-point equations. We are able to follow the Lee-Yang universality class ($n=1$) down to two dimensions, and numerically determine the scaling dimension of the fundamental field as a function of $d$. On the other hand, within the LPA', multicritical Lee-Yang fixed points with $n>1$ cannot be continued to $d=2$ due to the existence of unexpected non-perturbative fixed points that annihilate with the $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ fixed points.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15087v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：Lee-Yang 普适性类的多临界概括是作为具有复杂 $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ 相互作用、$n\in\mathbb{N}$ 的标量场论的重正化群不动点而出现的，$n\in\mathbb{N}$ 正好低于其上临界维度。最近推测它们的二维延拓对应于非酉共形最小模型 $\mathcal{M}(2,2n+3)$。受此启发，我们重新审视局域势近似中复杂 $\mathcal{P}\mathcal{T}$ 对称标量场理论的泛函重整化群方法，无论是否有波函数重整化（分别为 LPA 和 LPA'），旨在探索 $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ 理论从上临界维度到二维的命运。 $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ 不动点是使用函数定点方程在其上临界维度附近的微扰展开来识别的，并且通过完整方程的数值积分将它们跟踪到较低维度。复数 $\mathcal{P}\mathcal{T}$ 对称势的一个特殊特征是，不动点由实数但负的异常维度 $\eta$ 表征，在低维度 $d$ 中，这可能导致缩放维度 $\Delta=(d-2+\eta)/2$ 的符号发生变化，因此需要对函数定点方程的解析性质进行新颖的分析。我们能够遵循 Lee-Yang 普适性类 ($n=1$) 直至二维，并以数值方式确定基本场的缩放维数作为 $d$ 的函数。另一方面，在 LPA' 内，$n>1$ 的多临界 Lee-Yang 不动点不能继续到 $d=2$，因为存在意外的非微扰不动点，这些不动点与 $i\varphi^{2n+1}$ 不动点湮灭。
