## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10294v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: A family of ferrimagnets (CoV2O4, GdCo, TbCo) exhibits out-of-plane magnetic anisotropy when strained compressively and in-plane magnetic anisotropy when strained expansively (or vice versa). If such a ferrimagnetic thin film is placed on top of a topological insulator (TI) thin film and its magnetic anisotropy is modulated with strain, then interfacial exchange coupling between the ferrimagnet (FM) and the underlying TI will modulate the surface current flowing through the latter. If the strain is varied continuously, the current will also vary continuously and if the strain alternates in time, the current will also alternate with the frequency of the strain modulation, as long as the frequency is not so high that the period is smaller than the switching time of the FM. If the strain is generated with a gate voltage by integrating a piezoelectric underneath the FM/TI stack, then that can implement a transconductance amplifier or a synapse for neuromorphic computation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10294v1 公告类型：新 摘要：亚铁磁体家族（CoV2O4、GdCo、TbCo）在压缩应变时表现出面外磁各向异性，在膨胀应变时表现出面内磁各向异性（反之亦然）。如果将这样的亚铁磁薄膜放置在拓扑绝缘体 (TI) 薄膜的顶部，并且其磁各向异性通过应变进行调制，则亚铁磁体 (FM) 和下面的 TI 之间的界面交换耦合将调制流经后者的表面电流。如果应变连续变化，电流也会连续变化，如果应变随时间交替，电流也会随着应变调制的频率交替变化，只要频率不太高以致周期小于FM的切换时间。如果通过在 FM/TI 堆栈下方集成压电器件来利用栅极电压生成应变，则可以实现跨导放大器或用于神经形态计算的突触。
