## Abstract
Covalency is implicated in spin-phonon coupling in molec- ular qubits, yet direct, element-resolved measures of spin delocalization in working systems are scarce. Here, we combine multi-edge X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS)– including Cu L2,3-edge XAS to quantify metal character and S K-edge XAS to quantify ligand character–with pulsed and continuous-wave EPR and magnetic circular dichro- ism (MCD) spectroscopy. We correlate T1 with orbital reduction factors η calculated from both EPR and XAS measurements using ligand-field excitation energies from MCD. As symmetry-matched model complexes, we syn- thesized the S = 1 2 dithiolene complexes [Cu(dtsq)2]2− and [VO(dtsq)2]2− (dtsq2- = dithiosquarate) and com- pared them to the maleonitriledithiolate (mnt2-) bench- mark set. Resonance Raman spectroscopy and temperature- dependent pulsed EPR indicate that local vibrational modes do not dominate the observed differences in spin-lattice re- laxation (T1) in these systems, supporting a regime in which variations in SPC are dominated by electronic factors. This integrated analysis lays the groundwork for a covalency– relaxation relationship and shows that η tracks inversely with XAS-derived measures of spin delocalization.

## 摘要（中文）
共价性与分子量子位中的自旋声子耦合有关，但工作系统中自旋离域的直接、元素解析的测量方法却很少。在这里，我们将多边缘 X 射线吸收光谱 (XAS)（包括用于量化金属特征的 Cu L2、3 边缘 XAS 和用于量化配体特征的 S K 边缘 XAS）与脉冲和连续波 EPR 以及磁圆二色性 (MCD) 光谱相结合。我们将 T1 与使用 MCD 配体场激发能通过 EPR 和 XAS 测量计算得出的轨道折减因子 η 相关联。作为对称匹配模型配合物，我们合成了 S = 1 2 二硫醇配合物 [Cu(dtsq)2]2− 和 [VO(dtsq)2]2− （dtsq2- = 二硫方酸），并将它们与马来腈二硫醇 (mnt2-) 基准组进行比较。共振拉曼光谱和温度相关脉冲 EPR 表明，局部振动模式并不主导这些系统中观察到的自旋晶格弛豫 (T1) 差异，支持 SPC 变化由电子因素主导的状态。这种综合分析为共价-弛豫关系奠定了基础，并表明 η 轨迹与 XAS 导出的自旋离域测量值成反比。
