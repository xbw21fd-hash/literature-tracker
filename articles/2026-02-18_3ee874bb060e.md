# Planar Structures of Medium-Sized Gold Clusters Become Ground States upon Ionization

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15646v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study investigates the structural stability of ionized gold clusters of sizes ranging from 22 to 100 atoms, contrasting compact, cage and planar structures. While it is well known that neutral clusters in the upper part of this size range predominantly favor compact structures, our results reveal that positively ionized gold clusters exhibit structural transitions in which planar structures become energetically preferred once the charge is sufficiently large. In addition, we study the finite-temperature stability of the structures and find that thermodynamic effects further stabilize planar configurations relative to their compact counterparts. To explore the potential energy surface, we use the Minima Hopping algorithm combined with a machine-learned potential. Since the machine-learned potential does not apply to ionized clusters, we introduce a charge-correction term to incorporate Coulomb interactions and charge screening.

arXiv:2602.15646v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本研究研究了尺寸从 22 到 100 个原子的电离金团簇的结构稳定性，对比了紧凑型、笼型和平面结构。虽然众所周知，该尺寸范围上部的中性团簇主要有利于致密结构，但我们的结果表明，正电离金团簇表现出结构转变，其中一旦电荷足够大，平面结构就会在能量上变得优选。此外，我们研究了结构的有限温度稳定性，发现热力学效应相对于紧凑结构进一步稳定了平面结构。为了探索势能面，我们使用最小跳跃算法与机器学习势相结合。由于机器学习的势不适用于电离团簇，因此我们引入了电荷校正项来结合库仑相互作用和电荷筛选。
