## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01904v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: There have recently been many efforts to create machine learnt atmospheric emulators designed to replace physical models. So far these have mainly focused on medium-range weather forecasting, where these `Machine Learnt Weather Prediction' (MLWP) models can outperform leading operational forecasting centres. However, because of this focus on shorter timescales, many of these emulators ignore the effects of the ocean, and take no ocean variables as inputs. We hypothesise that such MLWP models have learnt a best-guess of the evolution of the atmosphere, by implicitly inferring ocean conditions from atmospheric states, with no access to ocean data. Turning this limitation into a strength, we use it as a means to study the role of the oceans on the evolution of the atmosphere. By exploring how model forecast errors relate to properties of the air-sea interface, we infer what ocean information these atmospheric emulators are able to derive from atmospheric data alone, and what they cannot. This highlights the regions and processes through which the ocean independently influences the atmosphere on fast timescales. We perform this analysis for GraphCast, finding clear relationships between air-sea properties and the forecast errors over the ocean, including clear seasonal effects. We then explore what this reveals about GraphCast's internal representation of the ocean. In addition to understanding real-world ocean-atmosphere interactions, this analysis provides guidance for improving forecast skill and physical realism in MLWP models, and for informing how future machine learning models should use ocean information on short timescales.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01904v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近，人们做出了许多努力来创建旨在取代物理模型的机器学习大气模拟器。到目前为止，这些主要集中在中期天气预报，这些“机器学习天气预报”（MLWP）模型可以超越领先的业务预报中心。然而，由于关注较短的时间尺度，许多模拟器忽略了海洋的影响，并且不采用海洋变量作为输入。我们假设，此类 MLWP 模型通过从大气状态隐式推断海洋状况，无需获取海洋数据，就已经学会了对大气演化的最佳猜测。将这种限制转化为优势，我们用它作为研究海洋对大气演化的作用的手段。通过探索模型预测误差与海气界面特性的关系，我们推断出这些大气模拟器能够仅从大气数据中得出哪些海洋信息，以及不能得出哪些信息。这突出了海洋在快速时间尺度上独立影响大气的区域和过程。我们对 GraphCast 进行此分析，发现海气特性与海洋预测误差之间的明确关系，包括明显的季节性影响。然后我们探索这揭示了 GraphCast 海洋的内部表征。除了了解现实世界的海洋-大气相互作用之外，该分析还为提高 MLWP 模型的预测技能和物理真实性提供指导，并告知未来的机器学习模型应如何在短时间尺度上使用海洋信息。
