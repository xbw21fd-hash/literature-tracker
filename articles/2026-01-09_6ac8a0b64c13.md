## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04837v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study how fermions in molybdenum disulfide MoS$_2$ interact with a laser field and a static potential barrier, focusing on the transmission probability. Our aim is to understand and control photon-assisted quantum transport in this two-dimensional material under external driving. We use the Floquet approximation to describe the wave functions in the three regions of the system. By applying continuity conditions at the boundaries, we obtain a set of equations involving an infinite number of Floquet modes. We explicitly determine transmissions involving the central band $E$ and the first sidebands $E \pm \hbar\omega$. As for higher-order bands, we use the transfer matrix approach together with current density to compute the associated transmissions. Our results reveal that the transmission probability oscillates for both spin-up and spin-down electrons. The oscillations of spin-down electrons occur over nearly twice the period of spin-up electrons. Among all bands, the central one consistently shows the highest transmission. We also find that stronger laser fields and wider barriers both lead to reduced transmission. Moreover, laser irradiation enables controllable channeling and filtering of transmission bands by tuning the laser intensity and system parameters. This highlights the potential of laser-driven MoS$_2$ structures for highly sensitive electromagnetic sensors and advanced optoelectronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04837v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究二硫化钼 MoS$_2$ 中的费米子如何与激光场和静态势垒相互作用，重点关注传输概率。我们的目标是了解和控制这种二维材料在外部驱动下的光子辅助量子传输。我们使用 Floquet 近似来描述系统三个区域的波函数。通过在边界处应用连续性条件，我们获得了一组涉及无限多个 Floquet 模式的方程。我们明确确定涉及中心带 $E$ 和第一边带 $E \pm \hbar\omega$ 的传输。对于高阶频带，我们使用传输矩阵方法和电流密度来计算相关的传输。我们的结果表明，自旋向上和自旋向下电子的传输概率都会振荡。自旋向下电子的振荡周期几乎是自旋向上电子的两倍。在所有频段中，中心频段始终显示出最高的传输率。我们还发现更强的激光场和更宽的屏障都会导致传输减少。此外，激光照射可以通过调整激光强度和系统参数来实现传输频带的可控通道和过滤。这凸显了激光驱动的 MoS$_2$ 结构在高灵敏度电磁传感器和先进光电设备中的潜力。
