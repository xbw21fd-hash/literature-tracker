## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05814v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Evidence-based education has become a central concept in science education, with meta-analyses often regarded as the gold standard for informing practice. This emphasis raises critical questions concerning the applicability, generalizability and transferability of research findings into classroom practice. It remains unclear both what kind of evidence education should be based on and whether science education research can provide the type of evidence required to guide decisions at different levels. This paper argues that theories play a crucial role in building bridges between research and practice. Drawing on literature from science education and the philosophy of science, we contrast the explanatory scope of meta-analyses with the predictive and integrative potential of theories, understood in a structuralist sense as systems of models with defined domains of applicability. We propose that science education research requires both fundamental and applied research, each contributing to theory development at different levels, ranging from local and context-specific models to more fundamental theoretical frameworks. Importantly, we argue that theories in science education should not be viewed merely as applications of psychological or pedagogical theories, but as fundamental theories in their own right. We conclude that the future development of science education research may benefit more from the systematic refinement and integration of theories than from the continued accumulation of isolated local findings, and we propose ways to support the development of such theories. A theory-guided understanding of evidence-based education can strengthen the scientific foundations of the field while simultaneously enhancing its practical relevance, thereby helping to narrow the long-standing theory-practice gap.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05814v1 公告类型：新 摘要：循证教育已成为科学教育的核心概念，荟萃分析通常被视为指导实践的黄金标准。这种强调提出了关于研究成果在课堂实践中的适用性、普遍性和可转移性的关键问题。目前尚不清楚教育应该基于什么样的证据，以及科学教育研究是否可以提供指导不同层次决策所需的证据类型。本文认为理论在构建研究与实践之间的桥梁方面发挥着至关重要的作用。借鉴科学教育和科学哲学的文献，我们将荟萃分析的解释范围与理论的预测和整合潜力进行了对比，从结构主义意义上理解为具有明确适用范围的模型系统。我们认为，科学教育研究需要基础研究和应用研究，每种研究都有助于不同层面的理论发展，从本地和特定背景的模型到更基础的理论框架。重要的是，我们认为科学教育理论不应仅仅被视为心理学或教育学理论的应用，而应被视为其本身的基础理论。我们的结论是，科学教育研究的未来发展可能更多地受益于理论的系统提炼和整合，而不是孤立的局部发现的持续积累，并提出了支持此类理论发展的方法。以理论为指导的对循证教育的理解可以加强该领域的科学基础，同时增强其实践相关性，从而有助于缩小长期存在的理论与实践差距。
