## Abstract
Over the past decade, magnetic nanoparticles have been actively used in biomedicine to develop new diagnostic and therapeutic methods based on the magnetic response of tissues/cells with embedded particles. A successful application requires the development of theoretical methods for predicting the properties of magnetic nanoparticle ensembles, taking into account the inherent magnetic interactions between particles. This paper presents a simple and universal modified mean-field approach that considers both the interparticle magnetic interactions in a straightforward manner, appropriate for engineering and biomedical uses, along with the superparamagnetic degrees of freedom of magnetic nanoparticles. The presented approach shows high efficiency in characterizing the static and dynamic magnetic responses of superparamagnetic nanoparticles, whether they are suspended in liquid matrices or fixed in solid materials.

## 摘要（中文）
在过去的十年中，磁性纳米颗粒已被积极应用于生物医学，以开发基于嵌入颗粒的组织/细胞的磁响应的新的诊断和治疗方法。成功的应用需要开发预测磁性纳米颗粒整体特性的理论方法，同时考虑颗粒之间固有的磁性相互作用。本文提出了一种简单且通用的改进平均场方法，该方法以适合工程和生物医学用途的直接方式考虑颗粒间磁相互作用，以及磁性纳米颗粒的超顺磁自由度。所提出的方法在表征超顺磁性纳米颗粒的静态和动态磁响应方面显示出高效率，无论它们是悬浮在液体基质中还是固定在固体材料中。
