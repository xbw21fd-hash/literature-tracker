## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05170v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Vertical integration of superconducting electronics requires fabrication strategies that preserve pristine interfaces while accommodating oxidation-sensitive elemental superconductors. However, existing van der Waals-based vertical Josephson junctions largely rely on transfer-based assembly schemes that are incompatible with elemental materials such as niobium (Nb). Here, we introduce a freestanding van der Waals membrane architecture that enables deposition-based fabrication of vertical Josephson junctions through double-sided processing of a single suspended two-dimensional layer. Using multilayer graphene suspended across lithographically defined through-holes in a SiNx membrane, we realize vertical Nb/multilayer graphene/Nb Josephson junctions without ambient exposure of buried interfaces. The resulting devices exhibit clear Josephson coupling, including reproducible supercurrents and a temperature dependence of the critical current consistent with short-junction behaviour. Well-defined magnetic interference patterns governed by the membrane-defined aperture geometry, together with sub-gap features that track a Bardeen-Cooper-Schrieffer (BCS)-like superconducting gap, further confirm the junction quality. This platform establishes a scalable route to vertical superconducting devices based on oxidation-sensitive elemental superconductors and van der Waals materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05170v1 公告类型：新 摘要：超导电子器件的垂直集成需要保持原始界面的制造策略，同时适应氧化敏感的元素超导体。然而，现有的基于范德华的垂直约瑟夫森结很大程度上依赖于与铌 (Nb) 等元素材料不兼容的基于转移的组装方案。在这里，我们介绍了一种独立式范德华膜结构，该结构能够通过单个悬浮二维层的双面处理来基于沉积制造垂直约瑟夫森结。使用悬浮在 SiNx 膜中光刻定义的通孔上的多层石墨烯，我们实现了垂直的 Nb/多层石墨烯/Nb 约瑟夫森结，而无需埋入界面暴露在环境中。由此产生的器件表现出明显的约瑟夫森耦合，包括可再现的超电流以及与短结行为一致的临界电流的温度依赖性。由膜定义的孔径几何形状控制的明确的磁干涉图案，以及跟踪类 Bardeen-Cooper-Schrieffer (BCS) 超导间隙的子间隙特征，进一步确认了结的质量。该平台建立了基于氧化敏感元素超导体和范德华材料的垂直超导器件的可扩展途径。
