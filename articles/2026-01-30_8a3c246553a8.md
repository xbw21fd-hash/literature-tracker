## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21599v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate flux dynamics in the high-entropy alloy superconductor (TaNb)0.7(HfZrTi)0.5 after annealing (as-cast, 500 {\deg}C, 550 {\deg}C, and 1000 {\deg}C) using a sensitive ac composite magnetoelectric method that measures the complex ac magnetostrictive coefficient (d{\lambda}/dH)ac. The resulting vortex phase diagrams show that intermediate annealing (500-550 {\deg}C) induces nanoscale clustering, enhances pinning, and produces a pronounced fishtail effect with successive elastic- and plastic-vortex-glass regimes. Flux-jump instabilities appear at 550 {\deg}C and persist at 1000 {\deg}C, indicating strong pinning and thermomagnetic instability in the low-temperature, low-field regime. Remarkably, the 1000 {\deg}C sample exhibits a two-step superconducting response-a double plateau or drop in d{\lambda}'/dH and two dissipation peaks in d{\lambda}''/dH-demonstrating the coexistence of two superconducting phases with distinct irreversibility and critical-field value. We further show that the resolvability of the two-step (d{\lambda}/dH)ac signature is governed by the topological connectivity of the phase-separated microstructure, which controls magnetic shielding between the TaNb-rich network and the (TaNb)0.7(HfZrTi)0.5 parent phase. These results establish a direct microstructure-vortex-state correlation and provide a route to tailoring flux pinning in chemically complex superconductors via thermal processing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21599v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用灵敏的交流复合磁电方法测量复杂的交流磁致伸缩系数，研究退火（铸态、500 {\deg}C、550 {\deg}C 和 1000 {\deg}C）后高熵合金超导体 (TaNb)0.7(HfZrTi)0.5 中的磁通动力学(d{\lambda}/dH)ac。由此产生的涡旋相图表明，中间退火（500-550°C）会诱导纳米级聚集，增强钉扎，并在连续的弹性和塑性涡旋玻璃状态下产生明显的鱼尾效应。磁通跳跃不稳定性出现在 550 {\deg}C 并持续到 1000 {\deg}C，表明低温、低场区域存在强钉扎和热磁不稳定性。值得注意的是，1000 {\deg}C 样品表现出两步超导响应 - d{\lambda}'/dH 的双平台或下降以及 d{\lambda}''/dH 的两个耗散峰值 - 证明了具有明显不可逆性和临界场值的两个超导相的共存。我们进一步表明，两步 (d{\lambda}/dH)ac 特征的分辨率受相分离微观结构的拓扑连通性控制，该拓扑连通性控制富 TaNb 网络和 (TaNb)0.7(HfZrTi)0.5 母相之间的磁屏蔽。这些结果建立了直接的微观结构-涡旋状态相关性，并提供了通过热处理定制化学复杂超导体中通量钉扎的途径。
