## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00369v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Quantum chaos in isolated quantum systems is intimately linked to thermalization and the rapid relaxation of observables. Although the spectral properties of the chaotic phase in the tilted Bose-Hubbard model have been well characterized, the corresponding dynamical signatures across the transition to regularity remain less explored . In this work, we investigate this transition by analyzing the time evolution of the survival probability, the single-site entanglement entropy, and the half-chain imbalance. Our results reveal a clear hierarchy in the sensitivity of these observables: the relaxation value of the entanglement entropy varies smoothly as a function of the Hamiltonian parameters across the chaos-regular transition, while the imbalance exhibits a more pronounced distinction. Most notably, the survival probability emerges as the most robust indicator of the transition between chaos and regularity. When appropriately scaled, all three observables converge onto a common behavior as a function of the Hamiltonian parameters for different numbers of sites and bosons,enabling a universal characterization of the transition between chaotic and regular dynamics.

arXiv:2602.00369v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：孤立量子系统中的量子混沌与热化和可观测量的快速弛豫密切相关。尽管倾斜 Bose-Hubbard 模型中混沌相的光谱特性已经得到了很好的表征，但在向规律性过渡过程中相应的动力学特征仍然很少被探索。在这项工作中，我们通过分析生存概率的时间演化、单点纠缠熵和半链不平衡来研究这种转变。我们的结果揭示了这些可观察量的敏感性的清晰层次结构：纠缠熵的松弛值作为混沌-正则转变中哈密顿参数的函数平滑变化，而不平衡则表现出更明显的区别。最值得注意的是，生存概率成为混沌与规律之间过渡的最有力指标。当适当缩放时，所有三个可观察量都会收敛到一个共同的行为，作为不同数量的位点和玻色子的哈密顿参数的函数，从而实现混沌和规则动力学之间过渡的通用表征。
