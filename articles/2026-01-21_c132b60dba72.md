## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12892v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Halide perovskites have emerged as promising materials for next-generation photovoltaics, laser sources and X-ray detectors. There is intense debate as to whether some photoactive halide perovskites exhibit ferroelectric behaviour and whether it might be possible to utilise the bulk photovoltaic effect to enhance the performance of halide perovskite solar cells. Here, using low-dose scanning transmission electron microscopy, we discover the existence of ferroelastic twin boundaries in vapor-deposited CsPbI3 thin films, parallel to {110} and {112}. Remarkably, despite photoactive CsPbI3 being centrosymmetric and non-polar, we observe directly that Pb atoms shift at {110} twin boundaries driving a local ferroelectric-like polarisation. These polar twin walls form an intrinsic array of nanoscale functional interfaces, spaced ~30-50 nm apart, embedded within the non-polar perovskite lattice. In contrast, {112} twin boundaries remain non-polar but strongly suppress octahedral tilt and off-centre Cs atom displacements, revealing a different untapped ferroic degree of freedom. These discoveries together uncover previously hidden ferroic functionality in halide perovskite semiconductors, opening opportunities for enhanced conductivity and photovoltaic behaviour through domain wall engineering.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12892v1 公告类型：新 摘要：卤化物钙钛矿已成为下一代光伏、激光源和 X 射线探测器的有前途的材料。关于某些光敏卤化物钙钛矿是否表现出铁电行为以及是否有可能利用体光伏效应来增强卤化物钙钛矿太阳能电池的性能，存在激烈的争论。在这里，使用低剂量扫描透射电子显微镜，我们发现气相沉积 CsPbI3 薄膜中存在平行于 {110} 和 {112} 的铁弹性孪晶边界。值得注意的是，尽管光活性 CsPbI3 是中心对称且非极性的，但我们直接观察到 Pb 原子在 {110} 孪晶边界处移动，驱动局部铁电状极化。这些极性双壁形成纳米级功能界面的固有阵列，间隔约 30-50 nm，嵌入非极性钙钛矿晶格内。相比之下，{112}孪晶边界仍然是非极性的，但强烈抑制八面体倾斜和偏心Cs原子位移，揭示了不同的未开发的铁自由度。这些发现共同揭示了卤化物钙钛矿半导体中先前隐藏的铁功能，为通过畴壁工程增强导电性和光伏行为提供了机会。
