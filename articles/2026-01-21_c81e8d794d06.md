## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13598v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Although the classification of crystalline materials can be generally handled by momentum-space-based approaches, topological classification of aperiodic materials remains an outstanding challenge, as the absence of translational symmetry renders such conventional approaches inapplicable. Here, we present a numerically efficient real-space framework for classifying parity-based $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topology in aperiodic systems based on the spectral localizer framework and the direct computation of the sign of a Pfaffian associated with a large sparse skew-symmetric matrix. Unlike projector-based or momentum-space-based approaches, our method does not rely on translational symmetry, spectral gaps in the Hamiltonian's bulk, or gapped auxiliary operators such as spin projections, and instead provides a local, energy-resolved topological invariant accompanied by an intrinsic measure of topological protection. A central contribution of this work is the development of a scalable sparse factorization algorithm that enables the reliable determination of the Pfaffian's sign for large sparse matrices, making the approach practical to realistic physical materials. We apply this framework to identify the quantum spin Hall effect in quasicrystalline class AII systems, including gapless heterostructures, and to diagnose fragile topology in a large $C_2 \mathcal{T}$-symmetric photonic quasicrystal. Overall, our results demonstrate that the spectral localizer, combined with efficient sparse numerical methods, provides a unified and robust tool for diagnosing parity-based topological phases in aperiodic electronic, photonic, and acoustic materials where conventional band-theoretic indexes are inapplicable.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13598v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：虽然晶体材料的分类通常可以通过基于动量空间的方法来处理，但非周期材料的拓扑分类仍然是一个突出的挑战，因为缺乏平移对称性使得这种传统方法不适用。在这里，我们提出了一种数值高效的实空间框架，用于基于谱定位器框架和与大型稀疏斜对称矩阵相关的普法夫符号的直接​​计算，对非周期系统中基于奇偶校验的 $\mathbb{Z}_2$ 拓扑进行分类。与基于投影仪或基于动量空间的方法不同，我们的方法不依赖于平移对称性、哈密顿量中的光谱间隙或有间隙的辅助算子（例如自旋投影），而是提供局部的、能量分辨的拓扑不变量，并伴随着拓扑保护的内在测量。这项工作的一个核心贡献是开发了一种可扩展的稀疏分解算法，该算法能够可靠地确定大型稀疏矩阵的普法夫符号，从而使该方法适用于现实的物理材料。我们应用这个框架来识别准晶 AII 类系统（包括无间隙异质结构）中的量子自旋霍尔效应，并诊断大型 $C_2 \mathcal{T}$ 对称光子准晶体中的脆弱拓扑。总的来说，我们的结果表明，光谱定位器与高效的稀疏数值方法相结合，为诊断非周期电子、光子和声学材料中基于奇偶校验的拓扑相提供了一个统一而强大的工具，而传统的能带理论指标不适用。
