## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15210v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The inverse design of metasurfaces faces inherent challenges due to the nonlinear and highly complex relationship between geometric configurations and their electromagnetic behavior. Traditional optimization approaches often suffer from excessive computational demands and a tendency to converge to suboptimal solutions. This study presents a diffusion-based generative framework that incorporates a dedicated consistency constraint and advanced posterior sampling methods to ensure adherence to desired electromagnetic specifications. Through rigorous validation on small-scale metasurface configurations, the proposed approach demonstrates marked enhancements in both accuracy and reliability of the generated designs.Furthermore, we introduce a scalable methodology that extends inverse design capabilities to large-scale metasurfaces, validated for configurations of up to $98 \times 98$ nanopillars. Notably, this approach enables rapid design generation completed in minute by leveraging models trained on substantially smaller arrays ($23 \times 23$). These innovations establish a robust and efficient framework for high-precision metasurface inverse design.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15210v1 发布类型：新 摘要：由于几何构型与其电磁行为之间的非线性且高度复杂的关系，超表面的逆设计面临着固有的挑战。传统的优化方法常常面临过多的计算需求，并且倾向于收敛到次优解决方案。这项研究提出了一种基于扩散的生成框架，该框架结合了专用的一致性约束和先进的后验采样方法，以确保遵守所需的电磁规范。通过对小规模超表面配置的严格验证，所提出的方法证明了所生成设计的准确性和可靠性的显着增强。此外，我们引入了一种可扩展的方法，将逆向设计能力扩展到大规模超表面，并针对高达 98 美元×98 美元的纳米柱的配置进行了验证。值得注意的是，这种方法通过利用在更小的阵列（23 美元×23 美元）上训练的模型，可以在几分钟内完成快速设计生成。这些创新为高精度超表面逆设计建立了强大而高效的框架。
