## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15340v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Physical neural networks typically train linear synaptic weights while treating device nonlinearities as fixed. We show the opposite - by training the synaptic nonlinearity itself, as in Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (KAN) architectures, we yield markedly higher task performance per physical resource and improved performance-parameter scaling than conventional linear weight-based networks, demonstrating ability of KAN topologies to exploit reconfigurable nonlinear physical dynamics. We experimentally realise physical KANs in silicon-on-insulator devices we term 'Synaptic Nonlinear Elements' (SYNEs), operating at room temperature, 0.1-1 microampere currents, and 2 MHz speeds with no observed degradation over 10^13 measurements and months-long timescales. We demonstrate nonlinear function regression, classification, and prediction of Li-Ion battery dynamics from noisy real-world multi-sensor data. Physical KANs outperform equivalently-parameterised software multilayer perceptron networks across all tasks, with up to two orders of magnitude fewer parameters, and two orders of magnitude fewer devices than linear weight based physical networks. These results establish learned physical nonlinearity as a hardware-native computational primitive for compact and efficient learning systems, and SYNE devices as effective substrates for heterogenous nonlinear computing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15340v1 公告类型：新 摘要：物理神经网络通常训练线性突触权重，同时将设备非线性视为固定。我们展示了相反的情况 - 通过训练突触非线性本身，如在 Kolmogorov-Arnold 网络 (KAN) 架构中，与传统的基于权重的线性网络相比，我们在每个物理资源上获得了显着更高的任务性能，并改进了性能参数缩放，证明了 KAN 拓扑利用可重新配置的非线性物理动力学的能力。我们通过实验在绝缘体上硅器件中实现了物理 KAN，我们称之为“突触非线性元件”(SYNE)，在室温、0.1-1 微安电流和 2 MHz 速度下运行，在 10^13 次测量和长达数月的时间尺度内没有观察到退化。我们通过嘈杂的现实世界多传感器数据演示了锂离子电池动力学的非线性函数回归、分类和预测。物理 KAN 在所有任务中都优于同等参数化的软件多层感知器网络，与基于线性权重的物理网络相比，参数减少了两个数量级，设备数量减少了两个数量级。这些结果将学习的物理非线性确立为紧凑高效学习系统的硬件本机计算原语，并将 SYNE 设备确立为异构非线性计算的有效基础。
