## Abstract
arXiv:2410.09002v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Full Waveform Inversion (FWI) is a critical technique in subsurface imaging, aiming to reconstruct high-resolution subsurface properties from surface measurements. Acoustic FWI involves two physical modalities, seismic waveforms and velocity maps, which are governed by the acoustic wave equation. Prior works primarily focus on the inverse problem, modeling the relationship between seismic and velocity as an image-to-image translation task. In this work, we study their relationship from a generative perspective. Our aim is to explore and characterize the latent space structure, and identify latent vectors that generate seismic-velocity pairs consistent with the governing partial differential equation (PDE). Specifically, we model seismic and velocity data jointly from a shared latent space via a diffusion process. In experiments, we find that diffusion progressively refines arbitrary latent vectors into ones that yield approximately physics-consistent seismic-velocity pairs, even without explicit physics constraints. This provides empirical evidence of PDE-consistency in latent diffusion, where sampling is biased toward PDE-valid solutions. In latent space, satisfying the acoustic wave equation can be approximated through sampling and gradient descent. We formalize this physics-consistent latent modeling task and quantify it through extensive experiments. On large-scale OpenFWI benchmarks, our approach produces high-fidelity, diverse, and physically consistent seismic-velocity pairs, demonstrating the potential of a data-driven latent diffusion for physically consistent generation in a complex scientific domain.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2410.09002v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：全波形反演 (FWI) 是地下成像的一项关键技术，旨在从地表测量中重建高分辨率的地下特性。声学 FWI 涉及两种物理模态：地震波形和速度图，它们由声波方程控制。先前的工作主要关注反演问题，将地震和速度之间的关系建模为图像到图像的转换任务。在这项工作中，我们从生成的角度研究它们的关系。我们的目标是探索和表征潜在空间结构，并识别生成与控制偏微分方程（PDE）一致的地震速度对的潜在矢量。具体来说，我们通过扩散过程对来自共享潜在空间的地震和速度数据进行联合建模。在实验中，我们发现扩散逐渐将任意潜在向量细化为产生近似物理一致的地震速度对的向量，即使没有明确的物理约束。这提供了潜在扩散中 PDE 一致性的经验证据，其中采样偏向于 PDE 有效解。在潜在空间中，可以通过采样和梯度下降来近似满足声波方程。我们将这种物理一致的潜在建模任务形式化，并通过大量实验对其进行量化。在大规模 OpenFWI 基准上，我们的方法生成高保真、多样化且物理一致的地震速度对，展示了数据驱动的潜在扩散在复杂科学领域中实现物理一致生成的潜力。
