## Abstract
arXiv:2505.02457v4 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Commercially used carbonate-based electrolytes in lithium-ion batteries are susceptible to many challenges, including flammability, volatility, and lower thermal stability. Solvated ionic liquids of LiTFSI salt (lithium bis(trifluoromethylsulfonyl)-amide) and glyme-based solvents are potential alternative candidates for commonly used electrolytes. We perform classical molecular dynamics (MD) simulations study the effect of concentration and temperature on the translational and rotational dynamics. The radial distribution function shows stronger coordination of Li$^+$ ions with tetraglyme(G4), as shown in earlier studies, and forms a stable [Li(G4)]$^+$ cation complex. The self-diffusion coefficients are lower than the values experimentally observed but show better improvement over other classical force fields. An increase in the salt concentrations leads to a higher viscosity of the system and reduces the overall ionic mobility of Li$^{+}$ ions. Diluting the system with a larger number of glyme molecules leads to shorter rotational relaxation times for both TFSI and tetraglyme. Ion-residence times show that Li$^+$ ions form stable and long-lasting complexes with G4 molecules than TFSI anions. The residence time of [Li(G4)]$^+$ complex increases at higher salt concentrations due to the availability of fewer G4 molecules to coordinate with a Li$^+$ ion. G4 is also seen to form polydentate complexes with Li$^+$ without a shared coordination, allowing rotation without breaking coordination, unlike TFSI, which requires coordination disruption for rotation. This distinction explains the poor correlation between rotation and residence time for G4 and the strong correlation for TFSI.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.02457v4 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：锂离子电池中商业使用的碳酸酯基电解质容易受到许多挑战，包括易燃性、挥发性和较低的热稳定性。 LiTFSI 盐（双（三氟甲基磺酰基）酰胺锂）和甘醇二甲醚溶剂的溶剂化离子液体是常用电解质的潜在替代品。我们进行经典分子动力学 (MD) 模拟，研究浓度和温度对平移和旋转动力学的影响。如早期研究所示，径向分布函数显示Li$^+$离子与四甘醇二甲醚(G4)有更强的配位，并形成稳定的[Li(G4)]$^+$阳离子络合物。自扩散系数低于实验观察到的值，但比其他经典力场表现出更好的改进。盐浓度的增加会导致系统粘度更高，并降低 Li$^{+}$ 离子的整体离子迁移率。用大量甘醇二甲醚分子稀释系统会导致 TFSI 和四甘醇二甲醚的旋转弛豫时间更短。离子停留时间表明，与 TFSI 阴离子相比，Li$^+$ 离子与 G4 分子形成稳定且持久的复合物。 [Li(G4)]$^+$ 络合物的停留时间在较高盐浓度下增加，因为与 Li$^+$ 离子配位的 G4 分子较少。 G4 还可以与 Li$^+$ 形成多齿复合物，而无需共享配位，从而可以在不破坏配位的情况下进行旋转，这与 TFSI 不同，TFSI 需要破坏配位才能进行旋转。这种区别解释了 G4 的旋转和停留时间之间的相关性较差，而 TFSI 的相关性很强。
