## Abstract
arXiv:2312.03219v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The brain criticality hypothesis has largely only characterized brain dynamics in terms of their self-similarity, although experimental evidence suggests that the brain exhibits significant multifractality. To understand how multifractality may emerge in critical-like systems modeling neuronal activity, we used a neural field model exhibiting neural oscillations and a critical phase transition. We find that multifractality emerges near a synchronization phase transition, and that the pattern of variation of multifractality changes when placing the model at a different phase transition. These findings show that multifractality in temporal dynamics emerges near criticality in neural fields, providing a formal basis for interpreting multifractality in brain recordings.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2312.03219v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：尽管实验证据表明大脑表现出显着的多重分形，但大脑临界假说很大程度上仅根据自相似性来描述大脑动力学。为了了解多重分形如何在模拟神经元活动的临界类系统中出现，我们使用了表现出神经振荡和临界相变的神经场模型。我们发现多重分形出现在同步相变附近，并且当将模型置于不同的相变时，多重分形的变化模式会发生变化。这些发现表明，时间动力学中的多重分形在神经领域中出现接近临界点，为解释大脑记录中的多重分形提供了正式基础。
