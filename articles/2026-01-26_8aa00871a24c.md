## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16735v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: By means of first-principles density-functional theory (DFT) calculations, we perform a comparative analysis of the electronic and magnetic properties of transition metal-doped TiO$_2$. The electronic band gaps of Ti$_x$M$_{1-x}$O$_2$, where M represents 3d-transition metals such as Sc, V, Cr, Mn, Fe, Co, Ni, Cu, and Zn have been determined using the PBE functional within the generalized-gradient approximation (GGA) scheme, and also using the hybrid HSE06 functional. In the context of pure TiO$_2$, the partial density of states (PDOS) reveals that the electronic band gap emerges between the O-2p and Ti-3d orbitals. It is suggested that the Ti-3d ($t_{2g}$) states play a more prominent role in bonding compared to the Ti-3d ($e_g$) states. We performed DFT calculations to investigate the impact of doping with other 3d transition metal atoms, leading to the emergence of impurity states within the band gap. The hybridization between the oxygen 2p orbitals and the titanium 3d orbitals in TiO$_2$ is altered by the introduction of doping with 3d transition metals because of the change in the oxidation state of titanium, shifting from solely 4+ to a combination of 4+ and 3+ states. The calculation of spin-polarized density demonstrates the emergence of ferromagnetic properties, particularly in titanium dioxide doped with chromium (Cr), manganese (Mn), and iron (Fe) with large magnetic moments. Our work demonstrates the significant impact of doping transition metals on TiO$_2$, allowing for the precise manipulation of electrical and magnetic properties, and thus holds great potential for the development of spin-based memory devices with possible neuromorphic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16735v1 发布类型：新 摘要：通过第一性原理密度泛函理论（DFT）计算，我们对过渡金属掺杂 TiO$_2$ 的电子和磁性能进行了比较分析。 Ti$_x$M$_{1-x}$O$_2$ 的电子带隙（其中 M 代表 Sc、V、Cr、Mn、Fe、Co、Ni、Cu 和 Zn 等 3d 过渡金属）已使用广义梯度近似 (GGA) 方案中的 PBE 泛函以及混合 HSE06 泛函确定。在纯 TiO$_2$ 的背景下，部分态密度 (PDOS) 揭示了 O-2p 和 Ti-3d 轨道之间出现电子带隙。建议与 Ti-3d ($e_g$) 态相比，Ti-3d ($t_{2g}$) 态在键合中发挥更重要的作用。我们进行了 DFT 计算，以研究其他 3d 过渡金属原子掺杂的影响，导致带隙内出现杂质态。由于钛氧化态的变化，TiO$_2$ 中氧 2p 轨道和钛 3d 轨道之间的杂化通过引入 3d 过渡金属而改变，从单独的 4+ 态转变为 4+ 和 3+ 态的组合。自旋极化密度的计算证明了铁磁特性的出现，特别是在掺杂有大磁矩的铬（Cr）、锰（Mn）和铁（Fe）的二氧化钛中。我们的工作证明了掺杂过渡金属对 TiO$_2$ 的显着影响，允许精确操纵电学和磁学特性，因此对于开发具有可能的神经形态应用的基于自旋的存储设备具有巨大的潜力。
