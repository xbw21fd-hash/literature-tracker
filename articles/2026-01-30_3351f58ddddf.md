## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21880v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Each cycle of a quantum computation requires a quantum state initialisation. For semiconductor-based quantum platforms, initialisation is typically performed via slow microwave processes and usually requires cooling to temperatures where only the lowest quantum level is occupied. In silicon, boron atoms are the most common impurities. They bind holes in orbitals including an effective spin-3/2 ground state as well as excited states analogous to the Rydberg series for hydrogen. Here we show that initialisation temperature demands may be relaxed and speeds increased over a thousand-fold by importing, from atomic physics, the procedure of optical pumping via excited orbital states to preferentially occupy a target ground state spin. Spin relaxation within the orbital ground state of unstrained silicon is too fast to measure for conventional pulsed microwave technology, except at temperatures below 2 K, implying a need not only for fast state preparation but also fast state readout. Circularly polarised ~10 THz photon pulses from a free electron laser meet both needs at temperatures above 3 K: a 9 ps pulse enhances the population of one spin eigenstate for the "1s"-like ground state orbital, and the second interrogates this imbalance in spin population. Using parameters given by our data, we calculate that it should be possible to initialise 99% of spins for boron in strained silicon within 250 ps at 3 K. The speedup of both state preparation and measurement gained for THz rather than microwave photons should be explored for the many other solid state quantum systems hosting THz excitations potentially useful as intermediate states.

arXiv:2601.21880v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：量子计算的每个周期都需要量子态初始化。对于基于半导体的量子平台，初始化通常通过缓慢的微波过程来执行，并且通常需要冷却到仅占据最低量子能级的温度。在硅中，硼原子是最常见的杂质。它们将空穴束缚在轨道上，包括有效的自旋 3/2 基态以及类似于氢的里德伯级数的激发态。在这里，我们表明，通过从原子物理学中引入通过激发轨道态进行光泵浦的过程以优先占据目标基态自旋，可以放宽初始化温度要求，并将速度提高一千倍以上。除低于 2 K 的温度外，无应变硅轨道基态内的自旋弛豫速度太快，无法用传统脉冲微波技术进行测量，这意味着不仅需要快速状态准备，还需要快速状态读出。来自自由电子激光器的圆偏振 ~10 THz 光子脉冲在 3 K 以上的温度下满足这两种需求：9 ps 脉冲增强了“1s”状基态轨道的一个自旋本征态的数量，而第二个则询问了自旋数量的这种不平衡。使用我们的数据给出的参数，我们计算出应该有可能在 3 K 下 250 ps 内初始化应变硅中硼的 99% 自旋。对于许多其他承载太赫兹激发的固态量子系统，应该探索太赫兹而不是微波光子获得的状态准备和测量加速，这些系统可能用作中间态。
