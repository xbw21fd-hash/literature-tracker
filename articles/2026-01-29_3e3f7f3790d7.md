## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20315v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In this work, we theoretically investigate how shape-induced anisotropy dispersion and magnetic field amplitude jointly control both the magnitude and heterogeneity of heating in magnetite nanoparticle assemblies under AC magnetic fields. Using real time Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert simulations with thermal fluctuations, and a macrospin model that includes both the intrinsic cubic magnetocrystalline anisotropy and a shape-induced uniaxial contribution, we analyze shape-polydisperse systems under clinically and technologically relevant field conditions. We show that for relatively large particles, around 25 to 30 nm, the relative dispersion of local (single-particle) losses exhibits a well-defined minimum at moderate field amplitudes (between 4 to 12 mT), hence identifying an optimal operating regime that minimizes heating heterogeneity while maintaining substantial power dissipation. The position of this critical field depends mainly on particle size and excitation frequency, and only weakly on shape dispersion, offering practical guidelines for improving heating uniformity in realistic MFH systems.

arXiv:2601.20315v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在这项工作中，我们从理论上研究了形状诱导的各向异性色散和磁场振幅如何共同控制交流磁场下磁铁矿纳米颗粒组件的加热幅度和不均匀性。使用具有热波动的实时Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert模拟以及包含固有立方磁晶各向异性和形状诱导单轴贡献的宏自旋模型，我们分析了临床和技术相关现场条件下的形状多分散系统。我们表明，对于相对较大的颗粒（大约 25 至 30 nm），局部（单颗粒）损耗的相对分散在中等场幅（4 至 12 mT 之间）下表现出明确定义的最小值，因此确定了一种最佳操作方案，可最大限度地减少加热不均匀性，同时保持大量功率耗散。该临界场的位置主要取决于颗粒尺寸和激发频率，并且仅微弱地取决于形状色散，为改善实际 MFH 系统中的加热均匀性提供了实用指南。
