## Abstract
Author(s): Li-Ming Fan, Ming-Gen Li, Tian-Fu Gao, and Jing-Dong Bao Roughness in a potential landscape is usually seen as a kinetic impediment that suppresses the diffusion of thermally driven particles. The authors demonstrate that for systems driven by nonequilibrium fluctuations, roughness on an asymmetric potential can instead act as an effective means to accelerate diffusion. #AdvancingField #TechnicalAdvancement [Phys.
