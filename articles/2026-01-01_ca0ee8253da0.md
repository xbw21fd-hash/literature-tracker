## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24219v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Tuning the valence state and electronic structure of catalytically active sites is crucial for improving Fenton and Fenton-like reactions, which rely on the efficient activation of the H2O2 molecule. Pure TiO2, however, has inadequate activity towards the H2O2 activation and is often constrained by the intrinsic electronic limitations of pristine TiO2. Herein, a rational approach has been demonstrated to improve the Fenton-like catalytic performance of TiO2 through multivalent vanadium (V) doping. A comprehensive characterization using X-Ray Diffraction (XRD), Raman spectroscopy, UV-Vis spectroscopy, X-Ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), Electron paramagnetic resonance (EPR), and Density functional theory (DFT) reveals that V incorporation substantially alters the electronic structure of TiO2. The DFT results, supported by experimental data, indicate that V doping enhances Ti-O covalence and introduces mid-gap states, resulting in a reduced band gap and improved charge transfer. XPS confirms the coexistence of multiple oxidation states of V, which serve as active centres for activating H2O2 and generating OH radicals. As a result, V-doped TiO2 exhibits significantly enhanced dark-catalytic activity in degrading the organic dye Rhodamine B (RhB). Overall, this study provides fundamental insights into multivalent-cation-induced valence state and electronic structure modulation in TiO2, offering a promising strategy for designing high-performance catalysts via defect engineering for sustainable environmental remediation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24219v1 公告类型：新 摘要：调整催化活性位点的价态和电子结构对于改善芬顿和类芬顿反应至关重要，这些反应依赖于 H2O2 分子的有效活化。然而，纯 TiO2 对 H2O2 的活化活性不足，并且常常受到原始 TiO2 固有电子限制的限制。在此，已经证明了一种通过多价钒（V）掺杂来提高 TiO2 类芬顿催化性能的合理方法。使用 X 射线衍射 (XRD)、拉曼光谱、紫外-可见光谱、X 射线光电子能谱 (XPS)、电子顺磁共振 (EPR) 和密度泛函理论 (DFT) 进行的综合表征表明，V 的掺入显着改变了 TiO2 的电子结构。得到实验数据支持的 DFT 结果表明，V 掺杂增强了 Ti-O 共价键并引入了中间能隙态，从而减小了带隙并改善了电荷传输。 XPS证实了V的多种氧化态共存，这些氧化态是激活H2O2并产生OH自由基的活性中心。因此，V 掺杂 TiO2 在降解有机染料罗丹明 B (RhB) 方面表现出显着增强的暗催化活性。总体而言，这项研究为 TiO2 中多价阳离子诱导的价态和电子结构调制提供了基本见解，为通过缺陷工程设计高性能催化剂以实现可持续环境修复提供了一种有前景的策略。
