## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10627v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: One of the long standing puzzles in strongly correlated materials is the microscopic origin of the quantum critical Planckian strange metal phase with universal linear in temperature scattering rate from which unconventional superconductivity directly emerges by lowering temperatures. Recently, the linear in temperature and linear in field resistivity have been simultaneously observed in high temperature cuprate superconductors, manifested by the universal field to temperature scaling in magnetoresistivity. To date, there has been a lack of coherent and unified understanding of these coexisting linear behaviors and their possible link to quantum criticality. In this work, we establish the universality in linear in temperature and linear in field Planckian behaviors in underdoped LSCO near optimal doping. Experimentally, we observe the linear in field Planckian scattering rate and its relation to its linear in temperature counterpart. Theoretically, we propose a spin based common microscopic mechanism based on Kondo-like charge fluctuations near local quantum criticality of heavy fermion formulated tJ model subject to a Zeeman term. Similar to frequency to temperature scaling near quantum criticality, we find the magnetic field here effectively introduces a Zeeman energy, reminiscent of an external energy in the quantum critical regime, leading to field to temperature scaling. Our analytically predicted universal field to temperature scaling in isotropic scattering rate and the relation between the linear in temperature and linear in field Planckian coefficients, unifies these two phenomena over an extended doping range, pointing toward a unified quantum-critical origin of Planckian transport in cuprates.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10627v1 公告类型：新 摘要：强关联材料中长期存在的难题之一是量子临界普朗克奇异金属相的微观起源，该金属相具有温度散射率的普遍线性，通过降低温度，非常规超导性直接出现。最近，在高温铜酸盐超导体中同时观察到了温度线性和场电阻率线性，这通过磁阻中普遍的场温度缩放来证明。迄今为止，对这些共存的线性行为及其与量子临界性的可能联系缺乏连贯和统一的理解。在这项工作中，我们建立了接近最佳掺杂的欠掺杂 LSCO 中温度线性和场线性普朗克行为的普遍性。通过实验，我们观察了现场线性普朗克散射率及其与温度线性对应物的关系。理论上，我们提出了一种基于自旋的通用微观机制，该机制基于受塞曼项约束的重费米子公式化 tJ 模型的局部量子临界点附近的近藤类电荷涨落。与接近量子临界点的频率到温度缩放类似，我们发现这里的磁场有效地引入了塞曼能量，让人想起量子临界状态下的外部能量，导致场到温度缩放。我们分析预测的各向同性散射率中的通用场到温度标度以及温度线性和场线性普朗克系数之间的关系，在扩展的掺杂范围内统一了这两种现象，指向铜酸盐中普朗克输运的统一量子临界起源。
