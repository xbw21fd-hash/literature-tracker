# von Neumann entropy of phase space structures in gyrokinetic plasma turbulence

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00600v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We introduce a data-driven diagnostic that combines the singular value decomposition (SVD) with an information-theoretic entropy to quantify the phase-space complexity of perturbed distribution functions in gyrokinetic turbulence. Applying this framework to nonlinear flux-tube simulations that solve the time evolution of the ion distribution function represented by Fourier modes with the wavenumber for real space, we define the von Neumann entropy (vNE) to analyze the velocity-space structure. A global survey in the wavenumber space reveals a wavenumber-dependent variation of the vNE in velocity-space structure: the vNE remains low at low wavenumber but increases across $k_\perp\rho_{t\mathrm{i}}\sim 1$. Hermite/Laguerre decompositions revealed that the finite Larmor radius (FLR) phase mixing in the perpendicular (magnetic-moment) direction is active. Simultaneously, the systematic increase in vNE for $k_\perp\rho_{t\mathrm{i}}$ correlates with the broadening of the Hermite spectrum, suggesting enhanced parallel phase mixing (Landau resonance) as the primary mechanism for the observed wave number dependence. These results demonstrate that the SVD-based vNE provides a compact measure of kinetic complexity without assuming a predefined basis and enables a global mapping of its wavenumber dependence of phase-mixing processes in gyrokinetic turbulence.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00600v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们引入了一种数据驱动的诊断，它将奇异值分解 (SVD) 与信息论熵相结合，以量化回旋湍流中扰动分布函数的相空间复杂性。将该框架应用于非线性通量管模拟，求解由傅里叶模式表示的离子分布函数与实空间波数的时间演化，我们定义冯诺依曼熵（vNE）来分析速度空间结构。波数空间中的全球调查揭示了速度空间结构中 vNE 的波数相关变化：vNE 在低波数时保持较低水平，但在 $k_\perp\rho_{t\mathrm{i}}\sim 1$ 范围内增加。 Hermite/Laguerre 分解表明，垂直（磁矩）方向上的有限拉莫尔半径 (FLR) 相混合是活跃的。同时，$k_\perp\rho_{t\mathrm{i}}$ 的 vNE 系统性增加与埃尔米特光谱的展宽相关，表明增强的平行相混合（朗道共振）是观察到的波数依赖性的主要机制。这些结果表明，基于 SVD 的 vNE 提供了一种紧凑的动力学复杂性测量方法，无需假设预定义的基础，并且能够对回旋湍流中相混合过程的波数依赖性进行全局映射。
