## Abstract
TLDR: It is proposed that hydrogen intercalation in antiferromagnetic transition-metal oxides is a promising way to realize multiferroics with strong magnetoelectric coupling and it is expected that ion intercalated materials will become a general way to design magnetOElectric and spintronic functional materials.
