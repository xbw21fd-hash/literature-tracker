## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15624v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In this work we introduce a generalized flavor, in the sense of generalized Kohn-Sham density functional theory (gKS-DFT), of the recently derived local potential functional embedding theory (LPFET) [J. Chem. Theory Comput. 2025, 21, 20, 10293], where the in-principle exact formalism of DFT is combined with that of density matrix embedding theory (DMET). In generalized LPFET (gLPFET), the embedding clusters are designed from a full-size gKS system where the (in-principle non-local) Hartree-Fock exchange potential is combined with a local (in the localized orbital representation) correlation potential. The latter is optimized self-consistently such that gKS and local embedding cluster's densities match. Unlike in DMET, which uses the same (global) chemical potential value in all clusters, each embedded orbital has its own chemical potential in gLPFET. We show analytically that, when electron correlation is strongly local, the latter potential becomes a simple functional of the correlation potential. Numerical calculations on model systems confirm the high accuracy of gLPFET in this regime, in contrast to DMET. Moreover, we show that gLPFET completely fixes the flaw of LPFET in weaker correlation regimes, through its appropriate description of the Hartree-exchange potential.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15624v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在这项工作中，我们从广义 Kohn-Sham 密度泛函理论 (gKS-DFT) 的意义上介绍了最近导出的局部势函数嵌入理论 (LPFET) 的广义风格 [J.化学。理论计算。 2025, 21, 20, 10293]，其中 DFT 原则上的精确形式主义与密度矩阵嵌入理论 (DMET) 相结合。在广义 LPFET (gLPFET) 中，嵌入簇是根据全尺寸 gKS 系统设计的，其中（原则上非局域）Hartree-Fock 交换势与局域（在局域轨道表示中）相关势相结合。后者经过自洽优化，使得 gKS 和本地嵌入簇的密度匹配。与 DMET 不同，DMET 在所有簇中使用相同的（全局）化学势值，而 gLPFET 中的每个嵌入轨道都有自己的化学势。我们通过分析表明，当电子相关性很强时，后一势成为相关势的简单函数。模型系统的数值计算证实了 gLPFET 在这种情况下与 DMET 相比具有高精度。此外，我们表明 gLPFET 通过对 Hartree 交换势的适当描述，完全修复了 LPFET 在较弱相关机制中的缺陷。
