## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20082v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Optical diffraction tomography (ODT) is a powerful technique for quantitative, label-free reconstruction of the three-dimensional refractive index (RI) distribution of biological samples. While ODT is well established for imaging thin, weakly scattering samples, it encounters significant challenges when applied to heterogeneous, strongly scattering thick samples such as tissues and organoids. In this work, a novel common-path interferometric approach to ODT is presented, specifically designed for the RI reconstruction of heterogeneous and highly scattering samples at high temporal stability. The proposed technique, termed lateral shearing (LS)-ODT, incorporates partial lateral shearing off-axis interferometry to suppress the effects of multiple scattering, similar to the mechanism in differential interference contrast (DIC) microscopy, which is widely used for imaging thick specimens. Additionally, the LS-ODT system uses dynamic speckle illumination to enhance both spatial phase and RI sensitivity compared to laser-based ODT systems. The effectiveness of this method is demonstrated through experiments on a cell phantom. Its robustness and accuracy are further validated across a wide range of samples, including mouse kidney tissue sections and brain organoids derived from human induced pluripotent stem cells (iPSCs), in both thin and thick sections. Furthermore, correlative fluorescence and RI tomography of the organoids highlights the potential of LS-ODT to enhance and support a broad spectrum of biomedical studies, particularly in the field of histology.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20082v1 公告类型：新 摘要：光学衍射断层扫描 (ODT) 是一种强大的技术，用于定量、无标记地重建生物样品的三维折射率 (RI) 分布。虽然 ODT 非常适合对薄的、弱散射的样品进行成像，但在应用于异质、强散射的厚样品（例如组织和类器官）时，它遇到了重大挑战。在这项工作中，提出了一种新颖的 ODT 共路径干涉测量方法，专门用于在高时间稳定性下对异质和高散射样品进行 RI 重建。所提出的技术称为横向剪切（LS）-ODT，采用部分横向剪切离轴干涉测量来抑制多重散射的影响，类似于广泛用于厚样本成像的微分干涉对比（DIC）显微镜的机制。此外，与基于激光的 ODT 系统相比，LS-ODT 系统使用动态散斑照明来增强空间相位和 RI 灵敏度。通过细胞模型的实验证明了该方法的有效性。其稳健性和准确性在广泛的样本中得到进一步验证，包括小鼠肾组织切片和源自人类诱导多能干细胞 (iPSC) 的大脑类器官的薄切片和厚切片。此外，类器官的相关荧光和 RI 断层扫描突显了 LS-ODT 增强和支持广泛生物医学研究的潜力，特别是在组织学领域。
