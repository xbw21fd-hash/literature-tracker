## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21179v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Non-Abelian topological charges (NATCs), characterized by their noncommutative algebra, offer a framework for describing multigap topological phases beyond conventional Abelian invariants. While higher-order topological phases (HOTPs) host boundary states at corners or hinges, their characterization has largely relied on Abelian invariants such as winding and Chern numbers. Here, we propose a coupled-wire scheme of constructing non-Abelian HOTPs and analyze a non-Abelian second-order topological insulator as its minimal model. The resulting Hamiltonian supports hybridized corner modes, protected by parity-time-reversal plus sublattice symmetries and described by a topological vector that unites a non-Abelian quaternion charge with an Abelian winding number. Corner states emerge only when both invariants are nontrivial, whereas weak topological edge states of non-Abelian origins arise when the quaternion charge is nontrivial, enriching the bulk-edge-corner correspondence. The system further exhibits both non-Abelian and Abelian topological phase transitions, providing a unified platform that bridges these two distinct topological classes. Our work extends the understanding of HOTPs into non-Abelian regimes and suggests feasible experimental realizations in synthetic quantum systems, such as photonic or acoustic metamaterials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21179v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：非阿贝尔拓扑荷（NATC）以其非交换代数为特征，提供了一个超越传统阿贝尔不变量来描述多间隙拓扑相的框架。虽然高阶拓扑相（HOTP）在角或铰链处具有边界态，但它们的表征在很大程度上依赖于阿贝尔不变量，例如缠绕数和陈数。在这里，我们提出了一种构造非阿贝尔 HOTP 的耦合线方案，并分析了非阿贝尔二阶拓扑绝缘体作为其最小模型。由此产生的哈密顿量支持混合角模式，受到宇称时间反转和子晶格对称性的保护，并由将非阿贝尔四元数电荷与阿贝尔绕数结合起来的拓扑向量进行描述。仅当两个不变量都非平凡时才会出现角态，而当四元数电荷非平凡时，就会出现非阿贝尔起源的弱拓扑边缘态，从而丰富了体边角对应关系。该系统进一步展示了非阿贝尔和阿贝尔拓扑相变，提供了一个统一的平台来连接这两个不同的拓扑类别。我们的工作将对 HOTP 的理解扩展到非阿贝尔体系，并提出了合成量子系统（例如光子或声学超材料）中可行的实验实现。
