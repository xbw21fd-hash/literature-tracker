# Current density distribution for the quantum Hall effect

## Abstract
arXiv:2410.07943v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Our microscopic understanding of the integer quantum Hall effect is still incomplete. For decades, there has been a controversial discussion about "where the current flows" if the Hall resistance is quantized. Here, we qualitatively analyze the current density distribution in a Hall bar based on the screening properties of a two-dimensional electron system in the quantum Hall regime. Beyond previous publications, we include a closed loop persistent current that exists inside a Hall bar if the Hall resistance is quantized. We find, that the persistent current density decreases with increasing Hall voltage. Accounting for this dependence, we find, that the current flows in the opposite directions along opposite edges of the Hall bar, while the imposed current flows unidirectionally and only on the side of the Hall bar connected with its higher electrical potential edge.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2410.07943v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们对整数量子霍尔效应的微观理解仍然不完整。几十年来，如果霍尔电阻被量子化，“电流流向哪里”一直存在争议。在这里，我们根据量子霍尔体系中二维电子系统的屏蔽特性定性分析霍尔条中的电流密度分布。除了之前的出版物之外，如果霍尔电阻被量化，我们还包括霍尔条内部存在的闭环持续电流。我们发现，持续电流密度随着霍尔电压的增加而降低。考虑到这种依赖性，我们发现电流沿着霍尔条的相对边缘以相反的方向流动，而施加的电流单向流动，并且仅在霍尔条与其较高电势边缘连接的一侧流动。
