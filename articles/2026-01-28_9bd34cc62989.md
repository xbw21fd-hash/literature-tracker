## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19892v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We report an approach to obtain effective pair potentials which describe the structure of two-dimensional systems of active Brownian particles. The pair potential is found by an inverse method, which matches the radial distribution function found from two different schemes. The inverse method, previously demonstrated via simulated equilibrium configurations of passive particles, has now been applied to a suspension of active particles. Interestingly, although active particles are inherently not in equilibrium, we still obtain effective interaction potentials which accurately describe the structure of the active system. Treating these effective potentials as if they were those of equilibrium systems, furthermore allows us to measure effective chemical potentials and pressures. Both the passive interactions and active motion of the active Brownian particles contribute to their effective interaction potentials.

arXiv:2601.19892v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们报告了一种获得有效对势的方法，该方法描述了活性布朗粒子的二维系统的结构。对势是通过反演方法找到的，该方法与从两个不同方案找到的径向分布函数相匹配。先前通过模拟被动粒子的平衡配置演示的反演方法现在已应用于主动粒子的悬浮液。有趣的是，尽管活性粒子本质上不处于平衡状态，但我们仍然获得了有效的相互作用势，可以准确地描述活性系统的结构。将这些有效势视为平衡系统的有效势，进一步使我们能够测量有效化学势和压力。活性布朗粒子的被动相互作用和主动运动都有助于其有效相互作用势。
