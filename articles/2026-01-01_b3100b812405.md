## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24045v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In this short review paper the detailed analysis of six two-dimensional quantum {\it superintegrable} systems in flat space is presented. It includes the Smorodinsky-Winternitz potentials I-II (the Holt potential), the Fokas-Lagerstrom model, the 3-body Calogero and Wolfes (equivalently, $G_2$ rational, or $I_6$) models, and the Tremblay-Turbiner-Winternitz (TTW) system with integer index $k$. It is shown that all of them are exactly-solvable, thus, confirming the Montreal conjecture (2001); they admit algebraic forms for the Hamiltonian and both integrals (all three can be written as differential operators with polynomial coefficients without a constant term), they have polynomial eigenfunctions with the invariants of the discrete symmetry group of invariance taken as variables, they have hidden (Lie) algebraic structure $g^{(k)}$ with various $k$, and they possess a (finite order) polynomial algebras of integrals. Each model is characterized by infinitely-many finite-dimensional invariant subspaces, which form the infinite flag. Each subspace coincides with the finite-dimensional representation space of the algebra $g^{(k)}$ for a certain $k$. In all presented cases the algebra of integrals is a 4-generated $(H, I_1, I_2, I_{12}\equiv[I_1, I_2])$ infinite-dimensional algebra of ordered monomials of degrees 2,3,4,5, which is a subalgebra of the universal enveloping algebra of the hidden algebra.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24045v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在这篇简短的评论论文中，详细分析了平坦空间中的六个二维量子{\it 超可积}系统。它包括 Smorodinsky-Winternitz 势 I-II（Holt 势）、Fokas-Lagerstrom 模型、3 体 Calogero 和 Wolfes（相当于 $G_2$ 有理数或 $I_6$）模型以及整数索引 $k$ 的 Tremblay-Turbiner-Winternitz (TTW) 系统。结果表明，它们都是完全可解的，从而证实了蒙特利尔猜想（2001）；它们承认哈密顿量和两个积分的代数形式（所有三个都可以写成具有不带常数项的多项式系数的微分算子），它们具有多项式本征函数，其中离散对称群的不变量被视为变量，它们具有隐藏（李）代数结构 $g^{(k)}$ 和各种 $k$，并且它们拥有积分的（有限阶）多项式代数。每个模型都具有无限多个有限维不变子空间的特征，这些子空间形成无限标志。每个子空间都与某个 $k$ 的代数 $g^{(k)}$ 的有限维表示空间一致。在所有提出的情况下，积分代数都是 4 生成的 $(H, I_1, I_2, I_{12}\equiv[I_1, I_2])$ 阶数为 2,3,4,5 的有序单项式的无限维代数，它是隐代数的通用包络代数的子代数。
