## Abstract
TLDR: It is demonstrated that room-temperature antiferromagnetic skyrmions can be stabilized in synthetic antiferromaagnets (SAFs), in which perpendicular magnetic anisotropy, antiferromeagnetic coupling and chiral order can be adjusted concurrently.
