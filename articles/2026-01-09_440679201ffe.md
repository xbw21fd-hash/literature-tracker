## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04640v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We construct asymptotic quantum many-body scars (AQMBS) in one-dimensional SU($N$) Hubbard chains ($N\geq 3$) by embedding the scar subspace into an auxiliary Hilbert subspace $\mathcal{H}_P$ and identifying a parent Hamiltonian within it, together with a corresponding extension of the restricted spectrum-generating algebra to the multi-ladder case. Unlike previous applications of the parent-Hamiltonian scheme, we show that the parent Hamiltonian becomes the SU($N$) ferromagnetic Heisenberg model rather than the spin-1/2 case, so that its gapless magnons realize explicit AQMBS of the original model. Working in the doublon-holon subspace, we derive this mapping, obtain the one-magnon dispersion for periodic and open boundaries, and prove (i) orthogonality to the tower of scar states, (ii) vanishing energy variance in the thermodynamic limit, and (iii) subvolume entanglement entropy with rigorous MPS/MPO bounds. Our results broaden the parent-Hamiltonian family for AQMBS beyond spin-1/2 and provide analytic, low-entanglement excitations in SU($N$)-symmetric systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04640v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们在一维 SU($N$) 哈伯德链 ($N\geq 3$) 中构造渐近量子多体疤痕 (AQMBS)，方法是将疤痕子空间嵌入辅助希尔伯特子空间 $\mathcal{H}_P$ 并识别其中的父哈密顿量，以及受限谱生成代数的相应扩展到多梯子的情况。与之前的父哈密顿方案的应用不同，我们证明父哈密顿方案成为 SU($N$) 铁磁海森堡模型而不是自旋 1/2 情况，因此其无间隙磁振子实现了原始模型的显式 AQMBS。在双完整子空间中，我们导出了这个映射，获得了周期性和开放边界的单磁振子色散，并证明了（i）与疤痕态塔的正交性，（ii）热力学极限中的能量方差消失，以及（iii）具有严格MPS/MPO界限的子体积纠缠熵。我们的结果将 AQMBS 的母哈密顿家族扩展到自旋 1/2 之外，并在 SU($N$) 对称系统中提供解析、低纠缠激发。
