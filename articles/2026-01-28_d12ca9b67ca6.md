## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18865v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We investigate induced Compton/Brillouin scattering of electromagnetic waves in magnetized electron and positron pair plasma by verifying kinetic theory with Particle-in-Cell simulations. Applying this to fast radio bursts (FRBs) in magnetar magnetospheres, we find that the scattering--although suppressed by the magnetic field--inevitably enters the linear growth stage. The subsequent evolution bifurcates: full scattering occurs when the density exceeds a critical value, whereas below it the scattering saturates and the FRB can escape. This eases the tension with observations of compact emission regions and may explain the observed diversity, including the presence or absence of FRBs associated with X-ray bursts.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18865v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们通过使用粒子内模拟验证动力学理论，研究磁化电子和正电子对等离子体中电磁波的诱导康普顿/布里渊散射。将其应用于磁星磁层中的快速射电爆发（FRB），我们发现散射——尽管受到磁场抑制——不可避免地进入线性增长阶段。随后的演变出现分歧：当密度超过临界值时，就会发生完全散射，而低于临界值时，散射就会饱和，FRB 就会逃逸。这缓解了紧凑发射区域观测的紧张局势，并可以解释观测到的多样性，包括与 X 射线爆发相关的 FRB 的存在或不存在。
