## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15774v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a minimal tight-binding model for the electronic interface layer of the LaAlO$_3$/SrTiO$_3$ heterostructure with oxygen vacancies. In this model, the effective carriers are subject to oxygen vacancy induced magnetic impurities. Both the effects of random on-site potentials and Zeeman-like exchange interactions between correlated carriers and magnetic impurities are taken into account. By applying the combined coherent potential approximation (CPA) and dynamical mean-field theory (DMFT) for a ferromagnetic state, we uncover a disordered Fermi-liquid regime for the majority-spins and a low energy scale which controls the transport of the minority-spin carriers, both induced by the magnetic impurities.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15774v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了带有氧空位的 LaAlO$_3$/SrTiO$_3$ 异质结构电子界面层的最小紧束缚模型。在该模型中，有效载流子受到氧空位引起的磁性杂质的影响。随机现场电势的影响以及相关载流子和磁性杂质之间的类塞曼交换相互作用都被考虑在内。通过将相干势近似（CPA）和动态平均场理论（DMFT）应用于铁磁态，我们发现了多数自旋的无序费米液体状态和控制少数自旋载流子传输的低能量尺度，两者都是由磁性杂质引起的。
