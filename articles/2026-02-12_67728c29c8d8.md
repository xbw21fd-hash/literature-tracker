## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10158v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) spectroscopy is fundamental for molecular structure elucidation, yet interpreting spectra at scale remains time-consuming and highly expertise-dependent. While recent spectrum-as-language modeling and retrieval-based methods have shown promise, they rely heavily on large corpora of computed spectra and exhibit notable performance drops when applied to experimental measurements. To address these issues, we build NMRSpec, a large-scale corpus of experimental $^1$H and $^{13}$C spectra mined from chemical literature, and propose NMRTrans, which models spectra as unordered peak sets and aligns the model's inductive bias with the physical nature of NMR. To our best knowledge, NMRTrans is the first NMR Transformer trained solely on large-scale experimental spectra and achieves state-of-the-art performance on experimental benchmarks, improving Top-10 Accuracy over the strongest baseline by +17.82 points (61.15% vs. 43.33%), and underscoring the importance of experimental data and structure-aware architectures for reliable NMR structure elucidation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10158v1 公告类型：新 摘要：核磁共振 (NMR) 光谱是分子结构阐明的基础，但大规模解释光谱仍然非常耗时且高度依赖专业知识。虽然最近的频谱即语言建模和基于检索的方法已显示出前景，但它们严重依赖于计算频谱的大型语料库，并且在应用于实验测量时表现出显着的性能下降。为了解决这些问题，我们构建了 NMRSpec，这是一个从化学文献中挖掘的实验 $^1$H 和 $^{13}$C 光谱的大规模语料库，并提出了 NMRTrans，它将光谱建模为无序峰集，并将模型的归纳偏差与 NMR 的物理性质保持一致。据我们所知，NMRTrans 是第一个仅在大规模实验光谱上进行训练的 NMR Transformer，并在实验基准上实现了最先进的性能，将 Top-10 精度比最强基线提高了 +17.82 点（61.15% vs. 43.33%），并强调了实验数据和结构感知架构对于可靠 NMR 结构阐明的重要性。
