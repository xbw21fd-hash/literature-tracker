## Abstract
arXiv:2509.07302v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Disorder can profoundly influence synchronization in networks of nonlinear oscillators, sometimes enhancing coherence through external tuning. In semiconductor lasers, however, achieving high-quality steady-state synchronization is desired, while intrinsic and typically uncontrollable disorder poses a major challenge. Under fixed frequency disorder, we investigate homogeneous fully coupled external-cavity semiconductor lasers governed by the complex, time-delayed Lang-Kobayashi equations with experimentally relevant parameters and identify an optimal coupling strength that maximizes steady-state synchronization in the weak-coupling regime, which we term disorder-mediated synchronization resonance. This optimum appears for any fixed configuration of intrinsic frequency detuning and scales inversely with the number of lasers, leading to a linear scaling of the total coupling cost with the number of lasers. A theory based on an effective thermodynamic potential explains this disorder-mediated optimization, revealing a general mechanism by which moderate coupling can overcome static heterogeneity in nonlinear physical systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.07302v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：无序会深刻影响非线性振荡器网络中的同步，有时通过外部调谐来增强相干性。然而，在半导体激光器中，需要实现高质量的稳态同步，而固有且通常不可控的无序构成了重大挑战。在固定频率无序下，我们研究了由复杂的时滞 Lang-Kobayashi 方程和实验相关参数控制的均匀全耦合外腔半导体激光器，并确定了在弱耦合状态下最大化稳态同步的最佳耦合强度，我们将其称为无序介导的同步谐振。对于固有频率失谐的任何固定配置，这种最佳效果都会出现，并且与激光器的数量成反比，从而导致总耦合成本与激光器的数量成线性比例。基于有效热力学势的理论解释了这种无序介导的优化，揭示了适度耦合可以克服非线性物理系统中的静态异质性的一般机制。
