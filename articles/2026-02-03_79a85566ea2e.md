## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00141v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a comprehensive comparison of convolutional and transformer-based models for distinguishing quark and gluon jets using simulated jet images from Pythia 8. By encoding jet substructure into a three-channel representation of particle kinematics, we evaluate the performance of convolutional neural networks (CNNs), Vision Transformers (ViTs), and Swin Transformers (Swin-Tiny) under both supervised and self-supervised learning setups. Our results show that fine-tuning only the final two transformer blocks of the Swin-Tiny model achieves the best trade-off between efficiency and accuracy, reaching 81.4% accuracy and an AUC (area under the ROC curve) of 88.9%. Self-supervised pretraining with Momentum Contrast (MoCo) further enhances feature robustness and reduces the number of trainable parameters. These findings highlight the potential of hierarchical attention-based models for jet substructure studies and for domain transfer to real collision data.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00141v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用 Pythia 8 中的模拟喷流图像，对基于卷积和变压器的模型进行了全面比较，用于区分夸克和胶子喷流。通过将喷流子结构编码为粒子运动学的三通道表示，我们评估了卷积神经网络 (CNN)、视觉变换器 (ViTs) 和 Swin 变换器 (Swin-Tiny) 在监督学习和自监督学习设置。我们的结果表明，仅微调 Swin-Tiny 模型的最后两个变压器块即可实现效率和准确度之间的最佳平衡，达到 81.4% 的准确度和 88.9% 的 AUC（ROC 曲线下面积）。使用动量对比 (MoCo) 的自监督预训练进一步增强了特征鲁棒性并减少了可训练参数的数量。这些发现凸显了基于分层注意力的模型在喷气机子结构研究和域转移到真实碰撞数据方面的潜力。
