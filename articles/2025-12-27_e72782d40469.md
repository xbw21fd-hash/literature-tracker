## Abstract
ABSTRACT Electrochemical ammonia (NH 3 ) synthesis offers a sustainable pathway for the chemical industry. However, the fundamental proton-coupled nitrogen (N 2 ) reduction process has led to the competing H 2 evolution and low energy efficiency, particularly at high current densities. Herein, we present the design of a looped Li–N 2 /H 2 battery that decouples N 2 reduction from protonation by two separate sub-reactions of electrocatalytic N 2 reduction in discharging (6Li + + 6e − + N 2 → 2Li 3 N) and electrocatalytic H 2 oxidation in charging (H 2 → 2H + + 2e − ), which are intercoupled into a synthetic loop to enable NH 3 synthesis (Li 3 N + 3H + → NH 3 + 3Li + ) without H 2 evolution. This approach achieves record-high energy efficiency (26.0% ± 0.9%), Faradaic efficiency (63.7% ± 2.3%), and high NH 3 production rate (1 mA cm −2 , 0.12 mol h −1 m −2 ) under mild conditions. These results significantly lower the cost of ammonia production compared to conventional electrochemical methods, highlighting its promising potential for practical applications.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 电化学氨（NH 3 ）合成为化学工业提供了一条可持续发展的途径。然而，基本的质子耦合氮（N 2 ）还原过程导致了竞争性的H 2 释放和低能量效率，特别是在高电流密度下。在此，我们提出了一种环形Li–N 2 /H 2 电池的设计，该电池通过放电时的电催化N 2 还原（6Li + + 6e − + N 2 → 2Li 3 N）和充电时的电催化H 2 氧化（H 2 → 2H + + 2e − ）两个独立的子反应，将N 2 还原与质子化解耦，这两个子反应相互耦合到一个合成循环中，以实现NH 3 合成（Li 3 N + 3H + → NH 3 + 3Li + )，无 H 2 放出。该方法在温和条件下实现了创纪录的高能量效率（26.0%±0.9%）、法拉第效率（63.7%±2.3%）和高NH 3 产率（1 mA cm -2 , 0.12 mol h -1 m -2 ）。与传统的电化学方法相比，这些结果显着降低了氨生产成本，凸显了其实际应用的广阔潜力。
