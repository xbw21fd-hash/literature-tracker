## Abstract
TLDR: Structural transitions of the molecular metal oxide are examined using single-crystal X-ray diffraction analysis, finding that the Na + encapsulated in the discrete metal-oxide anion exhibited a reversible order-disorder transition with distortion of the Mo-O molecular framework induced by temperature.
