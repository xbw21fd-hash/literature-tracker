## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03274v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In Calgary, November 2005, Chad Hedrick was the first to skate the 5,000 m below 6:10. His world record time 6:09.68 was then beaten a week later, in Salt Lake City, by Sven Kramer's 6:08.78. Further top races and world records followed over the ensuing seasons; up to and including the 2024-2025 season, a total of 126 races have been below 6:10, with Nils van der Poel's 2021 world record being 6:01.56. The appropriately hyped-up canonical question for the friends and followers and aficionados of speedskating has then been when (and by whom we for the first time would witness a below 6:00.00 race. In this note I first use extreme value statistics modelling to assess the state of affairs, as per the end of the 2024-2025 season, with predictions and probabilities for the 2025-2026 season. Under natural modelling assumptions the probability of seeing a new world record during this new season is shown to be about ten percent. We were indeed excited but in reality merely modestly surprised that a race better than van der Poel's record was clocked, by Timothy Loubineaud, in Salt Lake City, November 14, 2025. But Six-Minute Man Sander Eitrem's outstanding 5:58.52 in Inzell, on January 24, 2026, is truly beamonesquely shocking. I also use the modelling machinery to analyse the post-Eitrem situation, and suggest answers to the question of how fast the 5,000 m ever can be skated.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03274v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：2005 年 11 月在卡尔加里，Chad Hedrick 成为第一个在 6 分 10 秒内滑过 5,000 米的人。一周后，他在盐湖城创下的 6:09.68 世界纪录被斯文·克莱默 (Sven Kramer) 的 6:08.78 打破。在接下来的几个赛季中，更多的顶级比赛和世界纪录接踵而至。截至2024-2025赛季，共有126场比赛成绩低于6分10秒，尼尔斯·范德普尔2021年的世界纪录为6分01.56分。对于速度滑冰的朋友、追随者和爱好者来说，适当炒作的典型问题是，我们何时（以及由谁）第一次见证一场低于 6:00.00 的比赛。在本文中，我首先使用极值统计模型来评估 2024-2025 赛季结束时的事态，并对 2025-2026 赛季进行预测和概率。在自然建模假设下，概率我们确实很兴奋，但事实上，蒂莫西·鲁宾诺 (Timothy Loubineaud) 于 2025 年 11 月 14 日在盐湖城创下了比范德普尔记录更好的比赛成绩，这一点我们确实感到兴奋。但 2026 年 1 月 24 日，六分钟男子桑德·埃特雷姆 (Sander Eitrem) 在因泽尔 (Inzell) 取得的出色成绩 5:58.52 确实令人高兴。我还使用建模机制来分析 Eitrem 后的情况，并提出 5,000 米滑行速度有多快的问题的答案。
