## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13079v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spike timing offers a combinatorial address space, suggesting that timing-based spiking inference can be executed as lookup and routing rather than as dense multiply--accumulate. Yet most neuromorphic and photonic systems still digitize events into timestamps, bins, or rates and then perform selection in clocked logic. We introduce Polychronous Wave Computing (PWC), a timing-native address-selection primitive that maps relative spike latencies directly to a discrete output route in the wave domain. Spike times are phase-encoded in a rotating frame and processed by a programmable multiport interferometer that evaluates K template correlations in parallel; a driven--dissipative winner-take-all stage then performs a physical argmax, emitting a one-hot output port. We derive the operating envelope imposed by phase wrapping and mutual coherence, and collapse timing jitter, static phase mismatch, and dephasing into a single effective phase-noise budget whose induced winner--runner-up margin predicts boundary-first failures and provides an intensity-only calibration target. Simulations show that nonlinear competition improves routing fidelity compared with noisy linear intensity readout, and that hardware-in-the-loop phase tuning rescues a temporal-order gate from 55.9% to 97.2% accuracy under strong static mismatch. PWC provides a fast routing coprocessor for LUT-style spiking networks and sparse top-1 gates (e.g., mixture-of-experts routing) across polaritonic, photonic, and oscillator platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13079v1 公告类型：新 摘要：尖峰时序提供了组合地址空间，表明基于时序的尖峰推理可以作为查找和路由来执行，而不是作为密集乘法累加来执行。然而，大多数神经形态和光子系统仍然将事件数字化为时间戳、容器或速率，然后在时钟逻辑中执行选择。我们引入了多时波计算（PWC），这是一种计时本机地址选择原语，可将相对尖峰延迟直接映射到波域中的离散输出路径。尖峰时间在旋转框架中进行相位编码，并由可编程多端口干涉仪处理，该干涉仪并行评估 K 个模板相关性；然后，驱动耗散赢家通吃阶段执行物理 argmax，发出单热输出端口。我们推导了由相位缠绕和相互相干性、崩溃定时抖动、静态相位失配和移相所施加的操作包络，将其转化为单个有效的相位噪声预算，其诱导的优胜者-亚军裕度预测了边界优先故障，并提供了仅强度的校准目标。仿真表明，与噪声线性强度读数相比，非线性竞争提高了路由保真度，并且硬件在环相位调整在强静态失配下将时间顺序门的精度从 55.9% 提高到 97.2%。 PWC 为跨极化、光子和振荡器平台的 LUT 式尖峰网络和稀疏 top-1 门（例如，专家混合路由）提供快速路由协处理器。
