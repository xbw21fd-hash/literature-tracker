## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05003v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Liquid metals are central to energy-storage and nuclear technologies, yet quantitative knowledge of their thermophysical properties remains limited. While atomistic simulations offer a route to computing liquid properties directly from atomic motion, the most accurate approach, ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD), is computationally costly and restricted to short time and length scales. Machine learning interatomic potentials (MLPs) offer AIMD accuracy at far lower cost, but their application to liquids is limited by training datasets that inadequately sample atomic configurations, leading to unphysical force predictions and unstable trajectories. Here we introduce a physically motivated dataset-engineering strategy that constructs liquidlike training data synthetically rather than relying on AIMD configurations. The method exploits the established icosahedral short-range order of metallic liquids, twelvefold, near-close-packed local coordination, and generates "synthetic-liquid" structures by systematic perturbation of crystalline references. MLPs trained on these datasets close the sampling gaps that lead to unphysical predictions, remain numerically stable across temperatures, and reproduce experimental liquid densities, diffusivities, and melting temperatures for multiple elemental metals. The framework links atomic-scale sampling to long-term MD stability and provides a practical route to predictive modeling of liquid-phase thermophysical behavior beyond the limits of direct AIMD.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05003v1 公告类型：新 摘要：液态金属是能源储存和核技术的核心，但对其热物理性质的定量了解仍然有限。虽然原子模拟提供了直接从原子运动计算液体性质的途径，但最准确的方法是从头算分子动力学 (AIMD)，但计算成本高昂，并且仅限于短时间和长度尺度。机器学习原子间势 (MLP) 以低得多的成本提供 AIMD 准确性，但它们在液体中的应用受到训练数据集的限制，这些数据集对原子配置的采样不足，导致非物理力预测和不稳定的轨迹。在这里，我们介绍了一种物理驱动的数据集工程策略，该策略综合构建液体状训练数据，而不是依赖 AIMD 配置。该方法利用已建立的金属液体二十面体短程有序、十二重、近密堆积局部配位，并通过晶体参考的系统扰动生成“合成液体”结构。在这些数据集上训练的 MLP 缩小了导致非物理预测的采样间隙，在不同温度下保持数值稳定，并重现多种元素金属的实验液体密度、扩散率和熔化温度。该框架将原子尺度采样与长期 MD 稳定性联系起来，并提供了一种超越直接 AIMD 限制的液相热物理行为预测建模的实用途径。
