## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20660v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In the present study, two-different reduced-order models are proposed for $\text{H}_2\left(\text{X}^1\Sigma_g^+\right)$+$\text{H}\left({}^2\text{S}\right)$ system by leveraging first-principle quasi-classical trajectory simulations and in-depth master equation analyses. The most recent available ab-initio potential energy surface is adopted to construct a new set of rovibrational state-to-state kinetic database valid over a wide range of temperatures. Firstly, a modified two-temperature model is proposed by incorporating the master equation-informed model parameters, enabling the advanced treatment of the internal energy coupling and the nonequilibrium dissociation predictions. Secondly, a hybrid coarse-graining model is proposed by combining a graph-based approach optimized globally for a wide range of temperatures with a centrifugal-barrier-based coarse-graining method. The proposed reduced-order models offer significantly improved accuracy in predicting the nonequilibrium energy transfer and dissociation dynamics compared to the existing coarse-graining and 2T models in previous studies. In addition, aerothermal heating prediction relevant to Uranus planetary entry reveals 16.5% of convective heat flux discrepancy compared to the present modified 2T approach with the existing 2T, demonstrating the importance of accurate modeling of the chemical-kinetics in the $\text{H}_2\left(\text{X}^1\Sigma_g^+\right)$+$\text{H}\left({}^2\text{S}\right)$ system.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20660v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在本研究中，通过利用第一原理准经典轨迹模拟和深入的主方程分析，为 $\text{H}_2\left(\text{X}^1\Sigma_g^+\right)$+$\text{H}\left({}^2\text{S}\right)$ 系统提出了两种不同的降阶模型。采用最新可用的从头算势能面构建了一套新的在较宽温度范围内有效的旋转状态到状态动力学数据库。首先，通过结合主方程模型参数提出了改进的双温度模型，从而能够对内能耦合和非平衡解离预测进行高级处理。其次，通过将针对各种温度进行全局优化的基于图的方法与基于离心屏障的粗粒方法相结合，提出了一种混合粗粒模型。与先前研究中现有的粗粒度和 2T 模型相比，所提出的降阶模型在预测非平衡能量转移和解离动力学方面提供了显着提高的准确性。此外，与天王星行星进入相关的气动热加热预测揭示了与现有2T改进2T方法相比，对流热通量差异为16.5%，这证明了$\text{H}_2\left(\text{X}^1\Sigma_g^+\right)$+$\text{H}\left({}^2\text{S}\right)$系统中化学动力学精确建模的重要性。
