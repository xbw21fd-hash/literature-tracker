## Abstract
arXiv:2502.03986v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: In cuprate high-temperature superconductors the doping level is fixed during synthesis, hence the charge carrier density per CuO$_2$ plane cannot be easily tuned by conventional gating, unlike in 2D materials. Strain engineering has recently emerged as a powerful tuning knob for manipulating the properties of cuprates, in particular charge and spin orders, and their delicate interplay with superconductivity. In thin films, additional tunability can be introduced by the substrate surface morphology, particularly nanofacets formed by substrate surface reconstruction. Here we show a remarkable enhancement of the superconducting onset temperature $T_{\mathrm{c}}^{\mathrm{on}}$ and the upper critical magnetic field $H_{c,2}$ in nanometer-thin YBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_{7-\delta}$ films grown on a substrate with a nanofaceted surface. We theoretically show that the enhancement is driven by electronic nematicity and unidirectional charge density waves, where both elements are captured by an additional effective potential at the interface between the film and the uniquely textured substrate. Our findings show a new paradigm in which substrate engineering can effectively enhance the superconducting properties of cuprates. This approach opens an exciting frontier in the design and optimization of high-performance superconducting materials.

arXiv:2502.03986v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：在铜酸盐高温超导体中，掺杂水平在合成过程中是固定的，因此与 2D 材料不同，每个 CuO$_2$ 平面的电荷载流子密度无法通过传统选通轻松调节。应变工程最近已成为控制铜氧化物特性的强大调节旋钮，特别是电荷和自旋顺序，以及它们与超导性的微妙相互作用。在薄膜中，可以通过基底表面形态引入额外的可调性，特别是通过基底表面重构形成的纳米面。在这里，我们展示了在具有纳米面表面的基底上生长的纳米薄 YBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_{7-\delta}$ 薄膜中超导起始温度 $T_{\mathrm{c}}^{\mathrm{on}}$ 和上临界磁场 $H_{c,2}$ 的显着增强。我们从理论上证明，这种增强是由电子向列性和单向电荷密度波驱动的，其中两种元素都被薄膜和独特纹理基材之间界面处的额外有效电势捕获。我们的研究结果展示了一种新的范例，其中基底工程可以有效增强铜酸盐的超导性能。这种方法在高性能超导材料的设计和优化方面开辟了令人兴奋的前沿。
