## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18895v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: People may express preferences that differ from their privately held views, often under social pressure, and may fail to act on their stated intentions. Such inconsistencies are referred to as preference falsification and the intention-behavior gap, respectively. Both hamper collective decision-making and adaptation, complicating policy formulation and implementation. To simulate these phenomena, dual-layer opinion agent-based models are used, in which each agent holds both a private and an expressed (public) opinion. Within the $q$-voter framework, two expressed-private opinion (EPO) models have been introduced in which private and expressed opinions are updated synchronously; two variants differ only by the presence of self-anticonformity, a mechanism in which an agent may set its private opinion opposite to its current expressed opinion, breaking internal harmony and creating a state akin to cognitive dissonance. Here, we extend these models by introducing an asynchronous update: in each elementary step, an agent updates its private opinion with probability $\alpha$ or its expressed opinion with complementary probability; hence the name $\alpha$-EPO models. Using Monte Carlo simulations on both artificial and real organizational social networks, along with mean-field and pair approximation analyses, we show that self-anticonformity makes collective outcomes robust to behavioral volatility tuned by $\alpha$, enhances collective agreement, and suppresses hysteresis. In contrast, without self-anticonformity, $\alpha$ affects the nature of the transition between agreement and disagreement: higher values of $\alpha$ suppress hysteresis and enhance overall agreement.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18895v1 公告类型：新 摘要：人们可能会在社会压力下表达与其私人观点不同的偏好，并且可能无法按照其既定意图行事。这种不一致分别称为偏好伪造和意图行为差距。两者都会阻碍集体决策和适应，使政策制定和实施变得复杂。为了模拟这些现象，使用了基于双层意见代理的模型，其中每个代理都持有私人意见和表达的（公共）意见。在$q$-voter框架内，引入了两种表达私人意见（EPO）模型，其中私人意见和表达意见同步更新；两种变体的不同之处仅在于存在自我反整合，在这种机制中，主体可以将其私人意见与其当前表达的意见相反，打破内部和谐并创造一种类似于认知失调的状态。在这里，我们通过引入异步更新来扩展这些模型：在每个基本步骤中，代理以概率 $\alpha$ 更新其私人意见或以互补概率更新其表达的意见；因此命名为 $\alpha$-EPO 模型。通过对人工和真实组织社交网络进行蒙特卡罗模拟，以及平均场和配对逼近分析，我们表明，自我反整合使得集体结果对由 $\alpha$ 调整的行为波动具有鲁棒性，增强了集体一致性，并抑制了滞后现象。相反，在没有自我反整合的情况下，$\alpha$ 会影响同意与分歧之间过渡的性质：较高的 $\alpha$ 值会抑制滞后现象并增强整体一致性。
