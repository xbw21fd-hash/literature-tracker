## Abstract
TLDR: Additive Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction and spin-orbit torque of the tailored stack allow stable skyrmion bubbles at room temperature, significantly smaller threshold current density or higher speed for motion, and negligible skyrMion Hall effect, with a potential to be scaled down to nanometer dimensions.
