## Abstract
TLDR: 2H-VSe2 monolayer, where the spin--orbit coupling coexists with the intrinsic exchange interaction of transition-metal d electrons, is such a room-temperature ferrovalley material and it is predicted that such system could demonstrate many distinctive properties, for example, chirality-dependent optical band gap and anomalous valley Hall effect.
