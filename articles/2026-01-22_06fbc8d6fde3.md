## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14458v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study Gutzwiller-projected wavefunctions for triangular-lattice U(1) Dirac spin liquids in a Zeeman field, where we allow the U(1) gauge field to develop a gauge flux, resulting in (spin-split) spinon Landau levels. We find that at a given magnetization, the optimal candidate state has a finite flux chosen such that the spinon filling lies in a $|C|=1$ Landau-level gap: it gives the lowest variational energy and the smallest energy variance within our correlation-matrix reconstruction for local Heisenberg-type models. By symmetry, we argue that the finite gauge flux results in a non-zero (staggered) scalar spin chirality, as also numerically observed, and further find that the $|C|=1$ state exhibits dominant quasi-long-ranged $120^\circ$ magnetic correlations. Studying the next-to-optimal wavefunction with a $|C|=2$ Landau-level gap, we observe unusual spin-nematic correlations. Our results may provide guidance for analyzing the magnetic-field response of DSL candidate materials and offer numerical diagnostics that can connect to the underlying theory of spinons coupled to an emergent U(1) gauge field.

arXiv:2601.14458v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究塞曼场中三角晶格 U(1) 狄拉克自旋液体的 Gutzwiller 投影波函数，其中我们允许 U(1) 规范场产生规范通量，从而产生（自旋分裂）自旋朗道能级。我们发现，在给定的磁化强度下，最佳候选状态选择了有限的通量，使得自旋填充位于 $|C|=1$ 朗道能级间隙中：它在局部海森堡型模型的相关矩阵重建中给出了最低的变分能量和最小的能量方差。通过对称性，我们认为有限规范通量会导致非零（交错）标量自旋手性，这也在数值上观察到，并进一步发现 $|C|=1$ 状态表现出占主导地位的准长程 $120^\circ$ 磁相关性。研究具有 $|C|=2$ 朗道能级间隙的次优波函数，我们观察到不寻常的自旋向列相关性。我们的结果可以为分析 DSL 候选材料的磁场响应提供指导，并提供可以连接到与新兴 U(1) 规范场耦合的自旋子基本理论的数值诊断。
