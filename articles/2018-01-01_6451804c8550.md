## Abstract
We report here an experimental study of ultrafast response of the dielectric polarization in (Batextbackslash_0.8Srtextbackslash_0.2)TiOtextbackslash_3 thin films to a strong electric field of a nearly single--cycle THz pulse. The phenomenon of Second Harmonic Generation (SHG) is used as a probe of the polarization in the terahertz pump--optical probe experiment. SHG loops for THz pulses of different amplitudes were obtained. The SHG response is modelled assuming that the ferroelectric material is split into 180--degree domains. It is shown that intuitive model based on forced harmonic oscillator does not fully describe to the observed ultrafast ferroelectric response
