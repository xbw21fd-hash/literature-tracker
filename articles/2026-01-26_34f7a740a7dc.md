## Abstract
Quantum-mechanical descriptions of luminescence, excitation energy transfer, and resonant dipole–dipole interactions are usually formulated in terms of transition dipoles from the 1-particle density matrix. However, transition dipoles cannot adequately capture the retardation and polariton effects for large entities. A previous study [M.-W. Lee and L.-Y. Hsu, Phys. Rev. A, 107 , 053709 (2023)] showed that based on macroscopic quantum electrodynamics, the transition-current-density (TCD) approach not only enables the description of retardation effects but also accounts for the polariton effects arising from material structures and vacuum electromagnetic fields. Nevertheless, the quality of transition currents derived from ab initio calculations remains largely unexplored. In this study, we examine the numerical equivalence between transition dipoles derived from transition charge densities and those from TCDs for 1- and 2-electron systems, including H2+, HeH + , and H 2 . We further examine the continuity equation ∇ · J nm = − iω nm ρ nm by comparing the transition charge density ( ρ nm ) and the divergence of TCD ( J nm ), for a transition between states n and m . Despite close agreement of transition dipole moments, we find substantial violations of the continuity equation. The deviations manifest as spurious oscillations in ∇ · J nm due to the artifacts from the second-derivative features of the underlying Gaussian-type orbitals. To overcome this issue, we implement a reciprocal-space filtering technique that suppresses these non-physical oscillations, improving physical consistency for the TCD. Our study provides practical considerations for future calculations that require reliable transition currents.

发光、激发能量转移和共振偶极子-偶极子相互作用的量子力学描述通常根据 1 粒子密度矩阵的跃迁偶极子来表述。然而，过渡偶极子无法充分捕获大型实体的延迟和极化子效应。之前的一项研究[M.-W.李和 L.-Y。许，物理。 Rev. A, 107 , 053709 (2023)]表明，基于宏观量子电动力学，跃迁电流密度（TCD）方法不仅能够描述延迟效应，还可以解释材料结构和真空电磁场引起的极化子效应。然而，从头计算得出的过渡电流的质量在很大程度上仍未得到探索。在本研究中，我们研究了 1 电子和 2 电子系统（包括 H2+、HeH + 和 H 2 ）的过渡电荷密度衍生的过渡偶极子与 TCD 衍生的过渡偶极子之间的数值等效性。我们通过比较状态 n 和 m 之间跃迁的跃迁电荷密度 ( ρ nm ) 和 TCD 散度 ( J nm ) 来进一步检查连续性方程 ∇ · J nm = − iω nm ρ nm 。尽管过渡偶极矩非常一致，但我们发现严重违反连续性方程。由于基础高斯型轨道的二阶导数特征的伪影，偏差表现为 ∇·J nm 中的寄生振荡。为了克服这个问题，我们实施了一种倒易空间滤波技术来抑制这些非物理振荡，从而提高 TCD 的物理一致性。我们的研究为未来需要可靠过渡电流的计算提供了实际考虑。
