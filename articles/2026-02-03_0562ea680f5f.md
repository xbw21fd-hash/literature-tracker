## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01071v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We present a new inverse-time formulation of vortex stretching in the Navier-Stokes equations, based on a PDE framework inspired by score-based diffusion models. By absorbing the ill-posed backward Laplacian arising from time reversal into a drift term expressed through a score function, the inverse-time dynamics are formulated in a Lagrangian manner. Using a discrete Lagrangian flow of an axisymmetric vortex-stretching field, the score function is learned with a neural network and employed to construct backward-time particle trajectories. Numerical results demonstrate that information about initial positions is rapidly lost in the compressive direction, whereas it is relatively well preserved in the stretching direction.

arXiv:2602.01071v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们基于受基于分数的扩散模型启发的偏微分方程框架，提出了纳维-斯托克斯方程中涡旋拉伸的新反时公式。通过将时间反演产生的不适定向后拉普拉斯算子吸收为通过得分函数表达的漂移项，以拉格朗日方式表达反时动态。使用轴对称涡旋拉伸场的离散拉格朗日流，通过神经网络学习得分函数并用于构建向后时间粒子轨迹。数值结果表明，有关初始位置的信息在压缩方向上迅速丢失，而在拉伸方向上则相对较好地保留。
