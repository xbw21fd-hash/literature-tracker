## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02932v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We develop an operator-based framework to coarse-grain interacting particle systems that exhibit clustering dynamics. Starting from the particle-based transfer operator, we first construct a sequence of reduced representations: the operator is projected onto concentrations and then further reduced by representing the concentration dynamics on a geometric low-dimensional manifold and an adapted finite-state discretization. The resulting coarse-grained transfer operator is finally estimated from dynamical simulation data by inferring the transition probabilities between the Markov states. Applied to systems with multichromatic and Morse interaction potentials, the reduced model reproduces key features of the clustering process, including transitions between cluster configurations and the emergence of metastable states. Spectral analysis and transition-path analysis of the estimated operator reveal implied time scales and dominant transition pathways, providing an interpretable and efficient description of particle-clustering dynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02932v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们开发了一个基于算子的框架，用于展示聚类动态的粗粒度相互作用粒子系统。从基于粒子的转移算子开始，我们首先构造一系列简化表示：将算子投影到浓度上，然后通过在几何低维流形上表示浓度动态和适应的有限状态离散化来进一步简化。最终通过推断马尔可夫状态之间的转移概率从动态模拟数据估计得到的粗粒度传递算子。应用于具有多色和莫尔斯相互作用势的系统时，简化模型再现了聚类过程的关键特征，包括簇配置之间的转变和亚稳态的出现。估计算子的谱分析和跃迁路径分析揭示了隐含的时间尺度和主要跃迁路径，为粒子聚类动力学提供了可解释且有效的描述。
