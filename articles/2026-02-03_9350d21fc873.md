## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02030v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: All-optical image processing provides a fast and energy-efficient alternative to conventional electronic systems by directly manipulating optical wavefronts. However, metasurface-based optical processors reported to date are often limited in functionality, operating bandwidth, or input modality, which restricts their adaptability across different image processing tasks. Here, we demonstrate a broadband metasurface platform capable of performing diverse analog image processing operations on both amplitude- and phase-encoded inputs. This platform is realized using a single-layer dielectric metasurface designed through an end-to-end, task-driven inverse design framework. By tailoring the spatial-frequency components of incident image wavefronts, the metasurface implements analog operations such as edge detection and pattern recognition across a 200nm wavelength bandwidth in the visible spectrum. Furthermore, we develop a compact processor architecture that integrates imaging and computation within a reduced optical footprint. These results establish a flexible and compact metasurface-based optical processor with strong potential for integration into practical imaging and optical computing systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02030v1 公告类型：新 摘要：全光学图像处理通过直接操纵光学波前，为传统电子系统提供了快速、节能的替代方案。然而，迄今为止报道的基于超表面的光学处理器通常在功能、操作带宽或输入模式方面受到限制，这限制了它们在不同图像处理任务中的适应性。在这里，我们演示了一个宽带超表面平台，能够对幅度和相位编码输入执行各种模拟图像处理操作。该平台是使用通过端到端、任务驱动的逆向设计框架设计的单层电介质超表面来实现的。通过定制入射图像波前的空间频率分量，超表面可以在可见光谱的 200nm 波长带宽内实现边缘检测和模式识别等模拟操作。此外，我们开发了一种紧凑的处理器架构，将成像和计算集成到更小的光学足迹中。这些结果建立了一种灵活而紧凑的基于超表面的光学处理器，具有集成到实际成像和光学计算系统中的强大潜力。
