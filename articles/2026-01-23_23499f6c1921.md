## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15570v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum interference effects such as weak localization (WL) and universal conductance fluctuations (UCF) normally yield consistent electronic phase-coherence lengths in homogeneous conductors. Here we show that in individual carbon nanotube bundles exfoliated from highly conductive solution-spun fibers, different probes, including the field scales and magnitudes of WL and UCF and nonlocal magnetoconductance, lead to strikingly disparate estimates of coherence lengths. WL magnetoconductance measured in a perpendicular magnetic field yields a phase-coherence length of approximately 50 nm. In contrast, UCF amplitudes are comparable to e squared over h even for an 8 micrometer long segment, and nonlocal magnetoconductance persists across a 4 micrometer separation of electrodes, revealing phase-coherent transport over micrometer length scales within a single bundle. The coexistence of short- and long-range coherence implies that locally diffusive electrons remain partially phase-correlated among nanotubes within the same bundle. These findings challenge the conventional single-scale picture of mesoscopic coherence and establish carbon nanotube bundles as a model platform for emergent, network-level quantum transport.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15570v1 公告类型：新 摘要：弱局域化 (WL) 和通用电导涨落 (UCF) 等量子干扰效应通常会在同质导体中产生一致的电子相位相干长度。在这里，我们表明，在从高导电溶液纺丝纤维剥离的单个碳纳米管束中，不同的探针，包括 WL 和 UCF 的场尺度和大小以及非局域磁导，导致相干长度的估计值显着不同。在垂直磁场中测量的 WL 磁导产生大约 50 nm 的相位相干长度。相比之下，即使对于 8 微米长的线段，UCF 振幅也与 h 的 e 平方相当，并且非局域磁导在电极间隔 4 微米的范围内持续存在，揭示了单束内微米长度尺度上的相位相干传输。短程和长程相干性的共存意味着局部扩散电子在同一束内的纳米管之间保持部分相位相关。这些发现挑战了介观相干性的传统单尺度图像，并将碳纳米管束建立为新兴网络级量子传输的模型平台。
