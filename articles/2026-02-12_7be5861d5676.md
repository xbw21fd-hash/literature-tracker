## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10642v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Energetic-particle-driven modes in magnetically confined fusion plasmas often exhibit nonlinear frequency sweeping, reflecting complex wave-particle interactions near marginal stability. While the bump-on-tail (BOT) instability within the Berk-Breizman framework has served as a canonical model for understanding such phenomena, a unified nonlinear description remains incomplete when drag, diffusion, and Krook relaxation act simultaneously. In this work, we present a comprehensive numerical investigation of the BOT instability that explicitly retains all three collision operators together with external wave damping. Using a validated characteristic-based BOT code, we systematically scan the multi-dimensional collision parameter space and construct nonlinear regime maps and bifurcation diagrams. To organize the rich dynamics, we introduce a two-level categorization that combines global wave-energy evolution with chirping subtypes identified from spectral morphology. We find that diffusion and Krook relaxation regularize the nonlinear dynamics and promote ordered transitions from chaotic behaviors to periodic oscillations and steady saturation as collision strength increases, with the saturation level decreasing approximately exponentially with external damping. In contrast, drag alone does not admit steady solutions and instead drives persistent or chaotic chirping through convective deformation of resonant phase-space structures. When drag is combined with diffusion or Krook relaxation, clear transition sequences emerge: increasing drag breaks hole-clump symmetry, broadens the effective resonance region, and drives systematic transitions from transient to intermittent and persistent chirping.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10642v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁约束聚变等离子体中的高能粒子驱动模式通常表现出非线性频率扫描，反映了接近边缘稳定性的复杂波粒相互作用。虽然 Berk-Breizman 框架内的尾部碰撞 (BOT) 不稳定性已成为理解此类现象的规范模型，但当阻力、扩散和 Krook 松弛同时作用时，统一的非线性描述仍然不完整。在这项工作中，我们对 BOT 不稳定性进行了全面的数值研究，明确保留了所有三个碰撞算子以及外波阻尼。使用经过验证的基于特征的 BOT 代码，我们系统地扫描多维碰撞参数空间并构建非线性状态图和分叉图。为了组织丰富的动态，我们引入了两级分类，将全球波能演化与从光谱形态识别的啁啾子类型结合起来。我们发现，随着碰撞强度的增加，扩散和克鲁克松弛使非线性动力学规律化，并促进从混沌行为到周期性振荡和稳定饱和的有序转变，饱和水平随着外部阻尼的增加而近似指数下降。相比之下，单独的阻力不允许稳定解，而是通过共振相空间结构的对流变形驱动持续或混沌的啁啾。当阻力与扩散或克鲁克弛豫相结合时，会出现清晰的过渡序列：增加阻力会破坏空穴簇对称性，拓宽有效共振区域，并驱动从瞬态到间歇和持续啁啾的系统过渡。
