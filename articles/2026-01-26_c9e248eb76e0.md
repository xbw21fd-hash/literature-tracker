## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16630v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Unconventional superconductivity under strong Zeeman fields--particularly beyond the Pauli paramagnetic limit--remains a central challenge in condensed matter physics. The exotic Fulde-Ferrell-Larkin-Ovchinnikov (FFLO) state, in particular, remains in need of definitive study within fundamental electronic models. Here we employ state-of-the-art finite-temperature and ground-state tensor network approaches to systematically explore the superconducting (SC) phase diagram of the $t$-$t'$-$J$ model subjected to Zeeman fields. We find that zero-momentum $d$-wave superconductivity persists until the spin gap closes, coexisting with charge density waves. A novel $d$-wave FFLO phase emerges under a higher Zeeman field even above the Pauli limit, concomitant with a field-enhanced spin density waves. We identify these phases, characterized by the simultaneous presence of pairing condensate and density wave orders, as charge-2e supersolids. Analysis of Matsubara Green's function reveals that the FFLO pairing momentum is locked to the underlying Fermi surface. Our results provide microscopic insights into field-induced unconventional pairing mechanisms and reveal the long-sought FFLO state in a fundamental correlated electron model, offering a promising route for its realization in ultracold atom optical lattice.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16630v1 公告类型：新 摘要：强塞曼场下的非常规超导性（特别是超出泡利顺磁极限）仍然是凝聚态物理的核心挑战。尤其是奇异的 Fulde-Ferrell-Larkin-Ovchinnikov (FFLO) 态，仍然需要在基本电子模型中进行明确的研究。在这里，我们采用最先进的有限温度和基态张量网络方法来系统地探索受塞曼场影响的$t$-$t'$-$J$模型的超导（SC）相图。我们发现零动量 $d$ 波超导性一直持续到自旋间隙关闭为止，并与电荷密度波共存。在甚至高于泡利极限的更高塞曼场下，出现了一种新颖的 $d$ 波 FFLO 相，并伴随着场增强的自旋密度波。我们将这些相识别为电荷 2e 超固体，其特征是同时存在配对凝聚态和密度波级。对松原格林函数的分析表明，FFLO 配对动量被锁定在底层的费米面上。我们的研究结果提供了对场诱导的非常规配对机制的微观见解，并揭示了基本相关电子模型中长期寻找的 FFLO 态，为其在超冷原子光学晶格中的实现提供了一条有希望的途径。
