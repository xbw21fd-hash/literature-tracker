## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02936v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The two-dimensional molecular conductor $\kappa$-(BETS)$_2$Mn[N(CN)$_2$]$_3$ has been studied because of the intriguing magnetic coupling of the molecular $\pi$-electrons to the Mn$^{2+}$ ions. Utilizing X-band electron spin resonance spectroscopy we have performed comprehensive investigations of the magnetic properties, in particular on the temperature and angular dependences of the spin susceptibility, the $g$-factor and the linewidth. Due to the $\pi$-$d$-coupling, a rearrangement of the $\pi$-spins occurs: At low temperatures the $g$-factor shifts enormously with a pronounced in-plane anisotropy that flips as the temperature decreases; the lines broaden significantly; and the spin susceptibility increases upon cooling with a kink at the phase transition. By carefully analyzing the angular dependence of $g(\theta)$ and $\Delta H(\theta)$ we reveal the influence of anisotropic Zeeman interaction in addition to spin-phonon coupling. We conclude the presence of two magnetically distinct BETS chains and discuss the possibility of altermagnetic order.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02936v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于分子 $\pi$-电子与 Mn$^{2+}$ 离子之间有趣的磁耦合，二维分子导体 $\kappa$-(BETS)$_2$Mn[N(CN)$_2$]$_3$ 已被研究。利用 X 波段电子自旋共振光谱，我们对磁性进行了全面的研究，特别是自旋磁化率、$g$ 因子和线宽的温度和角度依赖性。由于$\pi$-$d$耦合，$\pi$自旋会发生重新排列：在低温下，$g$因子发生巨大变化，并随着温度降低而发生明显的面内各向异性；线条明显变宽；并且自旋敏感性在冷却时随着相变处的扭结而增加。通过仔细分析 $g(\theta)$ 和 $\Delta H(\theta)$ 的角度依赖性，我们揭示了除自旋声子耦合之外的各向异性塞曼相互作用的影响。我们得出结论，存在两个磁力不同的 BETS 链，并讨论了交替磁序的可能性。
