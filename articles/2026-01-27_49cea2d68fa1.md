## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18553v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Past kinetic simulations and spacecraft observations have shown that traveling foreshocks (TFs) are bounded by either foreshock compressional boundaries (FCBs) or foreshock bubbles (FBs). Here we present four TFs with a different kind of structure appearing at one of their edges. Two of them, observed by the Cluster mission, are bounded by a hot flow anomaly (HFA). In one case, the HFA was observed only by the spacecraft closest to the bow shock, while the other three probes observed an FCB. In addition, two other TFs were observed by the MMS spacecraft to be delimited by a structure that we call HFA-like FCB. In the spacecraft data, these structures present signatures similar to those of HFAs: dips in magnetic field magnitude and solar wind density, decelerated and deflected plasma flow and increased temperature. However, a detailed inspection of these events reveals the absence of heating of the SW beam. Instead, the beam almost disappears inside these events and the plasma moments are strongly influenced by the suprathermal particles. We suggest that HFA-like FCBs are related to the evolution and structure of the directional discontinuities of the interplanetary magnetic field whose thickness is larger than the gyroradious of suprathermal ions. We also show that individual TFs may appear together with several different types of transient upstream mesoscale structures, which brings up a question about their combined effect on regions downstream of the bow shock.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18553v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：过去的动力学模拟和航天器观测表明，移动前震 (TF) 受到前震压缩边界 (FCB) 或前震气泡 (FB) 的限制。在这里，我们展示了四个 TF，其中一个边缘出现了不同类型的结构。 Cluster 任务观测到的其中两个区域受到热流异常 (HFA) 的限制。在一种情况下，只有最靠近弓激波的航天器观察到了 HFA，而其他三个探测器则观察到了 FCB。此外，MMS 航天器还观察到另外两个 TF 被我们称为类 HFA FCB 的结构所界定。在航天器数据中，这些结构呈现出与 HFA 类似的特征：磁场强度和太阳风密度下降、等离子体流减速和偏转以及温度升高。然而，对这些事件的详细检查表明，短波束没有加热。相反，光束在这些事件中几乎消失，并且等离子体矩受到超热粒子的强烈影响。我们认为类HFA FCB与厚度大于超热离子回转半径的行星际磁场方向不连续性的演化和结构有关。我们还表明，单个TF可能与几种不同类型的瞬态上游中尺度结构一起出现，这提出了它们对弓激波下游区域的综合影响的问题。
