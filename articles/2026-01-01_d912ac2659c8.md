## Abstract
arXiv:2509.16751v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Entanglement dynamics are fundamental to quantum technologies, yet navigating their temporal profiles (trajectories) remains challenging. Here, we propose a scalable solid-state platform based on RKKY exchange, where two spin qubits couple to a central spin qudit that oscillatorily spin-polarizes the surrounding conduction electrons. We introduce the exchange-time integral (ETI), which maps the spatial motion of the qubits to a time-dependent exchange interaction and serves as an effective "trajectory clock" governing the system evolution. We focus specifically on entanglement trajectories initially near the entanglement-unentanglement boundary, with the distance to this boundary quantified by concurrence extended to include negative values. By alternating the sign changes of the exchange, implemented through vibrational motion of qubits, the ETI enables programmable entanglement trajectories. For in-phase and antiphase vibrations, including scenarios with controlled stopping at the RKKY exchange-free nodes, we identify distinctive trajectories: snake (repeatedly crossing the boundary), bouncing (immediately reversing upon reaching the boundary), boundary-residing (remaining at the transition point), and pulse (controllable entanglement intervals). The vibration phase creates asymmetric shifts to the trajectories. The proposed device offers built-in error correction against dephasing by utilizing both ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic regimes. Out-of-phase vibrations drive trajectories away from the boundary, accessing larger entanglement values but with irregular/unsteady final states. To stabilize these trajectories, we introduce a damping mechanism. Our framework offers a systematic method for navigating and engineering entanglement dynamics in quantum systems, with potential applications in quantum computation, cryptography, and metrology.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.16751v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：纠缠动力学是量子技术的基础，但驾驭其时间分布（轨迹）仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们提出了一个基于 RKKY 交换的可扩展固态平台，其中两个自旋量子位耦合到一个中心自旋量子位，该中心自旋量子位以振荡方式使周围的传导电子自旋极化。我们引入了交换时间积分（ETI），它将量子位的空间运动映射到依赖于时间的交换相互作用，并作为控制系统演化的有效“轨迹时钟”。我们特别关注最初靠近纠缠-非纠缠边界的纠缠轨迹，通过并发量化到该边界的距离扩展到包括负值。通过改变交换的符号变化（通过量子位的振动运动实现），ETI 能够实现可编程的纠缠轨迹。对于同相和反相振动，包括在 RKKY 无交换节点处受控停止的场景，我们确定了独特的轨迹：蛇形（反复跨越边界）、弹跳（到达边界后立即反转）、边界驻留（保持在过渡点）和脉冲（可控纠缠间隔）。振动阶段产生轨迹的不对称移动。所提出的器件通过利用铁磁和反铁磁机制提供针对失相的内置误差校正。异相振动使轨迹远离边界，获得更大的纠缠值，但最终状态不规则/不稳定。为了稳定这些轨迹，我们引入了阻尼机制。我们的框架提供了一种在量子系统中导航和工程纠缠动力学的系统方法，在量子计算、密码学和计量学方面具有潜在的应用。
