## Abstract
Accurate prediction of nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) shielding with machine learning (ML) models remains a central challenge for data-driven spectroscopy. We present atomic variants of the Coulomb matrix (aCM) and bag-of-bonds (aBoB) descriptors and extend them using radial basis functions (RBFs) to yield smooth, per-atom representations (aCM-RBF and aBoB-RBF). Local structural information is incorporated by augmenting each atomic descriptor with contributions from the n nearest neighbors, resulting in the family of descriptors, aCM-RBF( n ) and aBoB-RBF( n ). For 13 C shielding prediction on the QM9NMR dataset (831 925 shielding values across 130 831 molecules), aBoB-RBF(4) achieves an out-of-sample mean error of 1.69 ppm, outperforming models reported in previous studies. While explicit three-body descriptors further reduce errors at a higher cost, aBoB-RBF(4) offers the best balance of accuracy and efficiency. Benchmarking on external datasets comprising larger molecules (GDBm, Drug12/Drug40, and pyrimidinone derivatives) confirms the robustness and transferability of aBoB-RBF(4), establishing it as a practical tool for ML-based NMR shielding prediction.

## 摘要（中文）
使用机器学习 (ML) 模型准确预测核磁共振 (NMR) 屏蔽仍然是数据驱动光谱学的核心挑战。我们提出了库仑矩阵 (aCM) 和键袋 (aBoB) 描述符的原子变体，并使用径向基函数 (RBF) 对其进行扩展，以产生平滑的每原子表示（aCM-RBF 和 aBoB-RBF）。通过使用 n 个最近邻居的贡献来增强每个原子描述符，从而合并局部结构信息，从而产生描述符族 aCM-RBF( n ) 和 aBoB-RBF( n )。对于 QM9NMR 数据集（130 831 个分子的 831 925 屏蔽值）的 13 C 屏蔽预测，aBoB-RBF(4) 实现了 1.69 ppm 的样本外平均误差，优于之前研究中报告的模型。虽然显式三体描述符以更高的成本进一步减少错误，但 aBoB-RBF(4) 提供了准确性和效率的最佳平衡。对包含较大分子（GDBm、Drug12/Drug40 和嘧啶酮衍生物）的外部数据集进行基准测试证实了 aBoB-RBF(4) 的稳健性和可转移性，使其成为基于 ML 的 NMR 屏蔽预测的实用工具。
