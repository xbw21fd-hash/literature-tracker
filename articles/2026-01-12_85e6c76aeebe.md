## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05458v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: First-order magnetostructural phase transitions underpin giant magnetocaloric effects, yet the microscopic role of lattice dynamics in these transitions remains controversial. Here we use first-principles spin-lattice dynamics simulations to investigate the coupled evolution of magnetization and phonon dispersions across the magnetostructural transition in MnAs. Our simulations quantitatively reproduce the experimentally observed Curie temperature, lattice contraction, and free-energy crossing between hexagonal and orthorhombic phases. We show that below the Curie temperature, magnetic-field-induced hardening of soft phonon modes gives rise to a sizable lattice entropy contribution that enhances the total isothermal entropy change by approximately 23% under a 5 T field. In contrast, the lattice entropy change associated with the structural phase transition itself has an opposite sign and partially compensates the lattice contribution due to field-induced phonon hardening. This competition reconciles long-standing discrepancies in the interpretation of magnetocaloric entropy measurements across first-order transitions. In addition, we demonstrate that the strong magnetic-field dependence of the phonon spectrum near the transition enables large tunability of lattice thermal conductivity, highlighting MnAs as a promising platform for magnetic-field-controlled thermal switching. Our results establish a unified microscopic picture of spin-lattice coupling in first-order magnetocaloric materials and provide design principles for engineering enhanced caloric and thermal transport functionalities.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05458v1 公告类型：新 摘要：一阶磁结构相变支撑巨磁热效应，但晶格动力学在这些相变中的微观作用仍然存在争议。在这里，我们使用第一原理自旋晶格动力学模拟来研究锰砷磁结构转变过程中磁化强度和声子色散的耦合演化。我们的模拟定量地再现了实验观察到的居里温度、晶格收缩以及六方相和斜方相之间的自由能交叉。我们表明，在居里温度以下，磁场诱导的软声子模式硬化会产生相当大的晶格熵贡献，在 5 T 场下使总等温熵变增强约 23%。相反，与结构相变本身相关的晶格熵变化具有相反的符号，并且部分补偿了由于场致声子硬化而产生的晶格贡献。这项竞赛协调了一级跃迁磁热熵测量解释中长期存在的差异。此外，我们证明了过渡附近声子谱的强磁场依赖性使得晶格热导率具有很大的可调性，这凸显了 MnAs 作为磁场控制热开关的有前景的平台。我们的研究结果建立了一阶磁热材料中自旋晶格耦合的统一微观图像，并为工程增强的热量和热传输功能提供了设计原理。
