## Abstract
The electrocatalytic nitrogen reduction reaction offers a sustainable route for unconventional ammonia synthesis, yet its practical implementation is often hindered by catalyst performance. Herein, by using first-principles calculations, we systematically explore the eNRR performance of a series of single-cluster catalysts with monometallic triatomic clusters of 3d transition metals anchored on graphdiyne (TM 3 /GDY, TM = Sc–Zn). The Ti 3 /GDY, V 3 /GDY, Cr 3 /GDY, and Mn 3 /GDY all exhibit robust stability and feature spontaneous N 2 chemisorption and significant N≡N bond activation (elongation > 0.14 Å). Chemical bonding analysis uncovers σ–π synergistic donor–acceptor interactions between TM-3d orbitals and N 2 π* antibonding states as the origin of superior catalytic activity. Ti 3 /GDY achieves a low limiting potential of −0.39 V while effectively suppressing N 2 H 4 formation. This dual advantage stems from its elevated d-band center and high charge transfer. The titanium-based triatomic architecture is found to be a promising candidate for sustainable nitrogen fixation. This work offers a fundamental design principle for SCCs through atomic-level coordination engineering and electronic structure modulation.

## 摘要（中文）
电催化氮还原反应为非常规氨合成提供了一条可持续的途径，但其实际实施往往受到催化剂性能的阻碍。在此，通过使用第一性原理计算，我们系统地探索了一系列单簇催化剂的eNRR性能，该催化剂具有锚定在石墨二炔上的3d过渡金属单金属三原子簇（TM 3 /GDY，TM = Sc-Zn）。 Ti 3 /GDY、V 3 /GDY、Cr 3 /GDY 和 Mn 3 /GDY 均表现出强大的稳定性，并具有自发 N 2 化学吸附和显着的 N≡N 键活化（伸长率 > 0.14 Å）。化学键分析揭示了 TM-3d 轨道和 N 2 π* 反键态之间的 σ-π 协同供体-受体相互作用是优异催化活性的根源。 Ti 3 /GDY实现了-0.39 V的低极限电势，同时有效抑制了N 2 H 4 的形成。这种双重优势源于其较高的 d 带中心和高电荷转移。人们发现钛基三原子结构是可持续固氮的有希望的候选者。这项工作通过原子级协调工程和电子结构调制为 SCC 提供了基本设计原理。
