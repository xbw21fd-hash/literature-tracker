## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04816v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The successful synthesis of few-layer CrI3 has opened new avenues for research in two-dimensional magnetic materials. Owing to its simple crystal structure and excellent physical properties, layered CrI3 has been extensively studied in magneto-optical effects, excitons, tunneling transport, and novel memory devices. However, the most current theoretical studies rely heavily on the first-principles calculations, and a general analytical theoretical framework, particularly for electric-field modulation and transport properties, is still lacking. In this work, using a 28-band tight-binding model combined with linear response theory, we systematically investigate the optoelectronic response for monolayer CrI3 and its nanoribbons. The results demonstrate that: (1) a vertical electric field can selectively close the band gap of one spin channel while the other remains insulating, resulting a transition to an half-metallic state; (2) the electric field dynamically shifts the optical transition peaks, providing a theoretical basis for extracting band parameters from experimental photoconductivity spectra; (3) nanoribbons with different edge morphologies exhibit distinct edge-state distributions and electronic properties, indicating that optical transition can be dynamically modualted through edge design. The theoretical model developed in this study, which can describe external electric field effect, offers an efficient and flexible approach for analytically investigating the CrI3 family and related materials. This model overcomes the limitations of first-principles methods and provides a solid foundation for designing spintronic and optoelectronic devices controlled by electric fields and edge effect.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04816v1 公告类型：新 摘要：多层 CrI3 的成功合成为二维磁性材料的研究开辟了新的途径。由于其简单的晶体结构和优异的物理性能，层状 CrI3 在磁光效应、激子、隧道传输和新型存储器件等领域得到了广泛的研究。然而，当前的理论研究很大程度上依赖于第一性原理计算，并且仍然缺乏通用的分析理论框架，特别是电场调制和输运特性。在这项工作中，我们利用28带紧束缚模型结合线性响应理论，系统地研究了单层CrI3及其纳米带的光电响应。结果表明：（1）垂直电场可以选择性地关闭一个自旋通道的带隙，而另一个自旋通道保持绝缘，从而过渡到半金属态； (2)电场动态移动光学跃迁峰，为从实验光电导光谱中提取能带参数提供理论基础； （3）具有不同边缘形态的纳米带表现出不同的边缘态分布和电子特性，表明可以通过边缘设计动态调节光学跃迁。本研究开发的理论模型可以描述外部电场效应，为分析研究 CrI3 族及相关材料提供了一种有效且灵活的方法。该模型克服了第一原理方法的局限性，为设计由电场和边缘效应控制的自旋电子和光电器件提供了坚实的基础。
