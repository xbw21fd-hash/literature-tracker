## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15345v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Electronic structure calculations remain a major bottleneck in atomistic simulations and, not surprisingly, have attracted significant attention in machine learning (ML). Most existing approaches learn a direct map from molecular geometries, typically represented as graphs or encoded local environments, to molecular properties or use ML as a surrogate for electronic structure theory by targeting quantities such as Fock or density matrices expressed in an atomic orbital (AO) basis. Inspired by the Hohenberg-Kohn theorem, in this work, we propose an operator-centered framework in which the external (nuclear) potential, expressed in an AO basis, serves as the model input. From this operator, we construct hierarchical, body-ordered representations of atomic configurations that closely mirror the principles underlying several popular atom-centered descriptors. At the same time, the matrix-valued nature of the external potential provides a natural connection to equivariant message-passing neural networks. In particular, we show that successive products of the external potential provide a scalable route to equivariant message passing and enable an efficient description of long-range effects. We demonstrate that this approach can be used to model molecular properties, such as energies and dipole moments, from the external potential, or learn effective operator-to-operator maps, including mappings to the Fock matrix and the reduced density matrix from which multiple molecular observables can be simultaneously derived.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15345v1 公告类型：新 摘要：电子结构计算仍然是原子模拟的主要瓶颈，并且毫不奇怪地在机器学习 (ML) 领域引起了广泛关注。大多数现有方法都会学习从分子几何形状（通常表示为图形或编码的局部环境）到分子特性的直接映射，或者通过以原子轨道 (AO) 为基础表示的 Fock 或密度矩阵等量来使用机器学习作为电子结构理论的替代。受 Hohenberg-Kohn 定理的启发，在这项工作中，我们提出了一个以算子为中心的框架，其中以 AO 基础表示的外部（核）势作为模型输入。通过这个运算符，我们构建了原子配置的分层、体有序表示，这些表示密切反映了几种流行的以原子为中心的描述符背后的原理。同时，外部势的矩阵值性质提供了与等变消息传递神经网络的自然连接。特别是，我们证明了外部势的连续乘积为等变消息传递提供了可扩展的途径，并能够有效地描述长期影响。我们证明，这种方法可用于根据外部势对分子特性（例如能量和偶极矩）进行建模，或学习有效的算子到算子映射，包括到 Fock 矩阵和简化密度矩阵的映射，从中可以同时导出多个分子可观测值。
