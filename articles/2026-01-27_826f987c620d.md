## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18403v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the self-assembly of two-dimensional dodecagonal quasicrystals driven by cyclic shear, effectively replacing thermal fluctuations with plastic rearrangements. Using particles interacting via a smoothed square-shoulder potential, we demonstrate that cyclic shearing drives initially random configurations into ordered quasicrystalline states. The resulting non-equilibrium phase diagram qualitatively mirrors that of thermal equilibrium, exhibiting square, quasicrystalline, and hexagonal phases, as well as phase coexistence. Remarkably, the shear-stabilised quasicrystal appears even where the zero-temperature equilibrium ground state favours square-hexagonal coexistence, suggesting that mechanical driving can stabilise quasicrystalline order in a way analogous to entropic effects in thermal systems. The structural quality of the self-assembled state is maximised near the yielding transition, even though the dynamics are slowest there. Yet, the system still quickly forms monodomain quasicrystals without any complex annealing protocols, unlike at equilibrium, where thermal annealing would be required. Finite-size scaling analysis reveals that global orientational order decays slowly with system size, indicative of quasi-long-range order comparable to equilibrium hexatic phases. Overall, our results establish cyclic shear as an efficient pathway for the self-assembly of complex structures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18403v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了循环剪切驱动的二维十二角准晶的自组装，有效地用塑性重排代替了热波动。使用通过平滑方肩势相互作用的粒子，我们证明了循环剪切将最初的随机配置驱动为有序的准晶态。所得的非平衡相图定性地反映了热平衡相图，表现出方形、准晶和六方相以及相共存。值得注意的是，即使零温平衡基态有利于方形六边形共存，剪切稳定的准晶也会出现，这表明机械驱动可以以类似于热系统中熵效应的方式稳定准晶序。自组装状态的结构质量在屈服转变附近最大化，尽管那里的动力学最慢。然而，该系统仍然可以快速形成单畴准晶，无需任何复杂的退火协议，这与需要热退火的平衡状态不同。有限尺寸尺度分析表明，全局取向序随系统尺寸缓慢衰减，表明准长程序与平衡六方相相当。总的来说，我们的结果将循环剪切确立为复杂结构自组装的有效途径。
