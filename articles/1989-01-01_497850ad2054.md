## Abstract
TLDR: This work calculates the second-order magneto-optical response function in order to determine the nonlinear magneto/optical Kerr effect on the surface of ferromagnetic nickel and calculates nonlinear Kerr spectra which should be observable in surface second-harmonic-generation experiments.
