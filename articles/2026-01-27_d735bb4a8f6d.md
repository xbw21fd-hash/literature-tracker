## Abstract
arXiv:2511.18332v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: In this paper, we formulate the real-space Chern number in a supercell framework. In this framework, the overlap matrix between two corners of the Brillouin zone (BZ) is derived from diagonalizing the real-space Hamiltonian with periodic boundary conditions. The pass-ordered product of overlap matrices around the BZ boundary forms a Wilson loop, and defines the Chern number in real space. It is analytically shown that the real-space Chern number is quantized at integers for large enough systems and coincides with the Bott index used in the previous studies. The formulation is greatly simplified for the former so that it makes numerical computations more efficient. The real-space formula is used to numerically elucidate topological phases in a disordered Chern insulator. The Chern insulator is modeled by dimensional extension of the Rice-Mele (RM) model consisting of two sublattices, and is disordered by including a random onsite potential. As disorder strength increases, the nontrivial-to-trivial phase transition takes place for normal disorder with no sublattice polarization. By contrast, the phase diagram is almost unaffected by polarized disorder, indicating that nontrivial topology persists against disorder. These observations are supported by the linear conductance and the density of bulk states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.18332v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在本文中，我们在超单元框架中制定了实空间陈数。在此框架中，布里渊区 (BZ) 两个角之间的重叠矩阵是通过对具有周期性边界条件的实空间哈密顿量进行对角化而得出的。 BZ 边界周围重叠矩阵的传递有序积形成威尔逊环，并定义实空间中的陈数。分析表明，对于足够大的系统，实空间陈数被量化为整数，并且与先前研究中使用的 Bott 指数一致。前者的公式大大简化，使得数值计算更加高效。实空间公式用于数值阐明无序陈绝缘体中的拓扑相。 Chern 绝缘体通过由两个子晶格组成的 Rice-Mele (RM) 模型的维度扩展进行建模，并通过包含随机现场电势进行无序化。随着无序强度的增加，对于没有亚晶格极化的正常无序，会发生非平凡到平凡的相变。相比之下，相图几乎不受极化无序的影响，这表明非平凡的拓扑结构在无序情况下仍然存在。这些观察结果得到了线性电导和体态密度的支持。
