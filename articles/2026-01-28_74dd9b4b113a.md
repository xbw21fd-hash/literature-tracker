## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19603v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Strong Coulomb interactions can drive electrons to crystallize into a Wigner lattice. Achieving the bosonic analogue - a crystal of excitons - has remained elusive due to their short lifetimes and weaker interactions. Here, we report the observation of a thermodynamically stable exciton crystal in an excitonic insulator coupled to a moir\'e potential. Using an electron-hole bilayer composed of a monolayer MoSe2 and a WS2/WSe2 moir\'e superlattice, we construct a tunable extended Bose-Hubbard model with electrical control over exciton and charge doping in thermal equilibrium. Optical spectroscopy reveals spontaneous crystallization of long-lived excitons at one exciton filling per three moir\'e sites, evidenced by strong Umklapp scattering peaks in the optical spectrum. Exciton transport measurements further show a pronounced exciton resistance peak at the same filling, consistent with suppressed exciton hopping in a crystalline phase. When doped away from net charge neutrality, this moir\'e electron-hole bilayer can host new correlated insulating phases where dipolar excitonic insulators form on top of the background of a hole Mott insulator or generalized Wigner crystals in the moir\'e superlattice. These findings establish moir\'e excitonic insulators as a versatile platform for realizing correlated crystalline phases of bosons and fermions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19603v1 公告类型：新 摘要：强库仑相互作用可以驱动电子结晶成维格纳晶格。由于玻色子的寿命短且相互作用较弱，实现玻色子类似物（激子晶体）仍然难以实现。在这里，我们报告了在激子绝缘体中与莫尔势耦合的热力学稳定激子晶体的观察。利用由单层 MoSe2 和 WS2/WSe2 莫尔超晶格组成的电子空穴双层，我们构建了一个可调谐扩展 Bose-Hubbard 模型，该模型在热平衡中对激子和电荷掺杂进行电控制。光谱揭示了长寿命激子的自发结晶，每三个莫尔位点填充一个激子，光谱中强烈的乌姆克拉普散射峰证明了这一点。激子传输测量进一步显示在相同填充下存在明显的激子阻抗峰，这与结晶相中受抑制的激子跳跃一致。当掺杂远离净电荷中性时，这种莫尔电子-空穴双层可以容纳新的相关绝缘相，其中偶极激子绝缘体形成在空穴莫特绝缘体或莫尔超晶格中广义维格纳晶体的背景顶部。这些发现将莫尔激子绝缘体确立为实现玻色子和费米子相关晶相的通用平台。
