## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19291v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The magnetic compression experiment at General Fusion was a repetitive non-destructive test to study plasma physics applicable to magnetic target fusion compression. A compact torus (CT) is formed with a co-axial gun into a containment region with an hour-glass shaped inner flux conserver, and an insulating outer wall. External coil currents keep the CT off the outer wall (radial levitation) and then rapidly compress it inwards. The optimal external coil configuration greatly improved both the levitated CT lifetime and the recurrence rate of shots with good compressional flux conservation. As confirmed by spectrometer data, the improved levitation field profile reduced plasma impurity levels by suppressing the interaction between plasma and the insulating outer wall during the formation process. Significant increases in magnetic field, electron density, and ion temperature were routinely observed at magnetic compression in the final external coil configuration tested, despite the prevalence of an instability, thought be an external kink mode, at compression. Matching the decay rate of the levitation currents to that of the CT currents resulted in a reduced level of MHD activity associated with unintentional compression by the levitation field, and a higher probability of long-lived CTs. The DELiTE (Differential Equations on Linear Triangular Elements) framework was developed for spatial discretisation of partial differential equations on an unstructured triangular grid in axisymmetric geometry. The framework is based on discrete differential operators in matrix form, which are derived using linear finite elements and mimic some of the properties of their continuous counterparts. A single-fluid two-temperature MHD model is implemented in this framework.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19291v1 公告类型：新 摘要：General Fusion 的磁压缩实验是一项重复性无损测试，旨在研究适用于磁目标聚变压缩的等离子体物理。使用同轴枪将紧凑环面 (CT) 形成为具有沙漏形内部通量守恒器和绝缘外壁的安全壳区域。外部线圈电流使 CT 远离外壁（径向悬浮），然后迅速向内压缩。最佳的外部线圈配置极大地提高了悬浮 CT 的寿命和良好的压缩通量守恒的射击复发率。光谱仪数据证实，改进的悬浮场分布通过抑制形成过程中等离子体与绝缘外壁之间的相互作用来降低等离子体杂质水平。尽管在压缩时普遍存在不稳定性（被认为是外部扭结模式），但在最终测试的外部线圈配置中，在磁压缩时经常观察到磁场、电子密度和离子温度显着增加。将悬浮电流的衰减率与 CT 电流的衰减率相匹配，可以降低与悬浮场无意压缩相关的 MHD 活动水平，并提高 CT 寿命较长的可能性。 DELiTE（线性三角形单元微分方程）框架是为轴对称几何中非结构化三角网格上的偏微分方程的空间离散而开发的。该框架基于矩阵形式的离散微分算子，这些算子是使用线性有限元导出的，并模仿其连续对应物的一些属性。该框架实现了单流体两温度 MHD 模型。
