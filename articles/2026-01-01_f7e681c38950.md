## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24135v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We introduce and validate a machine learning-assisted protocol to classify time and space correlations of classical noise acting on a quantum system, using two interacting qubits as probe. We consider different classes of noise, according to their Markovianity and spatial correlations. Leveraging the sensitivity of a coherent population transfer protocol under three distinct driving conditions, the various noises are discriminated by only measuring the final transfer efficiencies. This approach reaches around 90% accuracy with a minimal experimental overhead.

arXiv:2512.24135v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们引入并验证了一种机器学习辅助协议，使用两个相互作用的量子位作为探针，对作用于量子系统的经典噪声的时间和空间相关性进行分类。我们根据马尔可夫性和空间相关性考虑不同类别的噪声。利用三种不同驾驶条件下相干群体转移协议的敏感性，仅通过测量最终转移效率来区分各种噪声。这种方法以最小的实验开销达到了 90% 左右的准确率。
