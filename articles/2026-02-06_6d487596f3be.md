## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05224v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We propose a tensor-network (TN) approach for solving classical optimization problems that is inspired by spectral filtering and sampling on quantum states. We first shift and scale an Ising Hamiltonian of the cost function so that all eigenvalues become non-negative and the ground states correspond to the the largest eigenvalues, which are then amplified by power iteration. We represent the transformed Hamiltonian as a matrix product operator (MPO) and form an immense power of this object via truncated MPO-MPO contractions, embedding the resulting operator into a matrix product state for sampling in the computational basis. In contrast to the density-matrix renormalization group, our approach provides a straightforward route to systematic improvement by increasing the bond dimension and is better at avoiding local minima. We also study the performance of this power method in the context of a higher-order Ising Hamiltonian on a heavy-hexagonal lattice, making a comparison with simulated annealing. These results highlight the potential of quantum-inspired algorithms for solving optimization problems and provide a baseline for assessing and developing quantum algorithms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05224v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们提出了一种张量网络（TN）方法来解决经典优化问题，该方法受到光谱滤波和量子态采样的启发。我们首先对成本函数的伊辛哈密顿量进行平移和缩放，使得所有特征值变为非负，并且基态对应于最大特征值，然后通过幂迭代来放大特征值。我们将变换后的哈密顿量表示为矩阵乘积算子（MPO），并通过截断的 MPO-MPO 收缩形成该对象的巨大幂，将所得算子嵌入到矩阵乘积状态中，以便在计算基础中进行采样。与密度矩阵重整化组相比，我们的方法通过增加键维数提供了系统改进的直接途径，并且更好地避免局部最小值。我们还研究了这种幂方法在重六方晶格上的高阶伊辛哈密顿量的背景下的性能，并与模拟退火进行了比较。这些结果凸显了量子启发算法在解决优化问题方面的潜力，并为评估和开发量子算法提供了基线。
