## Abstract
arXiv:2512.06313v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Moir\'e superlattices with long-range periodicity exhibit Hofstadter energy spectra under accessible magnetic fields, enabling the exploration of emergent quantum phenomena through a hierarchy of fractal states. However, higher-order features, located at elevated energies with narrow bandwidths, typically require high carrier densities and remain difficult to resolve using conventional electrical transport due to limited sensitivity and strong background conductivity. Here, we utilize acoustoelectric (AE) transport to probe high-order fractal states and the Hofstadter spectrum in graphene/hBN moir\'e superlattices. Surface acoustic waves on a ferroelectric LiNbO$_3$ substrate generate an AE voltage proportional to the derivative of electrical conductivity, significantly enhancing sensitivity to weak spectral features. Combined with substrate-induced high electron doping, this technique resolves fractal Brown-Zak oscillations up to the fifth-order and provides the first AE observation of the Hofstadter butterfly, revealing high-order fractal magnetic Bloch states and symmetry-broken Landau levels over a wide carrier density range. Our results establish AE transport as a powerful derivative-sensitive probe for emergent fractal quantum states in moir\'e-engineered 2D systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.06313v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：具有长程周期性的莫尔超晶格在可及磁场下表现出霍夫施塔特能谱，使得能够通过分形态层次结构探索涌现的量子现象。然而，位于高能量和窄带宽的高阶特征通常需要高载流子密度，并且由于有限的灵敏度和强的背景电导率，仍然难以使用传统的电传输来解析。在这里，我们利用声电（AE）传输来探测石墨烯/六方氮化硼莫尔超晶格中的高阶分形态和霍夫施塔特谱。铁电 LiNbO$_3$ 基底上的表面声波产生与电导率导数成比例的 AE 电压，显着增强对弱光谱特征的灵敏度。与衬底诱导的高电子掺杂相结合，该技术可将分形 Brown-Zak 振荡解析高达五阶，并首次对 Hofstadter 蝴蝶进行 AE 观察，揭示了在宽载流子密度范围内的高阶分形磁布洛赫态和对称破缺的朗道能级。我们的研究结果表明，AE 传输是一种强大的导数敏感探针，适用于莫尔电子工程二维系统中的涌现分形量子态。
