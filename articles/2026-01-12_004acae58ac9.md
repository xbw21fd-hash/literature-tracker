## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05784v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spintronics has emerged as a promising field for next-generation devices, offering functionalities beyond complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor (CMOS). A critical challenge in spintronics is to develop systems that can efficiently generate spin currents and enable their long-distance transport. Here, we demonstrate a graphene (Gr)/heavy metal (HM) heterostructure system that combines strong charge-spin interconversion efficiency, induced by the spin Hall effect, with a long spin diffusion length. By employing an industry-friendly magnetron sputtering technique, we deposit HM layers onto few-layer Gr while minimizing structural damage. The proximity effect from the HM enhances the spin Hall angle of Gr while limiting the reduction in its spin diffusion length. Additionally, the spin Hall angle can be tuned via an applied gate voltage, offering high controllability of the system. Importantly, these properties are observed across heterostructures composed of different HMs, indicating the generality of this approach. Our findings establish Gr/HM heterostructures as a scalable and versatile platform for spin current generation, paving the way for advanced spintronic devices with high efficiency, long spin propagation, and straightforward fabrication processes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05784v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自旋电子学已成为下一代器件的一个有前景的领域，提供超越互补金属氧化物半导体 (CMOS) 的功能。自旋电子学的一个关键挑战是开发能够有效产生自旋电流并实现其长距离传输的系统。在这里，我们展示了一种石墨烯（Gr）/重金属（HM）异质结构系统，该系统结合了由自旋霍尔效应引起的强大的电荷自旋相互转换效率和长自旋扩散长度。通过采用工业友好型磁控溅射技术，我们将 HM 层沉积到几层 Gr 上，同时最大限度地减少结构损坏。 HM 的邻近效应增强了 Gr 的自旋霍尔角，同时限制了其自旋扩散长度的减小。此外，自旋霍尔角可以通过施加的栅极电压进行调节，从而提供系统的高可控性。重要的是，这些特性是在由不同 HM 组成的异质结构中观察到的，表明了这种方法的通用性。我们的研究结果将 Gr/HM 异质结构确立为用于自旋电流生成的可扩展且多功能的平台，为具有高效率、长自旋传播和简单制造工艺的先进自旋电子器件铺平了道路。
