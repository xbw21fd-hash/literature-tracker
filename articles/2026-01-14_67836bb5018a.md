## Abstract
Polymer-polyoxometalate (POM) systems represent an emergent class of functional hybrid materials. However, the pH-dependent stability of POMs limits their scope in water. We show here that the water-mediated, so-called superchaotropic, binding of α-Keggin POMs to a non-ionic biopolymer, hydroxypropylcellulose (HPC), i) selectively stabilizes superchaotropic POMs in water, and ii) enables pH-responsive HPC solutions and hydrogels. Raman and NMR spectroscopy revealed that binding to HPC sterically protects the superchaotropic PW12O403- and SiW12O404- against hydrolysis, extending their stability from acidic to near-neutral pH. In turn, POMs with higher charge, stronger hydration and thus without the ability to bind to HPC, such as H2W12O406-, PW11O397- and SiW11O398-, do not get stabilized. Cloud points, small angle neutron scattering and rotational rheology showed that pH-induced conversion from superchaotropic PW12O403- to non-superchaotropic PW11O397- switches HPC from a bound, crosslinked to an unbound, non-crosslinked state enabling pH-switchable viscosity and gel-sol transitions. Superchaotropic stabilization and pH-switching are proposed as general phenomena in superchaotropic POM/solute systems highlighting the potential of superchaotropicity in aqueous soft materials.

## 摘要（中文）
聚合物-多金属氧酸盐（POM）系统代表了一类新兴的功能杂化材料。然而，POM 的 pH 依赖性稳定性限制了它们在水中的应用范围。我们在这里展示了 α-Keggin POM 与非离子生物聚合物羟丙基纤维素 (HPC) 的水介导的所谓超离液结合，i) 选择性地稳定水中的超离液 POM，ii) 能够形成 pH 响应型 HPC 溶液和水凝胶。拉曼和核磁共振光谱表明，与 HPC 的结合可以在空间上保护超离液 PW12O403- 和 SiW12O404- 免遭​​水解，从而将其稳定性从酸性延长到接近中性 pH。反过来，电荷较高、水合作用较强、因此无法与 HPC 结合的 POM（例如 H2W12O406-、PW11O397- 和 SiW11O398-）也不会变得稳定。浊点、小角中子散射和旋转流变学表明，pH 诱导的从超离液 PW12O403- 到非超离液 PW11O397- 的转化可将 HPC 从结合、交联状态切换为非结合、非交联状态，从而实现 pH 可切换的粘度和凝胶-溶胶转变。超离液稳定性和 pH 值转换被认为是超离液 POM/溶质体系中的普遍现象，突出了超离液在水性软材料中的潜力。
