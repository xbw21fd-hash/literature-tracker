## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05147v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The fragile quantum states of low-temperature quantum applications require protection from infrared radiation caused by higher-temperature stages or other sources. We propose a material system that can efficiently block radiation up to the optical range while transmitting photons at low gigahertz frequencies. It is based on the effect that incident photons are strongly scattered when their wavelength is comparable to the size of particles embedded in a weakly absorbing medium (Mie-scattering). The goal of this work is to tailor the absorption and transmission spectrum of an non-magnetic epoxy resin containing sapphire spheres by simulating its dependence on the size distribution. Additionally, we fabricate several material compositions, characterize them, as well as other materials, at optical, infrared, and gigahertz frequencies. In the infrared region (stop band) the attenuation of the Mie-scattering optimized material is high and comparable to that of other commonly used filter materials. At gigahertz frequencies (pass-band), the prototype filter exhibits a high transmission at millikelvin temperatures, with an insertion loss of less than $0.4\,$dB below $10\,$GHz.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05147v1 公告类型：新 摘要：低温量子应用的脆弱量子态需要保护免受高温阶段或其他来源引起的红外辐射。我们提出了一种材料系统，可以有效阻挡高达光学范围的辐射，同时以低千兆赫频率传输光子。它基于这样的效应：当入射光子的波长与嵌入弱吸收介质中的粒子的尺寸相当时（米氏散射），入射光子会被强烈散射。这项工作的目标是通过模拟包含蓝宝石球的非磁性环氧树脂对尺寸分布的依赖性来定制其吸收和透射光谱。此外，我们还制造了多种材料成分，并在光学、红外和千兆赫频率下表征它们以及其他材料。在红外区域（阻带），米氏散射优化材料的衰减很高，与其他常用滤光材料的衰减相当。在千兆赫频率（通带）下，原型滤波器在毫开尔文温度下表现出高传输率，插入损耗低于 $10\,$GHz，插入损耗小于 $0.4\,$dB。
