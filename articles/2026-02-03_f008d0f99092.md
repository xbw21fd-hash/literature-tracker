## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00958v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The mid-infrared spectral window is essential for molecular fingerprinting and atmospheric sensing, yet unlocking its full potential is currently constrained by a fundamental instrumental trade-off: existing systems cannot simultaneously deliver broad bandwidth, high photon flux, and metrological frequency fidelity. Here, we resolve this bottleneck by demonstrating a metrology-grade spectroscopic system based on difference frequency generation, driven by widely tunable, near-infrared diode lasers traceable to atomic standards. Our system achieves continuous tunability across the 3-3.7 $\mu$m atmospheric window and delivers output power exceeding 45 mW with an absolute frequency accuracy of 7.2 MHz. We harness this convergence to overcome a critical barrier in integrated photonics, unambiguously identifying and eliminating hydrogen-induced absorption in silicon nitride microresonators to achieve an 88-fold reduction in optical loss. We further reveal multi-phonon absorption in the silica cladding as the fundamental limit to mid-infrared integrated photonics. Finally, we demonstrate the system's versatility through scattering-resilient LiDAR capable of penetrating optically dense fog, and dual-modality sensing that simultaneously retrieves target distance and chemical composition. By unifying the rigor of frequency metrology with the versatility of broadband sensing, this architecture establishes a new paradigm for multi-dimensional perception in complex environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00958v1 公告类型：新 摘要：中红外光谱窗口对于分子指纹识别和大气传感至关重要，但目前释放其全部潜力受到基本仪器权衡的限制：现有系统无法同时提供宽带宽、高光子通量和计量频率保真度。在这里，我们通过演示基于差频生成的计量级光谱系统解决了这一瓶颈，该系统由可追溯至原子标准的广泛可调的近红外二极管激光器驱动。我们的系统在 3-3.7 $\mu$m 大气窗口内实现连续可调，并提供超过 45 mW 的输出功率，绝对频率精度为 7.2 MHz。我们利用这种融合来克服集成光子学中的关键障碍，明确识别并消除氮化硅微谐振器中的氢致吸收，从而将光学损耗减少 88 倍。我们进一步揭示了二氧化硅包层中的多声子吸收是中红外集成光子学的基本限制。最后，我们通过能够穿透光学浓雾的抗散射激光雷达和同时检索目标距离和化学成分的双模态传感来展示系统的多功能性。通过将频率计量的严格性与宽带传感的多功能性相结合，该架构为复杂环境中的多维感知建立了新的范例。
