## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24397v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The Gaussian phase approximation (GPA) underlies many standard diffusion magnetic resonance (MR) signal models, yet its validity is rarely scrutinized. Here, we assess the validity of the GPA by analytically deriving the excess phase kurtosis $\kappa_4/\kappa_2^2$, where $\kappa_n$ is the $n^{\text{th}}$ cumulant of the accumulated phase distribution due to motion. We consider the signal behavior of the spin echo with constant gradient amplitude $g$ and echo time $T$ in several one-dimensional model systems: (1) a stationary Poisson pore-hopping model with uniform pore spacing $\Delta x$ and mean inter-hop time $\tau_{\text{hop}}$; (2) a trapped-release model in which spin isochromats are initially immobilized and then released with diffusivity $D$ following an exponentially-distributed release time, $\tau_{\text{rel}}$; and (3) restricted diffusion in a domain of length $L$. To our knowledge, this is among the first systematic analytical treatments of spin echo phase kurtosis without assuming Gaussian compartments or infinitesimally short gradient pulses. In the pore-hopping system, $\kappa_4/\kappa^2_2 = (9/5)\tau_{\text{hop}}/T$, inversely proportional to the mean hop number, $T/\tau_{\text{hop}}$. In the trapped-release system, $\kappa_4/\kappa_2^2$ is positive and decreases roughly log-linearly with $T/\langle\tau_{\text{rel}}\rangle$, where $\langle\tau_{\text{rel}}\rangle$ is the average release time. For restriction, $\kappa_4/\kappa_2^2$ vanishes at small and large $L/\sqrt{DT}$, but has complicated intermediate behavior. There is a negative peak at $L/\sqrt{DT}\approx 1.2$ and a positive peak at $L/\sqrt{DT}\approx 4.4$. Monte Carlo simulations are included to validate the analytical findings. Overall, we find that the GPA does not generally hold for these systems under moderate experimental conditions, i.e., $T=10\;\mathrm{ms}$, $g\approx 0.2-0.6\;\mathrm{T/m}$.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24397v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：高斯相位近似（GPA）是许多标准扩散磁共振（MR）信号模型的基础，但其有效性很少受到审查。在这里，我们通过分析推导额外相位峰度 $\kappa_4/\kappa_2^2$ 来评估 GPA 的有效性，其中 $\kappa_n$ 是由于运动而累积的相位分布的 $n^{\text{th}}$ 累积量。我们考虑几个一维模型系统中具有恒定梯度幅度 $g$ 和回波时间 $T$ 的自旋回波的信号行为：（1）具有均匀孔间距 $\Delta x$ 和平均跳跃间时间 $\tau_{\text{hop}}$ 的稳态泊松孔跳跃模型； (2) 捕获释放模型，其中自旋等色体最初被固定，然后在指数分布的释放时间 $\tau_{\text{rel}}$ 后以扩散率 $D$ 释放； (3) 长度为 $L$ 的域中的受限扩散。据我们所知，这是第一个对自旋回波相位峰度进行系统分析处理的方法，无需假设高斯间隔或无限短的梯度脉冲。在孔跳跃系统中，$\kappa_4/\kappa^2_2 = (9/5)\tau_{\text{hop}}/T$，与平均跳跃数$T/\tau_{\text{hop}}$成反比。在捕获释放系统中，$\kappa_4/\kappa_2^2$ 为正值，并随 $T/\langle\tau_{\text{rel}}\rangle$ 大致呈对数线性递减，其中 $\langle\tau_{\text{rel}}\rangle$ 是平均释放时间。为了限制，$\kappa_4/\kappa_2^2$ 在小和大 $L/\sqrt{DT}$ 处消失，但具有复杂的中间行为。在 $L/\sqrt{DT}\approx 1.2$ 处有一个负峰值，在 $L/\sqrt{DT}\approx 4.4$ 处有一个正峰值。蒙特卡罗模拟被用来验证分析结果。总的来说，我们发现在中等实验条件下，即 $T=10\;\mathrm{ms}$、$g\approx 0.2-0.6\;\mathrm{T/m}$ 下，这些系统的 GPA 通常并不成立。
