## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04717v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Field geological studies highlighted the heterogeneous structure of fault zones from the meter- to millimeter scale, but such internal variability is not generally resolved by seismological techniques due to spatial resolution limits. The near-surface velocity structure of the Vado di Corno seismogenic fault zone was quantified at different length scales, from laboratory measurements of ultrasonic velocities (few centimeters rock samples, 1 MHz source) to high-resolution first-arrival seismic tomography (spatial resolution to a few meters). The fault zone juxtaposed structural units with contrasting ultrasonic velocities. The fault core cataclastic units were slower compared to damage zone units. A negative correlation between ultrasonic velocity and porosity was observed, with dispersion in fault core units related to varying degree of textural maturity and pore space sealing by calcite. Low-velocity outliers in the damage zone were instead linked to microfracture networks with local cataclasis and partial calcite sealing. P-wave high-resolution seismic tomography imaged distinct fault-bounded rock bodies, matching the geometry and size of field-mapped structural units. At this length scale, relatively fast fault core units and low-strain damage zones contrasted with a very slow intensely fractured high-strain damage zone. The discrepancy between higher ultrasonic velocities and lower tomography-derived ones was reconciled through an effective medium approach considering the effect of meso-scale fractures in each unit. This revealed a heterogeneous fault zone velocity structure with different scaling among structural units. Lastly, the persistence of a thick compliant high-strain damage zone at shallow depth may significantly affect fault zone mechanics and the distribution of near-surface deformations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04717v1 公告类型：新 摘要：野外地质研究强调了从米到毫米尺度的断层带的异质结构，但由于空间分辨率的限制，这种内部变化通常不能通过地震技术来解决。 Vado di Corno 发震断层带的近地表速度结构在不同长度尺度上进行了量化，从超声波速度的实验室测量（几厘米岩石样本，1 MHz 源）到高分辨率初至地震层析成像（空间分辨率达到几米）。断层带并置具有对比超声波速度的结构单元。与损伤区单元相比，断层核心碎裂单元的速度较慢。观察到超声波速度和孔隙度之间呈负相关，断层核心单元中的分散度与不同程度的结构成熟度和方解石封闭孔隙空间有关。相反，损伤区的低速异常值与具有局部碎裂和部分方解石封闭的微裂缝网络相关。 P 波高分辨率地震层析成像对不同的断层边界岩体进行成像，与现场测绘的结构单元的几何形状和尺寸相匹配。在这个长度尺度上，相对较快的断层核心单元和低应变损伤区与非常缓慢的强烈断裂的高应变损伤区形成对比。考虑到每个单元细观尺度裂缝的影响，通过有效的介质方法来协调较高的超声速度和较低的断层扫描速度之间的差异。这揭示了结构单元之间具有不同尺度的异质断层带速度结构。最后，浅层厚柔顺高应变损伤区的持续存在可能会显着影响断层带力学和近地表变形的分布。
