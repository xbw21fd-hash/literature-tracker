## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05342v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Oak Ridge National Laboratory STS Project will enhance the Spallation Neutron Source by adding a new neutron source. The upgrade includes a 30% increase in beam energy and a 50% boost in beam current, doubling the accelerator's power capability to 2.8 MW. The Ring-to-Second-Target Beam Transport (RTST) system is vital in directing high-energy proton beams to the new second target station. New magnets have to fit into a restricted space within the existing beamline to add the new extraction line. New, fast kicker magnets (Pulsed Dipole), a focusing quad with an aperture for both beamlines (Large Quad) and the replacement of existing 21Q40 Quads with 'Narrow Quads' having identical fields as the existing design are Fermilab's responsibility. The design of these magnets poses unique challenges, as in addition to the high requirements on the quality of magnetic fields, they are subject to major restrictions related to their dimensions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05342v1 公告类型：新 摘要：橡树岭国家实验室 STS 项目将通过添加新的中子源来增强散裂中子源。此次升级包括束流能量增加 30%，束流增加 50%，使加速器的功率能力增加一倍，达到 2.8 MW。环到第二目标束流传输（RTST）系统对于将高能质子束引导到新的第二目标站至关重要。新磁铁必须安装到现有光束线内的有限空间中才能添加新的提取线。费米实验室负责开发新型快速踢球磁体（脉冲偶极子）、具有双束线孔径的聚焦四极杆（大四极杆）以及用与现有设计具有相同磁场的“窄四极杆”替换现有的 21Q40 四极杆。这些磁体的设计提出了独特的挑战，因为除了对磁场质量的高要求之外，它们还受到与其尺寸相关的重大限制。
