## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02406v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In the current study, we present a mathematical and computational fluid dynamics (CFD) model for simulating open-cycle linear Faraday-type continuous-electrode channels of magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) power generators, operating on combustion plasma. The model extends the Favre-averaged Navier-Stokes equations to account for the electric properties of the flowing plasma gas and its reaction to the applied magnetic field. The model takes into account various effects, such as the Lorentz force, turbulence, compressibility, and energy extraction from the plasma, and it adopts an electric potential technique along with the low magnetic Reynolds number (Rem) approximation. The model is numerically implemented using the multiphysics open-source computer programming environment "OpenFOAM," which combines the finite volume method (FVM) and the object-oriented programming (OOP) concept. The capabilities of the model are demonstrated by simulating the supersonic channel of the large-scale pulsed MHD generator (PMHDG) called "Sakhalin", with the aid of collected data and empirical expressions in the literature about its tested operation. Sakhalin was the world's largest PMHDG, with a demonstrated peak electric power output of 510 MW. Sakhalin operated on solid-propellant plasma (SPP), and it had a single supersonic divergent Faraday-type continuous-electrode channel with a length of 4.5 m. We check the validity of the model through comparisons with independent results for the Sakhalin PMHDG. Then, we process our three-dimensional simulation results to provide scalar characteristics of the Sakhalin channel, one-dimensional profiles along the longitudinal centerline, and three-dimensional distributions in the entire channel.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02406v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在当前的研究中，我们提出了一种数学和计算流体动力学 (CFD) 模型，用于模拟在燃烧等离子体上运行的磁流体动力 (MHD) 发电机的开循环线性法拉第型连续电极通道。该模型扩展了法夫尔平均纳维-斯托克斯方程，以考虑流动等离子体气体的电特性及其对所施加磁场的反应。该模型考虑了洛伦兹力、湍流、压缩性和等离子体能量提取等各种影响，并采用电势技术和低磁雷诺数 (Rem) 近似。该模型使用多物理场开源计算机编程环境“OpenFOAM”进行数值实现，该环境结合了有限体积法（FVM）和面向对象编程（OOP）概念。该模型的功能通过模拟名为“萨哈林”的大型脉冲 MHD 发生器 (PMHDG) 的超音速通道，并借助收集的数据和文献中有关其测试运行的经验表达式来证明。萨哈林岛是世界上最大的 PMHDG，已证明峰值电力输出为 510 兆瓦。萨哈林号采用固体推进剂等离子体（SPP）运行，具有单个超音速发散法拉第型连续电极通道，长度为 4.5 m。我们通过与萨哈林岛 PMHDG 的独立结果进行比较来检查模型的有效性。然后，我们处理三维模拟结果，以提供萨哈林河道的标量特征、沿纵向中心线的一维剖面以及整个河道的三维分布。
