## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21721v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The third and fourth density and acoustic virial coefficients of neon were determined at temperatures between 10 and 5000 K from first principles employing the path-integral Monte Carlo (PIMC) approach. For these calculations, we used the pair potential of Hellmann $\textit{et al.}$ [J. Chem. Phys. 154, 164304 (2021)], which is based on supermolecular $\textit{ab initio}$ calculations with basis sets of up to octuple-zeta quality and levels of theory up to coupled cluster with single, double, triple, quadruple, and perturbative pentuple excitations [CCSDTQ(P)]. The potential also accounts for relativistic, retardation, and post-Born$-$Oppenheimer effects and is provided with reliable uncertainty estimates. To incorporate nonadditive interactions, we developed a nonadditive three-body potential based on extensive supermolecular CCSD(T), CCSDT, and CCSDT(Q) calculations with basis sets of up to sextuple-zeta quality. This potential also accounts for relativistic effects. The very small nonadditive four-body contributions to the fourth virial coefficients were considered using a relatively simple nonadditive four-body potential based on supermolecular CCSD(T) calculations. We calculated the third and fourth density and third acoustic virial coefficients directly by PIMC and the fourth acoustic virial coefficient indirectly using thermodynamic relations between the density and acoustic virial coefficients. The uncertainties of the pair potential and those estimated for our nonadditive three-body potential were rigorously propagated in the PIMC calculations into uncertainties for the virial coefficients. These uncertainties are distinctly smaller than those of almost all of the corresponding experimental virial coefficient data.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21721v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在 10 至 5000 K 之间的温度下，采用路径积分蒙特卡罗 (PIMC) 方法根据第一原理确定了氖气的第三和第四密度和声学维里系数。对于这些计算，我们使用了 Hellmann $\textit{et al.}$ [J.化学。物理。 154, 164304 (2021)]，它基于超分子 $\textit{ab initio}$ 计算，其基础集高达八重 zeta 质量，理论水平高达单重、双重、三重、四重和微扰五重激发的耦合簇 [CCSDTQ(P)]。该潜力还解释了相对论、迟滞和出生后奥本海默效应，并提供了可靠的不确定性估计。为了纳入非加性相互作用，我们基于广泛的超分子 CCSD(T)、CCSDT 和 CCSDT(Q) 计算开发了一种非加性三体势，其基础集高达六重 zeta 质量。这种势能也解释了相对论效应。使用基于超分子 CCSD(T) 计算的相对简单的非加性四体势来考虑对第四维里系数的非常小的非加性四体贡献。我们直接通过 PIMC 计算第三和第四密度以及第三声学维里系数，并利用密度和声学维里系数之间的热力学关系间接计算第四声学维里系数。电对势的不确定性和非加性三体势估计的不确定性在 PIMC 计算中严格传播为维里系数的不确定性。这些不确定性明显小于几乎所有相应的实验维里系数数据的不确定性。
