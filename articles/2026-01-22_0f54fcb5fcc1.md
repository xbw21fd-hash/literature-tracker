## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14670v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Inverse design is a commonly used methodology for creating devices that manipulate electromagnetic (EM) waves by algorithmically modifying device parameters to achieve a desired functionality. Utilizing plasma, a dynamically tunable medium, allows the optimization of the design process to be conducted directly on the experimental hardware (in-situ). A key advantage of this method is the creation of devices that are inherently switchable and dynamically reconfigurable. Bayesian optimization is used to tune the plasma density of 91 independent discharges that make up a plasma metamaterial (PMM) device to steer incoming EM waves to desired exit waveguides. Measurements were conducted in an automated loop where a vector network analyzer records the PMM transmission characteristics for each device setting. By relying only on measured scattering parameters, this gradient-free approach is robust to experimental drift and noise and does not require complex full-wave models. Significant performance improvements over traditional simulation-based (in-silico) inverse design are demonstrated, with in-situ Bayesian optimization achieving up to 10,000x higher isolation between ports than the best in-silico design at the same target frequency. This work also presents guidelines for applying Bayesian optimization to noisy, high-dimensional physical systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14670v1 公告类型：新 摘要：逆向设计是一种常用的方法，用于通过算法修改设备参数来创建操纵电磁 (EM) 波的设备，以实现所需的功能。利用等离子体这种动态可调介质，可以直接在实验硬件上（原位）进行设计过程的优化。该方法的一个关键优点是创建本质上可切换和动态可重新配置的设备。贝叶斯优化用于调整 91 个独立放电的等离子体密度，这些放电构成等离子体超材料 (PMM) 设备，以将传入的电磁波引导至所需的出口波导。测量在自动循环中进行，其中矢量网络分析仪记录每个设备设置的 PMM 传输特性。由于仅依赖测量的散射参数，这种无梯度方法对实验漂移和噪声具有鲁棒性，并且不需要复杂的全波模型。与传统的基于仿真（计算机模拟）逆向设计相比，性能得到了显着改进，在相同目标频率下，现场贝叶斯优化实现了比最佳计算机模拟设计高出 10,000 倍的端口间隔离度。这项工作还提出了将贝叶斯优化应用于嘈杂的高维物理系统的指南。
