## Abstract
arXiv:2512.13746v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Fiber reinforcement and polymer matrix respond differently to manufacturing conditions due to mismatch in coefficient of thermal expansion and matrix shrinkage during curing of thermosets. These heterogeneities generate residual stresses over multiple length scales, whose partial release leads to process-induced deformation (PID), requiring accurate prediction and mitigation via optimized non-isothermal cure cycles. This study considers a unidirectional AS4 carbon fiber/amine bi-functional epoxy prepreg and models PID using a two-mechanism framework that accounts for thermal expansion/shrinkage and cure shrinkage. The model is validated against manufacturing trials to identify initial and boundary conditions, then used to generate PID responses for a diverse set of non-isothermal cure cycles (time-temperature profiles). Building on this physics-based foundation, we develop a data-driven surrogate based on Deep Operator Networks (DeepONets). A DeepONet is trained on a dataset combining high-fidelity simulations with targeted experimental measurements of PID. We extend this to a Feature-wise Linear Modulation (FiLM) DeepONet, where branch-network features are modulated by external parameters, including the initial degree of cure, enabling prediction of time histories of degree of cure, viscosity, and deformation. Because experimental data are available only at limited time instances (for example, final deformation), we use transfer learning: simulation-trained trunk and branch networks are fixed and only the final layer is updated using measured final deformation. Finally, we augment the framework with Ensemble Kalman Inversion (EKI) to quantify uncertainty under experimental conditions and to support optimization of cure schedules for reduced PID in composites.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.13746v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：由于热固性材料固化过程中热膨胀系数和基体收缩率不匹配，纤维增强材料和聚合物基体对制造条件的响应不同。这些不均匀性会在多个长度尺度上产生残余应力，其部分释放会导致过程诱发变形 (PID)，需要通过优化的非等温固化循环进行准确预测和缓解。本研究考虑单向 AS4 碳纤维/胺双功能环氧预浸料，并使用双机制框架对 PID 进行建模，该框架考虑了热膨胀/收缩和固化收缩。该模型根据制造试验进行验证，以确定初始条件和边界条件，然后用于为一组不同的非等温固化循环（时间-温度曲线）生成 PID 响应。在此基于物理的基础上，我们开发了一种基于深度算子网络（DeepONets）的数据驱动代理。 DeepONet 在将高保真模拟与 PID 目标实验测量相结合的数据集上进行训练。我们将其扩展到特征线性调制（FiLM）DeepONet，其中分支网络特征由外部参数（包括初始固化程度）进行调制，从而能够预测固化程度、粘度和变形的时间历程。由于实验数据仅在有限的时间实例（例如最终变形）下可用，因此我们使用迁移学习：模拟训练的主干和分支网络是固定的，并且仅使用测量的最终变形更新最后一层。最后，我们通过集成卡尔曼反演 (EKI) 增强了该框架，以量化实验条件下的不确定性，并支持优化固化方案以减少复合材料中的 PID。
