## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20444v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the behavior of interstitial $^8$Li$^+$ implanted near the surface of 2H-MoTe$_2$ using $\beta$-detected NMR. We find that, unlike the muon, $^8$Li$^+$ does not show any signature of induced magnetism. This result is consistent with density functional theory, which identifies the Li stopping site at the 2a Wyckoff position in the van der Waals gap and confirms the absence of detectable Li-induced electronic spin polarization. Both the spin-lattice relaxation and the resonance lines show evidence of strong spin dynamics above $\sim 200$ K, reminiscent of local stochastic $^8$Li$^+$ motion within a cage. The resonance line shape consists of quadrupolar satellites on top of a broad central peak. To better understand the interaction of $^8$Li$^+$ with the host material, we employ a frequency-comb measurement, by simultaneously exciting four frequencies corresponding to the first-order quadrupolar satellite transitions, $\nu_0 \pm 3\nu_{\mathrm{comb}}$ and $\nu_0 \pm\nu_{\mathrm{comb}}$ around the Larmor frequency $\nu_0$ as a function of $\nu_{\mathrm{comb}}$. This offers an enhanced sensitivity to the quadrupolar split portion of the line. Using this method, we find a small decrease of the quadrupolar frequency with increasing temperature, showing the typical behavior associated with thermally excited phonons and the absence of any magnetic response which was observed with other defects in 2H-MoTe$_2$.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20444v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用 $\beta$ 检测到的 NMR 来研究植入在 2H-MoTe$_2$ 表面附近的间隙 $^8$Li$^+$ 的行为。我们发现，与 μ 子不同，$^8$Li$^+$ 没有表现出任何感应磁性的特征。这一结果与密度泛函理论一致，密度泛函理论确定了范德华间隙中 2a 威科夫位置处的 Li 停止位点，并证实了不存在可检测到的 Li 诱导的电子自旋极化。自旋晶格弛豫和共振线都显示出高于 $\sim 200$ K 的强自旋动力学的证据，让人想起笼内的局部随机 $^8$Li$^+$ 运动。共振线形状由宽中心峰顶部的四极卫星组成。为了更好地理解 $^8$Li$^+$ 与主体材料的相互作用，我们采用频率梳测量，通过同时激发对应于一阶四极卫星跃迁的四个频率，$\nu_0 \pm 3\nu_{\mathrm{comb}}$ 和 $\nu_0 \pm\nu_{\mathrm{comb}}$ 围绕拉莫尔频率 $\nu_0$ 作为函数$\nu_{\mathrm{comb}}$。这提高了线路四极分离部分的灵敏度。使用这种方法，我们发现四极频率随着温度的升高而小幅降低，显示出与热激发声子相关的典型行为，并且没有在 2H-MoTe$_2$ 中的其他缺陷中观察到的任何磁响应。
