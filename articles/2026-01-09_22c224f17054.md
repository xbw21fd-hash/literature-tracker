## Abstract
arXiv:2402.06677v5 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Quantum entanglement manifests itself in non-local correlations between the constituents of a system. In its simplest realization, a measurement on one subsystem is affected by a prior measurement on its partner, irrespective of their separation. For multiple parties, purely collective types of entanglement exist but their detection, even theoretically, remains an outstanding open question. Here, we argue that all forms of multipartite entanglement entirely disappear during the typical evolution of a physical state as it heats up, evolves in time in a large family of dynamical protocols, or as its parts become separated. We focus on the generic case where the system interacts with an environment. These results mainly follow from the geometry of the entanglement-free continent in the space of physical states, and hold in great generality. We illustrate these phenomena with a frustrated molecular quantum magnet in and out of equilibrium, and a quantum spin chain. In contrast, if the particles are fermions, such as electrons, another notion of entanglement exists that protects bipartite quantum correlations. However, genuinely collective fermionic entanglement disappears during typical evolution, thus sharing the same fate as in bosonic systems. These findings provide fundamental knowledge about the structure of entanglement in quantum matter and architectures, paving the way for its manipulation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2402.06677v5 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：量子纠缠表现为系统组成部分之间的非局域相关性。在最简单的实现中，一个子系统上的测量会受到其伙伴子系统上先前测量的影响，无论它们的分离程度如何。对于多方来说，存在纯粹集体类型的纠缠，但即使在理论上，它们的检测仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。在这里，我们认为，在物理状态的典型演化过程中，所有形式的多部分纠缠都完全消失，因为它会升温，在一大群动力学协议中随时间演化，或者当它的各个部分分离时。我们关注系统与环境交互的一般情况。这些结果主要来自物理态空间中无纠缠大陆的几何形状，并且具有很大的普遍性。我们用处于平衡和不平衡状态的受挫分子量子磁体以及量子自旋链来说明这些现象。相反，如果粒子是费米子，例如电子，则存在另一种纠缠概念来保护二分量子相关性。然而，真正的集体费米子纠缠在典型的进化过程中消失了，因此与玻色子系统有着相同的命运。这些发现提供了有关量子物质和体系结构中纠缠结构的基础知识，为其操纵铺平了道路。
