## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17150v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Potassium niobate (KNbO3) is a prototypical perovskite ferroelectric with large electro-optic and nonlinear optical responses, high optical damage thresholds and a rich sequence of temperature-driven phase transformations, making it a promising platform for tunable photonic devices. In this work, we develop a thermodynamic model for the coupled ferroelectric and optical properties of KNbO3. By separating the total polarization into lattice and electronic contributions, the model provides a unified description of both the anisotropic ferroelectric and optical properties. The thermodynamic coefficients are determined by fitting to experimental measurements of the spontaneous polarization, dielectric susceptibilities, lattice parameters, and refractive indices. Without any further fitting, the model quantitatively predicts the temperature dependence of the electro-optic and piezoelectric coefficients in close agreement with experimental measurements. By utilizing the electronic polarization equation of motion, the model further captures the optical dispersion in the near infrared to visible spectrum. This work provides the thermodynamic foundation for future studies of coupled ferroelectric and optical phenomena in KNbO3.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.17150v1 公告类型：新 摘要：铌酸钾 (KNbO3) 是一种典型的钙钛矿铁电体，具有大的电光和非线性光学响应、高光学损伤阈值和丰富的温度驱动相变序列，使其成为可调谐光子器件的有前景的平台。在这项工作中，我们开发了 KNbO3 耦合铁电和光学特性的热力学模型。通过将总极化分为晶格和电子贡献，该模型提供了各向异性铁电和光学特性的统一描述。热力学系数是通过拟合自发极化、介电磁化率、晶格参数和折射率的实验测量来确定的。无需进一步拟合，该模型即可定量预测电光系数和压电系数的温度依赖性，与实验测量结果非常一致。通过利用电子偏振运动方程，该模型进一步捕获近红外到可见光谱中的光学色散。这项工作为未来研究 KNbO3 中铁电和光学耦合现象提供了热力学基础。
