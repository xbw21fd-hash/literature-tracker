## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13242v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study explores how entanglement and quantum teleportation of magnons can be achieved in coupled spin chain systems. By utilizing different magnetic configurations, we show that parallel spin chains function like magnonic beam splitters, whereas anti-parallel chains produce two-magnon squeezing and strong entanglement. Combining these components, we design magnonic circuits capable of continuous-variable quantum entanglement and teleportation, supported by quantum Langevin simulations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13242v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项研究探讨了如何在耦合自旋链系统中实现磁振子的纠缠和量子隐形传态。通过利用不同的磁性配置，我们证明平行自旋链的功能类似于磁振子分束器，而反平行链则产生两个磁振子挤压和强纠缠。结合这些组件，我们设计了能够连续可变量子纠缠和隐形传态的磁波电路，并得到量子朗之万模拟的支持。
