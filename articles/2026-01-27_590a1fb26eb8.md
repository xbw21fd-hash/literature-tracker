## Abstract
arXiv:2404.14258v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Kohn-Sham Density Functional Theory (KS-DFT) provides the exact ground state energy and electron density of a molecule, contingent on the as-yet-unknown universal exchange-correlation (XC) functional. Recent research has demonstrated that neural networks can efficiently learn to represent approximations to that functional, offering accurate generalizations to molecules not present during the training process. With the latest advancements in quantum-enhanced machine learning (ML), evidence is growing that Quantum Neural Network (QNN) models may offer advantages in ML applications. In this work, we explore the use of QNNs for representing XC functionals, enhancing and comparing them to classical ML techniques. We present QNNs based on differentiable quantum circuits (DQCs) as quantum (hybrid) models for XC in KS-DFT, implemented across various architectures. We assess their performance on 1D and 3D systems. To that end, we expand existing differentiable KS-DFT frameworks and propose strategies for efficient training of such functionals, highlighting the importance of fractional orbital occupation for accurate results. Our best QNN-based XC functional yields energy profiles of the H$_2$ and planar H$_4$ molecules that deviate by no more than 1 mHa from the reference DMRG and FCI/6-31G results, respectively. Moreover, they reach chemical precision on a system, H$_2$H$_2$, not present in the training dataset, using only a few variational parameters. This work lays the foundation for the integration of quantum models in KS-DFT, thereby opening new avenues for expressing XC functionals in a differentiable way and facilitating computations of various properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2404.14258v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：Kohn-Sham 密度泛函理论 (KS-DFT) 提供了分子的精确基态能量和电子密度，具体取决于尚未可知的通用交换相关 (XC) 泛函。最近的研究表明，神经网络可以有效地学习表示该函数的近似值，从而为训练过程中不存在的分子提供准确的概括。随着量子增强机器学习 (ML) 的最新进展，越来越多的证据表明量子神经网络 (QNN) 模型可能在 ML 应用中提供优势。在这项工作中，我们探索使用 QNN 来表示 XC 泛函，增强它们并将其与经典的 ML 技术进行比较。我们提出基于可微分量子电路 (DQC) 的 QNN 作为 KS-DFT 中 XC 的量子（混合）模型，并在各种架构中实现。我们评估它们在 1D 和 3D 系统上的性能。为此，我们扩展了现有的可微分 KS-DFT 框架，并提出了有效训练此类泛函的策略，强调了分数轨道占据对于准确结果的重要性。我们最好的基于 QNN 的 XC 函数产生 H$_2$ 和平面 H$_4$ 分子的能量分布，其与参考 DMRG 和 FCI/6-31G 结果的偏差分别不超过 1 mHa。此外，他们仅使用几个变分参数就在训练数据集中不存在的系统 H$_2$H$_2$ 上达到了化学精度。这项工作为 KS-DFT 中量子模型的集成奠定了基础，从而为以可微分的方式表达 XC 泛函并促进各种属性的计算开辟了新途径。
