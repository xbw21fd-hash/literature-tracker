## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19721v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Advances in quantum technologies are accelerating the demand for optical quantum state sensors that combine high precision, versatility, and scalability within a unified hardware platform. Quantum reservoir computing offers a powerful route toward this goal by exploiting the nonlinear dynamics of quantum systems to process and interpret quantum information efficiently. Photonic neural networks are particularly well suited for such implementations, owing to their intrinsic sensitivity to photon-encoded quantum information. However, the practical realisation of photonic quantum reservoirs remains constrained by the inherently weak optical nonlinearities of available materials and the technological challenges of fabricating densely coupled quantum networks. To address these limitations, we introduce a hybrid quantum-classical detection protocol that integrates the advantages of quantum reservoirs with the adaptive learning capabilities of analogue neural networks. This synergistic architecture substantially enhances information-extraction accuracy and robustness, enabling low-cost performance improvements of quantum light sensors. Based on the proposed approach, we achieved significant improvements in quantum state classification, tomography, and feature regression, even for reservoirs with a relatively small nonlinearity-to-losses ratio $U/\gamma \approx 0.02$ in a network of only five nodes. By reducing reliance on material nonlinearity and reservoir size, the proposed approach facilitates the practical deployment of high-fidelity photonic quantum sensors on existing integrated platforms, paving the way toward chip-scale quantum processors and photonic sensing technologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19721v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：量子技术的进步正在加速对光学量子态传感器的需求，这些传感器在统一的硬件平台中结合了高精度、多功能性和可扩展性。量子库计算通过利用量子系统的非线性动力学来有效地处理和解释量子信息，为实现这一目标提供了一条强大的途径。光子神经网络特别适合这种实现，因为它们对光子编码的量子信息具有内在的敏感性。然而，光子量子库的实际实现仍然受到可用材料固有的弱光学非线性以及制造密集耦合量子网络的技术挑战的限制。为了解决这些限制，我们引入了一种混合量子经典检测协议，该协议将量子库的优势与模拟神经网络的自适应学习能力相结合。这种协同架构极大地提高了信息提取的准确性和鲁棒性，从而实现了量子光传感器的低成本性能改进。基于所提出的方法，我们在量子态分类、层析成像和特征回归方面取得了显着改进，即使对于只有五个节点的网络中非线性损失比 $U/\gamma \approx 0.02$ 相对较小的储层也是如此。通过减少对材料非线性和储层尺寸的依赖，所提出的方法促进了高保真光子量子传感器在现有集成平台上的实际部署，为芯片级量子处理器和光子传感技术铺平了道路。
