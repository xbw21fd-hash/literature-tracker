## Abstract
Nature Communications, Published online: 27 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41467-025-67884-1 Current superresolution imaging can beat the Rayleigh limit but struggles with complex imaging tasks. Here, the authors use a quantum-learning framework to address these challenges and introduce an improved method that enhances superresolution of nearby compact sources.
