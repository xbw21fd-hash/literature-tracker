## Abstract
TLDR: High-order Tensor message Passing interatomic Potential is proposed, an E(n) equivariant message passing neural network that extends the node embedding and message to an arbitrary order tensor and can make direct predictions of high order tensors such as dipole moments and polarizabilities without any modifications.
