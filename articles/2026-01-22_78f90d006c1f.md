## Abstract
arXiv:2507.08823v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Interferometric observations of the low-frequency radio sky ($<$ 1~GHz) are largely limited by systematic effects introduced by the ionosphere. In this study, we use a ten-hour nighttime observation of the bright radio source 3C48, carried out with the upgraded Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope (uGMRT), to examine how ionospheric conditions affect radio signals. We focus on measuring phase fluctuations caused by the ionosphere and analyse how these variations change with antenna separation using the spatial phase structure function. Our results, based on data from three sub-bands between 575 and 725~MHz, show a clear power-law trend consistent with turbulent behaviour. We introduce the diffractive scale as a scalar measure of ionospheric conditions, which could guide future calibration strategies. Furthermore, we find that the turbulence is anisotropic, with properties that vary by direction. The smallest diffractive scales do not always align with Earth's magnetic field, suggesting the influence of medium-scale Travelling Ionospheric Disturbances (MSTIDs). The contribution of these wave-like MSTIDs to the structure function follows a power-law with a slope close to 2.0, indicating their dominant role in shaping the observed anisotropic phase fluctuations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.08823v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：低频射电天空（$<$1~GHz）的干涉观测在很大程度上受到电离层引入的系统效应的限制。在这项研究中，我们使用升级后的巨型米波射电望远镜（uGMRT）对明亮的射电源 3C48 进行了十小时的夜间观测，以研究电离层条件如何影响无线电信号。我们专注于测量电离层引起的相位波动，并使用空间相位结构函数分析这些变化如何随天线分离而变化。我们的结果基于 575 至 725MHz 之间三个子带的数据，显示出与湍流行为一致的明显幂律趋势。我们引入衍射标度作为电离层条件的标量测量，这可以指导未来的校准策略。此外，我们发现湍流是各向异性的，其属性随方向而变化。最小的衍射尺度并不总是与地球磁场一致，这表明中等尺度的移动电离层扰动（MSTID）的影响。这些波状 MSTID 对结构函数的贡献遵循斜率接近 2.0 的幂律，表明它们在形成观测到的各向异性相位涨落中起主导作用。
