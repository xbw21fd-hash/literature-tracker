# U-TraCE: a conformal prediction approach to uncertainty quantification in black-box models

## Abstract
Modern machine learning (ML) systems, particularly deep neural networks, have become increasingly complex and opaque, making reliable uncertainty quantification (UQ) essential. Conventional UQ methods often prove inadequate for black-box model evaluation scenarios, either because model internals are not accessible or because the resulting uncertainty is expressed in terms of a related predictor rather than the model under evaluation. We introduce U-TraCE (Uncertainty Tracking for Complex Estimators), a novel UQ method built on the Conformal Prediction (CP) framework that provides formal and traceable uncertainty bounds for opaque ML systems. Unlike existing CP approaches, which describe uncertainty through confidence intervals or prediction set sizes at a fixed uncertainty level, U-TraCE yields an explicit numerical bound on the error probability of the standalone black-box model. U-TraCE was evaluated on diverse tasks, including MNIST classification and cardiac magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) segmentation, representing both benchmark and real-world domains. Across these settings, it produced conservative yet well-calibrated uncertainty bounds that closely follow empirical uncertainty, even with limited calibration data. By bridging theoretical UQ and operational model validation, U-TraCE establishes a foundation for objective and automated conformity assessment of complex ML systems, supporting trustworthy deployment in safety-critical environments.

## 摘要（中文）
现代机器学习 (ML) 系统，特别是深度神经网络，已经变得越来越复杂和不透明，因此可靠的不确定性量化 (UQ) 至关重要。传统的昆士兰大学方法通常不适用于黑盒模型评估场景，要么是因为模型内部无法访问，要么是因为由此产生的不确定性是用相关预测变量而不是评估中的模型来表达的。我们介绍了 U-TraCE（复杂估计器的不确定性跟踪），这是一种基于保形预测 (CP) 框架构建的新型 UQ 方法，为不透明的 ML 系统提供正式且可追踪的不确定性边界。现有的 CP 方法通过置信区间或固定不确定性水平下的预测集大小来描述不确定性，而 U-TraCE 与此不同，U-TraCE 对独立黑盒模型的错误概率产生了明确的数值界限。 U-TraCE 在多种任务上进行了评估，包括 MNIST 分类和心脏磁共振成像 (MRI) 分割，代表基准和现实世界领域。在这些设置中，它产生了保守但经过良好校准的不确定性界限，即使校准数据有限，也紧密遵循经验不确定性。通过连接理论 UQ 和操作模型验证，U-TraCE 为复杂 ML 系统的客观和自动化合格性评估奠定了基础，支持安全关键环境中的可信部署。
