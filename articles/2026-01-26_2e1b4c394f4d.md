## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16925v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Low Gain Avalanche Diodes (LGADs) are promising particle detectors for timing resolution better than $50$ ps under a high radiation environment. This study investigates n-in-p LGAD architecture, focusing on ultra-thin sensors of thickness less than $50\ \mu$m using the WeightField2 program. The capabilities of WeightField2 are demonstrated by comparing its results with irradiation measurements from an FBK LGAD wafer, showing good agreement across unirradiated and neutron-irradiated conditions. This paper presents device simulations in High Luminosity LHC conditions (lifetime integrated fluence $ \mathcal{O} (10^{14})\ \mathrm{n_{eq}~cm^{-2}}$, temperature $ \approx 243\ \mathrm{K} $), and taking into account radiation damage, gain reduction due to fluence, and lattice defects. It is shown that a 20 $\mu$m thick sensor achieves the best timing performance. Among Silicon (Si), Diamond (C), and 4H-Silicon Carbide (4H-SiC), we found 4H-SiC to be the most promising: it provides the highest gain value for a fixed thickness and gain implant layer configuration, and best retains high charge collection value and timing capability under increasing fluence up to $50\times10^{14}\ \mathrm{n_{eq}~cm^{-2}}$. A time resolution less than 25 ps is reported with different gain implant concentrations for a $20 \mu$m 4H-SiC sensor. This work presents the potential of SiC-based LGADs in high-radiation collider environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16925v1 公告类型：新 摘要：低增益雪崩二极管 (LGAD) 是有前途的粒子探测器，在高辐射环境下其定时分辨率优于 50 美元 ps。本研究研究 n-in-p LGAD 架构，重点关注使用 WeightField2 程序的厚度小于 50\ \mu$m 的超薄传感器。通过将其结果与 FBK LGAD 晶圆的辐照测量结果进行比较，证明了 WeightField2 的功能，在未辐照和中子辐照条件下显示出良好的一致性。本文介绍了高光度 LHC 条件下的器件模拟（寿命积分注量 $ \mathcal{O} (10^{14})\ \mathrm{n_{eq}~cm^{-2}}$，温度 $ \approx 243\ \mathrm{K} $），并考虑了辐射损伤、注量导致的增益降低和晶格缺陷。结果表明，20 $\mu$m 厚的传感器可实现最佳计时性能。在硅 (Si)、金刚石 (C) 和 4H-碳化硅 (4H-SiC) 中，我们发现 4H-SiC 是最有前途的：它为固定厚度和增益注入层配置提供最高的增益值，并且在能量密度增加至 $50\times10^{14}\ \mathrm{n_{eq}~cm^{-2}}$ 的情况下最好地保留高电荷收集值和定时能力。据报道，对于 20 µm 美元的 4H-SiC 传感器，不同增益注入浓度的时间分辨率小于 25 ps。这项工作展示了基于 SiC 的 LGAD 在高辐射对撞机环境中的潜力。
