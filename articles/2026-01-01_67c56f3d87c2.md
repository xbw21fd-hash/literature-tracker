## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24455v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Large-area deposition of Aluminium-Scandium-Nitride (Al1-xScxN) thin films with higher Sc content (x) remains challenging due to issues such as abnormal orientation growth, stress control, and the undesired crystal phase. These anomalies across the wafer hinder the development of high scandium-content AlScN films, which are critical for microelectromechanical systems applications. In this study, we report the sputter deposition of Al0.64Sc0.36N thin films from a 300 mm Al0.64Sc0.36 alloy target on 200 mm Si(100) wafers, achieving an exceptionally high deposition rate of 8.7 {\mu}m/h with less than 1% AOGs and controllable stress tuning. Comprehensive microstructural and electrical characterizations confirm the superior growth of high-quality Al0.64Sc0.36N films with exceptional wafer-average piezoelectric coefficients (d33,f =15.62 pm/V and e31,f = -2.9 C/m2) owing to low point defects density and grain mosaicity. This was accomplished through the implementation of an optimized seed layer and a refined electrode integration strategy, along with optimal process conditions. The wafer yield and device failure rates are analysed and correlated with the average stress of the films and their stress profiles along the diameter. The resulting films show excellent uniformity in structural, compositional, and piezoelectric properties across the entire 200 mm wafer, underscoring their strong potential for next-generation MEMS applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24455v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于异常取向生长、应力控制和不需要的晶相等问题，大面积沉积具有较高 Sc 含量 (x) 的铝钪氮化物 (Al1-xScxN) 薄膜仍然具有挑战性。晶圆上的这些异常现象阻碍了高钪含量 AlScN 薄膜的开发，而这对于微机电系统应用至关重要。在这项研究中，我们报道了在 200 mm Si(100) 晶圆上从 300 mm Al0.64Sc0.36 合金靶溅射沉积 Al0.64Sc0.36N 薄膜，实现了 8.7 {\mu}m/h 的极高沉积速率，AOG 小于 1%，并且应力可调。全面的微观结构和电气特性证实了高质量 Al0.64Sc0.36N 薄膜的优异生长，由于点缺陷密度和晶粒镶嵌性较低，因此具有出色的晶圆平均压电系数（d33,f = 15.62 pm/V 和 e31,f = -2.9 C/m2）。这是通过实施优化的种子层和精细的电极集成策略以及最佳的工艺条件来实现的。分析了晶圆良率和器件故障率，并将其与薄膜的平均应力及其沿直径的应力分布相关联。由此产生的薄膜在整个 200 mm 晶圆上表现出优异的结构、成分和压电特性均匀性，凸显了它们在下一代 MEMS 应用中的强大潜力。
