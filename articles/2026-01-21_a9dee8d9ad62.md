## Abstract
Cellular neural networks enable real-time, parallel computation for tasks such as high-speed image processing and solving partial differential equations. We found that low-power memristors are well suited for synaptic weights in such networks, even at extreme temperatures, allowing a single system-on-a-chip to be reconfigured for different tasks. von Neumann, J.
