## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12984v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Machine learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs) are routinely used to model diverse atomistic phenomena, yet parameterizing them to accurately capture solid-state phase transformations remains difficult. We present error metrics and data-generation schemes designed to streamline the parameterization of MLIPs for modeling precipitation in multi-component alloys. We developed an algorithm that enumerates symmetrically distinct transformation pathways connecting chemical decorations on different parent crystal structures. Additionally, we introduce the weighted Kendall-$\tau$ coefficient and its semi-grand canonical generalization as metrics for quantifying MLIP accuracy in predicting low-temperature thermodynamics. We apply these approaches to parameterize an MLIP for a dilute Mg-Nd alloy. The resulting potential reproduces the complex early-stage precipitation behavior observed in experiment. Large-scale atomistic simulations reveal competition between order-disorder and structural transformations. Furthermore, these results suggest a continuous transition between high-symmetry hcp and bcc crystal structures during aging heat treatments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12984v1 公告类型：新 摘要：机器学习原子间势（MLIP）通常用于模拟各种原子现象，但对其进行参数化以准确捕获固态相变仍然很困难。我们提出了误差度量和数据生成方案，旨在简化多组分合金中沉淀建模的 MLIP 参数化。我们开发了一种算法，可以枚举连接不同母体晶体结构上的化学装饰的对称不同的转化途径。此外，我们还引入了加权 Kendall-$\tau$ 系数及其半正则泛化，作为量化 MLIP 在预测低温热力学方面的准确性的指标。我们应用这些方法对稀 Mg-Nd 合金的 MLIP 进行参数化。由此产生的电势再现了实验中观察到的复杂的早期降水行为。大规模原子模拟揭示了有序-无序和结构转变之间的竞争。此外，这些结果表明在时效热处理过程中，高对称性 hcp 和 bcc 晶体结构之间会发生连续转变。
