## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02255v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The coherence of superconductivity and its suppression near a quantum phase transition is governed by the interplay between local pairing and macroscopic phase coherence. Using scanning SQUID, we image the local susceptibility in a hybrid Josephson junction array. On a square lattice of narrow islands, we simultaneously access both the amplitude and spatial phase structure of sensitive superconducting states. We observe periodic phase patterns at commensurate magnetic fillings. At zero field the long-range phase coherence is strongest. At a finite field, smaller than one percent of flux quantum per unit cell, the system fragments into large regions of constant superconducting phase, as a function of the applied field. Our results provide the first direct measurement of long-range phase coherence in a Josephson junction array.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02255v1 公告类型：新 摘要：量子相变附近的超导相干性及其抑制是由局部配对和宏观相干性之间的相互作用控制的。使用扫描 SQUID，我们对混合约瑟夫森结阵列中的局部磁化率进行成像。在窄岛的方格上，我们同时访问敏感超导态的振幅和空间相位结构。我们观察到相应磁性填充处的周期性相位模式。在零场处，远程相位相干性最强。在有限场（小于每晶胞通量量子的百分之一）下，系统会分裂成大片恒定超导相区域，作为所施加场的函数。我们的结果首次直接测量了约瑟夫森结阵列中的远程相位相干性。
