## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05889v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the low-temperature spin dynamics of epitaxial lithium aluminum ferrite (LAFO) thin films using broadband ferromagnetic resonance (FMR) spectroscopy from 0.44 K to 68 K. The results reveal a crossover from conventional cubic anisotropy-dominated behavior at higher temperatures to pronounced linewidth broadening and higher-order anisotropy contributions at cryogenic temperatures. With the magnetic field oriented along the [100] crystallographic direction, the resonance is well-captured by four-fold in-plane and out-of-plane uniaxial anisotropies. In contrast, measurements with the field along the [110] direction reveal the presence of an unusually large sixth-order cubic anisotropy term that is symmetry-suppressed for [100] but becomes apparent under this field orientation at ultralow temperatures, indicating a substantial modification of the anisotropy landscape. Independent linewidth analysis shows a pronounced peak near 8 K and a subtle monotonic enhancement with decreasing temperatures below 2 K, features consistent with dissipation mediated by a bath of two-level systems (TLS) arising from antisite defects and localized Fe$^{3+}$ moments. Comparison with TLS-based models demonstrates that both exchange-coupled impurities and nearly free paramagnetic centers contribute to the observed damping. Our results establish LAFO as a model ferrite system where disorder-induced TLS limit spin coherence at ultralow temperatures, providing new insights into anisotropy engineering, magnetic relaxation, and the design of ferrimagnetic insulators for coherent magnonics. These findings offer a framework for future optimization of growth conditions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05889v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用 0.44 K 至 68 K 的宽带铁磁共振 (FMR) 光谱研究了外延锂铝铁氧体 (LAFO) 薄膜的低温自旋动力学。结果揭示了从较高温度下传统的立方各向异性主导行为到低温下显着的线宽展宽和高阶各向异性贡献的交叉。当磁场沿 [100] 晶体方向取向时，四倍面内和面外单轴各向异性可以很好地捕获共振。相比之下，沿[110]方向的场测量揭示了异常大的六阶立方各向异性项的存在，该项对于[100]是对称性抑制的，但在超低温下在该场方向下变得明显，表明各向异性景观发生了实质性改变。独立线宽分析显示在 8 K 附近有一个明显的峰值，并且随着温度降低到 2 K 以下，出现了微妙的单调增强，这些特征与由反位缺陷和局部 Fe$^{3+}$ 矩引起的两能级系统 (TLS) 浴介导的耗散一致。与基于 TLS 的模型的比较表明，交换耦合杂质和几乎自由的顺磁中心都有助于观察到的阻尼。我们的研究结果将 LAFO 确立为一种模型铁氧体系统，其中无序诱导的 TLS 在超低温下限制自旋相干性，为各向异性工程、磁弛豫和相干磁子学的亚铁磁绝缘体设计提供了新的见解。这些发现为未来优化生长条件提供了框架。
