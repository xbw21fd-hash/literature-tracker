## Abstract
arXiv:2505.14849v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Rare earth/transition metal (RE/TM) multilayers with perpendicular magnetic anisotropy are key ingredients for the development of spintronic applications. Their compensation temperature depends on the ratio of the thicknesses of rare earth and transition metal, allowing their magnetic properties to be tuned with temperature while maintaining their anisotropy even in nanometer-scale devices. In this work, we performed a thorough structural characterization and systematically investigate the magnetic properties of a whole family of ferrimagnetic [Tb/Co]$_{\times 5}$ multilayers varying the Tb thickness in the range of 0.4 nm - 1.25 nm. A linear dependence of the compensation temperature on the Tb layer thickness was observed. Moreover, a uniaxial anisotropy constant of 330$\pm$30 kJ/m$^3$, which is close to the values reported by other authors, was estimated. Additionally, we proposed a model to gain a better understanding of the angular dependence of the magnetization loops and the linear dependence of the compensation temperature. We present strong evidence demonstrating that the perpendicular anisotropy must be tilted away from the perpendicular axis in order to explain the observed features, particularly the hysteresis in the in-plane loops. Our work advances the understanding of DC magnetic properties in thin RE/TM ferrimagnetic films, which has the potential to impact different fields where these materials are involved.

arXiv:2505.14849v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：具有垂直磁各向异性的稀土/过渡金属 (RE/TM) 多层材料是自旋电子应用开发的关键成分。它们的补偿温度取决于稀土和过渡金属的厚度之比，使得它们的磁性能可以随温度调节，同时即使在纳米级器件中也能保持其各向异性。在这项工作中，我们进行了彻底的结构表征，并系统地研究了整个亚铁磁性 [Tb/Co]$_{\times 5}$ 多层的磁性，其中 Tb 厚度在 0.4 nm - 1.25 nm 范围内变化。观察到补偿温度对 Tb 层厚度的线性依赖性。此外，估计单轴各向异性常数为 330$\pm$30 kJ/m$^3$，接近其他作者报告的值。此外，我们提出了一个模型，以更好地理解磁化环的角度依赖性和补偿温度的线性依赖性。我们提出了强有力的证据，证明垂直各向异性必须远离垂直轴倾斜，才能解释观察到的特征，特别是面内环路中的滞后现象。我们的工作增进了对 RE/TM 亚铁磁薄膜中直流磁特性的理解，这有可能影响这些材料所涉及的不同领域。
