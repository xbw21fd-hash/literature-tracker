## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05767v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Physical reservoir computing is a computational framework that offers an energy- and computation-efficient alternative to conventional training of neural networks. In reservoir computing, input signals are mapped into the high-dimensional dynamics of a nonlinear system, and only a simple readout layer is trained. In most physical implementations, the interactions that give rise to the dynamics cannot be tuned directly and high dimensionality is typically achieved through time-multiplexing, which can limit flexibility and efficiency. Here we introduce a reservoir composed of hundreds of hydrodynamically coupled active colloidal oscillators forming a fully parallel physical reservoir and whose coupling strength and fading-memory time can be tuned in situ. The collective dynamics of the active oscillators allow accurate predictions of chaotic time series from single reservoir readouts without time-multiplexing. We further demonstrate real-time detection of subtle hidden anomalies that preserve all instantaneous statistical properties of the signal yet disrupt its underlying temporal correlations. These results establish interacting active colloids as a reconfigurable platform for physical computation and edge-integrated intelligent sensing for model-free detection of irregularities in complex time signals.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05767v1 公告类型：新 摘要：物理储层计算是一种计算框架，为传统的神经网络训练提供了一种节能和计算高效的替代方案。在储层计算中，输入信号被映射到非线性系统的高维动力学中，并且仅训练简单的读出层。在大多数物理实现中，产生动态的相互作用无法直接调整，高维通常通过时分复用来实现，这会限制灵活性和效率。在这里，我们介绍了一种由数百个流体动力耦合的主动胶体振荡器组成的储层，形成一个完全并行的物理储层，其耦合强度和衰落记忆时间可以就地调节。有源振荡器的集体动力学允许从单个储层读数准确预测混沌时间序列，而无需时间复用。我们进一步演示了对微妙隐藏异常的实时检测，这些异常保留了信号的所有瞬时统计特性，但破坏了其潜在的时间相关性。这些结果将相互作用的活性胶体建立为物理计算和边缘集成智能传感的可重构平台，用于复杂时间信号中不规则性的无模型检测。
