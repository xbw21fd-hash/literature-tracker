## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04543v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Using quantum key distribution (QKD) protocols, a secret key is created between two distant users (transmitter and receiver) at a particular key rate. Quantum technology can facilitate secure communication for cryptographic applications, combining QKD with one-time-pad (OTP) encryption. In order to ensure the continuous operation of QKD in real-world networks, efforts have been concentrated on optimizing the use of components and effective QKD protocols to improve secret key rates and increase the transmission between multiple users. Generally, in experimental implementations, the secret key rates are limited by single-photon detectors, which are used at the receivers of QKD and create a bottleneck due to their limited detection rates (detectors with low detection efficiency and high detector dead-time). We experimentally show that secret key rates can be increased by combining the time-bin information of two such detectors on the data line of the receiver for the coherent-one-way (COW) QKD protocol with a minimal increase in quantum bit error rate (QBER, the proportion of erroneous bits). Further, we implement a point-to-multipoint COW QKD protocol, introducing an additional receiver module. The three users (one transmitter and two receivers) share the secret key in post-processing, relying on OTP encryption. Typically, the dual-receiver extension can improve the combined secret key rates of the system; however, one has to optimise the experimental parameters to achieve this within security margins. These methods are general and can be applied to any implementation of the COW protocol.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04543v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：使用量子密钥分发 (QKD) 协议，在两个远程用户（发送器和接收器）之间以特定密钥速率创建密钥。量子技术将 QKD 与一次性一密本 (OTP) 加密相结合，可以促进加密应用程序的安全通信。为了确保 QKD 在现实网络中的持续运行，我们一直致力于优化组件的使用和有效的 QKD 协议，以提高密钥率并增加多个用户之间的传输。一般来说，在实验实现中，密钥率受到单光子检测器的限制，单光子检测器用于 QKD 的接收器，并由于其有限的检测率（检测器检测效率低和检测器死区时间长）而产生瓶颈。我们通过实验证明，通过将相干单向 (COW) QKD 协议的接收器数据线上的两个此类检测器的时间仓信息相结合，可以提高密钥速率，同时量子比特错误率 (QBER，错误比特的比例) 的增加最小。此外，我们实现了点对多点 COW QKD 协议，引入了额外的接收器模块。三个用户（一个发送器和两个接收器）在后处理中共享密钥，依赖于 OTP 加密。通常，双接收扩展可以提高系统的组合密钥率；然而，必须优化实验参数才能在安全范围内实现这一目标。这些方法是通用的，可以应用于 COW 协议的任何实现。
