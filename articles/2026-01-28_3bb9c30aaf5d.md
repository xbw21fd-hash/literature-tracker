## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19598v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Established theoretical studies of diffusion in rugged (or rough) potential surfaces have largely focused on quenched energy landscapes. Here we study diffusion on a rugged energy landscape in the presence of dynamic disorder, a situation relevant to a wide range of disordered systems, including glasses, disordered solids, and biomolecular transport. For static (quenched) Gaussian disorder, Zwanzig derived a compact mean field expression for the diffusion constant, showing that increasing ruggedness leads to a sharp reduction of diffusive transport. Subsequent work demonstrated that in one-dimensional discrete lattices diffusion is further suppressed by rare but long-lived multi-site traps that lie beyond the mean-field description. In many physical systems, however, the local energy landscape is not frozen but fluctuates in time, there by modifying trap lifetimes and transport properties. In this work we develop a minimal, analytically tractable theory of diffusion on rugged energy landscapes with dynamic disorder by allowing site or barrier energies to fluctuate as dichotomic (telegraph) processes with given amplitude and flipping rate. Using a Kehr type formulation appropriate for discrete hopping processes, we derive an analytic expression for the diffusion constant in terms of mean waiting times. We show that dynamic disorder induces a continuous crossover from quasi-quenched, trap-dominated transport to an annealed, motional narrowing regime as the fluctuation rate increases. Explicit numerical calculations confirm this crossover, interpolating between rare-event-dominated diffusion and Zwanzig mean-field regime.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19598v1 公告类型：新 摘要：已建立的关于崎岖（或粗糙）电势表面扩散的理论研究主要集中在淬灭的能量景观上。在这里，我们研究在存在动态无序的情况下崎岖的能量景观上的扩散，这种情况与各种无序系统相关，包括玻璃、无序固体和生物分子传输。对于静态（淬火）高斯无序，Zwanzig 导出了扩散常数的紧凑平均场表达式，表明增加的粗糙度会导致扩散传输的急剧减少。随后的工作表明，在一维离散晶格中，扩散被罕见但长期存在的多位点陷阱进一步抑制，这些陷阱超出了平均场描述。然而，在许多物理系统中，局部能量景观并未冻结，而是随时间波动，从而改变陷阱寿命和传输特性。在这项工作中，我们通过允许位点或势垒能量以给定振幅和翻转率的二分（电报）过程的形式波动，开发了一种最小的、分析上易于处理的关于具有动态无序的崎岖能量景观的扩散理论。使用适用于离散跳跃过程的 Kehr 型公式，我们推导了以平均等待时间表示的扩散常数的解析表达式。我们表明，随着波动率的增加，动态无序会导致从准淬火、陷阱主导的传输到退火的运动狭窄状态的连续交叉。显式数值计算证实了这种交叉，在稀有事件主导的扩散和 Zwanzig 平均场状态之间进行插值。
