## Abstract
Understanding how charge distributions on aggregated chains change with microstructure under constant electrochemical potential is crucial for elucidating the behavior of polymeric organic mixed ionic–electronic conductors (OMEICs), yet it remains difficult to study. To address this challenge, we introduce a methodology to perform classical atomistic simulations of doped semiconductors at constant electrochemical potential. The method allows individual polymer chains to be oxidized and reduced, taking into account their individual redox potential and the externally tunable electrochemical potential. The implementation follows a grand-canonical molecular dynamics (GC-MD) scheme with the local modulation of the redox potential being described by a QM/MM Hamiltonian. Applied to a semicrystalline polymer with ordered layered and lamellar structures, the method reproduces the experimentally observed minimal structural changes over the electrochemical potentials and charging levels considered. Near the redox potential, charging levels fluctuate more strongly, and variations in the inter-lamellar angle (defined by the normal of adjacent lamellae) are most pronounced. Moreover, analysis of the local environment reveals no detectable correlation between a chain’s redox reaction and the charge distribution of neighboring chains, except at the most negative potentials, where redox events occur preferentially in more positively charged surroundings. Lastly, examination of individual chains shows minimal chain-chain charge correlation, and the single-chain conformation remains closely linked to its redox behavior. Overall, this work provides a robust framework for investigating charge distributions in dynamically doped systems and offers new conceptual routes for studying polymer structural responses under constant electrochemical potentials.

## 摘要（中文）
了解在恒定电化学势下聚集链上的电荷分布如何随微观结构变化对于阐明聚合物有机混合离子电子导体（OMEIC）的行为至关重要，但研究起来仍然很困难。为了应对这一挑战，我们引入了一种在恒定电化学势下对掺杂半导体进行经典原子模拟的方法。该方法允许单个聚合物链被氧化和还原，同时考虑到它们各自的氧化还原电位和外部可调的电化学电位。该实现遵循大规范分子动力学 (GC-MD) 方案，其中氧化还原电位的局部调制由 QM/MM 哈密顿量描述。该方法应用于具有有序层状和层状结构的半结晶聚合物，在所考虑的电化学势和充电水平上再现了实验观察到的最小结构变化。在氧化还原电位附近，充电水平波动更强烈，并且层间角度（由相邻层的法线定义）的变化最明显。此外，对局部环境的分析表明，链的氧化还原反应与相邻链的电荷分布之间没有可检测到的相关性，除非在最负电势下，氧化还原事件优先发生在带正电的环境中。最后，对各个链的检查显示出最小的链链电荷相关性，并且单链构象仍然与其氧化还原行为密切相关。总的来说，这项工作为研究动态掺杂系统中的电荷分布提供了一个强大的框架，并为研究恒定电化学势下的聚合物结构响应提供了新的概念途径。
