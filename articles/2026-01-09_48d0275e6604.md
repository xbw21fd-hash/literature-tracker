# Magnetization generation and giant nonlinear transport at symmetry-engineered interfaces

## Abstract
Interfaces in heterostructures possess inherent inversion asymmetry and display diverse physical effects, however, the pristine in-plane mirror symmetries of the constituent layers are usually preserved at the interface. On-demand manipulation of these symmetries remains challenging. Here, we demonstrate a strategy to control the in-plane mirror symmetries of interfaces by engineering the crystallographic orientation of heterostructures. We design a workhorse system with a new orientation, i.e., the LaAlO3/SrTiO3heterostructure with metallic interfaces in the (112)-plane. Such a high index orientation leads to the breaking of all the pristine mirror symmetries except the mirror plane perpendicular to the\([1\bar{1}0]\)direction (\({M}_{[1\bar{1}0]}\)), resulting in theCspoint symmetry with a metallic conduction. Consequently, this interface exhibits a giant nonlinear Hall effect characterized by a large Berry curvature dipole, a circular photogalvanic effect, and current-induced out-of-plane magnetization, all functional at room temperature. The magnitude of the nonlinear Hall effect rivals the Weyl and Dirac systems. Our work establishes a new strategy in exploring emerging electronic properties with nontrivial quantum geometry by designing the interface symmetry.

## 摘要（中文）
异质结构中的界面具有固有的反演不对称性并表现出不同的物理效应，然而，组成层的原始面内镜面对称性通常保留在界面处。按需操纵这些对称性仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们展示了一种通过设计异质结构的晶体取向来控制界面的面内镜对称性的策略。我们设计了一种具有新取向的主力系统，即在 (112) 平面具有金属界面的 LaAlO3/SrTiO3 异质结构。如此高的折射率取向导致除垂直于\([1\bar{1}0]\)方向(\({M}_{[1\bar{1}0]}\))的镜面之外的所有原始镜面对称性被破坏，从而产生具有金属传导的Cs点对称性。因此，该界面表现出巨大的非线性霍尔效应，其特征是大贝里曲率偶极子、圆形光电效应和电流感应的面外磁化，所有这些都在室温下起作用。非线性霍尔效应的强度可与韦尔和狄拉克系统相媲美。我们的工作建立了一种新策略，通过设计界面对称性来探索具有非平凡量子几何的新兴电子特性。
