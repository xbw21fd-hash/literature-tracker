## Abstract
arXiv:2502.13185v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Accurate and efficient climate simulations are crucial for understanding Earth's evolving climate. However, current general circulation models (GCMs) face challenges in capturing unresolved physical processes, such as cloud and convection. A common solution is to adopt cloud resolving models, that provide more accurate results than the standard subgrid parametrisation schemes typically used in GCMs. However, cloud resolving models, also referred to as super paramtetrizations, remain computationally prohibitive. Hybrid modeling, which integrates deep learning with equation-based GCMs, offers a promising alternative but often struggles with long-term stability and accuracy issues. In this work, we find that water vapor oversaturation during condensation is a key factor compromising the stability of hybrid models. To address this, we introduce CondensNet, a novel neural network architecture that embeds a self-adaptive physical constraint to correct unphysical condensation processes. CondensNet effectively mitigates water vapor oversaturation, enhancing simulation stability while maintaining accuracy and improving computational efficiency compared to super parameterization schemes. We integrate CondensNet into a GCM to form PCNN-GCM (Physics-Constrained Neural Network GCM), a hybrid deep learning framework designed for long-term stable climate simulations in real-world conditions, including ocean and land. PCNN-GCM represents a significant milestone in hybrid climate modeling, as it shows a novel way to incorporate physical constraints adaptively, paving the way for accurate, lightweight, and stable long-term climate simulations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2502.13185v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：准确高效的气候模拟对于了解地球不断变化的气候至关重要。然而，当前的大气环流模型（GCM）在捕获未解决的物理过程（例如云和对流）方面面临挑战。一种常见的解决方案是采用云解析模型，该模型比 GCM 中通常使用的标准子网格参数化方案提供更准确的结果。然而，云解析模型（也称为超级参数化）在计算上仍然令人望而却步。混合建模将深度学习与基于方程的 GCM 相结合，提供了一种有前途的替代方案，但常常面临长期稳定性和准确性问题。在这项工作中，我们发现冷凝过程中的水蒸气过饱和是影响混合模型稳定性的关键因素。为了解决这个问题，我们引入了 CondensNet，这是一种新颖的神经网络架构，它嵌入了自适应物理约束来纠正非物理凝结过程。与超参数化方案相比，CondensNet 有效缓解水蒸气过饱和，增强模拟稳定性，同时保持精度并提高计算效率。我们将 CondensNet 集成到 GCM 中，形成 PCNN-GCM（物理约束神经网络 GCM），这是一种混合深度学习框架，专为现实世界条件（包括海洋和陆地）的长期稳定气候模拟而设计。 PCNN-GCM 代表了混合气候建模的一个重要里程碑，因为它展示了一种自适应地合并物理约束的新方法，为准确、轻量级和稳定的长期气候模拟铺平了道路。
