## Abstract
We report a new strategy for coupling magnetic and electric dipoles within a single nanoparticle by exploiting structural and chemical anisotropy. The approach is based on ferrimagnetic barium hexaferrite nanoplatelets (NPLs) selectively hybridized on only one basal plane with organic polar ligands bearing a large electric dipole and a phosphonic anchoring group. The resulting Janus magneto-electric hybrids exhibit magnetic and electric dipoles normal to the basal plane of the NPLs. To experimentally realize these hybrids, we developed a surface-selective hybridization protocol at the solid–liquid interface. Each processing step—including substrate surface optimization, magnetically-directed monolayer assembly of NPLs, surface-selective hybridization, and particle harvesting—was systematically investigated. In parallel, we established an optical method to probe and verify the simultaneous response of the hybrid NPL dispersions to external electric and magnetic fields employing a unique transport mechanism, a multidipole electrophoresis in a magnetic field (MEMF). Field-induced birefringence changes were also measured, demonstrating coupled magneto-electric and optical responses. These findings identify Janus magneto-electric NPLs as a versatile materials platform for contactless optical sensing, including external-field sensors, as well as for photonic devices enabling advanced light manipulation.

## 摘要（中文）
我们报告了一种通过利用结构和化学各向异性在单个纳米颗粒内耦合磁偶极子和电偶极子的新策略。该方法基于亚铁磁性钡六铁氧体纳米片（NPL），仅在一个基面上与带有大电偶极子和膦锚定基团的有机极性配体选择性杂交。由此产生的 Janus 磁电混合体表现出垂直于 NPL 基面的磁偶极子和电偶极子。为了通过实验实现这些杂化，我们开发了固液界面的表面选择性杂化方案。每个处理步骤——包括基板表面优化、NPL 的磁定向单层组装、表面选择性杂交和颗粒收获——都经过系统研究。与此同时，我们建立了一种光学方法来探测和验证混合 NPL 分散体对外部电场和磁场的同时响应，采用独特的传输机制，即磁场中的多偶极电泳 (MEMF)。还测量了场引起的双折射变化，展示了耦合的磁电和光学响应。这些发现表明，Janus 磁电 NPL 是一种多功能材料平台，适用于非接触式光学传感（包括外场传感器）以及用于实现高级光操纵的光子器件。
