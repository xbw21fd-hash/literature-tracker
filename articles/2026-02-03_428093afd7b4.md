## Abstract
arXiv:2505.08675v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Altermagnetism is an unconventional form of antiferromagnetism characterized by momentum-dependent spin polarization of electronic states and zero net magnetization, arising from specific crystalline symmetries. In the presence of spin-orbit coupling (SOC) and broken time-reversal symmetry, altermagnets can exhibit finite net magnetization and anomalous Hall effect (AHE), phenomena typically associated with ferromagnets. Due to the dependence of AHE on magnetization, understanding the interplay between spin and orbital contributions to magnetization is essential for interpreting experiments and designing altermagnetic devices. In this work, we use density functional theory to investigate the intrinsic spin and orbital magnetization of the magnetic ground state of the prototypical altermagnet {\alpha}-MnTe. We find that SOC induces weak ferromagnetism through spin canting, accompanied by a slight in-plane rotation of the N\'eel vector. Notably, we identify a significant net orbital magnetization of 0.176 {\mu}B per unit cell oriented along the z-axis, while the spin magnetization in the same direction is much smaller at 0.002 {\mu}B. By varying the chemical potential, we show that the spin magnetization is tunable through hole doping, whereas the orbital magnetization remains robust against carrier density changes. These results highlight the important role of orbital magnetization and establish its relevance for orbital-based phenomena in altermagnets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.08675v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：交变磁性是一种非常规形式的反铁磁性，其特征是电子态的动量相关自旋极化和零净磁化强度，源自特定的晶体对称性。在存在自旋轨道耦合 (SOC) 和打破时间反转对称性的情况下，交变磁体会表现出有限的净磁化强度和反常霍尔效应 (AHE)，这些现象通常与铁磁体相关。由于 AHE 对磁化强度的依赖性，了解自旋和轨道对磁化强度的贡献之间的相互作用对于解释实验和设计交流磁器件至关重要。在这项工作中，我们使用密度泛函理论来研究原型交流磁体 {\alpha}-MnTe 的磁性基态的本征自旋和轨道磁化强度。我们发现 SOC 通过自旋倾斜引起弱铁磁性，并伴随着 N'eel 矢量的轻微面内旋转。值得注意的是，我们发现沿 z 轴取向的每个晶胞的净轨道磁化强度为 0.176 {\mu}B，而同一方向的自旋磁化强度要小得多，为 0.002 {\mu}B。通过改变化学势，我们表明自旋磁化强度可以通过空穴掺杂进行调节，而轨道磁化强度对于载流子密度的变化仍然保持稳健。这些结果强调了轨道磁化的重要作用，并确立了其与交流磁体中基于轨道的现象的相关性。
