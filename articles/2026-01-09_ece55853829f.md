## Abstract
arXiv:2501.02505v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Ranking items based on pairwise comparisons is common, from using match outcomes to rank sports teams to using purchase or survey data to rank consumer products. Statistical inference-based methods such as the Bradley-Terry model, which extract rankings based on an underlying generative model, have emerged as flexible and powerful tools to tackle ranking in empirical data. In situations with limited and/or noisy comparisons, it is often challenging to confidently distinguish the performance of different items based on the evidence available in the data. However, most inference-based ranking methods choose to assign each item to a unique rank or score, suggesting a meaningful distinction when there is none. Here, we develop a principled nonparametric Bayesian method, adaptable to any statistical ranking method, for learning partial rankings (rankings with ties) that distinguishes among the ranks of different items only when there is sufficient evidence available in the data. We develop a fast agglomerative algorithm to perform Maximum A Posteriori (MAP) inference of partial rankings under our framework and examine the performance of our method on a variety of real and synthetic network datasets, finding that it frequently gives a more parsimonious summary of the data than traditional ranking, particularly when observations are sparse.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2501.02505v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：基于成对比较对项目进行排名很常见，从使用比赛结果对运动队进行排名到使用购买或调查数据对消费品进行排名。基于统计推断的方法（例如 Bradley-Terry 模型）基于底层生成模型提取排名，已成为解决经验数据排名问题的灵活而强大的工具。在比较有限和/或嘈杂的情况下，根据数据中可用的证据自信地区分不同项目的性能通常具有挑战性。然而，大多数基于推理的排名方法选择为每个项目分配一个唯一的排名或分数，这表明在没有区别时存在有意义的区别。在这里，我们开发了一种原则性的非参数贝叶斯方法，适用于任何统计排名方法，用于学习部分排名（有联系的排名），仅当数据中有足够的证据时才能区分不同项目的排名。我们开发了一种快速凝聚算法，在我们的框架下执行部分排名的最大后验（MAP）推理，并检查我们的方法在各种真实和合成网络数据集上的性能，发现它经常比传统排名提供更简洁的数据摘要，特别是当观察稀疏时。
