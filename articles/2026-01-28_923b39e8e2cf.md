## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18817v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: This study presents a hybrid reduced-order modeling (ROM) framework for turbulent incompressible flows on collocated finite volume grids. The methodology employs the "discretize-then-project" consistent flux strategy, which ensures mass conservation and pressure-velocity coupling without requiring auxiliary stabilization like boundary control or pressure stabilization techniques. However, because standard Galerkin projection fails to yield physically consistent results for the turbulent viscosity field, a hybrid strategy is adopted: velocity and pressure are resolved via intrusive projection, while the turbulent viscosity is reconstructed using a non-intrusive data-driven closure. We evaluate three neural network architectures, Multilayer Perceptron (MLP), Transformers, and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM), to model the temporal evolution of the viscosity coefficients. Validated against a 3D Large Eddy Simulation of a lid-driven cavity, the LSTM-based closure demonstrates superior performance in capturing transient dynamics, achieving relative errors of 0.7\% for velocity and 4\% for turbulent viscosity. The resulting framework effectively combines the mathematical rigor of the consistent flux formulation with the adaptability of deep learning for turbulence modeling.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18817v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：本研究提出了一种混合降阶建模 (ROM) 框架，适用于并置有限体积网格上的湍流不可压缩流。该方法采用“离散然后投影”一致通量策略，确保质量守恒和压力-速度耦合，而不需要边界控制或压力稳定技术等辅助稳定。然而，由于标准伽辽金投影无法为湍流粘度场产生物理上一致的结果，因此采用了混合策略：通过侵入式投影求解速度和压力，同时使用非侵入式数据驱动闭包来重建湍流粘度。我们评估了三种神经网络架构：多层感知器 (MLP)、变压器和长短期记忆 (LSTM)，以模拟粘度系数的时间演化。经过盖驱动腔的 3D 大涡模拟验证，基于 LSTM 的闭合在捕获瞬态动力学方面表现出卓越的性能，速度相对误差为 0.7%，湍流粘度相对误差为 4%。由此产生的框架有效地将一致通量公式的数学严谨性与深度学习对湍流建模的适应性结合起来。
