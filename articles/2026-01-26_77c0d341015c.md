## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16633v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Exploiting current-induced spin-orbit torques (SOTs) to manipulate the magnetic state of dipolar-coupled nanomagnet systems with in-plane magnetic anisotropy, such as artificial spin ices, provides a route to local, electrically-programmable control of the magnetization, with relevance for applications including neuromorphic computing. Here, we demonstrate how the orientation of a nanomagnet relative to the direction of an applied electrical current impacts the threshold current density needed for all-electrical magnetization switching, and how dipolar coupling between the nanomagnets influences the switching of interacting pairs and ensembles of nanomagnets. Using a material system designed to generate SOTs in response to electrical currents, we find that the current required to switch the magnetization of isolated nanomagnets varies non-monotonically as the angle between the nanomagnet long axis and the current increases. In small artificial spin ice systems, we observe similar angular dependence of the switching current, which can be used to control the magnetization orientation of specific subsets of nanomagnets. These experimental results are supported by micromagnetic modeling, which illustrates how the various current induced torques can be exploited to control magnetization switching in nanomagnetic systems. These results establish SOT switching as a practical method for programmable manipulation of dipolar nanomagnetic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16633v1 公告类型：新 摘要：利用电流感应自旋轨道扭矩 (SOT) 来操纵具有面内磁各向异性的偶极耦合纳米磁体系统（例如人造自旋冰）的磁状态，为本地、电可编程磁化控制提供了一条途径，与包括神经形态计算在内的应用相关。在这里，我们演示了纳米磁体相对于施加电流方向的方向如何影响全电磁体磁化切换所需的阈值电流密度，以及纳米磁体之间的偶极耦合如何影响纳米磁体相互作用对和整体的切换。使用设计用于响应电流生成 SOT 的材料系统，我们发现，随着纳米磁体长轴与电流之间角度的增加，切换隔离纳米磁体磁化强度所需的电流会非单调变化。在小型人造自旋冰系统中，我们观察到开关电流的类似角度依赖性，这可用于控制纳米磁体特定子集的磁化方向。这些实验结果得到了微磁模型的支持，该模型说明了如何利用各种电流感应扭矩来控制纳米磁系统中的磁化切换。这些结果确立了 SOT 开关作为偶极纳米磁性系统可编程操纵的实用方法。
