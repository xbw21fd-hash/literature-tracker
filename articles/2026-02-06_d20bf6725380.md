## Abstract
Rotating magnon wave packets carrying orbital moments offer a pathway to unconventional transport phenomena. Here, we investigate magnon orbital moments and the magnon orbital Nernst effect in the prototypical altermagnets RuO2and CrSb using first-principles calculations, linear response theory, and symmetry analysis. While symmetry constraints enforce vanishing equilibrium magnon orbital moments, we find that in thermal non-equilibrium a finite and robust magnon orbital Nernst effect emerges from the anisotropic Heisenberg exchange, regardless of spin-orbit coupling. This effect is intrinsically tied to the unique exchange splitting of magnon dispersions in altermagnets and is absent in conventional antiferromagnets. Magnon orbital moment transport displays markedly reduced sensitivity to the orientation of the Néel vector, temperature gradient, and magnetic domain structure compared to the magnon spin Seebeck and spin Nernst effects, enabling its persistence even in polycrystalline samples with arbitrary domain configurations. Our results position magnon orbital transport as a promising and robust functional mechanism for orbitronic and spintronic devices, and as a potential indirect probe of altermagnetism in disordered insulating systems.

## 摘要（中文）
携带轨道矩的旋转磁振子波包为非常规输运现象提供了一条途径。在这里，我们使用第一性原理计算、线性响应理论和对称分析研究了原型交替磁体 RuO2 和 CrSb 中的磁振子轨道矩和磁振子轨道能斯特效应。虽然对称约束强制消失平衡磁振子轨道矩，但我们发现在热非平衡中，无论自旋轨道耦合如何，各向异性海森堡交换都会出现有限且鲁棒的磁振子轨道能斯特效应。这种效应本质上与交流磁体中磁振子色散的独特交换分裂有关，并且在传统反铁磁体中不存在。与磁振子自旋塞贝克和自旋能斯特效应相比，磁振子轨道矩输运对尼尔矢量方向、温度梯度和磁域结构的敏感性显着降低，即使在具有任意域配置的多晶样品中也能保持其持久性。我们的结果将磁振子轨道输运定位为轨道电子和自旋电子器件的一种有前途且强大的功能机制，并且作为无序绝缘系统中交变磁性的潜在间接探针。
