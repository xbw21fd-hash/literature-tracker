## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21044v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Superconducting materials are of significant technological relevance for a broad range of applications, and intense research efforts aim at enhancing the critical temperature $T_{c}$. Intriguingly, while numerous studies have explored different computational and machine-learning routes to predict $T_{c}$, the fundamental role of the critical magnetic field has so far been overlooked. Here we open a new frontier in superconductor discovery by presenting a consistent computational database of critical fields $H_{c}$, $H_{c1}$, and $H_{c2}$ for over 7300 electron-phonon-paired superconductors covering distinct materials classes. A theoretical framework is developed that combines $\alpha^2F(\omega)$ spectral functions and highly accurate Fermi surfaces from density functional theory with clean-limit Eliashberg theory to obtain the coherence lengths, London penetration depths, and Ginzburg-Landau parameters. We discover an unexpectedly large number of Type-I superconductors and show that larger unit cells generically support higher critical fields and Type-II behavior. We identify the importance of going beyond BCS theory by including strong-coupling corrections to the superconducting gap and electron-phonon renormalizations of the effective mass for predictions of critical fields across materials. These results provide a framework for foundational AI models that realize the concept of inverse materials design for high-$T_{c}$ and high-critical-field superconductors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21044v1 公告类型：新 摘要：超导材料对于广泛的应用具有重要的技术相关性，大量的研究工作旨在提高临界温度 $T_{c}$。有趣的是，虽然许多研究探索了不同的计算和机器学习途径来预测 $T_{c}$，但临界磁场的基本作用迄今为止一直被忽视。在这里，我们通过提供涵盖不同材料类别的超过 7300 个电子声子对超导体的关键场 $H_{c}$、$H_{c1}$ 和 $H_{c2}$ 的一致计算数据库，开辟了超导体发现的新前沿。开发了一个理论框架，将密度泛函理论中的$\alpha^2F(\omega)$谱函数和高精度费米面与干净极限Eliashberg理论相结合，以获得相干长度、伦敦穿透深度和Ginzburg-Landau参数。我们发现了数量出乎意料的大量 I 型超导体，并表明较大的晶胞通常支持更高的临界场和 II 型行为。我们确定了超越 BCS 理论的重要性，包括对超导能隙的强耦合校正和有效质量的电子声子重整化，以预测跨材料的临界场。这些结果为基础人工智能模型提供了一个框架，实现了高$T_{c}$和高临界场超导体的逆材料设计概念。
