## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16310v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The photovoltaic solar cell is a mature technology, with silicon-based technologies deployed at scale, yet current technologies are limited by the Shockley-Queisser thermodynamic limit, known since the early 1960s. The single-junction non-concentrator hot carrier solar cell operating at ambient temperature - with its theoretically predicted ultimate power conversion efficiency limit of nearly 70% that is twice the Shockley-Queisser limit and is higher than what can be achieved with even n=6 multijunction solar cells - has remained an elusive yet hot research target since the early 1980s. Metal halide perovskite semiconductors were discovered in the late 1970s and photovoltaic applications have been intensively researched and developed since the early 2010s. Current technology development of perovskite solar cells is heavily motivated by their expected cheap processing costs relative to other Shockley-Queisser limited technologies. History has shown that very few absorber materials develop into viable solar cell technologies, and it has been recognized that given the declining costs of silicon-based technologies, a new material must offer potential for both lower cost and higher efficiencies than the Shockley-Queisser limit. Slow cooling of photocarriers with energy in excess of the band edges (hot carriers), which is the first prerequisite of a solar absorbing material for building a hot carrier solar cell technology, has been reported in perovskites since the 2010s. The goal of this review is to illuminate the path towards a single-junction perovskite hot carrier solar cell technology by emphasizing uncertainties in understanding slow hot carrier cooling and recommending approaches to resolve them.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16310v1 公告类型：新 摘要：光伏太阳能电池是一项成熟的技术，硅基技术已大规模部署，但目前的技术受到自 20 世纪 60 年代初期以来已知的肖克利-奎瑟热力学极限的限制。在环境温度下工作的单结非聚光热载流子太阳能电池，其理论上预测的最终功率转换效率极限接近 70%，是 Shockley-Queisser 极限的两倍，甚至比 n=6 多结太阳能电池所能达到的效率还要高，自 20 世纪 80 年代初以来，它一直是一个难以捉摸的热门研究目标。金属卤化物钙钛矿半导体于 20 世纪 70 年代末被发现，光伏应用自 2010 年代初以来得到深入研究和开发。钙钛矿太阳能电池当前的技术发展主要是由于其相对于其他肖克利-奎瑟有限技术的预期低廉的加工成本。历史表明，很少有吸收材料能够发展成为可行的太阳能电池技术，并且人们已经认识到，鉴于硅基技术的成本不断下降，新材料必须具有比肖克利-奎瑟极限更低的成本和更高的效率的潜力。自 2010 年代以来，钙钛矿中就报道了能量超过能带边缘（热载流子）的光载流子的缓慢冷却，这是构建热载流子太阳能电池技术的太阳能吸收材料的首要先决条件。本次综述的目的是通过强调理解缓慢热载流子冷却的不确定性并推荐解决这些问题的方法来阐明单结钙钛矿热载流子太阳能电池技术的发展道路。
