# Non-Hermitian higher-order topological insulators enabled by altermagnet engineering

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23951v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We show that proximitizing an altermagnet to a non-Hermitian topological insulator provides a powerful mechanism for engineering non-Hermitian higher-order topological phases. The altermagnetic order opens a gap at the topological edge states and drives a topological phase transition from a first-order to a second-order topological phase. When combined with nonreciprocal hopping, the system exhibits both the non-Hermitian skin effect and a hybrid skin-topological effect, whereby first-order edge states and second-order corner states accumulate at selected corners of the lattice. We demonstrate that the spectral winding number of the edge states under cylindrical geometry dictates this corner localization and can be reversed by tuning the altermagnetic order. Consequently, both edge and corner modes become directionally controllable. Our results establish altermagnets as a versatile platform for realizing and tuning skin-topological phenomena in non-Hermitian higher-order topological systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23951v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们表明，将交流磁体靠近非厄米拓扑绝缘体为设计非厄米高阶拓扑相提供了强大的机制。交变磁序在拓扑边缘态处打开间隙并驱动从一阶拓扑相到二阶拓扑相的拓扑相变。当与不可逆跳跃结合时，系统表现出非厄米集肤效应和混合集肤拓扑效应，从而一阶边缘态和二阶角态在晶格的选定角处累积。我们证明，圆柱几何结构下边缘态的谱绕数决定了这种角点定位，并且可以通过调整交变磁序来反转。因此，边缘和拐角模式都变得方向可控。我们的结果将交流磁体确立为一个通用平台，用于在非厄米特高阶拓扑系统中实现和调整皮肤拓扑现象。
