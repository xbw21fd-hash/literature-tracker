## Abstract
Author(s): Aline Ramires Superconductivity-induced symmetry-breaking conditions for the transmutation of altermagnets from pure to mixed is investigated. Based on this analysis, scenarios are put forward that could explain the apparent onset of time-reversal symmetry breaking at the superconducting critical temperature in Sr 2 RuO 4 and the hidden magnetic phase and magnetic memory in 4Hb-TaS 2 . [Phys. Rev. Research 8, L012025] Published Mon Jan 26, 2026
