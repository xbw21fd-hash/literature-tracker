## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19988v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Optically addressable spins at material surfaces have represented a long-standing ambition in quantum sensing, providing atomic resolution and quantum-limited sensitivity. However, they are constrained by a finite depth at which the quantum spins can be stabilized. Here, we demonstrate a hybrid molecular-2D architecture that realizes quantum spin sensors directly on top of the surface. By anchoring spin-active molecules onto hexagonal boron nitride (hBN), we eliminate the depth of the quantum sensor while also exhibiting robust spin properties from 4~K to room temperature (RT). The Hahn-echo spin coherence time exceeds \(T_2 = 3.4~\upmu\text{s}\) at 4~K, outperforming values in bulk organic crystals and overturning the prevailing expectation that spin inevitably deteriorates upon approaching the surface. By chemically tuning the molecule through deuteration, \(T_2\) improves by more than 10-fold, and under dynamic decoupling, coherence is prolonged to the intrinsic lifetime limit, exceeding 300~\(\upmu\text{s}\). Proximal proton spins and the magnetic response of two-dimensional magnets beneath the hBN layer have been detected at RT. These molecular spins form surface quantum sensors with long coherence, optical addressability, and interfacial versatility, enabling a scalable, adaptable architecture beyond what conventional solid-state platforms offer.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19988v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：材料表面的光学可寻址自旋代表了量子传感领域的长期抱负，提供原子分辨率和量子限制灵敏度。然而，它们受到量子自旋可以稳定的有限深度的限制。在这里，我们展示了一种混合分子二维架构，可以直接在表面顶部实现量子自旋传感器。通过将自旋活性分子锚定到六方氮化硼 (hBN) 上，我们消除了量子传感器的深度，同时还表现出从 4~K 到室温 (RT) 的强大自旋特性。哈恩回波自旋相干时间在 4~K 时超过 \(T_2 = 3.4~\upmu\text{s}\)，优于块状有机晶体中的值，并推翻了接近表面时自旋不可避免地恶化的普遍预期。通过氘化对分子进行化学调控，\(T_2\) 提高了 10 倍以上，并且在动态解耦下，相干性延长至固有寿命极限，超过 300~\(\upmu\text{s}\)。在室温下已检测到近端质子自旋和六方氮化硼层下方二维磁体的磁响应。这些分子自旋形成具有长相干性、光学可寻址性和界面多功能性的表面量子传感器，从而实现超越传统固态平台所提供的可扩展、适应性强的架构。
