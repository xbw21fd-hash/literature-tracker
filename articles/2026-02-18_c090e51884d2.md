## Abstract
arXiv:2510.14868v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Altermagnets (AMs) - magnetic materials that have spin-split bandstructure with zero net spin polarization can be classified as weak or strong depending upon the strength of altermagnetic term in the Hamiltonian. We theoretically investigate electron transport in junctions between the two AMs in strong and weak altermagnetic phases. The charge and spin conductivities are analyzed as functions of angle $\theta$ between the N\'eel vectors of the two AMs. In the strong AM regime, the charge conductivity vanishes as $\theta \to \pi$, while in the weak AM regime it remains finite. Introducing a normal metal (NM) between two AMs leads to Fabry-P\'erot-type oscillations in charge conductivity which can be controlled by an applied gate voltage. In the strong regime, transport in AM-NM-AM junctions is dominated by up-spin electrons, whereas both spin channels contribute in the weak regime. These results highlight the potential of AM-based heterostructures for spintronic applications, such as spin filters, and quantum interference-based spintronic devices, where tunable spin-dependent transport and interference effects can be utilized in electronic devices without a need for externally applied magnetic field.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.14868v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：交变磁体 (AM) - 具有自旋分裂能带结构且净自旋极化为零的磁性材料，可根据哈密顿量中交变磁项的强度分为弱或强。我们从理论上研究强交变磁相和弱交变磁相下两个 AM 之间连接处的电子传输。电荷和自旋电导率被分析为两个 AM 的 N'eel 矢量之间的角度 $\theta$ 的函数。在强 AM 状态下，电荷电导率随着 $\theta \to \pi$ 的变化而消失，而在弱 AM 状态下，电荷电导率仍然有限。在两个 AM 之间引入普通金属 (NM) 会导致电荷电导率发生法布里-P 型振荡，该振荡可以通过施加的栅极电压进行控制。在强状态下，AM-NM-AM 结中的传输由上自旋电子主导，而两个自旋通道在弱状态下都有贡献。这些结果凸显了基于AM的异质结构在自旋电子应用中的潜力，例如自旋滤波器和基于量子干涉的自旋电子器件，其中可调谐的自旋相关输运和干涉效应可以在电子器件中利用，而无需外部施加磁场。
