## Abstract
Conventional photovoltaic devices based on PN or Schottky junctions are inherently constrained by the material band gap, resulting in limited photoelectric conversion efficiency. The van der Waals ferroelectric material CuInP2S6offers strong potential for efficient photocurrent generation via bulk photovoltaic effect, while heterostructure engineering provides additional degrees of freedom for performance modulation. Yet, the specificity in asymmetric CuInP2S6heterojunctions remains poorly understood. Here, we design and fabricate an asymmetric photovoltaic device, consisting of Pt/CuInP2S6/Graphene heterostructure. The ferroelectric photovoltaic effect couples with the Cu+migration dynamics, which could be manipulated by asymmetric interface barriers. Remarkably, the photovoltaic current is increased by 10 times and exhibits a positive-negative switching through polarization modulation. The heterojunction device achieves on-demand photovoltaic programming for in-sensor computing tasks, including edge detection with recognition rate F-score ~ 1 and binary pattern classification with 100% accuracy, which provides new insights for next-generation visual technology.

## 摘要（中文）
基于PN或肖特基结的传统光伏器件本质上受到材料带隙的限制，导致光电转换效率有限。范德瓦尔斯铁电材料 CuInP2S6 通过体光伏效应提供了高效光电流产生的强大潜力，而异质结构工程为性能调制提供了额外的自由度。然而，不对称 CuInP2S6 异质结的特异性仍然知之甚少。在这里，我们设计并制造了一种由 Pt/CuInP2S6/石墨烯异质结构组成的不对称光伏器件。铁电光伏效应与 Cu+ 迁移动力学耦合，可以通过不对称界面势垒来控制。值得注意的是，光伏电流增加了10倍，并通过偏振调制表现出正负切换。该异质结器件实现了传感器内计算任务的按需光伏编程，包括识别率F-score ~ 1的边缘检测和100%准确度的二元模式分类，为下一代视觉技术提供了新的见解。
