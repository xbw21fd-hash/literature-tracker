## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18953v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Many challenges arising in Quantum Technology can be successfully addressed using a set of machine learning algorithms collectively known as reinforcement learning (RL), based on adaptive decision-making through interaction with the quantum device. After a concise and intuitive introduction to RL aimed at a broad physics readership, we discuss the key ideas and core concepts in reinforcement learning with a particular focus on quantum systems. We then survey recent progress in RL in all relevant areas. We discuss state preparation in few- and many-body quantum systems, the design and optimization of high-fidelity quantum gates, and the automated construction of quantum circuits, including applications to variational quantum eigensolvers and architecture search. We further highlight the interactive capabilities of RL agents, emphasizing recent progress in quantum feedback control and quantum error correction, and briefly discuss quantum reinforcement learning as well as applications to quantum metrology. The review concludes with a discussion of open challenges -- such as scalability, interpretability, and integration with experimental platforms -- and outlines promising directions for future research. Throughout, we highlight experimental implementations that exemplify the increasing role of reinforcement learning in shaping the development of quantum technologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18953v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：使用一组统称为强化学习 (RL) 的机器学习算法可以成功解决量子技术中出现的许多挑战，这些算法基于通过与量子设备交互的自适应决策。在针对广大物理学读者对强化学习进行了简洁直观的介绍之后，我们讨论了强化学习的关键思想和核心概念，特别关注量子系统。然后，我们调查了强化学习在所有相关领域的最新进展。我们讨论少体和多体量子系统中的状态准备、高保真量子门的设计和优化以及量子电路的自动构建，包括在变分量子本征求解器和体系结构搜索中的应用。我们进一步强调了 RL 代理的交互能力，强调了量子反馈控制和量子纠错方面的最新进展，并简要讨论了量子强化学习以及在量子计量中的应用。该评论最后讨论了开放性挑战，例如可扩展性、可解释性以及与实验平台的集成，并概述了未来研究的有希望的方向。在整个过程中，我们重点介绍了实验实施，这些实验证明了强化学习在塑造量子技术发展方面日益重要的作用。
