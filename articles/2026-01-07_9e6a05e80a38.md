## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02770v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Radiation-tolerant semiconductors have traditionally been engineered by the principle of suppressing defect accumulation and amorphization, based on the assumption that radiation damage is inherently stochastic. Here we show that, in monoclinic $\beta$-\ce{Ga2O3}, a promising ultrawide-bandgap semiconductor, surface crystallographic orientation deterministically governs radiation tolerance through highly anisotropic kinetics of the $\beta$-to-$\gamma$ phase transition. Using machine-learning molecular dynamics coupled with a local configurational-entropy descriptor, we quantitatively map anisotropic $\beta$-to-$\gamma$ transition kinetics, showing that the critical dose, transition-layer depth, and kinetic stability of the $\gamma$-phase are fundamentally governed by surface orientation. Under ion irradiation, non-channeling surfaces such as (100), (001), and (-201) undergo severe surface amorphization, whereas the strongly channeling (010) surface resists damage accumulation and promotes subsurface $\gamma$-phase nucleation. During thermal annealing recovery process, these initial states follow two distinct recovery pathways: the channeling (010) surface reverts directly from $\gamma$-to-$\beta$, whereas non-channeling surfaces follow a sequential amorphous-to-$\gamma$-to-$\beta$ transition pathway. This work establishes surface orientation as a fundamental design principle for achieving radiation tolerance through controlled polymorphic transitions, providing a universal framework for engineering functional materials capable of withstanding extreme irradiation environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02770v1 公告类型：新 摘要：传统上，基于辐射损伤本质上是随机的假设，采用抑制缺陷积累和非晶化的原理来设计耐辐射半导体。在这里，我们表明，在单斜晶体$\beta$-\ce{Ga2O3}（一种有前途的超宽带隙半导体）中，表面晶体取向通过$\beta$到$\gamma$相变的高度各向异性动力学确定性地控制辐射耐受性。使用机器学习分子动力学与局部构型熵描述符相结合，我们定量地绘制了各向异性 $\beta$ 到 $\gamma$ 转变动力学，表明 $\gamma$ 相的临界剂量、过渡层深度和动力学稳定性从根本上由表面取向决定。在离子辐照下，非沟道表面如(100)、(001)和(-201)发生严重的表面非晶化，而强沟道(010)表面抵抗损伤积累并促进次表面$\gamma$相成核。在热退火恢复过程中，这些初始状态遵循两种不同的恢复路径：沟道 (010) 表面直接从 $\gamma$ 恢复到 $\beta$，而非沟道表面遵循连续的非晶态到 $\gamma$ 到 $\beta$ 转变路径。这项工作将表面取向确立为通过受控多晶型转变实现辐射耐受性的基本设计原则，为能够承受极端辐射环境的工程功能材料提供了通用框架。
