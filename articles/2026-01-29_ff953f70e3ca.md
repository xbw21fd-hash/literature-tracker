## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19980v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a fermionic gauge theory for deconfined quantum criticality on the Shastry-Sutherland lattice and reveal its shared low-energy field-theoretic structure with the square lattice. Starting from an SU(2) $\pi$-flux parent state, we construct a continuum theory of Dirac spinons coupled to an SU(2) gauge field and adjoint Higgs fields whose condensates drive transitions to a staggered-flux U(1) spin liquid and a gapless $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ Dirac spin liquid. While the Shastry-Sutherland lattice permits additional symmetry-allowed fermion bilinears compared to the square lattice, the quantum field theories are identical up to additional irrelevant terms. Consequently, the Higgs potential structure and the leading low-energy theory coincide with the square-lattice case at the quantum critical point. The SO(5) critical point is expected to realize conformal deconfined criticality: we analyze it in a large flavor expansion, calculate its critical exponents, and identify the Yukawa coupling between the fermions and Higgs fields as the relevant perturbation that destabilizes it, consistent with pseudocritical behavior observed in recent Monte Carlo studies. We show that the emergent SO(5) order parameter acquires a large anomalous dimension at the critical point, leading to strongly enhanced N\'eel and VBS susceptibilities-a hallmark of fermionic deconfined quantum criticality consistent with numerical studies. Our results place recent numerical evidence for a gapless $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ Dirac spin liquid on the Shastry-Sutherland lattice within a controlled field-theoretic framework and demonstrate that fermionic deconfined criticality on the square lattice-including critical exponents and stability-extends to frustrated lattices with reduced symmetry.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19980v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种用于 Shastry-Sutherland 晶格上的解禁量子临界性的费米子规范理论，并揭示了其与方晶格共享的低能场论结构。从 SU(2) $\pi$-通量母态开始，我们构建了耦合到 SU(2) 规范场和伴随希格斯场的狄拉克自旋子的连续介质理论，其凝聚驱动转变为交错通量 U(1) 自旋液体和无间隙 $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ 狄拉克自旋液体。虽然与方晶格相比，Shastry-Sutherland 晶格允许额外的对称性费米子双线性，但量子场论在附加不相关项之前是相同的。因此，希格斯势结构和领先的低能理论与量子临界点的方晶格情况一致。 SO(5) 临界点有望实现共形去限制临界点：我们在大风味展开中对其进行分析，计算其临界指数，并将费米子和希格斯场之间的汤川耦合识别为使其不稳定的相关扰动，这与最近蒙特卡罗研究中观察到的伪临界行为一致。我们表明，新兴的 SO(5) 序参数在临界点获得了大的反常尺寸，导致 N\'eel 和 VBS 敏感性大大增强——这是与数值研究一致的费米子解限域量子临界性的标志。我们的研究结果在受控场论框架内提供了 Shastry-Sutherland 晶格上无间隙 $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ 狄拉克自旋液体的最新数值证据，并证明了方晶格上的费米子去限制临界性（包括临界指数和稳定性）扩展到对称性降低的受抑晶格。
