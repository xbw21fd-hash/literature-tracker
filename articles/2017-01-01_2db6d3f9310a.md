## Abstract
TLDR: This review shines light on CPs and MOFs as optical media for state-of-the-art photonic phenomena such as multi-photon absorption, triplet-triplet annihilation (TTA) and stimulated emission, and outlines design principles, employing both intramolecular and intermolecular strategies, that are necessary for maximizing the NLO response.
