## Abstract
arXiv:2505.18001v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Liouvillian skin effects, manifested as the localization of Liouvillian eigenstates around the boundary, are distinctive features of non-Hermitian systems and are particularly notable for their impact on system dynamics. Despite their significance, Liouvillian skin effects have not been sufficiently explored in electron systems. In this work, we demonstrate that a two-dimensional electron system on a substrate exhibits $\mathbb{Z}$ and $\mathbb{Z}_2$ Liouvillian skin effects due to the interplay among energy dissipations, spin-orbit coupling, and a transverse magnetic field. In addition, our analysis of the temperature dependence reveals that these Liouvillian skin effects become pronounced below the energy scale of band splitting induced by the spin-orbit coupling and the magnetic field. While our $\mathbb{Z}$ Liouvillian skin effect leads to charge accumulation under quench dynamics, its relaxation time is independent of the system size, in contrast to that of previously reported Liouvillian skin effects. This difference is attributed to the scale-free behavior of the localization length, which is analogous to non-Hermitian critical skin effects.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.18001v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：刘维尔趋肤效应，表现为刘维尔本征态在边界周围的局域化，是非厄米系统的显着特征，尤其因其对系统动力学的影响而引人注目。尽管刘维尔趋肤效应具有重要意义，但其在电子系统中尚未得到充分探索。在这项工作中，我们证明了基板上的二维电子系统由于能量耗散、自旋轨道耦合和横向磁场之间的相互作用而表现出 $\mathbb{Z}$ 和 $\mathbb{Z}_2$ 刘维尔集肤效应。此外，我们对温度依赖性的分析表明，这些刘维尔趋肤效应在自旋轨道耦合和磁场引起的能带分裂的能量尺度以下变得明显。虽然我们的 $\mathbb{Z}$ 刘维尔趋肤效应会导致淬灭动力学下的电荷积累，但其弛豫时间与系统尺寸无关，这与之前报道的刘维尔趋肤效应不同。这种差异归因于定位长度的无标度行为，这类似于非厄米临界集肤效应。
