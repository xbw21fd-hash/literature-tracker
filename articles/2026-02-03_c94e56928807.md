# Triadic percolation on multilayer networks

多层网络上的三元渗透

## Abstract
arXiv:2510.09341v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Triadic interactions are special types of higher-order interactions that occur when regulator nodes modulate the interactions between other two or more nodes. In presence of triadic interactions, a percolation process occurring on a single-layer network becomes a fully-fledged dynamical system, characterized by period-doubling and a route to chaos. Here, we generalize the model to multilayer networks and name it as the multilayer triadic percolation (MTP) model. We find a much richer dynamical behavior of the MTP model than its single-layer counterpart. MTP displays a Neimark-Sacker bifurcation, leading to oscillations of arbitrarily large period or pseudo-periodic oscillations. Moreover, MTP admits period-two oscillations without negative regulatory interactions, whereas single-layer systems only display discontinuous hybrid transitions. This comprehensive model offers new insights on the importance of regulatory interactions in real-world systems such as brain networks, climate, and ecological systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.09341v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：三元相互作用是特殊类型的高阶相互作用，当调节器节点调节其他两个或多个节点之间的相互作用时发生。在存在三元相互作用的情况下，单层网络上发生的渗透过程成为一个成熟的动态系统，其特征是周期加倍和通往混沌的途径。在这里，我们将该模型推广到多层网络，并将其命名为多层三元渗滤（MTP）模型。我们发现 MTP 模型比单层模型具有更丰富的动态行为。 MTP 显示 Neimark-Sacker 分岔，导致任意大周期振荡或伪周期振荡。此外，MTP 允许二周期振荡，而没有负调控相互作用，而单层系统仅表现出不连续的混合跃迁。这个综合模型为大脑网络、气候和生态系统等现实世界系统中监管相互作用的重要性提供了新的见解。
