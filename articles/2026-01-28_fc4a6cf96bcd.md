## Abstract
arXiv:2304.14306v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: It is well known that the Hamiltonian of an $n$-dimensional isotropic oscillator admits an $SU(n)$ symmetry, making the system maximally superintegrable. However, the dynamical symmetries of the anisotropic oscillator are much more subtle. We introduce a novel set of canonical transformations that map an $n$-dimensional anisotropic oscillator to the corresponding isotropic problem. Consequently, the anisotropic oscillator is found to possess the same number of conserved quantities as the isotropic oscillator, making it maximally superintegrable too (commensurate case). The first integrals are explicitly calculated in the case of a two-dimensional anisotropic oscillator and remarkably, they admit closed-form expressions.

arXiv:2304.14306v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：众所周知，$n$ 维各向同性振荡器的哈密顿量承认 $SU(n)$ 对称性，使得系统最大可超积。然而，各向异性振荡器的动力学对称性要微妙得多。我们引入了一组新颖的规范变换，将 $n$ 维各向异性振荡器映射到相应的各向同性问题。因此，发现各向异性振子具有与各向同性振子相同数量的守恒量，使其也具有最大超可积性（相称情况）。第一个积分是在二维各向异性振荡器的情况下显式计算的，值得注意的是，它们允许封闭形式的表达式。
