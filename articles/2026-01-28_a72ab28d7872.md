## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18898v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Relativistic spin effects drive subtle molecular phenomena ranging from intersystem crossing in photodynamic therapy to spin-mediated catalysis and high-resolution spectroscopy. These effects are described by the Pauli-Breit Hamiltonian, which extends the nonrelativistic electronic Hamiltonian by including one- and two-electron spin-orbit and spin-spin interactions. First-principles simulations of the full Pauli-Breit Hamiltonian rapidly become intractable on classical computers due to the exponential growth of the Hilbert space and the complexity of two-body spin-dependent terms. We propose a fault-tolerant quantum algorithm for computing molecular energy levels and properties governed by the Pauli-Breit Hamiltonian. Our approach block-encodes the relativistic Hamiltonian in a second-quantized, doubly factorized representation. By reformulating the Hamiltonian in a symmetry-adapted Majorana basis, we construct efficient linear-combination-of-unitaries circuits that encode spin-orbit interactions without effective or mean-field approximations. We introduce spin-controlled Pauli-SWAP networks that decouple spin and orbital control logic, enabling a unified treatment of relativistic spin mixing with only modest overhead relative to spin-free simulations. We analyze quantum resources in terms of logical qubits and T-gate complexity, showing that explicit spin degrees of freedom do not worsen the asymptotic scaling. The prefactor is reduced by a factor of two compared to direct linear-combination-of-unitaries approaches. Finally, we outline a hybrid quantum-classical workflow for designing photodynamic therapy photosensitizers, artificial photosynthesis catalysts, and other systems where accurate relativistic spin effects are essential.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18898v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：相对论自旋效应驱动微妙的分子现象，从光动力疗法中的系间穿越到自旋介导的催化和高分辨率光谱。这些效应由泡利-布赖特哈密顿量描述，该哈密顿量通过包括一电子和二电子自旋轨道以及自旋-自旋相互作用来扩展非相对论电子哈密顿量。由于希尔伯特空间的指数增长和二体自旋相关项的复杂性，完整泡利-布赖特哈密顿量的第一性原理模拟在经典计算机上很快变得棘手。我们提出了一种容错量子算法，用于计算由泡利-布赖特哈密顿量控制的分子能级和性质。我们的方法以第二量化、双因式分解的表示形式对相对论哈密顿量进行块编码。通过在对称适应的马约拉纳基础上重新表述哈密顿量，我们构建了有效的酉线性组合电路，该电路可以在没有有效或平均场近似的情况下编码自旋轨道相互作用。我们引入了自旋控制的 Pauli-SWAP 网络，该网络可以解耦自旋和轨道控制逻辑，从而能够统一处理相对论自旋混合，相对于无自旋模拟而言，只需适度的开销。我们从逻辑量子位和 T 门复杂性方面分析了量子资源，表明显式自旋自由度不会恶化渐进缩放。与直接酉线性组合方法相比，前因子减少了两倍。最后，我们概述了一种混合量子经典工作流程，用于设计光动力疗法光敏剂、人工光合作用催化剂和其他需要精确相对论自旋效应的系统。
