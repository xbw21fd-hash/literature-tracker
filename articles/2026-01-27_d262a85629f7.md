## Abstract
arXiv:2510.26471v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Uniaxial spintronic heterostructures constitute compact THz emitters under femtosecond optical excitation, with emission amplitude and polarization governed by the applied magnetic field is presented. We demonstrate here efficient magnetically tunable THz amplitude control in ultrathin, exchange-biased Co/Pt/Co/IrMn spin valve. Terahertz spintronic magnetometry resolves reversible switching between parallel and antiparallel magnetization states and correlates the high- and low-emission regimes with constructive and destructive interference of charge transients generated by the inverse spin Hall effect in the Pt spacer. A residual low-emission signal is traced to spin-to-charge conversion in IrMn. Phase inversion under front- versus back-side excitation confirms the ISHE origin of the emission, while a macrospin Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert model reproduces the field dependence of its amplitude and separates layer-specific contributions. Together, these results define a wafer-compatible spin valve device architecture that enables efficient, low-field THz amplitude control.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.26471v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：单轴自旋电子异质结构在飞秒光激发下构成紧凑的太赫兹发射器，其发射幅度和偏振由所施加的磁场控制。我们在这里展示了超薄、交换偏置 Co/Pt/Co/IrMn 自旋阀中有效的磁可调太赫兹振幅控制。太赫兹自旋电子磁力测量解决了平行和反平行磁化状态之间的可逆切换，并将高发射和低发射状态与 Pt 间隔物中逆自旋霍尔效应产生的电荷瞬变的相长和相消干扰相关联。残余的低发射信号可追溯到 IrMn 中的自旋电荷转换。正面与背面激励下的相反转证实了发射的 ISHE 起源，而大自旋 Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert 模型再现了其振幅的场依赖性并分离了层特定的贡献。这些结果共同定义了一种晶圆兼容的自旋阀器件架构，可实现高效、低场太赫兹振幅控制。
