## Abstract
Author(s): Sarang Gopalakrishnan, Ewan McCulloch, and Romain Vasseur A monitored fluctuating hydrodynamics framework is introduced to study what can be learned about classical many-body dynamics from partial data. It is found that classical stochastic processes undergo phase transitions in learnability, mirroring striking effects in quantum systems. [Phys. Rev. X 16, 011024] Published Thu Feb 12, 2026
