## Abstract
The development of robust, synthetically accessible organic cages remains a major challenge in supramolecular chemistry, as most shape-persistent architectures require lengthy multistep routes or rely on dynamic, moisture-sensitive imine linkages. Here we report a rapid and operationally simple four-step route to a spacious pyridyl hexa-amide cage featuring an introverted bis-amide cavity ideally suited for anion recognition. The key benzylic tris-aniline precursor is obtained on multigram scale without chromatography, enabling fast and quantitative cage formation under microwave-assisted imine condensation. Pinnick oxidation efficiently converts all six imine bonds into chemically inert amides within the confined framework, affording a permanently locked nanocapsule in high overall yield. Structural analysis (NMR, DOSY, single-crystal XRD) reveals a rigid C3-symmetric host lined with six convergent N–H donors forming a dense polar environment. In solution, the cage binds mono- and dicarboxylates in competitive media (DMSO), while a C3-symmetric tricarboxylate – nitrilotripropionate (NTP) – forms a highly complementary 1:1 complex, observable by NMR under slow-exchange regime and confirmed crystallographically. This work establishes a modular platform for accessing robust hydrogen-bonding cavities without chromatographic purification and highlights their potential for the selective recognition of polycarboxylates, with perspectives in molecular sensing and confined catalysis.

## 摘要（中文）
开发坚固的、可合成的有机笼仍然是超分子化学中的一个主要挑战，因为大多数形状持久的结构需要漫长的多步骤路线或依赖于动态、湿度敏感的亚胺键。在这里，我们报告了一种快速且操作简单的四步路线，以获得宽敞的吡啶基六酰胺笼，该笼具有内向双酰胺腔，非常适合阴离子识别。关键的苄基三苯胺前体无需色谱法即可以数克规模获得，从而能够在微波辅助亚胺缩合下快速定量地形成笼。 Pinnick 氧化可有效地将所有六个亚胺键转化为受限框架内的化学惰性酰胺，从而以高总产率提供永久锁定的纳米胶囊。结构分析（NMR、DOSY、单晶 XRD）揭示了刚性的 C3 对称主体，排列有六个会聚的 N-H 供体，形成致密的极性环境。在溶液中，笼在竞争性介质 (DMSO) 中结合单羧酸盐和二羧酸盐，而 C3 对称三羧酸盐 - 次氮基三丙酸盐 (NTP) - 形成高度互补的 1:1 复合物，可在慢交换状态下通过 NMR 观察到并通过晶体学证实。这项工作建立了一个模块化平台，无需色谱纯化即可获得强大的氢键腔，并从分子传感和受限催化的角度突出了它们选择性识别聚羧酸盐的潜力。
