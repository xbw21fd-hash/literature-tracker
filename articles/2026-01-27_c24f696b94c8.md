## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17523v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Sleep is thought to support memory consolidation and the recovery of optimal energetic regime by reorganizing synaptic connectivity, yet how plasticity across hierarchical brain circuits contributes to abstraction and energy efficiency remains unclear. Here we study a spiking multi-layer network alternating wake-like and deep-sleep-like states, with state-dependent dendritic integration and synaptic plasticity in a biologically inspired thalamo-cortical framework. During wakefulness, the model learns from few perceived examples, while during deep sleep it undergoes spontaneous replay driven by slow oscillations. Plasticity enabled not only within intra-layer connections, but also in inter-layer pathways, is critical for memory consolidation and energetic downshift. Compared to restricted plasticity, full inter-layer plasticity yields higher post-sleep visual classification accuracy and promotes the emergence of sharper class-specific associations. Furthermore, we introduce a biophysically grounded estimator of metabolic power expressing network energy consumption in ATP units, partitioned into baseline, synaptic maintenance, action potential, and transmission costs. We find that inter-layer plasticity in sleep leads to a larger reduction in firing rates, synaptic strength and synaptic activity, corresponding to a substantially larger decrease in power consumption. This work suggests promising elements to be integrated in neuromorphic/energy-efficient AI learning systems, supported by brain state-specific apical mechanisms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.17523v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：睡眠被认为可以通过重组突触连接来支持记忆巩固和最佳能量状态的恢复，但分层大脑回路的可塑性如何有助于抽象和能量效率仍不清楚。在这里，我们研究了一个尖峰多层网络，它交替唤醒类和深度睡眠类状态，在受生物学启发的丘脑皮质框架中具有状态依赖的树突整合和突触可塑性。在清醒期间，该模型从少数感知到的例子中学习，而在深度睡眠期间，它会经历由缓慢振荡驱动的自发重放。可塑性不仅在层内连接中启用，而且在层间路径中启用，对于记忆巩固和能量下降至关重要。与受限可塑性相比，完全的层间可塑性可产生更高的睡眠后视觉分类精度，并促进更清晰的特定类别关联的出现。此外，我们引入了一种基于生物物理的代谢能力估计器，以 ATP 单位表示网络能量消耗，分为基线、突触维护、动作电位和传输成本。我们发现，睡眠中的层间可塑性会导致放电率、突触强度和突触活动大幅下降，相应地功耗也会大幅下降。这项工作提出了在神经形态/节能人工智能学习系统中集成有前景的元素，并得到大脑状态特定的顶端机制的支持。
