## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17176v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic nanoparticles beyond the single-domain limit often develop vortex-like magnetization textures arising from the competition between exchange and magnetostatic energies. While such states are routinely studied using micromagnetic simulations, transparent analytical descriptions of vortex-mediated hysteresis and nucleation remain scarce. Here, we develop a semi-analytical minimal framework for vortex states in spherical magnetic nanoparticles. Guided by micromagnetic simulations, we introduce a parametrized vortex magnetization Ansatz based on hyperbolic functions that continuously interpolates between uniform and vortex states. In this way, we achieve a complexity reduction leading to a minimal Hamiltonian, which enables the efficient computation of magnetization curves and provides insight into vortex-mediated magnetization reversal. As an application, we derive analytical estimates for the critical vortex nucleation radius and field, recovering the functional form of Brown's classic result and extending it within a variational framework.

arXiv:2601.17176v1 发布类型：新 摘要：超出单域极限的磁性纳米粒子经常会因交换能和静磁能之间的竞争而产生涡旋状磁化纹理。虽然通常使用微磁模拟来研究这种状态，但对涡旋介导的磁滞和成核的透明分析描述仍然很少。在这里，我们开发了球形磁性纳米颗粒中涡旋态的半解析最小框架。在微磁模拟的指导下，我们引入了基于双曲函数的参数化涡旋磁化 Ansatz，该函数在均匀状态和涡旋状态之间连续插值。通过这种方式，我们实现了复杂性的降低，从而获得最小的哈密顿量，从而能够有效地计算磁化曲线，并提供对涡旋介导的磁化反转的深入了解。作为一种应用，我们得出了临界涡旋成核半径和场的分析估计，恢复了布朗经典结果的函数形式并将其扩展到变分框架内。
