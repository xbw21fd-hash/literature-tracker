## Abstract
Author(s): Grégoire F. M. Tomassi, Daniël Veldhuizen, Bruno Melo, Davide Candoli, Andreu Riera-Campeny, Oriol Romero-Isart, Nadine Meyer, and Romain Quidant By subjecting a 125 nm silica nanoparticle to an inverted dark potential, this study demonstrates exponentially fast delocalization of a massive particle’s center-of-mass thermal state. A 952-fold expansion is achieved within 260 μs, reaching a position uncertainty of 43.4 nm, a scale comparable to the particle’s physical size. [Phys. Rev. Research 8, L012026] Published Mon Jan 26, 2026

作者：Grégoire F. M. Tomassi、Daniël Veldhuizen、Bruno Melo、Davide Candoli、Andreu Riera-Campeny、Oriol Romero-Isart、Nadine Meyer 和 Romain Quidant 通过将 125 nm 二氧化硅纳米粒子置于反向暗势下，这项研究证明了大质量粒子的质心热态的指数快速离域。在 260 μs 内实现了 952 倍的膨胀，达到了 43.4 nm 的位置不确定度，这一尺度与粒子的物理尺寸相当。 [物理。 Rev.
