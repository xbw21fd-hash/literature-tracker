## Abstract
arXiv:2511.16578v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Magnetic fields typically suppress superconductivity once the Zeeman energy exceeds the pairing gap, unless mechanisms such as unconventional pairing, strong spin-orbit coupling, or intrinsic magnetism intervene. Several graphene platforms realize such mitigating routes, exhibiting superconductivity resilient to magnetic fields. Here we report superconductivity in rhombohedral heptalayer graphene that is both induced and stabilized by in-plane magnetic field ($B_{\parallel}$), with critical fields far beyond the Pauli paramagnetic limit. The superconductivity spans a wide gate range and emerges from a sharp zero-field resistive ridge that tracks approximately constant conduction band filling. The presence of zero-field superconductivity and the evolution of the critical temperature with $B_{\parallel}$ are highly gate sensitive. We also observe a weak superconducting diode effect in several distinct regimes within the superconducting phase, including nearby to an integer quantum anomalous Hall state generated by a boron nitride moir\'e superlattice, indicating a potential coexistence of valley imbalance and superconductivity. These results establish several intriguing new properties of spin-triplet, field-induced superconductivity in a thick rhombohedral graphene stack.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.16578v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：一旦塞曼能量超过配对间隙，磁场通常会抑制超导性，除非非常规配对、强自旋轨道耦合或固有磁性等机制介入。一些石墨烯平台实现了这种缓解途径，表现出对磁场有弹性的超导性。在这里，我们报告了菱面体七层石墨烯的超导性，它是由面内磁场 ($B_{\parallel}$) 诱导和稳定的，其临界场远远超出泡利顺磁极限。超导性跨越了很宽的栅极范围，并从尖锐的零场电阻脊中出现，该脊跟踪近似恒定的导带填充。零场超导性的存在以及 $B_{\parallel}$ 临界温度的演变对栅极高度敏感。我们还观察到超导相内几个不同区域的弱超导二极管效应，包括氮化硼莫尔超晶格产生的整数量子反常霍尔态附近，这表明谷不平衡和超导性可能共存。这些结果确立了厚菱面体石墨烯堆叠中自旋三重态、场致超导性的几个有趣的新特性。
