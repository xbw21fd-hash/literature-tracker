## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03438v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Non-equilibrium Green's function (NEGF) formalism is a particularly powerful method to simulate the quantum transport properties of nanoscale devices such as transistors, photo-diodes, or memory cells, in the ballistic limit of transport or in the presence of various scattering sources such as electronphonon, electron-photon, or even electron-electron interactions. The inclusion of all these mechanisms has been first demonstrated in small systems, composed of a few atoms, before being scaled up to larger structures made of thousands of atoms. Also, the accuracy of the models has kept improving, from empirical to fully ab-initio ones, e.g., density functional theory (DFT). This paper summarizes key (algorithmic) achievements that have allowed us to bring DFT+NEGF simulations closer to the dimensions and functionality of realistic systems. The possibility of leveraging graph neural networks and machine learning to speed up ab-initio device simulations is discussed as well.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03438v1 公告类型：新 摘要：非平衡格林函数 (NEGF) 形式是一种特别强大的方法，可以在传输的弹道极限或存在各种散射源（例如电子声子、电子-光子，甚至电子-电子相互作用）的情况下模拟晶体管、光电二极管或存储单元等纳米级器件的量子传输特性。所有这些机制的包含首先在由几个原子组成的小系统中得到证明，然后扩展到由数千个原子组成的更大结构。此外，模型的准确性不断提高，从经验模型到完全从头算模型，例如密度泛函理论（DFT）。本文总结了关键（算法）成就，这些成就使我们能够使 DFT+NEGF 模拟更接近现实系统的维度和功能。还讨论了利用图神经网络和机器学习来加速从头开始的设备模拟的可能性。
