## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02228v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Central to interatomic potential efficiency is the radial envelope function that enables linear scaling with computational cost by defining a local neighborhood of atoms. This has enabled MLIPs to revolutionize materials science over the past decade by providing DFT accuracy with linear scaling computational cost in molecular dynamics workflows. However, MLIPs still have a relatively high computational cost compared to empirical interatomic potentials, preventing them from transforming molecular dynamics workflows. A central issue is that MLIPs use relatively large cutoff radii, converging to 6A over the last few years. The large cutoffs prioritize accuracy of any material over efficiency in any particular region of phase space, capturing dispersion effects and low density materials at the expense of increased computational cost in higher density materials. Past work has aimed to address this with KNN graph sparsification, which, while significantly reducing cost, has the drawback of breaking energy conservation. In this work, we propose higher-order envelope functions that prune local atomic neighborhoods through physically inspired geometric functions to provide the memory and efficiency benefits of KNN graph sparsification while eliminating non-conservative energy dynamics. Through numerical experiments on solids and liquids with 5-8A cutoffs, we show that triplet envelope functions complement radial envelope functions by doubling training and inference speed, tripling memory efficiency, and increasing simulation stability while not impacting accuracy or data efficiency for the most common 6A cutoff. Moreover, experiments with 8A radial cutoffs show triplet envelope functions create a pathway to larger cutoff radii for efficiently and accurately modeling open structures with large interatomic distances, showing a promising new direction for engineering MLIP efficiency.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02228v1 公告类型：新 摘要：原子间势效率的核心是径向包络函数，它通过定义原子的局部邻域来实现具有计算成本的线性缩放。这使得 MLIP 在过去十年中通过在分子动力学工作流程中提供 DFT 精度和线性缩放计算成本而彻底改变了材料科学。然而，与经验原子间势相比，MLIP 的计算成本仍然相对较高，这阻碍了它们改变分子动力学工作流程。一个核心问题是 MLIP 使用相对较大的截止半径，在过去几年中收敛到 6A。大截断优先考虑任何材料的准确性，而不是任何特定相空间区域的效率，捕捉色散效应和低密度材料，但代价是增加高密度材料的计算成本。过去的工作旨在通过 KNN 图稀疏化来解决这个问题，这在显着降低成本的同时，也存在破坏能量守恒的缺点。在这项工作中，我们提出了高阶包络函数，通过物理启发的几何函数修剪局部原子邻域，以提供 KNN 图稀疏化的内存和效率优势，同时消除非保守能量动力学。通过对具有 5-8A 截止值的固体和液体进行数值实验，我们表明三重包络函数通过使训练和推理速度加倍、使内存效率提高三倍并提高模拟稳定性来补充径向包络函数，同时不影响最常见的 6A 截止值的精度或数据效率。此外，8A 径向截止实验表明，三重态包络函数创建了一条通向更大截止半径的途径，可有效、准确地建模具有大原子间距离的开放结构，为工程 MLIP 效率展示了一个有前途的新方向。
