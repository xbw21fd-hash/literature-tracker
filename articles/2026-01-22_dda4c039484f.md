## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14463v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a comprehensive analysis and experimental study of THz hot-electron bolometer (HEB) mixers made from 40-nm-thick sputtered magnesium diboride (MgB2) thin films on high-resistivity silicon substrates. Using a lumped-element bolometric model, we achieve strong quantitative agreement with measurements of conversion gain, noise temperature, and local-oscillator (LO) coupling to the HEB devices. Our analysis shows that the sensitivity of current HEB devices is primarily limited by on-chip optical losses, with both Johnson and thermal-fluctuation noise contributing significantly to the overall noise temperature. Simulations of an optimized device with near-ideal optical coupling suggest that Johnson noise remains a substantial factor even with improved coupling. Further reduction of the noise temperature may require additional suppression of Johnson noise (via improved intrinsic conversion gain) beyond optimizing optical coupling efficiency. We emphasize the importance of accurate modeling to achieve good numerical agreement with experiments, thereby enabling understanding of the causes of sensitivity loss.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14463v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们对高电阻率硅基板上由 40 nm 厚溅射二硼化镁 (MgB2) 薄膜制成的太赫兹热电子测辐射热计 (HEB) 混合器进行了全面分析和实验研究。使用集总元件测辐射热模型，我们与 HEB 器件的转换增益、噪声温度和本地振荡器 (LO) 耦合的测量结果实现了高度的定量一致性。我们的分析表明，当前 HEB 器件的灵敏度主要受到片上光学损耗的限制，约翰逊噪声和热波动噪声对整体噪声温度影响很大。对具有近乎理想光耦合的优化器件的仿真表明，即使耦合得到改善，约翰逊噪声仍然是一个重要因素。除了优化光耦合效率之外，进一步降低噪声温度可能还需要额外抑制约翰逊噪声（通过改进的固有转换增益）。我们强调精确建模对于实现与实验良好的数值一致性的重要性，从而能够了解灵敏度损失的原因。
