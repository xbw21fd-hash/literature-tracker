## Abstract
arXiv:2503.23583v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Magnetoelectric multiferroics promise direct cross-control between coexisting ferroelectric and ferromagnetic orders, which is of interest for applications in magnetism and spintronics. A particularly interesting type of cross-control is found in spin-spiral multiferroic Mn$_2$GeO$_4$, where a ferroelectric multi-domain distribution can be globally inverted by a single magnetic field sweep. In this work we consider the initial domain evolution from zero-field cooling, imaging the evolution of domains under both magnetic and electric fields via optical second harmonic generation. We find that polarization and magnetization domains form independently when entering the multiferroic phase, and a single deterministic initialisation procedure, spanning three quarters of a field cycle, is required to achieve reliable magnetoelectric cross-coupling. This initialisation behaviour originates from a deterministic pathway from metastable to equilibrium domain patterns, in contrast to more common and less reliable domain "training" procedures that require repeated field cycles. Understanding the initial domain evolution thus enables reliable cross-control in magnetoelectric devices with highly interlinked order parameters.

arXiv:2503.23583v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：磁电多铁性材料有望在共存的铁电和铁磁有序之间进行直接交叉控制，这对于磁学和自旋电子学中的应用很有意义。在自旋螺旋多铁性 Mn$_2$GeO$_4$ 中发现了一种特别有趣的交叉控制类型，其中铁电多域分布可以通过单个磁场扫描全局反转。在这项工作中，我们考虑零场冷却的初始域演化，通过光学二次谐波生成对磁场和电场下域的演化进行成像。我们发现，当进入多铁相时，极化域和磁化域独立形成，并且需要跨越四分之三场周期的单个确定性初始化程序来实现可靠的磁电交叉耦合。这种初始化行为源自从亚稳态到平衡域模式的确定性路径，这与需要重复现场循环的更常见且不太可靠的域“训练”程序形成鲜明对比。因此，了解初始磁域演化可以实现具有高度互连的有序参数的磁电器件中的可靠交叉控制。
