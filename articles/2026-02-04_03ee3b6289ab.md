## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02692v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The interplay of twist and strain in bilayer graphene enables the formation of moir\'e patterns and narrow bands that host correlated and topological phases. While magic-angle twisted bilayer graphene has been widely studied, strain provides an additional and realistic control knob for band engineering. In this work, we first generate a global method to construct commensurate supercells for arbitrary twist and strain. Then, using atomistic tight-binding and strain-extended continuum models to study the commensurate structures, we identify configurations that minimize the bandwidth beyond the magic angle. The results reveal a strong dependence of band narrowing and topology on strain type, magnitude, direction and lattice relaxation. Particularly, shear strain produces a stronger distortion than uniaxial strain. Including electron-electron interactions through a self-consistent Hartree potential shows that strain broadens the bare bands while reducing electrostatic renormalization. Strain also drives topological transitions as the narrow and remote bands hybridize, establishing twisted and strained bilayer graphene as a tunable platform for flat-band and topological phenomena.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02692v1 公告类型：新 摘要：双层石墨烯中扭曲和应变的相互作用使得能够形成莫尔图案和承载相关相和拓扑相的窄带。虽然魔角扭曲双层石墨烯已被广泛研究，但应变为能带工程提供了额外且现实的控制旋钮。在这项工作中，我们首先生成一种全局方法来构造任意扭曲和应变的相称的超晶胞。然后，使用原子紧束缚和应变扩展连续体模型来研究相应的结构，我们确定了最小化超出魔角的带宽的配置。结果揭示了能带变窄和拓扑结构对应变类型、大小、方向和晶格弛豫的强烈依赖性。特别是，剪切应变比单轴应变产生更强的变形。通过自洽哈特里势包括电子-电子相互作用表明，应变拓宽了裸带，同时减少了静电重正化。当窄带和远程带混合时，应变还驱动拓扑转变，建立扭曲和应变的双层石墨烯作为平带和拓扑现象的可调平台。
