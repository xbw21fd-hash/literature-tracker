## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05117v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a framework for a global description of the dynamics of complex flows via clusterized spatial representations of the flow, isolating and identifying local dynamics, retrieving different Space-Time Cluster-Based Network Models (ST-CNMs). The key enabler is the partitioning of the domain based on a nonlinear manifold learning approach, in which spatial points are clustered based on the similarity of their dynamics, as observed in their compact embedding in manifold coordinates. The method receives as input time-resolved flow fields. From these, the spatial manifold is computed through isometric mapping applied to the vorticity time histories at each spatial location. An unsupervised clustering method, applied in the manifold space, partitions the full flow domain into subdomains. The dynamics of each subdomain are then described with cluster-based modeling. The method is demonstrated on two flow-field datasets obtained with a direct numerical simulation of a fluidic pinball under periodic forcing and with two-dimensional particle image velocimetry measurements of a transitional jet flow. The spatial manifold-based flow partitioning identifies regions with similar dynamics in an automated way. For both cases, ST-CNM identifies local dynamics that are not captured by a global approach. In particular, vortex shedding and vortex pairing dynamics are isolated in the jet flow experiment. The proposed fully automated domain partitioning method will benefit the structural description of controlled flows and unveil the actuation mechanisms at play.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05117v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一个框架，通过流的集群空间表示、隔离和识别局部动态、检索不同的基于时空集群的网络模型（ST-CNM）来全局描述复杂流的动态。关键的推动因素是基于非线性流形学习方法的域划分，其中空间点根据其动力学的相似性进行聚类，正如在流形坐标中的紧凑嵌入中观察到的那样。该方法接收时间分辨流场作为输入。由此，通过应用于每个空间位置处的涡度时间历史的等距映射来计算空间流形。应用于流形空间的无监督聚类方法将全流域划分为子域。然后使用基于集群的建模来描述每个子域的动态。该方法在两个流场数据集上进行了演示，这两个流场数据集是通过周期性强迫下的流体弹球的直接数值模拟和过渡射流的二维粒子图像测速测量获得的。基于空间流形的流分区以自动化的方式识别具有相似动态的区域。对于这两种情况，ST-CNM 都可以识别全局方法未捕获的局部动态。特别是，在射流实验中分离出了涡旋脱落和涡旋配对动力学。所提出的全自动域划分方法将有利于受控流的结构描述并揭示起作用的驱动机制。
