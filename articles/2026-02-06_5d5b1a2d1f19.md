## Abstract
Neural network quantum states (NQSs) excel at approximating ground states of quantum many-body systems, but approximating all states of a degenerate manifold is nevertheless computationally expensive. We propose a single-trunk multi-head (ST-MH) NQS ensemble that shares a feature-extracting trunk while attaching lightweight heads for each target state. Using a cost function that also has an orthogonality term, we derive exact analytic gradients and overlap derivatives needed to train ST-MH within standard variational Monte Carlo (VMC) workflows. We prove that ST-MH can represent every degenerate eigenstate exactly whenever the feature map of latent width h, augmented with a constant, has column space containing the linear span of the targets’ log-moduli and (chosen) phase branches together with the constant on the common support where all states are non-vanishing. Under this condition, ST-MH reduces the parameter count and can reduce the leading VMC cost by a factor equal to the degeneracy K relative to other algorithms when K is modest and in trunk dominated regimes. As a numerical proof-of-principle, we validate and benchmark the ST-MH approach on the frustrated spin- J1–J2 Heisenberg model at the Majumdar–Ghosh (MG) point on periodic ring lattices of up to 32 sites. By obtaining the two ground states that furnish the MG ground space, we demonstrate that ST-MH attains high fidelity and energy accuracy across degenerate ground state manifolds while using significantly lower computing resources. Lastly we provide a qualitative computational cost analysis which incentivise the applicability of the ST-MH ensemble under certain criteria on the latent width.

## 摘要（中文）
神经网络量子态（NQS）擅长近似量子多体系统的基态，但近似简并流形的所有状态在计算上仍然是昂贵的。我们提出了一种单主干多头（ST-MH）NQS 集成，它共享一个特征提取主干，同时为每个目标状态附加轻量级头。使用也具有正交项的成本函数，我们得出在标准变分蒙特卡罗 (VMC) 工作流程中训练 ST-MH 所需的精确解析梯度和重叠导数。我们证明，只要潜在宽度 h 的特征图（用常数增强）具有包含目标对数模和（选择的）相位分支的线性跨度的列空间以及公共支持上的常数（其中所有状态均不为零），ST-MH 就可以准确地表示每个简并本征态。在这种情况下，当 K 适中并且处于主干主导状态时，ST-MH 减少了参数数量，并且可以将领先的 VMC 成本降低等于相对于其他算法的简并 K 的因子。作为数值原理验证，我们在最多 32 个位点的周期性环晶格的 Majumdar-Ghosh (MG) 点处的受挫自旋 J1-J2 海森堡模型上验证和基准化了 ST-MH 方法。通过获得提供 MG 地面空间的两个基态，我们证明 ST-MH 在简并基态流形上获得了高保真度和能量精度，同时使用显着较低的计算资源。最后，我们提供了定性的计算成本分析，该分析在潜在宽度的某些标准下激励了 ST-MH 系综的适用性。
