## Abstract
arXiv:2505.03152v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Light possesses both spin and orbital angular momentum. In spatially asymmetric optical fields, these properties undergo spontaneous coupling, referred to as optical spin-orbit coupling. The study of the coupling has recently become central in modern optics due to its substantial applications in communications, sensing, and quantum control. A key challenge is to clarify the relationship between the origins of spatially asymmetric optical fields and the resulting spin-orbit coupling. Current research focuses on materials and configurations exhibiting spatial asymmetry, such as focusing lenses, interfaces, inhomogeneous media, and metasurfaces. However, Maxwell's equations indicate that matter can introduce both spatial and temporal asymmetry into optical fields. For instance, magnetic ordering breaks the time-reversal symmetry of interacting optical fields via the magneto-optical effect, introducing nonreciprocity in the resulting optical phenomena. Despite the importance, optical phenomena involving both spatially and temporally asymmetric optical fields remain unexplored. Here, we demonstrate that breaking time and spatial symmetries through magnons and light focusing, respectively, transforms an input Gaussian beam into a specific optical vortex beam in a nonreciprocal manner. This phenomenon is quantitatively explained by integrating the physics of magnon-induced Brillouin light scattering with optical spin-orbit coupling. The observed conservation of total angular momentum, encompassing both magnons and photons, further indicates that magnons can control both spin and orbital angular momentum of light. Finally, we outline future research directions enabled by asymmetric optical fields in both space and time.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.03152v3 发布类型：replace-cross 摘要：光同时具有自旋角动量和轨道角动量。在空间不对称光场中，这些特性会发生自发耦合，称为光学自旋轨道耦合。由于耦合在通信、传感和量子控制方面的广泛应用，耦合研究最近已成为现代光学的核心。一个关键的挑战是阐明空间不对称光场的起源与由此产生的自旋轨道耦合之间的关系。目前的研究重点是表现出空间不对称性的材料和配置，例如聚焦透镜、界面、非均匀介质和超表面。然而，麦克斯韦方程表明物质可以在光场中引入空间和时间不对称性。例如，磁有序通过磁光效应打破了相互作用光场的时间反转对称性，在所得光学现象中引入了非互易性。尽管很重要，但涉及空间和时间不对称光场的光学现象仍未得到探索。在这里，我们证明了分别通过磁振子和光聚焦打破时间和空间对称性，以非互易的方式将输入高斯光束转换为特定的光学涡旋光束。通过将磁振子引起的布里渊光散射物理学与光学自旋轨道耦合相结合，可以定量地解释这种现象。观察到的总角动量守恒，包括磁子和光子，进一步表明磁子可以控制光的自旋和轨道角动量。最后，我们概述了空间和时间上不对称光场实现的未来研究方向。
