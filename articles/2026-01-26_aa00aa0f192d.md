## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16350v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Learning the full family of solutions to parameterized partial differential equations (PDEs) is a central challenge to our ability to model the behavior of heterogeneous systems, with a variety of fundamental and application-oriented implications in fields such as hydrogeology where system properties exhibit significant (and often uncertain) spatial heterogeneity. We address this by reformulating a Physics-Informed Neural Network (PINN) as a differentiable solver that learns the continuous solution manifold for steady-state Darcy flow. Our framework requires only a single training run, circumventing the need for costly re-training for each new parameter instance. Its versatility is demonstrated through two representations of spatially heterogeneous hydraulic conductivity fields: a direct analytical form and a novel data-driven formulation resting on an autoencoder to create a low-dimensional latent encoding. A key innovation is the integration of the differentiable decoder into the physics-informed loss function, enabling on-the-fly reconstruction of complex conductivity fields via automatic differentiation. The approach yields accurate, mass-conserving flow solutions and supports efficient uncertainty quantification, providing a general methodology for physics-constrained data-driven modeling of heterogeneous systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16350v1 公告类型：新 摘要：学习参数化偏微分方程 (PDE) 的全系列解是我们对异质系统行为进行建模的能力的一个核心挑战，在水文地质学等系统属性表现出显着（且通常不确定）空间异质性的领域具有各种基础和面向应用的影响。我们通过将物理信息神经网络（PINN）重新表述为可微分求解器来解决这个问题，该求解器学习稳态达西流的连续解流形。我们的框架只需要一次训练运行，避免了对每个新参数实例进行昂贵的重新训练的需要。它的多功能性通过空间异质水力传导率场的两种表示来证明：直接分析形式和基于自动编码器以创建低维潜在编码的新颖数据驱动公式。一项关键创新是将可微分解码器集成到物理信息损失函数中，从而能够通过自动微分即时重建复杂的电导率场。该方法产生准确、质量守恒的流动解决方案，并支持有效的不确定性量化，为异构系统的物理约束数据驱动建模提供通用方法。
