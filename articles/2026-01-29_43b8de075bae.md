## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20236v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Significance: Voltage imaging microscopy has emerged as a powerful tool to investigate neural activity both in vivo and in vitro. Various imaging approaches have been developed, including point-scanning, line-scanning and wide-field microscopes, however the effects of their different temporal sampling methods on signal fidelity have not yet been fully investigated. Aim: To provide an analysis of the inherent advantages and disadvantages of temporal sampling in scanning and wide-field microscopes and their effect on the fidelity of voltage spike detection. Approach: We develop a mathematical framework based on a mixture of analytical modeling and computer simulations with Monte-Carlo approaches. Results: Scanning microscopes outperform wide-field microscopes in low signal-to-noise conditions and when only a small subset of spikes needs to be detected. Wide-field microscopes outperform scanning microscopes when the measurement is temporally undersampled and a large fraction of the spikes needs to be detected. Both modalities converge in performance as sampling increases and the frame rate reaches the decay rate of the voltage indicator. Conclusions: Our work provides guidance for the selection of optimal temporal sampling parameters for voltage imaging. Most importantly it advises against using scanning voltage imaging microscopes at frame rates below 500 Hz.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20236v1 公告类型：新 摘要：意义：电压成像显微镜已成为研究体内和体外神经活动的强大工具。人们已经开发了各种成像方法，包括点扫描、线扫描和宽视场显微镜，但其不同时间采样方法对信号保真度的影响尚未得到充分研究。目的：分析扫描和宽视场显微镜中时间采样的固有优点和缺点及其对电压尖峰检测保真度的影响。方法：我们开发了一个基于分析建模和计算机模拟与蒙特卡罗方法相结合的数学框架。结果：在低信噪比条件下以及仅需要检测一小部分尖峰时，扫描显微镜的性能优于宽视场显微镜。当测量暂时采样不足并且需要检测大部分尖峰时，宽视场显微镜的性能优于扫描显微镜。随着采样增加并且帧速率达到电压指示器的衰减率，两种模式的性能趋于一致。结论：我们的工作为选择电压成像的最佳时间采样参数提供了指导。最重要的是，它建议不要在低于 500 Hz 的帧速率下使用扫描电压成像显微镜。
