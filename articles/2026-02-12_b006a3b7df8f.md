## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10288v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The sublattice-symmetry breaking in the $\alpha-T_3$ lattice leads to a bandgap opening. A defect line in the substrate on which the $\alpha-T_3$ lattice is deposited can be viewed as a topological change in the substrate that induces translational in-plane symmetry breaking, resulting in mid-gap states. These topologically protected states are confined along the defect line and exhibit preferential directional motion, with different signs for the different Dirac valleys. Within this context, we investigate how these unidirectional interface chiral states are affected in the presence of a perpendicular magnetic field and how they can be tuned by varying the controlling system parameter $\alpha$. The latter tunes the $\alpha-T_3$ structure from a honeycomb-like lattice ($\alpha=0$) to a dice lattice ($\alpha=1$). Our theoretical framework is based on the continuum approximation described by a $3\times 3$ matrix Hamiltonian with a sublattice symmetry-breaking term given by $\Delta(x) diag(1,\quad -1,\quad 1)$, assuming $\Delta(x)$ as a kink-like mass potential profile. Results for dispersion relations and wavefunction distributions for different $\alpha$ parameters and magnetic field amplitudes are discussed. We demonstrate lifting of Landau levels degeneracy and of valley degeneracy. Our findings pave the way for proposing valley filter devices based on any evolutionary stage between the honeycomb-like and dice lattice structures of the $\alpha-T_3$ phase, controlled by external fields.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10288v1 公告类型：新 摘要：$\alpha-T_3$ 晶格中的亚晶格对称性破缺导致带隙开口。沉积$\alpha-T_3$晶格的衬底中的缺陷线可以被视为衬底中的拓扑变化，其引起平移面内对称性破缺，从而产生中间能隙态。这些拓扑保护态沿着缺陷线被限制，并表现出优先方向运动，不同的狄拉克谷具有不同的迹象。在此背景下，我们研究了这些单向界面手性态在垂直磁场存在下如何受到影响，以及如何通过改变控制系统参数 $\alpha$ 来调整它们。后者将 $\alpha-T_3$ 结构从蜂窝状晶格 ($\alpha=0$) 调整为骰子晶格 ($\alpha=1$)。我们的理论框架基于 $3\times 3$ 矩阵哈密顿量描述的连续体近似，其亚晶格对称破缺项由 $\Delta(x) diag(1,\quad -1,\quad 1)$ 给出，假设 $\Delta(x)$ 为扭结状质量势分布。讨论了不同 $\alpha$ 参数和磁场幅度的色散关系和波函数分布的结果。我们证明了朗道能级简并性和谷简并性的提升。我们的研究结果为提出基于 $\alpha-T_3$ 相的蜂窝状和骰子晶格结构之间的任何进化阶段的谷过滤装置铺平了道路，并由外部场控制。
