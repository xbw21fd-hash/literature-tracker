## Abstract
The chiral edge states in the quantum anomalous Hall effect enable dissipationless longitudinal transport without requiring an external magnetic field, making them promising for the realization of low-power, high-speed electronic devices. However, realizing high-Chern-number quantum anomalous Hall effect with tunable edge channels and large band gaps remains challenging. Here, we propose a stable two-dimensional monolayer kagome ferromagnet, Yb2(C6H4)3, as a quantum anomalous Hall insulator with a band gap of 102.4 meV and a non-trivial Chern number\({\mathcal{C}}=-1\). Through AB-stacking of monolayer Yb2(C6H4)3, the material can exhibit tunable high Chern numbers (for example,\({\mathcal{C}}=-2\)for the bilayer) due to ferromagnetic interlayer interactions, thereby providing additional conducting channels. Additionally, these Chern numbers can be effectively modulated by an external electric field, enabling devices with electric-field-controllable conductance. This dual tunability-through both layer stacking and electric gating-establishes Yb2(C6H4)3as a potential platform for high-temperature, multi-channel topological electronics.

## 摘要（中文）
量子反常霍尔效应中的手性边缘态能够实现无耗散的纵向传输，而不需要外部磁场，这使得它们有望实现低功耗、高速电子设备。然而，实现具有可调边缘通道和大带隙的高陈数量子反常霍尔效应仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们提出了一种稳定的二维单层戈薇铁磁体 Yb2(C6H4)3，作为量子反常霍尔绝缘体，其带隙为 102.4 meV 且具有非平凡的陈数\({\mathcal{C}}=-1\)。通过单层 Yb2(C6H4)3 的 AB 堆叠，由于铁磁层间相互作用，该材料可以表现出可调节的高陈数（例如，双层的\({\mathcal{C}}=-2\)），从而提供额外的导电通道。此外，这些陈数可以通过外部电场有效地调制，从而使器件具有电场可控电导。这种通过层堆叠和电门控的双重可调性使 Yb2(C6H4)3 成为高温、多通道拓扑电子器件的潜在平台。
