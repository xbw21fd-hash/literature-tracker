## Abstract
arXiv:2511.00214v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We study the necessary condition to detect, by means of spectral modularity optimization, the ground-truth partition in networks generated according to the weighted planted-partition model with two equally sized communities. We analytically derive a general expression for the maximum level of mixing tolerated by the algorithm to retrieve community structure, showing that the value of this detectability threshold depends on the first two moments of the distributions of node degree and edge weight. We focus on the standard case of Poisson-distributed node degrees and compare the detectability thresholds of five edge-weight distributions: Dirac, Poisson, exponential, geometric, and signed Bernoulli. We show that Dirac distributed weights yield the smallest detectability threshold, while exponentially distributed weights increase the threshold by a factor $\sqrt{2}$, with other distributions exhibiting distinct behaviors that depend, either or both, on the average values of the degree and weight distributions. Our results indicate that larger variability in edge weights can make communities less detectable. In cases where edge weights carry no information about community structure, incorporating weights in community detection is detrimental.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.00214v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们研究了通过谱模块化优化来检测根据具有两个同等大小社区的加权种植分区模型生成的网络中的真实分区的必要条件。我们分析推导了检索社区结构的算法所容忍的最大混合水平的通用表达式，表明该可检测性阈值的值取决于节点度和边权重分布的前两个矩。我们关注泊松分布节点度的标准情况，并比较五种边权重分布的可检测性阈值：狄拉克分布、泊松分布、指数分布、几何分布和符号伯努利分布。我们表明，狄拉克分布权重产生最小的可检测性阈值，而指数分布权重将阈值增加 $\sqrt{2}$ 倍，而其他分布则表现出取决于度数和权重分布的平均值或两者的不同行为。我们的结果表明，边缘权重的较大变化可能会使社区更难以检测到。在边缘权重不携带有关社区结构的信息的情况下，将权重纳入社区检测是有害的。
