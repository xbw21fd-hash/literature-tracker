## Abstract
arXiv:2512.18963v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Traditional thermodynamic trade-off relations usually apply to quantities that depend linearly on probability distributions. In contrast, many important information-theoretic measures, such as entropies, are nonlinear and thus difficult to analyze with existing frameworks. Motivated by replica ideas in quantum information and spin-glass theory, we introduce \emph{replica Markov processes}, i.e., Markovian dynamics of $K$ independent, identical copies, and derive trade-off relations that bound relative moments of replica observables in terms of dynamical activity. By choosing appropriate replica observables, these inequalities translate into bounds on nonlinear quantities of the original single process. Focusing on entropic measures of uncertainty, we obtain upper bounds on the time derivative and the value of the Tsallis entropy for general trajectory-observable distributions, and corresponding bounds for diffusion on networks. We also illustrate how replicas can handle other nonlinear functionals such as extreme-value observables. Finally, we extend the construction to continuously monitored open quantum systems governed by Lindblad dynamics, where the bounds are expressed in terms of the quantum dynamical activity. These results provide entropic counterparts to activity-based uncertainty relations and establish a general method for constraining nonlinear information-theoretic quantities in stochastic and quantum thermodynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.18963v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：传统的热力学权衡关系通常适用于线性依赖于概率分布的数量。相比之下，许多重要的信息论度量（例如熵）是非线性的，因此很难用现有框架进行分析。受量子信息和自旋玻璃理论中的复制思想的启发，我们引入了 \emph{复制马尔可夫过程}，即 $K$ 独立、相同副本的马尔可夫动力学，并推导出在动力学活动方面限制复制可观测量相对矩的权衡关系。通过选择适当的复制可观测量，这些不等式转化为原始单个过程的非线性量的界限。着眼于不确定性的熵度量，我们获得了一般轨迹可观测分布的时间导数和 Tsallis 熵值的上限，以及网络上扩散的相应界限。我们还说明了副本如何处理其他非线性函数，例如极值可观测量。最后，我们将结构扩展到由 Lindblad 动力学控制的连续监控的开放量子系统，其中边界用量子动力学活动来表示。这些结果为基于活动的不确定性关系提供了熵对应物，并建立了一种约束随机和量子热力学中非线性信息论量的通用方法。
