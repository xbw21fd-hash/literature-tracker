## Abstract
arXiv:2511.09358v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We propose a protocol to generate an antiferromagnetic S=1/2 Heisenberg model with the exact ground state based on a tree graph. The generated model has a correspondence with a tree graph and possesses the product state of singlet dimers as its unique ground state. A procedure for constructing a model with exact, massively degenerate ground states is also introduced.
