## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05418v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Purpose: To accelerate MRI further, rapid B0 field modulations can be applied during oversampled readout to capture additional physical information, e.g., Wave-CAIPI, FRONSAC, local B0 coils modulations. These methods, however, introduce additional non-Fourier-encoded dimension that cannot be resolved by FFT, posing significant reconstruction challenges particularly in compressed-sensing or neural-network frameworks. Theory and methods: Because B0 modulations vary slowly relative to the oversampled ADC dwell time, we exploit this encoding redundancy by compressing k-space patch-by-patch across subregions, each of which is jointly encoded by a distinct subset of B0 and RF (receive) spatial encoding functions. For each patch, a compression matrix is computed once and reused to compress all patches encoded by the same B0/RF spatial modulations. This can be implemented by feeding subsets of B0 and RF spatial encoding maps into an adapted conventional RF array compression algorithm, mimicking an expanded set of virtual receiver channels. This approach was evaluated on ex-vivo/in-vivo human brain scans at 9.4T/3T. Results: The proposed group-patch joint compression achieves substantially higher compression factors than conventional RF-only compression, while minimally compromising encoding efficiency. Typically, joint compression factors of 11x-20x led to negligible encoding loss, dramatically reducing reconstruction time and peak memory usage. For example, compressed-sensing reconstruction took 1.4-5.1s/2D slice, 177s-10.1min/3D volume, on a high-memory CPU node. Conclusion: Given joint encoding of dynamic B0 and static RF fields, compressing multidimensional k-space patches in separate groups outperforms compressing RF receiver channels alone. This substantially mitigates a fundamental computational bottleneck that arises when combining rapid B0 and RF-receiver modulations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05418v1 公告类型：新 摘要：目的：为了进一步加速 MRI，可以在过采样读出期间应用快速 B0 场调制以捕获额外的物理信息，例如 Wave-CAIPI、FRONSAC、局部 B0 线圈调制。然而，这些方法引入了 FFT 无法解决的额外非傅里叶编码维度，带来了重大的重建挑战，特别是在压缩感知或神经网络框架中。理论和方法：由于 B0 调制相对于过采样 ADC 驻留时间变化缓慢，因此我们通过跨子区域逐块压缩 k 空间补丁来利用这种编码冗余，每个子区域都由 B0 和 RF（接收）空间编码函数的不同子集联合编码。对于每个补丁，压缩矩阵计算一次并重新用于压缩由相同 B0/RF 空间调制编码的所有补丁。这可以通过将 B0 和 RF 空间编码图的子集馈送到改编的传统 RF 阵列压缩算法中来实现，模仿一组扩展的虚拟接收器通道。该方法在 9.4T/3T 的离体/体内人脑扫描中进行了评估。结果：所提出的组补丁联合压缩比传统的仅 RF 压缩实现了更高的压缩因子，同时最小化了编码效率的影响。通常，11x-20x 的联合压缩因子导致的编码损失可以忽略不计，从而大大减少重建时间和峰值内存使用量。例如，在高内存CPU节点上，压缩感知重建需要1.4-5.1s/2D切片，177s-10.1min/3D体积。结论：给定动态 B0 和静态 RF 场的联合编码，在单独的组中压缩多维 k 空间补丁优于单独压缩 RF 接收器通道。这大大缓解了结合快速 B0 和 RF 接收器调制时出现的基本计算瓶颈。
