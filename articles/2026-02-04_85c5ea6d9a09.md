## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03724v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We analyze \emph{Parker Solar Probe} and \emph{Solar Orbiter} measurements of magnetic-field reversals (``switchbacks'') across the Alfv\'en surface ($M_a\simeq 1$), where $M_a$ is the Alfv\'en Mach number. The reported ``sub-Alfv\'enic switchback dropout'' follows from two diagnostic biases: conditioning on an instantaneous $M_a$, which is transiently elevated above unity by radial-velocity enhancements during large-amplitude Alfv\'enic rotations, and short-window local-mean backgrounds that partially track these rotations and suppress deflection angles. Treating $M_a$ as a bulk-stream property via rolling medians and referencing deflections to event-independent backgrounds -- a Parker-spiral direction or a sufficiently long rolling median -- recovers sub-Alfv\'enic switchbacks systematically. The mean deflection $\langle \theta \rangle$ separates into two regimes with $M_a$. For $M_a \lesssim 1$, $\langle \theta \rangle$ rises rapidly with weak dependence on the background window, consistent with expansion-driven amplification of Alfv\'enic fluctuations. For $M_a \gtrsim 1$, the evolution becomes scale dependent: large-scale $\langle \theta \rangle$ continues to grow with $M_a$ at reduced rate, while small-scale growth saturates, consistent with turbulent decay and dissipation. Collectively, these results indicate that switchbacks need not originate only in the super-Alfv\'enic solar wind. Instead, they are consistent with a formation pathway in which coronal fluctuations are amplified by large-scale expansion through the sub-Alfv\'enic regime, with subsequent propagation into the super-Alfv\'enic wind where turbulent decay modifies their scale-dependent properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03724v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们分析了 \emph{Parker Solar Probe} 和 \emph{Solar Orbiter} 对 Alfv\'en 表面 ($M_a\simeq 1$) 磁场反转（“之字形”）的测量结果，其中 $M_a$ 是 Alfv\'en 马赫数。报告的“亚Alfv\'enic折返辍学”源于两个诊断偏差：以瞬时$M_a$为条件，在大振幅Alfv\'enic旋转期间，该瞬时$M_a$通过径向速度增强而暂时升高到统一之上，以及部分跟踪这些旋转并抑制偏转角的短窗口局部平均背景。通过滚动中位数将 $M_a$ 视为批量流属性，并参考与事件无关的背景的偏转（帕克螺旋方向或足够长的滚动中位数），系统地恢复亚阿尔夫夫\'enic之字形。平均偏转 $\langle \theta \rangle$ 通过 $M_a$ 分成两个区域。对于 $M_a \lesssim 1$，$\langle \theta \rangle$ 快速上升，对背景窗口的依赖性较弱，与 Alfv\'enic 波动的扩张驱动放大一致。对于 $M_a \gtrsim 1$，演化变得依赖于尺度：大规模 $\langle \theta \rangle$ 继续随 $M_a$ 以降低的速率增长，而小规模增长饱和，与湍流衰减和耗散一致。总的来说，这些结果表明之字形不一定仅起源于超阿尔夫太阳风。相反，它们与形成路径一致，在该路径中，日冕波动通过亚阿尔弗风区的大规模扩张而被放大，随后传播到超阿尔弗风风中，其中湍流衰变改变了它们的尺度相关特性。
