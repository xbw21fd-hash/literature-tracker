## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05091v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Autonomous mission planning for Active Debris Removal (ADR) must balance efficiency, adaptability, and strict feasibility constraints on fuel and mission duration. This work compares three planners for the constrained multi-debris rendezvous problem in Low Earth Orbit: a nominal Masked Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) policy trained under fixed mission parameters, a domain-randomized Masked PPO policy trained across varying mission constraints for improved robustness, and a plain Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) baseline. Evaluations are conducted in a high-fidelity orbital simulation with refueling, realistic transfer dynamics, and randomized debris fields across 300 test cases in nominal, reduced fuel, and reduced mission time scenarios. Results show that nominal PPO achieves top performance when conditions match training but degrades sharply under distributional shift, while domain-randomized PPO exhibits improved adaptability with only moderate loss in nominal performance. MCTS consistently handles constraint changes best due to online replanning but incurs orders-of-magnitude higher computation time. The findings underline a trade-off between the speed of learned policies and the adaptability of search-based methods, and suggest that combining training-time diversity with online planning could be a promising path for future resilient ADR mission planners.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05091v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：主动碎片清除 (ADR) 的自主任务规划必须平衡效率、适应性以及对燃料和任务持续时间的严格可行性限制。这项工作比较了近地轨道中受限多碎片交会问题的三种规划器：在固定任务参数下训练的名义屏蔽近端策略优化（PPO）策略、在不同任务约束下训练的域随机屏蔽 PPO 策略以提高鲁棒性，以及简单的蒙特卡罗树搜索（MCTS）基线。评估是在高保真轨道模拟中进行的，其中包括加油、真实传输动力学和随机碎片场，在标称、减少燃料和减少任务时间场景下的 300 个测试案例中进行。结果表明，名义 PPO 在条件匹配训练时实现最佳性能，但在分布变化下急剧下降，而域随机 PPO 表现出更高的适应性，而名义性能仅适度损失。由于在线重新规划，MCTS 始终能够最好地处理约束变化，但会导致计算时间增加几个数量级。研究结果强调了学习政策的速度和基于搜索的方法的适应性之间的权衡，并表明将培训时间多样性与在线规划相结合可能是未来弹性 ADR 任务规划者的一条有希望的道路。
