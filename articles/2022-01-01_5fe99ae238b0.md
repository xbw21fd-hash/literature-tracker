## Abstract
TLDR: An important group of MLPs relying on artificial neural networks to establish a mapping from the atomic structure to the potential energy are discussed, which concern the dimensionality of the systems, the inclusion of long-range electrostatic interactions, global phenomena like nonlocal charge transfer, and the type of descriptor used to represent the Atomic structure.
