# A comparison of Fraunhofer-type diffraction from an atomic single-slit and a molecular double-slit

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17797v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We measured the Q-value and the scattering angle distributions for non-dissociative state selective single electron capture in collisions of 7.5 keV H$^+$ and 15 keV H$_2^+$ with He. The experimental data are compared with semiclassical close-coupling calculations and predictions from the classical trajectory Monte Carlo simulations. By analogy with Fraunhofer diffraction, we also developed a toy model to reconstruct an imaginary screen that reflects the reaction impact-parameter dependence, in channels where the magnetic quantum number remains unchanged. It is well established that H$_2^+$ acts as a molecular double-slit in scattering processes. By demodulating the Young's double-slit-type interference pattern, we extracted the individual slit diffraction pattern of H$_2^+$ and compared it with that of the H$^+$ atomic single-slit. For ground state electron capture, we found that the single and the double slit diffraction patterns have equal fringe width, whereas for excited state electron capture, diffraction patterns are quite different.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.17797v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们测量了 7.5 keV H$^+$ 和 15 keV H$_2^+$ 与 He 碰撞中非解离态选择性单电子捕获的 Q 值和散射角分布。将实验数据与半经典紧耦合计算和经典轨迹蒙特卡罗模拟的预测进行比较。通过与弗劳恩霍夫衍射进行类比，我们还开发了一个玩具模型来重建一个假想的屏幕，该屏幕反映了在磁量子数保持不变的通道中反应影响参数的依赖性。众所周知，H$_2^+$ 在散射过程中充当分子双缝。通过解调杨氏双缝型干涉图样，我们提取了H$_2^+$的单缝衍射图样，并将其与H$^+$原子单缝衍射图样进行了比较。对于基态电子捕获，我们发现单缝和双缝衍射图案具有相同的条纹宽度，而对于激发态电子捕获，衍射图案则有很大不同。
