## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13582v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Multiphase fluid flows comprising of mesoscale solid particles, liquid droplets, or gas bubbles are common in both natural and man-made systems, but quantifying the energy transfer is challenging due to complex bubble-particle interactions. In this study, we used ultrafast synchrotron X-ray imaging to study the mesoscale dynamic interactions among ultrasonic cavitation bubbles and hydrophobic particles or clusters. Critical dynamic information and data were extracted from the vast amount of X-ray images and then fed into the hybrid analytical-numerical model for calculating the energy transfer from the oscillating bubble and the imploding bubble to the nearby hydrophobic particles. Using the Ni spherical microparticles as an example, at bubble oscillation approximately 16% (80-320 nJ) of the local energy was transferred to the particle. At bubble implosion, the transferred energy increased approximately 26% (0.135-1.09 uJ). Local energy transfer occurred on timescales of 1 us to 1 ms and length scales of 1 um to 1 mm. Within each ultrasound cycle, kinetic and potential energy underwent complex exchanges, with local energy exhibiting a stepwise decay at the end of each cycle. The transferred energy was mainly consumed for enabling highly efficient particle dispersion. This research provides quantitative insights into optimizing hydrophobic nanomaterial dispersion and has broader implications for interfacial energy transfer processes such as making suspensions, composite materials and exfoliated 2D materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13582v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：由中尺度固体颗粒、液滴或气泡组成的多相流体流在自然和人造系统中都很常见，但由于复杂的气泡-颗粒相互作用，量化能量传递具有挑战性。在本研究中，我们使用超快同步加速器X射线成像来研究超声空化气泡与疏水颗粒或团簇之间的介观动态相互作用。从大量 X 射线图像中提取关键的动态信息和数据，然后输入混合分析数值模型，用于计算从振荡气泡和内爆气泡到附近疏水颗粒的能量转移。以 Ni 球形微粒为例，气泡振荡时，大约 16% (80-320 nJ) 的局部能量转移到颗粒上。气泡内爆时，传递的能量增加了约 26% (0.135-1.09 uJ)。局部能量转移发生在 1 us 至 1 ms 的时间尺度和 1 um 至 1 mm 的长度尺度上。在每个超声周期内，动能和势能经历复杂的交换，局部能量在每个周期结束时表现出逐步衰减。转移的能量主要用于实现高效的颗粒分散。这项研究为优化疏水性纳米材料分散提供了定量见解，并对界面能量转移过程（例如制备悬浮液、复合材料和剥离二维材料）具有更广泛的影响。
