## Abstract
Electrolyte design has played a crucial role in the development of lithium batteries for a range of applications. Of late, there is a renewed interest in developing a new generation of aqueous batteries to cheaply store energy for the grid. Given the amount of research our community has carried out developing lithium electrolytes, an intriguing possibility is to leverage these learnings to accelerate the development of aqueous electrolytes - especially when battery development typically requires decades of effort. We herein examine this argument to guide aqueous battery electrolyte research. We discuss electrolyte design heuristics, transport mechanisms across molecular and continuum scales, and the criteria assessing electrolyte advances for improved ion transport.

## 摘要（中文）
电解质设计在一系列应用的锂电池的开发中发挥了至关重要的作用。最近，人们重新燃起了开发新一代水性电池的兴趣，以廉价地为电网储存能量。考虑到我们社区在锂电解质开发方面所做的大量研究，一个有趣的可能性是利用这些知识来加速水性电解质的开发——特别是当电池开发通常需要数十年的努力时。我们在此研究这一论点以指导水性电池电解质的研究。我们讨论了电解质设计启发法、跨分子和连续尺度的传输机制，以及评估电解质改进以改善离子传输的标准。
