## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19560v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnetism (AM), a collinear magnetic phase with momentum-dependent spin splitting, is a promising candidate for strong magnetoelectric coupling. However, realizing direct and tunable coupling between ferroelectricity (FE) and AM within a single two-dimensional (2D) material remains an outstanding challenge. Here, based on first-principles calculations, we identify the distorted phase of monolayer Janus VOClBr as an intrinsic 2D FE-AM. This phase demonstrates robust magnetoelectric coupling, as evidenced by a complete reversal of momentum-space spin polarization upon FE switching, and further supported by spin texture analysis and the magneto-optical Kerr effect. Notably, the FE properties are highly strain-tunable: biaxial compression strain of -4% reduces the FE polarization switching barrier by approximately 87%, whereas a tensile strain of +3% induces a phase transition to an antiferromagnet. Leveraging the lock-in between the electrically controlled spin texture and the magneto-optical Kerr effect signal, we propose a non-volatile, polymorphic spintronic memory device featuring all-electrical writing and optical readout. This work establishes 2D FE-AMs as a versatile platform for coupled ferroic orders and paves the way for voltage-controlled, multifunctional spin-logic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19560v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交变磁性 (AM) 是一种具有动量相关自旋分裂的共线磁相，是强磁电耦合的有前途的候选者。然而，在单一二维 (2D) 材料中实现铁电 (FE) 和 AM 之间的直接且可调谐的耦合仍然是一个突出的挑战。在这里，基于第一性原理计算，我们将单层 Janus VOClBr 的扭曲相位识别为本征 2D FE-AM。这一阶段展示了强大的磁电耦合，如有限元切换时动量空间自旋极化的完全反转所证明的那样，并得到自旋纹理分析和磁光克尔效应的进一步支持。值得注意的是，FE 属性具有高度应变可调性：-4% 的双轴压缩应变可将 FE 极化切换势垒降低约 87%，而 +3% 的拉伸应变会引发向反铁磁体的相变。利用电控自旋纹理和磁光克尔效应信号之间的锁定，我们提出了一种具有全电写入和光学读出功能的非易失性、多态自旋电子存储器件。这项工作将 2D FE-AM 建立为耦合铁序的多功能平台，并为压控多功能自旋逻辑器件铺平了道路。
