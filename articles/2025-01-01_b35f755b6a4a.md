## Abstract
TLDR: This work builds the dataset aiming to achieve ``massive atomic diversity'' (MAD) by aggressively modifying these structures and utilizing highly consistent electronic-structure settings for property calculations, and introduces low-dimensional structural latent space descriptors that can be used as a general-purpose materials cartography tool.
