## Abstract
arXiv:2509.03955v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Beam monitoring is crucial for particle accelerators to achieve high luminosity. We describe an upgrade of the LABM, a beam monitoring device utilizing observation of the beamstrahlung, radiation emitted by a beam of charged particles when it accelerates in the electromagnetic field of another beam of charged particles.
