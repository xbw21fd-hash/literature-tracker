## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00643v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: As quantum computing transitions from theoretical physics to engineering applications, there is a growing need for accessible simulation tools that bridge the gap between abstract linear algebra and practical implementation. While text-based frameworks (like Qiskit or Cirq) are standard, they often present a steep learning curve for students and engineers accustomed to graphical system design. This paper introduces QuVI (Quantum Virtual Instrument), an open-source quantum circuit toolkit developed natively within the NI LabVIEW environment. Moving beyond initial proof-of-concept models, QuVI establishes a robust framework that leverages LabVIEW's "dataflow" paradigm-where wires represent data and nodes represent operations-to provide an intuitive, visual analog to standard quantum circuit notation while enabling the seamless integration of classical control structures like loops and conditionals. The toolkit's capabilities are demonstrated through the construction and visualization of fundamental quantum algorithms, verifying results against theoretical predictions. By translating "Block Diagrams" directly into quantum state evolutions ("Bloch Spheres"), QuVI offers educators and researchers a powerful platform for prototyping quantum logic without leaving the graphical engineering workspace.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00643v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：随着量子计算从理论物理向工程应用的转变，人们越来越需要可访问的模拟工具来弥合抽象线性代数和实际实现之间的差距。虽然基于文本的框架（如 Qiskit 或 Cirq）是标准的，但它们通常会给习惯图形系统设计的学生和工程师带来陡峭的学习曲线。本文介绍了 QuVI（量子虚拟仪器），这是一个在 NI LabVIEW 环境中本地开发的开源量子电路工具包。 QuVI 超越了最初的概念验证模型，建立了一个强大的框架，利用 LabVIEW 的“数据流”范式（其中连线代表数据，节点代表操作）为标准量子电路符号提供直观、可视化的模拟，同时实现循环和条件等经典控制结构的无缝集成。该工具包的功能通过基本量子算法的构建和可视化来展示，并根据理论预测验证结果。通过将“框图”直接转换为量子态演化（“布洛赫球体”），QuVI 为教育工作者和研究人员提供了一个强大的平台，无需离开图形工程工作区即可对量子逻辑进行原型设计。
