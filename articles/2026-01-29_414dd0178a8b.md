## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20836v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The layer-dependent Hamiltonians of parallel-stacked MoTe$_2$ and WSe$_2$ homobilayer moir\'e materials are topologically non-trivial, both in real space and in momentum space, and have been shown to support integer and fractional quantum anomalous Hall states, as well as antiferromagnetic and superconducting states. Here, we address the interplay between the antiferromagnetic and superconducting states observed in tWSe$_2$ when the Fermi level is close to its $M$-point van Hove singularity and the displacement field is small. We combine DFT with path-integrals to construct a minimal moir\'e band model that accounts for lattice relaxation along the $c$-axis and perform Hartree-Fock calculations to identify competing charge and spin ordered states. For tWSe$_2$ at $\theta=2.7^\circ$ and $\theta=3.65^\circ$, we find that a layer antiferromagnet (AFM), a stripe spin-density-wave (SDW), and the ferromagnetic Chern insulator (FM) are the primary candidates for the ground state at zero displacement field, and argue that antiferromagnetic spin interactions on the next neighbor bond $J_2$ can induce a time-reversal symmetry breaking chiral superconducting state.

arXiv:2601.20836v1 公告类型：新 摘要：平行堆叠的 MoTe$_2$ 和 WSe$_2$ 同双层云纹材料的层相关哈密顿量在实空间和动量空间中都是拓扑非平凡的，并且已被证明支持整数和分数量子反常霍尔态，以及反铁磁和超导态。在这里，我们讨论了当费米能级接近其 $M$ 点范霍夫奇点且位移场较小时在 tWSe$_2$ 中观察到的反铁磁态和超导态之间的相互作用。我们将 DFT 与路径积分结合起来构建最小莫尔带模型，该模型解释沿 $c$ 轴的晶格弛豫，并执行 Hartree-Fock 计算来识别竞争电荷和自旋有序态。对于 $\theta=2.7^\circ$ 和 $\theta=3.65^\circ$ 处的 tWSe$_2$，我们发现层反铁磁体 (AFM)、条带自旋密度波 (SDW) 和铁磁陈绝缘体 (FM) 是零位移场基态的主要候选者，并认为下一个相邻键 $J_2$ 上的反铁磁自旋相互作用可以引起时间反转对称性破缺的手性超导态。
