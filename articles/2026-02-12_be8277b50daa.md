## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10896v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum Electrodynamics predicts that the vacuum must behave as a nonlinear optical medium:the vacuum optical index should increase when vacuum is stressed by intense electromagnetic fields.The DeLLight (Deflection of Light by Light) project aims to measure it by using intense and ultra-short laser pulses delivered by the LASERIX facility at IJCLab (Paris-Saclay University). Theprinciple is to measure by interferometry the deflection of a low-intensity probe pulse when crossingthe vacuum optical index gradient produced by an external high-intensity pump pulse. The detectionof the expected signal requires measuring the position of the interference intensity profile with a highspatial resolution, limited by the ultimate quantum noise. However, the spatial resolution is highlydegraded by the phase noise induced by the mechanical vibrations of the interferometer. In order tosuppress this interferometric phase noise, we have developed a new method, named High-FrequencyPhase Noise Suppression (HFPNS) method, based on the use of a delayed reference signal to correctany noise-related signal appearing in the probe beam. In this work, we present the experimentalvalidation of this novel method. The results demonstrate a robust path toward picometer-scalesensitivity and provide a key step toward the observation of QED-induced vacuum refraction.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10896v1 公告类型：新 摘要：量子电动力学预测真空必须表现为非线性光学介质：当真空受到强电磁场的压力时，真空光学指数应该增加。DeLLight（光的偏转）项目旨在通过使用 IJCLab（巴黎萨克雷大学）的 LASERIX 设施提供的强超短激光脉冲来测量它。其原理是通过干涉测量法测量低强度探测脉冲穿过外部高强度泵浦脉冲产生的真空光学折射率梯度时的偏转。预期信号的检测需要以高空间分辨率测量干涉强度分布的位置，但受到最终量子噪声的限制。然而，干涉仪机械振动引起的相位噪声严重降低了空间分辨率。为了抑制这种干涉相位噪声，我们开发了一种新方法，称为高频相位噪声抑制（HFPNS）方法，该方法基于使用延迟参考信号来校正探测光束中出现的任何与噪声相关的信号。在这项工作中，我们提出了这种新方法的实验验证。结果证明了实现皮米级灵敏度的可靠途径，并为观察 QED 引起的真空折射迈出了关键一步。
