## Abstract
Author(s): Mei-Xiong Zhu, Yun-Long Tang, Yin-Lian Zhu, Xiu-Liang Ma, and Yu-Jia Wang The authors introduce here an extended phase-field model with a biquadratic polarization-gradient coupling that enables the spontaneous emergence of Bloch-type polarization in ferroelectric skyrmion bubbles. Using SrTiO 3 /PbTiO 3 /SrTiO 3 heterostructures as a model system, the authors find a coupling-driven transition from achiral to chiral skyrmions and ultimately to mixed-chiral bubbles. While random initial states yield racemic chirality, homochiral vortex precursors deterministically imprint uniform skyrmion handedness under an applied electric field. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 014118] Published Wed Jan 28, 2026

## 摘要（中文）
作者：Mei-Xiong Zhu、Yun-Long Tang、Yin-Lian Zhu、Xiu-Liang Ma 和 Yu-Jia Wang 作者在此介绍了一种具有双二次极化梯度耦合的扩展相场模型，该模型能够在铁电斯格明子气泡中自发出现布洛赫型极化。作者使用 SrTiO 3 /PbTiO 3 /SrTiO 3 异质结构作为模型系统，发现了从非手性到手性斯格明子并最终到混合手性气泡的耦合驱动转变。虽然随机初始状态产生外消旋手性，但同手性涡旋前体在施加的电场下确定性地印记均匀的斯格明子旋向性。 [物理。 Rev. B 113, 014118] 2026 年 1 月 28 日星期三发布
