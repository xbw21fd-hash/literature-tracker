## Abstract
arXiv:2509.18845v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Classical spin liquids are disordered magnetic phases, governed by local constraints that often give rise to flat-band ground states. When constraints take the form of a zero-divergence field within a cluster of spins, the spin liquid is often described by an emergent Coulomb gauge theory. Here we introduce an interaction $\eta$ between these clusters of spins which compete with the zero-divergence field. Using a framework embracing both the connectivity matrices of graph theory and the topology of band structures, we develop a generic theory of interacting-cluster Hamiltonians. We show how flat bands remain at zero energy up to finite interaction $\eta$, until a dispersive band becomes negative, stabilizing a spiral spin liquid with a hypersurface of ground-state manifold in reciprocal space. This hypersurface can be interpreted as an effective Fermi surface in the spectrum of the parent system, acting as a tunable energy selector despite the absence of particle filling. This effective Fermi surface serves as a mold for the apparition of the half-moon patterns in the equal-time structure factor. Our generic approach enables to extend the notion of half moons to the perturbation of higher-rank Coulomb fields and pinch-line spin liquids. In particular, multi-fold half moons appear when unconventional gauge charges, such as potential fractons, are stabilized in the ground state. Finally, half-moon phases can be tuned across the equivalent of a Lifshitz transition, when the hypersurface manifold changes topology.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.18845v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：经典自旋液体是无序磁相，受局部约束控制，通常会产生平带基态。当约束采取自旋簇内的零发散场的形式时，自旋液体通常由新兴的库仑规范理论来描述。在这里，我们引入了这些与零散度场竞争的自旋簇之间的相互作用$\eta$。使用包含图论连通矩阵和能带结构拓扑的框架，我们开发了相互作用簇哈密顿量的通用理论。我们展示了平带如何在有限相互作用 $\eta$ 下保持为零能量，直到色散带变为负值，从而稳定具有倒易空间中基态流形超曲面的螺旋自旋液体。该超曲面可以解释为母系统光谱中的有效费米面，尽管没有粒子填充，但仍充当可调能量选择器。该有效费米面充当等时结构因子中半月图案显现的模具。我们的通用方法能够将半月的概念扩展到更高阶库仑场和夹线自旋液体的扰动。特别是，当非常规规范电荷（例如潜在分形子）稳定在基态时，会出现多重半月。最后，当超曲面流形改变拓扑时，半月相可以在相当于 Lifshitz 跃迁的范围内进行调整。
