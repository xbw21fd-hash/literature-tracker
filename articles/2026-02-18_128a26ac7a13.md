## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15592v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spatiotemporal flows govern diverse phenomena across physics, biology, and engineering, yet modelling their multiscale dynamics remains a central challenge. Despite major advances in physics-informed machine learning, existing approaches struggle to simultaneously maintain long-term temporal evolution and resolve fine-scale structure across chaotic, turbulent, and physiological regimes. Here, we introduce Uni-Flow, a unified autoregressive-diffusion framework that explicitly separates temporal evolution from spatial refinement for modelling complex dynamical systems. The autoregressive component learns low-resolution latent dynamics that preserve large-scale structure and ensure stable long-horizon rollouts, while the diffusion component reconstructs high-resolution physical fields, recovering fine-scale features in a small number of denoising steps. We validate Uni-Flow across canonical benchmarks, including two-dimensional Kolmogorov flow, three-dimensional turbulent channel inflow generation with a quantum-informed autoregressive prior, and patient-specific simulations of aortic coarctation derived from high-fidelity lattice Boltzmann hemodynamic solvers. In the cardiovascular setting, Uni-Flow enables task-level faster than real-time inference of pulsatile hemodynamics, reconstructing high-resolution pressure fields over physiologically relevant time horizons in seconds rather than hours. By transforming high-fidelity hemodynamic simulation from an offline, HPC-bound process into a deployable surrogate, Uni-Flow establishes a pathway to faster-than-real-time modelling of complex multiscale flows, with broad implications for scientific machine learning in flow physics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15592v1 公告类型：新 摘要：时空流支配着物理学、生物学和工程学中的多种现象，但对其多尺度动力学建模仍然是一个核心挑战。尽管基于物理的机器学习取得了重大进展，但现有方法难以同时维持长期时间演化并解决混沌、湍流和生理状态下的精细尺度结构。在这里，我们介绍 Uni-Flow，一个统一的自回归扩散框架，它明确地将时间演化与空间细化分开，以建模复杂的动力系统。自回归组件学习低分辨率潜在动力学，以保留大规模结构并确保稳定的长范围推出，而扩散组件则重建高分辨率物理场，以少量去噪步骤恢复精细尺度特征。我们跨规范基准验证 Uni-Flow，包括二维 Kolmogorov 流、具有量子信息自回归先验的三维湍流通道流入生成，以及源自高保真格玻尔兹曼血流动力学求解器的主动脉缩窄患者特定模拟。在心血管环境中，Uni-Flow 能够比脉动血流动力学的实时推断更快地实现任务级别，在几秒而不是几小时内重建生理相关时间范围内的高分辨率压力场。通过将高保真血流动力学模拟从离线的 HPC 绑定过程转变为可部署的替代方案，Uni-Flow 建立了一条比实时更快的复杂多尺度流建模途径，对流物理学中的科学机器学习具有广泛的影响。
