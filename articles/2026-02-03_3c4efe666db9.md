## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02210v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a method for absolute calibration of received radio-frequency in the ultra low frequency (ULF), and very low frequency (VLF) range. This is achieved with the use of a radio frequency optically pumped magnetometer (RF-OPM). We describe a method using an optically pumped sample where the RF broadening of the Cs magnetic resonance allows the magnitude of the received field to be calibrated against the ground-state gyromagnetic ratio of the Cs atoms. This frequency-based calibration avoids the geometric and electrostatic response functions that affect inductive sensors, such as fluxgates, search coils, and SQUID magnetometers. We demonstrate calibration of magnetic measurement using oscillating magnetic fields in the 300 Hz - 20 kHz range and a sensor noise floor of 15 fT.Hz-1/2. This radio-frequency sensor may be used as a widely tunable narrowband receiver for communication, ranging, or penetrative conductivity imaging.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02210v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种在超低频 (ULF) 和甚低频 (VLF) 范围内对接收射频进行绝对校准的方法。这是通过使用射频光泵磁力计 (RF-OPM) 来实现的。我们描述了一种使用光泵浦样品的方法，其中铯磁共振的射频展宽允许根据铯原子的基态旋磁比来校准接收场的大小。这种基于频率的校准避免了影响感应传感器（例如磁通门、搜索线圈和 SQUID 磁力计）的几何和静电响应函数。我们使用 300 Hz - 20 kHz 范围内的振荡磁场和 15 fT.Hz-1/2 的传感器本底噪声演示了磁性测量的校准。该射频传感器可用作广泛可调的窄带接收器，用于通信、测距或穿透式电导率成像。
