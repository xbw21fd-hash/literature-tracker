## Abstract
arXiv:2511.08466v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Van der Waals (vdW) superconductors remain superconducting down to the monolayer limit, enabling the exploration of emergent physical phenomena and functionality driven by reduced dimensionality. Here, we report the characterization of the kinetic inductance of atomically thin NbSe$_2$, a two-dimensional van der Waals superconductor, using superconducting coplanar waveguides and microwave measurement techniques familiar to circuit quantum electrodynamics (cQED). The kinetic inductance scales inversely with the number of NbSe$_2$ layers, reaching 1.2 nH/$\Box$ in the monolayer limit. Furthermore, the measured kinetic inductance exhibits a thickness-dependent crossover from clean- to dirty-limit behavior, with enhanced dirty-limit contributions emerging in the ultra-thin regime. These effects are likely driven by increased surface scattering, multi-band superconductivity, and geometric confinement. Additionally, the self-Kerr nonlinearity of the NbSe$_2$ films ranges from $K/2\pi$ = -0.008 to -14.7 Hz/photon, indicating its strong potential in applications requiring compact, nearly linear, high-inductance superconducting quantum devices and detectors. The fabrication and characterization techniques demonstrated here are extensible to the investigation of other two-dimensional superconductors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.08466v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：范德华 (vdW) 超导体在低至单层极限的情况下仍保持超导性，从而能够探索由降维驱动的新兴物理现象和功能。在这里，我们使用电路量子电动力学 (cQED) 熟悉的超导共面波导和微波测量技术，报告了原子薄 NbSe$_2$（一种二维范德华超导体）的动态电感的表征。动态电感与 NbSe$_2$ 层数成反比，在单层极限下达到 1.2 nH/$\Box$。此外，测量的动态电感表现出从清洁极限到脏极限行为的厚度依赖性交叉，并且在超薄区域中出现了增强的脏极限贡献。这些效应可能是由表面散射增加、多带超导性和几何限制驱动的。此外，NbSe$_2$ 薄膜的自克尔非线性范围为 $K/2\pi$ = -0.008 至 -14.7 Hz/光子，表明其在需要紧凑、近线性、高电感超导量子器件和探测器的应用中具有强大的潜力。这里演示的制造和表征技术可以扩展到其他二维超导体的研究。
