## Abstract
arXiv:2509.03941v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Motivated by the recent observation of giant room-temperature magnon spin conductivity in an ultrathin ferromagnetic insulator [X.-Y. Wei et al., Nat. Mater. 21, 1352 (2022)], we investigate thickness-dependent magnon spin transport in thin antiferromagnetic insulators (AFIs). We study the prototypical AFI hematite, known for its exceptionally low magnetic damping and two distinct magnetic phases: a low-temperature uniaxial easy-axis phase and a high-temperature biaxial easy-plane phase. Using stochastic micromagnetic simulations, we investigate thickness-dependent magnon spin transport across both magnetic phases. Our results uncover a crossover from quasi-three-dimensional to quasi-two-dimensional magnon spin transport at a critical thickness, determined by the frequency or energy of the excited magnons. Below this critical thickness, we observe a pronounced enhancement in the magnon diffusion length in both magnetic phases. This rise is attributed to a change in the effective magnon density of states, reflecting the reduced phase space available for scattering in the thinner, quasi-two-dimensional regime. Understanding and controlling long-distance magnon spin transport in AFIs is crucial for developing next-generation spintronic nanodevices, especially as materials approach the two-dimensional limit.

arXiv:2509.03941v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：受到最近在超薄铁磁绝缘体中观察到巨型室温磁振子自旋电导率的启发 [X.-Y.魏等人，纳特。马特。 21, 1352 (2022)]，我们研究了薄反铁磁绝缘体（AFI）中厚度相关的磁振子自旋输运。我们研究了典型的 AFI 赤铁矿，该赤铁矿以其极低的磁阻尼和两个不同的磁相而闻名：低温单轴易轴相和高温双轴易平面相。使用随机微磁模拟，我们研究了两个磁相上依赖于厚度的磁振子自旋输运。我们的结果揭示了临界厚度处从准三维到准二维磁振子自旋输运的交叉，该临界厚度由受激磁振子的频率或能量决定。低于这个临界厚度，我们观察到两个磁相中磁振子扩散长度的显着增强。这种上升归因于有效磁振子密度的变化，反映了在更薄的准二维区域中可用于散射的相空间减少。了解和控制 AFI 中的长距离磁振子自旋输运对于开发下一代自旋电子纳米器件至关重要，特别是当材料接近二维极限时。
