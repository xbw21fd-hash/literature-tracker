# How to Engage Active Pedagogy with Physics Faculty: Watch Out for Powerlessness

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02493v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Despite the large body of research showing that students in STEM classes at all levels learn better via active learning than they do via lecture, post-secondary physics and astronomy (P&A) faculty members continue to primarily use teacher-focused, lecture pedagogy in their classes. Methods include answers from eight faculty members, and interviews with five faculty members who self-identified as primarily using lecture were conducted to determine their perceptions of why they use lecture. During analysis coding, results show that an unanticipated theme not sufficiently represented in the pre-existing literature rose to the forefront: that many of these faculty members feel the decision of pedagogy is out of their control. In conclusion, a grounded theory was developed and is proposed herein that these faculty feel a sense of powerlessness. Reasons offered include administrators often make decisions based on the financial needs of the school, which then force the faculty into using lecture as their primary pedagogy. Implications include that providing professional development in active pedagogies may not be sufficient to help faculty members change pedagogy, as they may need to be convinced that they have the power to make change and use student-centered, active learning pedagogies within their own individual constraints and settings. Understanding that some instructors may feel powerless in choosing how to teach is an important step for professional development providers toward ensuring that faculty have a voice and can choose the best teaching methods for their classrooms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02493v1 公告类型：新 摘要：尽管大量研究表明，各级 STEM 课程的学生通过主动学习比通过讲座学习得更好，但中学后物理和天文学 (P&A) 的教职员工仍然在课堂上主要使用以教师为中心的讲座教学法。方法包括八名教员的回答，并对五名自认为主要使用讲座的教员进行访谈，以确定他们对为什么使用讲座的看法。在分析编码过程中，结果表明，现有文献中未充分体现的一个未预料到的主题上升到了最前沿：许多教职人员认为教学法的决定超出了他们的控制范围。总之，本文提出并提出了一个扎根的理论，即这些教师感到一种无力感。提供的理由包括管理人员经常根据学校的财务需求做出决定，从而迫使教师使用讲座作为主要教学法。其含义包括，提供主动教学法的专业发展可能不足以帮助教师改变教学法，因为他们可能需要确信自己有能力做出改变，并在自己的个人限制和环境内使用以学生为中心的主动学习教学法。对于专业发展提供者来说，了解一些教师可能会在选择教学方式方面感到无能为力，这是确保教师拥有发言权并能够为课堂选择最佳教学方法的重要一步。
