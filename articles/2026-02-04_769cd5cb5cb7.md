## Abstract
arXiv:2510.24564v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Kagome metals are an intriguing class of quantum materials as the presence of both flat bands and Dirac points provides access to functional properties present in strongly correlated and topological materials. To fully harness these electronic features, the ability to tune the Fermi level relative to the band positions is needed. Here we explore the structural, electronic and magnetic impacts of substitutional alloying within ferromagnetic kagome metal Fe3Sn2 in thin films grown by molecular beam epitaxy. Transition metals Mn and Co are chosen as substitutes for Fe to reduce or increase the d-band electron count, thereby moving the Fermi level accordingly. We find that Co is not incorporated into the Fe3Sn2 structure but instead results in a two-phase Fe-Co and (Fe,Co)Sn composite. In contrast, Fe3-xMnxSn2 films are realized with x up to 1.0, retaining crystalline quality comparable to the parent phase. The incorporation of Mn repositions the flat bands relative to the Fermi level in a manner consistent with hole-doping, as revealed by hard x-ray photoemission and density functional theory. The Fe3-xMnxSn2 films retain room temperature ferromagnetism, with x-ray magnetic circular dichroism measurements confirming that the Fe and Mn moments are ferromagnetically aligned. The ability to hole-dope this magnetic kagome metal provides a platform for tuning properties such as anomalous Hall and Nernst responses.

arXiv:2510.24564v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：Kagome 金属是一类有趣的量子材料，因为平带和狄拉克点的存在提供了对强相关和拓扑材料中存在的功能特性的访问。为了充分利用这些电子功能，需要能够相对于能带位置调整费米能级。在这里，我们探讨了分子束外延生长的薄膜中铁磁 kagome 金属 Fe3Sn2 中替代合金化的结构、电子和磁性影响。选择过渡金属 Mn 和 Co 作为 Fe 的替代品，以减少或增加 d 带电子数，从而相应地移动费米能级。我们发现 Co 没有并入 Fe3Sn2 结构中，而是产生了两相 Fe-Co 和 (Fe,Co)Sn 复合材料。相比之下，Fe3-xMnxSn2 薄膜的 x 高达 1.0，保持了与母相相当的结晶质量。正如硬 X 射线光电子发射和密度泛函理论所揭示的那样，Mn 的掺入以与空穴掺杂一致的方式相对于费米能级重新定位了平带。 Fe3-xMnxSn2 薄膜保留了室温铁磁性，X 射线磁圆二色性测量证实 Fe 和 Mn 矩是铁磁性排列的。这种磁性戈薇金属的空穴掺杂能力为调节反常霍尔和能斯特响应等特性提供了一个平台。
