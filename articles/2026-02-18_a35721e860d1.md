## Abstract
Author(s): Sergey Prosandeev and L. Bellaiche By using atomistic simulations, the authors discover here that some complex ferroelectric materials behave in a similar fashion when driven out of equilibrium by applying terahertz electric pulses, even though these materials have different initial, intermediate (hidden) and final states. Five different temporal regions occur in all of them, with one of them exhibiting negative capacitance, which is forbidden under equilibrium. A D-like shape of the polarization-versus-electric field loop is also reported, along with the origin of these unusual features. The present results may guide the design of a variety of original devices, including for neuromorphic computing and novel electric circuits. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 064111] Published Wed Feb 18, 2026

## 摘要（中文）
作者：Sergey Prosandeev 和 L. Bellaiche 通过使用原子模拟，作者发现一些复杂的铁电材料在通过太赫兹电脉冲失去平衡时表现出类似的行为，即使这些材料具有不同的初始状态、中间状态（隐藏状态）和最终状态。所有这些区域都存在五个不同的时间区域，其中一个区域表现出负电容，这在平衡状态下是被禁止的。还报道了类似 D 形状的极化与电场环路，以及这些不寻常特征的起源。目前的结果可以指导各种原始设备的设计，包括神经形态计算和新颖的电路。 [物理。 Rev. B 113, 064111] 2026 年 2 月 18 日星期三发布
