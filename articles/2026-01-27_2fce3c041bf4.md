## Abstract
arXiv:2511.21640v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We present an efficient relativistic implementation of algebraic diagrammatic construction (ADC) theory up to third order for the treatment of electronic ionization potentials (IP), electron affinities (EA), and excitation energies (EE) in heavy-element systems using an exact two-component atomic mean-field (X2CAMF) Hamiltonian. The approach combines Cholesky decomposition (CD) of two-electron integrals with frozen natural spinors (FNS) to significantly reduce the computational cost without compromising accuracy. To improve the description of excited states, we have implemented a state-specific frozen natural spinor (SS-FNS) framework and applied it to both electron affinity and excitation energy calculations. In addition to the standard relativistic ADC(3) method, we investigate a semi-empirically scaled variant in which the third-order contribution to the ADC secular matrix is multiplied by a scaling factor (x), denoted as FNS/SS-FNS-[ADC(2)+(x)(3)]. This [ADC(2)+(x)(3)] approach shows systematic improvements over conventional ADC(3) in a variety of cases. Substantial computational savings are achieved through the use of FNS and SS-FNS schemes when compared to canonical calculations, resulting in significant speedups for ionization, attachment, and excitation energy computations. The current implementation accurately reproduces the canonical four-component ADC(3) results while significantly reducing computational cost. The efficiency and robustness of the method are demonstrated through applications to medium and large-sized molecular systems, including systems with 70 atoms and over 2600 basis functions.

arXiv:2511.21640v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出了一种高效的代数图解构造 (ADC) 理论的相对论实现，其高达三阶，用于使用精确的二分量原子平均场 (X2CAMF) 哈密顿量来处理重元素系统中的电子电离势 (IP)、电子亲和力 (EA) 和激发能 (EE)。该方法将双电子积分的乔列斯基分解 (CD) 与冻结自然旋量 (FNS) 相结合，可在不影响精度的情况下显着降低计算成本。为了改进激发态的描述，我们实现了特定态冻结自然旋量（SS-FNS）框架，并将其应用于电子亲和力和激发能计算。除了标准相对论 ADC(3) 方法之外，我们还研究了一种半经验缩放变体，其中 ADC 世俗矩阵的三阶贡献乘以缩放因子 (x)，表示为 FNS/SS-FNS-[ADC(2)+(x)(3)]。这种 [ADC(2)+(x)(3)] 方法在各种情况下都显示出比传统 ADC(3) 更系统的改进。与规范计算相比，通过使用 FNS 和 SS-FNS 方案可以节省大量计算量，从而显着加快电离、附着和激发能量计算的速度。当前的实现准确地再现了规范的四分量 ADC(3) 结果，同时显着降低了计算成本。该方法的效率和稳健性通过对中型和大型分子系统（包括具有 70 个原子和超过 2600 个基函数的系统）的应用得到了证明。
