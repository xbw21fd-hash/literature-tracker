# Engineering interactions shape in resonantly driven bosonic gas

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15582v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In systems with fast periodic driving, there are special subsets of (resonant) states, which behavior can be described with effective, time-independent Hamiltonian in a rotating reference frame. Here, we show that experimentally feasible system of ultracold bosonic atoms on a ring with rapidly oscillating scattering length can be used to simulate time-independent two-component atomic mixture with exotic, long-range interactions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15582v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在具有快速周期性驱动的系统中，存在特殊的（共振）状态子集，其行为可以在旋转参考系中用有效的、与时间无关的哈密顿量来描述。在这里，我们证明了实验上可行的超冷玻色子原子在具有快速振荡散射长度的环上的系统可用于模拟具有奇异的长程相互作用的时间无关的二组分原子混合物。
