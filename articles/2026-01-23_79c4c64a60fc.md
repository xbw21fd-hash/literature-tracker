## Abstract
We present a non-iterative triples correction to the relativistic quadratic unitary coupled cluster singles and doubles (qUCCSD) method, denoted as qUCCSD[T]. The method builds upon the Hermitian structure of the similarity-transformed Hamiltonian in the unitary coupled cluster method and can be derived by using perturbational truncation to the unitary coupled cluster energy functional. Relativistic effects are incorporated using the exact two-component atomic mean-field Hamiltonian, and the computational cost is further reduced through the frozen natural spinor and the Cholesky decomposition approximations. Benchmark results demonstrate that qUCCSD[T] outperforms previously proposed triples corrections to the unitary coupled cluster method and yields excellent agreement with experimental data and Full CI results. In addition, the method shows high accuracy in computing the bond dissociation enthalpies, molecular geometries, vibrational frequencies, ionization potentials, and electron affinities of heavy-element-containing systems. The new qUCCSD[T] method is competitive to popular CCSD(T) even on a classical computer.

## 摘要（中文）
我们提出了一种对相对论二次酉耦合簇单数和双数 (qUCCSD) 方法的非迭代三元组校正，表示为 qUCCSD[T]。该方法建立在酉耦合簇方法中相似变换哈密顿量的埃尔米特结构之上，并且可以通过对酉耦合簇能量泛函使用微扰截断来导出。使用精确的二分量原子平均场哈密顿量合并相对论效应，并通过冻结自然旋量和 Cholesky 分解近似进一步降低计算成本。基准测试结果表明，qUCCSD[T] 优于之前提出的酉耦合聚类方法的三重校正，并且与实验数据和完整 CI 结果非常一致。此外，该方法在计算含重元素系统的键解离焓、分子几何形状、振动频率、电离势和电子亲和势方面显示出高精度。即使在经典计算机上，新的 qUCCSD[T] 方法也能与流行的 CCSD(T) 竞争。
