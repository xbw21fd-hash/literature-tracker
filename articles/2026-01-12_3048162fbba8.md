## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05809v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Electrospun poly(\epsilon-caprolactone) (PCL)nanofibrous mats are widely considered for blood-contacting wound dressings and small-diameter vascular applications; however, their intrinsic hydrophobicity limits rapid wetting and controlled interaction with blood. In this work, we modulate the interfacial response of PCL nanofibers by incorporating oxide-shelled silicon nanoparticles (SiNPs) synthesized by pulsed laser ablation in liquid, a ligand-free approach that avoids organic stabilizers and preserves surface reactivity. Two composite architectures were designed: SiNPs embedded within the fiber bulk (PAC1,4,16) and SiNPs preferentially exposed at the fiber surface (SPAC1,4,16), with systematically increasing nanoparticle loadings. Structural characterization confirmed the retention of a homogeneous fibrous morphology and the targeted nanoparticle distribution. The dynamic interaction with whole blood was quantified using time-resolved contact-angle measurements, complemented by top-view optical microscopy and three-dimensional profilometry of dried droplets. Pristine PCL remained strongly hydrophobic whereas a hydrophilic PCL functionalized with APTES showed rapid spreading. Incorporation of SiNPs within the fiber volume led to only a moderate enhancement of wettability, and dried droplets retained compact morphologies with limited spreading. In contrast, surface-decorated mats displayed a sharp, concentration-dependent transition toward highly wettable behavior: for SPAC16, the contact angle fell below 20, droplet profiles became markedly flattened, and microscopy revealed extended plasma-rich regions surrounding a red-cell-rich core, indicative of pronounced phase separation within the nanofibrous network.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05809v1 公告类型：新 摘要：静电纺聚（ε-己内酯）（PCL）纳米纤维垫被广泛考虑用于血液接触伤口敷料和小直径血管应用；然而，它们固有的疏水性限制了快速润湿和与血液的受控相互作用。在这项工作中，我们通过掺入通过脉冲激光烧蚀在液体中合成的氧化物壳硅纳米颗粒（SiNP）来调节PCL纳米纤维的界面响应，这是一种无配体的方法，避免了有机稳定剂并保留了表面反应性。设计了两种复合结构：嵌入纤维本体中的 SiNP (PAC1,4,16) 和优先暴露在纤维表面的 SiNP (SPAC1,4,16)，并系统地增加纳米粒子负载。结构表征证实了均匀纤维形态和目标纳米颗粒分布的保留。使用时间分辨接触角测量来量化与全血的动态相互作用，并辅以顶视光学显微镜和干燥液滴的三维轮廓测定法。原始 PCL 保持强疏水性，而用 APTES 功能化的亲水性 PCL 显示出快速扩散。在纤维体积中掺入 SiNP 仅导致润湿性适度增强，并且干燥的液滴保持紧凑的形态，但铺展有限。相比之下，表面修饰的垫表现出向高度可润湿行为的急剧的、浓度依赖性的转变：对于SPAC16，接触角降至20以下，液滴轮廓变得明显平坦，显微镜显示出围绕富含红细胞的核心的扩展的富含血浆的区域，表明纳米纤维网络内存在明显的相分离。
