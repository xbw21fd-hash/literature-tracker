# Quantum mechanics and neural networks

## 中文标题
量子力学和神经网络

## Abstract
We demonstrate that any Euclidean-time quantum mechanical theory may be represented as a neural network (NN), ensured by the Kosambi–Karhunen–Loève theorem, mean-square path continuity, and finite two-point functions. The additional constraint of reflection positivity, which is related to unitarity, may be achieved by a number of mechanisms, such as imposing NN parameter space splitting or the Markov property. Non-differentiability of the networks is related to the appearance of non-trivial commutators. Neural networks acting on Markov processes are no longer Markov, but still reflection positive, which facilitates the definition of deep NN quantum systems. We illustrate these principles in several examples using numerical implementations, recovering classic quantum mechanical results such as Heisenberg uncertainty, non-trivial commutators, and the spectrum.

## 摘要（中文）
我们证明任何欧几里得时间量子力学理论都可以表示为神经网络（NN），并由 Kosambi-Karhunen-Loève 定理、均方路径连续性和有限两点函数保证。与幺正性相关的反射正性的附加约束可以通过多种机制来实现，例如施加神经网络参数空间分裂或马尔可夫性质。网络的不可微性与非平凡换向器的出现有关。作用于马尔可夫过程的神经网络不再是马尔可夫，但仍然是反射正的，这有利于深度神经网络量子系统的定义。我们使用数值实现在几个示例中说明了这些原理，恢复了经典的量子力学结果，例如海森堡不确定性、非平凡换向器和频谱。
