## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10477v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Coronal mass ejections (CMEs) are the primary drivers of adverse space-weather events, yet their initiation and onset prediction remain insufficiently understood due to the complexity of the magnetic topology and physical processes in real solar source regions. Here, based on fully observational-data-driven magnetohydrodynamic simulation, we successfully reproduce the initiation of a CME originating from the super active region AR 13663, with only a one-minute time lag between the flare peak in observations and the velocity peak of the rising flux rope in the simulation. Moreover, the eruptive structure exhibits a multi-stage kinematic evolution: an initial slow acceleration, a plateau at a nearly stationary height, and a subsequent impulsive acceleration. These stages correspond to torus instability, the downward tension force exerted by the overlying toroidal field, and fast magnetic reconnection, respectively. Our results highlight the inherently multistage nature of CME initiation in real events. In configurations with strong overlying toroidal fields, the downward toroidal-field-induced tension force can suppress the rise of the flux rope and produce a plateau phase at a nearly stable height, even when torus instability occurs. In contrast, the subsequent fast magnetic reconnection beneath the flux rope can drive the impulsive eruption more effectively. The close agreement between the observed and simulated peak times over one minute demonstrates the strong potential of our data-driven model for predicting CME onset.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10477v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：日冕物质抛射 (CME) 是不利空间天气事件的主要驱动因素，但由于真实太阳源区域中磁拓扑和物理过程的复杂性，其引发和发生预测仍然没有得到充分的了解。在这里，基于完全观测数据驱动的磁流体动力学模拟，我们成功地再现了源自超级活跃区 AR 13663 的日冕物质抛射的启动，观测中的耀斑峰值与模拟中上升磁绳的速度峰值之间仅存在一分钟的时滞。此外，喷发结构表现出多阶段的运动学演化：最初的缓慢加速，在几乎静止高度的平台，以及随后的脉冲加速。这些阶段分别对应于环面不稳定性、上覆环形场施加的向下张力以及快速磁重联。我们的结果强调了真实事件中 CME 启动的固有的多阶段性质。在具有强叠加环形场的配置中，向下的环形场引起的张力可以抑制磁通绳的上升，并在几乎稳定的高度处产生平台相，即使发生环面不稳定时也是如此。相比之下，磁绳下方随后的快速磁重联可以更有效地驱动脉冲喷发。一分钟内观察到的峰值时间与模拟的峰值时间之间的接近一致性表明我们的数据驱动模型在预测 CME 发作方面具有强大的潜力。
