## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05108v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The nonlinear development of ballooning instability and the subsequently induced plasmoid formation in the near-Earth magnetotail demonstrated in MHD simulations has been proposed as a potential trigger mechanism for substorm onset over the past decade, and their connections to the in-situ satellite and ground all-sky auroral optical observations have been a subject of continued research. In this work, a set of THEMIS substorm onset events with good conjunction of auroral observations has been selected for comparative simulation study, whose pre-onset magnetotail configuration and conditions are inferred from in-situ data and compared with the onset conditions of ballooning instability obtained in our MHD simulations. The evolution of the near-Earth magnetotail is followed, where the signatures of ballooning instability and the plasmoid formation are extracted from simulations and compared with the magnetic fields and flow patterns within the magnetotail region from observation data. The field-aligned current (FAC) density is evaluated at the Earth side boundary of the magnetotail domain of simulation, which is further mapped along magnetic field lines to the auroral ionosphere and compared with the auroral pattern and evolution there in terms of growth rate, dominant wavenumber, and absolute auroral intensities. Such validation efforts are also the first step towards the development of a self-consistent coupling model that includes the magnetotail-ionosphere interaction in the substorm onset process.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05108v1 公告类型：新 摘要：MHD 模拟中演示的气球状不稳定性的非线性发展以及随后在近地磁尾中诱导等离子体团的形成已被提议作为过去十年中亚暴爆发的潜在触发机制，并且它们与原位卫星和地面全天极光光学观测的联系一直是持续研究的主题。本工作选择了一组与极光观测结合良好的THEMIS亚暴爆发事件进行对比模拟研究，根据现场数据推断其爆发前的磁尾结构和条件，并与MHD模拟中获得的气球状不稳定的爆发条件进行比较。跟踪近地磁尾的演化，从模拟中提取气球不稳定和等离子体形成的特征，并将其与观测数据中磁尾区域内的磁场和流动模式进行比较。在模拟磁尾域的地球侧边界评估场对准电流（FAC）密度，进一步沿着磁场线映射到极光电离层，并在增长率、主波数和绝对极光强度方面与那里的极光模式和演化进行比较。这种验证工作也是开发自洽耦合模型的第一步，该模型包括亚暴爆发过程中的磁尾-电离层相互作用。
