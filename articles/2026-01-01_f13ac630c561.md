## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24290v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Optical-readout Time Projection Chambers (TPCs) produce megapixel-scale images whose fine-grained topological information is essential for rare-event searches, but whose size challenges real-time data selection. We present an unsupervised, reconstruction-based anomaly-detection strategy for fast Region-of-Interest (ROI) extraction that operates directly on minimally processed camera frames. A convolutional autoencoder trained exclusively on pedestal images learns the detector noise morphology without labels, simulation, or fine-grained calibration. Applied to standard data-taking frames, localized reconstruction residuals identify particle-induced structures, from which compact ROIs are extracted via thresholding and spatial clustering. Using real data from the CYGNO optical TPC prototype, we compare two pedestal-trained autoencoder configurations that differ only in their training objective, enabling a controlled study of its impact. The best configuration retains (93.0 +/- 0.2)% of reconstructed signal intensity while discarding (97.8 +/- 0.1)% of the image area, with an inference time of approximately 25 ms per frame on a consumer GPU. The results demonstrate that careful design of the training objective is critical for effective reconstruction-based anomaly detection and that pedestal-trained autoencoders provide a transparent and detector-agnostic baseline for online data reduction in optical TPCs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24290v1 公告类型：新 摘要：光学读出时间投影室 (TPC) 产生百万像素级图像，其细粒度拓扑信息对于罕见事件搜索至关重要，但其尺寸对实时数据选择提出了挑战。我们提出了一种无监督的、基于重建的异常检测策略，用于快速提取感兴趣区域（ROI），该策略直接在经过最少处理的相机帧上运行。专门针对基座图像进行训练的卷积自动编码器无需标签、模拟或细粒度校准即可学习探测器噪声形态。应用于标准数据采集框架时，局部重建残差可识别粒子引起的结构，通过阈值处理和空间聚类从中提取紧凑的 ROI。使用来自 CYGNO 光学 TPC 原型的真实数据，我们比较了两种基座训练的自动编码器配置，这两种配置仅在训练目标上有所不同，从而能够对其影响进行受控研究。最佳配置保留 (93.0 +/- 0.2)% 的重建信号强度，同时丢弃 (97.8 +/- 0.1)% 的图像区域，在消费级 GPU 上每帧的推理时间约为 25 毫秒。结果表明，训练目标的精心设计对于有效的基于重建的异常检测至关重要，并且基座训练的自动编码器为光学 TPC 中的在线数据缩减提供了透明且与检测器无关的基线。
