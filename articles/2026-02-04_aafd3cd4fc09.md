## Abstract
arXiv:2512.02292v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We present the solitary Alfv\'en wave, an exact nonlinear solution of the ideal magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) equations, and construct a three-dimensional numerical model -- an \emph{Alfv\'enon}. The model is characterized by an unperturbed far field, quasi-constant $|\boldsymbol{B}|$, and open field-line topology. Direct MHD simulations of the Alfv\'enon demonstrate remarkable stability, confirming that it behaves as a nonlinear solitary Alfv\'enic solution under ideal MHD evolution.
