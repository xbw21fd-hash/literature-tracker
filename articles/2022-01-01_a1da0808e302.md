## Abstract
TLDR: The self-consistent field neural network is introduced --- a general approach for learning the long-range response of molecular systems in neural network potentials that relies on a physically meaningful separation of the interatomic interactions --- and is demonstrated by modeling liquid water with and without applied fields.
