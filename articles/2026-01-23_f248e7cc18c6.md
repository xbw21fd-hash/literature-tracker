## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16000v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a theoretical framework for non-collinear antiferromagnetic spin torque oscillators (NC-AFM STO) by unifying spin dynamics under the Poisson Bracket formalism. Shifting from traditional torque-based descriptions to an operational symmetry perspective, we develop two complementary viewpoints: a vector perspective identifying infinite degenerate Rigid Body Precession (RBP) states where exchange energy depends solely on the total magnetic momentum, and a particle perspective decomposing dynamics into Center-of-Mass (CM) translation and Relative Motion (RM) oscillation. Using time-dependent rotational and translational transformation techniques, we analytically resolve the rapid (~10 ps) transient evolution into a stable RBP state driven by SOT and damping. We demonstrate that the out-of-plane anisotropy (OPA) lifts the exchange degeneracy, triggering a long-term (~1 ns) oscillatory decay toward a steady state characterized by uniform spin z-components and a 120-degree inter-spin locking angle. This state is accurately governed by our Terminal Velocity Motion (TVM) model [arXiv:2305.14013], where exchange coupling transforms into kinetic energy with a light effective mass. The model precisely predicts SOT-driven transients, hysteretic excitation, and the dynamic phase diagram. Finally, we account for the sub-critical current regime mismatch by identifying a 'Rigid-Body Breaking' effect: a surge in effective friction caused by the self-resonance of RM variables induced by CM translation, mediated by the in-plane anisotropy (IPA).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16000v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们通过统一泊松括号形式下的自旋动力学，提出了非共线反铁磁自旋扭矩振荡器（NC-AFM STO）的理论框架。从传统的基于扭矩的描述转向操作对称视角，我们开发了两个互补的观点：矢量视角识别无限简并刚体进动（RBP）状态，其中交换能量仅取决于总磁动量；粒子视角将动力学分解为质心（CM）平移和相对运动（RM）振荡。使用依赖时间的旋转和平移变换技术，我们分析地将快速（~10 ps）瞬态演化解析为由 SOT 和阻尼驱动的稳定 RBP 状态。我们证明，面外各向异性 (OPA) 提升了交换简并性，触发长期 (~1 ns) 振荡衰减，趋向稳定状态，其特征是均匀自旋 z 分量和 120 度自旋间锁定角。这种状态由我们的终端速度运动 (TVM) 模型 [arXiv:2305.14013] 精确控制，其中交换耦合转化为具有轻有效质量的动能。该模型精确预测 SOT 驱动的瞬态、迟滞激励和动态相图。最后，我们通过识别“刚体破坏”效应来解释次临界电流状态不匹配：由面内各向异性 (IPA) 介导的 CM 平移引起的 RM 变量自谐振引起的有效摩擦力激增。
