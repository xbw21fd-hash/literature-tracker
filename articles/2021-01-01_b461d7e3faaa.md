## Abstract
TLDR: How the delicate interplay between stepwise changed dynamics of organic cations and cooperative deformation of the inorganic framework induces ferroelectric and ferroelastic phase transitions at 311 K and 455 K, respectively, provides important clues for designing advanced multiferroic materials based on organic--inorganic hybrid crystals.
