## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04445v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Raman spectroscopy is a key tool for graphene characterization, yet its application to graphene grown on silicon carbide (SiC) is strongly limited by the intense and variable second-order Raman response of the substrate. This limitation is critical for buffer layer graphene, a semiconducting interfacial phase, whose vibrational signatures are overlapped with the SiC background and challenging to be reliably accessed using conventional reference-based subtraction, due to strong spatial and experimental variability of the substrate signal. Here we present SpectraFormer, a transformer-based deep learning model that reconstructs the SiC Raman substrate contribution directly from post-growth partially masked spectroscopic data without relying on explicit reference measurements. By learning global correlations across the entire Raman shift range, the model captures the statistical structure of the SiC background and enables accurate reconstruction of its contribution in mixed spectra. Subtraction of the reconstructed substrate signal reveals weak vibrational features associated with ZLG that are inaccessible through conventional analysis methods. The extracted spectra are validated by ab initio vibrational calculations, allowing assignment of the resolved features to specific modes and confirming their physical consistency. By leveraging a state-of-the-art attention-based deep learning architecture, this approach establishes a robust, reference-free framework for Raman analysis of graphene on SiC and provides a foundation, compatible with real-time data acquisition, to its integration into automated, closed-loop AI-assisted growth optimization.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04445v1 公告类型：新 摘要：拉曼光谱是石墨烯表征的关键工具，但其在碳化硅 (SiC) 上生长的石墨烯的应用受到基底强烈且可变的二阶拉曼响应的强烈限制。这种限制对于缓冲层石墨烯（一种半导体界面相）至关重要，其振动特征与 SiC 背景重叠，并且由于基底信号的强烈空间和实验可变性，使用传统的基于参考的减法来可靠地访问具有挑战性。在这里，我们介绍 SpectraFormer，这是一种基于变压器的深度学习模型，可直接从生长后部分屏蔽的光谱数据重建 SiC 拉曼基底贡献，而不依赖于明确的参考测量。通过学习整个拉曼位移范围内的全局相关性，该模型捕获 SiC 背景的统计结构，并能够准确重建其在混合光谱中的贡献。减去重建的基质信号揭示了与 ZLG 相关的微弱振动特征，这是通过传统分析方法无法获得的。提取的光谱通过从头算振动计算进行验证，允许将解析的特征分配给特定模式并确认其物理一致性。通过利用最先进的基于注意力的深度学习架构，该方法为 SiC 上石墨烯的拉曼分析建立了一个强大的、无参考的框架，并为其集成到自动化、闭环人工智能辅助生长优化中提供了与实时数据采集兼容的基础。
