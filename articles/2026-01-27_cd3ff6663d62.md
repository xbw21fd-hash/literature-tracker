## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17066v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Antioxidants operate in biological environments where solvent effects dramatically alter their redox properties. Using ascorbic acid (vitamin C) as a paradigmatic example, we present a comprehensive quantum-chemical investigation of its global chemical reactivity indices -- ionization potential, electron affinity, HOMO-LUMO gap, hardness, softness, electronegativity, electrophilicity, and electrodonating/accepting powers -- computed at the compound chemistry CBS-QB3 and various DFT levels in vacuo and across six solvents. The results demonstrate that solvation stabilizes charged species so strongly that reactivity indices shift by several electronvolts, following a roughly Born-like dependence on dielectric constant. Most importantly, we show unequivocally that Koopmans' theorem, often used to estimate these indices from orbital energies, fails catastrophically in solution: it predicts solvent-independent values that are qualitatively and quantitatively wrong, missing the essential physics of dielectric screening and geometric relaxation. We therefore conclude that Koopmans' theorem must be abandoned for antioxidant studies in condensed phases; adiabatic calculations with solvent are mandatory for meaningful predictions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.17066v1 公告类型：新 摘要：抗氧化剂在生物环境中发挥作用，其中溶剂效应会显着改变其氧化还原特性。以抗坏血酸（维生素 C）作为范例，我们对其整体化学反应指数（电离势、电子亲和力、HOMO-LUMO 能隙、硬度、柔软度、电负性、亲电性和供电子/接受能力）进行了全面的量子化学研究，这些指数是在真空中和六种溶剂中在化合物化学 CBS-QB3 和各种 DFT 水平上计算的。结果表明，溶剂化作用对带电物质的稳定作用如此强烈，以至于反应指数发生了几个电子伏特的变化，遵循对介电常数的大致玻恩式依赖性。最重要的是，我们明确地表明，库普曼斯定理通常用于根据轨道能量估计这些指数，但在解决方案中却遭遇了灾难性的失败：它预测的与溶剂无关的值在定性和定量上都是错误的，忽略了介电屏蔽和几何弛豫的基本物理原理。因此，我们得出结论，在凝聚相抗氧化研究中必须放弃库普曼斯定理；对于有意义的预测，必须使用溶剂进行绝热计算。
