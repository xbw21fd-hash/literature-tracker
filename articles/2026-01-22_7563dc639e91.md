## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14372v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Orbital magnetism is a purely quantum phenomenon that reflects intrinsic electronic properties of solids, yet its microscopic description in interacting multiband systems remains incomplete. We develop a general quantum many-body framework for orbital magnetic responses based on the Luttinger-Ward functional. Starting from the Dyson equation, we reformulate the thermodynamic potential in a weak magnetic field and construct a controlled expansion in powers of $B$ applicable to correlated electron systems. A key technical advance is a modified ``Fourier'' representation using noncommutative coordinates, which allows the thermodynamic potential to be expressed in an effective momentum space where the magnetic field acts perturbatively. This formulation makes analytic progress possible within the Moyal algebra. As an application, we derive the spontaneous orbital magnetization and express it entirely in terms of the zero-field Hamiltonian renormalized by the self-energy. For frequency-dependent but Hermitian self-energies, we generalize the orbital magnetic moment and Berry curvature to momentum-frequency space and identify two gauge-invariant contributions built from these quantities. For frequency-independent self-energies the result reduces to the familiar geometric formula for noninteracting systems. This framework provides a unified foundation for computing orbital magnetic responses in correlated multiband materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14372v1 公告类型：新 摘要：轨道磁性是一种纯粹的量子现象，反映了固体的固有电子特性，但其在相互作用的多带系统中的微观描述仍然不完整。我们开发了基于 Luttinger-Ward 泛函的轨道磁响应的通用量子多体框架。从戴森方程出发，我们重新表述了弱磁场中的热力学势，并构造了适用于相关电子系统的 $B$ 幂的受控展开式。一项关键的技术进步是使用非交换坐标的改进的“傅里叶”表示，它允许在磁场发生扰动的有效动量空间中表达热力学势。这个公式使得莫亚尔代数的分析进展成为可能。作为一个应用，我们推导了自发轨道磁化强度，并将其完全用自能重整化的零场哈密顿量来表示。对于频率相关但厄米特自能，我们将轨道磁矩和贝里曲率推广到动量频率空间，并确定了从这些量构建的两个规范不变的贡献。对于频率无关的自能，结果可简化为非相互作用系统的常见几何公式。该框架为计算相关多带材料中的轨道磁响应提供了统一的基础。
