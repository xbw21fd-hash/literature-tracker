## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01737v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Neural operators have emerged as powerful deep learning frameworks for approximating solution operators of parameterized partial differential equations (PDE). However, current methods predominantly rely on multilayer perceptrons (MLPs) for mapping inputs to solutions, which impairs training robustness in physics-informed settings due to inherent spectral biases and fixed activation functions. To overcome the architectural limitations, we introduce the Physics-Informed Chebyshev Polynomial Neural Operator (CPNO), a novel mesh-free framework that leverages a basis transformation to replace unstable monomial expansions with the numerically stable Chebyshev spectral basis. By integrating parameter dependent modulation mechanism to main net, CPNO constructs PDE solutions in a near-optimal functional space, decoupling the model from MLP-specific constraints and enhancing multi-scale representation. Theoretical analysis demonstrates the Chebyshev basis's near-minimax uniform approximation properties and superior conditioning, with Lebesgue constants growing logarithmically with degree, thereby mitigating spectral bias and ensuring stable gradient flow during optimization. Numerical experiments on benchmark parameterized PDEs show that CPNO achieves superior accuracy, faster convergence, and enhanced robustness to hyperparameters. The experiment of transonic airfoil flow has demonstrated the capability of CPNO in characterizing complex geometric problems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01737v1 公告类型：新 摘要：神经算子已成为强大的深度学习框架，用于逼近参数化偏微分方程 (PDE) 的解算子。然而，当前的方法主要依赖多层感知器（MLP）将输入映射到解决方案，由于固有的谱偏差和固定的激活函数，这削弱了物理信息设置中的训练鲁棒性。为了克服架构限制，我们引入了基于物理的切比雪夫多项式神经算子（CPNO），这是一种新颖的无网格框架，它利用基变换以数值稳定的切比雪夫谱基代替不稳定的单项式展开。通过将参数相关的调制机制集成到主网，CPNO 在接近最优的函数空间中构建 PDE 解决方案，将模型与 MLP 特定的约束解耦并增强多尺度表示。理论分析证明了切比雪夫基的近极小极大均匀逼近特性和优越的条件作用，勒贝格常数随度数呈对数增长，从而减轻谱偏差并确保优化期间稳定的梯度流。基准参数化偏微分方程的数值实验表明，CPNO 实现了卓越的精度、更快的收敛速度以及对超参数的增强的鲁棒性。跨音速翼型流实验证明了CPNO表征复杂几何问题的能力。
