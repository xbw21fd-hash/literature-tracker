## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04921v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) have recently emerged as a promising alternative for extracting unknown quantities from experimental data. Despite this potential, much of the recent literature has relied on sparse, high-fidelity data from direct numerical simulations (DNS) rather than experimental sources like particle image velocimetry (PIV), which are not suitable for validating all reconstructed quantities. In the case of PIV, for example, pressure is not directly measured and the data have imperfections such as noise contamination or a limited field of view. To overcome these limitations, we present a novel methodology where PINNs are first trained on a RANS simulation such that it learns all states at every location in the domain. We then apply transfer learning which updates the PINN using sub-sampled PIV data. The resulting predictions are in significantly better agreement with the full PIV dataset than PINNs which are trained on experimental data only. This work builds on the recent literature by integrating a Spalart-Allmaras turbulence model and applying hard constraints to the no-slip wall boundary condition. We apply this new methodology to a two-dimensional NACA 0012 airfoil inclined at an angle of attack, $\alpha$ = 15 degrees, for two Reynolds numbers of Re = 10,000 and 75,000. The proposed methodology is initially validated using large eddy simulation (LES) data and then demonstrated on experimental PIV data. Our transfer learning approach results in improved predictions and a reduction in training time when compared to using a random network initialisation.

arXiv:2601.04921v1 公告类型：新 摘要：物理信息神经网络 (PINN) 最近已成为从实验数据中提取未知量的有前途的替代方案。尽管有这种潜力，但最近的许多文献仍然依赖于直接数值模拟（DNS）的稀疏、高保真数据，而不是粒子图像测速（PIV）等实验来源，后者不适合验证所有重建量。例如，就 PIV 而言，压力不是直接测量的，并且数据存在缺陷，例如噪声污染或视野有限。为了克服这些限制，我们提出了一种新颖的方法，其中 PINN 首先在 RANS 模拟上进行训练，以便它学习域中每个位置的所有状态。然后，我们应用迁移学习，使用子采样 PIV 数据更新 PINN。与仅根据实验数据进行训练的 PINN 相比，所得预测与完整 PIV 数据集的一致性明显更好。这项工作建立在最近文献的基础上，通过集成 Spalart-Allmaras 湍流模型并对无滑移壁边界条件应用硬约束。我们将这种新方法应用于二维 NACA 0012 翼型，该翼型以 $\alpha$ = 15 度的攻角倾斜，两个雷诺数 Re = 10,000 和 75,000。所提出的方法最初使用大涡模拟 (LES) 数据进行验证，然后在实验 PIV 数据上进行演示。与使用随机网络初始化相比，我们的迁移学习方法可以改进预测并减少训练时间。
