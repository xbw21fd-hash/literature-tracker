## Abstract
arXiv:2310.01060v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Mott insulators, in which strong Coulomb interactions fully localize electrons on single atomic sites, play host to an incredibly rich and exciting array of strongly correlated physics. One can naturally extend this concept to cluster Mott insulators, wherein electrons localize not on single atoms but across clusters of atoms, forming "molecules in solids". The resulting localized degrees of freedom incorporate the full spectrum of electronic degrees of freedom, spin, orbital, and charge. These serve as the building blocks for cluster Mott insulators, and understanding them is an important first step toward understanding the many-body physics that emerges in candidate cluster Mott insulators. Here, we focus on elementary building blocks, neglecting some of the complexity present in real materials which can often obfuscate the underlying principles at play. Through an extensive set of exact theoretical calculations on clusters of varying geometry, number of orbitals, and number of electrons, we uncover some of the basic organizing principles of cluster Mott phases, particularly when interactions dominate and negate a simple single-particle picture. These include, for example, the identification of an additional "cluster Hund's rule", of cluster ground states that are best understood from a purely interacting perspective, and of several localized degrees of freedom which are protected by an unusual combination of discrete spatial or orbital symmetries. Finally, we discuss the impact of adding additional terms, relevant to material candidates, on the phase diagrams presented throughout, as well as the potential next steps in the journey to building a more complete picture of cluster Mott insulators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2310.01060v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：莫特绝缘体，其中强库仑相互作用将电子完全局域在单个原子位点上，承载着令人难以置信的丰富且令人兴奋的强相关物理阵列。人们可以自然地将这一概念扩展到簇状莫特绝缘体，其中电子不是集中在单个原子上，而是集中在原子簇上，形成“固体中的分子”。由此产生的局部自由度包含了完整的电子自由度、自旋、轨道和电荷自由度。它们是簇莫特绝缘体的构建块，了解它们是理解候选簇莫特绝缘体中出现的多体物理的重要的第一步。在这里，我们专注于基本构建块，忽略了真实材料中存在的一些复杂性，这些复杂性通常会混淆所起作用的基本原理。通过对不同几何形状、轨道数量和电子数量的团簇进行大量精确的理论计算，我们揭示了团簇莫特相的一些基本组织原理，特别是当相互作用主导并否定简单的单粒子图像时。例如，这些包括识别额外的“簇亨德规则”，从纯粹相互作用的角度最好理解的簇基态，以及受离散空间或轨道对称性的不寻常组合保护的几个局部自由度。最后，我们讨论了添加与候选材料相关的附加术语对整个相图的影响，以及构建更完整的簇莫特绝缘体图的过程中潜在的后续步骤。
