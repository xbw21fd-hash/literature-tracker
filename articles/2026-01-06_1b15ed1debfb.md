# Unraveling vibronic interactions in molecules functionalized with optical cycling centers

## Abstract
We report detailed characterization of the vibronic interactions between the first two electronically excited states, Ã and B̃, in SrOPh (Ph = phenyl, –C 6 H 5 ) and its deuterated counterpart, SrOPh-d 5 (-C 6 D 5 ). The vibronic interactions, which arise due to non-adiabatic coupling between the two electronic states, mix the B̃,ν0 state with the energetically close vibronic level, Ã,ν21ν33, resulting in extra transition probability into the latter state. This state mixing is more prominent in the deuterated molecule because of the smaller energy gap between the interacting states. We model the mixing of the Ã and B̃ states using the Köppel–Domcke–Cederbaum (KDC) Hamiltonian parameterized in the diabatic framework of Ichino, Gauss, and Stanton on the basis of equation-of-motion coupled-cluster calculations. The simulation attributes the observed mixing to a second-order effect mediated by linear quasi-diabatic couplings between the Ã–C̃ and B̃–C̃ states. Based on the measured spectra, we deduce an effective coupling strength of ∼0.5 cm −1 . Non-adiabatic couplings between different electronic states are an important factor that should be considered in the design of laser-cooling protocols for complex molecules.

## 摘要（中文）
我们报告了 SrOPh (Ph = 苯基, –C 6 H 5 ) 及其氘代对应物 SrOPh-d 5 (-C 6 D 5 ) 中前两个电子激发态 à 和 B̃ 之间的电子振动相互作用的详细表征。由于两个电子态之间的非绝热耦合而产生的振动相互作用，将 B̃,ν0 态与能量上接近的振动能级 ν,ν21ν33 混合，导致额外的跃迁概率到后一种状态。由于相互作用态之间的能隙较小，这种状态混合在氘代分子中更为突出。我们基于运动方程耦合簇计算，使用在 Ichino、Gauss 和 Stanton 的非绝热框架中参数化的 Köppel-Domcke-Cederbaum (KDC) 哈密顿量对 à 和 B̃ 态的混合进行建模。模拟将观察到的混合归因于由 àC̃ 和 B̃–C̃ 状态之间的线性准非绝热耦合介导的二阶效应。根据测量的光谱，我们推导出~0.5 cm -1 的有效耦合强度。不同电子态之间的非绝热耦合是设计复杂分子激光冷却方案时应考虑的重要因素。
