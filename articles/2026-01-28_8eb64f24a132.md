## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19586v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This paper introduces a nonlocal, dual-band reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS) designed for full-space beam synthesis at 4.0 GHz and 6.3 GHz. The constituent unit cells comprise a pair of interleaved sub-cells that are specifically engineered to operate independently at their respective target frequencies. This hardware-level decoupling facilitates an efficient synthesis framework based on microwave network theory (MNT) that rigorously accounts for mutual coupling within both bands. Under this framework, the optimal biasing for sub-cells is determined to achieve precise full-space beam synthesis at both frequencies. The proposed method is numerically and experimentally validated with an RIS comprising 14 X 14 varactor-loaded unit cells that can be individually biased. We experimentally demonstrate arbitrary beam profile synthesis beyond simple beam steering, including dual-beam and sector patterns in full space. Experimental and simulation results show good agreement with the MNT model, confirming the effectiveness of the proposed method.

arXiv:2601.19586v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本文介绍了一种非局部、双频段可重构智能表面 (RIS)，专为 4.0 GHz 和 6.3 GHz 下的全空间波束合成而设计。组成单位单元包括一对交错的子单元，这些子单元经过专门设计以在各自的目标频率下独立操作。这种硬件级解耦促进了基于微波网络理论 (MNT) 的高效合成框架，该框架严格考虑了两个频段内的相互耦合。在此框架下，确定子单元的最佳偏置以在两个频率下实现精确的全空间波束合成。所提出的方法通过包含 14 X 14 个可单独偏置的变容二极管负载单元的 RIS 进行了数值和实验验证。我们通过实验演示了超越简单波束控制的任意波束轮廓合成，包括全空间中的双波束和扇形图案。实验和仿真结果与MNT模型吻合良好，证实了该方法的有效性。
