## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03487v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic skyrmions are intriguing topological spin textures that have attracted great attention due to their potential for future spintronic devices. Skyrmions have so far been explored in different magnetic materials, such as ferromagnets, antiferromagnets, and ferrimagnets. Here, we propose a new type of unconventional skyrmions stabilized in noncollinear magnets. Using first-principles calculations and atomistic spin simulations, we demonstrate that a noncollinear ground state can be stabilized in Rh/Co and Pd/Co atomic bilayers on the Re(0001) surface by four spin exchange interactions, although Co -- a material often used in applications -- is a prototypical ferromagnet with strong pairwise exchange interaction. We further show that unconventional skyrmion lattices and isolated skyrmions can emerge on this noncollinear magnetic background. Transition-state theory calculations reveal that these metastable skyrmions are protected by large energy barriers, suggesting that they could be observed in experiments. These unconventional types of skyrmions in noncollinear magnets might open new possibilities for topological spin transport or magnet-superconductor hybrid systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03487v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁性斯格明子是一种有趣的拓扑自旋纹理，由于其在未来自旋电子器件中的潜力而引起了极大的关注。迄今为止，斯格明子已在不同的磁性材料中进行了探索，例如铁磁体、反铁磁体和亚铁磁体。在这里，我们提出了一种稳定在非共线磁体中的新型非常规斯格明子。利用第一性原理计算和原子自旋模拟，我们证明了 Re(0001) 表面上的 Rh/Co 和 Pd/Co 原子双层可以通过四种自旋交换相互作用来稳定非共线基态，尽管 Co（应用中经常使用的材料）是具有强成对交换相互作用的典型铁磁体。我们进一步表明，非常规斯格明子晶格和孤立的斯格明子可以在这种非共线磁背景下出现。过渡态理论计算表明，这些亚稳态斯格明子受到大能垒的保护，这表明它们可以在实验中观察到。非共线磁体中这些非常规类型的斯格明子可能为拓扑自旋输运或磁体-超导体混合系统开辟新的可能性。
