# The dimensionality of the Hopfield model

Hopfield 模型的维数

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17427v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We use the Binary Intrinsic Dimension (BID), a geometrical measure designed for binary data, to analyze the Hopfield model, a paradigmatic spin system from statistical mechanics, machine learning and neuroscience. The BID allows us to characterize the phases and transitions of this system, and moreover it is robust against finite-size effects that interfere with the correct numerical estimation of the spin-glass order parameter ($q$). We observe that the BID scales linearly with system size in the retrieval and paramagnetic phases, where the correlations between spins are small, and exhibits sublinear scaling in the whole spin-glass phase, highlighting its correlated structure. Furthermore, we establish a direct relationship between the BID and the overlap distribution, unveiling a novel connection between the geometry of the state-space and standard spin order parameters.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.17427v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用二进制内在维度（BID）（一种为二进制数据设计的几何度量）来分析 Hopfield 模型，这是一个来自统计力学、机器学习和神经科学的范例自旋系统。 BID 使我们能够表征该系统的相和转变，而且它对于干扰自旋玻璃有序参数 ($q$) 的正确数值估计的有限尺寸效应具有鲁棒性。我们观察到，在恢复和顺磁相中，BID 随系统尺寸呈线性缩放，其中自旋之间的相关性很小，并且在整个自旋玻璃相中表现出亚线性缩放，突出了其相关结构。此外，我们在 BID 和重叠分布之间建立了直接关系，揭示了状态空间几何结构和标准自旋序参数之间的新颖联系。
