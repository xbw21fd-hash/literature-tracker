# Ultrafast switching of antiferromagnetic order by field-derivative torque

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15192v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Control of magnetic order in antiferromagnets is a central challenge in the development of next-generation spintronic devices. Here, we propose and analyze magnetization switching driven by the field-derivative torque, a torque that originates from the time-derivative of an applied THz pulse acting on the staggered order parameter. Using atomistic spin simulations, we show that the field-derivative torque couples efficiently to the N\'eel vector, enabling deterministic switching without net spin accumulation. Further, we show that using the circularly polarised THz pulse, the FDT-induced magnetization switching reduces the required THz magnetic field by two-fold. To this end, we compute the switching and non-switching areas as a function of THz pulse width, THz magnetic field, and damping of the antiferromagnetic material. We find that the switching and non-switching areas are completely deterministic in antiferromagnets. Moreover, the switching area increases by about 55% when the FDT is considered.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15192v1 公告类型：新 摘要：反铁磁体中磁序的控制是下一代自旋电子器件开发的核心挑战。在这里，我们提出并分析了由场导数扭矩驱动的磁化切换，该扭矩源自作用于交错阶次参数的所施加太赫兹脉冲的时间导数。使用原子自旋模拟，我们表明场导数扭矩有效地耦合到 N'eel 矢量，从而实现确定性切换而无需净自旋累积。此外，我们还表明，使用圆偏振太赫兹脉冲，FDT 引起的磁化切换将所需的太赫兹磁场减少了两倍。为此，我们将开关和非开关区域计算为太赫兹脉冲宽度、太赫兹磁场和反铁磁材料阻尼的函数。我们发现反铁磁体中的开关区域和非开关区域是完全确定的。此外，当考虑FDT时，开关面积增加了约55%。
