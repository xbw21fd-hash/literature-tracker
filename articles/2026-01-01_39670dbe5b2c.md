## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23724v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum condensed states in solids often reveal their fundamental nature via interactions with impurities, as epitomized by Yu-Shiba-Rusinov (YSR) bound states at magnetic impurities in superconductors. Although analogous YSR bound states were predicted within quantum condensates of excitons several decades ago, their existence has been elusive. Here, we directly visualize in-gap electronic states bound to impurities inside an exciton condensate phase of a van der Waals crystal Ta2Pd3Te5, utilizing scanning tunneling microscopy and spectroscopy. We find that the energies of in-gap states are strongly correlated with the excitonic band gap, which is systematically tuned by local strain and carrier injection. Our theoretical analyses reveal that these in-gap states are induced by charge dipoles associated with Ta vacancies through a charge-exciton version of the YSR mechanism. Our findings establish both the YSR physics in exciton condensates and a novel microscopic tool to probe and control quantum properties in exciton condensates persisting up to room temperature.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23724v1 公告类型：新 摘要：固体中的量子凝聚态通常通过与杂质的相互作用揭示其基本性质，如超导体中磁性杂质的 Yu-Shiba-Rusinov (YSR) 束缚态所体现的那样。尽管几十年前就在激子的量子凝聚态中预测了类似的 YSR 束缚态，但它们的存在一直难以捉摸。在这里，我们利用扫描隧道显微镜和光谱法直接可视化与范德华晶体 Ta2Pd3Te5 激子凝聚相内的杂质结合的带隙内电子态。我们发现带隙内态的能量与激子带隙密切相关，激子带隙通过局部应变和载流子注入进行系统调节。我们的理论分析表明，这些带隙内态是由与 Ta 空位相关的电荷偶极子通过 YSR 机制的电荷激子版本引起的。我们的研究结果既确立了激子凝聚体中的 YSR 物理特性，又建立了一种新颖的微观工具来探测和控制激子凝聚体中持续至室温的量子特性。
