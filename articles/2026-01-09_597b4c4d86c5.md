## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05100v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We theoretically investigate quantum spin transfer from spin-correlated conduction-electron pairs to localized spins in a ferromagnet, given that electrons are correlated intrinsically. We show that even spin-singlet pairs and triplet pairs with $m=0$, both carrying no net spin, can transfer finite angular momentum through the quantum fluctuation term inherent to the $sd$ exchange interaction. The amount of transferred spin differs between the singlet and triplet $m=0$ states due to quantum interference. The difference is such that the independent-electron approximation remains valid for spin transfer when injected spin currents are completely incoherent. However, in partially coherent systems, like superconductor/ferromagnet junctions, coherent spin-singlet currents can directly convert into equal-spin triplet currents in generic ferromagnets, without requiring magnetic inhomogeniety or spin-orbit coupling.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05100v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们从理论上研究了从自旋相关的传导电子对到铁磁体中的局域自旋的量子自旋转移，假设电子本质上是相关的。我们证明，即使 $m=0$ 的自旋单重态对和三重态对（两者都没有净自旋）也可以通过 $sd$ 交换相互作用固有的量子涨落项传递有限的角动量。由于量子干涉，单线态和三线态 $m=0$ 之间转移的自旋量有所不同。区别在于，当注入的自旋电流完全不相干时，独立电子近似对于自旋转移仍然有效。然而，在部分相干系统中，例如超导体/铁磁体结，相干自旋单线态电流可以直接转换为通用铁磁体中的等自旋三线态电流，而不需要磁不均匀性或自旋轨道耦合。
