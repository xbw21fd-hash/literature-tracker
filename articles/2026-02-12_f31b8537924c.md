## Abstract
arXiv:2511.22158v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: The accurate treatment of electron correlation in extended molecular systems remains computationally challenging using classical electronic structure methods. Hybrid quantum-classical algorithms offer a potential route to overcome these limitations; however, their practical deployment on existing quantum computers requires strategies that both reduce problem size and mitigate hardware noise. In this work, we combine Density Matrix Embedding Theory (DMET) with Sample-based Quantum Diagonalization (SQD) to compute ground-state energies of a set of natural ligand-like molecules in the minimal Slater Type Orbital (STO-3G) basis set. DMET provides a systematic fragmentation of a molecule into embedded impurity subproblems, while SQD enables construction and classical diagonalization of reduced configuration spaces through quantum sampling enhanced by iterative configuration recovery. The resulting embedded Hamiltonians are solved on IBM's Eagle R3 superconducting quantum hardware (IBM Sherbrooke). The DMET-SQD energies obtained for all systems considered exhibit strong agreement with DMET-FCI benchmark values within chemical accuracy (1 kcal/mol). These results demonstrate that sample-based quantum methods, when integrated with a robust embedding framework, can reliably extend quantum computation towards simulation of chemically relevant molecular systems, showcasing potential applications in the field of drug discovery.

arXiv:2511.22158v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：使用经典电子结构方法精确处理扩展分子系统中的电子相关性在计算上仍然具有挑战性。混合量子经典算法提供了克服这些限制的潜在途径；然而，它们在现有量子计算机上的实际部署需要既能减小问题规模又能减轻硬件噪声的策略。在这项工作中，我们将密度矩阵嵌入理论（DMET）与基于样本的量子对角化（SQD）相结合，计算最小斯莱特型轨道（STO-3G）基组中一组天然配体类分子的基态能量。 DMET 提供了将分子系统地分裂为嵌入式杂质子问题的方法，而 SQD 则通过迭代构型恢复增强的量子采样实现了缩减构型空间的构造和经典对角化。由此产生的嵌入式哈密顿量在 IBM 的 Eagle R3 超导量子硬件 (IBM Sherbrooke) 上求解。对于所考虑的所有系统获得的 DMET-SQD 能量在化学精度 (1 kcal/mol) 内与 DMET-FCI 基准值非常一致。这些结果表明，基于样本的量子方法在与强大的嵌入框架集成时，可以可靠地将量子计算扩展到化学相关分子系统的模拟，展示了在药物发现领域的潜在应用。
