## Abstract
Herein, we report the continuous-flow conditions enabling efficient halogen dance reactions on various p-excedent heterocycles with good to excellent yields. The developed protocol was applied to the synthesis of various and diversely decorated heterocycles, showcasing its potential to generate molecular diversity. The protocol was scalable and operationally simple and allow the rapid generation of complex heterocyclic platforms.
