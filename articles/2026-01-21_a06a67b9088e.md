## Abstract
arXiv:2601.11993v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a theory of the emission spectrum of magnetoexcitons interacting with a $\nu = 1$ quantum Hall state of massive Dirac fermions in monolayer transition metal dichalcogenides in high magnetic fields. Using an ab initio-parametrized massive Dirac fermion model including valley and spin degrees of freedom, combined with exact diagonalization techniques, we show that interband emission from the massive Dirac Fermion magnetoexciton interacting with $\nu = 1$ state directly probes intra-conduction-band excitations of the $\nu = 1$. Many-body interactions with the filled massive Dirac fermion $\nu = 1$ level yield a strong renormalization of the emission spectrum, including fully polarized emission, a pronounced redshift, and broadening relative to neutral and charged excitons. The calculated spectra are consistent with recent experiments [1-3], establishing magneto-spectroscopy as a probe of finite carrier densities in massive Dirac systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.11993v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种磁激子的发射光谱理论，该理论涉及高磁场中单层过渡金属二硫属化物中的巨量狄拉克费米子的 $\nu = 1$ 量子霍尔态相互作用。使用包括谷和自旋自由度的从头参数化的大规模狄拉克费米子模型，结合精确对角化技术，我们表明，与 $\nu = 1$ 态相互作用的大规模狄拉克费米子磁激子的带间发射直接探测 $\nu = 1$ 的导带内激发。与填充的大量狄拉克费米子 $\nu = 1$ 能级的多体相互作用产生了发射光谱的强烈重整化，包括完全偏振发射、明显的红移以及相对于中性和带电激子的展宽。计算出的光谱与最近的实验一致[1-3]，将磁谱学确立为大规模狄拉克系统中有限载流子密度的探针。
