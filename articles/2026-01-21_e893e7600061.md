## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12755v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quaternary Heusler alloys offer a versatile platform for engineering magnetic and topological transport phenomena through chemical flexibility and tunable disorder. Here, we report a comprehensive experimental and theoretical investigation of the magnetic, magnetotransport, and anomalous Hall properties of the quaternary Heusler alloy CoRuFeSi. The compound crystallizes in the LiMgPdSn-type structure with significant Co--Ru antisite disorder and exhibits soft ferromagnetism with a saturation magnetization of $4.21~\mu_{\mathrm{B}}/\mathrm{f.u.}$ at low temperature and a Curie temperature well above room temperature. Hall measurements reveal a robust anomalous Hall effect persisting up to 300~K, with an anomalous Hall conductivity of $\sim 74$~S/cm that is nearly temperature independent. Scaling analysis demonstrates that the anomalous Hall response is dominated by the intrinsic Berry-curvature mechanism. First-principles calculations identify CoRuFeSi as a topologically nontrivial nodal-line semimetal in its ordered phase. Incorporation of experimentally relevant Co--Ru antisite disorder redistributes the Berry curvature and quantitatively reproduces the experimentally observed anomalous Hall conductivity, while preserving half-metallicity. These results establish CoRuFeSi as a disorder-tolerant half-metallic ferromagnet with a sizable intrinsic anomalous Hall effect at room temperature, highlighting its potential for spintronic and Hall-based device applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12755v1 公告类型：新 摘要：四元 Heusler 合金通过化学灵活性和可调无序性为工程磁性和拓扑输运现象提供了一个多功能平台。在这里，我们报告了四元霍斯勒合金 CoRuFeSi 的磁性、磁输运和反常霍尔特性的全面实验和理论研究。该化合物结晶为LiMgPdSn型结构，具有明显的Co-Ru反位无序，并表现出软铁磁性，低温下饱和磁化强度为$4.21~\mu_{\mathrm{B}}/\mathrm{f.u.}$，居里温度远高于室温。霍尔测量揭示了持续高达 300~K 的强大反常霍尔效应，反常霍尔电导率为 $\sim 74$~S/cm，几乎与温度无关。标度分析表明，反常霍尔响应由固有的贝里曲率机制主导。第一性原理计算将 CoRuFeSi 确定为有序相的拓扑非平凡节点线半金属。结合实验相关的Co-Ru反位无序重新分布贝里曲率并定量再现实验观察到的反常霍尔电导率，同时保留半金属性。这些结果表明 CoRuFeSi 是一种抗紊乱半金属铁磁体，在室温下具有相当大的本征反常霍尔效应，凸显了其在自旋电子和霍尔器件应用中的潜力。
