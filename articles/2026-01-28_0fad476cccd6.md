## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19034v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Although electrostatics can be incorporated into machine-learned interatomic potentials, existing approaches are computationally very demanding, limiting large-scale, long-time simulations of electrostatics-driven phenomena such as dielectric response, infrared activity, and field-matter coupling. Here, we extend the neuroevolution potential (NEP), a highly efficient machine-learned interatomic potential, to a charge-aware framework (qNEP) by introducing explicit, environment-dependent partial charges. Each ionic partial charge is represented by a neural network as a function of the local descriptor vector, analogous to the NEP site-energy model. This formulation enables the direct prediction of the Born effective charge tensor for each ion and, consequently, the polarization. As a result, dielectric properties, infrared spectra, and coupling to external electric fields can be evaluated within a unified framework. We derive consistent expressions for the forces and virials that explicitly account for the position dependence of the partial charges. The qNEP method has been implemented in the free-and-open-source GPUMD package, with support for both Ewald summation and particle-particle particle-mesh treatments of electrostatics. We demonstrate the accuracy and efficiency of the qNEP approach through representative applications to water, Li7La3Zr2O12, BaTiO3, and a magnesium-water interface. These results show that qNEP enables accurate atomistic simulations with explicit long-range electrostatics, scalable to million-atom systems on nanosecond time scales using consumer-grade GPUs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19034v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：虽然静电可以合并到机器学习的原子间势中，但现有方法对计算的要求非常高，限制了静电驱动现象（例如介电响应、红外活动和场物质耦合）的大规模、长时间模拟。在这里，我们通过引入显式的、依赖于环境的部分电荷，将神经进化势（NEP）（一种高效的机器学习原子间势）扩展到电荷感知框架（qNEP）。每个离子部分电荷由神经网络表示为局部描述符向量的函数，类似于 NEP 位点能量模型。该公式能够直接预测每个离子的玻恩有效电荷张量，从而预测极化。因此，可以在统一的框架内评估介电性能、红外光谱和与外部电场的耦合。我们推导出力和维里的一致表达式，明确解释了部分电荷的位置依赖性。 qNEP 方法已在免费开源 GPUMD 包中实现，支持静电学的 Ewald 求和和粒子-粒子粒子-网格处理。我们通过对水、Li7La3Zr2O12、BaTiO3 和镁-水界面的代表性应用来证明 qNEP 方法的准确性和效率。这些结果表明，qNEP 能够通过显式的远程静电进行精确的原子模拟，并可使用消费级 GPU 在纳秒时间尺度上扩展到百万个原子系统。
