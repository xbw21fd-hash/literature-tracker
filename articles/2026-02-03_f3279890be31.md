## Abstract
arXiv:2506.20339v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Deterministically positioned pyramidal InGaAs quantum dots (QDs) exhibit exceptional quantum properties, making them highly promising candidates for scalable on-chip quantum information processing. In this work, we investigate the coherent dynamics of positively charged excitons under the influence of strong magnetic fields in the Faraday configuration. Pyramidal quantum dots exhibit a fourfold splitting of the charged excitons even in the Faraday configuration, giving rise to an optically addressable double-Lambda{\Lambda} system akin to self-assembled quantum dots in oblique magnetic fields. Here, we investigate ultrafast complete coherent control of the trion to ground state transition utilizing advanced optical resonant excitation techniques and we observe quantum coherence over timescales that are similar to other prominent quantum dot platforms. These results pave the way towards establishing site-controlled pyramidal InGaAs QDs as scalable platforms for quantum information processing, expanding the reach of coherent control to new quantum systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.20339v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：确定性定位的金字塔形 InGaAs 量子点 (QD) 表现出卓越的量子特性，使其成为可扩展片上量子信息处理的极有希望的候选者。在这项工作中，我们研究了法拉第构型中强磁场影响下带正电激子的相干动力学。即使在法拉第配置中，金字塔形量子点也表现出带电激子的四倍分裂，从而产生类似于倾斜磁场中自组装量子点的光学可寻址双 Lambda{\Lambda} 系统。在这里，我们利用先进的光学谐振激发技术研究了三重子到基态跃迁的超快完全相干控制，并观察了与其他著名量子点平台类似的时间尺度上的量子相干性。这些结果为建立位点控制的金字塔形 InGaAs 量子点作为量子信息处理的可扩展平台铺平了道路，从而将相干控制的范围扩展到新的量子系统。
