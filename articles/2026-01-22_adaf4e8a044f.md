## Abstract
arXiv:2409.20446v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We investigate the phase relationship between the oscillating (rf) excitation field and the detected (light) power modulation in scalar rf-driven optically pumped magnetometers (OPMs), in particular in the $M_x$ configuration. While the static dependence of the demodulation phase on the direction of the external static magnetic field vector can be largely mitigated by aligning the oscillating rf field along the light propagation direction, we demonstrate that a dynamic (transient) phase response arises under magnetic field tilts. We analytically solve the corresponding modified Bloch equation and confirm agreement with experimental observations obtained using an $M_x$ magnetometer incorporating a paraffin-coated Cs vapor cell. The results reveal fundamental limitations of $M_x$ magnetometers regarding response time and accuracy, in particular when employed with active electronic feedback, such as a phase-locked loop. Therefore, this work is highly relevant to important magnetometry applications where the direction of the quasi-static magnetic field of interest is unknown \textit{a priori} or varies over time, or in measurements requiring a large detection bandwidth. Such conditions are encountered in applications such as geomagnetic surveying, particularly with mobile platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2409.20446v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们研究了标量射频驱动光泵磁力计 (OPM) 中振荡 (rf) 激励场与检测到的（光）功率调制之间的相位关系，特别是在 $M_x$ 配置中。虽然通过沿着光传播方向对准振荡射频场可以很大程度上减轻解调相位对外部静态磁场矢量方向的静态依赖性，但我们证明了在磁场倾斜下会出现动态（瞬态）相位响应。我们分析求解了相应的修改后的布洛赫方程，并确认与使用包含石蜡涂层铯蒸气池的 $M_x$ 磁力计获得的实验观察结果一致。结果揭示了 $M_x$ 磁力计在响应时间和精度方面的基本局限性，特别是在与锁相环等有源电子反馈一起使用时。因此，这项工作与重要的磁力测量应用高度相关，其中感兴趣的准静态磁场的方向未知\textit{先验}或随时间变化，或者在需要大检测带宽的测量中。这种情况在地磁勘测等应用中会遇到，特别是移动平台。
