## Abstract
TLDR: A centrosymetric to non-centrosymmetric (R3c) transition in metallic LiOsO3 that is structurally equivalent to the ferroelectric transition of LiNbO3 is reported, which establishes a new class of materials whose properties may differ from those of normal metals.
