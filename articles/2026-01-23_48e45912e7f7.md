## Abstract
arXiv:2508.12444v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Symmetry-protected non-Abelian (SPNA) statistics opens new frontiers in quantum statistics and enriches the schemes for topological quantum computing. In this work, we propose a new paradigm of SPNA statistics in one-dimensional correlated bosonic symmetry-protected topological (SPT) phases and uncover exotic universal features from a systematic investigation. In particular, we show that for generic bosonic SPT phases described by real Hamiltonians, the SPNA statistics of topological zero modes fall into two distinct classes. The first class exhibits conventional braiding of hard-core bosonic zero modes. Furthermore, we discover a second class of unconventional braiding statistics characterized by a nonlinear transformation, featuring a fractionalization of the first class and reminiscent of the non-Abelian statistics of symmetry-protected Majorana pairs. The two distinct classes of statistics have topological origin in classifying non-Abelian Berry phases for braiding processes of real-Hamiltonian systems, distinguished by whether the holonomy involves a reflection operation. To illustrate, we focus on a specific bosonic SPT phase with particle-hole symmetry, and demonstrate that both classes of braiding statistics can be feasibly realized in a tri-junction with and without the aid of a controlled defect, respectively. Analytic and numerical results are given. We demonstrate how to encode logical qubits and implement both single- and two-qubit gates using the two classes of SPNA statistics. Finally, we propose feasible experimental schemes to observe these predictions and identify the parameter regimes for the high-fidelity braiding, paving the way for the experimental observation of our results in the near future.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.12444v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：对称保护非阿贝尔（SPNA）统计开辟了量子统计的新领域，丰富了拓扑量子计算的方案。在这项工作中，我们在一维相关玻色对称保护拓扑（SPT）相中提出了 SPNA 统计的新范式，并通过系统研究揭示了奇异的普遍特征。特别是，我们表明，对于真实哈密顿量描述的通用玻色子 SPT 相，拓扑零模态的 SPNA 统计分为两个不同的类别。第一类展示了硬核玻色子零模式的传统编织。此外，我们发现了第二类非常规编织统计，其特征是非线性变换，其特征是第一类的分数化，让人想起对称保护马约拉纳对的非阿贝尔统计。这两类不同的统计数据在对实哈密尔顿系统的编织过程的非阿贝尔贝里相进行分类方面具有拓扑起源，通过完整学是否涉及反射运算来区分。为了说明这一点，我们重点关注具有粒子孔对称性的特定玻色子 SPT 相，并证明两类编织统计数据都可以分别在有或没有受控缺陷的帮助下在三结中实现。给出了分析和数值结果。我们演示了如何使用两类 SPNA 统计数据对逻辑量子位进行编码并实现单量子位门和双量子位门。最后，我们提出了可行的实验方案来观察这些预测并确定高保真编织的参数范围，为不久的将来对我们的结果进行实验观察铺平道路。
