## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02938v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In computational physics and materials science, first-principles methods, particularly density functional theory, have become central tools for electronic structure prediction and materials design. Recently, rapid advances in artificial intelligence (AI) have begun to reshape the research landscape, giving rise to the emerging field of deep-learning electronic structure calculations. Despite numerous pioneering studies, the field remains in its early stages; existing software implementations are often fragmented, lacking unified frameworks and standardized interfaces required for broad community adoption. Here we present DeepH-pack, a comprehensive and unified software package that integrates first-principles calculations with deep learning. By incorporating fundamental physical principles into neural-network design, such as the nearsightedness principle and the equivariance principle, DeepH-pack achieves robust cross-scale and cross-material generalizability. This allows models trained on small-scale structures to generalize to large-scale and previously unseen materials. The toolkit preserves first-principles accuracy while accelerating electronic structure calculations by several orders of magnitude, establishing an efficient and intelligent computational paradigm for large-scale materials simulation, high-throughput materials database construction, and AI-driven materials discovery.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02938v1 发布类型：新 摘要：在计算物理和材料科学中，第一性原理方法，特别是密度泛函理论，已成为电子结构预测和材料设计的核心工具。近年来，人工智能（AI）的快速发展开始重塑研究格局，催生了深度学习电子结构计算这一新兴领域。尽管有许多开创性的研究，但该领域仍处于早期阶段。现有的软件实现往往是分散的，缺乏广泛社区采用所需的统一框架和标准化接口。在这里，我们推出 DeepH-pack，这是一个将第一性原理计算与深度学习集成在一起的全面且统一的软件包。通过将基本物理原理融入神经网络设计中，例如近视原理和等方差原理，DeepH-pack 实现了强大的跨尺度和跨材料的泛化性。这使得在小规模结构上训练的模型能够推广到大规模和以前未见过的材料。该工具包在保持第一原理精度的同时，将电子结构计算速度提高了几个数量级，为大规模材料模拟、高通量材料数据库构建和人工智能驱动的材料发现建立了高效、智能的计算范式。
