## Abstract
arXiv:2510.02231v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Organic Quantum Chains (OQCs) represent a newly synthesized class of carbon-based nanostructures whose quasi-one-dimensional nature gives rise to unconventional electronic and transport phenomena. Here we investigate the electronic and transport properties of recently synthesized OQCs [Nature Communications, 12, 5895 (2021)]. Structural stability was first assessed through molecular dynamics relaxation combined with density functional theory (DFT). The optimized coordinates are then used in a tight-binding model with exponentially decaying hopping parameterization, which reproduces the DFT results with high accuracy. Our calculations reveal a robust and nearly constant energy gap across several OQC configurations, in agreement with experimental data. We also identify emergent hierarchical states, characterized by distinct localization behaviors within sets of localized bands. Finally, we analyze different transport responses in scenarios involving the one-dimensional OQC coupled to carbon corrals, as observed in the experimental data, highlighting their potential as promising systems for application in carbon nanodevices.

arXiv:2510.02231v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：有机量子链（OQC）代表了一类新合成的碳基纳米结构，其准一维性质产生了非常规的电子和传输现象。在这里，我们研究了最近合成的 OQC 的电子和传输特性 [Nature Communications, 12, 5895 (2021)]。首先通过分子动力学弛豫结合密度泛函理论（DFT）评估结构稳定性。然后将优化后的坐标用于具有指数衰减跳跃参数化的紧束缚模型中，从而以高精度再现 DFT 结果。我们的计算揭示了几种 OQC 配置中强大且几乎恒定的能隙，与实验数据一致。我们还识别了新兴的分层状态，其特征是局部带集中的不同局部化行为。最后，我们分析了实验数据中观察到的涉及一维 OQC 与碳畜栏耦合的场景中的不同传输响应，强调了它们作为碳纳米器件应用的有前途的系统的潜力。
