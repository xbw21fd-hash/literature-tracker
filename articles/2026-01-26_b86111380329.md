## Abstract
arXiv:2504.07508v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Parton distribution functions (PDFs) describe the inner, non-perturbative structure of hadrons. Their computation involves matrix elements with a Wilson line along a direction on the light cone, posing significant challenges in Euclidean lattice calculations, where the time direction is not directly accessible. We propose implementing the light-front Wilson line within the Hamiltonian formalism using tensor network techniques. The approach is demonstrated in the massive Schwinger model (quantum electrodynamics in 1+1 dimensions), a toy model that shares key features with quantum chromodynamics. We present accurate continuum results for the fermion PDF of the vector meson at varying fermion masses, obtained from first-principle calculations directly in Minkowski space. Our strategy also provides a useful path for quantum simulations and quantum computing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.07508v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：帕顿分布函数（PDF）描述了强子的内部、非微扰结构。他们的计算涉及沿光锥方向具有威尔逊线的矩阵元素，这对无法直接访问时间方向的欧几里得晶格计算提出了重大挑战。我们建议使用张量网络技术在哈密顿形式主义中实现光锋威尔逊线。该方法在大规模 Schwinger 模型（1+1 维度的量子电动力学）中得到了演示，这是一个与量子色动力学共享关键特征的玩具模型。我们提出了不同费米子质量下矢量介子的费米子 PDF 的准确连续谱结果，这些结果是直接在 Minkowski 空间中通过第一原理计算获得的。我们的策略还为量子模拟和量子计算提供了一条有用的途径。
