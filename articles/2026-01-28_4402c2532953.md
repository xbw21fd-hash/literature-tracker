## Abstract
arXiv:2407.18188v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Coronal mass ejections (CMEs) are powerful drivers of space weather, with magnetic flux ropes (MFRs) widely regarded as their primary precursors. However, the variation in reconnection flux during the evolution of MFR during CME eruptions remains poorly understood. In this paper, we develop a realistic 3D magneto-hydrodynamic model using which we explore the temporal evolution of reconnection flux during the MFR evolution using both numerical simulations and observational data. Our initial coronal configuration features an isothermal atmosphere and a potential arcade magnetic field beneath which an MFR emerges at the lower boundary. As the MFR rises, we observe significant stretching and compression of the overlying magnetic field beneath it. Magnetic reconnection begins with the gradual formation of a current sheet, eventually culminating with the impulsive expulsion of the flux rope. We analyze the temporal evolution of reconnection fluxes during two successive MFR eruptions while continuously emerging the twisted flux rope through the lower boundary. We also conduct a similar analysis using observational data from the Helioseismic and Magnetic Imager (HMI) and the Atmospheric Imaging Assembly (AIA) for an eruptive event. Comparing our MHD simulation with observational data, we find that reconnection flux play a crucial role in determination of CME speeds. From the onset to the eruption, the reconnection flux shows a strong linear correlation with the velocity. This nearly realistic simulation of a solar eruption provides important insights into the complex dynamics of CME initiation and progression.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2407.18188v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：日冕物质抛射 (CME) 是太空天气的强大驱动因素，磁通绳 (MFR) 被广泛认为是其主要前兆。然而，人们对日冕物质抛射期间 MFR 演化过程中重联通量的变化仍知之甚少。在本文中，我们开发了一个真实的 3D 磁流体动力学模型，利用数值模拟和观测数据探索 MFR 演化过程中重联通量的时间演化。我们最初的日冕结构具有等温大气层和潜在的拱形磁场，在该磁场下，磁流率出现在下边界。随着 MFR 的上升，我们观察到其下方的上覆磁场发生显着的拉伸和压缩。磁重联开始于电流片的逐渐形成，最终以磁通绳的脉冲排出而达到顶峰。我们分析了两次连续 MFR 喷发期间重联通量的时间演化，同时通过下边界不断出现扭曲的通量绳。我们还使用日震磁成像仪 (HMI) 和大气成像组件 (AIA) 的观测数据对喷发事件进行了类似的分析。将 MHD 模拟与观测数据进行比较，我们发现重联通量在确定 CME 速度方面起着至关重要的作用。从开始到喷发，重联通量与速度呈现出很强的线性相关性。这种近乎真实的太阳喷发模拟为日冕物质抛射的启动和进展的复杂动态提供了重要的见解。
