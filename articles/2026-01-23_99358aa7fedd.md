## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15525v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The ab-initio thermodynamic properties of simple cubic polonium ({\alpha}-Po) were studied within the quasi-harmonic approximation (QHA), where both lattice vibrations (phonons) and electronic excitations contributions are included in the Helmholtz free energy. We investigate the influence of spin-orbit coupling (SOC) by comparing the scalar relativistic (SR) and fully relativistic (FR) pseudopotentials on the thermodynamic properties of polonium and evaluate the performance of three popular exchange-correlation functionals, GGA (PBE and PBEsol), and LDA (PZ). Temperature and pressure-dependent thermodynamic properties were compared with the available experimental and theoretical studies. We found that the effect of electronic excitations is negligible for all the thermodynamic properties. LDA+SOC provides a better agreement with the experimental volume, while the thermal expansion coefficients from LDA+SOC and PBEsol + SOC closely match experimental values. SOC effects appear insignificant for the isobaric heat capacity but substantially contribute to the adiabatic bulk modulus. The phonon dispersions and mode-Gr\"uneisen parameters ({\gamma}_q{\eta}) were interpolated at the lattice constant corresponding to 301 K. The SR dispersions exhibit several anomalies in all directions, which were suppressed significantly by the inclusion of SOC. The role of SOC on the elastic constant-coefficient (C_ij) and elastic anisotropy factor at 0 K is also studied. The Pugh ratio confirmed that simple cubic polonium is ductile, and we observed that the elastic anisotropy factor and Pugh ratio decreased with increasing pressure. The effect of SOC on the Cauchy pressure is also determined.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15525v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在准调和近似 (QHA) 内研究了简单立方钋 ({\alpha}-Po) 的从头算热力学性质，其中晶格振动（声子）和电子激发贡献都包含在亥姆霍兹自由能中。我们通过比较标量相对论 (SR) 和完全相对论 (FR) 赝势对钋热力学性质的影响来研究自旋轨道耦合 (SOC) 的影响，并评估三种流行的交换相关泛函 GGA（PBE 和 PBEsol）和 LDA (PZ) 的性能。将与温度和压力相关的热力学性质与现有的实验和理论研究进行了比较。我们发现电子激发的影响对于所有热力学性质来说可以忽略不计。 LDA+SOC 与实验体积更加一致，而 LDA+SOC 和 PBEsol + SOC 的热膨胀系数与实验值非常匹配。 SOC 效应对于等压热容似乎不显着，但对绝热体积模量有很大贡献。声子色散和模Gr\"uneisen参数({\gamma}_q{\eta})在对应于301 K的晶格常数处进行插值。SR色散在各个方向上表现出一些异常，这些异常被SOC的包含所显着抑制。还研究了SOC对0 K下弹性常数系数(C_ij)和弹性各向异性因子的作用。Pugh比证实了简单立方钋延性，我们观察到弹性各向异性因子和 Pugh 比随着压力的增加而降低。还确定了 SOC 对柯西压力的影响。
