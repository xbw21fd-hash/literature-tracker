## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03369v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Universal machine-learning interatomic potentials (uMLIPs) have become powerful tools for accelerating computational materials discovery by replacing expensive first-principles calculations in crystal structure prediction (CSP). However, their effectiveness in identifying new, complex materials remains uncertain. Here, we systematically assess the capability of a uMLIP (i.e.,M3GNet) to accelerate CSP in quaternary oxides. Through extensive exploration of the Sr-Li-Al-O and Ba-Y-Al-O systems, we show that uMLIP can rediscover experimentally known materials absent from its training set and identify seven new thermodynamically and dynamically stable compounds. These include a new polymorph of Sr2LiAlO4 (P3221) and a new disordered phase, Sr2Li4Al2O7 (P1_bar). Furthermore, our results show stability predictions based on the semilocal PBE functional require cross-validation with higher-level methods, such as SCAN and RPA, to ensure reliability. While uMLIPs substantially reduce the computational cost of CSP, the primary bottleneck has shifted to the efficiency of search algorithms in navigating complex structural spaces. This work highlights both the promise and current limitations of uMLIP-driven CSP in the discovery of new materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03369v1 公告类型：新 摘要：通用机器学习原子间势（uMLIP）已成为通过取代晶体结构预测（CSP）中昂贵的第一原理计算来加速计算材料发现的强大工具。然而，它们在识别新的复杂材料方面的有效性仍然不确定。在这里，我们系统地评估了 uMLIP（即 M3GNet）加速四元氧化物中 CSP 的能力。通过对 Sr-Li-Al-O 和 Ba-Y-Al-O 体系的广泛探索，我们表明 uMLIP 可以重新发现其训练集中缺少的实验已知材料，并识别出七种新的热力学和动态稳定化合物。其中包括新的多晶型 Sr2LiAlO4 (P3221) 和新的无序相 Sr2Li4Al2O7 (P1_bar)。此外，我们的结果表明，基于半局部 PBE 函数的稳定性预测需要与 SCAN 和 RPA 等更高级别的方法进行交叉验证，以确保可靠性。虽然 uMLIP 大大降低了 CSP 的计算成本，但主要瓶颈已转移到搜索算法在复杂结构空间中导航的效率。这项工作强调了 uMLIP 驱动的 CSP 在新材料发现方面的前景和当前的局限性。
