## Abstract
arXiv:2504.16847v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: One route to the control of quantum magnetism at ultrafast timescales is magnetophononics, the modulation of magnetic interactions by coherently driven lattice excitations. Theoretical studies of a gapped quantum magnet subject to continuous, single-frequency driving of one strongly coupled phonon mode find intriguing phenomena including mutually repelling phonon-bitriplon excitations and global renormalization of the spin excitation spectrum. Because experiments are performed with ultrashort pulses that contain a wide range of driving frequencies, we investigate phonon-bitriplon physics under pulsed laser driving. We use the equations of motion to compute the transient response of the driven and dissipative spin-phonon system, which we characterize using the phonon displacement, phonon number, and triplon occupations. In the Fourier transforms of each quantity we discover a low-frequency energetic oscillation between the lattice and spin sectors, which is an intrinsically nonequilibrium collective mode, and demonstrate its origin as a beating between mutually repelling composite excitations. We introduce a phonon-bitriplon approximation that captures all the physics of hybridization, collective mode formation, and difference-frequency excitation, and show that sum-frequency phenomena also leave clear signatures in the repsonse. We model the appearance of such magnetophononic phenomena in the strongly-coupled spin-chain compound CuGeO$_3$, whose overlapping phonon and spin excitation spectra are well characterized, to deduce the criteria for their possible observation in quantum magnetic materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.16847v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在超快时间尺度上控制量子磁性的一种途径是磁声学，即通过相干驱动的晶格激发来调制磁相互作用。对带隙量子磁体进行连续、单频驱动的强耦合声子模式的理论研究发现了有趣的现象，包括相互排斥的声子-双子激发和自旋激发谱的全局重正化。由于实验是使用包含广泛驱动频率的超短脉冲进行的，因此我们研究了脉冲激光驱动下的声子双三子物理。我们使用运动方程来计算驱动和耗散自旋声子系统的瞬态响应，我们使用声子位移、声子数和三重子占据来表征该系统。在每个量的傅立叶变换中，我们发现晶格和自旋扇区之间存在低频能量振荡，这是一种本质上非平衡的集体模式，并证明其起源是相互排斥的复合激发之间的跳动。我们引入了声子双三子近似，它捕获了杂化、集体模式形成和差频激励的所有物理原理，并表明和频现象也在响应中留下了清晰的特征。我们对强耦合自旋链化合物 CuGeO$_3$ 中此类磁声现象的出现进行了建模，其重叠声子和自旋激发光谱得到了很好的表征，从而推导出在量子磁性材料中可能观察到的标准。
