## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12833v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a method to prepare and certify Gaussian quantum states of the ferromagnetic resonance spin-wave modes in ferromagnets using a longitudinal drive. Contrary to quantum optics-based strategies, our approach harnesses a purely magnonic feature - the spin-wave nonlinearity - to generate magnon squeezing. This resource is used to prepare vacuum-squeezed states, as well as entangled states between modes of different magnets coupled via a microwave cavity. We propose methods to detect such states with classical methods, such as ferromagnetic resonance or local pickup coils, and quantify the required detection efficiency. We analytically solve the case of ellipsoidal yttrium iron garnet ferrimagnets, but our method applies to a vast range of shapes and sizes. Our work enables quantum magnonics experiments without single-magnon sources or detectors (qubits), thus bringing the quantum regime within reach of the wider magnonics community.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12833v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种使用纵向驱动来制备和验证铁磁体中铁磁共振自旋波模式的高斯量子态的方法。与基于量子光学的策略相反，我们的方法利用纯磁振子特征（自旋波非线性）来产生磁振子挤压。该资源用于准备真空压缩态，以及通过微波腔耦合的不同磁体模式之间的纠缠态。我们提出了用经典方法（例如铁磁共振或局部拾波线圈）检测此类状态的方法，并量化所需的检测效率。我们分析解决了椭圆体钇铁石榴石亚铁磁体的情况，但我们的方法适用于各种形状和尺寸。我们的工作能够在没有单磁振子源或探测器（量子位）的情况下进行量子磁振子实验，从而使更广泛的磁振子社区能够接触到量子体系。
