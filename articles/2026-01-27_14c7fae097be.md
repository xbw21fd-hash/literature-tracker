## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18291v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Linear electric circuits composed of inductors and capacitors can serve as analogues of tight-binding models that describe the electronic band structure of materials. This mapping provides a versatile approach for exploring topological phenomena within engineered electrical lattices. In this work, the two-dimensional Su-Schrieffer-Heeger model is examined through electric circuit analogues to study the interplay between higher-order topology, bound states in the continuum, and disorder. Building upon this model, the effect of introducing next-nearest-neighbour interactions that preserve chiral and spatial symmetries of the system is analyzed. The results reveal that even without Hamiltonian separability, corner-localized bound states in the continuum remain protected by symmetry in the long-range coupled lattice. This robustness highlights the potential of circuit-based platforms for probing advanced topological phenomena in a highly controllable setting.

arXiv:2601.18291v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由电感器和电容器组成的线性电路可以作为描述材料电子能带结构的紧束缚模型的模拟。这种映射为探索工程电晶格内的拓扑现象提供了一种通用的方法。在这项工作中，通过电路模拟来检验二维 Su-Schrieffer-Heeger 模型，以研究高阶拓扑、连续体中的束缚态和无序之间的相互作用。在此模型的基础上，分析了引入保持系统手性和空间对称性的次近邻相互作用的效果。结果表明，即使没有哈密顿可分性，连续体中的角局域束缚态仍然受到长程耦合晶格中对称性的保护。这种鲁棒性凸显了基于电路的平台在高度可控的环境中探测先进拓扑现象的潜力。
