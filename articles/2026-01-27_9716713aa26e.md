## Abstract
arXiv:2511.14553v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We propose a precise and efficient physics-augmented neural network (PANN) to model strain-induced crystallization in rubbery polymers. We demonstrate that the model can be flexibly employed for both unfilled and filled natural rubber (NR). The approach is based on a two potential framework, similar to the concept of generalized standard materials (GSMs). To describe the material behavior, neural network-based free energy and dissipation potentials are employed. The evolution of crystallinity is derived from the two potentials. To ensure boundedness of the crystallinity, a novel constrained GSM-type evolution problem is proposed. To this end, two additional Lagrange multipliers together with the corresponding Karush-Kuhn-Tucker conditions are introduced. As a result, it is guaranteed that crystallinity can be interpreted as a variable of concentration type. The neural network-based potentials ensure all physically desirable properties by construction. Most importantly, objectivity, material symmetry and thermodynamic consistency are automatically fulfilled. In addition, an alternative derivation of the governing model equations in time-discrete form is presented based on an incremental variational framework, which also serves as the basis for a finite element implementation. We demonstrate the predictive capability of the PANN using three different experimental data sets from literature, considering both stress and crystallinity evolution at material point level as well as the corresponding field distributions in a notched specimen. Moreover, we show that model parameterization is also possible when experimental crystallinity data is not available, still enabling suitable stress predictions.

arXiv:2511.14553v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出了一种精确高效的物理增强神经网络（PANN）来模拟橡胶聚合物中应变诱导的结晶。我们证明该模型可以灵活地应用于未填充和填充天然橡胶（NR）。该方法基于两个潜在框架，类似于通用标准材料（GSM）的概念。为了描述材料行为，采用基于神经网络的自由能和耗散势。结晶度的演变源自这两种势。为了确保结晶度的有界性，提出了一种新颖的约束GSM型演化问题。为此，引入了两个附加的拉格朗日乘子以及相应的卡鲁什-库恩-塔克条件。因此，可以保证结晶度可以解释为浓度类型的变量。基于神经网络的势能通过构造确保所有物理上所需的特性。最重要的是，客观性、材料对称性和热力学一致性是自动实现的。此外，基于增量变分框架提出了时间离散形式的控制模型方程的替代推导，这也作为有限元实现的基础。我们使用文献中的三个不同的实验数据集证明了 PANN 的预测能力，考虑了材料点水平上的应力和结晶度演化以及缺口样本中相应的场分布。此外，我们表明，当实验结晶度数据不可用时，模型参数化也是可能的，仍然可以进行适当的应力预测。
