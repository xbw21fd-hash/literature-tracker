# Moir\'e magnetism in a bilayer Ising model

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18955v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Moir\'e patterns in magnetic bilayers generate spatially modulated interlayer exchange interactions that can give rise to nonuniform magnetic textures. We study a minimal classical bilayer Ising model with a moir\'e-modulated interlayer coupling, generated either by relative twist or differential strain between the layers. Using large-scale classical Monte Carlo simulations, we show that the ordering transition remains in the conventional two-dimensional Ising universality class, even when the low-temperature state is domain-textured. At low temperatures, we find a smooth crossover between a uniform ferromagnet and domain-textured state, in which the spins locally follow the sign of the interlayer exchange. We demonstrate that there is no breaking of layer symmetry for twisted bilayers. The location of the crossover is determined by a simple geometric energy balance between bulk interlayer exchange and intralayer domain-wall costs. Our results provide a minimal framework for understanding how moir\'e-modulated magnetic textures can emerge from geometric energetics without requiring a thermodynamic phase transition.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18955v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁性双层中的莫尔图案会产生空间调制的层间交换相互作用，从而产生不均匀的磁性纹理。我们研究了一个最小的经典双层 Ising 模型，该模型具有莫尔条纹调制的层间耦合，该耦合是由层之间的相对扭曲或差应变生成的。使用大规模经典蒙特卡罗模拟，我们表明即使低温状态是域纹理的，有序转变仍然保持在传统的二维伊辛普适性类中。在低温下，我们发现均匀铁磁体和域纹理状态之间存在平滑交叉，其中自旋局部遵循层间交换的符号。我们证明了扭曲双层的层对称性没有被破坏。交叉的位置由块层间交换和层内畴壁成本之间的简单几何能量平衡决定。我们的结果为理解如何在不需要热力学相变的情况下从几何能量学中出现摩尔纹调制的磁性纹理提供了一个最小的框架。
