## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03620v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Technological developments and the impact of artificial intelligence (AI) are omnipresent themes and concerns of the present day. Much has been written on these topics but applications of quantitative models to understand the techno-social landscape have been much more limited. We propose a mathematical model that can help understand in a unified manner the patterns underlying technological development and also identify the different regimes in which the technological landscape evolves. First, we develop a model of innovation diffusion between different technologies, the growth of each reinforcing the development of the others. The model has a variable that quantifies the level of development (or innovation, discovery) potential for a given technology. The potential, or market capacity, increases via diffusion from related technologies, reflecting the fact that a technology does not develop in isolation. Hence, the growth of each technology is influenced by how developed its neighboring (related) technologies are. This allows us to reproduce long-term trends seen in computing technology and large language models (LLMs). We then present a three-dimensional system of supply, demand, and investment which shows oscillations (business cycles) emerging if investment is too high into a given technology, product, or market. We finally combine the two models through a common variable and show that if investment or diffusion is too high in the network context, chaotic boom-bust cycles can emerge. These quantitative considerations allow us to reproduce the boom-bust patterns seen in non-fungible token (NFT) transaction data and also have deep implications for the development of AI which we highlight, such as the arrival of a new AI winter.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03620v1 公告类型：新 摘要：技术发展和人工智能 (AI) 的影响是当今无所不在的主题和关注点。关于这些主题已经有很多文章，但用于理解技术社会景观的定量模型的应用却受到更多限制。我们提出了一个数学模型，可以帮助以统一的方式理解技术发展的模式，并识别技术景观演变的不同机制。首先，我们开发了不同技术之间的创新扩散模型，每种技术的增长都促进了其他技术的发展。该模型有一个变量，用于量化给定技术的开发（或创新、发现）潜力水平。潜力或市场容量通过相关技术的扩散而增加，反映了技术并不是孤立发展的事实。因此，每项技术的发展都受到其邻近（相关）技术的发展程度的影响。这使我们能够重现计算技术和大型语言模型 (LLM) 中的长期趋势。然后，我们提出了一个供给、需求和投资的三维系统，该系统显示，如果对特定技术、产品或市场的投资过高，就会出现振荡（经济周期）。最后，我们通过一个共同变量将这两个模型结合起来，并表明如果网络环境中的投资或扩散过高，就会出现混乱的繁荣-萧条周期。这些定量考虑使我们能够重现不可替代代币（NFT）交易数据中看到的繁荣-萧条模式，并且对我们强调的人工智能的发展产生深远的影响，例如新的人工智能冬天的到来。
