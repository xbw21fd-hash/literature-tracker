## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20040v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Particle entrainment in confined gas-particle systems driven by moving boundaries is central to many industrial and natural processes, including pharmaceutical manufacturing, food processing, and chemical engineering. Although often termed a "suction effect," its physical origin remains unclear, especially under unsteady flow, strong particle interactions, and transient force networks. Here we study suction-induced entrainment in a prototypical confined system using high-fidelity coupled CFD-DEM simulations resolving unsteady gas flow and discrete particle motion with moving boundaries. By decomposing the forces on individual particles, we show that suction is not purely pressure-driven, but results from the combined action of pressure-gradient and unsteady drag forces generated by boundary-accelerated flow. Despite the heterogeneous and transient force fields, the final entrained mass is found to be governed primarily by a single energetic measure: the mechanical work performed on the particle assembly in the entrainment direction during boundary motion, rather than by peak instantaneous forces. Varying boundary kinematics demonstrates that changes in displacement or velocity history control entrainment mainly by modifying the duration over which fluid-particle forces perform work. These results reveal an organizing principle for suction-driven entrainment and establish a work-based framework for boundary-induced particle transport in confined gas-particle systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20040v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由移动边界驱动的受限气体粒子系统中的粒子夹带是许多工业和自然过程的核心，包括制药、食品加工和化学工程。尽管通常被称为“吸力效应”，但其物理起源仍不清楚，特别是在不稳定流动、强粒子相互作用和瞬态力网络的情况下。在这里，我们使用高保真耦合 CFD-DEM 模拟研究原型受限系统中吸力引起的夹带，解决不稳定气流和具有移动边界的离散粒子运动。通过分解单个颗粒上的力，我们表明吸力并非纯粹由压力驱动，而是由压力梯度和边界加速流产生的不稳定阻力共同作用的结果。尽管存在非均质和瞬态力场，但最终的夹带质量主要由单一能量测量控制：边界运动期间在夹带方向上对颗粒组件执行的机械功，而不是峰值瞬时力。不同的边界运动学表明，位移或速度历史的变化主要通过修改流体-粒子力做功的持续时间来控制夹带。这些结果揭示了吸力驱动夹带的组织原理，并为受限气体粒子系统中边界诱导粒子输运建立了基于工作的框架。
