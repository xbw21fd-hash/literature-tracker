## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03697v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Two-magnon modes are terahertz-frequency magnetic excitations in antiferromagnets, governed by exchange interactions and involving magnons from the entire Brillouin zone. The ability to couple to light promotes two-magnon modes as contenders for ultrafast optical manipulation of the magnetic state, beyond conventional zone-center magnonics. While magnon-magnon interactions are known to critically shape the two-magnon line in spontaneous Raman scattering spectra, their role in coherent time-domain excitations remains unexplored. We report a detailed experimental and theoretical study of the influence of magnon-magnon interactions on coherent two-magnon modes in a cubic antiferromagnet excited via Impulsive Stimulated Raman scattering. We reveal the nontrivial evolution of coherent magnetic dynamics in the time domain and the corresponding spectrum and compare it with the spontaneous Raman scattering spectrum. By extending the spin-correlations based theory for two-magnon modes, we derive a unified description of their spectra in Raman Scattering and Impulsive Stimulated Raman Scattering and highlight the role of magnon-magnon interactions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03697v1 公告类型：新 摘要：双磁振子模式是反铁磁体中的太赫兹频率磁激励，受交换相互作用控制，涉及整个布里渊区的磁振子。与光耦合的能力使双磁振子模式成为超越传统区域中心磁振子的磁态超快光学操纵的竞争者。虽然已知磁振子-磁振子相互作用对自发拉曼散射光谱中的双磁振子线具有重要影响，但它们在相干时域激发中的作用仍未被探索。我们报告了磁振子-磁振子相互作用对通过脉冲受激拉曼散射激发的立方反铁磁体中相干双磁振子模式的影响的详细实验和理论研究。我们揭示了时域相干磁动力学的非平凡演化和相应的光谱，并将其与自发拉曼散射光谱进行比较。通过扩展基于自旋相关的双磁振子模式理论，我们得出了拉曼散射和脉冲受激拉曼散射光谱的统一描述，并强调了磁振子-磁振子相互作用的作用。
