## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15361v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The (111)-oriented 4d ferromagnetic perovskite SrRuO3 (SRO) offers a unique triangular-lattice geometry, making it a promising platform for exploring Berry-curvature-driven and spin-orbit-coupled transport. Here, we present a systematic study of the structure, magnetism, and magnetotransport of high-quality SRO (111) thin films with thicknesses t = 1.2-60 nm grown on SrTiO3 (111) substrates by machine-learning-assisted molecular beam epitaxy. We achieved a residual resistivity ratio of 45.5 in a 60 nm-thick film, the highest reported for this orientation, enabling access to intrinsic electronic and magnetic behavior. Temperature-dependent resistivity confirms Fermi-liquid transport below 15 K in both coherently strained (t = 10, 20 nm) and strain-relaxed (t = 60 nm) films, thereby enabling detailed magnetotransport and magnetic measurements. The linear, non-saturating positive magnetoresistance persists up to 14 T, while Hall-effect measurements and temperature scaling separate intrinsic (Karplus-Luttinger) and extrinsic (side-jump) contributions to the anomalous Hall effect, with the relative weight tuned by (111) epitaxial strain. X-ray magnetic circular dichroism at the Ru M2,3 and O K edges, together with SQUID magnetometry, demonstrates an intrinsically low-spin Ru ground state for both coherently strained and relaxed films, resolving ambiguities among prior reports. These detailed crystalline, electrical, and magnetic characterizations provide a rigorous foundation for understanding and engineering quantum transport in SRO (111).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15361v1 公告类型：新 摘要：(111) 取向的 4d 铁磁钙钛矿 SrRuO3 (SRO) 提供了独特的三角晶格几何形状，使其成为探索贝里曲率驱动和自旋轨道耦合输运的有前途的平台。在这里，我们对通过机器学习辅助分子束外延在 SrTiO3 (111) 基板上生长的厚度 t = 1.2-60 nm 的高质量 SRO (111) 薄膜的结构、磁性和磁输运进行了系统研究。我们在 60 nm 厚的薄膜中实现了 45.5 的残余电阻率比，这是该方向报道的最高值，从而能够获得固有的电子和磁性行为。与温度相关的电阻率证实了相干应变 (t = 10, 20 nm) 和应变松弛 (t = 60 nm) 薄膜中 15 K 以下的费米液体输运，从而实现了详细的磁输运和磁测量。线性、非饱和正磁阻持续高达 14 T，而霍尔效应测量和温标将异常霍尔效应的内在 (Karplus-Luttinger) 和外在 (侧跳) 贡献分开，相对重量由 (111) 外延应变调整。 Ru M2,3 和 O K 边缘的 X 射线磁性圆二色性以及 SQUID 磁力测量表明，相干应变和松弛薄膜都具有本质上低自旋的 Ru 基态，解决了先前报告中的歧义。这些详细的晶体、电学和磁学特征为理解和设计 SRO 中的量子传输提供了严格的基础 (111)。
