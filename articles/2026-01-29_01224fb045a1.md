## Abstract
arXiv:2504.20031v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The non-Abelian statistics of Majorana zero modes (MZMs) are central to fault-tolerant topological quantum computing. Planar Josephson junctions provide a particularly versatile platform for realizing robust topological superconductivity hosting MZMs over a broad parameter space. However, it is generally believed that such topological superconductivity is restricted to a narrow range of in-plane magnetic field orientations, posing a major obstacle to scalable and noncollinear junction-network architectures. Here, we uncover that the apparent suppression of MZMs under misaligned fields does not arise from the destruction of topological superconductivity itself, but instead originates from emergent shifted bulk states at other momenta that obscure the global excitation gap and MZMs. By introducing spatial modulations along the junction to scatter and gap out these bulk states, we restore a global topological gap and recover MZMs for arbitrary in-plane magnetic field orientations. Remarkably, such modulations can be naturally realized by transforming a straight junction into a curved geometry, rendering the topological gap robust against field misalignment and enabling MZMs survival in complex junction networks. Building on this robustness, we propose a scalable protocol for MZMs braiding and fusion using gate or superconducting-phase control, opening new routes toward scalable topological quantum computing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.20031v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：马约拉纳零模 (MZM) 的非阿贝尔统计量是容错拓扑量子计算的核心。平面约瑟夫森结提供了一个特别通用的平台，用于在广泛的参数空间上实现托管 MZM 的稳健拓扑超导性。然而，人们普遍认为这种拓扑超导性仅限于面内磁场方向的狭窄范围，这对可扩展和非共线的结网络架构构成了主要障碍。在这里，我们发现，错位场下 MZM 的明显抑制并非源于拓扑超导本身的破坏，而是源于其他动量处的突现位移体态，从而掩盖了全局激励间隙和 MZM。通过沿着结引入空间调制来分散和隔离这些体态，我们恢复了全局拓扑间隙并恢复了任意面内磁场方向的 MZM。值得注意的是，这种调制可以通过将直连接转变为弯曲几何形状来自然地实现，使拓扑间隙对场错位具有鲁棒性，并使 MZM 在复杂的连接网络中生存。基于这种稳健性，我们提出了一种使用门或超导相控制的 MZM 编织和融合的可扩展协议，为可扩展拓扑量子计算开辟了新途径。
