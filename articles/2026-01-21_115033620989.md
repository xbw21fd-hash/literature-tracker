## Abstract
arXiv:2301.10382v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We systematically characterize the dynamical evolution of time-parity (PT )-symmetric two-level systems with spin-dependent dissipations. If the control parameters of the gap are linearly tuned with time, the dynamical evolution can be characterized with parabolic cylinder equations which can be analytically solved. We find that the asymptotic behaviors of particle probability on the two levels show initial-state-independent redistribution in the slow-tuning-speed limit as long as the system is nonadiabatically driven across exceptional points. Equal distributions appear when the nondissipative Hamiltonian shows gap closing. So long as the nondissipative Hamiltonian displays level anticrossing, the final distribution becomes unbalanced. The ratios between the occupation probabilities are given analytically. These results are confirmed with numerical simulations. The predicted equal distribution phenomenon may be used to identify the closing of the energy gap from anti-crossing between two energy bands.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2301.10382v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：我们系统地描述了具有自旋相关耗散的时间宇称（PT）对称两能级系统的动态演化。如果间隙的控制参数随时间线性调整，则动力学演化可以用可解析求解的抛物柱面方程来表征。我们发现，只要系统非绝热地驱动穿过异常点，两个层面上的粒子概率的渐近行为就会在慢速调整速度限制下表现出与初始状态无关的重新分布。当非耗散哈密顿量显示间隙闭合时，就会出现相等分布。只要非耗散哈密顿量表现出水平反交叉，最终的分布就会变得不平衡。分析给出了占领概率之间的比率。这些结果通过数值模拟得到证实。预测的均匀分布现象可用于识别两个能带之间的反交叉能隙的闭合。
