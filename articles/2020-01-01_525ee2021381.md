## Abstract
TLDR: A new one-dimensional halide perovskite ferroelectric N,N-dimethylallylammoniumCdCl3 (DMAACdCl 3) exhibits an above room-temperature ferro electric phase transition with a saturated polarization of 1.9 $mu$C cm-2 and a coercive field of 5.0 kV cm-1.
