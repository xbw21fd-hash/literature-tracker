## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05941v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Recent progress in ultrafast x-ray sources helped establish x-rays as an important tool for probing lattice and magnetic dynamics initiated by femtosecond optical pulses. Here, we explore the potential of ultrashort hard x-ray pulses for driving magnetic dynamics. We use a transient grating technique in which a spatially periodic x-ray excitation pattern gives rise to material excitations at a well-defined wave vector, whose dynamics are monitored via diffraction of an optical probe pulse. The excitation of a ferrimagnetic gadolinium bismuth iron garnet film placed in an external tilted magnetic field by x-rays at the Gd L3 edge results in both magnetic and non-magnetic transient gratings whose contributions to the diffracted signal are separated by polarization analysis. We observe the magnetization precession at both longitudinal acoustic and spin wave frequencies. An analysis with the Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert equation indicates that the magnetization precession is driven by strain resulting from thermal expansion induced by absorbed x-rays. The results establish x-ray transient gratings as a tool for driving coherent phonons and magnons, with the potential of accessing wave vectors across the entire Brillouin zone.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05941v1 公告类型：新 摘要：超快 X 射线源的最新进展有助于将 X 射线确立为探测飞秒光脉冲引发的晶格和磁动力学的重要工具。在这里，我们探索超短硬 X 射线脉冲驱动磁动力学的潜力。我们使用瞬态光栅技术，其中空间周期性 X 射线激发图案以明确的波矢量产生材料激发，其动态通过光学探针脉冲的衍射进行监测。通过 Gd L3 边缘处的 X 射线对置于外部倾斜磁场中的亚铁磁性钆铋铁石榴石薄膜进行激发，产生磁性和非磁性瞬态光栅，其对衍射信号的贡献可通过偏振分析来分离。我们观察纵向声波和自旋波频率下的磁化进动。 Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert 方程的分析表明，磁化进动是由吸收 X 射线引起的热膨胀产生的应变驱动的。研究结果确立了 X 射线瞬态光栅作为驱动相干声子和磁振子的工具，具有访问整个布里渊区波矢量的潜力。
