## Abstract
arXiv:2510.17019v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: The control of interactions among quantum emitters through nanophotonic structures offers significant opportunities for quantum technologies. However, a rigorous theoretical description of the interaction of multiple quantum emitters with complex, dispersive dielectric objects remains challenging. Here, we introduce an approach based on the modified Langevin noise formalism that unveils the roles of both the noise polarization currents of the dielectrics and the vacuum fluctuations of the electromagnetic field scattered by the dielectrics. This work extends Refs. \cite{miano_quantum_2025} and \cite{miano_spectral_2025} to the general case of an arbitrary number of emitters. The proposed approach allows us to describe the dynamics of the quantum emitters for arbitrary initial quantum states of the electromagnetic environment, consisting of two independent bosonic reservoirs, a medium-assisted reservoir and a scattering-assisted reservoir, each characterized by its own spectral density matrix. Specifically, we examine situations where both reservoirs are initially in thermal quantum states but have different temperatures. Understanding how these reservoirs shape the dynamics of the emitters is crucial for understanding light-matter interactions in complex electromagnetic environments and for improving intrinsic emitter properties within structured environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.17019v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：通过纳米光子结构控制量子发射器之间的相互作用为量子技术提供了重大机遇。然而，对多个量子发射器与复杂、色散介电物体相互作用的严格理论描述仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们介绍了一种基于修正的朗之万噪声形式主义的方法，该方法揭示了电介质的噪声极化电流和电介质散射的电磁场的真空涨落的作用。这项工作扩展了参考文献。 \cite{miano_quantum_2025} 和 \cite{miano_spectral_2025} 是任意数量发射器的一般情况。所提出的方法使我们能够描述电磁环境任意初始量子态的量子发射器的动力学，由两个独立的玻色子储层、介质辅助储层和散射辅助储层组成，每个储层都有自己的谱密度矩阵。具体来说，我们研究了两个储层最初都处于热量子态但具有不同温度的情况。了解这些储层如何塑造发射器的动力学对于理解复杂电磁环境中的光与物质相互作用以及改善结构化环境中发射器的固有特性至关重要。
