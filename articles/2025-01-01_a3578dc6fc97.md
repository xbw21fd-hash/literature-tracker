## Abstract
TLDR: This roadmap offers an appraisal of the current machine learning-based interatomic potential landscape, delineates the associated challenges, and envisages how progress in this domain can empower atomic-scale modeling of the composition-processing-microstructure-property relationship, underscoring its significance in materials science and engineering.
