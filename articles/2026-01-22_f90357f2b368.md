## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14956v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Freestanding perovskite oxide membranes provide a novel platform for elastic strain engineering, enabling the manipulation of phonon transport free from substrate clamping. In this work, we investigate the thermal transport properties of strontium titanate (SrTiO3) and barium titanate (BaTiO3) membranes subjected to self-formed crease induced inhomogeneous strain. By integrating spatially resolved Frequency-Domain Thermoreflectance (FDTR) with micro-Raman spectroscopy, we observe a sharp, localized suppression of thermal conductivity (k) in high-curvature regions. Specifically, k is reduced from 4.43 to 3.62 W/(m K) in SrTiO3 and from 2.27 to 1.81 W/(m K) in BaTiO3 at the crease centers, directly correlating with the local strain distribution. First-principles calculations reveal that, unlike uniform strain, the symmetry breaking induced by strain gradients significantly broadens phonon dispersion and enhances scattering rates. These findings not only elucidate the microscopic mechanisms governing phonon-strain coupling but also demonstrate the potential of inhomogeneous strain fields as a potent tool for designing dynamic solid-state thermal switches and active thermal management devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14956v1 公告类型：新 摘要：独立式钙钛矿氧化物膜为弹性应变工程提供了一个新颖的平台，能够在不受基底夹持的情况下操纵声子传输。在这项工作中，我们研究了钛酸锶（SrTiO3）和钛酸钡（BaTiO3）膜在自形成折痕诱导的不均匀应变下的热传输特性。通过将空间分辨频域热反射 (FDTR) 与微拉曼光谱相结合，我们观察到高曲率区域中热导率 (k) 的急剧局部抑制。具体而言，在折痕中心处，SrTiO3 的 k 从 4.43 W/(m·K) 降低至 3.62 W/(m·K)，BaTiO3 的 k 从 2.27 W/(m·K) 降低至 1.81 W/(m·K)，与局部应变分布直接相关。第一原理计算表明，与均匀应变不同，应变梯度引起的对称性破缺显着拓宽了声子色散并提高了散射率。这些发现不仅阐明了控制声子应变耦合的微观机制，而且还证明了非均匀应变场作为设计动态固态热开关和主动热管理器件的有效工具的潜力。
