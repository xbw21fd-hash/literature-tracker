## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02245v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Sampling a quantum systems underlying probability distributions is an important computational task, e.g., for quantum advantage experiments and quantum Monte Carlo algorithms. Tensor networks are an invaluable tool for efficiently representing states of large quantum systems with limited entanglement. Algorithms for sampling one-dimensional (1D) tensor networks are well-established and utilized in several 1D tensor network methods. In this paper we introduce two novel sampling algorithms for two-dimensional (2D) isometric tensor network states (isoTNS) that can be viewed as extensions of algorithms for 1D tensor networks. The first algorithm we propose performs independent sampling and yields a single configuration together with its associated probability. The second algorithm employs a greedy search strategy to identify K high-probability configurations and their corresponding probabilities. Numerical results demonstrate the effectiveness of these algorithms across quantum states with varying entanglement and system size.

arXiv:2602.02245v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：对潜在概率分布的量子系统进行采样是一项重要的计算任务，例如对于量子优势实验和量子蒙特卡罗算法。张量网络是有效表示具有有限纠缠的大型量子系统状态的宝贵工具。用于采样一维 (1D) 张量网络的算法已经很成熟，并在多种一维张量网络方法中得到应用。在本文中，我们介绍了两种用于二维（2D）等距张量网络状态（isoTNS）的新颖采样算法，它们可以被视为一维张量网络算法的扩展。我们提出的第一个算法执行独立采样并产生单一配置及其相关概率。第二种算法采用贪婪搜索策略来识别 K 个高概率配置及其相应的概率。数值结果证明了这些算法在具有不同纠缠和系统尺寸的量子态下的有效性。
