## Abstract
arXiv:2505.04363v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Orbital angular momentum transport has emerged as a promising route for manipulating magnetic devices, yet its generation has largely relied on the conventional orbital Hall effect. Here, we show that ferro-rotational order enables the electrical generation of unconventional orbital currents. These orbital currents represent the orbital counterparts of spin currents due to ferromagnetic order, but arise from rotation-induced symmetry breaking rather than time-reversal symmetry breaking or spin-orbit coupling. Using tight-binding models, we identify the underlying intrinsic, nonrelativistic mechanism categorized as an electric hexadecapole moment and corroborate our findings with first-principles calculations for the ferro-rotational material TiAu$_4$. We further show that these rotation-induced orbital currents lead to surface orbital accumulation and unconventional orbital torque in a ferro-rotational/ferromagnetic metallic bilayer, allowing deterministic field-free switching. Our findings unveil a novel pathway for generating orbital currents beyond the conventional orbital Hall effect, broadening the landscape of orbitronics research to include novel ferroic materials and higher-order electric multipoles.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.04363v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：轨道角动量输运已成为操纵磁性器件的一种有前途的途径，但其产生很大程度上依赖于传统的轨道霍尔效应。在这里，我们证明铁旋转顺序能够产生非常规轨道电流。这些轨道电流代表了由于铁磁序而产生的自旋电流的轨道对应物，但源自旋转引起的对称性破缺，而不是时间反转对称性破缺或自旋轨道耦合。使用紧束缚模型，我们确定了潜在的内在非相对论机制，归类为电十六极矩，并通过铁旋转材料 TiAu$_4$ 的第一性原理计算证实了我们的发现。我们进一步表明，这些旋转引起的轨道电流导致铁旋转/铁磁金属双层中的表面轨道积累和非常规轨道扭矩，从而允许确定性的无场切换。我们的研究结果揭示了一种超越传统轨道霍尔效应产生轨道电流的新途径，拓宽了轨道电子学研究的领域，包括新型铁质材料和高阶电多极子。
