## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16228v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Diffusion model, the state-of-the-art generative machine learning architecture, has shown promising results airfoil inverse designs. In this study, we implemented and trained a series of diffusion models on three different airfoil geometry data encoding formats -- principal component weights, ordered $x$-$y$ coordinates, and 2D signed distance functions (SDF) -- to generate 2D airfoils. By systematically comparing the performance of diffusion models trained on different data structures, it is found that for 2D airfoil design problems, the diffusion model performs the best when directly trained with coordinates. Training with latent space (PCA weights in this study) limits the model's design freedom, and decreases the training effectiveness. Although the 2D SDF data appears to result in the least performing model, it proves its feasibility in aerodynamic shape generation, paving the way towards 3D problems where SDF is more favored. This study also investigated deploying the diffusion model in practical engineering applications. A multi-target optimization procedure is proposed based on the stochastic nature of the diffusion process, which drastically simplifies the procedure compared to conventional methods. The extrapolation performance of the model is also investigated by tasking the model with both aerodynamic and flow condition labels that are extrapolated beyond the training set boundaries.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16228v1 公告类型：新 摘要：扩散模型是最先进的生成机器学习架构，在翼型逆设计中显示出了有希望的结果。在这项研究中，我们在三种不同的翼型几何数据编码格式（主成分权重、有序 $x$-$y$ 坐标和 2D 有符号距离函数 (SDF)）上实现并训练了一系列扩散模型，以生成 2D 翼型。通过系统比较在不同数据结构上训练的扩散模型的性能，发现对于二维翼型设计问题，直接用坐标训练时扩散模型表现最好。使用潜在空间（本研究中的 PCA 权重）进行训练限制了模型的设计自由度，并降低了训练效果。尽管 2D SDF 数据似乎会产生性能最差的模型，但它证明了其在空气动力学形状生成方面的可行性，为解决 SDF 更受青睐的 3D 问题铺平了道路。本研究还研究了扩散模型在实际工程应用中的部署。基于扩散过程的随机性质提出了多目标优化过程，与传统方法相比，大大简化了过程。还通过为模型分配空气动力学和流动条件标签来研究模型的外推性能，这些标签外推到训练集边界之外。
