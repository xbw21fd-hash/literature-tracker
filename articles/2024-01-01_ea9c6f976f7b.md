## Abstract
TLDR: This study introduces a universal electronic Hamiltonian model trained on Hamiltonian matrices obtained from first-principles DFT calculations of nearly all crystal structures on the Materials Project, and demonstrates its generality in predicting electronic structures across the whole periodic table.
