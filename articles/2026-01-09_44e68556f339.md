## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05097v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Crystal structure prediction (CSP) is emerging as a powerful method for the computational design of metal-organic frameworks (MOFs). In this article we demonstrate the high-throughput exploration of the crystal energy landscape of zinc imidazolate (ZnIm2), a highly polymorphic member of the zeolitic imidazolate (ZIF) family, with at least 24 reported structural and topological forms, with new polymorphs still being regularly discovered. With the aid of custom-trained machine-learned interatomic potentials (MLIPs) we have performed a high-throughput sampling of over 3 million randomly-generated crystal packing arrangements and identified 9626 energy minima characterized by 1493 network topologies, including 864 topologies that have not been reported before. Comparisons with previously reported structures revealed 13 topological matches to the experimentally-observed structures of ZnIm2, demonstrating the power of the CSP method in sampling experimentally-relevant ZIF structures. Finally, through a combination of topological analysis, density and porosity considerations, we have identified a set of structures representing promising targets for future experimental screening. Finally, we demonstrate how CSP can be used to assist in the identification of the products of the mechanochemical synthesis.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05097v1 公告类型：新 摘要：晶体结构预测 (CSP) 正在成为金属有机框架 (MOF) 计算设计的强大方法。在本文中，我们展示了对咪唑酸锌 (ZnIm2) 晶体能量景观的高通量探索，咪唑锌是沸石咪唑盐 (ZIF) 家族的高度多晶型成员，至少有 24 种已报道的结构和拓扑形式，并且仍在定期发现新的多晶型。借助定制训练的机器学习原子间势 (MLIP)，我们对超过 300 万个随机生成的晶体堆积排列进行了高通量采样，并确定了 9626 个能量最小值，其特征在于 1493 个网络拓扑，其中包括 864 个以前从未报道过的拓扑。与之前报道的结构的比较揭示了 13 个与实验观察到的 ZnIm2 结构的拓扑匹配，证明了 CSP 方法在对实验相关的 ZIF 结构进行采样方面的强大功能。最后，通过结合拓扑分析、密度和孔隙率考虑，我们确定了一组代表未来实验筛选有希望目标的结构。最后，我们演示了如何使用 CSP 来协助机械化学合成产物的鉴定。
