## Abstract
Halide-based solid electrolytes have recently attracted considerable attention owing to their wide electrochemical windows and moderate ionic conductivities. Here, we demonstrate a mixed-halide strategy to enhance the ionic conductivity of lithium tetrahaloaluminates, LiAlX4 (X = Cl, Br, I). Twenty compositions, including single-, binary-, and ternary-halide systems, were synthesized via a mechanochemical route. Their ionic conductivities were evaluated by electrochemical impedance spectroscopy, while the local environments of Li and Al were investigated using solid-state NMR in combination with powder X-ray diffraction (XRD). A conductivity map was constructed from the experimental data using a ternary diagram. The highest conductivity is observed near the center of the Cl-Br-I compositional triangle, where the configurational entropy is maximized. XRD patterns indicate that both binary and ternary mixed-halide samples retain the overall framework structure. 27Al magic-angle spinning (MAS) NMR reveals multiple X combinations of AlX4- units in the binary and ternary systems, consistent with random anion mixing. 7Li MAS NMR spectra exhibit motional narrowing that correlates with enhanced ionic conductivity, particularly in the ternary compositions. The activation energy and pre-exponential factor extracted from Arrhenius plots of the conductivity follow the Meyer-Neldel rule. These results suggest that the Li+ migration barrier can be overcome through the annihilation of multi-phonon excitation modes. The enhanced ionic conductivity achieved via increased compositional complexity highlights the potential of entropy-driven design strategies for next-generation solid-state batteries.

卤化物固体电解质由于其宽的电化学窗口和适度的离子电导率最近引起了相当大的关注。在这里，我们展示了一种混合卤化物策略来增强四卤铝酸锂 LiAlX4（X = Cl、Br、I）的离子电导率。通过机械化学途径合成了二十种组合物，包括单卤化物、二元卤化物和三元卤化物系统。通过电化学阻抗谱评估了它们的离子电导率，同时使用固态核磁共振和粉末X射线衍射（XRD）研究了Li和Al的局部环境。使用三元图根据实验数据构建电导率图。在 Cl-Br-I 组成三角形的中心附近观察到最高的电导率，此处构型熵最大。 XRD 图谱表明二元和三元混合卤化物样品均保留了整体骨架结构。 27Al 魔角旋转 (MAS) NMR 揭示了二元和三元系统中 AlX4- 单元的多个 X 组合，与随机阴离子混合一致。 7Li MAS NMR 谱表现出运动变窄，这与离子电导率的增强相关，特别是在三元组合物中。从电导率阿伦尼乌斯图中提取的活化能和指前因子遵循 Meyer-Neldel 规则。这些结果表明，Li+迁移势垒可以通过多声子激发模式的湮灭来克服。通过增加成分复杂性实现的增强的离子电导率凸显了下一代固态电池的熵驱动设计策略的潜力。
