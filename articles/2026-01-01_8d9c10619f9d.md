## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23931v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the topological properties of modified Dirac Hamiltonians with an altermagnetic mass term and reveal a novel mechanism for realizing altermagnetic Weyl semimetals. Unlike the conventional Wilson mass, the altermagnetic mass drives direct transitions between nontrivial Chern phases of opposite sign and fundamentally reshapes the band inversion surface. By extending this framework to three dimensions, we construct a minimal lattice model that hosts pairs of Weyl nodes as well as coexisting helical Fermi arcs with opposite chirality on the same surface, which is a phenomenon not found in conventional magnetic Weyl semimetals. We further propose a practical scheme to realize these phases in multilayer structures of 2-dimensional Rashba metal with engineered $d$-wave altermagnetic order. Our results deepen the theoretical understanding of mass terms in Dirac systems and provide concrete guidelines for the experimental detection and realization of altermagnetic Weyl semimetals.

arXiv:2512.23931v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了具有交磁质量项的修正狄拉克哈密顿量的拓扑性质，并揭示了一种实现交磁韦尔半金属的新机制。与传统的威尔逊质量不同，交变磁质量驱动相反符号的非平凡陈相之间的直接转变，并从根本上重塑能带反转表面。通过将该框架扩展到三维，我们构建了一个最小晶格模型，其中包含成对的韦尔节点以及在同一表面上共存的具有相反手性的螺旋费米弧，这是传统磁性韦尔半金属中未发现的现象。我们进一步提出了一种实用的方案，通过设计的 $d$ 波交变磁序在二维 Rashba 金属多层结构中实现这些相。我们的研究结果加深了对狄拉克系统中质量项的理论理解，并为交磁韦尔半金属的实验检测和实现提供了具体指导。
