## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23962v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Two of the iconic phases of the hole-doped cuprate materials are the intermediate temperature pseudogap metal and the lower temperature $d$-wave superconductor. Following the prescient suggestion of P.W. Anderson, there were numerous early theories of these phases as doped quantum spin liquids. However, these theories have had difficulties with two prominent observations: (i) angle-dependent magnetoresistance measurements (ADMR), including observation of the Yamaji effect, present convincing evidence of small hole pockets which can tunnel coherently between square lattice layers, and (ii) the velocities of the nodal Bogoliubov quasiparticles in the $d$-wave superconductor are highly anisotropic, with $v_F \gg v_\Delta$. These lecture notes review how the fractionalized Fermi Liquid (FL*) state, which dopes quantum spin liquids with gauge-neutral electron-like quasiparticles, resolves both difficulties. Theories of insulating quantum spin liquids employing fractionalization of the electron spin into bosonic or fermionic partons are discussed. Doping the bosonic parton theory leads to a holon metal theory: while not appropriate for the cuprate pseudogap, this theory is argued to apply to the Lieb lattice. Doping the fermionic parton theory leads to a $d$-wave superconductor with nearly isotropic quasiparticle velocities. The construction of the FL* state is described using a quantum dimer model, followed by a more realistic description using the Ancilla Layer Model (ALM), which is then used to obtain the theory of the pseudogap and the $d$-wave superconductor.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23962v1 公告类型：新 摘要：空穴掺杂铜酸盐材料的两个标志性物相是中温赝能隙金属和低温$d$波超导体。遵循 P.W. 有先见之明的建议安德森博士，有许多早期理论将这些相视为掺杂的量子自旋液体。然而，这些理论在两个突出的观察方面遇到了困难：（i）角度相关磁阻测量（ADMR），包括对 Yamaji 效应的观察，提供了小孔穴的令人信服的证据，这些小孔穴可以在方形晶格层之间相干隧道，以及（ii）$d$ 波超导体中的节点 Bogoliubov 准粒子的速度是高度各向异性的，$v_F \gg v_\Delta$。这些讲义回顾了分数化费米液体 (FL*) 态如何解决这两个问题，该态在量子自旋液体中掺杂了规范中性电子状准粒子。讨论了利用电子自旋分裂为玻色子或费米子部分子的绝缘量子自旋液体的理论。掺杂玻色子部分子理论导致了完整金属理论：虽然不适用于铜酸盐赝能隙，但该理论被认为适用于利布晶格。掺杂费米子部分子理论导致$d$波超导体具有接近各向同性的准粒子速度。使用量子二聚体模型描述 FL* 态的构造，然后使用 Ancilla 层模型 (ALM) 进行更真实的描述，然后使用该模型获得赝能隙和 $d$ 波超导体的理论。
