## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05450v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The large-scale limit or outer scale of turbulence in the solar wind is associated with the correlation length of the magnetic field. Determining correlation lengths from magnetic field time series in magnetic clouds is complicated by the presence of the global flux rope: without removal of the flux rope trend, correlation length measurements will be sensitive to the flux rope as well as the turbulence, and give overestimates of the outer scale when turbulence amplitudes at the outer scale are small relative to the flux rope amplitude. We have used force-free flux rope fits to detrend magnetic field time series measured by Parker Solar Probe in two magnetic clouds and calculated the turbulence correlation length in the clouds using the detrended data. The detrended correlation length in terms of the proton inertial length, $d_p$, was $2.7\times10^{4} d_p$ in one cloud (observed at 0.77 au) and $1.6\times10^{4}d_p$ in the other (observed at 0.39 au), significantly smaller than the values obtained without detrending. Increments in the flux rope fits scaled equivalently to a $k^{-3}$ wavenumber power spectrum; this contribution from the flux rope considerably steepened the total spectrum at the largest scales but had a negligible effect in the inertial range, where scaling in both clouds equivalent to $\sim$$k^{-5/3}$ was observed. Finally, we discuss the possible relation of turbulence correlation lengths to mesoscale structure in magnetic clouds.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05450v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：太阳风中湍流的大尺度极限或外尺度与磁场的相关长度有关。由于全局磁绳的存在，从磁云中的磁场时间序列确定相关长度变得复杂：在不去除磁绳趋势的情况下，相关长度测量将对磁绳和湍流敏感，并且当外部尺度的湍流幅度相对于磁绳幅度较小时，会高估外部尺度。我们使用无力磁通绳拟合来去趋势帕克太阳探测器在两个磁云中测量的磁场时间序列，并使用去趋势数据计算云中的湍流相关长度。以质子惯性长度 $d_p$ 表示的去趋势相关长度在一个云中为 $2.7\times10^{4} d_p$（在 0.77 au 处观察），在另一云中为 $1.6\times10^{4}d_p$（在 0.39 au 处观察），明显小于未去趋势时获得的值。磁通绳的增量适合按比例缩放，相当于 $k^{-3}$ 波数功率谱；磁通绳的这种贡献极大地使最大尺度下的总光谱变得陡峭，但在惯性范围内的影响可以忽略不计，在惯性范围内观察到相当于 $\sim$$k^{-5/3}$ 的缩放。最后，我们讨论了湍流相关长度与磁云中介尺度结构的可能关系。
