## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05481v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Controlling mesoscale and nanoscale material structures and properties through self-organized atomic behavior is essential for atomic-scale manufacturing. However, direct and visual studies on the cross-scale effects of such atomic self-organization on mesoscopic structures remain scarce. Here, we report the intertwined atomic-nanoscale-mesoscale structures via the intralayer Fe-chains in the sandwich-like layered FePd2Te2 crystal by scanning tunneling microscopy (STM) and atomic force microscopy (AFM). The hierarchical orthogonal corrugated morphologies are directly revealed and attributed to its chain-orientation-determined twinning-domain effect. Both Fe-chains of middle-sublayer and two kinds of Te atoms of top-sublayer are further atomically resolved at the sub-{\AA} level, indicating the critical effects of Pd-atoms/voids on the intra-layer anisotropic Fe-chains and the interlayer structural alignment. The thermal-induced and strain-related structural transitions of surface layer are further investigated and discussed based on the proposed filling model of Pd-voids by the intralayer Pd-atoms. Our work not only provides deep understanding of this exotic layered magnetic material, and will inspire more perspectives for tailoring its anisotropic atomic-to-mesoscale structures and properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05481v1 公告类型：新 摘要：通过自组织原子行为控制介观和纳米尺度材料的结构和性能对于原子尺度制造至关重要。然而，关于这种原子自组织对介观结构的跨尺度效应的直接和视觉研究仍然很少。在这里，我们通过扫描隧道显微镜（STM）和原子力显微镜（AFM）报告了三明治状层状 FePd2Te2 晶体中通过层内 Fe 链相互缠绕的原子-纳米尺度-介观尺度结构。分层正交波纹形态被直接揭示并归因于其链方向决定的孪生域效应。中间子层的 Fe 链和顶部子层的两种 Te 原子在 sub-{\AA} 水平上进一步原子分辨，表明 Pd 原子/空隙对层内各向异性 Fe 链和层间结构排列的关键影响。基于所提出的层内 Pd 原子填充 Pd 空隙模型，进一步研究和讨论了表面层的热致和应变相关的结构转变。我们的工作不仅提供了对这种奇异的层状磁性材料的深入理解，还将激发更多的视角来定制其各向异性原子到介观尺度的结构和性能。
