## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15162v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We analyze the WAHUHA Floquet protocol recently applied to arrays of Rydberg atoms and derive beyond-leading-order corrections in the high-frequency expansion of the effective spin theory. We find that an appropriate choice of the pulses times can enforce an approximate symmetry corresponding to the conservation of the total magnetization. The interaction channels emerging from higher-order Floquet terms affect three-body bound states (\emph{trimers}), which gain a significant mobility. We estimate the corresponding enhancement in 1D spin chains and conclude that their dynamics is within experimental reach. Detrimental effects due to the proliferation of particles outside of the trimer magnetization sector are found to occur and spread on time-scales slower than the trimer propagation. Moreover, these can be suppressed in higher dimensional lattices, e.g. in 2D triangular lattices, as the lattice geometry brings these processes off resonance. Our results establish a concrete route to realizing mobile multiparticle bound states in Floquet-engineered Rydberg platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15162v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们分析了最近应用于里德伯原子阵列的 WAHUHA Floquet 协议，并在有效自旋理论的高频展开中推导了超前阶修正。我们发现，适当选择脉冲时间可以强制实现与总磁化强度守恒相对应的近似对称性。高阶 Floquet 项中出现的相互作用通道会影响三体束缚态（\emph{trimers}），从而获得显着的迁移率。我们估计了一维自旋链的相应增强，并得出结论，它们的动力学在实验范围内。发现由于三聚体磁化扇区之外的粒子增殖而产生的有害影响在比三聚体传播慢的时间尺度上发生和传播。此外，这些可以在更高维的晶格中被抑制，例如在二维三角晶格中，因为晶格几何形状使这些过程脱离共振。我们的结果建立了一条在 Floquet 设计的 Rydberg 平台中实现移动多粒子束缚态的具体途径。
