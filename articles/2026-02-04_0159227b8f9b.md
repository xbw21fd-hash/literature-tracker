## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02807v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Li$_2$MnO$_3$ remains a crucial component of the Li-excess layered cathode family, $(1-x)\,\mathrm{LiMO_2} + x\,\mathrm{Li_2MnO_3}$ ($M$ = Mn, Ni, Co, \dots), but its role in limiting Li-ion mobility remains under debate. Here we combine DFT+$U$, finite-temperature DMFT with a continuous-time quantum Monte Carlo impurity solver, and nudged-elastic-band (NEB) calculations to investigate Li$^+$ migration for a single Li vacancy in paramagnetic Li$_2$MnO$_3$. Dynamical electronic correlations within DMFT substantially reduce the activation energies of the lowest-barrier pathways, yielding $E_a = 0.18$ eV for the shortest-range Li jump and $E_a = 0.50$ eV for the next-lowest pathway. The 0.18 eV barrier quantitatively reproduces the short-range activation energy extracted from $\mu^+$SR measurements, whereas the 0.50 eV barrier is consistent with the long-range transport activation energy obtained from ac-impedance measurements. This single-vacancy, paramagnetic DMFT description therefore provides a coherent explanation of both local and macroscopic probes without requiring highly clustered vacancy configurations or strong extrinsic disorder, an assumption compatible with nearly stoichiometric Li$_2$MnO$_3$ powders. Our results highlight the importance of finite-temperature dynamical correlations for Li-ion migration in correlated oxides and provide a framework for incorporating strong Coulomb interactions in future studies of transition-metal oxide battery materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02807v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Li$_2$MnO$_3$ 仍然是过量锂层状正极系列 $(1-x)\,\mathrm{LiMO_2} + x\,\mathrm{Li_2MnO_3}$ ($M$ = Mn, Ni, Co, \dots) 的重要组成部分，但其在限制锂离子迁移率方面的作用仍存在争议。在这里，我们将 DFT+$U$、有限温度 DMFT 与连续时间量子蒙特卡罗杂质求解器以及微推弹性带 (NEB) 计算相结合，研究顺磁性 Li$_2$MnO$_3$ 中单个 Li 空位的 Li$^+$ 迁移。 DMFT 内的动态电子相关性大大降低了最低势垒路径的激活能，对于最短程 Li 跳跃产生 $E_a = 0.18$ eV，对于次低势垒路径产生 $E_a = 0.50$ eV。 0.18 eV 势垒定量地再现了从 $\mu^+$SR 测量中提取的短程激活能，而 0.50 eV 势垒与从交流阻抗测量中获得的长程传输激活能一致。因此，这种单空位、顺磁 DMFT 描述为局部和宏观探针提供了连贯的解释，而不需要高度聚集的空位配置或强的外在无序，这一假设与近化学计量的 Li$_2$MnO$_3$ 粉末兼容。我们的结果强调了有限温度动态相关性对于相关氧化物中锂离子迁移的重要性，并为在过渡金属氧化物电池材料的未来研究中纳入强库仑相互作用提供了一个框架。
