## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11071v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The inclusion of long-range electrostatics in atomistic machine learning (ML) is receiving increasing attention for achieving quantum-mechanical accuracy in predicting a wide range of molecular and material properties. However, there is still no general prescription on how long-range physical effects should be incorporated into the model while preserving well-established locality principles underlying most transferable ML representations. Here, we provide a physical perspective on the problem, by discussing how distinct contributions to the system's electrostatics can be captured through the adoption of different learning paradigms. Specifically, we discern between local charge models, which rely either on explicit charge-density decompositions or implicit auxiliary variables, and models where a notion of nonlocality is deliberately introduced, either via self-consistent procedures or by using nonlocal descriptors and learning architectures. We further address the related aspect of incorporating finite-field effects through the coupling with the system's polarization, relevant for the application of an external electric bias. We conclude by discussing the implications for the simulation of electrochemical interfaces, where long-range electrostatics are essential to capture the interplay between charge redistribution, interfacial dynamics, and ionic screening, and for ionic transport phenomena, which, although less explored, appear far less sensitive to their inclusion.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11071v1 公告类型：新 摘要：将远程静电纳入原子机器学习 (ML) 中，由于在预测各种分子和材料特性方面实现量子力学精度，因此受到越来越多的关注。然而，对于如何将远程物理效应纳入模型，同时保留大多数可转移机器学习表示背后的既定局部性原则，仍然没有通用的规定。在这里，我们通过讨论如何通过采用不同的学习范式来捕获对系统静电的不同贡献，从而提供了对该问题的物理视角。具体来说，我们区分局部电荷模型和模型，局部电荷模型依赖于显式电荷密度分解或隐式辅助变量，而模型则通过自洽过程或使用非局部描述符和学习架构故意引入非局部性概念。我们进一步解决了通过与系统极化耦合合并有限场效应的相关问题，这与外部电偏压的应用相关。最后，我们讨论了电化学界面模拟的影响，其中长程静电对于捕获电荷重新分布、界面动力学和离子屏蔽之间的相互作用至关重要，以及离子传输现象的影响，尽管对离子传输现象的探索较少，但似乎对其包含的敏感性要低得多。
