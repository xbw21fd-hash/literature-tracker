## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12047v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The spin properties of excitons and charge carriers in CsPbBr$_3$ lead halide perovskite crystals are investigated by spin-dependent light scattering in magnetic fields up to 10 T. Spin-flip Raman scattering spectra measured under resonant excitation of exciton-polaritons show a rich variety of features provided by the Zeeman splittings of excitons and of electrons and holes interacting with the excitons. The magnitudes and anisotropies of their Land\'e $g$-factors are measured. A detailed consideration of the responsible mechanisms is presented and discussed in relation to the experimental data, in particular on the polarization properties of the Raman spectra. We consider several mechanisms for the combined spin-flip Raman scattering processes involving resident carriers and photoexcited excitons and suggest new ones, involving trions in the intermediate scattering state. A double electron spin-flip caused by the exciton interaction with two localized or donor-bound electrons is revealed. The spectral lines of Brillouin light scattering on exciton-polaritons shift in energy and become polarization-sensitive in magnetic field, evidencing the splitting of the exciton-polariton dispersion.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12047v1 公告类型：新 摘要：通过在高达 10 T 的磁场中自旋相关光散射研究 CsPbBr$_3$ 卤化铅钙钛矿晶体中激子和载流子的自旋特性。在激子极化激元共振激发下测量的自旋翻转拉曼散射光谱显示了激子以及电子和空穴的塞曼分裂提供的丰富多样的特征与激子相互作用。测量其 Land\'e $g$ 因子的大小和各向异性。结合实验数据，特别是拉曼光谱的偏振特性，详细考虑了相关机制。我们考虑了涉及驻留载流子和光激发激子的组合自旋翻转拉曼散射过程的几种机制，并提出了涉及中间散射态三重子的新机制。揭示了由激子与两个局域或施主束缚电子相互作用引起的双电子自旋翻转。激子极化子上的布里渊光散射的谱线发生能量变化并在磁场中变得对偏振敏感，证明了激子极化子色散的分裂。
