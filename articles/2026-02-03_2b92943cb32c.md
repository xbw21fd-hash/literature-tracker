## Abstract
arXiv:2511.06929v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Interpreting the impedance response of perovskite solar cells (PSCs) is challenging due to the complex coupling of ionic and electronic motion. While drift-diffusion (DD) modelling is a reliable method, its mathematical complexity makes directly extracting physical parameters from experimental data infeasible. This work uses DD modelling to generate a large synthetic dataset of impedance spectra for a standard TiO2/MAPI/spiro configuration. This dataset trains machine learning (ML) models to predict recombination and ionic parameters from impedance measurements. A Gradient Boosting Regressor, using features from a generalized equivalent circuit, showed the best performance. Interpretative analysis indicates that open-circuit impedance experiments best probe recombination losses, while short-circuit conditions are more adequate for extracting ionic features like concentrations and mobilities. The trained ML models were tested on experimental spectra, confirming the inferred physical parameters could reproduce the data. For the studied configuration, predicted ion concentrations were (1.3-3.3)e17 cm-3, ion mobilities were (5-7)e-11 cm2V-1s-1, and surface recombination velocities were 7-9 and 23-40 m/s. This approach provides insights into the physical information extractable from impedance measurements and paves the way for ML models to unambiguously derive efficiency-determining parameters for solar cells.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.06929v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：由于离子和电子运动的复杂耦合，解释钙钛矿太阳能电池 (PSC) 的阻抗响应具有挑战性。虽然漂移扩散（DD）建模是一种可靠的方法，但其数学复杂性使得直接从实验数据中提取物理参数是不可行的。这项工作使用 DD 建模来生成标准 TiO2/MAPI/spiro 配置的阻抗谱的大型综合数据集。该数据集训练机器学习 (ML) 模型，以根据阻抗测量预测重组和离子参数。使用广义等效电路的特征的梯度提升回归器显示出最佳性能。解释分析表明，开路阻抗实验可以实现最佳的探针复合损失，而短路条件更适合提取浓度和迁移率等离子特征。经过训练的机器学习模型在实验光谱上进行了测试，确认推断的物理参数可以重现数据。对于所研究的配置，预测离子浓度为(1.3-3.3)e17 cm-3，离子淌度为(5-7)e-11 cm2V-1s-1，表面复合速度为7-9和23-40 m/s。这种方法提供了对从阻抗测量中提取的物理信息的洞察，并为机器学习模型明确推导太阳能电池效率决定参数铺平了道路。
