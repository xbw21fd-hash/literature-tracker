## Abstract
arXiv:2601.11775v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Autonomous materials science, where active learning is used to navigate large compositional phase space, has emerged as a powerful vehicle to rapidly explore new materials. A crucial aspect of autonomous materials science is exploring new materials using as little data as possible. Gaussian process-based active learning allows effective charting of multi-dimensional parameter space with a limited number of training data, and thus is a common algorithmic choice for autonomous materials science. An integral part of the autonomous workflow is the application of kernel functions for quantifying similarities among measured data points. A recent theoretical breakthrough has shown that quantum kernel models can achieve similar performance with less training data than classical models. This signals the possible advantage of applying quantum kernel machine learning to autonomous materials discovery. In this work, we compare quantum and classical kernels for their utility in sequential phase space navigation for autonomous materials science. Specifically, we compute a quantum kernel and several classical kernels for x-ray diffraction patterns taken from an Fe-Ga-Pd ternary composition spread library. We conduct our study on both IonQ's Aria trapped ion quantum computer hardware and the corresponding classical noisy simulator. We experimentally verify that a quantum kernel model can outperform some classical kernel models. The results highlight the potential of quantum kernel machine learning methods for accelerating materials discovery and suggest complex x-ray diffraction data is a candidate for robust quantum kernel model advantage.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.11775v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自主材料科学利用主动学习来导航大型组合相空间，已成为快速探索新材料的强大工具。自主材料科学的一个重要方面是使用尽可能少的数据探索新材料。基于高斯过程的主动学习允许使用有限数量的训练数据有效地绘制多维参数空间图表，因此是自主材料科学的常见算法选择。自主工作流程的一个组成部分是应用核函数来量化测量数据点之间的相似性。最近的一项理论突破表明，量子核模型可以用比经典模型更少的训练数据实现相似的性能。这标志着将量子内核机器学习应用于自主材料发现的可能优势。在这项工作中，我们比较了量子内核和经典内核在自主材料科学的顺序相空间导航中的实用性。具体来说，我们计算了一个量子核和几个经典核，用于取自 Fe-Ga-Pd 三元成分扩散库的 X 射线衍射图。我们在 IonQ 的 Aria 俘获离子量子计算机硬件和相应的经典噪声模拟器上进行研究。我们通过实验验证量子核模型可以优于一些经典核模型。结果凸显了量子核机器学习方法在加速材料发现方面的潜力，并表明复杂的 X 射线衍射数据是强大的量子核模型优势的候选者。
