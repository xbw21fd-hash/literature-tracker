## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18416v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In our previous work, we developed a CCSD(T)-level range-separated water force field that combines the power of physics-driven and machine learning models. However, it was found that expensive CCSD(T)/CBS calculations lead to limited number of QM data as well as the missing of force labels, both of which lead to training instability issues. Bulk properties show large variations that cannot be resolved by simply reducing the fitting error in small cluster QM dataset. Such instability in bulk phase simulation is a universal problem in the training of machine learning potentials (MLPs), and is particularly severe at CCSD(T) level of theory.In this work, using our range-separated water model as an example, we aim to overcome these limitations by developing a new training workflow. It is composed by several techniques including: 1. an active learning protocol that ensures more thorough sampling in different temperatures and densities; 2. an intermediate force label technique employing machine learning density functional; and 3. an ensemble knowledge distillation (EKD) method. These techniques significantly stabilize the resulting water model, consistently achieving sub-chemical accuracies in both cluster energies and experimental properties. Benchmarks are carried out for various properties including densities, radial distribution functions (RDFs), dielectric constants, diffusivity, and infrared spectra, all showing state-of-the-art (SOTA) performances and proving the effectiveness of the training protocol.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18416v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在我们之前的工作中，我们开发了一个 CCSD(T) 级范围分离的水力场，它结合了物理驱动和机器学习模型的力量。然而，人们发现昂贵的 CCSD(T)/CBS 计算导致 QM 数据数量有限以及力标签缺失，这两者都会导致训练不稳定问题。批量属性显示出较大的变化，无法通过简单地减少小集群 QM 数据集中的拟合误差来解决。体相模拟中的这种不稳定性是机器学习势 (MLP) 训练中的普遍问题，在 CCSD(T) 理论水平上尤其严重。在这项工作中，以我们的范围分离水模型为例，我们的目标是通过开发新的训练工作流程来克服这些限制。它由多种技术组成，包括： 1. 主动学习协议，确保在不同温度和密度下进行更彻底的采样； 2.采用机器学习密度泛函的中间力标签技术； 3.集成知识蒸馏（EKD）方法。这些技术显着稳定了所得的水模型，在团簇能量和实验特性方面始终实现亚化学精度。针对各种属性进行了基准测试，包括密度、径向分布函数 (RDF)、介电常数、扩散率和红外光谱，所有这些都显示了最先进的 (SOTA) 性能并证明了训练协议的有效性。
