## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15534v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Decoding the self-assembly mechanism of metal-organic frameworks is a crucial step in reducing trial-and-error tests in their synthesis protocols. Atomistic simulations have proven essential in revealing molecular-level features of MOF nucleation, but they still exhibit limitations in the simulation setups due to size constraints (inability of reaching realistic concentrations or exploring non-stoichiometric metal:ligand ratios). In this contribution, we develop a methodology to derive reactive coarse grained force fields based on multiscale coarse graining methods. We apply our novel methodology to the case of the archetypal zeolitic-imidazolate framework ZIF-8. Our coarse grained force field, which we call nb-CG-ZIF-FF, does not contain any explicit connectivity information, but learns the tetrahedral Zn-connectivity from many body correlations within an atomistic benchmark. nb-CG-ZIF-FF quantitatively reproduces the features of bulk, crystalline ZIF-8 as well as the structural evolution of pre-nucleation species in terms of Zn n-fold coordination populations from the atomistic benchmark. While the range of rings that are formed along the synthesis process are well captured by nb-CG-ZIF-FF, the model cannot exactly reproduce ring populations. Our reactive CG force field fitting approach can be applied to any MOF, opening new research avenues in modeling MOF formation, decomposition, defect dynamics and phase transition processes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15534v1 公告类型：新 摘要：解码金属有机框架的自组装机制是减少合成方案中试错测试的关键一步。原子模拟已被证明对于揭示 MOF 成核的分子水平特征至关重要，但由于尺寸限制（无法达到实际浓度或探索非化学计量金属：配体比率），它们在模拟设置中仍然表现出局限性。在本文中，我们开发了一种基于多尺度粗粒化方法推导反应性粗粒力场的方法。我们将我们的新颖方法应用于原型沸石-咪唑酯框架 ZIF-8 的情况。我们的粗粒度力场，我们称之为 nb-CG-ZIF-FF，不包含任何显式的连通性信息，但从原子基准内的许多体相关性中学习四面体 Zn-连通性。 nb-CG-ZIF-FF 定量再现了块状结晶 ZIF-8 的特征以及原子基准中 Zn n 倍配位群体的成核前物质的结构演化。虽然 nb-CG-ZIF-FF 很好地捕获了合成过程中形成的环范围，但该模型无法准确再现环群。我们的反应 CG 力场拟合方法可应用于任何 MOF，为 MOF 形成、分解、缺陷动力学和相变过程建模开辟了新的研究途径。
