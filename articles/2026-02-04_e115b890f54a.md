## Abstract
arXiv:2509.24926v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Control of transcription presides over a vast array of biological processes, including those mediated by gene regulatory circuits that exhibit multistability. Within these circuits, two- and three-gene network motifs are particularly critical to the repertoire of metabolic and developmental pathways. Theoretical models of these circuits, however, often vary parameters such as dissociation constants, transcription rates, and degradation rates without specifying precisely how these parameters are controlled biologically. In this study, we examine the role of effector molecules, which can alter the concentrations of the active transcription factors that control regulation, and are ubiquitous in regulatory processes across many biological settings. We specifically consider allosteric regulation in the context of extending the standard bistable switch to three-gene networks, and explore the rich multistable dynamics exhibited in these architectures as a function of effector concentrations. We then analyze how the dynamics evolve under various interpretations of regulatory circuit mechanics, underlying inducer activity, and perturbations thereof. Notably, the biological mechanism by which we model effector control over dual-function proteins transforms not only the phenotypic trend of dynamic tuning but also the set of available dynamic regimes. In this way, we determine key parameters and regulatory features that drive phenotypic decisions, and offer an experimentally tunable structure for encoding inducible multistable behavior arising from both single and dual-function allosteric transcription factors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.24926v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：转录控制负责大量的生物过程，包括由表现出多稳定性的基因调控电路介导的过程。在这些回路中，两基因和三基因网络基序对于代谢和发育途径的全部功能尤其重要。然而，这些电路的理论模型通常会改变解离常数、转录速率和降解速率等参数，而没有准确说明如何从生物学角度控制这些参数。在这项研究中，我们研究了效应分子的作用，它可以改变控制调节的活性转录因子的浓度，并且在许多生物环境的调节过程中普遍存在。我们在将标准双稳态开关扩展到三基因网络的背景下特别考虑变构调节，并探索这些架构中作为效应子浓度的函数所表现出的丰富的多稳态动力学。然后，我们分析了在调节回路机制、潜在诱导物活动及其扰动的各种解释下动态如何演变。值得注意的是，我们对双功能蛋白质的效应器控制进行建模的生物机制不仅改变了动态调节的表型趋势，而且改变了可用的动态机制的集合。通过这种方式，我们确定了驱动表型决策的关键参数和调控特征，并提供了一种实验可调结构，用于编码由单功能和双功能变构转录因子产生的诱导多稳态行为。
