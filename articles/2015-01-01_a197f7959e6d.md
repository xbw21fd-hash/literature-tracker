## Abstract
TLDR: This topical review seminal experimental findings gained on electric and magnetic domain walls in multiferroic bulk materials are addressed and a special focus is put on the physical properties that emerge at so-called charged domain walls and the added functionality that arises from coexisting magnetic order.
