## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00985v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a combined imaginary-time/real-time time-dependent (TD) approach for evaluating linear absorption spectra of open-shell systems at the electron attachment (EA) and ionization potential (IP) equation-of-motion coupled-cluster (EOMCC) levels of theory and within the exact two-component relativistic framework. The absorption lineshape is given by the Fourier transform of the electric dipole autocorrelation function, which is obtained from a real-time simulation. Approximations of the lowest-energy EA- and IP-EOMCC eigenstates, which are required as initial states for the real-time simulation, are generated by propagating a Koopman EA/IP state in imaginary time. TD-EA/IP-EOMCC linear absorption spectra of open-shell atomic systems (Na, K, Rb, F, Cl, and Br) closely reproduce those obtained from standard TD-EA/IP procedures carried out in the frequency domain. We find that the existence of low-lying states with non-negligible overlap with the Koopman determinant impacts the length of the imaginary-time propagation required to obtain an initial state that produces correct absolute energies and peak height intensities in spectra extracted from the subsequent real-time TD-EA/IP-EOMCC calculations.

arXiv:2602.00985v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种虚时/实时时间相关 (TD) 相结合的方法，用于评估开壳系统在电子附着 (EA) 和电离势 (IP) 运动方程耦合簇 (EOMCC) 理论水平以及精确的二分量相对论框架内的线性吸收光谱。吸收线形由电偶极子自相关函数的傅里叶变换给出，该函数是通过实时模拟获得的。最低能量 EA- 和 IP-EOMCC 本征态的近似值（实时模拟所需的初始状态）是通过在虚时间内传播 Koopman EA/IP 状态生成的。开壳层原子系统（Na、K、Rb、F、Cl 和 Br）的 TD-EA/IP-EOMCC 线性吸收光谱与频域中执行的标准 TD-EA/IP 程序获得的光谱非常接近。我们发现，低位态的存在与库普曼行列式具有不可忽略的重叠，会影响获得初始态所需的虚时传播长度，该初始态在从后续实时 TD-EA/IP-EOMCC 计算中提取的光谱中产生正确的绝对能量和峰高强度。
