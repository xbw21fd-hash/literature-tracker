## Abstract
TLDR: It is shown that including the electrostatic potential arising from the charge distribution in the atomic environments significantly improves the quality and transferability of the potentials and allows current limitations of two- and three-body based feature vectors to be overcome regarding artificially degenerate atomic environments.
