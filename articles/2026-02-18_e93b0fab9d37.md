## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15759v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Despite significant advances in lead-free perovskite photovoltaics, achieving a balance among environmental safety and high optoelectronic performance remains challenging. The inorganic double perovskite Cs2AgBiBr6 has emerged as a promising candidate owing to its robust three-dimensional crystal structure and suitable visible-range bandgap. However, best power conversion efficiencies (PCEs) for Cs2AgBiBr6 solar cells reported so far - 6.37% experimentally and 27.78% in numerical studies - remain below the theoretical performance potential, largely due to suboptimal charge transport layers, and interface-related recombination losses. Here, we address this gap using a 3D finite-element method (FEM) implemented in COMSOL Multiphysics, which couples optical simulations with semiconductor drift-diffusion transport. To our knowledge, this work represents the first comprehensive 3D FEM-based study of a double halide perovskite solar cell. Screening of 25 electron transport layer (ETL)-hole transport layer (HTL) combinations identifies CeO2 and P3HT as the optimal ETL and HTL respectively. Device performance is further analyzed through systematic variation of layer thicknesses, doping concentrations and defect densities within the FTO/CeO2/Cs2AgBiBr6/P3HT/Au architecture. Under optimized parameters, the simulated device achieves a PCE of 31.76%, representing the theoretical upper bound predicted by the model. Overall, this work demonstrates 3D physics-based device engineering as a decisive pathway for overcoming efficiency bottlenecks in lead-free double perovskite photovoltaics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15759v1 公告类型：新 摘要：尽管无铅钙钛矿光伏发电取得了重大进展，但在环境安全和高光电性能之间实现平衡仍然具有挑战性。无机双钙钛矿Cs2AgBiBr6因其坚固的三维晶体结构和合适的可见光范围带隙而成为一种有前途的候选材料。然而，迄今为止报道的 Cs2AgBiBr6 太阳能电池的最佳功率转换效率 (PCE)（实验为 6.37%，数值研究为 27.78%）仍然低于理论性能潜力，这主要是由于电荷传输层不理想以及与界面相关的复合损失。在这里，我们使用 COMSOL Multiphysics 中实现的 3D 有限元方法 (FEM) 来解决这一差距，该方法将光学模拟与半导体漂移扩散传输结合起来。据我们所知，这项工作代表了第一个基于 3D FEM 的双卤化物钙钛矿太阳能电池的全面研究。对 25 种电子传输层 (ETL)-空穴传输层 (HTL) 组合的筛选表明，CeO2 和 P3HT 分别是最佳 ETL 和 HTL。通过 FTO/CeO2/Cs2AgBiBr6/P3HT/Au 架构内层厚度、掺杂浓度和缺陷密度的系统变化，进一步分析器件性能。在优化参数下，仿真器件的PCE达到31.76%，达到了模型预测的理论上限。总的来说，这项工作证明了基于 3D 物理的器件工程是克服无铅双钙钛矿光伏效率瓶颈的决定性途径。
