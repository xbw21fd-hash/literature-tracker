# Gut microbiota-derived metabolite isovalerylcarnitine modulates salt sensitivity of blood pressure and incident hypertension: a multicenter dietary salt intervention trial

## Abstract
Nature Communications, Published online: 26 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41467-025-67513-x Here, combining data from a clinical trial and animal models, the authors show a role for a gut-acylcarnitine axis in the development of salt-sensitive hypertension, and identify isovalerylcarnitine as a key microbiota-derived modulator and potential target for the precision prevention of hypertension.
