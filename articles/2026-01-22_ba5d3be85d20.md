## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14972v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Two-dimensional transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDCs) have been extensively investigated due to their tunable properties. In this work, density functional theory (DFT) is employed to investigate the adsorption behavior and sensing characteristics of HCN on pristine and doped MoS2 monolayers (X-MoS2, where X = P, N, Si, Al, B, Cl). The structural, electronic, and optical characteristics of all systems are examined to study the sensing properties of various doped MoS2 monolayers. In particular, the Al-MoS2 system demonstrates the strongest adsorption characterized by chemisorption, while the remaining systems show interactions of physisorption type. Recovery time and changes in electronic and optical properties reveal that Si-MoS2 possesses an ultrafast response of the order of microseconds, while Al-MoS2 exhibits a significantly longer recovery time, making it unsuitable for reusable sensors. P-MoS2, Si-MoS2, and Al-MoS2 monolayers show pronounced changes in their properties after HCN adsorption. To explore tunability in adsorption strength and recovery behavior, systems with two and three dopant atoms are further studied for P, Si, and Al doping. The results indicate that double doping enhances adsorption strength, whereas triple symmetric doping weakens it. Based on adsorption energy, recovery time, and electronic response, 2P-MoS2 and 3Al-MoS2 are identified as promising candidates for electrochemical and chemiresistive sensing of HCN. Additionally, the observed optical response in the ultraviolet region highlights their potential in UV-range optical sensor design.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14972v1 公告类型：新 摘要：二维过渡金属二硫属化物 (TMDC) 由于其可调特性而得到了广泛研究。在这项工作中，采用密度泛函理论 (DFT) 研究 HCN 在原始和掺杂 MoS2 单层 (X-MoS2，其中 X = P、N、Si、Al、B、Cl) 上的吸附行为和传感特性。检查所有系统的结构、电子和光学特性，以研究各种掺杂 MoS2 单层的传感特性。特别是，Al-MoS2 系统表现出最强的化学吸附特征，而其余系统则表现出物理吸附类型的相互作用。恢复时间以及电子和光学特性的变化表明，Si-MoS2 具有微秒量级的超快响应，而 Al-MoS2 的恢复时间明显更长，使其不适合可重复使用的传感器。 P-MoS2、Si-MoS2 和 Al-MoS2 单层在 HCN 吸附后性能发生显着变化。为了探索吸附强度和恢复行为的可调性，进一步研究了具有两个和三个掺杂原子的系统的 P、Si 和 Al 掺杂。结果表明，双重掺杂增强了吸附强度，而三重对称掺杂则削弱了吸附强度。基于吸附能、恢复时间和电子响应，2P-MoS2 和 3Al-MoS2 被认为是 HCN 电化学和化学电阻传感的有前途的候选材料。此外，在紫外线区域观察到的光学响应凸显了它们在紫外线范围光学传感器设计中的潜力。
