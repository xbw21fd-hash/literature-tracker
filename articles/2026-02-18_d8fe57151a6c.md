## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15465v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Moir\'e semiconductors offer flat bands where Coulomb interactions and band topology intertwine, while interlayer coupling plays a central role in forming the moir\'e potential. However, limited interlayer coupling strength and the lack of efficient tuning methods hinder further exploration of correlated phenomena in moir\'e semiconductors. Here we introduce a cryogenic dual-gated diamond-anvil platform using helium as a pressure medium, enabling reversible hydrostatic tuning together with magneto-optical spectroscopy in twisted bilayer WSe2. Pressure enhances the moir\'e potential, redshifts excitons, and stabilizes Stoner ferromagnetism otherwise absent at a 3.1-degree twist. Simultaneously, the half-filled C = 1 Chern insulating state strengthens, exhibiting a reduced saturation field. Moreover, we observe a topological phase transition from a Chern insulator to a Mott insulator at around 2 GPa. First-principles calculations reveal that a Gamma-to-K valence-band-maximum switching drives this transition by converting an Ising-like topological K-valley miniband into a spin-degenerate trivial Gamma miniband. Our findings demonstrate hydrostatic pressure as a powerful, continuous control axis for correlated magnetism and topological band engineering in moir\'e materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15465v1 公告类型：新 摘要：莫尔半导体提供库仑相互作用和能带拓扑交织在一起的平坦能带，而层间耦合在形成莫尔势方面发挥着核心作用。然而，有限的层间耦合强度和缺乏有效的调谐方法阻碍了对莫尔半导体中相关现象的进一步探索。在这里，我们介绍了一种使用氦气作为压力介质的低温双门金刚石砧平台，可在扭曲双层 WSe2 中实现可逆流体静力学调谐和磁光光谱。压力增强莫尔势，使激子红移，并稳定斯通纳铁磁性，否则在 3.1 度扭曲时不存在。同时，半填充的 C = 1 Chern 绝缘态增强，表现出饱和场减小。此外，我们在 2 GPa 左右观察到从陈绝缘体到莫特绝缘体的拓扑相变。第一性原理计算表明，伽玛到 K 价带最大转换通过将类伊辛拓扑 K 谷微带转换为自旋简并平凡伽玛微带来驱动这种转变。我们的研究结果表明，静水压力是莫尔材料中相关磁性和拓扑带工程的强大、连续的控制轴。
