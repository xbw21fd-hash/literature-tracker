## Abstract
ABSTRACT Twisted transition metal dichalcogenide (tTMD) provides a highly tunable platform to explore the interplay between strong correlation and topology. The properties of tTMD involving the charge degree of freedom have been extensively studied, while those related to spin are much less investigated. Motivated by the recent discovery of integer and fractional quantum anomalous Hall effects in tMoTe$_2$, for which flat-band ferromagnetism is an essential prerequisite, we theoretically investigate the spin excitations out of the flat-band ferromagnetic ground state in tMoTe$_2$. Remarkably, we identify the itinerant magnons and spin excitons with nontrivial topology. We further show that the topology of these itinerant spin excitations, which are described as particle-hole bound states, is inherited directly from that of the underlying electrons and is fundamentally different from that in local spin systems. Thus, we establish a direct topology relationship between the many-body excitations and their fundamental constituents. We further demonstrate that by tuning the displacement field, a topological transition of these excitations occurs, leading to a step-like change and bifurcation in the thermal Hall conductivity, which could serve as unique and compelling evidence to be tested experimentally. Our work deepens the understanding of spin excitations in itinerant electron systems with flat bands, provides a new paradigm to manipulate the magnon topology by electrical methods and paves the way towards future investigation of magnonics in tTMDs.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 扭曲过渡金属二硫属化物 (tTMD) 提供了一个高度可调的平台来探索强相关性和拓扑之间的相互作用。涉及电荷自由度的 tTMD 特性已被广泛研究，而与自旋相关的特性则研究较少。受最近在 tMoTe$_2$ 中发现整数和分数量子反常霍尔效应的启发，平带铁磁性是其必要的先决条件，我们从理论上研究了 tMoTe$_2$ 中平带铁磁基态的自旋激发。值得注意的是，我们识别出了具有非平凡拓扑的巡回磁振子和自旋激子。我们进一步表明，这些巡回自旋激发的拓扑结构（被描述为粒子空穴束缚态）直接继承自底层电子的拓扑结构，并且与局部自旋系统中的拓扑结构有根本的不同。因此，我们在多体激励与其基本成分之间建立了直接的拓扑关系。我们进一步证明，通过调整位移场，这些激励会发生拓扑转变，导致霍尔热导率发生阶梯式变化和分叉，这可以作为实验测试的独特且令人信服的证据。我们的工作加深了对平带巡回电子系统中自旋激发的理解，提供了通过电学方法操纵磁振子拓扑的新范例，并为未来研究 tTMD 磁振子学铺平了道路。
