## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12581v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Recent observations of large unconventional spin-orbit torques in van der Waals (vdW) materials are driving intense interest for energy-efficient spintronic applications. A key limitation of ferromagnet (FM)/vdW heterostructures is their lower value of damping-like torque conductivity ($\sigma{\rm_{DL}^{y}}$) compared to the conventional heavy metal-based systems, limiting their prospects for commercial spintronic devices. Here, we report both a giant $\sigma{\rm_{DL}^{y}}$ of $-(1.25 \pm 0.11)\times 10^{5}~\hbar/ 2e~\Omega^{-1}$m$^{-1}$ and an unconventional spin-orbit torque in a heterostructure comprising an FM (Ni$_{80}$Fe$_{20}$) and the vdW material GeTe. The value of $\sigma{\rm_{DL}^{y}}$ represents the highest reported torque conductivity for any FM/vdW interface and is comparable to benchmark heavy metal heterostructures. First-principles calculations reveal that this substantial torque originates from the cooperative interplay of the spin Hall effect, orbital Hall effect, and orbital Rashba effect, assisted by interfacial charge transfer. These findings demonstrate the potential of carefully engineered vdW heterostructures to achieve highly efficient electrical manipulation of magnetization at room temperature, paving the way for next-generation low-power spintronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12581v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近对范德华 (vdW) 材料中巨大的非常规自旋轨道扭矩的观察引起了人们对节能自旋电子应用的浓厚兴趣。与传统的基于重金属的系统相比，铁磁体（FM）/vdW异质结构的一个关键限制是它们的类阻尼扭矩传导率（$\sigma{\rm_{DL}^{y}}$）较低，限制了它们在商业自旋电子器件中的前景。在这里，我们报告了由 FM (Ni$_{80}$Fe$_{20}$) 和vdW材料GeTe。 $\sigma{\rm_{DL}^{y}}$ 的值代表任何 FM/vdW 界面报告的最高扭矩传导率，并且与基准重金属异质结构相当。第一性原理计算表明，这种巨大的扭矩源自自旋霍尔效应、轨道霍尔效应和轨道拉什巴效应的相互作用，并辅以界面电荷转移。这些发现证明了精心设计的 vdW 异质结构在室温下实现高效磁化电操纵的潜力，为下一代低功耗自旋电子器件铺平了道路。
