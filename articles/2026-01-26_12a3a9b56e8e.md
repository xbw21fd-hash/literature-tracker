## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16850v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present comprehensive inelastic neutron spectroscopic maps of the magnetic field-induced disordered phase of the Kitaev quantum spin liquid candidate material $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$. For fields along both in-plane high-symmetry directions we observe that the spin excitation spectrum at and above a magnetic field of 8~T is gapped. Excitation modes then sharpen for increasing field but are consistently broader than experimental resolution even at 13.5~T. The out-of-plane dispersion diminishes in the 7-10~T regime, signifying enhanced two-dimensional behavior as the in-plane liquid correlations are established. In this regime, excitations are very broad and largely flat for all accessible energy-momenta, which is kinematically at odds with a magnon-decay picture. By contrast, a continuum of fractionalized excitations naturally yields a broad continuum response, which crucially may be accompanied by sharper modes of bound states of fractionalized excitations. Their damping by the continuum accounts for the observed spectral broadening and field dependence. Our results provide strong evidence for the existence of fractionalized excitations in $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$ in a magnetic field.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16850v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了 Kitaev 量子自旋液体候选材料 $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$ 的磁场诱导无序相的全面非弹性中子谱图。对于沿两个面内高对称方向的场，我们观察到 8~T 磁场及以上的自旋激发谱是有间隙的。然后，激发模式随着场的增加而锐化，但始终比实验分辨率更宽，即使在 13.5~T 下也是如此。面外色散在 7-10~T 范围内减小，表明随着面内液体相关性的建立，二维行为增强。在这种情况下，所有可获取的能量动量的激发范围非常广泛且基本平坦，这在运动学上与磁振子衰变图不一致。相比之下，连续的分段激发自然会产生广泛的连续响应，至关重要的是，这可能伴随着更尖锐的分段激发束缚态模式。它们的连续谱阻尼解释了观察到的光谱展宽和场依赖性。我们的结果为磁场中 $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$ 中存在分段激发提供了有力的证据。
