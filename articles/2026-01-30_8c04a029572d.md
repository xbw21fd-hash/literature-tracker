# Trapping and cooling mechanisms in blue-detuned magneto-optical traps of molecules

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21097v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In red-detuned magneto-optical traps (MOTs) of molecules, sub-Doppler heating competes with Doppler cooling, resulting in high temperature and low density. A solution is offered by the blue-detuned MOT where sub-Doppler cooling dominates and the cloud is compressed. Several blue-detuned molecular MOTs have been implemented. A recent implementation relies on a pair of orthogonally polarized components whose frequency separation is smaller than the transition linewidth. We identify the trapping force in these MOTs. At a certain magnetic field, there is a state that is dark to the laser propagating in one direction, but not to the counter-propagating one. This Zeeman-induced dark state (ZIDS) sets up an imbalance in the photon scattering rate, leading to a restoring force. We also study the role of the moving lattices generated by the closely-spaced frequency components of the light. We show that there is a velocity-dependent force that drives the molecules towards the speeds of these moving lattices, and that over a relevant range of magnetic fields this combines with the ZIDS force to transport molecules towards the centre of the MOT. Here, gray molasses cooling, assisted by non-adiabatic transitions driven by the time-varying polarization of the light field, cools the molecules towards zero velocity. We study these mechanisms for model systems with simple level structures, then extend them to molecules with ground state hyperfine structure.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21097v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在分子的红失谐磁光陷阱（MOT）中，亚多普勒加热与多普勒冷却竞争，导致高温和低密度。蓝色失谐 MOT 提供了一种解决方案，其中亚多普勒冷却占主导地位并且云被压缩。几种蓝色失谐分子 MOT 已经实现。最近的实现依赖于一对正交偏振组件，其频率间隔小于过渡线宽。我们确定了这些 MOT 中的捕获力。在一定的磁场下，存在一种对于沿一个方向传播的激光来说是暗的状态，但对于反向传播的激光则不是。这种塞曼诱发的暗态（ZIDS）会造成光子散射率不平衡，从而产生恢复力。我们还研究了由光的紧密间隔的频率分量产生的移动晶格的作用。我们证明，存在一种与速度相关的力，可以驱动分子朝着这些移动晶格的速度移动，并且在相关的磁场范围内，这种力与 ZIDS 力相结合，将分子输送到 MOT 的中心。在这里，灰糖蜜冷却，在光场时变偏振驱动的非绝热跃迁的辅助下，将分子冷却到零速度。我们研究具有简单能级结构的模型系统的这些机制，然后将它们扩展到具有基态超精细结构的分子。
