## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24398v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Many advanced energy harvesting technologies rely on advanced control of thermal emission. Recently, it has been shown that the emissivity and absorptivity of thermal emitters can be controlled independently in nonreciprocal emitters. While significant progress has been made in engineering these nonreciprocal thermal emitters, realizing a highly efficient, pattern-free emitter capable of supporting dual-polarization nonreciprocal emission remains a challenging task. Existing solutions are largely based on metamaterials and exhibit polarization-dependent behavior. This work proposes pattern-free multilayer heterostructures combining magneto-optical and magnetic Weyl semimetal materials and systematically evaluates their nonreciprocal emission performance for p- and s-polarized waves. The findings show that omnidirectional polarization-independent nonreciprocity can be achieved utilizing multilayer structures with different magnetization directions that do not follow simple vector summation. To further enhance the performance, Pareto optimization is employed to tune the key design parameters, enabling the maximization of nonreciprocal thermal emission in a given wavelength range. This approach offers a versatile strategy for designing high-performance thermal emitters tailored for multi-objective optical functionalities.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24398v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：许多先进的能量收集技术依赖于先进的热排放控制。最近，研究表明，在不可逆发射器中，热发射器的发射率和吸收率可以独立控制。尽管在设计这些不可逆热发射器方面已经取得了重大进展，但实现能够支持双偏振不可逆发射的高效、无图案发射器仍然是一项具有挑战性的任务。现有的解决方案主要基于超材料并表现出与偏振相关的行为。这项工作提出了结合磁光和磁性 Weyl 半金属材料的无图案多层异质结构，并系统地评估了它们对 p 和 s 偏振波的不可逆发射性能。研究结果表明，利用具有不同磁化方向的多层结构（不遵循简单的矢量求和）可以实现全向偏振无关的非互易性。为了进一步提高性能，采用帕累托优化来调整关键设计参数，从而在给定波长范围内实现不可逆热发射的最大化。这种方法提供了一种通用策略，用于设计专为多目标光学功能定制的高性能热发射器。
