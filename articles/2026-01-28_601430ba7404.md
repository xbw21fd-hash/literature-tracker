## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19343v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnets break a combination of time-reversal and rotational symmetries without generating a net magnetization. As such, the order parameter of $d$-wave altermagnets has the same symmetry as magnetic multipoles, and couples to the product of a magnetic field and uniaxial strain. We combine elastocaloric experiments, free-energy modeling, and first-principles calculations on MnF$_2$ to establish a thermodynamic probe of the predicted finite-temperature altermagnetic critical point. These results pave the way to explore altermagnetic quantum criticality in $d$-wave materials and beyond.

arXiv:2601.19343v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交替磁铁打破了时间反转和旋转对称性的组合，而不产生净磁化强度。因此，$d$波交变磁体的有序参数与磁多极具有相同的对称性，并且与磁场和单轴应变的乘积耦合。我们结合弹热实验、自由能建模和 MnF$_2$ 的第一性原理计算，建立了预测的有限温度交变磁临界点的热力学探针。这些结果为探索$d$波材料及其他材料中的交磁量子临界性铺平了道路。
