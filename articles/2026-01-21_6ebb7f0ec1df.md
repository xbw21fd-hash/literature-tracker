## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12510v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin-wave spectrum of monolayer CrSBr waveguides was studied by numerically diagonalizing the Bogoliubov-de Gennes Hamiltonian derived from linearising the Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert equation. In contrast to its short-range counterparts, the long-range dipolar field acts statically as a confining potential for spin wave, while the dynamic part couples the spin and orbit degrees of freedom, thus giving rise to spin-orbit coupling for spin wave. Due to the inversion symmetry of the Hamiltonian and the spinor structure of the wave function, spin-wave eigenstates form doublets with definite parity. Micromagnetic simulation tallies well with numerical calculation. Our study on spin-wave eigenstates in CrSBr waveguides sheds light on the nature of exchange-dipole spin wave in ferromagnetic slabs. We confirm particularly that the robustness of the Damon-Eshbach mode is not derived from topology, but rather from the static dipolar field. Moreover, a thorough knowledge on spin wave in monolayer CrSBr itself represents a step forward to understanding the more complicated antiferromagnetic resonance in bulk CrSBr.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12510v1 公告类型：新 摘要：通过对线性化 Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert 方程导出的 Bogoliubov-de Gennes 哈密顿量进行数值对角化，研究了单层 CrSBr 波导的自旋波谱。与短程偶极场相比，长程偶极场静态地充当自旋波的限制势，而动态部分耦合自旋和轨道自由度，从而引起自旋波的自旋轨道耦合。由于哈密顿量的反演对称性和波函数的旋量结构，自旋波本征态形成具有确定宇称的双峰。微磁模拟与数值计算吻合良好。我们对 CrSBr 波导中自旋波本征态的研究揭示了铁磁板中交换偶极子自旋波的性质。我们特别确认 Damon-Eshbach 模式的鲁棒性并非源自拓扑，而是源自静态偶极场。此外，对单层 CrSBr 中自旋波本身的透彻了解代表了理解块状 CrSBr 中更复杂的反铁磁共振的进步。
