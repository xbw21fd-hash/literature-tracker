## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15431v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Flux pumping was achieved in recent hybrid scenario experiments in the ASDEX Upgrade (AUG) tokamak, which is characterized by a sawtooth-free helical quiescent state and the anomalous radial redistribution of toroidal current density and poloidal magnetic flux. In this article, the self-regulation mechanism of the AUG core plasma during flux pumping is investigated at realistic parameters using the JOREK code based on the two-temperature, nonlinear, full magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) model. A key milestone in AUG flux pumping modelling is achieved by quantitatively reproducing the clamped current density and safety factor profiles in the plasma core, demonstrating the effectiveness of the dynamo effect in sustaining the flux pumping state. The dynamo term, that is of particular interest, is primarily generated by the pressure-gradient driven m/n = 1/1 quasi-interchange-like MHD instability. The work systematically extrapolates the parameter regimes of flux pumping from the above AUG base case by scanning dissipation coefficients and plasma beta. The simulation results reveal bifurcated plasma behaviours at different Hartmann numbers, including distinct states such as flux pumping (helical core with a flat current density), sawteeth (periodic kink-cycling), single crash (without subsequent cycle), and quasi-stationary magnetic island (peaked current density). Transitions from marginal flux pumping state to sawteeth are observed in long-term simulations. The relationships between system dissipation, plasma beta, and different plasma states are carefully analyzed. For practical purposes, the potential operational window for flux pumping, as determined by plasma density and temperature, is estimated. The modelling efforts advance the understanding of flux pumping and facilitate the development of a fast surrogate model for efficient evaluation of flux pumping.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15431v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近在 ASDEX Upgrade (AUG) 托卡马克的混合场景实验中实现了通量泵浦，其特点是无锯齿螺旋静止状态以及环形电流密度和极向磁通量的异常径向重新分布。在本文中，使用基于双温度、非线性、全磁流体动力学 (MHD) 模型的 JOREK 代码，在实际参数下研究了 AUG 核心等离子体在通量泵浦过程中的自调节机制。 AUG 通量泵浦建模的一个关键里程碑是通过定量再现等离子体核心中的钳位电流密度和安全系数曲线来实现的，证明了发电机效应在维持通量泵浦状态方面的有效性。特别令人感兴趣的发电机项主要是由压力梯度驱动的 m/n = 1/1 准交换式 MHD 不稳定性产生的。这项工作通过扫描耗散系数和等离子体β，从上述 AUG 基本情况系统地推断出通量泵浦的参数范围。模拟结果揭示了不同哈特曼数下的分叉等离子体行为，包括不同的状态，例如通量泵浦（具有平坦电流密度的螺旋核）、锯齿（周期性扭结循环）、单次碰撞（无后续循环）和准稳态磁岛（峰值电流密度）。在长期模拟中观察到从边际通量泵浦状态到锯齿状的转变。仔细分析了系统耗散、等离子体β和不同等离子体状态之间的关系。出于实际目的，估计了由等离子体密度和温度确定的通量泵浦的潜在操作窗口。建模工作增进了对通量泵送的理解，并促进了快速替代模型的开发，以有效评估通量泵送。
