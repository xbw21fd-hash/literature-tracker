## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24091v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Synthetic ferrimagnetic (SFiM) multilayers offer a versatile platform for hosting skyrmions with tunable magnetic properties, combining the advantages of ferromagnets and antiferromagnets. Unlike synthetic antiferromagnets, SFiMs retain a finite magnetization that allows direct observation of magnetic textures while still benefiting from reduced dipolar fields and a suppressed skyrmion Hall effect. However, a systematic investigation of their temperature and field dependent magnetization evolution, including the labyrinthine-to-skyrmion transition in Co/Gd-based SFiMs, remains less explored. Here, we demonstrate the stabilization of 70 nm-radius skyrmions at room temperature and reveal how the Co and Gd sublattices influence the temperature-dependent net magnetization. Further, we develop a microscopic spin model for SFiM incorporating the relevant magnetic interactions, which reproduces the experimental observations and captures the temperature-dependent magnetic phase evolution. This framework highlights the interplay of fundamental interactions controlling skyrmion stability in SFiM and provides a pathway for engineering heterostructures for topological spintronic applications.

arXiv:2512.24091v1 公告类型：新 摘要：合成亚铁磁 (SFiM) 多层膜结合了铁磁体和反铁磁体的优点，为承载具有可调磁性能的斯格明子提供了一个多功能平台。与合成反铁磁体不同，SFiM 保留了有限的磁化强度，可以直接观察磁性纹理，同时仍然受益于减少的偶极场和抑制的斯格明子霍尔效应。然而，对其温度和场相关磁化演化的系统研究，包括基于 Co/Gd 的 SFiM 中的迷宫到斯格明子的转变，仍然很少被探索。在这里，我们展示了 70 nm 半径斯格明子在室温下的稳定性，并揭示了 Co 和 Gd 亚晶格如何影响温度相关的净磁化强度。此外，我们为 SFiM 开发了一个结合相关磁相互作用的微观自旋模型，该模型重现了实验观察结果并捕获了与温度相关的磁相演化。该框架强调了 SFiM 中控制斯格明子稳定性的基本相互作用的相互作用，并为拓扑自旋电子应用的工程异质结构提供了一条途径。
