## Abstract
TLDR: By stacking two identical monolayer TMDs in parallel, this work obtains electrically switchable rhombohedral-stacking configurations, with out-of-plane polarization that is flipped by in-plane sliding motion, and quantifies the ferroelectric built-in interlayer potential, in good agreement with first-principles calculations.
