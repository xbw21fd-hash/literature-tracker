## Abstract
S-Adenosyl methionine (SAM) is a biomolecule containing an adenosyl cation bonded to the sulfur atom of methionine (Met). SAM is a primary methyl donor and the precursor of the Gluthatione (GSH). Due to the role played by SAM in the proper functioning of cellular metabolism, it is an interesting biomolecule to be investigated as a potential therapeutic agent for the treatment of various pathologies and disorders and in scavenging hydroxyl radicals. In this work, the oxidation process of SAM induced by gamma-radiolysis has been investigated by collision-induced dissociation tandem mass spectrometry (CID-MS2) and Infrared Multiple Photon Dissociation Spectroscopy (IRMPD) to reveal the modified sites and to characterize the 3D structure of the oxo-forms of SAM in the gas phase. Both CID-MS2 and IRMPD experiments coupled with DFT calculations revealed that only the adenine moiety of the molecule is oxidized, protecting the methionine from oxidation and preserving its function.

## 摘要（中文）
S-腺苷甲硫氨酸 (SAM) 是一种生物分子，含有与甲硫氨酸 (Met) 的硫原子键合的腺苷阳离子。 SAM 是主要甲基供体，也是谷胱甘肽 (GSH) 的前体。由于 SAM 在细胞代谢正常功能中发挥的作用，它是一种有趣的生物分子，可作为治疗各种病理和疾病以及清除羟基自由基的潜在治疗剂进行研究。在这项工作中，通过碰撞诱导解离串联质谱 (CID-MS2) 和红外多光子解离光谱 (IRMPD) 研究了伽马放射解诱导的 SAM 氧化过程，以揭示修饰位点并表征气相中 SAM 氧代形式的 3D 结构。 CID-MS2 和 IRMPD 实验与 DFT 计算相结合表明，只有分子的腺嘌呤部分被氧化，从而保护了蛋氨酸免受氧化并保留了其功能。
