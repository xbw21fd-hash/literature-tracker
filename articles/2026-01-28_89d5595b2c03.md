## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19073v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic islands strongly influence cross-field electron transport in magnetized plasmas. In particular, bifurcations of the island topology modify the number and location of O-points, X-points, and separatrix boundaries, thereby altering diffusion pathways. In recent DIII-D experiments, external magnetic perturbations were used to rotate and periodically bifurcate the island on the q = 2 surface, causing a switchback between a q = 2/1-dominated structure and a narrower q = 4/2-dominated structure. To investigate how this topological change affects electron transport, we employ the field line tracing code TRIP3D with an implemented collisional operator. Thermal, tracer electrons launched from O-points, X-points, and outside separatrix boundaries reveal distinct diffusion regimes, including classical, subdiffusive, and superdiffusive behavior, depending on both the dominant island mode and launch location. These results suggest that island bifurcation can alter electron diffusion across rational surfaces, with direct implications for particle confinement. While the present work emphasizes diffusion as a general framework, the findings provide insight into the conditions under which electron trapping into an island or stochastization of the island's separatrix can enable additional mechanisms, such as the generation of energetic electrons.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19073v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁岛强烈影响磁化等离子体中的交叉场电子传输。特别是，岛拓扑的分叉会改变 O 点、X 点和分界线边界的数量和位置，从而改变扩散路径。在最近的 DIII-D 实验中，外部磁扰动被用来旋转和周期性地分叉 q = 2 表面上的岛，导致 q = 2/1 主导结构和较窄 q = 4/2 主导结构之间的折返。为了研究这种拓扑变化如何影响电子传输，我们采用场线追踪代码 TRIP3D 和实现的碰撞算子。从 O 点、X 点和分界线外边界发射的热示踪电子揭示了不同的扩散机制，包括经典扩散、次扩散和超扩散行为，具体取决于主要岛模式和发射位置。这些结果表明，岛分叉可以改变电子在有理表面上的扩散，对粒子限制有直接影响。虽然目前的工作强调扩散作为一个总体框架，但这些发现提供了对电子俘获到岛中或岛分界线的随机化可以实现额外机制（例如高能电子的产生）的条件的深入了解。
