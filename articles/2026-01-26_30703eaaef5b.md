## Abstract
arXiv:2508.05127v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Magnetically confined fusion plasmas exhibit predator-prey-like cyclic oscillations through the self-regulating interaction between drift-wave turbulence and zonal flow. To elucidate the detailed mechanism and causality underlying this phenomenon, we construct a simple stochastic predator-prey model that incorporates intrinsic fluctuations and analyze its statistical properties from an information-theoretic perspective. We first show that the model exhibits persistent fluctuating cyclic oscillations called quasi-cycles due to amplification of intrinsic noise. This result suggests the possibility that the previously observed periodic oscillations in a toroidal plasma are not limit cycles but quasi-cycles, and that such quasi-cycles may be widely observed under various conditions. For this model, we further prove that information of the zonal flow is propagated to turbulence. This result suggests that turbulence behavior may be predictable to a certain extent based on zonal flow characteristics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.05127v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：磁约束聚变等离子体通过漂移波湍流和纬向流之间的自调节相互作用表现出捕食者-猎物般的循环振荡。为了阐明这种现象背后的详细机制和因果关系，我们构建了一个简单的随机捕食者-被捕食者模型，该模型包含内在波动，并从信息论的角度分析其统计特性。我们首先表明，由于固有噪声的放大，该模型表现出持续波动的循环振荡，称为准循环。这一结果表明，先前观察到的环形等离子体中的周期性振荡可能不是极限循环而是准循环，并且这种准循环可以在各种条件下广泛观察到。对于这个模型，我们进一步证明了纬向流的信息可以传播到湍流。这一结果表明，根据层流特征，湍流行为在一定程度上是可预测的。
