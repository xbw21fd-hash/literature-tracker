## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02706v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Electric Propulsion Electrostatic Analyzer Experiment (\`EP\`EE) is a compact ion energy bandpass filter deployed on the International Space Station (ISS) in March 2023 and providing continuous measurements through April 2024. This period coincides with the Solar Cycle 25 maximum, capturing unique observations of solar activity extremes in the mid- to low-latitude regions of the topside ionosphere. From these in situ spectra we derive plasma parameters that inform space-weather impacts on satellite navigation and radio communication. We present a statistical processing pipeline for \`EP\`EE that (i) estimates the instrument noise floor, (ii) accounts for irregular temporal sampling, and (iii) extracts ionospheric signals. Rather than discarding noisy data, the method learns a baseline noise model and fits the measurement surface using a scaled Vecchia Gaussian process approximation, recovering values typically rejected by thresholding. The resulting products increase data coverage and enable noise-assisted monitoring of ionospheric variability.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02706v1 公告类型：新 摘要：电推进静电分析仪实验 (\`EP\`EE) 是一种紧凑型离子能量带通滤波器，于 2023 年 3 月部署在国际空间站 (ISS) 上，并在 2024 年 4 月之前提供连续测量。这一时期与第 25 个太阳活动周期最大值一致，捕获了上层电离层中低纬度地区太阳活动极端情况的独特观测结果。从这些原位光谱中，我们得出等离子体参数，这些参数可告知空间天气对卫星导航和无线电通信的影响。我们提出了 EP EE 的统计处理流程，它 (i) 估计仪器本底噪声，(ii) 考虑不规则时间采样，以及 (iii) 提取电离层信号。该方法不是丢弃噪声数据，而是学习基线噪声模型，并使用缩放的韦基亚高斯过程近似来拟合测量表面，恢复通常被阈值处理拒绝的值。由此产生的产品增加了数据覆盖范围，并实现了电离层变化的噪声辅助监测。
