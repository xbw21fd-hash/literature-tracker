## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15598v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the problem of mechanical stability in two-dimensional Bose-Fermi mixtures at zero temperature, focusing on systems with a tunable Bose-Fermi (BF) interaction and a weak but finite boson-boson (BB) repulsion. The analysis is carried out within the framework of the lowest-order constrained variational (LOCV) approach, which allows for a non-perturbative treatment of strong interspecies correlations while retaining analytical transparency. The BF interaction is modeled by a properly regularized attractive contact potential, enabling the exploration of both the attractive and repulsive energy branches. We determine the minimal BB repulsion required to ensure mechanical stability of the mixture by evaluating the inverse compressibility matrix over the full range of BF coupling strengths, within the domain of validity of the LOCV approximation. The interaction contribution to the energy is benchmarked against available experimental data and Quantum Monte Carlo results in the single-impurity limit, showing good agreement. Our analysis reveals how the critical BB coupling depends on interaction strength, density imbalance, and mass ratio. In particular, we find that mixtures with equal boson and fermion masses exhibit enhanced stability, requiring the smallest BB repulsion to prevent mechanical instability. In this case, a relatively small BB interaction is sufficient to stabilize attractive mixtures for all values of the BF interaction. These results provide a theoretical framework for assessing stability conditions in experimentally realizable two-dimensional Bose-Fermi mixtures with tunable interactions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15598v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了零温下二维玻色-费米混合物的机械稳定性问题，重点关注具有可调玻色-费米 (BF) 相互作用和弱但有限的玻色子-玻色子 (BB) 斥力的系统。该分析是在最低阶约束变分（LOCV）方法的框架内进行的，该方法允许对强种间相关性进行非微扰处理，同时保持分析透明度。 BF 相互作用通过适当正则化的吸引接触势进行建模，从而能够探索吸引和排斥能量分支。我们通过在 LOCV 近似的有效性范围内评估整个 BF 耦合强度范围内的逆压缩矩阵，确定确保混合物机械稳定性所需的最小 BB 斥力。相互作用对能量的贡献以可用的实验数据为基准，并且单杂质极限的量子蒙特卡罗结果显示出良好的一致性。我们的分析揭示了临界 BB 耦合如何取决于相互作用强度、密度不平衡和质量比。特别是，我们发现玻色子和费米子质量相等的混合物表现出增强的稳定性，需要最小的 BB 排斥力来防止机械不稳定。在这种情况下，相对较小的 BB 相互作用足以稳定所有 BF 相互作用值的有吸引力的混合物。这些结果为评估可通过实验实现的具有可调相互作用的二维玻色-费米混合物的稳定性条件提供了理论框架。
