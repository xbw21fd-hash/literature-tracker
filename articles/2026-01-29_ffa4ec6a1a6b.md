## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20626v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The CYGNO experiment employs an optical-readout Time Projection Chamber (TPC) to search for rare low-energy interactions using finely resolved scintillation images. While the optical readout provides rich topological information, it produces large, sparse megapixel images that challenge real-time triggering, data reduction, and background discrimination. We summarize two complementary machine-learning approaches developed within CYGNO. First, we present a fast and fully unsupervised strategy for online data reduction based on reconstruction-based anomaly detection. A convolutional autoencoder trained exclusively on pedestal images (i.e. frames acquired with GEM amplification disabled) learns the detector noise morphology and highlights particle-induced structures through localized reconstruction residuals, from which compact Regions of Interest (ROIs) are extracted. On real prototype data, the selected configuration retains (93.0 +/- 0.2)% of reconstructed signal intensity while discarding (97.8 +/- 0.1)% of the image area, with ~25 ms per-frame inference time on a consumer GPU. Second, we report a weakly supervised application of the Classification Without Labels (CWoLa) framework to data acquired with an Americium--Beryllium neutron source. Using only mixed AmBe and standard datasets (no event-level labels), a convolutional classifier learns to identify nuclear-recoil-like topologies. The achieved performance approaches the theoretical limit imposed by the mixture composition and isolates a high-score population with compact, approximately circular morphologies consistent with nuclear recoils.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20626v1 公告类型：新 摘要：CYGNO 实验采用光学读出时间投影室 (TPC)，利用精细解析的闪烁图像来搜索罕见的低能量相互作用。虽然光学读出提供了丰富的拓扑信息，但它会产生大而稀疏的百万像素图像，这对实时触发、数据缩减和背景辨别提出了挑战。我们总结了 CYGNO 中开发的两种互补的机器学习方法。首先，我们提出了一种基于重建的异常检测的快速且完全无监督的在线数据缩减策略。专门在基座图像（即禁用 GEM 放大时获取的帧）上训练的卷积自动编码器学习探测器噪声形态，并通过局部重建残差突出显示粒子引起的结构，从中提取紧凑的感兴趣区域 (ROI)。在真实原型数据上，所选配置保留 (93.0 +/- 0.2)% 的重建信号强度，同时丢弃 (97.8 +/- 0.1)% 的图像区域，在消费级 GPU 上每帧推理时间约为 25 毫秒。其次，我们报告了无标签分类（CWoLa）框架在镅-铍中子源获取的数据上的弱监督应用。仅使用混合 AmBe 和标准数据集（无事件级标签），卷积分类器学习识别类似核反冲的拓扑。所实现的性能接近混合物成分所施加的理论极限，并分离出具有与核反冲一致的紧凑、近似圆形形态的高分群体。
