## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20741v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Star-like microgels have recently emerged as a promising class of thermoresponsive soft colloids, that have an internal architecture similar to that of star polymers. Here, we perform extensive monomer-resolved simulations to theoretically establish this analogy. First, we characterize the effective potential between star-like microgels, finding that it is Gaussian for an extended range of distances, in stark contrast to the Hertzian-like one of standard microgels, but almost identical to that of star polymers with a core partially covered by chains. Next, we investigate the ratio between gyration and hydrodynamic radii across the volume-phase transition, showing qualitative agreement with both star polymers and experimental data. Finally, we estimate the bulk modulus, finding star-like microgels significantly softer than standard microgels and comparable to star polymers. The present work thus demonstrates that star-like microgels behave as ultrasoft particles, akin to star polymers, paving the way for their exploration at high concentrations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20741v1 公告类型：新 摘要：星状微凝胶最近作为一类有前途的热响应软胶体出现，其内部结构与星状聚合物相似。在这里，我们进行了广泛的单体解析模拟，从理论上建立了这种类比。首先，我们表征了星状微凝胶之间的有效势，发现它在较长距离范围内呈高斯分布，与标准微凝胶的赫兹状微凝胶形成鲜明对比，但与核心部分被链覆盖的星形聚合物几乎相同。接下来，我们研究了整个体积相变中的回转半径和流体动力学半径之间的比率，显示出与星形聚合物和实验数据的定性一致性。最后，我们估计了体积模量，发现星形微凝胶比标准微凝胶软得多，并且与星形聚合物相当。因此，目前的工作表明，星状微凝胶的行为类似于超软颗粒，类似于星状聚合物，为它们在高浓度下的探索铺平了道路。
