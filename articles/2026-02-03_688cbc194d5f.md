## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02111v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The theory of inter-spin-subband electric dipole spin resonance in transition metal dichalcogenide heterobilayers is proposed. Our symmetry analysis demonstrates that, in contrast to monolayers, the reduced symmetry of heterobilayers enables coupling between conduction band spin subbands by an electric field. We establish the optical selection rules for all six high-symmetry stacking configurations. The microscopic mechanism of the effect is identified as the spin-orbit coupling induced mixing of Bloch states from different conduction bands, which generates a non-zero momentum matrix element between the spin-split states. It also leads to the linear-in-wavevector spin-dependent terms in the effective Hamiltonian, i.e., the Rashba effect. Our estimates show that the rate of electric-dipole spin-flip transitions exceeds by far that of the magnetic-dipole transitions in transition metal dichalcogenide heterobilayers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02111v1 发布类型：新 摘要：提出了过渡金属二硫属化物异质双层中自旋子带间电偶极子自旋共振理论。我们的对称性分析表明，与单层相比，异双层的对称性降低使得导带自旋子带之间可以通过电场进行耦合。我们为所有六种高对称堆叠配置建立了光学选择规则。该效应的微观机制被认为是自旋轨道耦合引起来自不同导带的布洛赫态的混合，从而在自旋分裂态之间产生非零动量矩阵元素。它还导致有效哈密顿量中的波矢量线性自旋相关项，即拉什巴效应。我们的估计表明，过渡金属二硫属化物异质双层中电偶极子自旋翻转跃迁的速率远远超过磁偶极子跃迁的速率。
