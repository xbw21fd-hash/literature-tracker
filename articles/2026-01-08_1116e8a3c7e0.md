## Abstract
Ultracold hydrogen-exchange reactions have long provided a key platform for identifying subtle quantum mechanical effects in triatomic systems. The long-range interactions, diagonal Born–Oppenheimer correction (DBOC), and geometric phase (GP) have been predicted to influence such reactions, yet their individual effects have not been investigated. Here, we investigate the D + H 2 ( v = 4, j = 0) → H + HD reaction using time-dependent wave packet calculations that incorporate a newly constructed long-range potential, DBOC, and a diabatic GP treatment. The inclusion of the accurate long-range interactions substantially alters the cold reaction dynamics by removing spurious short-range barriers and strongly modifying partial-wave resonances, including a pronounced shift and nearly 70% enhancement of the J = 2 resonance. The DBOC produces a modest overall reduction in the reaction rate, whereas the GP introduces the dominant suppression and governs the interference pattern in both total and state-resolved rates. These results demonstrate that the accurate inclusion of long-range interactions, DBOC, and GP is essential for reliably describing ultracold hydrogen–deuterium exchange reactions, clarifying the distinct roles each contribution plays in the reaction dynamics.

超冷氢交换反应长期以来一直为识别三原子系统中微妙的量子力学效应提供了一个关键平台。长程相互作用、对角玻恩-奥本海默校正 (DBOC) 和几何相位 (GP) 已被预测会影响此类反应，但尚未研究它们的个体效应。在这里，我们使用依赖时间的波包计算研究了 D + H 2 ( v = 4, j = 0) → H + HD 反应，该计算结合了新构建的长程势、DBOC 和非绝热 GP 处理。包含精确的长程相互作用通过消除寄生的短程势垒和强烈改变部分波共振（包括 J = 2 共振的显着转变和近 70% 的增强）来显着改变冷反应动力学。 DBOC 会适度降低反应速率，而 GP 则引入显性抑制并控制总分辨速率和状态分辨速率的干扰模式。这些结果表明，准确包含长程相互作用、DBOC 和 GP 对于可靠地描述超冷氢-氘交换反应、阐明每种贡献在反应动力学中发挥的不同作用至关重要。
