## Abstract
Author(s): Jie Chen, Maobin Hu, Ming Li, Fulong Chen, and Jinde Cao Transport on complex networks is examined using a non-Markovian traffic dynamics with congestion-dependent link travel times. The results identify fast-is-fast, slow-is-fast, and Parrondo-like efficiency regimes controlled by routing preferences. [Phys. Rev. Research 8, L012021] Published Thu Jan 22, 2026

## 摘要（中文）
作者：Jie Chen、Maobin Hu、Ming Li、Fulong Chen 和 Jinde Cao 使用非马尔可夫交通动态和拥塞相关的链路旅行时间来检查复杂网络上的交通。结果确定了由路由偏好控制的快就是快、慢就是快和类似 Parrondo 的效率机制。 [物理。 Rev. Research 8, L012021] 2026 年 1 月 22 日星期四发布
