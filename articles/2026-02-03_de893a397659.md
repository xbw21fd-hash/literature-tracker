## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00692v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Collective electronic excitations, including plasmons, excitons, and intra- and interband transitions, play a central role in determining the dynamic screening, optical response, and energy transport properties of materials. Recent advances in momentum- and frequency-resolved spectroscopies, such as electron energy-loss spectroscopy (EELS) and inelastic x-ray scattering (IXS), together with progress in first-principles many-body perturbation theory (MBPT) calculations, now allow collective excitations to be mapped with considerable precision across the Brillouin zone. This topical review surveys current developments in the representation and interpretation of both experimental and theoretical dielectric-response spectra. Particular emphasis is placed on recent ways of representing spectral band structures (SBS) of the direct and inverse dielectric functions, such as analytical approaches based on multipole-Pad\'e approximants in momentum and frequency (MPA($\q$)), which provide a combined band-like description of the dispersion of the main collective excitations. We discuss how features observed in metals, semiconductors, and low dimensional systems reflect the interplay between electronic structure, screening strength, and local-field effects, and how post-processing procedures can improve the quantitative comparison between experiment and theory. Finally, we provide perspectives on open challenges and potential developments in quantitative dielectric-function analyses.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00692v1 公告类型：新 摘要：集体电子激发，包括等离子体激元、激子以及带内和带间跃迁，在确定材料的动态筛选、光学响应和能量传输特性方面发挥着核心作用。动量和频率分辨光谱学的最新进展，例如电子能量损失光谱 (EELS) 和非弹性 X 射线散射 (IXS)，以及第一原理多体微扰理论 (MBPT) 计算的进展，现在允许在整个布里渊区以相当高的精度绘制集体激发图。本专题综述调查了实验和理论介电响应谱的表示和解释的当前进展。特别强调最近表示正介电函数和逆介电函数的谱带结构（SBS）的方法，例如基于动量和频率的多极Pad\'e近似的分析方法（MPA（$\q$）），它提供了主要集体激励色散的组合带状描述。我们讨论在金属、半导体和低维系统中观察到的特征如何反映电子结构、屏蔽强度和局域场效应之间的相互作用，以及后处理程序如何改进实验与理论之间的定量比较。最后，我们提供了定量介电函数分析中的开放挑战和潜在发展的观点。
