## Abstract
arXiv:2511.18069v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The kinetics of hydrogen diffusion in C15 cubic and C14 hexagonal TiCr$_2$H$_x$ (0 < $x$ <= 4) Laves-phase hydrogen storage alloys is investigated with density functional theory (DFT) and machine learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs). Generalized solid-state nudged elastic band calculations are conducted based on DFT for all symmetrically inequivalent paths between the first-nearest-neighbor face-sharing interstitial sites. The hydrogen migration barriers are substantially higher for the paths that require breaking a Ti-H bond than for those that require breaking a Cr-H bond. Molecular dynamics (MD) simulations with the MLIPs also demonstrate that hydrogen migration occurs more frequently within the hexagonal rings made of the A$_2$B$_2$ interstitial paths, each requiring the breaking of Cr-H bonds, than along the inter-ring paths. The diffusion coefficients of hydrogen obtained from the MD simulations reveal a non-monotonic dependence on hydrogen concentration, which is more pronounced at lower temperatures. Time-averaged radial distribution functions of hydrogen further show that hydrogen avoids face-sharing positions during diffusion and that the hydrogen occupancy at the second-nearest-neighbor edge-sharing positions increases with increasing hydrogen concentration. The diffusion coefficients of hydrogen within 400-1000 K follow an Arrhenius relationship, with activation barriers consistent with most experimental values. One-order of magnitude overestimation of diffusion coefficients compared with some experiments suggests a substantial impact of hydrogen trapping by defects such as Cr vacancies and Ti anti-sites in non-stoichiometric TiCr$_2$ in experiments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.18069v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：利用密度泛函理论 (DFT) 和机器学习原子间势 (MLIP) 研究了 C15 立方和 C14 六方 TiCr$_2$H$_x$ (0 < $x$ <= 4) Laves 相储氢合金中的氢扩散动力学。基于 DFT 对第一最近邻面共享间隙位点之间的所有对称不等价路径进行广义固态微移弹性带计算。需要破坏 Ti-H 键的路径的氢迁移势垒明显高于需要破坏 Cr-H 键的路径。 MLIP 的分子动力学 (MD) 模拟还表明，氢迁移在由 A$_2$B$_2$ 间隙路径组成的六角环内发生得更频繁，每个间隙路径都需要断裂 Cr-H 键，而不是沿着环间路径。 MD 模拟获得的氢扩散系数揭示了氢浓度的非单调依赖性，这在较低温度下更为明显。氢的时间平均径向分布函数进一步表明，氢在扩散过程中避免了共面位置，并且第二近邻共边位置处的氢占有率随着氢浓度的增加而增加。 400-1000 K 范围内氢的扩散系数遵循阿伦尼乌斯关系，活化势垒与大多数实验值一致。与一些实验相比，扩散系数高估了一个数量级，这表明实验中非化学计量 TiCr$_2$ 中 Cr 空位和 Ti 反位点等缺陷对氢捕获有重大影响。
