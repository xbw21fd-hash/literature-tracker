## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03326v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic memory and logic devices, including prospective ones based on skyrmions, inevitably produce heat. Thus, controlling heat flow is essential for their performance. Here we study how non-collinear spin arrangement affects the magnon contribution to thermal conductivity. As a paradigm system, we consider the most basic non-collinear magnet with a spin spiral ground state. Spin noncollinearity leads to anharmonic terms, resulting in magnon fusion and decay processes. These processes determine the magnon lifetime, which can be used to estimate thermal conductivity in a single-mode approximation. However, by solving the full Boltzmann equation numerically, we find a much higher thermal conductivity. This signifies that heat is carried not by individual magnons but by their linear combinations -- relaxons. The thermal conductivity is found to increase with the diminishing spiral pitch, consistent with recent experiments. The results provide the blueprint for calculating magnetic thermal transport in non-collinear magnets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03326v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁存储器和逻辑器件，包括基于斯格明子的前瞻性器件，不可避免地会产生热量。因此，控制热流对于其性能至关重要。在这里，我们研究非共线自旋排列如何影响磁振子对热导率的贡献。作为一个范例系统，我们考虑具有自旋螺旋基态的最基本的非共线磁体。自旋非线性会导致非调和项，从而导致磁振子聚变和衰变过程。这些过程决定了磁振子寿命，可用于估计单模近似中的热导率。然而，通过数值求解完整的玻尔兹曼方程，我们发现导热率要高得多。这意味着热量不是由单个磁振子携带的，而是由它们的线性组合——弛豫子携带的。发现热导率随着螺距的减小而增加，这与最近的实验一致。结果为计算非共线磁体中的磁热传输提供了蓝图。
