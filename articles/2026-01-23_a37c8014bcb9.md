## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15735v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a framework for generating magnetic structures, inspired by the concept of oriented spin space groups (SSGs): magnetic structures are first generated as totally symmetric representations of an SSG and are then rotated such that they belong to the maximal magnetic space group of the SSG, which we term spin-symmetry-adapted (SSA) structures and oriented SSA structures, respectively. This is a natural framework to enforce fixed magnetic moment magnitudes on the symmetry-equivalent sites as well as to exploit the spin-orbit coupling (SOC)-induced hierarchy of energy scales. To examine the present scheme, we analyze the MAGNDATA database and find that 77% of the reported structures are reproducible at the SSG level, among which 82% are fully reproduced within the oriented SSG scheme, regardless of their spin-only group types or propagation vectors. To quantitatively assess computational and predictive performance, we perform spin density functional theory calculations for 283 materials, first carrying out self-consistent calculations for SSA structures without SOC, followed by fixed-charge calculations including SOC for the descendant oriented SSA structures. The experimental magnetic structures are reproduced as energetically most stable in 82% of cases at the SSG level without SOC and in 76% of cases at the oriented SSG level with SOC, showing that the fixed-charge scheme enables accurate evaluation of SOC-induced energy differences at low computational cost. The characteristic energy scale among oriented SSA structures is only $\sim$0.29 meV per magnetic atom, about 300 times smaller than that of distinct SSA structures. These results demonstrate that oriented SSG-based enumeration, combined with the two-step calculations for SSA and oriented SSA structures, provides an efficient and robust route for large-scale magnetic-structure prediction.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15735v1 公告类型：新 摘要：受定向自旋空间群（SSG）概念的启发，我们提出了一种生成磁性结构的框架：磁性结构首先生成为 SSG 的完全对称表示，然后进行旋转，使其属于 SSG 的最大磁性空间群，我们分别将其称为自旋对称适应（SSA）结构和定向 SSA 结构。这是一个自然的框架，可以在对称等效位点上强制固定磁矩大小，并利用自旋轨道耦合（SOC）引起的能量尺度层次结构。为了检查目前的方案，我们分析了 MAGNDATA 数据库，发现 77% 的报告结构在 SSG 水平上是可重现的，其中 82% 在定向 SSG 方案中完全重现，无论其纯自旋基团类型或传播向量如何。为了定量评估计算和预测性能，我们对 283 种材料进行了自旋密度泛函理论计算，首先对没有 SOC 的 SSA 结构进行自洽计算，然后对后代定向 SSA 结构进行包括 SOC 的固定电荷计算。在没有 SOC 的 SSG 水平上，82% 的情况下，在有 SOC 的定向 SSG 水平上，76% 的情况下，实验磁结构在能量上最稳定，这表明固定电荷方案能够以较低的计算成本准确评估 SOC 引起的能量差异。定向 SSA 结构的特征能级仅为每个磁性原子 $\sim$0.29 meV，比不同的 SSA 结构小约 300 倍。这些结果表明，基于定向 SSG 的枚举与 SSA 和定向 SSA 结构的两步计算相结合，为大规模磁结构预测提供了有效且稳健的途径。
