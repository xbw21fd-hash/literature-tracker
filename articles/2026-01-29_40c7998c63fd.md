## Abstract
arXiv:2505.22734v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: The Lottery Ticket Hypothesis (LTH) posits that within overparametrized neural networks, there exist sparse subnetworks that are capable of matching the performance of the original model when trained in isolation from the original initialization. We extend this hypothesis to the unsupervised task of approximating the ground state of quantum many-body Hamiltonians, a problem equivalent to finding a neural-network compression of the lowest-lying eigenvector of an exponentially large matrix. Focusing on two representative quantum Hamiltonians, the transverse field Ising model (TFIM) and the toric code (TC), we demonstrate that sparse neural networks can reach accuracies comparable to their dense counterparts, even when pruned by more than an order of magnitude in parameter count. Crucially, and unlike the original LTH, we find that performance depends only on the structure of the sparse subnetwork, not on the specific initialization, when trained in isolation. Moreover, we identify universal scaling behavior that persists across network sizes and physical models, where the boundaries of scaling regions are determined by the underlying Hamiltonian. At the onset of high-error scaling, we observe signatures of a sparsity-induced quantum phase transition that is first-order in shallow networks. Finally, we demonstrate that pruning enhances interpretability by linking the structure of sparse subnetworks to the underlying physics of the Hamiltonian.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.22734v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：彩票假说（LTH）假设，在过参数化神经网络中，存在稀疏子网络，在与原始初始化隔离的情况下进行训练时，这些子网络能够匹配原始模型的性能。我们将这一假设扩展到近似量子多体哈密顿量的基态的无监督任务，这个问题相当于找到指数大矩阵的最低特征向量的神经网络压缩。重点关注两个具有代表性的量子哈密顿量，横向场伊辛模型（TFIM）和环面码（TC），我们证明稀疏神经网络可以达到与密集神经网络相当的精度，即使在参数计数上修剪超过一个数量级时也是如此。至关重要的是，与原始 LTH 不同的是，我们发现在单独训练时，性能仅取决于稀疏子网络的结构，而不取决于具体的初始化。此外，我们确定了跨网络规模和物理模型持续存在的通用缩放行为，其中缩放区域的边界由底层哈密顿量确定。在高误差缩放开始时，我们观察到稀疏引起的量子相变的特征，该相变在浅层网络中是一阶的。最后，我们证明剪枝通过将稀疏子网络的结构与哈密顿量的基础物理联系起来来增强可解释性。
