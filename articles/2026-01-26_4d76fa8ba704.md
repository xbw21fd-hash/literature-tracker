## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16942v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetoelastic coupling in van der Waals (vdW) magnetic materials enables a unique interplay between the spin and lattice degrees of freedom. Characterizing the elastic responses with atomic and femtosecond resolution across the magnetic transition is essential for guiding the design of magnetically tunable actuators and strain-mediated spintronic devices. Here, ultrafast x-ray diffraction employed at a free-electron laser reveals that the atomic displacements, wave vectors, and dispersion relations of acoustic phonon modes in a vdW antiferromagnet FePS$_3$ are coupled with the magnetic order, by tracking both in-plane and out-of-plane Bragg peaks upon optical excitation across the N\'eel temperature (T$_N$). One transverse mode shows that a quasi-out-of-plane atomic displacement undergoes a significant directional change across T$_N$. Its quasi-in-plane wave vector is derived by the comparison between the measured sound velocity and the first-principles calculations. The other transverse mode is an interlayer shear acoustic mode whose amplitude is strongly enhanced in the antiferromagnetic phase, exhibiting eight times stronger amplitude than the longitudinal acoustic mode below T$_N$. The atomically resolved characterization of acoustic phonon dynamics that couple with magnetic ordering opens opportunities for harnessing unique magnetoelastic coupling in vdW magnets on ultrafast timescales.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16942v1 公告类型：新 摘要：范德华 (vdW) 磁性材料中的磁弹性耦合能够实现自旋和晶格自由度之间独特的相互作用。用原子和飞秒分辨率表征整个磁转变的弹性响应对于指导磁可调执行器和应变介导的自旋电子器件的设计至关重要。在这里，自由电子激光器中采用的超快 X 射线衍射揭示了 vdW 反铁磁体 FePS$_3$ 中声学声子模式的原子位移、波矢量和色散关系与磁序耦合，通过跟踪 N\'eel 温度 (T$_N$) 光学激发时的面内和面外布拉格峰。一种横模表明准面外原子位移在 T$_N$ 上经历了显着的方向变化。通过实测声速与第一性原理计算的比较，推导出其准面内波矢。另一种横模是层间剪切声模，其振幅在反铁磁相中强烈增强，其振幅比T$_N$以下的纵声模强八倍。与磁有序耦合的声声声子动力学的原子分辨表征为在超快时间尺度上利用 vdW 磁体中独特的磁弹性耦合提供了机会。
