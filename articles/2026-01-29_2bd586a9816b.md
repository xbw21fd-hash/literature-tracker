## Abstract
arXiv:2508.12153v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Spin-wave excitations are fundamental to understanding the behavior of magnetic materials and hold promise for future information and communication technologies. Yet, modeling these accurately in transition-metal compounds remains challenging, starting from the self-interaction errors affecting localized and partially filled $d$-orbitals in density-functional theory (DFT) with (semi-)local functionals. In this work, we compare three advanced first-principles approaches for computing magnetic exchange parameters and magnon dispersions in NiO and MnO, all based on a common DFT+$U$ ground state with ab initio Hubbard $U$ values obtained from density-functional perturbation theory. Two methods extract exchange parameters directly: one via total-energy differences using the four-state mapping ($\Delta E$), and the other via the magnetic force theorem (MFT) using infinitesimal spin rotations. Magnon dispersions are then obtained from a Heisenberg Hamiltonian through linear spin-wave theory (LSWT). The third approach, time-dependent density-functional perturbation theory with $U$ (TDDFPT+$U$), yields magnon dispersions directly from the dynamical spin susceptibility, with exchange parameters fitted a posteriori, for comparison, via LSWT. Our results show that TDDFPT+$U$ and the Heisenberg model based on $\Delta E$-derived parameters align well with experimental neutron scattering data, whereas the MFT-based approach shows larger discrepancies, possibly due to some inherent approximations and limitations of the particular implementation used. This study benchmarks the accuracy of state-of-the-art first-principles techniques for spin-wave modeling and contributes to advancing reliable computational tools for the study and design of magnetic materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.12153v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：自旋波激励是理解磁性材料行为的基础，并为未来的信息和通信技术带来希望。然而，在过渡金属化合物中准确建模这些仍然具有挑战性，从影响具有（半）局部泛函的密度泛函理论（DFT）中局部和部分填充 $d$ 轨道的自相互作用误差开始。在这项工作中，我们比较了计算 NiO 和 MnO 中磁交换参数和磁振子色散的三种先进的第一原理方法，所有这些方法都基于共同的 DFT+$U$ 基态，以及从密度泛函微扰理论获得的从头算哈伯德 $U$ 值。有两种方法直接提取交换参数：一种通过使用四态映射 ($\Delta E$) 的总能量差，另一种通过使用无穷小自旋旋转的磁力定理 (MFT)。然后通过线性自旋波理论 (LSWT) 从海森堡哈密顿量获得磁振子色散。第三种方法是采用 $U$ (TDDFPT+$U$) 的时间相关密度泛函扰动理论，直接从动态自旋磁化率产生磁振子色散，并通过 LSWT 进行后验拟合交换参数以进行比较。我们的结果表明，TDDFPT+$U$ 和基于 $\Delta E$ 导出参数的海森堡模型与实验中子散射数据吻合良好，而基于 MFT 的方法显示出更大的差异，可能是由于所使用的特定实现的一些固有近似和限制。这项研究对最先进的自旋波建模第一原理技术的准确性进行了基准测试，并有助于推进用于磁性材料研究和设计的可靠计算工具。
