## Abstract
arXiv:2402.11259v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The field of electron optics exploits the analogy between the movement of electrons or charged quasiparticles, primarily in two-dimensional materials subjected to electric and magnetic (EM) fields and the propagation of electromagnetic waves in a dielectric medium with varied refractive index. We significantly extend this analogy by introducing an electronic analogue of Fourier optics dubbed as Fourier electron optics (FEO) with massless Dirac fermions (MDF), namely the charge carriers of single-layer graphene under ambient conditions, by considering their scattering from a two-dimensional quantum dot lattice (TDQDL) treated within Lippmann-Schwinger formalism. By considering the scattering of MDF from TDQDL with a defect region, as well as the moir\'{e} pattern of twisted TDQDLs, we establish an electronic analogue of Babinet's principle in optics. Exploiting the similarity of the resulting differential scattering cross-section with the Fraunhofer diffraction pattern, we construct a dictionary for such FEO. Subsequently, we evaluate the resistivity of such scattered MDF using the Boltzmann approach as a function of the angle made between the direction of propagation of these charge-carriers and the symmetry axis of the dot-lattice, and Fourier analyze them to show that the spatial frequency associated with the angle-resolved resistivity gets filtered according to the structural changes in the dot lattice, indicating wider applicability of FEO of MDF.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2402.11259v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：电子光学领域利用了电子或带电准粒子的运动（主要是在受到电场和磁场 (EM) 场影响的二维材料中）与电磁波在具有不同折射率的电介质中的传播之间的类比。我们通过引入傅里叶光学的电子模拟（称为傅里叶电子光学（FEO））和无质量狄拉克费米子（MDF）（即环境条件下单层石墨烯的电荷载流子）来显着扩展这一类比，并考虑它们在李普曼-施温格形式主义中处理的二维量子点晶格（TDQDL）的散射。通过考虑具有缺陷区域的 TDQDL 中的 MDF 散射以及扭曲 TDQDL 的莫尔图案，我们建立了巴比内光学原理的电子模拟。利用所得的微分散射截面与夫琅和费衍射图案的相似性，我们为这种 FEO 构建了一个字典。随后，我们使用玻尔兹曼方法评估了这种分散的 MDF 的电阻率，作为这些电荷载流子的传播方向与点阵对称轴之间的角度的函数，并对它们进行傅里叶分析，表明与角度分辨电阻率相关的空间频率根据点阵的结构变化而被过滤，这表明 MDF 的 FEO 具有更广泛的适用性。
