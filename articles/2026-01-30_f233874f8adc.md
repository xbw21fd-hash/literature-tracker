## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21808v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This work inspects the thermally activated transfer of solute particles across the interface between two interstitial solid solution phases that equilibrate internally by fast diffusion on conserved arrays of sites. When each phase is considered as an ergodic ensemble of particles, statistical mechanics predicts the occupancy of the transition states at equilibrium to depend on the barrier energy and on the chemical potentials and vacancy fractions in each of the phases. A rate law for the non-equilibrium interfacial transfer, based on a constant transition probability between activated states, naturally satisfies the principle of detailed balance. Contrary to Butler-Volmer-type laws, values of the particle chemical potentials enter explicitly rather than through their difference. This, along with the dependency on the vacancy fractions, implies here an exchange flux density that depends explicitly on the compositions at equilibrium. The results can explain experimental observations of a drastic slow-down in the charging of metal hydrides near phase transformations or miscibility-gap critical points.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21808v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项工作检查了溶质颗粒在两个间隙固溶体相之间的界面上的热激活转移，这两个间隙固溶体相通过在保守位点阵列上的快速扩散实现内部平衡。当每个相被视为粒子的遍历系综时，统计力学预测平衡时过渡态的占据情况取决于势垒能以及每个相中的化学势和空位分数。基于激活态之间恒定的转移概率的非平衡界面转移速率定律自然满足详细平衡原理。与巴特勒-沃尔默型定律相反，粒子化学势的值是明确输入的，而不是通过它们的差异。这连同对空位分数的依赖性一起意味着这里交换通量密度明确地取决于平衡时的组合物。这些结果可以解释实验观察到的金属氢化物在相变或混溶间隙临界点附近充电急剧减慢的现象。
