## Abstract
Nature Machine Intelligence, Published online: 17 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s42256-025-01156-x Grosjean et al. present a network-aware, self-supervised learning approach for screening neuronal activity dynamics. They demonstrate its applicability across a range of neural interventions.
