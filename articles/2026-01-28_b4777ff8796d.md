## Abstract
arXiv:2509.24196v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Traditional metasurface design is limited by the computational cost of full-wave simulations, preventing thorough exploration of complex configurations. Data-driven approaches have emerged as a solution to this bottleneck, replacing costly simulations with rapid neural network evaluations and enabling near-instant design for meta-atoms. Despite advances, implementing a new optical function still requires building and training a task-specific network, along with exhaustive searches for suitable architectures and hyperparameters. Pre-trained large language models (LLMs), by contrast, sidestep this laborious process with a simple fine-tuning technique. However, applying LLMs to the design of nanophotonic devices, particularly for arbitrarily shaped metasurfaces, is still in its early stages; as such tasks often require graphical networks. Here, we show that an LLM, fed with descriptive inputs of arbitrarily shaped metasurface geometries, can learn the physical relationships needed for spectral prediction and inverse design. We further benchmarked a range of open-weight LLMs and identified relationships between accuracy and model size at the billion-parameter level. We demonstrated that 1-D token-wise LLMs provide a practical tool to designing 2-D arbitrarily shaped metasurfaces. Linking natural-language interaction to electromagnetic modelling, this "chat-to-chip" workflow represents a step toward more user-friendly data-driven nanophotonics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.24196v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：传统的超表面设计受到全波模拟计算成本的限制，无法彻底探索复杂的配置。数据驱动的方法已经成为这一瓶颈的解决方案，用快速神经网络评估取代昂贵的模拟，并实现元原子的近乎即时设计。尽管取得了进步，实现新的光学功能仍然需要构建和训练特定于任务的网络，以及对合适的架构和超参数进行详尽的搜索。相比之下，预训练的大型语言模型 (LLM) 通过简单的微调技术避开了这一费力的过程。然而，将法学硕士应用于纳米光子器件的设计，特别是任意形状的超表面，仍处于早期阶段。因为此类任务通常需要图形网络。在这里，我们表明，法学硕士在输入任意形状的超表面几何形状的描述性输入后，可以学习光谱预测和逆向设计所需的物理关系。我们进一步对一系列开放权重法学硕士进行了基准测试，并确定了十亿参数级别的准确性和模型大小之间的关系。我们证明了一维 token-wise LLM 为设计二维任意形状的超表面提供了实用的工具。这种“聊天到芯片”的工作流程将自然语言交互与电磁建模联系起来，代表着朝着更加用户友好的数据驱动纳米光子学迈出了一步。
