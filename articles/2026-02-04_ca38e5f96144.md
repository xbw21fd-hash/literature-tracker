## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03199v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: High-harmonic generation (HHG) from liquids offers a potential pathway to attosecond spectroscopy in chemically complex and disordered environments, yet fundamental questions remain open: whether liquid harmonic emission preserves well-defined attosecond synchronization, and whether harmonic emission can involve simultaneous contributions from multiple quantum trajectories with distinct excursion times despite strong disorder and scattering. Here, we address these issues experimentally by resolving the trajectory-dependent temporal structure of liquid HHG. By optimizing the laser focusing geometry, we achieve clear spatial discrimination of short- and long-trajectory contributions, providing direct evidence for the existence of multiple quantum trajectories in liquids. Using a phase-controlled two-color driving field, we independently retrieve the attochirp associated with each trajectory and demonstrate opposite energy-time correlations for short and long trajectories, establishing a trajectory-resolved energy-time mapping in liquid HHG. All observations are well reproduced by semiclassical recollision simulations. These results place liquid HHG on the same conceptual footing as gas- and solid-phase HHG and establish a robust foundation for attosecond-resolved spectroscopy of ultrafast electronic and chemical dynamics in liquid environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03199v1 公告类型：新 摘要：液体的高次谐波产生（HHG）为化学复杂和无序环境中的阿秒光谱提供了潜在的途径，但基本问题仍然悬而未决：液体谐波发射是否保持明确的阿秒同步，以及谐波发射是否可以涉及来自多个量子轨迹的同时贡献，尽管存在强烈的无序和散射，但具有不同的偏移时间。在这里，我们通过解决液体 HHG 的轨迹依赖时间结构来通过实验解决这些问题。通过优化激光聚焦几何结构，我们实现了短轨迹和长轨迹贡献的清晰空间区分，为液体中存在多个量子轨迹提供了直接证据。使用相控双色驱动场，我们独立检索与每个轨迹相关的 attochirp，并证明短轨迹和长轨迹相反的能量-时间相关性，在液体 HHG 中建立轨迹解析的能量-时间映射。所有观察结果都可以通过半经典重碰撞模拟很好地再现。这些结果将液态 HHG 置于与气相和固相 HHG 相同的概念基础上，并为液体环境中超快电子和化学动力学的阿秒分辨光谱奠定了坚实的基础。
