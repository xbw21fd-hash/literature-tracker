## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14398v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Deconfined quantum critical point (DQCP) describes direct, non-fine-tuned quantum phase transition between two ordered phases that break distinct and seemingly unrelated symmetries, providing a route to continuous phase transition beyond the conventional Ginzburg--Landau paradigm. In this work we extend the DQCP paradigm to systems with internal supersymmetry (SUSY), where the on-site Hilbert space furnishes a representation of a Lie superalgebra, and the Hamiltonian is invariant under the corresponding Lie supergroup. Focusing on the minimal supersymmetric generalization of spin $SU(2)$, namely $OSp(1|2)$, we propose a supersymmetric deconfined quantum critical point (sDQCP) between a phase that breaks internal $OSp(1|2)$ and a phase that instead breaks lattice rotation symmetry. We formulate a non-linear sigma model on the supersphere target space that captures the symmetry intertwinement characteristic of the sDQCP, and we further develop a gauge theory description to address its dynamical properties, including a heuristic argument for 3D XY critical behavior. Finally, we show that explicitly breaking $OSp(1|2)$ down to $SU(2)$ continuously connects our sDQCP to the conventional DQCP scenario.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14398v1 公告类型：新 摘要：去约束量子临界点 (DQCP) 描述了两个有序相之间直接的、非微调的量子相变，打破了不同且看似不相关的对称性，为超越传统金兹堡-朗道范式的连续相变提供了一条途径。在这项工作中，我们将 DQCP 范式扩展到具有内部超对称性 (SUSY) 的系统，其中现场希尔伯特空间提供了李超代数的表示，并且哈密顿量在相应的李超群下是不变的。着眼于自旋 $SU(2)$ 的最小超对称推广，即 $OSp(1|2)$，我们提出了一个超对称解禁量子临界点 (sDQCP)，它位于打破内部 $OSp(1|2)$ 的相和打破晶格旋转对称性的相之间。我们在超球体目标空间上制定了一个非线性 sigma 模型，该模型捕获了 sDQCP 的对称交织特征，并且我们进一步开发了规范理论描述来解决其动态特性，包括 3D XY 临界行为的启发式论证。最后，我们表明，显式地将 $OSp(1|2)$ 分解为 $SU(2)$ 可以持续将我们的 sDQCP 与传统的 DQCP 场景连接起来。
