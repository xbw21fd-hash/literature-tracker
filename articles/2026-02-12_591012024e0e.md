## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10190v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$ is a leading material for proximate Kitaev magnetism. We address the origin of the broad, $\Gamma$-point centered excitation continuum observed by inelastic neutron scattering at elevated temperatures in this compound. Using stochastic Landau-Lifshitz dynamics augmented with quantum-equivalent corrections, we reproduce the temperature-dependent dynamical spin structure factor across both the correlated and conventional paramagnetic regimes. A meta-analysis of 38 published exchange parameter sets identifies those most consistent with the full temperature evolution. A Bayesian optimization procedure is used to derive parameters that capture the low-energy star-like momentum dependence and the overall bandwidth of the continuum. Rescaling temperatures by the Curie--Weiss scale produces a collapse of spectral measures, demonstrating that the high-$T$ dynamics are governed by correlated paramagnetism below $\theta_{\mathrm{CW}}$ rather than by the Kitaev crossover to fractionalized excitations. Complementary 24-site exact diagonalization clarifies finite-size systematics at low temperature and the proximity to zigzag/incommensurate ordering. Beyond $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$, our simulation pipeline provides a reproducible, data-driven framework to infer effective spin models in magnets that exhibit broad continua.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10190v1 公告类型：新 摘要：$\alpha$-RuCl$_3$ 是一种近似基塔耶夫磁性的领先材料。我们解决了在这种化合物中高温下通过非弹性中子散射观察到的宽的、以 $\Gamma$ 点为中心的激发连续谱的起源。使用随机 Landau-Lifshitz 动力学和量子等效校正增强，我们在相关和传统顺磁体系中重现了与温度相关的动态自旋结构因子。对 38 个已发表的交换参数集进行荟萃分析，确定了与完整温度演变最一致的参数集。贝叶斯优化过程用于导出捕获低能量星状动量依赖性和连续体整体带宽的参数。通过居里-韦斯尺度重新调整温度会导致光谱测量的崩溃，这表明高 $T$ 动力学是由低于 $\theta_{\mathrm{CW}}$ 的相关顺磁性决定的，而不是由基塔耶夫交叉到分数化激励决定的。互补的 24 位点精确对角化阐明了低温下的有限尺寸系统学以及接近之字形/不相称的排序。除了 $\alpha$-RuCl$_3$ 之外，我们的模拟管道还提供了一个可重复的、数据驱动的框架，用于推断表现出广泛连续体的磁体中的有效自旋模型。
