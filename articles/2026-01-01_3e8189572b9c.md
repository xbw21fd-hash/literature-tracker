## Abstract
arXiv:2511.21727v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Metal-free porphyrins can switch between hydrogen-bonded tautomers, potentially enabling reversible control in molecular electronics. However, electric field gating of porphyrin tautomerism, which is critical for device integration, has not been fully realized. We propose metal-free benziporphyrins (MFBPs), in which one pyrrole ring is replaced with a phenol group, as a new platform for tautomer-based molecular electronics. This approach introduces asymmetry, which allows for three distinct tautomers, each possessing a characteristic aromatic or antiaromatic electronic structure. Density functional theory and quantum transport calculations show that: i) experimentally realisable electric fields can selectively stabilize each tautomer, and ii) each tautomer exhibits a characteristic conductance profile. The strong switching capability of MFBPs is demonstrated by ON/OFF ratios exceeding 500 at low bias. Fused MFBPs further expand functionality by providing multiple tautomeric states for multistate molecular registers and enabling wire-like architectures with enhanced conductance. These results establish MFBPs as versatile building blocks for electric-field-responsive molecular devices and open new research opportunities for molecular-scale logic and memory.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.21727v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：无金属卟啉可以在氢键互变异构体之间切换，从而有可能实现分子电子学中的可逆控制。然而，对于器件集成至关重要的卟啉互变异构的电场门控尚未完全实现。我们提出无金属苯并卟啉（MFBP），其中一个吡咯环被苯酚基团取代，作为基于互变异构体的分子电子学的新平台。这种方法引入了不对称性，从而产生了三种不同的互变异构体，每种互变异构体都具有特征性的芳香族或反芳香族电子结构。密度泛函理论和量子输运计算表明：i）实验上可实现的电场可以选择性地稳定每个互变异构体，并且 ii）每个互变异构体表现出特征电导分布。 MFBP 的强大开关能力通过低偏置下超过 500 的开/关比得到证明。融合的 MFBP 通过为多态分子寄存器提供多种互变异构状态并实现具有增强电导的线状架构来进一步扩展功能。这些结果使 MFBP 成为电场响应分子器件的通用构建模块，并为分子级逻辑和存储器开辟了新的研究机会。
