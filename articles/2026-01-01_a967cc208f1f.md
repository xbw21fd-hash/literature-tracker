## Abstract
arXiv:2505.09846v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The semi-empirical pseudopotential method (SEPM) has been widely applied to provide computational insights into the electronic structure, photophysics, and charge carrier dynamics of nanoscale materials. We present "DeepPseudopot", a machine-learned atomistic pseudopotential model that extends the SEPM framework by combining a flexible neural network representation of the local pseudopotential with parameterized non-local and spin-orbit coupling terms. Trained on bulk quasiparticle band structures and deformation potentials from GW calculations, the model captures many-body and relativistic effects with very high accuracy across diverse semiconducting materials, as illustrated for silicon and group III-V semiconductors. DeepPseudopot's accuracy, efficiency, and transferability make it well-suited for data-driven in silico design and discovery of novel optoelectronic nanomaterials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.09846v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：半经验赝势方法 (SEPM) 已被广泛应用于为纳米级材料的电子结构、光物理和载流子动力学提供计算见解。我们提出了“DeepPseudopot”，这是一种机器学习的原子赝势模型，它通过将局部赝势的灵活神经网络表示与参数化的非局部和自旋轨道耦合项相结合来扩展 SEPM 框架。该模型根据巨量准粒子能带结构和引力波计算的变形势进行训练，能够以非常高的精度捕获各种半导体材料的多体效应和相对论效应，如硅和 III-V 族半导体所示。 DeepPseudopot 的准确性、效率和可转移性使其非常适合数据驱动的计算机设计和新型光电纳米材料的发现。
