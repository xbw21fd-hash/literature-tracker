## Abstract
arXiv:2511.16995v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: This study proposes a rotationally invariant data-driven subgrid-scale (SGS) model for large-eddy simulation (LES) of wall-bounded turbulent flows. Building upon the multiscale convolutional neural network subgrid-scale model, which outputs SGS stress tensors ($\tau_{ij}$) as the baseline, the deep neural network (DNN) architecture is modified to satisfy the principle of material objectivity by removing the bias terms and batch normalization layers while incorporating a spatial transformer network (STN) algorithm. The model was trained on a turbulent channel flow at $\mathrm{Re}_\tau = 180$ and evaluated using both non-rotated and rotated inputs. The results show that the model performs well in predicting $\tau_{ij}$ and key turbulence statistics, including dissipation, backscatter, and SGS transport. These quantities reflect the ability of the model to reproduce the energy transfer between the resolved scale and SGS. Moreover, it effectively generalizes to unseen rotated inputs, accurately predicting $\tau_{ij}$ despite the input configurations not being encountered during the training. These findings highlight that modifying the DNN architecture and integrating the STN-based algorithm improves the ability to recognize and correctly respond to rotated inputs. The proposed data-driven SGS model addresses the key limitations of common data-driven SGS approaches, particularly their sensitivity to rotated input conditions. It also marks an important advancement in data-driven SGS modeling for LES, particularly in flow configurations where rotational effects are non-negligible.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.16995v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：本研究提出了一种旋转不变数据驱动的次网格尺度 (SGS) 模型，用于壁界湍流的大涡模拟 (LES)。以输出 SGS 应力张量 ($\tau_{ij}$) 作为基线的多尺度卷积神经网络亚网格模型为基础，深度神经网络 (DNN) 架构经过修改，通过删除偏差项和批量归一化层并结合空间变换网络 (STN) 算法来满足物质客观性原则。该模型在 $\mathrm{Re}_\tau = 180$ 的湍流通道流上进行训练，并使用非旋转和旋转输入进行评估。结果表明，该模型在预测 $\tau_{ij}$ 和关键湍流统计数据（包括耗散、反向散射和 SGS 传输）方面表现良好。这些量反映了模型重现解析尺度和 SGS 之间能量传递的能力。此外，它可以有效地推广到看不见的旋转输入，尽管在训练期间没有遇到输入配置，但仍可以准确预测 $\tau_{ij}$。这些发现强调，修改 DNN 架构并集成基于 STN 的算法可以提高识别和正确响应旋转输入的能力。所提出的数据驱动 SGS 模型解决了常见数据驱动 SGS 方法的主要局限性，特别是它们对旋转输入条件的敏感性。它还标志着 LES 数据驱动 SGS 建模的重要进步，特别是在旋转效应不可忽略的流动配置中。
