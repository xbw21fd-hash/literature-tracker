## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10150v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Accurate simulation of turbulent flows is fundamental to scientific and engineering applications. Direct numerical simulation (DNS) offers the highest fidelity but is computationally prohibitive, while existing data-driven alternatives struggle with stable long-horizon rollouts, physical consistency, and faithful simulation of small-scale structures. These challenges are particularly acute in three-dimensional (3D) settings, where the cubic growth of spatial degrees of freedom dramatically amplifies computational cost, memory demand, and the difficulty of capturing multi-scale interactions. To address these challenges, we propose a Physics-Enhanced Swin Transformer (PEST) for 3D turbulence simulation. PEST leverages a window-based self-attention mechanism to effectively model localized PDE interactions while maintaining computational efficiency. We introduce a frequency-domain adaptive loss that explicitly emphasizes small-scale structures, enabling more faithful simulation of high-frequency dynamics. To improve physical consistency, we incorporate Navier--Stokes residual constraints and divergence-free regularization directly into the learning objective. Extensive experiments on two representative turbulent flow configurations demonstrate that PEST achieves accurate, physically consistent, and stable autoregressive long-term simulations, outperforming existing data-driven baselines.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10150v1 发布类型：新 摘要：湍流的精确模拟是科学和工程应用的基础。直接数值模拟 (DNS) 提供最高的保真度，但计算量却令人望而却步，而现有的数据驱动替代方案则难以稳定的长期部署、物理一致性和小规模结构的忠实模拟。这些挑战在三维 (3D) 设置中尤其严重，其中空间自由度的立方增长极大地增加了计算成本、内存需求以及捕获多尺度交互的难度。为了应对这些挑战，我们提出了一种用于 3D 湍流模拟的物理增强型 Swin Transformer (PEST)。 PEST 利用基于窗口的自注意力机制来有效地模拟局部偏微分方程相互作用，同时保持计算效率。我们引入了频域自适应损失，明确强调小规模结构，从而能够更忠实地模拟高频动态。为了提高物理一致性，我们将纳维-斯托克斯残差约束和无散正则化直接纳入学习目标中。对两种代表性湍流配置的大量实验表明，PEST 实现了准确、物理一致且稳定的自回归长期模拟，优于现有的数据驱动基线。
