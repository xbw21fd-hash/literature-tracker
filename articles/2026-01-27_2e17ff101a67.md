## Abstract
arXiv:2511.00857v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We engineer lumped-element superconducting resonators that maximize magnetic coupling to molecular spin qubits, achieving record single-spin couplings up to $100$ kHz and collective couplings exceeding $10$ MHz. The resonators interact with PTMr organic free radicals, model spin systems with $S=1/2$ and a quasi-isotropic $g \simeq 2$, dispersed in polymer matrices. The highest collective spin-photon coupling strengths are attained with resonators having large inductors, which therefore interact with most spins in the molecular ensemble. By contrast, the coupling of each individual spin $G_{1}$ is maximized in resonators having a minimum size inductor, made of a single wire. The same platform has been used to study spin relaxation and spin coherent dynamics in the dispersive regime, when spins are energetically detuned from the resonator. We find evidences for the Purcell effect, i.e. the photon induced relaxation of those spins that are most strongly coupled to the circuit. The rate of this process gives access to the distribution of single spin photon couplings in a given device. For resonators with a $50$ nm wide constriction at the inductor center, single maximum $G_{1}$ values reach $\sim 100$ kHz. Pumping the spins with strong pulses fed through an independent transmission line induces coherent Rabi oscillations. The spin excitation then proceeds via either direct resonant processes induced by the main pulse frequency or, in the case of square-shaped pulses, via the excitation of the cavity by sideband frequency components. The latter process measures the cavity mode hybridization with the spins and can be eliminated by using Gaussian shaped pulses. These results establish a scalable route toward integrated molecular-spin quantum processors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.00857v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：我们设计了集总元件超导谐振器，可最大限度地提高与分子自旋量子位的磁耦合，实现创纪录的高达 100 美元 kHz 的单自旋耦合和超过 10 美元 MHz 的集体耦合。谐振器与 PTMr 有机自由基相互作用，模型自旋系统具有 $S=1/2$ 和准各向同性 $g \simeq 2$，分散在聚合物基质中。使用具有大电感器的谐振器可以获得最高的集体自旋光子耦合强度，因此大电感器与分子系综中的大多数自旋相互作用。相比之下，每个单独自旋 $G_{1}$ 的耦合在具有由单线制成的最小尺寸电感器的谐振器中最大化。当自旋从谐振器中能量失谐时，相同的平台已用于研究色散状态下的自旋弛豫和自旋相干动力学。我们找到了珀塞尔效应的证据，即光子引起与电路耦合最强的自旋的弛豫。该过程的速率可以了解给定器件中单自旋光子耦合的分布。对于电感器中心具有 $50$ nm 宽收缩的谐振器，单个最大 $G_{1}$ 值达到 $\sim 100$ kHz。通过独立传输线馈送的强脉冲泵送自旋会引起相干拉比振荡。然后，自旋激发通过主脉冲频率引起的直接共振过程进行，或者在方形脉冲的情况下，通过边带频率分量对腔体的激发进行。后一个过程测量自旋的腔模式杂化，并且可以通过使用高斯整形脉冲来消除。这些结果为集成分子自旋量子处理器建立了一条可扩展的途径。
