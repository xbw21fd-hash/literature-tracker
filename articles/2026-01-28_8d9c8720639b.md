## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19777v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Non-Hermitian systems display spectral and topological phenomena absent in Hermitian physics; yet, their geometric characterization can be hindered by an intrinsic ambiguity rooted in the eigenspace of non-Hermitian Hamiltonians, which becomes especially pronounced in the pure quantum regime. Because left and right eigenvectors are not related by conjugation, their norms are not fixed, giving rise to a biorthogonal ${\rm GL}(N,{\mathbb C})$ gauge freedom. Consequently, the standard Berry connection admits four inequivalent definitions depending on how left and right eigenvectors are paired, giving rise to distinct Berry phases and generally complex-valued holonomies. Here we show that these ambiguities and the emergence of complex phases are fully resolved by introducing a covariant-derivative formalism built from the metric tensor of the Hilbert space of the underlying non-Hermitian Hamiltonian. The resulting unique Berry connection remains real-valued under an arbitrary ${\rm GL}(N,{\mathbb C})$ frame change, and transforms as an affine gauge potential, while reducing to the conventional Berry (or Wilczek-Zee) connection in the Hermitian limit. This establishes an unambiguous and gauge-consistent geometric framework for Berry phases, non-Abelian holonomies, and topological invariants in quantum systems described by non-Hermitian Hamiltonians.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19777v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：非厄米系统显示厄米物理学中不存在的光谱和拓扑现象；然而，它们的几何表征可能会受到非厄米哈密顿量本征空间中固有的模糊性的阻碍，这种模糊性在纯量子态中变得尤其明显。由于左右特征向量不通过共轭相关，因此它们的范数不固定，从而产生双正交 ${\rm GL}(N,{\mathbb C})$ 规范自由度。因此，标准 Berry 连接根据左右特征向量的配对方式接受四个不等价的定义，从而产生不同的 Berry 相和通常为复值的完整函数。在这里，我们表明，通过引入从底层非厄米哈密顿量的希尔伯特空间的度量张量构建的协变导数形式主义，可以完全解决这些模糊性和复杂阶段的出现。由此产生的唯一 Berry 连接在任意 ${\rm GL}(N,{\mathbb C})$ 框架变化下保持实值，并转换为仿射规范势，同时在 Hermitian 极限下减少到传统的 Berry（或 Wilczek-Zee）连接。这为非厄米哈密顿量描述的量子系统中的贝里相、非阿贝尔完整律和拓扑不变量建立了一个明确且规范一致的几何框架。
