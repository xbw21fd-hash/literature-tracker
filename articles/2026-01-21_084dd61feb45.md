## Abstract
arXiv:2505.04100v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We provide an analytical framework for analyzing the quality of stochastic Verlet-type integrators for simulating the Langevin equation. Focusing only on basic objective measures, we consider the ability of an integrator to correctly simulate two characteristic configurational quantities of transport, a) diffusion on a flat surface and b) drift on a tilted planar surface, as well as c) statistical sampling of a harmonic potential. For any stochastic Verlet-type integrator expressed in its configurational form, we develop closed form expressions to directly assess these three most basic quantities as a function of the applied time step. The applicability of the analysis is exemplified through twelve representative integrators developed over the past five decades, and algorithm performance is conveniently visualized through the three characteristic measures for each integrator. The GJ set of integrators stands out as the only option for correctly simulating diffusion, drift, and Boltzmann distribution in linear systems, and we therefore suggest that this general method is the one best suited for high quality thermodynamic simulations of nonlinear and complex systems, including for relatively high time steps compared to simulations with other integrators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.04100v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：我们提供了一个分析框架，用于分析用于模拟 Langevin 方程的随机 Verlet 型积分器的质量。仅关注基本的客观测量，我们考虑积分器正确模拟输运的两个特征配置量的能力，a）平坦表面上的扩散和b）倾斜平面上的漂移，以及c）谐波势的统计采样。对于以其配置形式表示的任何随机 Verlet 型积分器，我们开发了封闭形式表达式来直接评估这三个最基本的量作为所应用时间步长的函数。该分析的适用性通过过去 50 年开发的 12 个代表性积分器进行了例证，并且通过每个积分器的三个特征测量可以方便地可视化算法性能。 GJ 积分器集是正确模拟线性系统中扩散、漂移和玻尔兹曼分布的唯一选择，因此我们建议这种通用方法最适合非线性和复杂系统的高质量热力学模拟，包括与其他积分器的模拟相比相对较高的时间步长。
