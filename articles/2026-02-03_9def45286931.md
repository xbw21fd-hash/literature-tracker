## Abstract
arXiv:2509.24914v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Trained attention layers exhibit striking and reproducible spectral structure of the weights, including low-rank collapse, bulk deformation, and isolated spectral outliers, yet the origin of these phenomena and their implications for generalization remain poorly understood. We study empirical risk minimization in a single-head tied-attention layer trained on synthetic high-dimensional sequence tasks generated from the attention-indexed model. Using tools from random matrix theory, spin-glass theory, and approximate message passing, we obtain an exact high-dimensional characterization of training and test error, interpolation and recovery thresholds, and the spectrum of the key and query matrices. Our theory predicts the full singular-value distribution of the trained query-key map, including low-rank structure and isolated spectral outliers, in qualitative agreement with observations in more realistic transformers. Finally, for targets with power-law spectra, we show that learning proceeds through sequential spectral recovery, leading to the emergence of power-law scaling laws.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.24914v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：经过训练的注意力层表现出惊人且可重现的权重谱结构，包括低阶崩溃、体积变形和孤立的谱异常值，但这些现象的起源及其对泛化的影响仍然知之甚少。我们研究了单头绑定注意力层中的经验风险最小化，该层是根据注意力索引模型生成的合成高维序列任务进行训练的。使用随机矩阵理论、自旋玻璃理论和近似消息传递的工具，我们获得了训练和测试误差、插值和恢复阈值以及密钥和查询矩阵的频谱的精确高维表征。我们的理论预测了训练后的查询键映射的完整奇异值分布，包括低秩结构和孤立的光谱异常值，与更现实的变压器中的观察结果定性一致。最后，对于具有幂律谱的目标，我们表明学习是通过顺序谱恢复进行的，从而导致幂律缩放定律的出现。
