## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14382v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We show that the Kasteleyn transition, the abrupt proliferation of infinite strings of defects in classical dimer and related models, can also be relevant for frustrated 2d quantum magnets. This is explicitly demonstrated in a phase of the spin-1/2 Heisenberg diamond-decorated honeycomb lattice where a family of exact eigenstates built as products of dimer and plaquette singlets can be mapped onto the dimer coverings of the honeycomb lattice. The low-temperature properties of this phase are accurately described by an effective dimer model with anisotropic activities and a small, tunable density of monomers, leading to an arbitrarily sharp crossover version of the Kasteleyn transition. The generalization to other geometries and the possibility to realize this model in organo-metallic compounds are briefly discussed.

arXiv:2601.14382v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们表明，Kasteleyn 转变（经典二聚体和相关模型中无限串缺陷的突然扩散）也可能与受挫二维量子磁体相关。这在自旋 1/2 海森堡金刚石装饰的蜂窝晶格的一个相中得到了明确的证明，其中作为二聚体和斑块单线态产物构建的一系列精确本征态可以映射到蜂窝晶格的二聚体覆盖物上。该相的低温特性可以通过有效的二聚体模型准确地描述，该二聚体模型具有各向异性活性和小且可调的单体密度，从而产生任意尖锐的 Kasteleyn 转变交叉版本。简要讨论了对其他几何形状的推广以及在有机金属化合物中实现该模型的可能性。
