## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04345v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The stated overarching goal of the highly active field of quantum simulation of high-energy physics (HEP) is to achieve the capability to study \textit{ab-initio} real-time microscopic dynamics of $3+1$D quantum chromodynamics (QCD). However, existing experimental realizations and theoretical proposals for future ones have remained restricted to one or two spatial dimensions. Here, we take a big step towards this goal by proposing a concrete experimentally feasible scalable cold-atom quantum simulator of a U$(1)$ quantum link model of quantum electrodynamics (QED) in three spatial dimensions, employing \textit{linear gauge protection} to stabilize gauge invariance. Using tree tensor network simulations, we benchmark the performance of this quantum simulator through near- and far-from-equilibrium observables, showing excellent agreement with the ideal gauge theory. Additionally, we introduce a method for \textit{analog quantum error mitigation} that accounts for unwanted first-order tunneling processes, vastly improving agreement between quantum-simulator and ideal-gauge-theory results. Our findings pave the way towards realistic quantum simulators of $3+1$D lattice gauge theories that can probe regimes well beyond classical simulability.

arXiv:2601.04345v1 公告类型：新 摘要：高能物理（HEP）量子模拟这一高度活跃领域的总体目标是实现研究 $3+1$D 量子色动力学（QCD）\textit{ab-initio} 实时微观动力学的能力。然而，现有的实验实现和未来的理论建议仍然局限于一两个空间维度。在这里，我们向这一目标迈出了一大步，提出了一种具体的实验上可行的可扩展冷原子量子模拟器，用于三个空间维度的量子电动力学（QED）的 U$(1)$ 量子链接模型，并采用 \textit{线性规范保护} 来稳定规范不变性。使用树张量网络模拟，我们通过近平衡和远平衡可观测量对该量子模拟器的性能进行基准测试，显示出与理想规范理论的良好一致性。此外，我们引入了一种 \textit{模拟量子误差缓解} 方法，该方法可以解决不需要的一阶隧道过程，从而极大地提高量子模拟器与理想规范理论结果之间的一致性。我们的研究结果为 $3+1$D 晶格规范理论的现实量子模拟器铺平了道路，该模拟器可以探测远远超出经典可模拟性的体系。
