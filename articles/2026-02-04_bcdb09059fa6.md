## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03437v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Implementing electrophysiological recordings within an MRI environment is challenging due to complex interactions between recording probes and MRI-generated fields, which can affect both safety and data quality. This study aims to develop and evaluate a hybrid electromagnetic (EM) simulation framework for efficient and accurate assessment of such interactions. Methods: A hybrid EM strategy integrating the Huygens' Box (HB) method with sub-gridding was implemented in an FDTD solver (Sim4Life). RF coil models for mouse and rat head were simulated with and without intracortical (IC) and epicortical (EC) graphene-based micro-transistor arrays. Three-dimensional multi-layered probe models were reconstructed from two-dimensional layouts, and transmit field ($B_{1}^{+}$), electric field ($E$), and specific absorption rate (SAR) distributions were evaluated. Performance was benchmarked against conventional full-wave multi-port (MP) simulations using Bland-Altman analysis and voxel-wise percentage differences. Results: HB simulations reduced computational time by approximately 70-80%, while preserving spatial patterns of $|B_{1}^{+}|$, $|E|$, and SAR, including transmit-field symmetry and localized high-field regions. Deviations from MP were minimal for $|B_{1}^{+}|$ (median $\Delta$% 0.02-0.07% in mice, -3.7% to -1.7% in rats) and modest for $|E|$ and SAR, with absolute SAR values remaining well below human safety limits. Graphene-based arrays produced negligible effects on RF transmission and SAR deposition. Conclusion: The HB approach enables computationally efficient, high-resolution evaluation of EM interactions involving microscopic probes in MRI environments, supporting simulations that are otherwise impractical with full-wave MP modeling.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03437v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于记录探针和 MRI 生成场之间复杂的相互作用，在 MRI 环境中实现电生理记录具有挑战性，这可能会影响安全性和数据质量。本研究旨在开发和评估混合电磁（EM）仿真框架，以高效、准确地评估此类相互作用。方法：在 FDTD 求解器 (Sim4Life) 中实施将惠更斯盒 (HB) 方法与子网格相结合的混合 EM 策略。小鼠和大鼠头部的射频线圈模型在有或没有皮质内 (IC) 和皮质外 (EC) 石墨烯微晶体管阵列的情况下进行了模拟。从二维布局重建三维多层探针模型，并评估发射场（$B_{1}^{+}$）、电场（$E$）和比吸收率（SAR）分布。使用 Bland-Altman 分析和体素百分比差异对传统全波多端口 (MP) 模拟进行性能基准测试。结果：HB 模拟将计算时间减少了约 70-80%，同时保留了 $|B_{1}^{+}|$、$|E|$ 和 SAR 的空间模式，包括发射场对称性和局部高场区域。 $|B_{1}^{+}|$ 与 MP 的偏差最小（小鼠中位 $\Delta$% 为 0.02-0.07%，大鼠为 -3.7% 至 -1.7%），$|E|$ 和 SAR 的偏差也较小，绝对 SAR 值仍远低于人类安全限值。基于石墨烯的阵列对射频传输和 SAR 沉积产生的影响可以忽略不计。结论：HB 方法能够对 MRI 环境中涉及微观探针的电磁相互作用进行计算高效、高分辨率的评估，支持全波 MP 建模无法进行的模拟。
