## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01841v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The band complex formed by multiple topological states has attracted extensive attention for the emergent properties produced by the interplay among the constituent states. Here, based on group theory analysis, we present a scheme for rapidly identifying the Weyl-Dirac nodal lines (a complex of Weyl and Dirac nodal lines) in bosonic systems. We find only 5 of the 230 space groups host Weyl-Dirac nodal line phonons. Notably, the Dirac nodal line resides along the high-symmetry line, whereas the Weyl nodal line is distributed on the high-symmetry plane and is interconnected with the Dirac nodal line, jointly forming a composite nodal network structure. Unlike traditional nodal nets, this nodal network exhibits markedly distinct surface states on different surfaces, which can be attributed to the fundamental differences in the topological properties between the Weyl and Dirac nodal lines. This unique property thus allows the material to present distinct surface states in a termination-selective manner. Furthermore, by first-principles calculations, we identify the materials NdRhO$_{3}$ and ZnSe$_{2}$O$_{5}$ as candidate examples to elaborate the Weyl-Dirac nodal line and their related topological features. Our work provides an insight for exploring and leveraging topological properties in systems with coexisting multiple topological states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01841v1 发布类型：新 摘要：由多个拓扑态形成的能带复合体因其组成态之间相互作用所产生的涌现性质而引起了广泛的关注。在这里，基于群论分析，我们提出了一种快速识别玻色子系统中的外尔-狄拉克节点线（外尔和狄拉克节点线的复合体）的方案。我们发现 230 个空间群中只有 5 个拥有 Weyl-Dirac 节线声子。值得注意的是，狄拉克节点线位于高对称线上，而韦尔节点线分布在高对称面上，并与狄拉克节点线互连，共同形成复合节点网络结构。与传统的节点网络不同，该节点网络在不同的表面上表现出明显不同的表面状态，这可以归因于Weyl节点线和狄拉克节点线之间拓扑性质的根本差异。因此，这种独特的特性允许材料以终止选择性的方式呈现不同的表面状态。此外，通过第一性原理计算，我们确定材料 NdRhO$_{3}$ 和 ZnSe$_{2}$O$_{5}$ 作为候选示例，以详细阐述 Weyl-Dirac 节点线及其相关拓扑特征。我们的工作为探索和利用共存多种拓扑状态的系统中的拓扑特性提供了见解。
