## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12098v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The exploration of quantum phenomena in complex materials such as moir\'e superlattices is limited by the O(N^3) scaling of conventional electronic structure methods. Here we introduce a high-performance tight-binding framework that reduces the complexity to O(N^1.5) by transforming the Hamiltonian into a real symmetric form and combining Sylvester's inertia law with LDL decomposition. This approach enables efficient band structure calculations for large systems: solving magic angle twisted bilayer graphene in minutes on a laptop and scaling to 1.5 million atoms within days on a workstation. We apply it to the previously inaccessible ultra-low twist-angle regime (less than 0.16 degree) with mechanical strain relaxation and find robust flat bands persisting down to 0.09 degree. Our framework bridges density functional theory accuracy with large-scale quantum simulation, opening a route to systematic data-driven exploration of mesoscale quantum materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12098v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：对莫尔超晶格等复杂材料中量子现象的探索受到传统电子结构方法 O(N^3) 缩放的限制。在这里，我们引入了一种高性能的紧束缚框架，通过将哈密顿量转换为实数对称形式并将西尔维斯特惯性定律与 LDL 分解相结合，将复杂度降低到 O(N^1.5)。这种方法可以实现大型系统的高效能带结构计算：在笔记本电脑上几分钟内求解魔角扭曲双层石墨烯，并在工作站上几天内扩展到 150 万个原子。我们将其应用于以前无法实现的具有机械应变松弛的超低扭转角区域（小于 0.16 度），并发现稳定的平带持续低至 0.09 度。我们的框架将密度泛函理论的准确性与大规模量子模拟联系起来，为中尺度量子材料的系统数据驱动探索开辟了道路。
