## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19829v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We discuss a possibility of superconductivity in the p-wave magnets. These are recently discovered materials that have zero net magnetization by symmetry and finite non-relativistic spin splitting of electron bands, like in altermagnets. Similarly, the spin polarizations is collinear in the momentum space. Yet, as opposed to altermagnets, the magnetization is noncollinear in the real space, and the spin splitting obeys time-reversal symmetry in the momentum space. As a result, if such material harbors superconductivity (due to phonons, or any other mechanism), the only supported superconducting symmetry is Ising superconductivity, an exotic symmetry where any Cooper pair is a 50:50 mix of singlet and triplet. This unusual behavior is also in stark contrast to regular antiferromagnet, which can support Cooper pairs of any parity, and altermagnets, which can only support nonunitary triplet pairs. The presence of large triplet component and enhanced resilience against pair breaking is inherent to the p-wave magnets and as such is unconventional as it does not materialize in conventional spin-orbit coupling induced Ising superconductors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19829v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们讨论 p 波磁体中超导的可能性。这些是最近发现的材料，它们通过对称性和电子带的有限非相对论自旋分裂而具有零净磁化强度，就像交流磁体一样。类似地，自旋极化在动量空间中是共线的。然而，与交流磁体相反，磁化强度在真实空间中是非线性的，并且自旋分裂在动量空间中遵循时间反转对称性。因此，如果这种材料具有超导性（由于声子或任何其他机制），唯一支持的超导对称性就是伊辛超导性，这是一种奇特的对称性，其中任何库珀对都是单重态和三重态的 50:50 混合。这种不寻常的行为也与常规反铁磁体形成鲜明对比，常规反铁磁体可以支持任何宇称的库珀对，而交替磁体则只能支持非酉三重态对。大三重态分量的存在和增强的抗断对弹性是 p 波磁体所固有的，因此是非常规的，因为它在传统的自旋轨道耦合感应伊辛超导体中没有实现。
