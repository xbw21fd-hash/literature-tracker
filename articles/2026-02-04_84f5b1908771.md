## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03804v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic anisotropy (MA) plays a crucial role in deciding both static and dynamic behaviour of magnetic thin films. It controls various phenomena, such as magnetization reversal, domain formation, domain-wall motion, spin-wave generation, and spin-wave propagation etc. We investigate the mixed anisotropies in face-centred-cubic Permalloy (fcc-Py) and amorphous Cobalt (a-Co) thin films deposited via rf magnetron sputtering on Si (100) substrate with thicknesses, d = 5-125 nm and t = 5-150 nm, respectively. X-ray diffraction technique, atomic force microscopy, and vibrating sample magnetometry are employed to study the structural, morphological, and magnetic properties. We adopt a qualitative approach to understand the nature of different anisotropies present in both materials. Mixed anisotropies evolve with film thicknesses for both fcc-Py and a-Co films. The role of growth conditions in the emergence of specific anisotropies is discussed in detail. An alteration of the magnetization easy axis from the conventional in-plane orientation is evidenced due to the collective influence of these mixed anisotropies. Based on the dominance of anisotropy components, their origin, and the direction of magnetization tilt, we categorize our samples as belonging to specific regimes. Introduction of magnetization tilt has been proven to be an extremely innovative way to improve the performance of spintronic devices so far. The one-to-one comparison between a sputter-deposited crystalline and an amorphous magnetic material could be beneficial for building a stronger foundation for that.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03804v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁各向异性 (MA) 在决定磁性薄膜的静态和动态行为方面起着至关重要的作用。它控制着各种现象，如磁化反转、磁畴形成、磁畴壁运动、自旋波产生和自旋波传播等。我们研究了通过射频磁控管溅射在厚度分别为 d = 5-125 nm 和 t = 5-150 nm 的 Si (100) 衬底上沉积的面心立方坡莫合金 (fcc-Py) 和非晶钴 (a-Co) 薄膜的混合各向异性。 X射线衍射技术、原子力显微镜和振动样品磁力测量用于研究结构、形态和磁性。我们采用定性方法来了解两种材料中存在的不同各向异性的本质。对于 fcc-Py 和 a-Co 薄膜，混合各向异性随着薄膜厚度的变化而变化。详细讨论了生长条件在出现特定各向异性时的作用。由于这些混合各向异性的集体影响，证明了易磁化轴相对于传统面内取向的改变。根据各向异性成分的主导地位、它们的起源和磁化倾斜方向，我们将样品分类为属于特定的区域。迄今为止，磁化倾斜的引入已被证明是提高自旋电子器件性能的一种极具创新性的方法。溅射沉积晶体和非晶磁性材料之间的一对一比较可能有利于为此奠定更牢固的基础。
