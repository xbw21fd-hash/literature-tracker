## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15268v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Proton-conducting solid acids could enable water-free operation of high-temperature fuel cells. However, systematic materials screening has, hitherto, been computationally prohibitive. Here, we introduce a two-stage high-throughput screening strategy that directly computes proton diffusion coefficients, enabled by machine-learned interatomic potentials fine-tuned to ab initio data. Starting from more than six million materials, our screening -- based on structural motifs rather than empirical descriptors -- identifies $27$ high-performing proton conductors, including over ten previously unexplored compounds. These include sustainable and commercially available materials, candidates that have not yet been synthesized, organic systems that fall outside conventional design rules, and known proton conductors that validate our approach. Importantly, our findings reveal a universal oxygen--oxygen distance of approximately $2.5$~{\AA} at the moment of proton transfer across diverse chemistries, providing mechanistic insight and showing that macroscopic proton conductivity emerges from the interplay between anion rotational dynamics, hydrogen-bond network connectivity, and proton-transfer probability.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15268v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：质子传导固体酸可以实现高温燃料电池的无水运行。然而，迄今为止，系统的材料筛选在计算上仍然令人望而却步。在这里，我们引入了一种两阶段高通量筛选策略，该策略直接计算质子扩散系数，通过机器学习的原子间势对从头数据进行微调来实现。从超过 600 万种材料开始，我们的筛选（基于结构主题而不是经验描述符）确定了价值 27 美元的高性能质子导体，其中包括十多种以前未探索过的化合物。其中包括可持续和商业可用的材料、尚未合成的候选材料、不符合传统设计规则的有机系统以及验证我们方法的已知质子导体。重要的是，我们的研究结果揭示了质子在不同化学物质之间转移时，普遍的氧-氧距离约为 $2.5$~{\AA}，提供了机理见解，并表明宏观质子电导率是由阴离子旋转动力学、氢键网络连通性和质子转移概率之间的相互作用产生的。
