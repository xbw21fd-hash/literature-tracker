## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16420v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Hafnia (HfO2) is a silicon-compatible dielectric material, yet stabilizing its desired but metastable ferroelectric phase remains challenging. Phase stability predictions by density functional theory (DFT) have provided crucial guidance, but most simulations neglected or only treated finite temperature effects with (quasi-)harmonic approximation due to high computational cost of DFT. Here, we develop a machine learning force field and perform thermodynamic calculations for HfO2 using self-consistent phonon theory to address growing evidence of anharmonicity. Our results reveal that the ferroelectric orthorhombic phase oIII exhibits metastability below 0.1kBT under most conditions within the simulated regime of temperature and pressure (600 K <= T <= 1500 K and 0 <= p <= 7.5 GPa), contradicting previous harmonic predictions of metastability above 1500 K at ambient pressure. We further report evidence for temperature- and pressure-dependent ferroelectric parent phase despite efforts to identify a universal one. This study highlights the importance of anharmonicity and provides an effective approach for its treatment in the design of HfO2-based ferroelectrics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16420v1 公告类型：新 摘要：二氧化铪 (HfO2) 是一种与硅兼容的介电材料，但稳定其所需但亚稳态的铁电相仍然具有挑战性。密度泛函理论 (DFT) 的相稳定性预测提供了重要的指导，但由于 DFT 的计算成本较高，大多数模拟都忽略或仅使用（准）谐波近似处理有限温度效应。在这里，我们开发了一个机器学习力场，并使用自洽声子理论对 HfO2 进行热力学计算，以解决越来越多的非谐性证据。我们的结果表明，在模拟温度和压力范围内的大多数条件下（600 K <= T <= 1500 K 和 0 <= p <= 7.5 GPa），铁电正交相 oIII 表现出低于 0.1kBT 的亚稳态，这与之前在环境压力下高于 1500 K 的亚稳态谐波预测相矛盾。尽管我们努力寻找一种通用的铁电母相，但我们仍进一步报告了温度和压力依赖性铁电母相的证据。这项研究强调了非谐性的重要性，并为其在 HfO2 基铁电体设计中的处理提供了有效的方法。
