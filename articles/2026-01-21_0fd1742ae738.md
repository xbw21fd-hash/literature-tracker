# Finite-momentum bound pairs of two electrons in an altermagnetic metal

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12905v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We solve the two-electron problem on a square lattice with $d$-wave altermagnetism, considering both on-site and nearest-neighbor attractive interactions. The altermagnetic spin-splitting in the single-particle dispersion naturally gives rise to a ground state of two-electron bound pairs with nonzero center-of-mass momentum. This finite-momentum pairing can be interpreted as a two-body mechanism underlying the recently proposed altermagnetism-induced Fulde--Ferrell--Larkin--Ovchinnikov (FFLO) superconducting state. Additionally, when the nearest-neighbor attraction is strong, the resulting finite-momentum bound pairs exhibit a mixture of both spin-singlet and spin-triplet characteristics, suggesting the possibility of unconventional superconductors, where spin-singlet and spin-triplet pairings coexist.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12905v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们利用 $d$ 波交变磁力解决了方晶格上的双电子问题，同时考虑了现场和最近邻吸引相互作用。单粒子分散体中的交变自旋分裂自然会产生具有非零质心动量的双电子束缚对的基态。这种有限动量配对可以解释为最近提出的交变磁感应富尔德-费雷尔-拉金-奥夫钦尼科夫（FFLO）超导态背后的二体机制。此外，当最近邻吸引力很强时，所得的有限动量束缚对表现出自旋单重态和自旋三重态特征的混合，这表明非常规超导体的可能性，其中自旋单重态和自旋三重态配对共存。
