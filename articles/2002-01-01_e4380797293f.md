## Abstract
TLDR: This work describes a method for iteratively minimizing an electric enthalpy functional expressed in terms of occupied Bloch-like states on a uniform grid of k points and illustrates the procedure by computing the piezoelectric and nonlinear dielectric susceptibility tensors of III-V semiconductors.
