## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12726v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quasi-one-dimensional RbMn$_{6}$Bi$_{5}$, the first pressure-induced ternary Mn-based superconductor, exhibits a phase diagram analogous to those of cuprate and iron-based superconductors, with superconductivity neighboring antiferromagnetic order. Here, we use $^{55}$Mn and $^{87}$Rb nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) to unravel its magnetic structure and fluctuations. Above the N\'eel temperature ($T_{\rm N}$), strong antiferromagnetic fluctuations dominate, characteristic of a paramagnetic state with pronounced spin-lattice relaxation rate enhancement. Below $T_{\rm N}$, a first-order phase transition establishes a commensurate antiferromagnetic order, where Mn atoms at the pentagon corners exhibit distinct magnetic moments with different orientations, while the central Mn atom carries no magnetic moment. The complex magnetic architecture, revealed by zero-field and high-magnetic-field NMR spectra, contrasts with earlier neutron diffraction models proposing uniform spin density waves, instead supporting localized moments ordering with charge rearrangement. The proximity of robust antiferromagnetic fluctuations to the high-pressure superconducting phase suggests a potential role for magnetic excitations in mediating unconventional Cooper pairing, akin to paradigmatic high-$T_c$ systems. These findings provide critical insights into the interplay between geometric frustration, magnetic order, and superconductivity in manganese-based materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12726v1 公告类型：新 摘要：准一维 RbMn$_{6}$Bi$_{5}$ 是第一个压力诱导的三元锰基超导体，表现出与铜酸盐和铁基超导体类似的相图，具有邻近反铁磁序的超导性。在这里，我们使用 $^{55}$Mn 和 $^{87}$Rb 核磁共振（NMR）来揭示其磁性结构和波动。高于 N\'eel 温度 ($T_{\rm N}$) 时，强烈的反铁磁涨落占主导地位，这是顺磁态的特征，具有明显的自旋晶格弛豫率增强。在$T_{\rm N}$以下，一阶相变建立了相应的反铁磁序，其中五边形角处的Mn原子表现出具有不同方向的不同磁矩，而中心Mn原子不携带磁矩。零场和高磁场核磁共振谱揭示的复杂磁性结构与早期提出均匀自旋密度波的中子衍射模型形成鲜明对比，而是支持电荷重排的局域矩排序。强大的反铁磁涨落与高压超导相的接近表明磁激发在调节非常规库珀配对中的潜在作用，类似于典型的高 $T_c$ 系统。这些发现为锰基材料中几何挫败、磁序和超导性之间的相互作用提供了重要的见解。
