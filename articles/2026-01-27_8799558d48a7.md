## Abstract
arXiv:2511.02787v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We propose and theoretically study minimal models of N\'{e}el ordered collinear (compensated) antiferromagnets that show the anomalous Hall effect. For simplicity, we first consider two-dimensional models of antiferromagnets with two magnetic sublattices on a square lattice. We provide explicit examples of a N\'{e}el ordered ferrimagnet and a Dzyaloshinskii weak ferromagnet. Then antiferromagnet on the rutile lattice, that belongs to the class of weak ferromagnets, is studied. We analyze Dzyaloshinskii's invariants for the existence of spontaneous magnetization in these N\'{e}el ordered systems. Microscopic calculations of the Berry curvature for the studied systems confirm the validity of these Dzyaloshinskii's invariants. It is shown that the anomalous Hall effect mechanism in these antiferromagnets arises either from the interplay of momentum-dependent exchange interaction of conducting fermions with the N\'{e}el order and the spin-orbit coupling. These physical processes originate from the broken symmetries that permit the Dzyaloshinskii's invariant in the system.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.02787v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出并从理论上研究了显示反常霍尔效应的 N\'{e}el 有序共线（补偿）反铁磁体的最小模型。为了简单起见，我们首先考虑在方形晶格上具有两个磁性子晶格的反铁磁体的二维模型。我们提供了 N\'{e}el 有序亚铁磁体和 Dzyaloshinskii 弱铁磁体的明确示例。然后研究了金红石晶格上的反铁磁体，属于弱铁磁体类别。我们分析了这些 N\'{e}el 有序系统中是否存在自发磁化的 Dzyaloshinskii 不变量。对所研究系统的贝里曲率的微观计算证实了这些 Dzyaloshinskii 不变量的有效性。结果表明，这些反铁磁体中的反常霍尔效应机制是由具有 N\'{e}el 级的传导费米子的动量依赖交换相互作用和自旋轨道耦合的相互作用引起的。这些物理过程源于对称性破缺，这种对称性使得 Dzyaloshinskii 在系统中保持不变。
