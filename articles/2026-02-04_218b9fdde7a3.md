## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02941v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Recent neutron measurement in the icosahedral quasicrystal approximant Au-SM-Tb (SM=Al, Ga) has revealed unique noncollinear magnetic order ``whirling-anti-whirling states''. Here, we report theoretical analysis on the magnetic-field-direction dependence on the whirling-anti-whirling order in the 1/1 approximant crystal. By performing exact-diagonalization calculation for the effective model taking into account the uniaxial magnetic anisotropy arising from the crystalline electric field, we show the metamagnetic transition takes place simultaneously with the topological transition under the magnetic field along the (111) direction. After the metamagnetic transition, the emergent fictious magnetic field induced by the chirality of noncoplanar magnetic moments appears, the analysis of which concludes that the topological Hall effect is expected to be observed in the electrical conductivity $\sigma_{xy}$ and $\sigma_{yz}$ for the applied field direction from (111) to (001).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.02941v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近对二十面体准晶近似 Au-SM-Tb (SM=Al, Ga) 的中子测量揭示了独特的非共线磁序“旋转-反旋转状态”。在这里，我们报告了 1/1 近似晶体中磁场方向对旋转-反旋转顺序依赖性的理论分析。通过对有效模型进行精确对角化计算，考虑晶体电场引起的单轴磁各向异性，我们发现变磁转变与沿（111）方向的磁场下的拓扑转变同时发生。变磁转变后，出现由非共面磁矩的手性引起的虚构磁场，对其分析得出结论，对于从(111)到(001)的施加场方向，预计在电导率$\sigma_{xy}$和$\sigma_{yz}$中观察到拓扑霍尔效应。
