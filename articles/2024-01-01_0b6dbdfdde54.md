## Abstract
TLDR: This paper introduces a machine learning framework to accelerate EPC calculations by utilizing atomic orbital-based Hamiltonian matrices and gradients predicted by an equivariant graph neural network and demonstrates that this method not only yields EPC values in close agreement with first-principles results but also enhances calculation efficiency by several orders of magnitude.
