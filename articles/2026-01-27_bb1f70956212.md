## Abstract
arXiv:2110.10647v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: The study of magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) significantly boosts the understanding and development of solar physics, planetary dynamics and controlled nuclear fusion. Dynamical properties of the MHD system involve nonlinear interactions of waves with multiple travelling speeds (the fast and slow magnetosonic waves, the Alfv\'{e}n wave and the entropy wave). One intriguing topic is the shock phenomena accompanied by the magnetic field, which have been affirmed by astronomical observations. However, permitting the residence of all above multi-speed waves, mathematically, whether one can prove shock formation for three dimensional (3D) MHD is still open. The multiple-speed nature of the MHD system makes it fascinating and challenging. In this paper, we report our recent progress in answering the above question. For 3D ideal compressible MHD, we construct planar symmetric examples of shock formation allowing the presence of all characteristic waves with multiple wave speeds. This also answers a question raised by Majda on conservation law in 1984. Building on our construction, we further prove that the Cauchy problem for 3D ideal MHD is $H^2$ ill-posed. And this is caused by the shock formation. In particular, when the magnetic field is absent, we also provide a desired low-regularity ill-posedness result for the 3D compressible Euler equations, and it is sharp with respect to the regularity of the fluid velocity. Our proof for 3D MHD is based on a coalition of a carefully designed algebraic approach and a geometric approach. To trace the nonlinear interactions of various waves, we algebraically decompose the 3D ideal MHD equations into a $7\times 7$ non-strictly hyperbolic system. Via detailed calculations, we reveal its hidden subtle structures. With them we give a complete description of MHD dynamics up to the earliest singular event, when a shock forms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2110.10647v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：磁流体动力学（MHD）的研究极大地促进了对太阳物理学、行星动力学和受控核聚变的理解和发展。 MHD 系统的动力学特性涉及多种传播速度的波（快磁声波和慢磁声波、Alfv\'{e}n 波和熵波）的非线性相互作用。一个有趣的话题是伴随磁场的激波现象，这一现象已被天文观测所证实。然而，如果允许以上所有多速波存在，从数学上讲，能否证明三维 (3D) MHD 激波的形成仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。 MHD 系统的多速特性使其令人着迷且具有挑战性。在本文中，我们报告了我们在回答上述问题方面的最新进展。对于 3D 理想可压缩 MHD，我们构建了激波形成的平面对称示例，允许存在具有多种波速的所有特征波。这也回答了 Majda 在 1984 年提出的关于守恒定律的问题。在我们的构造基础上，我们进一步证明 3D 理想 MHD 的柯西问题是 $H^2$ 不适定的。这是由激波形成引起的。特别是，当磁场不存在时，我们还为 3D 可压缩欧拉方程提供了所需的低规律性不适定结果，并且它相对于流体速度的规律性是尖锐的。我们对 3D MHD 的证明基于精心设计的代数方法和几何方法的结合。为了追踪各种波的非线性相互作用，我们将 3D 理想 MHD 方程代数分解为 $7\times 7$ 非严格双曲系统。通过详细的计算，我们揭示了其隐藏的微妙结构。通过它们，我们可以完整地描述 MHD 动力学，直到最早的奇异事件（激波形成时）。
