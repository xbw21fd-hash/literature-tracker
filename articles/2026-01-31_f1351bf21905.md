## Abstract
Flavonoid diversity arises from modifications of a few precursors by tailored enzymes, yet the complexity of their evolved metabolic networks complicates pathway elucidation and design. Here, by combining chromosome-scale genome assembly with large-scale transcriptome analysis, we identify and reconstruct the glabridin biosynthetic pathway in yeast, enabling de novo production of this antioxidant flavonoid fromGlycyrrhiza glabraL. By analyzing 183 licorice transcriptomes, we discover four classes of enzymes capable of modify isoflavan scaffolds. Among 13 theoretical routes, we validate six functional pathways, revealing a ladder-like, multi-route tailoring network. This network features a multi-step “protection–deprotection” mechanism driven by a dynamic methylation–demethylation cycle that regulate intermediate hydrophilicity and enzyme affinity, thereby enabling species-specific glabridin synthesis. Reconstruction of this network in yeast shows that metabolic redundancy and interconnectivity enhance robustness and yield compared to single-route designs. These results establish a biosynthetic paradigm with broad implications for natural product discovery and biomanufacturing.

## 摘要（中文）
类黄酮的多样性源于通过定制酶对一些前体的修饰，但其进化的代谢网络的复杂性使途径阐明和设计变得复杂。在这里，通过将染色体规模的基因组组装与大规模转录组分析相结合，我们鉴定并重建了酵母中的光甘草定生物合成途径，从而能够从光果甘草中从头生产这种抗氧化剂黄酮类化合物。通过分析 183 个甘草转录组，我们发现了四类能够修饰异黄烷支架的酶。在 13 条理论路线中，我们验证了 6 条功能途径，揭示了一个阶梯状的多路线定制网络。该网络具有由动态甲基化-去甲基化循环驱动的多步骤“保护-去保护”机制，可调节中间亲水性和酶亲和力，从而实现物种特异性光甘草定的合成。在酵母中重建该网络表明，与单途径设计相比，代谢冗余和互连性增强了稳健性和产量。这些结果建立了一个生物合成范式，对天然产物发现和生物制造具有广泛的影响。
