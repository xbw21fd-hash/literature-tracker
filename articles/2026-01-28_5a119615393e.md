## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19538v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Electron-atom collisions in warm dense plasmas are crucial for astrophysics and controlled fusion research, where calculating short-range scattering matrices under screening plasma potentials is essential. While electron-neutral atom collisions are tractable using the standard Riccati-Bessel wavefunctions in the asymptotic region, electron-ion collisions face challenges due to the extended range of the screened Coulomb potential, which lacks analytical solutions or numerical code packages for asymptotic regular and irregular wavefunctions. We introduce an R-matrix theoretical framework for general screened potentials and develop a numerical method to compute these asymptotic wavefunctions efficiently. Our approach yields short-range scattering phase shifts that remain invariant with respect to the matching point in the asymptotic region. Applying the Debye screening potential as an illustrative example, we calculate elastic and electron-impact excitation collision strengths for H-like ions (He, C, Ne) across varying temperatures and densities. The calculations show that Debye screening systematically modifies resonance structures and progressively lowers excitation thresholds. Nevertheless, the effective collision strengths and rate coefficients exhibit approximate scaling laws. These findings enable convenient access to electron collision data in plasma environments, advancing plasma diagnostics and modeling.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19538v1 公告类型：新 摘要：热致密等离子体中的电子原子碰撞对于天体物理学和受控聚变研究至关重要，其中在筛选等离子体势下计算短程散射矩阵至关重要。虽然使用渐近区域中的标准 Riccati-Bessel 波函数可以处理电子-中性原子碰撞，但由于屏蔽库仑势的范围扩大，电子-离子碰撞面临着挑战，库仑势缺乏渐近规则和不规则波函数的解析解或数值代码包。我们引入了一般屏蔽势的 R 矩阵理论框架，并开发了一种有效计算这些渐近波函数的数值方法。我们的方法产生短程散射相移，该相移相对于渐近区域中的匹配点保持不变。以德拜屏蔽势为例，我们计算了类 H 离子（He、C、Ne）在不同温度和密度下的弹性和电子碰撞激发碰撞强度。计算表明，德拜筛选系统地修改了共振结构并逐渐降低了激发阈值。然而，有效碰撞强度和速率系数表现出近似的比例定律。这些发现使得能够方便地获取等离子体环境中的电子碰撞数据，从而推进等离子体诊断和建模。
