# The theory of planar ballistic SNS junctions at $T=0$

## Abstract
arXiv:2503.21583v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The Letter presents the theory of planar ballistic SNS junctions at $T=0$ for any normal layer thickness $L$ taking into account phase gradients in superconducting leads. The current-phase relation was derived in the model of the steplike pairing potential analytically and is exact in the limit of large ratio of the Fermi energy to the superconducting gap. At small $L$ (short junction) the obtained current-phase relation is essentially different from that in the previous theory neglecting phase gradients. It was confirmed by recent numerical calculations and was observed in the experiment on short InAs nanowire Josephson junctions. The analysis resolves the problem with the charge conservation law in the steplike pairing potential model.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2503.21583v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：这封信介绍了在考虑超导引线中的相位梯度的情况下，对于任何正常层厚度 $L$，$T=0$ 处的平面弹道 SNS 结点的理论。在阶梯配对势模型中解析推导了电流-相位关系，并且在费米能级与超导能隙之比较大的情况下是精确的。在小$L$（短结）处，所获得的电流-相位关系与先前忽略相位梯度的理论中的电流-相位关系本质上不同。最近的数值计算证实了这一点，并在短 InAs 纳米线约瑟夫森结的实验中观察到了这一点。该分析解决了阶梯配对电势模型中电荷守恒定律的问题。
