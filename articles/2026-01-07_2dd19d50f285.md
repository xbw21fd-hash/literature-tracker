## Abstract
Low-rank factorizations of the two-electron repulsion integral (ERI) tensor are central to reducing fault-tolerant costs of Hamiltonian simulation in quantum chemistry, particularly under qubitization/LCU constructions where both the block-encoding normalization constant λ and the complexity of Prepare/Select depend on the chosen decomposition. We present a CPU-only, FCIDUMP-first workflow that (i) constructs a spectral double factorization (DF) of the ERI supermatrix G_(pq,rs) = (pq|rs), (ii) truncates the factorization at rank r and reports reconstruction residuals, (iii) evaluates DF resource proxies via λ_LCU^DF and λ_Burg^DF, and (iv) selects an operational rank by enforcing an observable-level constraint on a differential quantity: the MP2 correlation contribution to a model adsorption energy, ΔE_ads,corr. Using NH3 as a minimal Lewis-basic site and CO2 as adsorbate in STO-3G, we sweep intermolecular separations d_N···C ∈ {2.2, 2.6, 3.0} Å and determine the worst-case feasible rank r_worst as the smallest rank satisfying a predefined adsorption-error tolerance across the geometry set. In this toy benchmark, λ-metrics for the complex saturate at moderate ranks while the differential adsorption observable remains sensitive to intermediate-rank components, demonstrating that λ convergence alone can be insufficient for rank selection when targeting chemically relevant differences. The resulting protocol provides a reproducible bridge between DF-based resource estimation and observable-constrained truncation criteria for qubitization-oriented Hamiltonians.

## 摘要（中文）
双电子排斥积分 (ERI) 张量的低秩因式分解对于降低量子化学中哈密顿模拟的容错成本至关重要，特别是在量子位化/LCU 结构下，其中块编码归一化常数 λ 和准备/选择的复杂性都取决于所选的分解。我们提出了一种仅使用 CPU、FCIDUMP 优先的工作流程，其中 (i) 构建 ERI 超级矩阵 G_(pq,rs) = (pq|rs) 的谱双分解 (DF)，(ii) 在等级 r 处截断分解并报告重建残差，(iii) 通过 λ_LCU^DF 和 λ_Burg^DF 评估 DF 资源代理，以及 (iv) 通过强制执行操作等级来选择操作等级微分量的可观测水平约束：MP2 相关性对模型吸附能 ΔE_ads,corr 的贡献。使用 NH3 作为 STO-3G 中的最小路易斯碱性位点和 CO2 作为吸附物，我们扫描分子间分离 d_N·C ∈ {2.2, 2.6, 3.0} Å 并确定最坏情况可行等级 r_worst 作为满足整个几何集合中预定义吸附误差容限的最小等级。在这个玩具基准中，复杂的 λ 度量在中等等级饱和，而可观察到的差异吸附仍然对中间等级成分敏感，这表明当针对化学相关差异时，仅 λ 收敛不足以进行等级选择。由此产生的协议在基于 DF 的资源估计和面向量子化的哈密顿量的可观察约束截断标准之间提供了可重复的桥梁。
