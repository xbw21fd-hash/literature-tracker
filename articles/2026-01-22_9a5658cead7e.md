## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14886v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In piecewise omnigenous magnetic fields, charged particles remain perfectly confined in the abscence of collisions and turbulence. This concept extends the traditional notion of omnigenity, the theoretical principle upon which most of existing magnetic fusion reactor designs, including tokamaks, are based. While piecewise omnigenity broadens the range of potentially viable stellarator reactor candidates, it is achieved by relaxing the requirement of continuity in the magnetic field strength, which could appear to pose significant challenges for the design of magnetohydrodynamic equilibria. In this work, a stellarator magnetic configuration is presented that satisfies the ideal magnetohydrodynamic equilibrium equation and that achieves unprecedented levels of piecewise omnigenity. As a result, it exhibits favorable transport characteristics, including reduced bulk radial (neoclassical and turbulent transport), bootstrap current and fast ion losses. In addition, the configuration displays robust MHD stability across a range of \b{eta} values and possesses a rotational transform profile compatible with an island divertor. Collectively, these features satisfy the standard set of physics criteria required for a viable reactor candidate which, until now, were believed to be attainable only by certain types of omnigenous stellarators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14886v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在分段全向磁场中，带电粒子在没有碰撞和湍流的情况下保持完美的限制。这一概念扩展了传统的全源性概念，这是大多数现有磁聚变反应堆设计（包括托卡马克）所基于的理论原理。虽然分段全向性扩大了潜在可行的仿星器反应堆候选范围，但它是通过放宽磁场强度连续性要求来实现的，这似乎对磁流体动力学平衡的设计构成重大挑战。在这项工作中，提出了一种仿星器磁配置，它满足理想的磁流体动力学平衡方程，并实现了前所未有的分段全向性水平。因此，它表现出良好的传输特性，包括减少体积径向（新古典和湍流传输）、自举电流和快速离子损失。此外，该配置在一系列 β 值范围内显示出强大的 MHD 稳定性，并拥有与岛偏滤器兼容的旋转变换轮廓。总的来说，这些特征满足了可行的候选反应堆所需的物理标准，迄今为止，人们认为只有某些类型的全能仿星器才能实现这些标准。
