## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20121v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The interplanetary magnetic field exhibits a distinctive $1/f$ spectral density from frequencies of around $\unit[10^{-6}]{Hz}$ to around $\unit[10^{-4}]{Hz}$, ranging from harmonics of the solar rotation to the reciprocal of the turbulence correlation time in the spacecraft frame. Various theories have been proposed to explain its origin, typically invoking either processes in the lower corona or in the solar interior, or local interplanetary dynamics. Here, we investigate the {\it superposition principle} that underlies explanations of the solar/coronal types, which in principle can generate the full observed range of $1/f$ noise. Using synthetic time series with scale-invariant or log-normal distributions of correlation times, we examine the efficacy of several superposition approaches in generating a $1/f$ regime. The persistence of $1/f$ spectrum is further illustrated with decade-long {\it in situ} magnetic field measurements from the ACE spacecraft. Together, these results help explain the ubiquity of $1/f$ noise under the unavoidable superposition inherent in long-duration heliospheric data.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20121v1 公告类型：新 摘要：从 $\unit[10^{-6}]{Hz}$ 左右到 $\unit[10^{-4}]{Hz}$ 左右的频率，行星际磁场表现出独特的 $1/f$ 谱密度，范围从太阳自转的谐波到航天器框架中湍流相关时间的倒数。人们提出了各种理论来解释其起源，通常涉及日冕下部或太阳内部的过程，或局部行星际动力学。在这里，我们研究了解释太阳/日冕类型的基础的{\it叠加原理}，原则上可以产生完整观测范围的$1/f$噪声。使用具有尺度不变或相关时间对数正态分布的合成时间序列，我们检查了几种叠加方法在生成 $1/f$ 机制方面的功效。 ACE 航天器长达十年的原位磁场测量进一步说明了 $1/f$ 谱的持久性。总之，这些结果有助于解释在长期日光层数据固有的不可避免的叠加下 $1/f$ 噪声的普遍存在。
