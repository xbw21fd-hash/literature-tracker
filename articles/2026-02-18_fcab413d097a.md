## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15768v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Latent heat flux is a primary pathway for ocean-atmosphere exchange of heat and moisture, yet the influence of sea surface temperature variability at fine scales ($\leq$ 100 km) on latent heat flux variability, particularly over the Southern Ocean, remains poorly understood. Here we quantify the scale-dependent drivers of latent heat flux (LHF) variability using a year-long, global, fully coupled ocean-atmosphere simulation with kilometer-scale resolution. Annual-mean LHF in eddy-rich regions reaches $\approx$ 215 W m$^{-2}$, approximately three times larger than in eddy-poor regions. Spectral analyses show that ocean mesoscale [$\mathcal{O}$(100 km)] and submesoscale [$\mathcal{O}$(1-10 km)] variability accounts for up to $\approx$ 80% of the total LHF variance in eddy-rich sectors, but as little as 10% in eddy-poor regions, and increases proportionally with eddy kinetic energy and sea surface temperature (SST) variance. We also find that strong submesoscale SST fronts ($\approx$ 5 $^\circ$C over 10 km) force a localized secondary circulation that extends well above the marine boundary layer into the mid-troposphere. Comparison with ERA5 shows that fine ocean scales, responsible for about 17% of the ocean-driven LHF variance in the simulation, are largely unresolved in the reanalysis, leading to a muted atmospheric response lacking any secondary circulation. Despite a strong heterogeneity in LHF variability, the atmospheric dynamics are mostly uniform across the domain, suggesting a non local atmospheric response to ocean forcing. These results highlight the potential for ocean meso- and submesoscales, commonly under-resolved in climate models and reanalysis, to influence Southern Ocean air-sea coupling and atmosphere both locally and remotely.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15768v1 公告类型：新 摘要：潜热通量是海洋-大气热量和水分交换的主要途径，但细尺度（$\leq$ 100 km）海面温度变化对潜热通量变化（尤其是南大洋）的影响仍知之甚少。在这里，我们使用长达一年的全球性、完全耦合的海洋-大气模拟（具有千米级分辨率）来量化潜热通量 (LHF) 变化的尺度相关驱动因素。涡流丰富地区的年平均 LHF 达到 $\approx$ 215 W m$^{-2}$，大约是涡流贫乏地区的三倍。光谱分析表明，海洋中尺度[$\mathcal{O}$(100 km)]和亚中尺度[$\mathcal{O}$(1-10 km)]变异性在涡流丰富的扇区中占LHF总方差的高达$\approx$ 80%，但在涡流贫乏的区域中仅占10%，并且随着涡动能和海面温度(SST)方差的增加而成比例地增加。我们还发现，强大的次中尺度海温锋（$\approx$ 5 $^\circ$C超过10公里）迫使局部二次环流延伸到海洋边界层上方远至对流层中部。与 ERA5 的比较表明，精细海洋尺度在模拟中约占海洋驱动 LHF 方差的 17%，但在重新分析中基本上未得到解决，从而导致缺乏任何次级环流的大气响应减弱。尽管LHF变化具有很强的异质性，但大气动力学在整个域内大多是均匀的，这表明大气对海洋强迫的非局域响应。这些结果凸显了海洋中尺度和亚中尺度在本地和远程影响南大洋海气耦合和大气的潜力，而这些在气候模型和再分析中通常未得到解决。
