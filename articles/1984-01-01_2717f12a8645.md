## Abstract
The minimal base of muffin-tin orbitals is transformed exactly into a tight-binding base. The linear transformations, the orbitals, and the Hamiltonian, overlap, and Green's function matrices are expressed in terms of one matrix, the canonical structure matrix $S$ij. It vanishes beyond second-nearest neighbors and is tabulated. Tight-binding two-center forms with transfer integrals proportional to $S$ij are derived.
