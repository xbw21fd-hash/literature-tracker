## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01379v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Machine learning (ML) offers transformative potential for computational fluid dynamics (CFD), promising to accelerate simulations, improve turbulence modelling, and enable real-time flow prediction and control-capabilities that could fundamentally change how engineers approach fluid dynamics problems. However, the exploration of ML in fluid dynamics is critically hampered by the scarcity of large, diverse, and high-fidelity datasets suitable for training robust models. This limitation is particularly acute for highly turbulent flows, which dominate practical engineering applications yet remain computationally prohibitive to simulate at scale. High-Reynolds number turbulent datasets are essential for ML models to learn the complex, multi-scale physics characteristic of real-world flows, enabling generalisation beyond the simplified, low-Reynolds number regimes often represented in existing datasets. This paper introduces WAKESET, a novel, large-scale CFD dataset of highly turbulent flows, designed to address this critical gap. The dataset captures the complex hydrodynamic interactions during the underwater recovery of an autonomous underwater vehicle by a larger extra-large uncrewed underwater vehicle. It comprises 1,091 high-fidelity Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes simulations, augmented to 4,364 instances, covering a wide operational envelope of speeds (up to Reynolds numbers of 1.09 x 10^8) and turning angles. This work details the motivation for this new dataset by reviewing existing resources, outlines the hydrodynamic modelling and validation underpinning its creation, and describes its structure. The dataset's focus on a practical engineering problem, its scale, and its high turbulence characteristics make it a valuable resource for developing and benchmarking ML models for flow field prediction, surrogate modelling, and autonomous navigation in complex underwater environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01379v1 公告类型：新 摘要：机器学习 (ML) 为计算流体动力学 (CFD) 提供了变革潜力，有望加速模拟、改进湍流建模并实现实时流动预测和控制功能，从而从根本上改变工程师处理流体动力学问题的方式。然而，由于缺乏适合训练鲁棒模型的大型、多样化和高保真数据集，机器学习在流体动力学领域的探索受到严重阻碍。这种限制对于高度湍流尤其严重，高度湍流在实际工程应用中占主导地位，但在计算上仍然难以进行大规模模拟。高雷诺数湍流数据集对于机器学习模型学习现实世界流动的复杂、多尺度物理特征至关重要，从而能够超越现有数据集中通常表示的简化、低雷诺数状态。本文介绍了 WAKESET，这是一种新颖的大规模高湍流 CFD 数据集，旨在解决这一关键差距。该数据集捕获了大型超大型无人水下航行器在水下回收自主水下航行器期间复杂的水动力相互作用。它包含 1,091 个高保真雷诺平均纳维斯托克斯模拟，已扩展到 4,364 个实例，涵盖了广泛的速度操作范围（高达 1.09 x 10^8 的雷诺数）和转向角度。这项工作通过审查现有资源详细介绍了这个新数据集的动机，概述了支持其创建的流体动力学建模和验证，并描述了其结构。该数据集专注于实际工程问题，其规模和高湍流特性使其成为复杂水下环境中流场预测、代理建模和自主导航的机器学习模型的开发和基准测试的宝贵资源。
