## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09166v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Recent experiments on crosslinked gels quenched from solvent-rich to solvent-poor conditions show solvent-rich domains embedded in a gel-rich matrix. These domains coarsen and then undergo kinetic arrest at micron scales for hours, before macroscopic drainage to equilibrium over an even longer time. Motivated by these observations, we develop a minimal model that couples capillarity-driven Darcy permeation to the viscoelastic-to-elastic crossover of the polymer network. In the viscoelastic regime, the Young--Laplace boundary traction (due to surface tension) at curved solvent--gel interfaces generates an isotropic pressure gradient in the solvent pores of the gel that drives solvent flow and coarsening. In the elastic regime, the same interfacial traction is balanced by a long-ranged deviatoric (non-isotropic) stress field, and the elastic equilibrium solution yields a spatially uniform pore pressure outside a domain, eliminating the Darcy driving force and arresting coarsening. Using the kinetic criterion $t(\lambda_{\rm arrest})\sim\tau_{\rm el}$, we predict stiffness-dependent growth laws and arrest lengths. For melt-like, polymer-rich gels, $\lambda(t)\sim G^{-1/2}t^{1/4}$ and $\lambda_{\rm arrest}\sim G^{-1/2}$. For low polymer fractions where the mesh size controls transport, $\lambda(t)\sim G^{-1/3}t^{1/3}$ and $\lambda_{\rm arrest}\sim G^{-1/3}$. The predicted $G^{-1/2}$ arrest scaling for melt-like gels is consistent with measurements.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09166v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：最近关于从富溶剂条件到贫溶剂条件淬灭的交联凝胶的实验表明，富含溶剂的域嵌入在富含凝胶的基质中。这些区域变粗，然后在微米尺度上经历数小时的动力学停滞，然后在更长的时间内宏观排水达到平衡。受这些观察的启发，我们开发了一个最小模型，将毛细管作用驱动的达西渗透与聚合物网络的粘弹性到弹性交叉耦合起来。在粘弹性状态下，弯曲溶剂-凝胶界面处的杨-拉普拉斯边界牵引力（由于表面张力）在凝胶的溶剂孔中产生各向同性压力梯度，驱动溶剂流动和粗化。在弹性状态下，相同的界面牵引力由长程偏向（非各向同性）应力场平衡，弹性平衡解在域外产生空间均匀的孔隙压力，消除了达西驱动力并阻止粗化。使用动力学准则 $t(\lambda_{\rm parking})\sim\tau_{\rm el}$，我们预测依赖于刚度的生长定律和停顿长度。对于熔体状、富含聚合物的凝胶，$\lambda(t)\sim G^{-1/2}t^{1/4}$ 和 $\lambda_{\rm parking}\sim G^{-1/2}$。对于网格尺寸控制传输的低聚合物组分，$\lambda(t)\sim G^{-1/3}t^{1/3}$ 和 $\lambda_{\rm parking}\sim G^{-1/3}$。类熔体凝胶的预测 $G^{-1/2}$ 停滞比例与测量结果一致。
