## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13960v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Scanning Probe Microscopy (SPM) is the primary tool for exploring nanoscale functionality, yet standard single-frequency operation is fundamentally limited, because the dynamic tip-sample interaction is mathematically underdetermined. While advanced methods such as Dual Amplitude Resonance Tracking (DART) and Band Excitation (BE) address this by tracking resonance, they face critical limitations: DART suffers from feedback instability on complex topographies, while Band Excitation is constrained by severe trade-offs between spectral resolution and acquisition speed. Here, we introduce Dynamic Multiband Microscopy (DMM), a general framework that bridges these gaps by combining multifrequency excitation with continuous frequency sweeping. We implement this within an automated experimental workflow that autonomously identifies and targets measurement points of interest. In combination with quantitative interferometric detection, this approach brings SPM to the fundamental limits of noise and spectral sensitivity. Validated on ferroelectric nanofibers, this platform enables simultaneous, crosstalk-free 3D polarization mapping, establishing a universal framework for autonomous, high-fidelity nanoscale metrology.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13960v1 公告类型：新 摘要：扫描探针显微镜 (SPM) 是探索纳米级功能的主要工具，但标准单频操作从根本上受到限制，因为动态尖端-样品相互作用在数学上是不确定的。虽然双振幅共振跟踪 (DART) 和带激励 (BE) 等先进方法通过跟踪共振来解决这个问题，但它们面临着严重的局限性：DART 受到复杂地形上反馈不稳定的影响，而带激励则受到光谱分辨率和采集速度之间严重权衡的限制。在这里，我们介绍动态多波段显微镜（DMM），这是一种通过将多频激励与连续频率扫描相结合来弥补这些差距的通用框架。我们在自动化实验工作流程中实现这一点，该工作流程可以自主识别和定位感兴趣的测量点。与定量干涉检测相结合，这种方法使 SPM 达到了噪声和光谱灵敏度的基本极限。该平台在铁电纳米纤维上进行了验证，可实现同步、无串扰的 3D 偏振映射，为自主、高保真纳米级计量建立通用框架。
