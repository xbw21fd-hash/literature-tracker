## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04908v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Sawtooth chain magnets have been a subject of historical interest in the field of frustrated magnetism, with classical olivine family $M_2TX_4$, ($M$ - 3d, $T$ - 4p, $X$ - chalcogen elements) typically realizing simple $\mathbf{k} = (000)$ states. The magnetism of the Mn$_2$GeO$_4$ olivine is surprisingly complex, proceeding from commensurate states to a multiferroic commensurate + incommensurate phase. Here we report inelastic neutron scattering results from a Mn$_2$GeO$_4$ single crystal and develop an effective Hamiltonian including long-distance bilinear and dipolar interactions. The magnetic interactions are predominantly antiferromagnetic and span a three-dimensional exchange network consisting of coupled sawtooth chains. Based on the determined strength of the couplings, the dominant sawtooth chains appear at third- and fourth- rather than next-nearest-neighbor. However the next-nearest-neighbor interaction is, along with a modest Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction, important for modeling the observed incommensurability. We use the best-fit Hamiltonian as the basis for Langevin dynamics simulations and Luttinger-Tisza calculations of the high-temperature commensurate transition.

arXiv:2601.04908v1 公告类型：新 摘要：锯齿链磁体一直是受抑磁领域历史关注的主题，经典橄榄石族 $M_2TX_4$（$M$ - 3d，$T$ - 4p，$X$ - 硫属元素）通常实现简单的 $\mathbf{k} = (000)$ 状态。 Mn$_2$GeO$_4$ 橄榄石的磁性出人意料地复杂，从相称状态发展到多铁性相称 + 不相称相。在这里，我们报告了 Mn$_2$GeO$_4$ 单晶的非弹性中子散射结果，并开发了一种有效的哈密顿量，包括长距离双线性和偶极相互作用。磁相互作用主要是反铁磁的，跨越由耦合锯齿链组成的三维交换网络。根据确定的耦合强度，主要的锯齿链出现在第三和第四，而不是第二个最近的邻居。然而，次近邻相互作用以及适度的 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 相互作用对于模拟观察到的不可通约性非常重要。我们使用最适合的哈密顿量作为朗之万动力学模拟和高温相称转变的 Luttinger-Tisza 计算的基础。
