## Abstract
arXiv:2305.16134v3 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Inference in cosmology often starts with noisy observations of random fields on the celestial sphere, such as maps of the microwave background radiation, continuous maps of cosmic structure in different wavelengths, or maps of point tracers of the cosmological fields. Almanac uses Hamiltonian Monte Carlo sampling to infer the underlying all-sky noiseless maps of cosmic structures, in multiple redshift bins, together with their auto- and cross-power spectra. It can sample many millions of parameters, handling the highly variable signal-to-noise of typical cosmological signals, and it provides science-ready posterior data products. In the case of spin-weight 2 fields, Almanac infers $E$- and $B$-mode power spectra and parity-violating $EB$ power, and, by sampling the full posteriors rather than point estimates, it avoids the problem of $EB$-leakage. For theories with no $B$-mode signal, inferred non-zero $B$-mode power may be a useful diagnostic of systematic errors or an indication of new physics. Almanac's aim is to characterise the statistical properties of the maps, with outputs that are completely independent of the cosmological model, beyond an assumption of statistical isotropy. Inference of parameters of any particular cosmological model follows in a separate analysis stage. We demonstrate our signal extraction on a CMB-like experiment.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2305.16134v3 公告类型：cross 摘要：宇宙学的推论通常从天球上随机场的噪声观测开始，例如微波背景辐射图、不同波长的宇宙结构连续图或宇宙场点示踪器图。年鉴使用哈密顿蒙特卡罗采样来推断多个红移箱中的底层全天无噪声宇宙结构图及其自功率谱和互功率谱。它可以对数百万个参数进行采样，处理典型宇宙信号的高度可变的信噪比，并提供科学就绪的后验数据产品。在自旋权重 2 场的情况下，年鉴推断 $E$- 和 $B$- 模式功率谱以及违反奇偶校验的 $EB$ 功率，并且通过对完整后验而不是点估计进行采样，它避免了 $EB$ 泄漏问题。对于没有 $B$ 模式信号的理论，推断的非零 $B$ 模式功率可能是系统误差的有用诊断或新物理的指示。年鉴的目的是表征地图的统计特性，其输出完全独立于宇宙学模型，超出了统计各向同性的假设。任何特定宇宙学模型的参数推断都在单独的分析阶段进行。我们在类似 CMB 的实验中演示了我们的信号提取。
