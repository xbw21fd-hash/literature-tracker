## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13620v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Twisted bilayer systems host a wealth of emergent phenomena, such as flat-band superconductivity, ferromagnetism, and ferroelectricity, arising from moir\'e superlattices and unconventional interlayer coupling. Despite their central role, direct and quantitative access to the out-of-plane atomic structure in these systems has remained elusive due to their nanoscale dimensions. Here, we introduce an automated dark-field electron tomography technique that enables three-dimensional structural analysis of atomically thin materials with sub-angstrom precision. Applying this method to twisted bilayer WSe$_2$, we uncover a significant expansion of the interlayer spacing compared to the bulk configuration, exceeding 0.1 angstrom, along with a remarkable temperature-driven interlayer decoupling unique to the twisted bilayer. Ultrafast measurement further reveals optically induced interlayer separation of ~0.2 angstrom on the picosecond timescale, attributed to transient exciton formation. These findings not only establish a powerful approach for visualizing hidden out-of-plane structures in atomically thin micro-flake materials, but also uncover the intrinsic fragility and dynamical tunability of interlayer coupling in moir\'e-engineered 2-dimensional materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13620v1 公告类型：新 摘要：扭曲双层系统具有大量由莫尔超晶格和非常规层间耦合引起的新兴现象，例如平带超导性、铁磁性和铁电性。尽管它们发挥着核心作用，但由于其纳米级尺寸，直接定量地获取这些系统中的面外原子结构仍然难以实现。在这里，我们介绍了一种自动暗场电子断层扫描技术，该技术能够以亚埃精度对原子薄材料进行三维结构分析。将此方法应用于扭曲双层 WSe$_2$，我们发现与块状结构相比，层间间距显着扩大，超过 0.1 埃，以及扭曲双层独有的显着的温度驱动层间去耦。超快测量进一步揭示了皮秒时间尺度上 ~0.2 埃的光致层间分离，归因于瞬态激子的形成。这些发现不仅建立了一种强大的方法来可视化原子薄微薄片材料中隐藏的面外结构，而且还揭示了莫尔电子工程二维材料中层间耦合的内在脆弱性和动态可调性。
