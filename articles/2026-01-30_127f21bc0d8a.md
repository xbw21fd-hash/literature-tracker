## Abstract
arXiv:2507.05303v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In this paper, we study complex-valued neural network (CVNNs) with tensor-valued hidden-to-output weights within the framework of neural-network quantum field theory (NN-QFT). For standard CVNNs with scalar weights, we derive the generating functional and identify the exact Gaussian process that arises in the infinite-width limit, together with its associated effective quantum state. When the last-layer weights are promoted to Clifford-algebra-valued tensors, the network output becomes complex matrix-valued, and a fermion-like sign structure in the large-width correlation functions of the network output is induced. We show that, in the infinite-width limit, correlators with equal numbers of $f^{\dag}$ and $f$ obey fermionic Wick rules and can be written as determinants built from a scalar Euclidean kernel $S(x,y)=\langle f^{\dag}(x)f(y)\rangle$. This provides a sign-structured extension of NN-QFT at the level of Euclidean correlators and Feynman rules, even though a microscopic Grassmann path integral representation for the network parameters has not yet been constructed. Our analysis thus pushes the NN-QFT correspondence beyond purely bosonic Gaussian fields and suggests a possible route to encoding fermion-like symmetries in neural architectures for QFT correspondence.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.05303v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：在本文中，我们在神经网络量子场论 (NN-QFT) 框架内研究具有张量值隐藏到输出权重的复值神经网络 (CVNN)。对于具有标量权重的标准 CVNN，我们推导生成函数并识别在无限宽度限制中出现的精确高斯过程及其相关的有效量子态。当最后一层权重提升为 Clifford 代数值张量时，网络输出变为复矩阵值，并且在网络输出的大宽度相关函数中引入类似费米子的符号结构。我们证明，在无限宽度限制下，具有相同数量的 $f^{\dag}$ 和 $f$ 的相关器遵循费米威克规则，并且可以写成从标量欧几里得核 $S(x,y)=\langle f^{\dag}(x)f(y)\rangle$ 构建的行列式。这提供了欧几里得相关器和费曼规则级别的 NN-QFT 的符号结构扩展，即使尚未构建网络参数的微观格拉斯曼路径积分表示。因此，我们的分析将 NN-QFT 对应推向了纯玻色高斯场之外，并提出了一种在 QFT 对应的神经架构中编码类费米子对称性的可能途径。
