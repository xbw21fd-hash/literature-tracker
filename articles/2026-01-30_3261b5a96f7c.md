## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21273v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Layered NbOX$_2$ ($X=\mathrm{Cl,\,Br,\,I}$), a member of the van der Waals ferroelectric family, exhibits intrinsic ferroelectricity and pronounced nonlinear optical responses, making it a promising candidate for integrated nanophotonics applications. While previous studies have emphasized the material's strong second-order nonlinear responses, higher-order nonlinear responses are still mostly unexplored. This work systematically investigates NbOI$_2$ using high harmonic generation (HHG) spectroscopy. Driven by an intense mid-infrared laser field centered at $\sim4~\mu\mathrm{m}$ wavelength, highly anisotropic odd- and even-order harmonics up to the 16th order are generated at a low peak intensity of $0.4~\mathrm{TW\,cm^{-2}}$, extending beyond the material's bandgap. Both bulk and flake forms of NbOI$_2$ display pronounced harmonic emission from the near-infrared to the deep-ultraviolet spectral region, with a notably high overall conversion efficiency compared to other known materials. Polarization-resolved measurements reveal that even-order harmonics remain aligned with the crystal polar axis regardless of the driving-field orientation, whereas odd-order harmonics are dynamically affected. First-principles calculations suggest that the flat valence band associated with Peierls dimerization enhances HHG efficiency through electron correlation. These findings provide fresh perspectives on HHG in van der Waals ferroelectric materials and facilitate the development of compact and tunable quantum light sources.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21273v1 公告类型：新 摘要：层状 NbOX$_2$ ($X=\mathrm{Cl,\,Br,\,I}$) 是范德华铁电家族的成员，表现出固有的铁电性和明显的非线性光学响应，使其成为集成纳米光子学应用的有前途的候选者。虽然之前的研究强调了材料的强二阶非线性响应，但高阶非线性响应大多仍未被探索。这项工作使用高谐波发生 (HHG) 光谱系统地研究了 NbOI$_2$。在以 $\sim4~\mu\mathrm{m}$ 波长为中心的强中红外激光场的驱动下，在 $0.4~\mathrm{TW\,cm^{-2}}$ 的低峰值强度下产生高达 16 阶的高度各向异性的奇数和偶数谐波，延伸超出材料的带隙。 NbOI$_2$ 的块状和片状形式都显示出从近红外到深紫外光谱区域的显着谐波发射，与其他已知材料相比，具有显着高的整体转换效率。偏振分辨测量表明，无论驱动场方向如何，偶次谐波都保持与晶体极轴对齐，而奇次谐波则受到动态影响。第一原理计算表明，与 Peierls 二聚化相关的平坦价带通过电子相关性提高了 HHG 效率。这些发现为范德华铁电材料中的 HHG 提供了新的视角，并促进了紧凑且可调谐量子光源的开发。
