## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18495v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The nonlinear decay of Langmuir/$\mathcal{Z}$-mode waves into electromagnetic $\mathcal{Z}$-mode wave radiation at the plasma frequency is observed for the first time in the solar wind. This finding was enabled by the unprecedented high-resolution electric and magnetic field measurements provided by the Radio Plasma Waves (RPW) instrument aboard the Solar Orbiter spacecraft, which encountered an electron beam associated with a Type III radio burst. The decay process is definitively identified through multiple lines of evidence: satisfaction of frequency and wavevector resonance conditions, strong phase coherence and temporal coincidence between the interacting waves, exclusion of competing mechanisms, and full agreement with theoretical predictions. Particle-in-cell simulations, conducted under close beam-plasma conditions, successfully reproduce the key features of the observations. Notably, they suggest that the wave packet observed by Solar Orbiter may be trapped within an extended, nearly flat-bottomed density well, where the decay process is not overcome by wave scattering on random density fluctuations and subsequent mode conversion effects.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18495v1 公告类型：新 摘要：首次在太阳风中观察到等离子体频率下 Langmuir/$\mathcal{Z}$ 模波非线性衰减为等离子体频率下的电磁 $\mathcal{Z}$ 模波辐射。这一发现是由太阳轨道飞行器上的射电等离子体波 (RPW) 仪器提供的前所未有的高分辨率电场和磁场测量实现的，该仪器遇到了与 III 型无线电爆发相关的电子束。衰变过程通过多种证据明确确定：满足频率和波矢量共振条件、相互作用波之间的强相位相干性和时间重合、排除竞争机制以及与理论预测完全一致。在近距离束等离子体条件下进行的细胞内粒子模拟成功地再现了观察到的关键特征。值得注意的是，他们认为太阳轨道飞行器观测到的波包可能被困在一个延伸的、几乎平底的密度井内，其中衰减过程不会被随机密度波动的波散射和随后的模式转换效应所克服。
