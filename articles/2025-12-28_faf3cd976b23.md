## Abstract
The oxidation of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) affects particulate matter and oxidant levels in the troposphere, making it vital to simulate for climate models and to predict air quality. Full-complexity oxidation mechanisms for VOCs such as isoprene, however, are too large to be simulated in a 3-dimensional atmospheric chemical transport model. Reduced VOC oxidation mechanisms have been developed, but all must make a small number of reactions represent a much larger set, leading to imprecision in stoichiometric coefficients. Some of the sacrifice of accuracy incurred in the reduction process can be recuperated by optimizing the stoichiometric coefficients after reduction. In this work, we present Coefficient Optimization for Automated MOdel REduction (CO-AMORE), a new set of tools for the optimization of reduced mechanism stoichiometric coefficients using graph theory-assisted gradient descent. These methods include the application of gradient descent and two new algorithms for preprocessing coefficients: Anti-Community Clustering (ACC) and Bipartite Graph Sorting (BGS). Our application of gradient descent with data preprocessed using our ACC algorithm improved the accuracy of a reduced isoprene mechanism by up to 40% in a box model simulation. Gradient descent with data preprocessed using BGS was able to achieve similar levels of optimization as ACC across multiple mechanisms. In addition, ACC was over four times faster than standard gradient descent, showing the promise of this novel methodology. Optimized reduced mechanisms allow atmospheric models to be more accurate, enabling a better understanding of particulate matter sources and offering the potential to benefit public health.

## 摘要（中文）
挥发性有机化合物 (VOC) 的氧化会影响对流层中的颗粒物和氧化剂水平，因此模拟气候模型和预测空气质量至关重要。然而，异戊二烯等 VOC 的完全复杂氧化机制太大，无法在 3 维大气化学传输模型中进行模拟。减少VOC的氧化机制已经被开发出来，但所有这些机制都必须使少量的反应代表一个更大的集合，从而导致化学计量系数的不精确。还原过程中产生的一些精度损失可以通过优化还原后的化学计量系数来弥补。在这项工作中，我们提出了自动模型缩减系数优化（CO-AMORE），这是一组使用图论辅助梯度下降来优化缩减机制化学计量系数的新工具。这些方法包括梯度下降的应用和两种用于预处理系数的新算法：反社区聚类（ACC）和二分图排序（BGS）。我们应用梯度下降法以及使用我们的 ACC 算法预处理的数据，在盒模型模拟中将减少的异戊二烯机制的准确性提高了高达 40%。使用 BGS 预处理数据的梯度下降能够跨多种机制实现与 ACC 类似的优化水平。此外，ACC 的速度比标准梯度下降快四倍以上，显示了这种新颖方法的前景。优化的简化机制使大气模型更加准确，从而能够更好地了解颗粒物来源并提供有益于公众健康的潜力。
