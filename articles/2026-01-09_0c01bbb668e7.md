## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04721v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The energy gap is a fundamental property of materials, directly related to their optical and electronic properties. The energy gap of ferroelectric compounds and its adjustment by compositional variation has particularly attracted attention in recent years due to potential application in energy conversion and/or catalytic devices. It is demonstrated that it is necessary to distinguish between the fundamental gap, $E_{\rm g}^{0}$, the optical gap, $E_{\rm g}^{\rm opt}$, and the transport gap, $E_{\rm g}^{\rm tr}$, of ferroelectrics, which can differ significantly. The situation is comparable to those in organic semiconductors and emerges from the presence of localized charges. The fundamental gap is a ground state property, i.e.\ the energy difference between the maximum of the fully occupied valence band and the minimum of the completely empty conduction band. In contrast, the optical and transport gaps are excited state properties involving localized (polaronic) electrons and/or holes at energies considerably different from the band edges. This work illustrates how the different energy gaps of ferroelectrics can be determined by combining optical measurements, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy and temperature and oxygen partial pressure dependent electrical conductivity measurements. We determine fundamental gaps of $\approx 4.5\,$eV for both materials, optical gaps of $3.25-3.45\,$eV/$3.5\,$eV and electrical gaps of $\approx 1.4\,$eV/$3.3\,$eV for Na$_{0.5}$Bi$_{0.5}$TiO$_3$-BaTiO$_3$/NaNbO$_{3}$, respectively.

arXiv:2601.04721v1 公告类型：新 摘要：能隙是材料的基本属性，与其光学和电子属性直接相关。由于在能量转换和/或催化装置中的潜在应用，铁电化合物的能隙及其通过成分变化的调节近年来特别引起人们的关注。事实证明，有必要区分铁电体的基本能隙 $E_{\rm g}^{0}$、光学能隙 $E_{\rm g}^{\rm opt}$ 和传输能隙 $E_{\rm g}^{\rm tr}$，它们可能存在显着差异。这种情况与有机半导体中的情况类似，并且是由于局部电荷的存在而出现的。基本能隙是基态属性，即完全占据的价带的最大值与完全空的导带的最小值之间的能量差。相反，光学和传输间隙是激发态特性，涉及能量与能带边缘显着不同的局域（极化）电子和/或空穴。这项工作说明了如何通过结合光学测量、X 射线光电子能谱以及温度和氧分压相关的电导率测量来确定铁电体的不同能隙。我们确定两种材料的基本能隙为$\约4.5\,$eV，光学能隙为$3.25-3.45\,$eV/$3.5\,$eV，电气能隙为$\约1.4\,$eV/$3.3\,$eV分别为Na$_{0.5}$Bi$_{0.5}$TiO$_3$-BaTiO$_3$/NaNbO$_{3}$。
