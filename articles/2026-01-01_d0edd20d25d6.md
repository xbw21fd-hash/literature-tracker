## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23744v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We demonstrate the spontaneous formation of acoustic black holes in exciton-polariton condensates triggered by discontinuous Riemann-type initial conditions. Starting from a quasi-conservative Gross-Pitaevskii model, we show that nonlinear dispersive shock waves naturally generate spatial regions where the local flow velocity exceeds the speed of sound, creating a self-induced transonic interface that functions as an acoustic horizon. Unlike previous schemes relying on externally engineered potentials or pump-loss landscapes, our approach reveals that the intrinsic nonlinear hydrodynamics of polariton fluids alone can lead to horizon formation. Using Whitham modulation theory and numerical simulations, we characterize the transition between subsonic and supersonic regimes and estimate the corresponding surface gravity and Hawking temperature. This mechanism opens a new route toward realizing polariton black holes and studying analogue gravitational effects, including Hawking-like emission, in Bose-Einstein quantum liquids.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23744v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们证明了由不连续黎曼型初始条件触发的激子极化子凝聚体中声学黑洞的自发形成。从准保守的 Gross-Pitaevskii 模型开始，我们表明非线性色散冲击波自然地产生局部流速超过声速的空间区域，从而创建了一个充当声学地平线的自感跨音速界面。与之前依赖于外部工程势或泵损失景观的方案不同，我们的方法表明，仅极化激元流体的固有非线性流体动力学就可以导致地平线的形成。利用惠瑟姆调制理论和数值模拟，我们描述了亚音速和超音速状态之间的转变，并估计了相应的表面重力和霍金温度。这种机制为实现极化子黑洞和研究玻色-爱因斯坦量子液体中的模拟引力效应（包括类霍金发射）开辟了一条新途径。
