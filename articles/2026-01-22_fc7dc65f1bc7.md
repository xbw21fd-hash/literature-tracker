## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14564v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Convection is ubiquitous in stellar and planetary interiors where it likely plays an integral role in the generation of magnetic fields. As the interiors of these objects remain hidden from direct observation, numerical models of convection are an important tool in the study of astrophysical dynamos. In such models, unrealistic large values of the viscous ($\nu$) and thermal ($\kappa$) diffusivity are routinely used as an ad-hoc representation of the effects of subgrid scale turbulence which is otherwise too small-scale to resolve numerically. However, the functional forms of these diffusion coefficients can vary greatly between studies, complicating efforts to compare between results and against observations. We explore this issue by considering a series of non-rotating, non-magnetic, solar-like convection models with varying radial functions for the diffusivities and differing boundary conditions. We find that the bulk kinetic energy scales similarly regardless of the diffusivity parameterization. This scaling is consistent with a free-fall scaling, wherein viscosity plays a subdominant role in the force balance. We do not, however, observe such diffusion-free behavior in the convective heat transport. Our results also indicate that the functional form adopted for the diffusion coefficients can impact the distribution of turbulence within the convective shell. These results suggest that some care should be taken when comparing solar convection models directly against helioseismic observations.

arXiv:2601.14564v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：对流在恒星和行星内部普遍存在，它可能在磁场的产生中发挥着不可或缺的作用。由于这些物体的内部仍然无法直接观察，对流数值模型是研究天体物理发电机的重要工具。在此类模型中，不切实际的粘性扩散率 ($\nu$) 和热扩散率 ($\kappa$) 扩散率通常用作亚网格尺度湍流影响的临时表示，否则其规模太小而无法进行数值解析。然而，这些扩散系数的函数形式在不同研究之间可能存在很大差异，使得结果之间和与观察结果之间的比较变得复杂化。我们通过考虑一系列非旋转、非磁性、类太阳对流模型来探讨这个问题，这些模型具有不同的扩散率径向函数和不同的边界条件。我们发现，无论扩散率参数化如何，体动能的比例都相似。这种缩放与自由落体缩放一致，其中粘度在力平衡中起次要作用。然而，我们没有在对流热传输中观察到这种无扩散行为。我们的结果还表明，扩散系数采用的函数形式可以影响对流壳内湍流的分布。这些结果表明，在将太阳对流模型直接与日震观测进行比较时应小心谨慎。
