## Abstract
arXiv:2509.25423v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Electropolishing is the premier surface preparation method for high-Q, high-gradient superconducting RF cavities made of Nb. This leaves behind an apparently smooth surface, yet the achievable peak magnetic fields fall well below the superheating field of Nb, in most cases. In this work, the ultimate surface finish of electropolishing was investigated by studying its effect on highly polished Nb samples. Electropolishing introduces high slope angle sloped-steps at grain boundaries. The magnetic field enhancement and superheating field suppression factors associated with such a geometry are calculated in the London theory. Despite the by-eye smoothness of electropolished Nb, such defects compromise the stability of the low-loss Meissner state, likely limiting the achievable peak accelerating fields in superconducting RF cavities. Finally, the impact of surface roughness on impurity diffusion is investigated which can link surface roughness to the effectiveness of heat treatments like low-temperature baking or nitrogen infusion in the vortex nucleation or hydride hypotheses. Surface roughness tends to decrease the effective dose of impurities as a result of the expansion of impurities into regions with greater internal angle. The effective dose of impurities can be protected by minimizing slope angles and step heights, ensuring uniformity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.25423v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：电解抛光是用于铌制成的高 Q、高梯度超导射频腔的首要表面处理方法。这留下了明显光滑的表面，但在大多数情况下，可实现的峰值磁场远低于铌的过热场。在这项工作中，通过研究电解抛光对高度抛光的铌样品的影响，研究了电解抛光的最终表面光洁度。电解抛光在晶界处引入了高倾斜角的倾斜台阶。与这种几何形状相关的磁场增强和过热场抑制因子在伦敦理论中计算。尽管电抛光铌的肉眼观察很光滑，但此类缺陷损害了低损耗迈斯纳态的稳定性，可能限制了超导射频腔中可实现的峰值加速场。最后，研究了表面粗糙度对杂质扩散的影响，这可以将表面粗糙度与涡旋成核或氢化物假设中的低温烘烤或氮气注入等热处理的有效性联系起来。由于杂质扩展到具有较大内角的区域，表面粗糙度往往会降低杂质的有效剂量。可以通过最小化倾斜角度和台阶高度来保护杂质的有效剂量，确保均匀性。
