## Abstract
arXiv:2512.17415v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Altermagnetism (AM) has brought renewed attention to the Lieb lattice. Here, we broaden the scope of altermagnetic models on the Lieb lattice by using a general scheme based on spin clusters. We design various altermagnetic models with d- and g-wave on the Lieb lattice, and investigate its interplay with spin-orbit coupling. While the altermagnetic unit cell reconstructs the topological edge states in the strip geometry and leads to the emergence of Dirac points, the in-plane magnetic moments of AM can induce gaps at these points. In an open square geometry, corner modes emerge within these gaps, realizing higher-order topological states. We further verify that the induction of higher-order topology is applicable to all altermagnetic configurations constructed here on the Lieb lattice, and is most pronounced for AM by comparing with the other types of magnetism such as ferromagnetism and ferrimagnetism. Our results highlight the exotic properties of AM, and suggest its potential applications in engineering topological quantum states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.17415v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：Altermagnetism (AM) 重新引起了人们对 Lieb 晶格的关注。在这里，我们通过使用基于自旋簇的通用方案扩大了利布晶格上交变磁模型的范围。我们在 Lieb 晶格上设计了各种具有 d 波和 g 波的交变磁模型，并研究了其与自旋轨道耦合的相互作用。虽然交变磁晶胞重建了条带几何形状中的拓扑边缘态并导致狄拉克点的出现，但增材制造的面内磁矩可以在这些点处产生间隙。在开放的方形几何结构中，角模式出现在这些间隙中，实现高阶拓扑状态。我们进一步验证了高阶拓扑的归纳适用于在 Lieb 晶格上构建的所有交变磁配置，并且通过与其他类型的磁性（例如铁磁性和亚铁磁性）进行比较，对于 AM 最为明显。我们的结果突出了增材制造的奇异特性，并提出了其在工程拓扑量子态中的潜在应用。
