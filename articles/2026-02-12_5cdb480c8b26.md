## Abstract
The symmetry breaking density waves and their interplay with spin and charge are crucial for understanding the correlated electronic states in quantum materials. However, symmetry-breaking density waves involving simultaneous charge and spin modulations remain largely unexplored. Here, we report the unconventional spin-intertwined charge density wave in magnetic kagome metal GdTi3Bi4by scanning tunneling spectroscopy. We observe a 3Q charge density wave that is incommensurate with the crystalline lattice in both periodicity and orientation and breaks all mirror and rotation symmetries. The CDW undergoes incommensurate-commensurate transitions in a magnetic field and 3Q-1Q transitions as a function of applied magnetic field and temperature. Remarkably, the quantum and thermal melting of the CDW exhibits a phase structure closely resembling the bulk magnetization phase diagram of GdTi3Bi4, which provides evidence for the observation and manipulation of spin-intertwined CDW as a bulk property. Our findings reveal the microscopic textures of the intricate magnetic phases with unconventional charge-spin coupling, and offer insights into a broad class of multi-components density wave formation in kagome and other correlated quantum materials.

## 摘要（中文）
对称破缺密度波及其与自旋和电荷的相互作用对于理解量子材料中的相关电子态至关重要。然而，涉及同时电荷和自旋调制的对称破缺密度波在很大程度上仍未被探索。在这里，我们通过扫描隧道光谱报告了磁性 kagome 金属 GdTi3Bi4 中非常规自旋交织的电荷密度波。我们观察到 3Q 电荷密度波，它在周期性和方向上与晶格不相称，并且打破了所有镜像对称和旋转对称。 CDW 在磁场中经历不相称-相称转变以及作为施加磁场和温度的函数的 3Q-1Q 转变。值得注意的是，CDW的量子和热熔化表现出与GdTi3Bi4的体磁化相图非常相似的相结构，这为观察和操纵自旋交织的CDW作为体特性提供了证据。我们的研究结果揭示了具有非常规电荷自旋耦合的复杂磁相的微观结构，并为 kagome 和其他相关量子材料中广泛的多组分密度波形成提供了见解。
