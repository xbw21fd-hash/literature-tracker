## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04363v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Fast Phase Logic (FPL) is a novel digital superconductor electronic (SCE) logic family specifically designed to address critical challenges in state-of-the-art SCE, such as low device density and integration levels. The FPL family improves circuit performance by employing various Josephson junction (JJ) structures, including high-$J_c$ self-shunted 0-JJ stacks, $\pi$-JJs, and 0/$\pi$-JJ stacks. FPL utilizes 0- and $\pi$-JJs to replace the bulky geometric inductors required in single flux quantum (SFQ) logic families like RSFQ. The proposed FPL family can deliver up to two orders of magnitude improvement in integration density over RSFQ logic with a five-fold reduction in the bias current requirements. Circuit performance is enhanced with reduced latency and increased throughput. Furthermore, the FPL family provides a higher output voltage level and higher impedance, which better match those of CMOS circuits. The much smaller flux storage loops in FPL greatly reduce susceptibility to trapped flux and crosstalk. Advancements in fabrication processes that would further benefit FPL implementation include the use of NbTiN-based JJs with higher critical current density and fabrication temperature range up to 400~$^\circ$C, or the use of stacked JJ structures. The resulting increased density makes very large-scale integration (VLSI) more practical. The FPL family has the potential to significantly advance SCE technology. Near-term applications are envisioned in accelerator cores for signal processing and artificial intelligence, with long-term potential in supercomputing applications. The advantages of FPL were demonstrated through an architectural study of a fast Fourier transform (FFT) circuit, comparing it with CMOS and SFQ technologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04363v1 公告类型：新 摘要：快相逻辑 (FPL) 是一种新颖的数字超导电子 (SCE) 逻辑系列，专门设计用于解决最先进的 SCE 中的关键挑战，例如低器件密度和集成水平。 FPL 系列通过采用各种约瑟夫森结 (JJ) 结构（包括高 $J_c$ 自分流 0-JJ 堆栈、$\pi$-JJ 和 0/$\pi$-JJ 堆栈）来提高电路性能。 FPL 利用 0- 和 $\pi$-JJ 来取代单通量量子 (SFQ) 逻辑系列（如 RSFQ）所需的笨重几何电感器。所提出的 FPL 系列可将集成密度比 RSFQ 逻辑提高两个数量级，同时将偏置电流要求降低五倍。电路性能随着延迟的减少和吞吐量的增加而增强。此外，FPL系列提供更高的输出电压电平和更高的阻抗，更好地匹配CMOS电路。 FPL 中更小的磁通存储环大大降低了对截留磁通和串扰的敏感性。制造工艺的进步将进一步有利于 FPL 的实施，包括使用具有更高临界电流密度和高达 400~$^\circ$C 的制造温度范围的 NbTiN 基 JJ，或使用堆叠 JJ 结构。由此产生的密度增加使得超大规模集成（VLSI）更加实用。 FPL 系列有潜力显着推进 SCE 技术。预计近期应用将用于信号处理和人工智能的加速器核心，而长期应用将应用于超级计算应用。通过对快速傅里叶变换 (FFT) 电路的架构研究，并将其与 CMOS 和 SFQ 技术进行比较，证明了 FPL 的优势。
