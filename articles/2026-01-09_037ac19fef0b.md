## Abstract
arXiv:2405.04130v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Multi-mode vortex light is a superposition of different orbital angular momentum (OAM) lights. However, as the number of OAM modes increases, the sampling constant changes. Using the traditional sparsely sampling will lead to severe loss of detail, reduced image resolution. To achieve high capacity and resolution of the OAM hologram, this paper prepares a multi-mode Bessel-Gaussian (MBG) selected hologram by stacking different mode combinations of BG phases on a MBG saved hologram in stages. Using a MBG beam with opposite combination parameters to illuminate the MBG OAM hologram, the target image can be reconstructed after the Fourier transform, and the sampling constant is flexible and controllable. The holograms encode MBG mode combination parameters. The additional degree of freedom provided by combining with MBG OAM beam offers more multiplexing channels and a higher security hologram. To further improve the quality of holograms, we first save the holograms and the corresponding MBG mode combination parameters when the quality of the obtained hologram is high based on Actor-Critic neural networks. Secondly, we gradually adjust the MBG mode combination parameters. Finally, we confirm the reasonable range of the MBG mode combination parameters.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2405.04130v2 发布类型：替换 摘要：多模涡旋光是不同轨道角动量（OAM）光的叠加。然而，随着OAM模式数量的增加，采样常数会发生变化。使用传统的稀疏采样会导致细节严重丢失，图像分辨率降低。为了实现OAM全息图的高容量和分辨率，本文通过在MBG保存的全息图上分阶段堆叠BG相位的不同模式组合来制备多模式贝塞尔高斯（MBG）选择全息图。采用相反组合参数的MBG光束照射MBG OAM全息图，经过傅里叶变换后即可重建目标图像，且采样常数灵活可控。全息图对 MBG 模式组合参数进行编码。与 MBG OAM 光束结合提供的额外自由度提供了更多的复用通道和更高的安全性全息图。为了进一步提高全息图的质量，我们首先基于Actor-Critic神经网络保存全息图以及当获得的全息图质量较高时相应的MBG模式组合参数。其次，我们逐步调整MBG模式组合参数。最后，我们确定了MBG模式组合参数的合理范围。
