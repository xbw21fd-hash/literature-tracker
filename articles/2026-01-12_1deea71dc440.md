## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05469v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Purpose: Pushing MRI speed further demands more spatially-encoded information captured per unit time, e.g., by superimposing additional field modulations during oversampled readout. However, this can introduce calibration errors and increase reconstruction time. Thus, we propose a continuous field calibration approach and an efficient k-space reconstruction technique. Theory and Methods: Our auto-calibration generalizes GRAPPA kernels to explicitly extract continuous B0 modulation kernels, solving interpolation relationships between two ACS regions differing only in the extra field modulation. The k-space locations sharing the same instantaneous image-space modulation are grouped, so that subsets of time-invariant kernels can be separately estimated, as a generalized solution for Wave-CAIPI/FRONSAC-type scans. This view further inspires a k-space subregion-wise reconstruction technique, as an efficient alternative to conventional hybrid-space reconstruction. At 9.4T, FLASH accelerated by a local B0 coil array and Wave-CAIPI were tested with retrospective undersampling. Results: Artifact-free images were reconstructed, under diverse rapid B0 modulation schemes, reaching maximum acceleration factors of 8-fold in 2D and 14.6-fold in 3D. Some nonlinear gradients modulation schemes reach similar sampling efficiency as linear gradients modulation. The proposed reconstruction shows potentials in reducing reconstruction time. Conclusion: Rapid B0 modulations and widely-adopted parallel imaging can share a common mathematical framework, and consequently, achieve similarly-robust reconstructions. Specifically, for scans using dynamic B0 and static RF kernels, not only signal encoding, but also auto-calibration and reconstruction can be performed in k-space. This paves the way to robustly remove eddy currents, and explore more complex B0 modulation strategies towards ultimate MRI speed.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05469v1 公告类型：新 摘要：目的：推动 MRI 速度进一步需要每单位时间捕获更多的空间编码信息，例如，通过在过采样读出期间叠加额外的场调制。然而，这可能会引入校准误差并增加重建时间。因此，我们提出了一种连续场校准方法和有效的 k 空间重建技术。理论和方法：我们的自动校准概括了 GRAPPA 内核，以显式提取连续 B0 调制内核，解决仅在额外场调制方面不同的两个 ACS 区域之间的插值关系。共享相同瞬时图像空间调制的 k 空间位置被分组，以便可以单独估计时不变核的子集，作为 Wave-CAIPI/FRONSAC 型扫描的通用解决方案。这一观点进一步激发了 k 空间子区域重建技术，作为传统混合空间重建的有效替代方案。在 9.4T 时，通过局部 B0 线圈阵列加速 FLASH，并通过回顾性欠采样测试了 Wave-CAIPI。结果：在多种快速 B0 调制方案下重建了无伪影图像，在 2D 中达到 8 倍，在 3D 中达到 14.6 倍的最大加速因子。一些非线性梯度调制方案达到与线性梯度调制相似的采样效率。所提出的重建显示出减少重建时间的潜力。结论：快速 B0 调制和广泛采用的并行成像可以共享一个共同的数学框架，从而实现同样稳健的重建。具体来说，对于使用动态B0和静态RF内核的扫描，不仅可以在k空间中执行信号编码，还可以执行自动校准和重建。这为强力消除涡流以及探索更复杂的 B0 调制策略以实现最终 MRI 速度铺平了道路。
