## Abstract
arXiv:2511.02218v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Deterministic quasiperiodicity in quantum systems has long been associated with localization, criticality, or glassy behavior, and has therefore been believed to suppress long-range order rather than stabilize it. Here we demonstrate the opposite: quasiperiodicity in interactions--without any quasiperiodic potential, disorder, or geometric modulation--can generate coherent, ordered quantum phases. We study hard-core bosons in one dimension with quasiperiodic long-range interactions, V_{ij}=V_0 \cos(\pi \alpha i)\cos(\pi \alpha j), where n=\alpha=(\sqrt{5}-1)/2 is the inverse golden ratio. Using large-scale path-integral quantum Monte Carlo simulations, we uncover thermodynamically stable incompressible plateaus at irrational densities tied to Fibonacci ratios. These plateaus exhibit sharp incommensurate Bragg peaks, signaling an emergent quasi-solid with long-range quasicrystalline density order. More strikingly, at nearby fillings and interaction strengths, we identify a quasi-supersolid phase that supports both Fibonacci density ordering and finite superfluid density--demonstrating that interaction-induced quasiperiodicity can stabilize supersolid coherence. Our results establish a new mechanism for realizing ordered quasicrystalline quantum matter, and provide realistic guidance for implementation in Rydberg atom arrays, multimode cavity-QED systems, and trapped-ion quantum simulators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.02218v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：量子系统中的确定性准周期性长期以来一直与局域化、临界性或玻璃行为相关，因此被认为会抑制长程有序而不是稳定它。在这里，我们证明了相反的情况：相互作用中的准周期性——没有任何准周期势、无序或几何调制——可以产生相干、有序的量子相。我们研究一维中具有准周期长程相互作用的硬核玻色子，V_{ij}=V_0 \cos(\pi \alpha i)\cos(\pi \alpha j)，其中n=\alpha=(\sqrt{5}-1)/2 是逆黄金比例。使用大规模路径积分量子蒙特卡罗模拟，我们发现了与斐波那契比率相关的非理性密度下的热力学稳定的不可压缩平台。这些平台表现出尖锐的不相称的布拉格峰，表明出现了具有长程准晶密度有序的准固体。更引人注目的是，在附近的填充和相互作用强度下，我们发现了一种支持斐波那契密度排序和有限超流体密度的准超固体相，这表明相互作用引起的准周期性可以稳定超固体相干性。我们的研究结果建立了一种实现有序准晶量子物质的新机制，并为里德堡原子阵列、多模腔 QED 系统和俘获离子量子模拟器的实现提供了现实指导。
