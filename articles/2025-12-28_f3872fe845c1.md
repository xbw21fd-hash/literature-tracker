## Abstract
Recycling and recovery of materials such as rare earth elements (REEs) required for the manufacturing of essential devices such as computers, magnets, and batteries is quickly becoming a major research focus. Primary REEs resources are located only in few countries, causing a high supply risk. However, large amounts of REEs could be recovered or from secondary sources. Compared to other separation technologies, capacitive deionization (CDI) has some energetic advantages by not requiring high-pressure pumps and heat sources and does not involve the use of costly and polluting chemicals. Therefore, CDI could be applied to REEs recovery from secondary water sources provided that selective electrode materials would be developed. In this work we applied carbon materials derived from a zeolitic imidazolate frameworks (ZIF-8) to the selective adsorption of lanthanide ions (i.e., Nd3+) in presence of other competing cations (Na+, K+, Mg2+, Ca2+). By investigating the effect of different materials and process variables on the performance of the selective adsorption via CDI, and comparing materials’ properties with performance, we demonstrate the potential of ZIF-8 derived carbon materials to be used for recovery of critical minerals, pointing out the need for future developments.

制造计算机、磁铁和电池等基本设备所需的稀土元素 (REE) 等材料的回收和回收正迅速成为主要研究焦点。原生稀土资源仅分布在少数国家，供应风险较高。然而，大量的稀土元素可以被回收或从二次来源中回收。与其他分离技术相比，电容去离子（CDI）具有一些能源优势，因为不需要高压泵和热源，并且不涉及使用昂贵且污染性的化学品。因此，只要开发出选择性电极材料，CDI就可以应用于从二次水源中回收稀土元素。在这项工作中，我们应用源自沸石咪唑酯骨架（ZIF-8）的碳材料在其他竞争性阳离子（Na+、K+、Mg2+、Ca2+）存在下选择性吸附镧系离子（即 Nd3+）。通过研究不同材料和工艺变量对 CDI 选择性吸附性能的影响，并将材料的特性与性能进行比较，我们证明了 ZIF-8 衍生碳材料用于回收关键矿物的潜力，指出了未来发展的必要性。
