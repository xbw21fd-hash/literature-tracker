## Abstract
arXiv:2507.10370v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: One-dimensional (1D) chains offer unique opportunities for nanoelectronics and spintronics, yet their experimental realization remains challenging because 1D motifs are often thermodynamically disfavored relative to higher-dimensional phases. Here we present a high-throughput first-principles exploration of 1D single-atomic transition-metal chalcogenide and halide chains, screening 6,832 candidates constructed from binary combinations of 28 metals and 8 non-metals. To assess kinetic accessibility, we compare the formation energetics of 1D chains with competing two-dimensional polymorphs at the nucleation stage across relevant chemical-potential windows, using nucleation-stage thermodynamic selectivity as a proxy. This workflow identifies 183 kinetically accessible 1D chains. Interpretable machine-learning analysis reveals two simple stability descriptors as key drivers of 1D stabilization. The accessible chains exhibit diverse magnetic configurations with different magnetic characters. We further uncover their pronounced magnetoelastic couplings, exemplified by CrTe with giant magnetostriction reaching 5.93%. Finally, we show that selected metallic ferromagnetic chains retain robust edge magnetism on superconducting substrates, laying the groundwork for proximity-induced topological superconductivity and Majorana zero modes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.10370v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：一维 (1D) 链为纳米电子学和自旋电子学提供了独特的机会，但其实验实现仍然具有挑战性，因为相对于高维相，一维基序通常在热力学上不受欢迎。在这里，我们提出了对一维单原子过渡金属硫族化物和卤化物链的高通量第一原理探索，筛选了由 28 种金属和 8 种非金属的二元组合构建的 6,832 个候选物。为了评估动力学可达性，我们使用成核阶段热力学选择性作为代理，在相关化学势窗口中比较一维链与竞争性二维多晶型物的形成能量。此工作流程识别了 183 个动力学上可访问的一维链。可解释的机器学习分析揭示了两个简单的稳定性描述符作为一维稳定性的关键驱动因素。可接近的链表现出具有不同磁性特征的多种磁性构型。我们进一步揭示了它们显着的磁弹性耦合，以 CrTe 为例，其巨磁致伸缩达到 5.93%。最后，我们表明，选定的金属铁磁链在超导基底上保留了强大的边缘磁性，为邻近感应拓扑超导和马约拉纳零模式奠定了基础。
