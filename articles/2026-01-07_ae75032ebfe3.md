## Abstract
High-resolution (HR) sea surface temperature (SST) is crucial for understanding ocean dynamics, climate variability, and nearshore ecosystems. While demand for fine-scale SST data in coastal regions grows, HR observations remain limited, and numerical modeling is constrained by prohibitive computational costs. Efficient and accurate downscaling approaches are therefore essential, and recent advances in artificial intelligence (AI) offer promising alternatives. However, current AI-based methods often use artificially degraded low-resolution (LR) data, overlooking the systematic mismatches where LR simulations misrepresent complex, fine-scale ocean features. This study employed a convolutional block attention module-enhanced UNet model trained on realistic LR-HR data pairs from the three-layer nested regional ocean model system (ROMS) simulations to enhance practical reliability. The LR multi-mode inputs include SST and sea surface currents, which help encode essential physical ocean processes. Compared with bilinear interpolation and a traditional UNet model using SST-only input, the proposed model reduced root mean square error by 21.93% and achieved a spatial Pearson correlation coefficient (R) of 0.86. In addition, interpretability analysis revealed the contribution of each input channel, aligning well with temperature transport and seasonal variability, and confirming the underlying natural physical constraints learned from real-world data. Beyond pixel-value precision, the physically interpretable behavior of the multi-mode downscaling method demonstrates its capability to reconstruct accurate and dynamically consistent HR SST fields, which is vital for operational applications.

## 摘要（中文）
高分辨率 (HR) 海面温度 (SST) 对于了解海洋动力学、气候变化和近岸生态系统至关重要。虽然沿海地区对精细海表温度数据的需求不断增长，但 HR 观测仍然有限，数值模型也受到计算成本过高的限制。因此，高效、准确的降尺度方法至关重要，而人工智能 (AI) 的最新进展提供了有希望的替代方案。然而，当前基于人工智能的方法经常使用人工降级的低分辨率（LR）数据，忽略了系统失配，其中LR模拟错误地表示了复杂的、精细尺度的海洋特征。本研究采用了卷积块注意模块增强型 UNet 模型，该模型在来自三层嵌套区域海洋模型系统 (ROMS) 模拟的真实 LR-HR 数据对上进行训练，以提高实际可靠性。 LR 多模式输入包括海温和海表流，有助于对重要的物理海洋过程进行编码。与双线性插值和仅使用 SST 输入的传统 UNet 模型相比，该模型将均方根误差降低了 21.93%，空间皮尔逊相关系数 (R) 为 0.86。此外，可解释性分析揭示了每个输入通道的贡献，与温度传输和季节变化很好地吻合，并确认了从现实世界数据中学到的潜在自然物理约束。除了像素值精度之外，多模式降尺度方法的物理可解释行为证明了其重建准确且动态一致的 HR SST 场的能力，这对于操作应用至关重要。
