## Abstract
arXiv:2502.14782v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Coastal regions and river floodplains are particularly vulnerable to the impacts of extreme weather events. Accurate real-time forecasting of hydrodynamic processes in these areas is essential for infrastructure planning and climate adaptation. Yet high-fidelity numerical models are often too computationally expensive for real-time use, and lower-cost approaches, such as traditional model order reduction algorithms or conventional neural networks, typically struggle to generalize to out-of-distribution conditions. In this study, we present the Multiple-Input Temporal Operator Network (MITONet), a novel autoregressive neural emulator that employs latent-space operator learning to efficiently approximate high-dimensional numerical solvers for complex, nonlinear problems that are governed by time-dependent, parameterized partial differential equations. We showcase MITONet's predictive capabilities by forecasting regional tide-driven dynamics in the Shinnecock Inlet in New York and riverine flow in a section of the Red River in Louisiana, both described by the two-dimensional shallow-water equations (2D SWE), while incorporating initial conditions, time-varying boundary conditions, and domain parameters such as the bottom friction coefficient. Despite the distinct flow regimes, the complex geometries and meshes, and the wide range of bottom friction coefficients studied, MITONet displays consistently high predictive skill, with anomaly correlation coefficients above 0.9, a maximum normalized root mean square error of 0.011, and computational speedups between 100x-1,250x, even for 175 days of autoregressive rollout forecast from random initial conditions and with unseen parameter values.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2502.14782v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：沿海地区和河漫滩特别容易受到极端天气事件的影响。准确实时预测这些地区的水动力过程对于基础设施规划和气候适应至关重要。然而，高保真数值模型对于实时使用而言通常计算成本过高，而成本较低的方法（例如传统模型降阶算法或传统神经网络）通常难以推广到分布外条件。在这项研究中，我们提出了多输入时间算子网络（MITONEt），这是一种新颖的自回归神经模拟器，它采用潜在空间算子学习来有效地近似高维数值求解器，以解决由时间相关的参数化偏微分方程控制的复杂非线性问题。我们通过预测纽约辛纳科克湾的区域潮汐驱动动态和路易斯安那州红河一段的河流流量来展示 MITONNet 的预测能力，两者均由二维浅水方程 (2D SWE) 描述，同时结合初始条件、时变边界条件和域参数（例如底部摩擦系数）。尽管研究的流态不同、几何形状和网格复杂、底部摩擦系数范围广泛，但 MITONet 始终表现出较高的预测能力，异常相关系数高于 0.9，最大归一化均方根误差为 0.011，计算加速在 100 倍到 1,250 倍之间，即使是从随机初始条件和未知参数值进行 175 天的自回归预测。
