## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18082v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Drug-drug interactions (DDIs) strongly affect the safety and efficacy of combination therapies. Despite the availability of large DDI databases, selecting optimal multi-drug combinations that balance safety, therapeutic benefit, and regimen size remains a challenging combinatorial optimization problem. Here, we present a quantum-control-based framework for DDI-aware drug combination optimization, in which known harmful and synergistic interactions are encoded into Ising Hamiltonians as penalties and rewards, respectively. The optimization is performed using the feedback-based quantum algorithm FALQON, a gradient-free variational approach. We study two clinically motivated tasks: the Maximum Safe Subset problem and the Synergy-Constrained Optimization problem. Numerical simulations using interaction data from Drugs.com and SYNERGxDB demonstrate efficient convergence and high-quality solutions for clinically relevant drug sets, including COVID-19 case studies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18082v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：药物相互作用（DDI）强烈影响联合疗法的安全性和有效性。尽管有大型 DDI 数据库，但选择平衡安全性、治疗效果和治疗方案规模的最佳多药物组合仍然是一个具有挑战性的组合优化问题。在这里，我们提出了一个基于量子控制的 DDI 感知药物组合优化框架，其中已知的有害和协同相互作用分别被编码为伊辛哈密顿量作为惩罚和奖励。使用基于反馈的量子算法 FALQON（一种无梯度变分方法）执行优化。我们研究两个临床动机的任务：最大安全子集问题和协同约束优化问题。使用 Drugs.com 和 SYNERGxDB 的交互数据进行的数值模拟证明了临床相关药物组（包括 COVID-19 案例研究）的高效收敛和高质量解决方案。
