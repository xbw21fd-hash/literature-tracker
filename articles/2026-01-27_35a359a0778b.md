## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18443v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose an enhanced wall-boundary treatment for the lattice Boltzmann method (LBM), designed for high-Reynolds-number turbulent flows on adaptively refined Cartesian grids. The method improves the slip-velocity bounce-back scheme by coupling it with a near-wall turbulence model based on an analytical wall function. The Spalart-Allmaras (negative) turbulence model is solved using a second-order finite-difference scheme and integrated within the LBM framework to statistically represent the Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes (RANS) equations (LBM-RANS). The approach is validated on two benchmark configurations: the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA) 0012 airfoil and the McDonnell Douglas (MD)-30P30N multi-element high-lift configuration. LBM-RANS results show good agreement with conventional finite-volume RANS solutions and experimental data for key aerodynamic quantities, including pressure and skin-friction distributions, as well as turbulent boundary layer velocity profiles and eddy-viscosity fields. The method delivers smooth and accurate predictions of skin friction, which are often challenging for immersed-boundary approaches on Cartesian grids. The auxiliary geometric data required for enforcing the turbulent boundary condition are minimal, making the method potentially well-suited for graphics processing unit (GPU)-based implementations. Moreover, no ad-hoc near-wall treatments are needed, as the boundary condition is applied naturally via the link-wise bounce-back scheme. These results illustrate that the proposed LBM-RANS framework can robustly and accurately simulate high-Reynolds-number turbulent two-dimensional (2D) flows over complex aerodynamic geometries under equilibrium or near-equilibrium conditions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18443v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种针对格子玻尔兹曼方法 (LBM) 的增强型壁边界处理方法，该方法专为自适应细化笛卡尔网格上的高雷诺数湍流而设计。该方法通过将滑移速度反弹方案与基于解析壁函数的近壁湍流模型耦合来改进滑移速度反弹方案。 Spalart-Allmaras（负）湍流模型使用二阶有限差分格式求解，并集成在 LBM 框架内，以统计方式表示雷诺平均纳维-斯托克斯 (RANS) 方程 (LBM-RANS)。该方法在两个基准配置上进行了验证：国家航空咨询委员会 (NACA) 0012 翼型和麦道 (MD)-30P30N 多元件高升力配置。 LBM-RANS 结果显示与传统有限体积 RANS 解和关键空气动力学量的实验数据非常一致，包括压力和表面摩擦分布，以及湍流边界层速度剖面和涡粘性场。该方法可以平滑、准确地预测皮肤摩擦力，这对于笛卡尔网格上的浸入式边界方法通常具有挑战性。强制执行湍流边界条件所需的辅助几何数据很少，因此该方法可能非常适合基于图形处理单元 (GPU) 的实现。此外，不需要特别的近壁处理，因为边界条件是通过链接方式的反弹方案自然应用的。这些结果表明，所提出的 LBM-RANS 框架可以稳健且准确地模拟平衡或接近平衡条件下复杂气动几何形状上的高雷诺数湍流二维 (2D) 流。
