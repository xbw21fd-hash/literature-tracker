## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00649v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a systematic theoretical and numerical investigation of the propagation properties of linear magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) waves in a spatially periodic magnetic field, referred to as a magneto-lattice. Two types of central equations, expressed in terms of $\left(\rho,\boldsymbol{B},\boldsymbol{v}\right)$ (where $\rho$ is perturbed mass density, $\boldsymbol{B}$ is perturbed magnetic field, and $\boldsymbol{v}$ is perturbed velocity) and the perturbation displacement $\boldsymbol{\xi}$, are established using the plane wave expansion (PWE) method. The validity of both equations is demonstrated through two numerical examples. This framework enables the identification of intrinsic frequency bandgaps and cutoff phenomena within the system. Our numerical results show that the bandgap width increases with the magnetic modulation ratio $B_{m}$, leading to the suppression of specific MHD wave modes. Furthermore, the periodicity of the magnetic field induces the splitting of Alfv\'{e}n waves into multiple branches\textemdash a phenomenon absent in uniform plasmas. These findings provide new insights for manipulating MHD waves in a crystalline lattice framework of structured plasmas.

arXiv:2602.00649v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们对空间周期性磁场（称为磁晶格）中线性磁流体动力（MHD）波的传播特性进行了系统的理论和数值研究。使用平面波展开建立两种类型的中心方程，以 $\left(\rho,\boldsymbol{B},\boldsymbol{v}\right)$ 表示（其中 $\rho$ 是扰动质量密度，$\boldsymbol{B}$ 是扰动磁场，$\boldsymbol{v}$ 是扰动速度）和扰动位移 $\boldsymbol{\xi}$ （PWE）方法。通过两个数值例子证明了两个方程的有效性。该框架能够识别系统内的固有频带隙和截止现象。我们的数值结果表明，带隙宽度随着磁调制比 $B_{m}$ 的增加而增加，从而抑制特定的 MHD 波模式。此外，磁场的周期性导致 Alfv\'{e}n 波分裂成多个分支\textemdash，这是均匀等离子体中不存在的现象。这些发现为在结构化等离子体的晶格框架中操纵 MHD 波提供了新的见解。
