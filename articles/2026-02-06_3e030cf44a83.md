## Abstract
Author(s): Mohit Kumar Jat, Kenji Watanabe, Takashi Taniguchi, and Aveek Bid Here, the authors show that a moiré superlattice in bilayer graphene aligned with hexagonal boron nitride drives a topological reconstruction of its electronic bands. Magnetotransport reveals the emergence of massless Dirac fermions in moiré minibands, coexisting with the parent bilayer’s massive carriers. The work highlights moiré engineering as a simple and versatile route to tunable band topology. [Phys. Rev. B 113, L081102] Published Fri Feb 06, 2026
