## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16246v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The historical and conceptual foundations of General Relativity are revisited, putting the main focus on the physical meaning of the invariant ds, the Equivalence Principle, and the precise interpretation of spacetime geometry. It is argued that Albert Einstein initially sought a dynamical formulation in which ds encoded the gravitational effects, without invoking curvature as a physical entity. The now more familiar geometrical interpretation (identifying gravitation with spacetime curvature) gradually emerged through his collaboration with Marcel Grossmann and the adoption of the Ricci tensor in 1915. Anyhow, in his 1920 Leiden lecture, Einstein explicitly reinterpreted spacetime geometry as the state of a physical medium (an ether endowed with metrical properties but devoid of mechanical substance) thereby actually rejecting geometry as an independent ontological reality. Building upon this mature view, gravitation is reconstructed from the Weak Equivalence Principle, understood as the exact compensation between inertial and gravitational forces acting on a body under a uniform gravitational field. From this fundamental principle, together with an extension of Fermat Principle to massive objects, the invariant ds is obtained, first in the static case, where the gravitational potential modifies the flow of proper time. Then, by applying the Lorentz transformation to this static invariant, its general form is derived for the case of matter in motion. The resulting invariant reproduces the relativistic form of Newton second law in proper time and coincides with the weak field limit of General Relativity in the harmonic gauge.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16246v1 发布类型：新 摘要：重新审视广义相对论的历史和概念基础，重点关注不变量 ds 的物理意义、等效原理以及时空几何的精确解释。有人认为，阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦最初寻求一种动力学公式，其中 ds 编码了引力效应，而不将曲率作为物理实体。现在更熟悉的几何解释（将引力与时空曲率等同）是通过他与马塞尔·格罗斯曼的合作以及1915年采用里奇张量而逐渐出现的。无论如何，在1920年的莱顿演讲中，爱因斯坦明确地将时空几何重新解释为物理介质的状态（一种赋予度量属性但缺乏机械实体的以太），从而实际上拒绝了几何作为独立的本体论现实。基于这一成熟的观点，引力是根据弱等效原理重建的，被理解为在均匀引力场下作用在物体上的惯性力和引力之间的精确补偿。根据这一基本原理，再加上费马原理对大质量物体的推广，首先在静态情况下获得了不变量 ds ，其中引力势改变了固有时间的流动。然后，通过对这个静态不变量应用洛伦兹变换，导出了运动物质情况下的一般形式。由此产生的不变量在适当的时间再现了牛顿第二定律的相对论形式，并且与谐波规范中广义相对论的弱场极限相一致。
