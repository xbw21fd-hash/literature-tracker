## Abstract
arXiv:2408.00826v4 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In quantum systems, purification can map mixed states into pure states and a non-unitary evolution into a unitary one by enlarging the Hilbert space. We establish a connection between the complexities of mixed quantum states and their purification, proposing new inequalities among these complexities. By examining single qubits, two-qubit Werner states, eight-dimensional Gaussian random unitary ensembles, and infinite-dimensional systems, we demonstrate how these relationships manifest across a broad class of systems. We find that the spread complexity of purification of a vacuum state evolving into a thermal state equals the average number of Rindler particles. This complexity is also shown to adhere to the Lloyd-like bound, indicating a further relation to the quantum speed limit. Finally, using mutual Krylov complexity, we observe subadditivity of the Krylov complexities, which contrasts with known results from holographic volume complexity. We put forward Krylov mutual complexity as a diagnosis of a potential gravity dual of Krylov complexities.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2408.00826v4 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：在量子系统中，净化可以通过扩大希尔伯特空间将混合态映射为纯态，将非酉演化映射为酉演化。我们在混合量子态的复杂性与其纯化之间建立了联系，提出了这些复杂性之间新的不等式。通过检查单个量子位、两个量子位沃纳态、八维高斯随机酉系综和无限维系统，我们展示了这些关系如何在广泛的系统中表现出来。我们发现真空态演化为热态的净化扩散复杂度等于 Rindler 粒子的平均数量。这种复杂性也被证明遵循类似劳埃德的界限，表明与量子速度限制的进一步关系。最后，使用相互克雷洛夫复杂度，我们观察了克雷洛夫复杂度的次可加性，这与全息体积复杂度的已知结果形成对比。我们提出克雷洛夫互复杂性作为克雷洛夫复杂性的潜在引力对偶的诊断。
