## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16927v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Understanding the coupling between lattice vibrations and magnetic order is crucial for controlling properties of two-dimensional magnetic materials. Here, we investigate the vibrational properties of bulk and thick-flake CrCl$_\textrm{3}$ using polarization-resolved Raman spectroscopy, complemented by photoluminescence, photoluminescence excitation, and optical absorption measurements. Symmetry analysis, supported by first-principles phonon calculations, enables the unambiguous assignment of all eight Raman-active modes, four $\textrm{A}_\textrm{g}$ and four $\textrm{E}_\textrm{g}$, previously predicted only theoretically. Excitation-energy-dependent measurements reveal that the strong enhancement of selected phonon modes originates primarily from interference effects rather than resonant Raman scattering. Temperature-dependent Raman spectroscopy further reveals pronounced signatures of spin-phonon coupling across the transition from a fully antiferromagnetic phase, through an intermediate regime with local, domain-like ferromagnetic order, to the paramagnetic phase, accompanied by a clear rhombohedral-to-monoclinic structural transition. Together, these results demonstrate how lattice, electronic, and magnetic degrees of freedom collectively govern the Raman response of CrCl$_\textrm{3}$.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16927v1 发布类型：新 摘要：理解晶格振动与磁序之间的耦合对于控制二维磁性材料的性能至关重要。在这里，我们使用偏振分辨拉曼光谱研究块状和厚片状 CrCl$_\textrm{3}$ 的振动特性，并辅以光致发光、光致发光激发和光吸收测量。在第一性原理声子计算的支持下，对称分析可以明确分配所有八种拉曼活性模式，即四种 $\textrm{A}_\textrm{g}$ 和四种 $\textrm{E}_\textrm{g}$，而此前仅在理论上进行了预测。与激发能量相关的测量表明，所选声子模式的强烈增强主要源于干涉效应，而不是共振拉曼散射。温度依赖性拉曼光谱进一步揭示了自旋声子耦合的明显特征，从完全反铁磁相，通过具有局部域状铁磁序的中间状态，到顺磁相，伴随着清晰的菱面体到单斜结构的转变。这些结果共同证明了晶格、电子和磁自由度如何共同控制 CrCl$_\textrm{3}$ 的拉曼响应。
