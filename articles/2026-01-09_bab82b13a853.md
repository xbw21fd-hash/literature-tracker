## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04590v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Compressible direct numerical simulations of turbulent channel flows in concentric annular ducts of height $2\delta$ are performed to study the low-wavenumber wall pressure fluctuations (WPF) over cylindrical walls at a bulk Mach number $M_b = 0.4$ and bulk Reynolds number $Re_b=3000$. The radius of the inner cylinder $R$ is varied between $0.2\delta$, $\delta$, $2\delta$ and $\infty$. As $R$ decreases, the one-point power spectral density of the WPF decreases at intermediate but increases at high frequencies. When $R$ decreases, the 1D (streamwise) wavenumber-frequency spectrum of the WPF decreases at high wavenumbers. At low wavenumbers, however, as $R$ reduces to $0.2\delta$ the 1D wavenumber-frequency spectrum exhibits multiple spectral peaks whose strengths increase with frequency. Examination of the 2D wavenumber-frequency spectra shows that these represent acoustic duct modes that closely match theoretical predictions. The acoustic modes of higher radial orders exhibit increasingly high amplitude on the inner than on the outer walls. The low-wavenumber components of the $0$th-order (azimuthal) 2D wavenumber-frequency spectrum are of great importance in practice, and their magnitude increases as $R$ reduces; this increase is increasingly pronounced at higher frequencies. Analytical modelling and numerical validation show that this increase appears to arise from the ``geometric'' effects connected with the Green's function, and they are generated mainly by radial and azimuthal disturbances. Disturbances closer to the wall are shown to be increasingly important in WPF generation as $R$ reduces, which highlights a potential in WPF control using wall treatment on thin cylinders.

arXiv:2601.04590v1 公告类型：新 摘要：对高度为 $2\delta$ 的同心环形管道中的湍流通道流进行可压缩直接数值模拟，以研究在大马赫数 $M_b = 0.4$ 和大雷诺数 $Re_b=3000$ 下圆柱壁上的低波数壁压力脉动 (WPF)。内圆柱体$R$的半径在$0.2\delta$、$\delta$、$2\delta$和$\infty$之间变化。随着 $R$ 的减小，WPF 的单点功率谱密度在中频处减小，但在高频处增大。当 $R$ 减小时，WPF 的 1D（流向）波数频谱在高波数处减小。然而，在低波数下，随着 $R$ 减少到 $0.2\delta$，一维波数-频谱呈现出多个谱峰，其强度随着频率的增加而增加。对二维波数频率谱的检查表明，这些代表的声波导模式与理论预测非常吻合。较高径向阶的声模在内壁上表现出比在外壁上更高的幅度。 $0$阶（方位角）二维波数频谱的低波数分量在实践中非常重要，其幅度随着$R$的减小而增加；这种增加在更高的频率下变得越来越明显。分析模型和数值验证表明，这种增加似乎是由与格林函数相关的“几何”效应引起的，并且它们主要是由径向和方位角扰动产生的。随着 $R$ 的减少，靠近壁面的干扰在 WPF 生成中变得越来越重要，这凸显了使用薄圆柱壁处理进行 WPF 控制的潜力。
