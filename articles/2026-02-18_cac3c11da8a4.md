## Abstract
arXiv:2511.09714v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We study the real-time chiral dynamics of Dirac fermions in AdS$_2$ and AdS$_2$ black hole backgrounds. The spacetime curvature generates a spin connection term, acting as an effective magnetic field and a position-dependent chiral chemical potential. This leads to strongly asymmetric wave propagation, confined within an inhomogeneous Lieb-Robinson cone. The front velocities decrease with increasing fermion mass and horizon radius. The entanglement entropy grows inside the causal cone, and it saturates due to screening/dephasing in the finite inhomogeneous chain. In dipole-dipole collision, the central bipartite entropy rises when the inward Lieb-Robinson fronts intersect, forming a bright ridge in the local entanglement profile. Charge and current correlators peak at the front arrival, providing a real-time diagnostic of chiral transport. These results establish a causality-respecting framework, linking curvature and horizons to transport and entanglement in (1+1)-dimensional fermionic matter.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.09714v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：我们研究 AdS$_2$ 和 AdS$_2$ 黑洞背景中狄拉克费米子的实时手性动力学。时空曲率产生自旋连接项，充当有效磁场和位置相关的手性化学势。这导致强烈不对称的波传播，被限制在不均匀的利布-罗宾逊锥体内。前沿速度随着费米子质量和视界半径的增加而降低。纠缠熵在因果锥内部增长，并且由于有限非均匀链中的屏蔽/相移而饱和。在偶极子-偶极子碰撞中，当向内的利布-罗宾逊锋面相交时，中心二分熵上升，在局部纠缠剖面中形成明亮的脊。电荷和电流相关器在前沿到达处达到峰值，提供手性传输的实时诊断。这些结果建立了一个尊重因果关系的框架，将曲率和视界与 (1+1) 维费米子物质中的传输和纠缠联系起来。
