## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15066v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Digital banking and online communication have made modern bank runs faster and more networked than the canonical queue-at-the-branch setting. While equilibrium models explain why strategic complementarities generate run risk, they offer limited guidance on how beliefs synchronize and propagate in real time. We develop a process-based agent-based model that makes the information and coordination layer explicit. Banks follow cash-first withdrawal processing with discounted fire-sale liquidation and an endogenous stress index. Depositors are heterogeneous in risk tolerance and in the weight placed on fundamentals versus social information, communicating on a heavy-tailed network calibrated to Twitter activity during March 2023. Depositor behavior is generated by a constrained large language model that maps each agent's information set into a discrete action and an optional post; we validate this policy against laboratory coordination evidence and theoretical benchmarks. Across 4,900 configurations and full LLM simulations, three findings emerge. Within-bank connectivity raises the likelihood and speed of withdrawal cascades holding fundamentals fixed. Cross-bank contagion exhibits a sharp phase transition near spillover rates of 0.10. Depositor overlap and network amplification interact nonlinearly, so channels weak in isolation become powerful in combination. In an SVB, First Republic, and regional bank scenario disciplined by crisis-era data, the model reproduces the observed ordering of failures and predicts substantially higher withdrawal rates among uninsured depositors. The results frame social correlation as a measurable amplifier of run risk alongside balance-sheet fundamentals.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15066v1 公告类型：新 摘要：数字银行和在线通信使现代银行的运行速度比规范的分行排队设置更快、更网络化。虽然均衡模型解释了战略互补性产生运行风险的原因，但它们对信念如何实时同步和传播提供了有限的指导。我们开发了一个基于流程的基于代理的模型，使信息和协调层变得明确。银行遵循现金优先的提款流程，并提供折扣甩卖清算和内生压力指数。储户的风险承受能力以及基本面与社会信息的权重存在差异，并在 2023 年 3 月期间在根据 Twitter 活动校准的重尾网络上进行通信。储户行为是由受约束的大型语言模型生成的，该模型将每个代理的信息集映射为离散的操作和可选的帖子；我们根据实验室协调证据和理论基准验证这一政策。通过 4,900 种配置和完整的法学硕士模拟，得出了三项发现。银行内部的连通性提高了在基本面固定的情况下发生级联提款的可能性和速度。跨银行传染在溢出率接近 0.10 时表现出急剧的相变。储户重叠和网络放大非线性相互作用，因此孤立的弱通道在组合中变得强大。在受危机时代数据约束的SVB、第一共和国和地区银行情景中，该模型重现了观察到的倒闭顺序，并预测未投保储户的提款率将大幅提高。结果将社会相关性视为与资产负债表基本面一起可衡量的运行风险放大器。
