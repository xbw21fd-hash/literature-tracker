## Abstract
TLDR: First-principles calculations based on density functional theory are presented to investigate systematically the electronic and magnetic properties of such novel organometallics (labeled as TMPc, TM = Cr-Zn) as free-standing sheets to provide theoretical insight leading to a better understanding of novel phthalocyanine-based 2D structures beyond graphene and BN sheets.
