## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16794v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In this work, we present a general form for the electron-ion diffractive potential derived from the quantum pair density matrix and fit to the improved Kelbg potential for atomic numbers up to $Z = 54$. We apply classical molecular dynamics using the improved Kelbg potential for carbon with various forms of the Pauli potential to compute internal energies and pressures for hot, dense plasma conditions. Our results are compared to an equation of state model based on path integral Monte Carlo and density functional theory simulations to examine the extent to which the improved Kelbg potential reproduces the internal energy and pressure of carbon plasmas. The regions of validity for carbon agree generally with those derived previously for hydrogen once pressure ionization effects are incorporated. Based on our carbon results and previously published hydrogen studies, we discuss the general applicability and limitations of these potentials for equation of state studies in warm dense matter and high energy density plasmas.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16794v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在这项工作中，我们提出了从量子对密度矩阵导出的电子-离子衍射势的一般形式，并适合原子序数高达 $Z = 54$ 的改进凯尔布格势。我们应用经典分子动力学，使用改进的碳凯尔布格势和各种形式的泡利势来计算热、稠密等离子体条件下的内部能量和压力。我们的结果与基于路径积分蒙特卡罗和密度泛函理论模拟的状态方程模型进行了比较，以检查改进的凯尔布格势在多大程度上再现了碳等离子体的内能和压力。一旦考虑了压力电离效应，碳的有效区域通常与先前针对氢得出的有效区域一致。根据我们的碳结果和之前发表的氢研究，我们讨论了这些势在热致密物质和高能量密度等离子体中状态方程研究的一般适用性和局限性。
