# The effect of germanium sublayer on the native corrosion of ultrathin copper films

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24180v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: To examine the process of native corrosion of ultrathin (about 10 nm) copper films deposited on quartz glass substrates $(SiO_2)$ with and without a germanium sublayer, the time dependences of the microwave reflection coefficient $R$ and direct current electrical resistivity $\rho$ of such samples exposed to air at room temperature have been studied. Under these conditions, the thickness of the oxide layer $d$ on $Cu/SiO_2$ films was found to increase over time $t$ according to a parabolic law, which is in contradiction with the predictions of existing theories of copper oxidation. A model is proposed that explains this behavior of $d(t)$ by the diffusion of atomic oxygen along the boundaries of oxide grains towards the copper film with its subsequent oxidation. The $R$ and $\rho$ of $Cu/Ge/SiO_2$ films were found to degrade much more slowly than similar characteristics of $Cu/SiO_2$ films of the same thickness. The high corrosion resistance of $Cu/Ge/SiO_2$ films is explained by the peculiarities of $Ge$ redistribution during the growth of the copper film on a germanium sublayer. The long-term retention by $Cu/Ge/SiO_2$ films of their characteristics allows them to be recommended as a cheap replacement for gold coating in electromagnetic interference protection devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24180v1 发布类型：新 摘要：为了研究沉积在有或没有锗层的石英玻璃基板 $(SiO_2)$ 上的超薄（约 10 nm）铜膜的自然腐蚀过程，研究了室温下暴露在空气中的此类样品的微波反射系数 $R$ 和直流电阻率 $\rho$ 的时间依赖性。在这些条件下，发现$Cu/SiO_2$薄膜上的氧化层$d$的厚度根据抛物线定律随时间$t$增加，这与现有铜氧化理论的预测相矛盾。提出了一个模型，通过原子氧沿着氧化物晶粒的边界向铜膜扩散及其随后的氧化来解释 $d(t)$ 的这种行为。研究发现，$Cu/Ge/SiO_2$ 薄膜的 $R$ 和 $\rho$ 的降解速度比相同厚度的 $Cu/SiO_2$ 薄膜的类似特性慢得多。 $Cu/Ge/SiO_2$ 薄膜的高耐腐蚀性是通过在锗底层上生长铜薄膜期间$Ge$ 再分布的特性来解释的。 $Cu/Ge/SiO_2$ 薄膜长期保留其特性，使其被推荐作为电磁干扰保护器件中金涂层的廉价替代品。
