## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19243v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Deep neural networks (DNNs) have recently been applied to inverse scattering problems (ISPs) due to their strong nonlinear mapping capabilities. However, supervised DNN solvers require large-scale datasets, which limits their generalization in practical applications. Untrained neural networks (UNNs) address this issue by updating weights from measured electric fields and prior physical knowledge, but existing UNN solvers suffer from long inference time. To overcome these limitations, this paper proposes a contrast-source-based physics-driven neural network (CSPDNN), which predicts the induced current distribution to improve efficiency and incorporates an adaptive total variation loss for robust reconstruction under varying contrast and noise conditions. The improved imaging performance is validated through comprehensive numerical simulations and experimental data.

arXiv:2601.19243v1 发布类型：cross 摘要：深度神经网络（DNN）由于其强大的非线性映射能力，最近被应用于逆散射问题（ISP）。然而，有监督的 DNN 求解器需要大规模数据集，这限制了它们在实际应用中的泛化。未经训练的神经网络 (UNN) 通过更新测量电场和先验物理知识的权重来解决这个问题，但现有的 UNN 求解器存在推理时间过长的问题。为了克服这些限制，本文提出了一种基于对比度源的物理驱动神经网络（CSPDNN），它可以预测感应电流分布以提高效率，并结合自适应总变差损失以在不同对比度和噪声条件下实现鲁棒重建。通过全面的数值模拟和实验数据验证了改进的成像性能。
