## Abstract
In this work, we present EquiNet, a neural network for predicting vapor–liquid equilibrium (VLE) in novel binary mixtures through direct estimation of activity coefficients and vapor pressures. The model embeds a classic excess-Gibbs free energy formulation, ensuring Gibbs–Duhem consistency on all predicted activity coefficients. Given molecular SMILES, liquid-phase composition, and temperature, EquiNet predicts vapor composition and pressure, enabling end-to-end training on experimental data without requiring precomputed activity coefficients. Across the UNIFAC-scope VLE dataset (covering 55,470 binary datapoints), EquiNet achieves a log10 Pressure (Pa) RMSE of 0.0659, outperforming UNIFAC (0.087) and the HANNA machine-learning model (0.101) under the same molecular-scope assumptions. For evaluation on previously unseen chemical systems, we follow a novel-mixture cross-validation protocol that demonstrated strong transferability across diverse functional groups and molecular classes. On the public Jaubert benchmark, restricted to UNIFAC-compatible pairs (2,099 points), EquiNet attains a comparable 0.03436 log10 Pressure (Pa) RMSE, substantially below HANNA (0.26743) and UNIFAC (1.55616). This work employs a modified Raoult’s law approach without vapor-phase fugacity or Poynting corrections and is limited to neutral, organic, non-reactive binary mixtures composed of supported elements. The method does not detect mixture criticality, and any supercritical examples should be interpreted qualitatively.

## 摘要（中文）
在这项工作中，我们提出了 EquiNet，这是一种通过直接估计活度系数和蒸气​​压来预测新型二元混合物中气液平衡（VLE）的神经网络。该模型嵌入了经典的过量吉布斯自由能公式，确保所有预测活度系数的吉布斯-迪昂一致性。给定分子 SMILES、液相成分和温度，EquiNet 可以预测蒸气成分和压力，从而能够对实验数据进行端到端训练，而无需预先计算活动系数。在 UNIFAC 范围的 VLE 数据集（覆盖 55,470 个二进制数据点）中，EquiNet 实现了 0.0659 的 log10 压力 (Pa) RMSE，在相同的分子范围假设下优于 UNIFAC (0.087) 和 HANNA 机器学习模型 (0.101)。为了评估以前未见过的化学系统，我们遵循一种新颖的混合物交叉验证协议，该协议证明了跨不同官能团和分子类别的强大可转移性。在公共 Jaubert 基准上，仅限于 UNIFAC 兼容对（2,099 点），EquiNet 获得了可比的 0.03436 log10 压力 (Pa) RMSE，远低于 HANNA (0.26743) 和 UNIFAC (1.55616)。这项工作采用了改进的拉乌尔定律方法，没有气相逸度或坡印廷校正，并且仅限于由负载元素组成的中性、有机、非反应性二元混合物。该方法不检测混合物临界性，任何超临界示例都应进行定性解释。
