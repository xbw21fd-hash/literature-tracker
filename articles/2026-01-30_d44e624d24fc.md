## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21322v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The ability to tune and switch magnetic anisotropy to a perpendicular orientation is a key challenge for implementing 2D magnets in spintronic devices. H-phase vanadium dichalcogenides V$X_2$ ($X$=Te, Se, S) are promising ferromagnetic semiconductors with large magnetic anisotropy energy (MAE). Recent work has shown that hole doping can switch their easy axis to out-of-plane, though the microscopic origin of this perpendicular magnetic anisotropy (PMA) remains unclear. Using density-functional-theory calculations, we demonstrate that the PMA enhancement arises from first-order spin-orbit coupling (SOC) acting on topmost degenerate valence states with nonzero orbital angular momentum projection ($m_l\ne 0$). In this case, the $\hat{L}_z\hat{S}_z$ term dominates for perpendicular magnetization, while in-plane orientations involve only weaker, second-order SOC contributions. The increased valence bandwidth leads to depletion of higher-energy states upon hole doping, stabilizing PMA. From this mechanism, we identify two transferable design principles for enhancing MAE under weak hole doping: (i) orbital degeneracy at the valence-band edge protected by point-group symmetry and (ii) finite SOC in the degenerate manifold. Notably, we identify multiple magnetic semiconductors that meet these criteria and display enhanced MAE under hole doping. Furthermore, we show that band engineering can strategically place these degenerate orbitals at the valence band edge, significantly boosting PMA when hole-doped. We also examine trends in VTe$_2$, VSe$_2$, and VS$_2$ to determine the influence of crystal-field splitting, exchange interaction, and orbital hybridization on the valence band edges. These results provide both a fundamental understanding of PMA switching upon hole doping and a transferable strategy for tuning magnetic anisotropy, essential for designing high-performance spintronic materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21322v1 公告类型：新 摘要：将磁各向异性调整和切换到垂直方向的能力是在自旋电子器件中实现 2D 磁体的关键挑战。 H相钒二硫化物V$X_2$ ($X$=Te, Se, S)是具有大磁各向异性能(MAE)的有前途的铁磁半导体。最近的研究表明，空穴掺杂可以将它们的易磁化轴切换到平面外，尽管这种垂直磁各向异性（PMA）的微观起源仍不清楚。使用密度泛函理论计算，我们证明 PMA 增强源于作用于具有非零轨道角动量投影 ($m_l\ne 0$) 的最顶层简并价态的一阶自旋轨道耦合 (SOC)。在这种情况下，$\hat{L}_z\hat{S}_z$ 项在垂直磁化中占主导地位，而面内方向仅涉及较弱的二阶 SOC 贡献。价带宽度的增加导致空穴掺杂时高能态的耗尽，从而稳定 PMA。从这个机制中，我们确定了在弱空穴掺杂下增强 MAE 的两个可转移设计原则：（i）受点群对称性保护的价带边缘的轨道简并性和（ii）简并流形中的有限 SOC。值得注意的是，我们确定了多种满足这些标准的磁性半导体，并在空穴掺杂下显示出增强的 MAE。此外，我们表明能带工程可以策略性地将这些简并轨道放置在价带边缘，从而在空穴掺杂时显着提高 PMA。我们还研究了 VTe$_2$、VSe$_2$ 和 VS$_2$ 的趋势，以确定晶体场分裂、交换相互作用和轨道杂化对价带边缘的影响。这些结果既提供了对空穴掺杂时 PMA 开关的基本理解，也提供了调节磁各向异性的可转移策略，这对于设计高性能自旋电子材料至关重要。
