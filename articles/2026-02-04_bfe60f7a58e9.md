## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03178v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We present an adaptive Chebyshev-based Boundary Integral Equation (CBIE) solver for electromagnetic scattering from smooth perfect electric conductor (PEC) objects. The proposed approach eliminates manual parameter tuning by introducing (i) a unified adaptive quadrature strategy for automatic selection of the near-singular interaction distance and (ii) an adaptive computation of all self- and near-singular precomputation integrals to a prescribed accuracy using Gauss-Kronrod (h-adaptive) or Clenshaw-Curtis (p-adaptive) rules and singularity-resolving changes of variables. Both h-adaptive and p-adaptive schemes are explored within this framework, ensuring high-order accuracy and robustness across a broad range of geometries without loss of efficiency. Numerical results for canonical and complex CAD geometries demonstrate that the adaptive solver achieves accuracy and convergence rates comparable to optimally tuned fixed-grid CBIE implementations, while offering automation and scalability to electrically large, geometrically complex problems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03178v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们提出了一种基于切比雪夫的自适应边界积分方程 (CBIE) 求解器，用于解决平滑完美电导体 (PEC) 物体的电磁散射问题。所提出的方法通过引入（i）用于自动选择近奇异交互距离的统一自适应求积策略和（ii）使用Gauss-Kronrod（h自适应）或Clenshaw-Curtis（p自适应）规则和变量的奇点解析变化对所有自和近奇异预计算积分进行自适应计算以达到规定的精度，从而消除了手动参数调整。在此框架内探索了 h 自适应和 p 自适应方案，确保在广泛的几何形状中实现高阶精度和鲁棒性，而不损失效率。规范和复杂 CAD 几何图形的数值结果表明，自适应求解器的精度和收敛速度与优化调整的固定网格 CBIE 实现相当，同时为电气大型、几何复杂问题提供自动化和可扩展性。
