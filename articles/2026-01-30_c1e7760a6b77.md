## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21103v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Simulating water from first principles remains a significant computational challenge due to the slow dynamics of the underlying system. Although machine-learned interatomic potentials (MLPs) can accelerate these simulations, they often fail to achieve the required level of accuracy for reliable uncertainty quantification. In this study, we use MACE - an equivariant graph neural network architecture that has been trained using an extensive RPBE-D3 database - to predict density isobars, diffusion constants, radial distribution functions, and melting points. Although equivariant MACE models are computationally more expensive than simpler architectures, such as kernel-based potentials (KbPs), their significantly lower total energy errors allow for reliable thermodynamic reweighting with minimal bias. Our results are consistent with those of previous studies using KbPs; however, equivariant models can be validated against the ground-truth density functional theory (DFT) ensemble, providing a critical advantage. These findings establish equivariant MLPs as robust and reliable tools for investigating the thermophysical properties of water with DFT-level accuracy.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21103v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：由于底层系统的动态缓慢，从第一原理模拟水仍然是一个重大的计算挑战。尽管机器学习的原子间势（MLP）可以加速这些模拟，但它们通常无法达到可靠的不确定性量化所需的精度水平。在本研究中，我们使用 MACE（一种等变图神经网络架构，已使用广泛的 RPBE-D3 数据库进行训练）来预测密度等压线、扩散常数、径向分布函数和熔点。尽管等变 MACE 模型在计算上比基于核势 (KbPs) 等更简单的架构更昂贵，但它们的总能量误差显着降低，可以以最小的偏差实现可靠的热力学重新加权。我们的结果与之前使用 KbPs 进行的研究结果一致；然而，等变模型可以根据真实密度泛函理论 (DFT) 系综进行验证，从而提供了关键优势。这些发现将等变 MLP 确立为强大而可靠的工具，用于以 DFT 级精度研究水的热物理性质。
