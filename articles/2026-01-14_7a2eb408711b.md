## Abstract
The spectra of CH5+ and its deuterated analogues are challenging to interpret due to the very flat potential surface and the highly delocalized ground state wave function. In this work, an approach is developed to model the spectra of CH5+ and CH4D+. In this approach, several thousand geometries are sampled from the ground-state probability amplitude, which is evaluated using diffusion Monte Carlo. For each structure, the potential energy is minimized with respect to the five CH bond lengths, and the frequencies and intensities for the five 1-0 transitions involving the CH or CD stretching vibrations are then evaluated using a five-dimensional harmonically-coupled anharmonic oscillator model and a sum of one-dimensional cuts through the dipole moment surface. This information is used to obtain the spectrum by two approaches. To model the lower-resolution laser-induced reaction spectrum, the calculated spectrum is obtained by convoluting the spectra obtained at all of the sampled structures. To model the helium droplet spectrum, in which the individual CH transitions are resolved, the spectrum is obtained by averaging the Hamiltonian matrices and transition moment vectors over the sampled structures. We find that these approaches reproduce reported spectra. Analysis of the helium droplet spectrum leads us to conclude that in this environment the isomerization of CH5+ in its ground state is restricted compared to the gas-phase. Overall, this approach provides a simple and physically transparent way to connect fluxional structures to CH-stretch spectral patterns, and offers frameworks for interpreting future spectroscopic studies.

## 摘要（中文）
由于非常平坦的势面和高度离域的基态波函数，CH5+ 及其氘代类似物的光谱很难解释。在这项工作中，开发了一种方法来模拟 CH5+ 和 CH4D+ 的光谱。在这种方法中，从基态概率幅度中采样数千个几何形状，并使用扩散蒙特卡罗进行评估。对于每种结构，相对于五个 CH 键长最小化势能，然后使用五维谐波耦合非简谐振子模型和通过偶极矩表面的一维切割之和来评估涉及 CH 或 CD 伸缩振动的五个 1-0 跃迁的频率和强度。该信息用于通过两种方法获取频谱。为了模拟较低分辨率的激光诱导反应光谱，通过对所有采样结构获得的光谱进行卷积来获得计算光谱。为了对氦液滴光谱进行建模（其中解析了各个 CH 跃迁），通过对采样结构上的哈密顿矩阵和跃迁矩向量进行平均来获得光谱。我们发现这些方法再现了报告的光谱。对氦液滴光谱的分析使我们得出结论，在这种环境下，与气相相比，基态 CH5+ 的异构化受到限制。总体而言，这种方法提供了一种简单且物理透明的方法来将流变结构与 CH 拉伸光谱图案连接起来，并为解释未来的光谱研究提供了框架。
