## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01981v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum many-body systems pose a formidable computational challenge due to the exponential growth of their Hilbert space. While machine learning (ML) has shown promise as an alternative paradigm, most applications remain at the proof-of-concept stage, focusing narrowly on energy estimation at the lower end of the spectrum. Here, we push ML beyond this frontier by extensively testing HubbardNet, a deep neural network architecture for the Bose-Hubbard model. Pushing improvements in the optimizer and learning rates, and introducing physics-informed output activations that can resolve extremely small wave-function amplitudes, we achieve ground-state energy errors reduced by orders of magnitude and wave-function fidelities exceeding 99%. We further assess physical relevance by analysing generalized inverse participation ratios and multifractal dimensions for ground and excited states in one and two dimensions, demonstrating that optimized ML models reproduce localization, delocalization, and multifractality trends across the spectrum. Crucially, these qualitative predictions remain robust across four decades of the interaction strength, e.g. spanning across superfluid, Mott-insulating, as well as quantum chaotic regimes. Together, these results suggest ML as a viable qualitative predictor of many-body structure, complementing the quantitative strengths of exact diagonalization and tensor-network methods.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01981v1 公告类型：新 摘要：量子多体系统由于希尔伯特空间的指数增长而带来了巨大的计算挑战。虽然机器学习 (ML) 已显示出作为替代范式的前景，但大多数应用仍处于概念验证阶段，仅专注于较低端的能量估计。在这里，我们通过广泛测试 HubbardNet（Bose-Hubbard 模型的深度神经网络架构）将机器学习推向了这一前沿。推动优化器和学习率的改进，并引入可以解析极小的波函数振幅的物理信息输出激活，我们实现了基态能量误差减少了几个数量级，波函数保真度超过 99%。我们通过分析一维和二维基态和激发态的广义逆参与比和多重分形维数，进一步评估物理相关性，证明优化的机器学习模型可以再现整个光谱中的局域化、离域和多重分形趋势。至关重要的是，这些定性预测在四十年的相互作用强度中仍然保持稳健，例如跨越超流体、莫特绝缘以及量子混沌体系。总之，这些结果表明机器学习是多体结构的可行定性预测器，补充了精确对角化和张量网络方法的定量优势。
