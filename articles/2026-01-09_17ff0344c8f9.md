## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05090v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The origin of carbon monoxide (CO) in Saturn's stratosphere remains uncertain, with proposed sources including internal thermochemical production, cometary impacts, and exogenic material from the rings and icy moons (i.e. Enceladus). We aim to constrain the vertical and meridional distribution of stratospheric CO and assess the relative contributions of these potential sources. Here, we analysed high-spectral-resolution ALMA observations of the CO (J=3-2) line obtained on 25 May 2018, sampling Saturn's limb from 20{\deg}S to 69{\deg}N. CO vertical profiles were retrieved using a line-by-line radiative transfer model combined with spectral inversion techniques, testing multiple prior scenarios representative of different source hypotheses. CO is confined to a narrow layer between 0.1 and 1 mbar, with a robust negative vertical gradient and mean abundances of (3.7+/- 0.8) x 10$^{-8}$ at 0.1 mbar and (7.2 +/- 0.9) x 10$^{-8}$ at 1 mbar. The meridional distribution is statistically homogeneous, with a marginal enhancement near 60{\deg} N plausibly related to Enceladus. No significant equatorial enhancement is detected. The absence of a strong equatorial enhancement rules out a long-lived steady source associated with ring infall. The observations are most consistent with a relatively recent ($\approx$200-year-old or younger) cometary impact whose material has since been horizontally mixed, while any Cassini Grand Finale ring influx was either too recent or inefficient to affect CO abundances at the probed pressure levels.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05090v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：土星平流层中一氧化碳 (CO) 的起源仍不确定，提议的来源包括内部热化学产生、彗星撞击以及来自环和冰卫星（即土卫二）的外源物质。我们的目标是限制平流层二氧化碳的垂直和经向分布，并评估这些潜在来源的相对贡献。在这里，我们分析了 2018 年 5 月 25 日获得的 CO (J=3-2) 线的高光谱分辨率 ALMA 观测结果，从南纬 20{\deg} 到北纬 69{\deg} 对土星边缘进行采样。使用逐行辐射传输模型结合光谱反演技术检索 CO 垂直剖面，测试代表不同源假设的多个先前情景。 CO 被限制在 0.1 至 1 mbar 之间的狭窄层内，具有强大的负垂直梯度，0.1 mbar 时的平均丰度为 (3.7+/- 0.8) x 10$^{-8}$，1 mbar 时的平均丰度为 (7.2 +/- 0.9) x 10$^{-8}$。经向分布在统计上是均匀的，在 60{\deg} N 附近有边际增强，这似乎与土卫二有关。未检测到明显的赤道增强。缺乏强烈的赤道增强排除了与环坠落相关的长期稳定源。这些观测结果与相对较新的（大约 200 岁或更年轻）彗星撞击最为一致，其物质此后已水平混合，而任何卡西尼大结局环的流入要么太新，要么效率低下，无法影响探测压力水平下的二氧化碳丰度。
