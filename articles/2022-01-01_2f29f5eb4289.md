## Abstract
TLDR: Four interatomic potentials for iron are developed and compared: a simple machine-learned embedded atom method (EAM) potential, a potential with machine- learned two and three-body-dependent terms, a potentially with machine's EAM and three body terms, and a Gaussian approximation potential with the SOAP descriptor.
