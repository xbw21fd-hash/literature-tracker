## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15262v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Infrasonic signatures of meteoroid fragmentation are frequently ambiguous: do multiple arrivals signify a complex breakup or merely the distorting effects of a layered atmosphere? Resolving this ambiguity is critical for accurate energy estimates and source reconstruction. In this study, we address this challenge by analyzing a unique regional dataset of well-constrained meteoroid events observed by the Southern Ontario Meteor Network and the co-located Elginfield Infrasound Array. We employ pseudo-differential parabolic equation (PPE) simulations to quantify how fine-scale gravity-wave structures in the stratosphere and lower thermosphere modify acoustic waveforms at ranges <300 km. Our modeling reveals that while fine-scale layering can stretch signals and generate diffuse oscillatory tails, it does not produce discrete, high-amplitude pulse splitting at ranges below ~140 km. By applying these results to the rare multi-arrival event 20060305, we demonstrate that its distinct double arrival at 100 km range is inconsistent with atmospheric multipathing and provides definitive evidence of separate fragmentation episodes. These findings establish new diagnostic criteria for separating source physics from propagation artifacts, improving the reliability of infrasound as a monitoring tool for natural bolides, space debris re-entries, and catastrophic launch failures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15262v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：流星体破碎的次声波特征常常是不明确的：多次到达是否意味着复杂的破碎，或者仅仅是分层大气的扭曲效应？解决这种模糊性对于准确的能量估计和源重建至关重要。在这项研究中，我们通过分析南安大略流星网络和位于同一地点的埃尔金菲尔德次声阵列观测到的受良好约束的流星体事件的独特区域数据集来应对这一挑战。我们采用伪微分抛物线方程 (PPE) 模拟来量化平流层和低层热层中的精细重力波结构如何改变 <300 km 范围内的声波波形。我们的模型表明，虽然精细分层可以拉伸信号并产生扩散振荡尾部，但它不会在约 140 公里以下的范围内产生离散的高振幅脉冲分裂。通过将这些结果应用于罕见的多重到达事件 20060305，我们证明了其在 100 公里范围内的独特双重到达与大气多路径不一致，并提供了单独碎片事件的明确证据。这些发现建立了新的诊断标准，用于将源物理与传播伪影分开，提高次声作为天然火流星、空间碎片再入和灾难性发射失败监测工具的可靠性。
