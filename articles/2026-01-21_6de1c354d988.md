## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13219v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Correlated materials with competing spin-orbit and crystal-field interactions can host composite spin-orbital magnons that are highly susceptible to structural and electronic perturbations, enabling control of magnetic dynamics beyond spin-only physics. Using Raman spectroscopy on Ca$_2$RuO$_4$, we show that the partial substitution of Ru by Mn reconstructs the magnon spectrum and leads to one-magnon modes that are hidden in the undoped state. We demonstrate that the transition-metal substitution activates otherwise symmetry-forbidden magnon modes through mirror-symmetry breaking of the underlying spin-orbital configuration. This effect can be theoretically explained by the local structural distortions induced in the RuO$_6$ octahedra near the dopant, that enable the observation of mixed-parity one-magnon modes. The uncovered mechanism demonstrates how spin-orbit-lattice entanglement can be exploited to control collective magnetic excitations in spin-orbit correlated materials.

arXiv:2601.13219v1 公告类型：新 摘要：具有竞争性自旋轨道和晶体场相互作用的相关材料可以容纳极易受到结构和电子扰动的复合自旋轨道磁振子，从而能够控制仅自旋物理之外的磁动力学。使用 Ca$_2$RuO$_4$ 上的拉曼光谱，我们表明用 Mn 部分取代 Ru 可以重建磁振子光谱，并导致隐藏在未掺杂状态下的单磁振子模式。我们证明，过渡金属取代通过底层自旋轨道构型的镜像对称破缺激活了对称性禁止的磁振子模式。从理论上讲，这种效应可以通过掺杂剂附近的 RuO$_6$ 八面体中引起的局部结构畸变来解释，从而能够观察到混合宇称单磁振子模式。未发现的机制展示了如何利用自旋轨道晶格纠缠来控制自旋轨道相关材料中的集体磁激发。
