## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20946v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate spin angular momentum transfer in the Einstein-de Haas effect within prototypical magnetic crystals, focusing on its partition between phonons and rigid-body rotation. Using the Eckart frame to decouple local vibrations (phonons) from rigid-body rotation, we demonstrate that spin angular momentum is simultaneously transferred into both phonons and rigid-body rotation in an asymmetric way: rigid-body rotation acquires the dominant share of angular momentum, while phonons absorb most of the resulting kinetic energy. This divergent transfer of angular momentum and energy identifies phonons as direct and indispensable participants in the Einstein-de Haas dynamics. Furthermore, we find that pseudo-dipolar anisotropy and Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction exert distinct control over the angular momentum transfer. Stronger pseudo-dipolar anisotropy increases the total amount of transferred angular momentum, whereas stronger Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction accelerates the transfer rate and increases the proportion of phonon angular momentum. Our work clarifies the microscopic picture of the Einstein-de Haas effect and enables targeted angular-momentum control in magneto-mechanical devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20946v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了原型磁性晶体内爱因斯坦-德哈斯效应中的自旋角动量传递，重点关注其在声子和刚体旋转之间的分配。使用埃卡特框架将局部振动（声子）与刚体旋转解耦，我们证明自旋角动量以不对称的方式同时传递给声子和刚体旋转：刚体旋转获得角动量的主要份额，而声子吸收大部分由此产生的动能。这种角动量和能量的发散转移将声子视为爱因斯坦-德哈斯动力学中直接且不可或缺的参与者。此外，我们发现赝偶极各向异性和 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 相互作用对角动量传递具有明显的控制作用。较强的赝偶极各向异性增加了转移角动量的总量，而较强的Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya相互作用则加速了转移速率并增加了声子角动量的比例。我们的工作阐明了爱因斯坦-德哈斯效应的微观图景，并实现了磁机械设备中的有针对性的角动量控制。
