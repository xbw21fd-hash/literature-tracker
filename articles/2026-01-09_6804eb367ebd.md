## Abstract
arXiv:2504.16250v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Enhancing spin-to-charge (S$\rightarrow$C) conversion efficiency remains a key challenge in spintronic materials research. In this work we investigate the effect of substrate-induced strains onto the S$\rightarrow$C efficiency. On one hand, we analyze strains-induced magnetic anisotropies in yttrium iron garnet (Y$_3$Fe$_5$O$_{12}$, YIG) by comparing the magnetic and structural properties of YIG films grown on Gd$_3$Ga$_5$O$_{12}$ (GGG) and (CaGd)$_3$(MgZrGa)$_5$O$_{12}$ (SGGG) substrates. Differences in lattice mismatch - YIG//GGG ($\eta = -0.06 \%$) and YIG//SGGG ($\eta = -0.83 \%$) - lead to out-of-plane tensile strains in the first case and unexpected compressive strain in the latter. On the other hand, we study the spin injection efficiency on Pt/YIG bilayers evaluated by the Inverse Spin Hall Effect (ISHE). We find that the resulting perpendicular magnetic anisotropy in YIG//SGGG, while not dominant over shape anisotropy, correlates with enhanced ISHE signals as observed in Spin Pumping Ferromagnetic Resonance (SP-FMR) and Spin Seebeck effect (SSE) experiments. Strain engineering proves effective in enhancing spin-to-charge conversion, providing insight into the design of efficient spintronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.16250v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：提高自旋电荷 (S$\rightarrow$C) 转换效率仍然是自旋电子材料研究中的一个关键挑战。在这项工作中，我们研究了底物诱导应变对 S$\rightarrow$C 效率的影响。一方面，我们通过比较在 Gd$_3$Ga$_5$O$_{12}$ (GGG) 和 (CaGd)$_3$(MgZrGa)$_5$O$_{12}$ (SGGG) 上生长的 YIG 薄膜的磁性和结构特性来分析钇铁石榴石 (Y$_3$Fe$_5$O$_{12}$, YIG) 中应变引起的磁各向异性基材。晶格失配的差异 - YIG//GGG ($\eta = -0.06 \%$) 和 YIG//SGGG ($\eta = -0.83 \%$) - 在第一种情况下导致平面外拉伸应变，在后者中导致意外的压缩应变。另一方面，我们研究了通过逆自旋霍尔效应（ISHE）评估的 Pt/YIG 双层的自旋注入效率。我们发现，YIG//SGGG 中产生的垂直磁各向异性虽然在形状各向异性上不占主导地位，但与自旋泵浦铁磁共振 (SP-FMR) 和自旋塞贝克效应 (SSE) 实验中观察到的增强 ISHE 信号相关。事实证明，应变工程可以有效增强自旋电荷转换，为高效自旋电子器件的设计提供深入见解。
