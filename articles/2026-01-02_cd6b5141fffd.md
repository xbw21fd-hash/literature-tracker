## Abstract
In this article, we examine new formalisms for the construction of the external space when correlating reference wavefunctions built from nonorthogonal determinant expansions. Defining the external space in nonorthogonal approaches is challenging, as every substitution from the reference wavefunction can potentially mix both internal and external configurations. As a result, post-nonorthogonal methods are plagued by internal contamination and linear dependencies in the external space, which may lead to correlation double counting that results from the overlap of the external and reference spaces. The removal of these internal configurations and orthonormalization of the excited space basis can be computationally expensive. In particular, as the excitation operators cannot be subdivided by their action on orbital subspaces, the external space cannot be partitioned into non-overlapping subsets as is possible in orthogonal methods. To resolve these issues, we propose both uncontracted and internally contracted approaches based on a natural excitation framework that allows for reduced scaling, more straightforward separation of excitation types that lead to external and internal spaces, and a facile translation of orthogonal methods to a nonorthogonal framework. Several proofs and a numerical demonstration using vanadium monohydride (VH) are provided to illustrate the viability of the proposed approach, using a method-agnostic presentation to highlight the generality of the approach.

## 摘要（中文）
在本文中，我们在关联从非正交行列式展开构建的参考波函数时，研究了构建外部空间的新形式。在非正交方法中定义外部空间具有挑战性，因为参考波函数的每次替换都可能混合内部和外部配置。因此，后非正交方法受到内部污染和外部空间线性依赖性的困扰，这可能导致由于外部空间和参考空间重叠而导致的相关重复计算。去除这些内部构型和激发空间基础的正交化在计算上可能是昂贵的。特别是，由于激励算子不能通过其对轨道子空间的作用来细分，因此外部空间不能像正交方法中那样划分为非重叠子集。为了解决这些问题，我们提出了基于自然激励框架的非收缩和内部收缩方法，该框架允许减少缩放，更直接地分离导致外部和内部空间的激励类型，以及将正交方法轻松转换为非正交框架。提供了一些证明和使用一氢化钒（VH）的数值演示来说明所提出方法的可行性，并使用与方法无关的演示来强调该方法的通用性。
