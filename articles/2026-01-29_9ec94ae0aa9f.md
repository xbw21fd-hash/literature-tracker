## Abstract
arXiv:2504.10349v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Rydberg atoms in dc electric fields acquire static dipole moments. When the atoms are close to a surface producing an inhomogeneous electric field, such as by the adsorbates on an atom chip, depending on the sign of the dipole moment of the Rydberg-Stark eigenstate, the atoms may experience a force toward or away from the surface. We show that by applying a bias electric field and coupling a desired Rydberg state by a microwave field of proper frequency to another Rydberg state with opposite sign of the dipole moment, we can create a trapping potential for the atom at a prescribed distance from the surface. Perfectly overlapping trapping potentials for several Rydberg states can also be created by multicomponent microwave fields. A pair of such trapped Rydberg states of an atom can represent a qubit. Finally, we discuss an optimal realization of the SWAP gate between pairs of such atomic Rydberg qubits separated by a large distance but interacting with a common mode of a planar microwave resonator at finite temperature.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.10349v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：直流电场中的里德伯原子获得静态偶极矩。当原子靠近产生不均匀电场的表面时，例如通过原子芯片上的吸附物，根据里德伯-斯塔克本征态的偶极矩的符号，原子可能会受到朝向或远离表面的力。我们证明，通过施加偏置电场并通过适当频率的微波场将所需的里德伯态耦合到具有相反偶极矩符号的另一个里德伯态，我们可以在距表面规定距离处为原子创建捕获势。多分量微波场也可以产生多个里德堡态的完全重叠的俘获势。原子的一对这样的俘获里德伯态可以代表一个量子位。最后，我们讨论了这种原子里德伯量子位对之间交换门的最佳实现，这些原子里德伯量子位相距很远，但在有限温度下与平面微波谐振器的共模相互作用。
