## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02036v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study spin-resolved transport in a ballistic quantum dot with Rashba spin-orbit coupling, focusing on charge-to-spin conversion and spin Hall effect. In the regime where the dot size is comparable to the Fermi wavelength, we identify a clear crossover from weak localization to weak antilocalization as the Rashba coupling increases. This transition is accompanied by gate-tunable spin currents of Edelstein and spin Hall type, whose behavior reflects the underlying electron wavefunction interference. Notably, the Edelstein current shows an inflection point at the critical Rashba strength, signaling the crossover from weak localization to weak antilocalization. In the presence of an in-plane magnetic field we also report a transition in angular periodicity of the magnetoresistance -- from $\pi$ to $2\pi$ -- arising from the interplay between spin-orbit interaction and Zeeman coupling. These results establish a direct link between quantum coherence, charge-to-spin conversion, and geometric confinement in mesoscopic systems.

arXiv:2602.02036v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究具有 Rashba 自旋轨道耦合的弹道量子点中的自旋解析输运，重点关注电荷自旋转换和自旋霍尔效应。在点尺寸与费米波长相当的情况下，随着拉什巴耦合的增加，我们发现从弱局域化到弱反局域化的明显交叉。这种转变伴随着埃德尔斯坦和自旋霍尔类型的栅极可调自旋电流，其行为反映了潜在的电子波函数干扰。值得注意的是，埃德尔斯坦电流在临界 Rashba 强度处显示出拐点，标志着从弱定位到弱反定位的交叉。在存在面内磁场的情况下，我们还报告了磁阻角周期性的转变——从$\pi$到$2\pi$——这是由自旋轨道相互作用和塞曼耦合之间的相互作用引起的。这些结果在介观系统中的量子相干性、电荷自旋转换和几何限制之间建立了直接联系。
