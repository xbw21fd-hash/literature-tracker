## Abstract
The Stoner–Wohlfarth antiferromagnet (AFM), an extension of the classical Stoner–Wohlfarth model originally describing the magnetization reversal in ferromagnetic nanoparticles1,2, refers to a single-domain AFM whose Néel vector can be coherently switched by the magnetic field. These AFMs not only retain the inherent advantages of antiferromagnetism but also feature controllable Néel vector and a perfect switching ratio, thus emerging as promising building blocks for ultradense magnetic memories and high-throughput computing systems3,4. However, bulk AFMs are not the Stoner–Wohlfarth AFMs owing to the hard-to-switch Néel vector and inevitable multidomain structure3,5,6,7. Here we report that CrPS4, a two-dimensional (2D) van der Waals (vdW) A-type AFM, exhibits ideal characteristics of the Stoner–Wohlfarth AFMs, because of the dominance of antiferromagnetic exchange over the magnetic anisotropy and high quality of vdW interfaces. The antiferromagnetic order undergoes a ferromagnet (FM)-like binary switching with the magnetic field rather than the layer-by-layer flipping observed in other 2D A-type AFMs. Moreover, we deduce the characteristic exchange length of several vdW A-type AFMs and propose a criterion for judging the Stoner–Wohlfarth AFMs. Our work therefore establishes a universal framework for understanding the magnetization reversal in layered AFMs and promotes the effective use of 2D AFMs in advanced spintronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
Stoner-Wohlfarth 反铁磁体 (AFM) 是经典 Stoner-Wohlfarth 模型的扩展，最初描述了铁磁纳米粒子 1,2 中的磁化反转，是指单域 AFM，其 Néel 矢量可以通过磁场相干切换。这些 AFM 不仅保留了反铁磁性的固有优势，而且还具有可控的尼尔矢量和完美的开关比，从而成为超密磁存储器和高通量计算系统的有前景的构建模块3,4。然而，由于难以切换的 Néel 矢量和不可避免的多域结构，体 AFM 不是 Stoner-Wohlfarth AFM 3,5,6,7。在此，我们报道 CrPS4 是一种二维 (2D) 范德华 (vdW) A 型 AFM，由于反铁磁交换对磁各向异性的主导作用以及 vdW 界面的高质量，因此表现出 Stoner-Wohlfarth AFM 的理想特性。反铁磁序随着磁场经历类似铁磁体 (FM) 的二元切换，而不是在其他 2D A 型 AFM 中观察到的逐层翻转。此外，我们推导了几种vdW A型AFM的特征交换长度，并提出了判断Stoner-Wohlfarth AFM的标准。因此，我们的工作建立了一个通用框架来理解层状原子力显微镜中的磁化反转，并促进二维原子力显微镜在先进自旋电子器件中的有效使用。
