## Abstract
arXiv:2412.18896v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Superconducting circuits are arguably taking a leading role in driving the ongoing quantum technological revolution. A detailed knowledge of the microscopic fluctuating electromagnetic properties plays an important role in advancing the circuitry design, testing, and material integration of cutting-edge superconducting quantum electronics. Here we report scanning nitrogen-vacancy (NV) quantum sensing of local magnetic noise environment of an on- chip superconducting resonator. We find that quasiparticle-induced fluctuating magnetic fields can drive NV spin relaxation, which shows a peak value around the superconducting transition point of niobium at the thermal equilibrium state. External microwave driving at the resonator mode frequency significantly increases the quasiparticle density, leading to enhancement of magnetic noise. We further perform optically detected magnetic resonance measurements to demonstrate quasiparticle magnetic noise mediated off-resonant dipole coupling between the NV center and niobium resonator. Our work reports experimental observation of the Hebel-Slichter peak signature by an external sensor outside of a superconductor. The presented study also highlights the advantages of quantum sensors in investigating miniaturized superconducting devices, providing insights into their future performance improvements.

arXiv:2412.18896v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：超导电路可以说在推动正在进行的量子技术革命中发挥着主导作用。对微观波动电磁特性的详细了解对于推进尖端超导量子电子学的电路设计、测试和材料集成发挥着重要作用。在这里，我们报告了片上超导谐振器局部磁噪声环境的扫描氮空位（NV）量子传感。我们发现准粒子引起的脉动磁场可以驱动NV自旋弛豫，其在热平衡状态下铌的超导转变点附近显示出峰值。谐振器模式频率下的外部微波驱动显着增加了准粒子密度，导致磁噪声增强。我们进一步进行光学检测磁共振测量，以证明 NV 中心和铌谐振器之间的准粒子磁噪声介导的非共振偶极子耦合。我们的工作报告了超导体外部的外部传感器对 Hebel-Slichter 峰值特征的实验观察。所提出的研究还强调了量子传感器在研究微型超导器件方面的优势，为它们未来的性能改进提供了见解。
