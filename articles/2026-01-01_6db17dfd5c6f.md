## Abstract
arXiv:2510.19188v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: NbOI$_2$ has recently emerged as a new van der Waals material combining semiconducting behavior with intrinsic in plane ferroelectricity and pronounced transport and optical anisotropy. However, its photocarrier dynamics remain largely unexplored. Here we report transient absorption spectroscopy of NbOI$_2$ using femtosecond pump probe reflectance measurements. A pronounced transient absorption feature is observed near the 2.34 eV excitonic resonance, arising from photocarrier induced excitonic energy shifts and saturation. The decay dynamics reveal an exciton lifetime of several tens of picoseconds and show density-dependent behavior consistent with exciton exciton annihilation and defect-assisted Auger recombination, yielding a rate of 0.4 cm$^2$ s$^{-1}$, which is comparable to that in monolayer transition metal dichalcogenides. Polarization resolved measurements further reveal a pronounced in-plane anisotropy in the transient response that follows the linear absorption anisotropy. These findings provide fundamental insight into photocarrier dynamics in NbOI$_2$ and establish key parameters for understanding and exploiting its optoelectronic behavior.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.19188v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：NbOI$_2$ 最近作为一种新型范德华材料出现，它将半导体行为与固有的平面铁电性以及明显的输运和光学各向异性相结合。然而，其光载流子动力学在很大程度上仍未被探索。在这里，我们使用飞秒泵浦探针反射测量报告了 NbOI$_2$ 的瞬态吸收光谱。在 2.34 eV 激子共振附近观察到明显的瞬态吸收特征，这是由光载流子引起的激子能量转移和饱和引起的。衰变动力学揭示了激子寿命为几十皮秒，并表现出与激子湮灭和缺陷辅助俄歇复合一致的密度依赖性行为，产生0.4 cm$^2$ s$^{-1}$的速率，与单层过渡金属二硫属化物中的速率相当。偏振分辨测量进一步揭示了线性吸收各向异性之后的瞬态响应中明显的面内各向异性。这些发现为 NbOI$_2$ 中的光载流子动力学提供了基本的见解，并建立了理解和利用其光电行为的关键参数。
