## Abstract
arXiv:1606.08693v5 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The nature of diffusion is usually studied for particles or time-evolving systems. Similar in principle, such studies can be conducted by tracking how a given function of observable properties evolves over time-akin to the evolution of observable functions-referred to as functional-diffusion. This is not the same as the system's individual trajectories, but can be regarded as a meta-trajectory. Following this idea, we measure how the approach to ergodicity evolves over time for the observed magnetization of a full Ising model with an external field. We compute the diffusive behavior of the functional across a range of temperatures via Metropolis and Glauber single-spin-flip dynamics. The system's ensemble-averaged dynamics are computed using expressions from the exact solution. Power-law behavior in the approach to ergodicity provides a classification of anomalies in functional-diffusion, demonstrating nonlinear anomalous behavior over different temperature and field ranges. Studying the ergodicity convergence of these meta-trajectories can help validate and enhance the pedagogical understanding of nonequilibrium thermodynamic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:1606.08693v5 公告类型：替换 摘要：扩散的本质通常是针对粒子或时间演化系统来研究的。原则上类似，此类研究可以通过跟踪可观察特性的给定函数如何随时间演变（类似于可观察函数的演变）（称为函数扩散）来进行。这与系统的个体轨迹不同，但可以被视为元轨迹。遵循这个想法，我们测量了对于具有外部场的完整伊辛模型的观察到的磁化强度，遍历性方法如何随着时间的推移而演变。我们通过 Metropolis 和 Glauber 单自旋翻转动力学计算了泛函在一定温度范围内的扩散行为。系统的整体平均动力学是使用精确解的表达式计算的。遍历性方法中的幂律行为提供了函数扩散异常的分类，展示了不同温度和场范围内的非线性异常行为。研究这些元轨迹的遍历收敛性可以帮助验证和增强对非平衡热力学系统的教学理解。
