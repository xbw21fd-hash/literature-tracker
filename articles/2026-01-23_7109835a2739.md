## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15439v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We investigate the dissipative dynamics of quantum population and coherence among different network topologies of a quantum network using a quantum spin model coupled to a thermal bosonic reservoir. Our study proceeds in two parts. First, we analyze a small network of Ising spins embedded in a large dissipative bath, modeled via the Lindblad master equation, where temperature arises naturally from system-bath coupling. This approach reveals how network topology shapes quantum dissipative dynamics, providing a basis for controlling quantum coherence through tailored network structures. Second, we propose a mean-field approach that extends the network to larger scales and captures dissipative dynamics in large-scale networks, connecting network topology to quantum coherence in complex systems and revealing the sensitivity of quantum coherence to network structure. Our results highlight how dissipative quantum dynamics depend on network topology, providing insight into the coherent dynamics of entangled states in networks. These results may be extended to dynamics in complex systems such as opinion propagation in social models, epidemiology, and various condensed-phase and biological systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15439v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们使用耦合到热玻色子库的量子自旋模型研究量子布居的耗散动力学以及量子网络的不同网络拓扑之间的相干性。我们的研究分两部分进行。首先，我们分析嵌入大型耗散浴中的伊辛自旋小型网络，通过 Lindblad 主方程建模，其中温度由系统浴耦合自然产生。这种方法揭示了网络拓扑如何塑造量子耗散动力学，为通过定制网络结构控制量子相干性提供了基础。其次，我们提出了一种平均场方法，将网络扩展到更大的尺度并捕获大规模网络中的耗散动力学，将网络拓扑与复杂系统中的量子相干性联系起来，并揭示量子相干性对网络结构的敏感性。我们的结果强调了耗散量子动力学如何依赖于网络拓扑，从而深入了解网络中纠缠态的相干动力学。这些结果可以扩展到复杂系统的动力学，例如社会模型、流行病学以及各种凝聚相和生物系统中的意见传播。
