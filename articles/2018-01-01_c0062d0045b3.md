## Abstract
TLDR: This work proposes a device employing a skyrmion gas to reshuffle a random signal into an uncorrelated copy of itself, and argues that its basic functionality, reminiscent of an integrate-and-fire neuron, qualifies it as a novel bio-inspired building block.
