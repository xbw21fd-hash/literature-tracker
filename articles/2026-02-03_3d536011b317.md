## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00756v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Incorporating Machine Learning (ML) into material property prediction has become a crucial step in accelerating materials discovery. A key challenge is the severe lack of training data, as many properties are too complicated to calculate with high-throughput first principles techniques. To address this, recent research has created experimental databases from information extracted from scientific literature. However, most existing experimental databases do not provide full atomic coordinate information, which prevents them from supporting advanced ML architectures such as Graph Neural Networks (GNNs). In this work, we propose to bridge this gap through an alignment process between experimental databases and Crystallographic Information Files (CIF) from the Inorganic Crystal Structure Database (ICSD). Our approach enables the creation of a database that can fully leverage state-of-the-art model architectures for material property prediction. It also opens the door to utilizing transfer learning to improve prediction accuracy. To validate our approach, we align NEMAD with the ICSD and compare models trained on the resulting database to those trained on NEMAD originally. We demonstrate significant improvements in both Mean Absolute Error (MAE) and Correct Classification Rate (CCR) in predicting the ordering temperatures and magnetic ground states of magnetic materials, respectively.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00756v1 公告类型：新 摘要：将机器学习 (ML) 纳入材料特性预测已成为加速材料发现的关键一步。一个关键的挑战是严重缺乏训练数据，因为许多属性过于复杂，无法使用高通量第一原理技术进行计算。为了解决这个问题，最近的研究根据从科学文献中提取的信息创建了实验数据库。然而，大多数现有的实验数据库不提供完整的原子坐标信息，这使得它们无法支持图神经网络（GNN）等高级机器学习架构。在这项工作中，我们建议通过实验数据库和无机晶体结构数据库（ICSD）中的晶体信息文件（CIF）之间的对齐过程来弥补这一差距。我们的方法可以创建一个数据库，该数据库可以充分利用最先进的模型架构进行材料性能预测。它还为利用迁移学习提高预测准确性打开了大门。为了验证我们的方法，我们将 NEMAD 与 ICSD 结合起来，并将在结果数据库上训练的模型与最初在 NEMAD 上训练的模型进行比较。我们在预测磁性材料的有序温度和磁性基态方面分别展示了平均绝对误差（MAE）和正确分类率（CCR）的显着改进。
