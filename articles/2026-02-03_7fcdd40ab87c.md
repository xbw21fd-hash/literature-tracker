## Abstract
arXiv:2404.14416v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Climate change exacerbates extreme weather events like heavy rainfall and flooding. As these events cause severe socioeconomic damage, accurate high-resolution simulation of precipitation is imperative. However, existing Earth System Models (ESMs) struggle to resolve small-scale dynamics and suffer from biases. Traditional statistical bias correction and downscaling methods fall short in improving spatial structure, while recent deep learning methods lack controllability and suffer from unstable training. Here, we propose a machine learning framework for simultaneous bias correction and downscaling. We first map observational and ESM data to a shared embedding space, where both are unbiased towards each other, and then train a conditional diffusion model to reverse the mapping. Only observational data is used for the training, so that the diffusion model can be employed to correct and downscale any ESM field without need for retraining. Our approach ensures statistical fidelity and preserves spatial patterns larger than a chosen spatial correction scale. We demonstrate that our approach outperforms existing statistical and deep learning methods especially regarding extreme events.

arXiv:2404.14416v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：气候变化加剧了强降雨和洪水等极端天气事件。由于这些事件造成严重的社会经济损害，因此必须对降水进行准确的高分辨率模拟。然而，现有的地球系统模型（ESM）难以解决小尺度动力学问题，并且存在偏差。传统的统计偏差校正和降尺度方法在改善空间结构方面存在不足，而最近的深度学习方法缺乏可控性并且训练不稳定。在这里，我们提出了一个用于同时偏差校正和缩小规模的机器学习框架。我们首先将观测数据和 ESM 数据映射到共享嵌入空间，其中两者彼此不存在偏见，然后训练条件扩散模型来反转映射。仅使用观测数据进行训练，因此可以采用扩散模型来校正和缩小任何 ESM 场，而无需重新训练。我们的方法确保统计保真度并保留大于所选空间校正比例的空间模式。我们证明，我们的方法优于现有的统计和深度学习方法，尤其是在极端事件方面。
