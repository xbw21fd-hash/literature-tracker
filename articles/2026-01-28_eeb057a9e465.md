## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19565v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Direct-current superconducting quantum interference devices (dc-SQUIDs) are one of the most sensitive magnetic detectors. These sensors are extensively used in the readout of superconducting transition edge sensors (TESs), which are used for the detection of weak signals. A cosmic microwave background (CMB) polarization telescope operating in 22-48 GHz is currently under developing. The TESs calorimeter of the telescope will be readout by a time-division multiplexer (TDM) SQUID readout system. We develop a two-stage dc-SQUID amplifier circuit, comprising an input-stage SQUID with 4 SQUID cells and a series SQUID array (SSA) with 100 SQUID cells. This configuration has been shown to achieve extremely high signal gain while effectively controlling system noise. We assess the system noise at $300$ $mK$ in an adiabatic demagnetisation refrigerator (ADR). The the measured magnetic flux noise of the two-stage SQUID circuit system is approximately $0.3$ ${\mu}\Phi_{0}/\sqrt{Hz}$ at $10$ $kHz$. The current noise equivalent to the input coil of input SQUID is about $2.4$ $pA/\sqrt{Hz}$. This result meets the low-noise readout requirements of the CMB TES and other applications with TES detectors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19565v1 发布类型：新 摘要：直流超导量子干涉器件（dc-SQUID）是最灵敏的磁探测器之一。这些传感器广泛用于超导过渡边缘传感器 (TES) 的读出，用于检测微弱信号。目前正在开发工作频率为 22-48 GHz 的宇宙微波背景 (CMB) 偏振望远镜。望远镜的 TES 热量计将通过时分多路复用器 (TDM) SQUID 读出系统进行读出。我们开发了一种两级dc-SQUID放大器电路，包括一个具有4个SQUID单元的输入级SQUID和一个具有100个SQUID单元的串联SQUID阵列（SSA）。事实证明，这种配置可以实现极高的信号增益，同时有效控制系统噪声。我们在绝热退磁制冷机 (ADR) 中评估系统噪声为 300$mK$。两级 SQUID 电路系统的测量磁通量噪声在 $10$ $kHz$ 时约为 $0.3$ ${\mu}\Phi_{0}/\sqrt{Hz}$。相当于输入SQUID的输入线圈的电流噪声约为$2.4$$pA/\sqrt{Hz}$。该结果满足 CMB TES 和其他带有 TES 探测器的应用的低噪声读出要求。
