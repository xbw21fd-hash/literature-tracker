## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13239v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The rotating magnetocaloric effect (RMCE) induced by self-demagnetization has been investigated in the giant magnetocaloric effect (GMCE) material Gd$_5$Si$_2$Ge$_2$. This shape-dependent effect had thus far only been reported in pure Gd, marking this as the first analysis of the effect in a sample with a magnetostructural first-order phase transition. By rotating the applied magnetic field vector while keeping its intensity constant, the demagnetizing field within a high-aspect ratio sample changes significantly, resulting in a RMCE. We characterize RMCE by determining the adiabatic temperature change ($\Delta T_{ad}^{rot}$) directly through temperature measurements, and the isothermal entropy change ($\Delta S_M^{rot}$) via magnetometry and magnetostatic simulations. We obtain a remarkable maximum $\Delta T_{ad}^{rot}$ of 1.77 K for a constant external field of 0.8 T, higher than that obtained under 1.0 T. The magnetostatic simulations not only corroborate the highly non-monotonous field-dependence of $|\Delta S_{M}^{rot}|$, which reaches 95\% of its maximum value at 0.8 T, 6.12 J K$^{-1}$ kg$^{-1}$ for the experimentally measured shape, but also estimate a 35\% increase in the maximum $|\Delta S_{M}^{rot}|$ up to 8.67 J K$^{-1}$ kg$^{-1}$ in a simulated shape with higher aspect ratio.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13239v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在巨磁热效应 (GMCE) 材料 Gd$_5$Si$_2$Ge$_2$ 中研究了自退磁引起的旋转磁热效应 (RMCE)。迄今为止，这种形状依赖性效应仅在纯 Gd 中被报道过，这标志着这是对具有磁结构一级相变的样品中的效应的首次分析。通过旋转所施加的磁场矢量并保持其强度恒定，高纵横比样品内的退磁场会发生显着变化，从而产生 RMCE。我们通过直接通过温度测量确定绝热温度变化 ($\Delta T_{ad}^{rot}$) 以及通过磁力测量和静磁模拟确定等温熵变化 ($\Delta S_M^{rot}$) 来表征 RMCE。对于 0.8 T 的恒定外场，我们获得了 1.77 K 的显着最大值 $\Delta T_{ad}^{rot}$，高于 1.0 T 下获得的值。静磁模拟不仅证实了 $|\Delta S_{M}^{rot}|$ 的高度非单调场依赖性，在 0.8 T、6.12 J 时达到其最大值的 95\% K$^{-1}$ kg$^{-1}$ 对于实验测量的形状，而且在具有更高纵横比的模拟形状中估计最大 $|\Delta S_{M}^{rot}|$ 增加 35\%，高达 8.67 J K$^{-1}$ kg$^{-1}$。
