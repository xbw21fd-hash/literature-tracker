## Abstract
This study investigates the protective potential of Juniperus Sabina L. essential oil against renal damage caused by gentamicin, a common antibiotic known for its nephrotoxic side effects. The chemical composition of the essential oil was analyzed using GC-MS, identifying Sabinene as a primary constituent. Rats were divided into a control group (gentamicin-induced) and treatment groups receiving 160 mg/kg and 320 mg/kg of the extract. Histopathological examination revealed that the control group exhibited severe tubular necrosis and degeneration. Treatment with 160 mg/kg of J. Sabina extract showed no significant pathological changes, indicating strong protection. The 320 mg/kg dose also demonstrated minimal degeneration and an absence of necrosis, although slight inflammatory cell infiltration was observed around renal tubules. The findings suggest that J. Sabina essential oil possesses significant nephroprotective properties, effectively attenuating structural damage induced by gentamicin.

## 摘要（中文）
这项研究调查了杜松精油对庆大霉素引起的肾损伤的保护潜力，庆大霉素是一种以其肾毒性副作用而闻名的常见抗生素。使用 GC-MS 分析精油的化学成分，确定桧烯为主要成分。将大鼠分为对照组（庆大霉素诱导）和接受 160 mg/kg 和 320 mg/kg 提取物的治疗组。组织病理学检查显示对照组出现严重的肾小管坏死和变性。用160 mg/kg的圆柏提取物处理，未见明显的病理变化，显示出较强的保护作用。尽管在肾小管周围观察到轻微的炎症细胞浸润，但 320 mg/kg 剂量也显示出最小的变性和不存在坏死。研究结果表明，J. Sabina 精油具有显着的肾保护特性，可有效减轻庆大霉素引起的结构损伤。
