## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04724v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Here, we demonstrate K-ion intercalation-mediated resistive switching in Prussian blue analogs (PBAs), a mechanism widely exploited in potassium batteries but not previously resolved at the nanoscale for memristive operation. Using C-AFM, we directly visualize and electrically control this intercalation process within sub-100-nm volumes, revealing reversible, localized conductance modulation driven by K-ion intercalation and Fe2+/Fe3+ redox reconfiguration. This nanoscale operability highlights the exceptional potential of PBAs for high-scalable and low-dimension memristor-based devices integration. Due to their modular composition, PBAs constitute a chemically rich, earth-abundant materials platform whose electronic and ionic properties can be precisely tuned for specific device functions. K-ion intercalation PBA-based memristor devices, with their singlestep, aqueous, and room-temperature fabrication, enable low-cost, large-scale processing compatible with CMOS, without any additional post-fabrication processing. Our findings establish PBAs as a new class of intercalation memristors with scalable nanoscale switching and exceptional materials versatility, toward highly integrated neuromorphic and non-volatile memory technologies. This work provides the first demonstration of intercalation-driven resistive switching under ultrafast voltage sweeps, with PW operating up to 200 V/s and PB up to 50 V/s. This unprecedented speed establishes PBAs as a distinct, high-rate class of K-ion intercalation memristors suitable for fast, high-density neuromorphic and memory applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04724v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在这里，我们演示了普鲁士蓝类似物 (PBA) 中钾离子嵌入介导的电阻切换，这是一种在钾电池中广泛利用的机制，但之前尚未在纳米尺度上解决忆阻操作的问题。使用 C-AFM，我们直接可视化并电气控制亚 100 nm 体积内的嵌入过程，揭示由 K 离子嵌入和 Fe2+/Fe3+ 氧化还原重构驱动的可逆、局部电导调制。这种纳米级的可操作性凸显了 PBA 在高可扩展性和低尺寸基于忆阻器的设备集成方面的巨大潜力。由于其模块化组成，PBA 构成了一个化学丰富、地球资源丰富的材料平台，其电子和离子特性可以针对特定的设备功能进行精确调整。基于 K 离子嵌入 PBA 的忆阻器器件采用单步、水相和室温制造，可实现与 CMOS 兼容的低成本、大规模处理，无需任何额外的后制造处理。我们的研究结果将 PBA 确立为一种新型嵌入忆阻器，具有可扩展的纳米级开关和卓越的材料多功能性，面向高度集成的神经形态和非易失性存储技术。这项工作首次演示了超快电压扫描下插层驱动的电阻开关，PW 运行速度高达 200 V/s，PB 运行速度高达 50 V/s。这种前所未有的速度使 PBA 成为一种独特的高速率 K 离子嵌入忆阻器，适用于快速、高密度的神经形态和记忆应用。
