## Abstract
arXiv:2510.26860v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Semiconductor quantum processors have potential to scale to modular quantum computers, in which qubit registers are coupled by quantum links, enabling high connectivity and space for control circuitry. Individual spin-qubit registers have progressed to two-dimensional systems and execution of small quantum algorithms. Separately, high-fidelity spin shuttling has been demonstrated in linear channels defined by individual gate electrodes. Here, we realize the first shared-control shuttling link integrated between distant qubit registers to demonstrate quantum entanglement in a basic modular quantum processor based on hole spin qubits in germanium. We develop a protocol to compensate for spin-orbit-induced rotations during qubit transfer, allowing for shuttling between qubit registers separated by more than one micrometer in approximately a hundred nanoseconds. Combining local qubit operation with coherent shuttling, we generate Bell states formed by spins residing in separate registers. Characterizing them using quantum state tomography, we demonstrate entanglement between spin qubits in distant registers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.26860v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：半导体量子处理器有潜力扩展到模块化量子计算机，其中量子位寄存器通过量子链路耦合，从而实现控制电路的高连接性和空间。单独的自旋量子位寄存器已经发展到二维系统和小型量子算法的执行。另外，高保真自旋穿梭已在由各个栅电极定义的线性通道中得到证明。在这里，我们实现了第一个在远程量子位寄存器之间集成的共享控制穿梭链路，以演示基于锗空穴自旋量子位的基本模块化量子处理器中的量子纠缠。我们开发了一种协议来补偿量子位传输过程中自旋轨道引起的旋转，允许在大约一百纳秒内在相距超过一微米的量子位寄存器之间穿梭。将局部量子位操作与相干穿梭相结合，我们生成由驻留在单独寄存器中的自旋形成的贝尔态。我们使用量子态断层扫描对它们进行表征，证明了远程寄存器中自旋量子位之间的纠缠。
