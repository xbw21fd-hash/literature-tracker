## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01941v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Autoregressive learning of time-stepping operators offers an effective approach to data-driven PDE simulation on grids. For conservation laws, however, long-horizon rollouts are often destabilized when learned updates violate global conservation and, in many applications, additional state bounds such as nonnegative mass and densities or concentrations constrained to [0,1]. Enforcing these coupled constraints via direct next-state regression remains difficult. We introduce a framework for learning conservative transport operators on regular grids, inspired by lattice Boltzmann-style discrete-velocity transport representations. Instead of predicting the next state, the model outputs local transport operators that update cells through neighborhood exchanges, guaranteeing discrete conservation by construction. For bounded quantities, we parameterize transport within a capacity-constrained feasible set, enforcing bounds structurally rather than by post-hoc clipping. We validate FluxNet on 1D convection-diffusion, 2D shallow water equations, 1D traffic flow, and 2D spinodal decomposition. Experiments on shallow-water equations and traffic flow show improved rollout stability and physical consistency over strong baselines. On phase-field spinodal decomposition, the method enables large time-steps with long-range transport, accelerating simulation while preserving microstructure evolution in both pointwise and statistical measures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01941v1 公告类型：新 摘要：时间步进算子的自回归学习为网格上数据驱动的 PDE 模拟提供了一种有效的方法。然而，对于守恒定律来说，当学习到的更新违反全局守恒定律时，长范围的推出通常会不稳定，并且在许多应用中，额外的状态边界（例如非负质量和密度或浓度限制为 [0,1]）。通过直接的下一状态回归来强制执行这些耦合约束仍然很困难。受格子玻尔兹曼式离散速度传输表示的启发，我们引入了一个在规则网格上学习保守传输算子的框架。该模型不是预测下一个状态，而是输出本地交通运营商，通过邻里交换更新单元格，通过构建保证离散保护。对于有界数量，我们在容量受限的可行集中参数化传输，从结构上强制执行边界，而不是通过事后裁剪。我们在 1D 对流扩散、2D 浅水方程、1D 交通流和 2D 旋节线分解上验证 FluxNet。浅水方程和交通流的实验表明，在强基线上，滚动稳定性和物理一致性得到了改善。在相场旋节线分解方面，该方法能够实现长程输运的大时间步长，加速模拟，同时保留逐点和统计测量中的微观结构演化。
