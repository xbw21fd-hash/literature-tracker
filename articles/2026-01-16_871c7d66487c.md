## Abstract
Elastin-derived peptides (EDPs) modulate key cellular processes through binding to the elastin-binding protein (EBP), yet the molecular basis underlying their differential activities remains unclear. In this study, we combined systematic molecular docking with duplicate 1 μs molecular dynamics (MD) simulations to characterize the binding modes and interaction networks of biologically active (LGTIPG, PGAIPG) and inactive (VVGPGA, VPVGGA, GGVPG) elastin-derived peptides (EDPs) within the V32 pocket of EBP. Our results reveal two partially overlapping functional regions within the V32 pocket: residues 98–114, preferentially engaged by biologically active peptides and galactosugars, and residues 83–104, primarily utilized by inactive peptides. This supports a model in which active peptides and galactosugars share a common region, while inactive peptides preferentially occupy an adjacent, partially overlapping subregion, challenging the notion of entirely separate EBP binding sites. Active peptides formed strong, persistent interactions, particularly through D98 and E99, whereas inactive peptides relied primarily on salt bridges with E137 and hydrogen bonds with S101. MM/PBSA binding free energies were in full agreement with experimental activity trends, indicating that binding affinity and pocket preference jointly determine biological activity. Notably, although type VIII β-turns are traditionally linked to EDP bioactivity, the peptides remained predominantly extended when bound to EBP, highlighting that binding-site selection and interaction networks, rather than β-turn formation, govern peptide activity. The surface residues P233 and I234 appeared as potential mediators of EBP’s interactions with Neu-1 and PPCA within the elastin receptor complex, supporting previous findings.

## 摘要（中文）
弹性蛋白衍生肽（EDP）通过与弹性蛋白结合蛋白（EBP）结合来调节关键的细胞过程，但其差异活性的分子基础仍不清楚。在本研究中，我们将系统分子对接与重复的 1 μs 分子动力学 (MD) 模拟相结合，以表征 EBP V32 口袋内生物活性 (LGTIPG、PGAIPG) 和非活性 (VVGPGA、VPVGGA、GGVPG) 弹性蛋白衍生肽 (EDP) 的结合模式和相互作用网络。我们的结果揭示了 V32 口袋内两个部分重叠的功能区域：残基 98-114，优先由生物活性肽和半乳糖参与，残基 83-104，主要由非活性肽利用。这支持了一种模型，其中活性肽和半乳糖共享一个共同区域，而无活性肽优先占据相邻的、部分重叠的子区域，挑战了完全独立的 EBP 结合位点的概念。活性肽形成强烈、持久的相互作用，特别是通过 D98 和 E99，而无活性肽主要依赖于与 E137 的盐桥和与 S101 的氢键。 MM/PBSA 结合自由能与实验活性趋势完全一致，表明结合亲和力和口袋偏好共同决定生物活性。值得注意的是，尽管 VIII 型 β 转角传统上与 EDP 生物活性相关，但这些肽在与 EBP 结合时仍然主要保持延伸，这凸显了控制肽活性的是结合位点选择和相互作用网络，而不是 β 转角形成。表面残基 P233 和 I234 似乎是 EBP 与弹性蛋白受体复合物内 Neu-1 和 PPCA 相互作用的潜在介质，支持了之前的发现。
