## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15031v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnons in antiferromagnets exhibit two chiral modes, providing an intrinsic degree of freedom for magnon-based computing architectures and spintronic devices. Electrical control of chiral splitting is crucial for applications, but remains challenging. Here, we propose the concept of extrinsic chiral splitting, involving alternating and ferrimagnet-like types, which can be induced and controlled by an electric field. A symmetry framework based on 464 collinear spin layer groups is established to classify chiral splitting characteristics and electric field responses in two-dimensional magnets. We further elucidate how the spin layer group determines the type of alternating chiral splitting and the dominant lowest-order magnetic exchange interaction. We demonstrate electric-field control over the magnitude and sign of the chiral splitting, enabling control of the spin Seebeck and Nernst effects related to thermal spin transport. This work provides a general theory for electric field manipulation of magnon chirality, paving the way for low-power magnonic logic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15031v1 公告类型：新 摘要：反铁磁体中的磁振子表现出两种手性模式，为基于磁振子的计算架构和自旋电子器件提供了内在的自由度。手性分裂的电控制对于应用至关重要，但仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们提出了外在手性分裂的概念，涉及交替和类亚铁磁体类型，可以通过电场感应和控制。建立了基于 464 个共线自旋层组的对称框架，对二维磁体中的手性分裂特性和电场响应进行分类。我们进一步阐明了自旋层组如何决定交替手性分裂的类型和占主导地位的最低阶磁交换相互作用。我们演示了电场对手性分裂的大小和符号的控制，从而能够控制与热自旋输运相关的自旋塞贝克和能斯特效应。这项工作为磁振子手性的电场操纵提供了一般理论，为低功耗磁振子逻辑器件铺平了道路。
