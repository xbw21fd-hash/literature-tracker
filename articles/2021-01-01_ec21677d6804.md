## Abstract
TLDR: A neuroevolution-potential framework for generating neural network based machine-learning potentials is developed and per-atom heat current is available in NEP, which paves the way for efficient and accurate MD simulations of heat transport in materials with strong phonon anharmonicity or spatial disorder.
