## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20702v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Chiral spin liquid (CSL) is a magnetic analogue of the fractional quantum Hall (FQH) liquid. Collective excitations play a vital role in shaping our understanding of these exotic quantum phases of matter and their quantum phase transitions. While the magneto-roton and long-wavelength chiral graviton modes in the FQH liquids have been extensively explored, the collective excitations of CSLs remain elusive. Here we explore the collective excitations in the SU(2) symmetric CSL phase of the spin-1/2 square-lattice $J_1-J_2-J_\chi$ model, where an intriguing quantum phase diagram was recently revealed. Combining exact diagonalization and time-dependent variational principle calculations, we observe two spin-singlet collective modes: a chiral p-wave (low-energy) roton mode at finite momentum and a d-wave (higher-energy) nematic mode at zero momentum, both of which are prominent across the CSL phase. Such exotic modes exhibit fingerprints distinct from those of FQH liquids, and to the best of our knowledge, are reported for the first time. By tuning $J_2$, we find the nematic mode to be pronouncedly soft, together with the spin-triplet two-spinon bound states, potentially promoting strong nematic and spin stripe instabilities. Our work paves the way for further understanding CSL from the dynamical perspective and provides new spectroscopic signatures for future experiments of CSL candidates.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20702v1 公告类型：新 摘要：手性自旋液体（CSL）是分数量子霍尔（FQH）液体的磁性类似物。集体激发在我们理解这些奇异的物质量子相及其量子相变方面发挥着至关重要的作用。虽然 FQH 液体中的磁旋子和长波长手性引力子模式已被广泛探索，但 CSL 的集体激发仍然难以捉摸。在这里，我们探索自旋 1/2 方晶格 $J_1-J_2-J_\chi$ 模型的 SU(2) 对称 CSL 相中的集体激发，其中最近揭示了一个有趣的量子相图。结合精确的对角化和时间相关变分原理计算，我们观察到两种自旋单重集体模式：有限动量下的手性 p 波（低能量）旋转模式和零动量下的 d 波（高能）向列模式，这两种模式在 CSL 相中都很突出。这种奇异模式表现出与 FQH 液体不同的指纹，据我们所知，这是首次报道。通过调整 $J_2$，我们发现向列模式明显较软，加上自旋三重态双自旋束缚态，可能会促进强向列和自旋条纹不稳定性。我们的工作为从动力学角度进一步理解 CSL 铺平了道路，并为 CSL 候选者的未来实验提供了新的光谱特征。
