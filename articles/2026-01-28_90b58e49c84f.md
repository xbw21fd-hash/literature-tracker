## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19289v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: After a very calm 24th solar activity cycle, the 25th cycle has already seen several interesting events. A Ground Level Enhancement GLE77 was observed on 11 November 2025 following an X5.1 class solar flare. A strong Forbush decrease occurred on 19 and 20 January 2026 during one of the most intense geomagnetic storms of Solar Cycle 25. Events were recorded coherently by the global neutron monitor network and by SEVAN detectors at multiple altitudes. Using spectrometric capabilities, we reconstruct energy spectra of missing neutrons and muons during the FD and compare them with corresponding spectra measured during GLE77. The analysis demonstrates that FD and GLE signatures are intrinsically asymmetric. FDs selectively suppress the preexisting galactic cosmic ray population, whereas GLEs introduce an additional, harder particle component. Neutron and muon channels exhibit markedly different spectral behavior, particularly at higher deposited energies, reflecting their sensitivity to different primary energy ranges. These results show that combined NM and SEVAN observations provide robust, complementary diagnostics of rigidity dependent cosmic ray modulation during extreme heliospheric disturbances.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19289v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在非常平静的第24个太阳活动周期之后，第25个周期已经出现了一些有趣的事件。 2025 年 11 月 11 日，在 X5.1 级太阳耀斑之后观测到地面增强 GLE77。 2026 年 1 月 19 日至 20 日，在第 25 太阳周期最强烈的地磁暴之一期间，福布什发生了强烈的减弱。全球中子监测网络和多个高度的 SEVAN 探测器对这些事件进行了连贯记录。利用光谱测量功能，我们重建了 FD 期间缺失的中子和 μ 子的能谱，并将它们与 GLE77 期间测量的相应能谱进行比较。分析表明 FD 和 GLE 签名本质上是不对称的。 FD 选择性地抑制先前存在的银河宇宙射线群，而 GLE 引入了额外的、更硬的粒子成分。中子和μ子通道表现出明显不同的光谱行为，特别是在较高的沉积能量下，反映了它们对不同初级能量范围的敏感性。这些结果表明，结合 NM 和 SEVAN 观测结果可以对极端日光层扰动期间刚性相关的宇宙射线调制提供稳健、互补的诊断。
