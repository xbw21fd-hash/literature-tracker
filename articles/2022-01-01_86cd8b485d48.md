## Abstract
TLDR: This work introduces a framework to transfer CCSD(T) accuracy of finite molecular clusters to extended condensed phase systems using a high-dimensional neural network potential, which allows one to perform high-quality coupled cluster molecular dynamics, CCMD, as it is demonstrated for liquid water including nuclear quantum effects.
