## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17775v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The nonlinear Nernst and Seebeck effects (NNE and NSE) offer promising routes for thermoelectric energy conversion in non-magnetic systems. While intrinsic mechanisms such as the nonlinear Drude and Berry-curvature-dipole terms are well established, extrinsic contributions to thermoelectric responses arising from disorder-induced asymmetric scattering remain comparatively less explored, despite growing experimental evidence of their dominance. Here, we develop a unified semiclassical theory of NNE and NSE that incorporates skew scattering and side-jump processes, identifying four distinct extrinsic contributions to NNE and two for NSE. A systematic symmetry analysis shows that these responses are allowed in time-reversal-symmetric non-magnets, PT-symmetric antiferromagnets, and non-centrosymmetric magnetic systems such as altermagnets. As a case study, we demonstrate that ABA-stacked trilayer graphene hosts large nonlinear Nernst and Seebeck responses dominated by extrinsic scattering, in excellent agreement with recent experiments. Our results establish the microscopic origin of these effects and provide guiding principles for designing high-efficiency nonlinear thermoelectric devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.17775v1 公告类型：新 摘要：非线性能斯特效应和塞贝克效应（NNE 和 NSE）为非磁系统中的热电能量转换提供了有前景的途径。虽然诸如非线性德鲁德和贝里曲率偶极子项之类的内在机制已经很成熟，但由无序引起的不对称散射引起的热电响应的外在贡献仍然相对较少被探索，尽管越来越多的实验证据表明它们占主导地位。在这里，我们开发了一种统一的 NNE 和 NSE 半经典理论，该理论结合了斜散射和侧跳过程，确定了 NNE 的四种不同的外在贡献和 NSE 的两种不同的外在贡献。系统对称分析表明，这些响应在时间反转对称非磁体、PT 对称反铁磁体和非中心对称磁系统（例如交流磁体）中是允许的。作为一个案例研究，我们证明了 ABA 堆叠的三层石墨烯具有由外在散射主导的大非线性能斯特和塞贝克响应，与最近的实验非常一致。我们的结果确立了这些效应的微观起源，并为设计高效非线性热电器件提供了指导原则。
