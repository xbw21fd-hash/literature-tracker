## Abstract
arXiv:2502.08389v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We numerically study the Kitaev honeycomb model with the additional XX Ising interaction between the nearest and the next nearest neighbors (Kitaev-Ising-$J_1$-$J_2$ model), by using the density matrix renormalization group (DMRG) method. Such additional interaction correspond to the nearest and diagonal interactions on the square lattice. Phase diagram of the bare Kitaev model consist of low entangled commensurate magnetic phases and entangled Kitaev spin liquid. Anisotropic Ising interaction allows the entangled quantum paramagnetic phases in the phase diagram, which in the absence of the magnetic field previously was predicted for more complex type of interaction. We study the scaling law of the entanglement entropy and the bond dimension of the matrix product state with the size of the system. In addition, we propose an optimization algorithm to prevent DMRG from getting stuck in the low-entangled phases.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2502.08389v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们通过使用密度矩阵重整化群 (DMRG) 方法，对最近邻和次近邻之间附加 XX Ising 相互作用的 Kitaev 蜂窝模型（Kitaev-Ising-$J_1$-$J_2$ 模型）进行数值研究。这种额外的相互作用对应于方形晶格上的最近的和对角线的相互作用。裸基塔耶夫模型的相图由低纠缠的相应磁相和纠缠的基塔耶夫自旋液体组成。各向异性伊辛相互作用允许相图中出现纠缠的量子顺磁相，这在没有磁场的情况下先前被预测为更复杂类型的相互作用。我们研究了纠缠熵和矩阵积态的键维数与系统尺寸的标度规律。此外，我们提出了一种优化算法来防止 DMRG 陷入低纠缠相。
