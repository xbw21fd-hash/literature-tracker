## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16417v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Jet tagging, identifying the origin of jets produced in particle collisions, is a critical classification task in high-energy physics. Despite the revolutionary impact of deep learning on jet tagging over the past decade, the paradigm has remained unchanged. In particular, jets are classified independently, one at a time. This single-jet approach ignores correlations, overlaps, and wider event context between jets. We introduce PanopTag, a new paradigm for jet tagging that departs from traditional single-jet tagging approaches. Rather than classifying jets independently, PanopTag simultaneously tags all jets by employing an encoder-decoder architecture that uses jet kinematics as queries to cross-attend to particle flow object embeddings. We evaluate PanopTag on heavy-flavor $(b/c)$-tagging and demonstrate remarkable performance improvements over state-of-the-art single-jet baselines that are only accessible by exploiting event-level features and correlations between jets.

arXiv:2601.16417v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：射流标记，识别粒子碰撞中产生的射流来源，是高能物理中的一项关键分类任务。尽管过去十年深度学习对飞机标记产生了革命性的影响，但范式仍然没有改变。特别是，喷气式飞机是独立分类的，一次一架。这种单喷气机方法忽略了喷气机之间的相关性、重叠和更广泛的事件背景。我们引入了 PanopTag，这是一种不同于传统单喷射标记方法的新喷射标记范例。 PanopTag 不是独立对射流进行分类，而是通过采用编码器-解码器架构来同时标记所有射流，该架构使用射流运动学作为查询来交叉参与粒子流对象嵌入。我们在重口味 $(b/c)$ 标记上评估 PanopTag，并展示了相对于最先进的单喷射基线的显着性能改进，这些基线只能通过利用事件级特征和喷射之间的相关性来访问。
