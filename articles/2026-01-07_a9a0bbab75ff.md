## Abstract
arXiv:2509.19932v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The fundamental role of magnetic materials in modern science and technology has driven a rapid surge in research on unconventional magnetism in recent years. In particular, altermagnets, which simultaneously exhibit zero net magnetization in real space and anisotropic spin splitting in momentum space, have garnered significant interest for both fundamental physics and technological applications. Among these, $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$ stands as the pioneering and most extensively studied altermagnet. While the intrinsic magnetic order of $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$ is still a subject of active debate, numerous exotic phenomena characteristic of altermagnetism have been observed in $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$ samples. In this review, we explore each facet of the altermagnetism through specific case studies in $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$, systematically surveying its crystal and magnetic structures, electronic band properties, and transport phenomena. We critically assess the debate surrounding the intrinsic magnetism in $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$, incorporating evidence from altermagnetic signatures in transport, as well as contrasting results from magnetic and spectroscopic measurements. Finally, possible future research directions in this field are discussed.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.19932v2 发布类型：替换 摘要：磁性材料在现代科学技术中的基础性作用，带动了近年来非常规磁性研究的快速激增。特别是，交替磁体同时在真实空间中表现出零净磁化强度，在动量空间中表现出各向异性自旋分裂，引起了基础物理和技术应用的极大兴趣。其中，$\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$ 是开创性且研究最广泛的交流磁体。虽然$\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$的内在磁序仍然是一个活跃争论的话题，但在$\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$样品中观察到了许多具有交变磁性特征的奇异现象。在这篇综述中，我们通过 $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$ 中的具体案例研究探索交变磁性的各个方面，系统地调查其晶体和磁性结构、电子能带特性和输运现象。我们批判性地评估了围绕 $\mathrm{RuO_{2}}$ 中固有磁性的争论，结合了运输中交变磁特征的证据，以及磁性和光谱测量的对比结果。最后，讨论了该领域未来可能的研究方向。
