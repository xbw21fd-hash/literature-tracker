## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05348v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Conventional radiotherapy dose calculation algorithms are often computationally slow and non-differentiable, creating bottlenecks for online adaptive radiotherapy (ART) and limiting end-to-end automatic planning. Deep learning provides consistent inference performance and a differentiable framework essential for rapid optimization. In this study, we developed a generalized, site-independent deep learning dose engine using a beamlet-based input strategy. This establishes a computationally consistent and differentiable module that enables end-to-end training for autoplanning while maintaining accuracy across diverse geometries. A dataset of 3,600 plans from 120 patients across six anatomical sites was used to train two 3D convolutional neural networks, a standard U-Net and a Cascade U-Net, to predict 3D dose distributions from CT images and divergent MLC/jaw projections. Performance was validated via 3D gamma analysis on an independent cohort of 60 VMAT plans. The optimal model (U-Net with MAE loss) achieved a mean gamma passing rate of $98.9 \pm 1.6\%$ (3%/2mm, 10% threshold). Performance remained robust across all sites (passing rates $>98\%$), demonstrating that the beamlet-based strategy generalizes effectively to complex geometries without site-specific training. These results indicate that a single, site-independent model can calculate radiotherapy dose distributions with clinical accuracy. This differentiable engine is highly suitable for integration into end-to-end automatic planning, online ART, and secondary dose verification workflows.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05348v1 公告类型：新 摘要：传统的放射治疗剂量计算算法通常计算速度慢且不可微分，为在线自适应放射治疗（ART）造成瓶颈并限制端到端自动规划。深度学习提供一致的推理性能和快速优化所必需的可微框架​​。在这项研究中，我们使用基于小束的输入策略开发了一种通用的、与站点无关的深度学习剂量引擎。这建立了一个计算上一致且可微分的模块，可以实现自动规划的端到端训练，同时保持不同几何形状的准确性。使用来自 6 个解剖部位的 120 名患者的 3,600 个计划的数据集来训练两个 3D 卷积神经网络（标准 U-Net 和级联 U-Net），以根据 CT 图像和发散的 MLC/下颌投影预测 3D 剂量分布。通过对 60 个 VMAT 计划的独立队列进行 3D 伽马分析来验证性能。最佳模型（具有 MAE 损失的 U-Net）实现了 $98.9 \pm 1.6\%$ 的平均伽玛通过率（3%/2mm，10% 阈值）。所有站点的性能仍然强劲（通过率 $>98\%$），这表明基于小束的策略可以有效地推广到复杂的几何形状，而无需进行特定站点的培训。这些结果表明，单一的、与部位无关的模型可以以临床准确性计算放射治疗剂量分布。这种可微分引擎非常适合集成到端到端自动规划、在线 ART 和二次剂量验证工作流程中。
