## Abstract
arXiv:2505.04976v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We systematically investigated crystal-field (CF) excitations of Kramers (Nd$^{3+}$, Er$^{3+}$, Yb$^{3+}$) and non-Kramers (Pr$^{3+}$, Ho$^{3+}$) rare-earth ions in $REFeO_3$ using optimized CF simulations. Internal magnetic fields from the Fe$^{3+}$ and RE$^{3+}$ sublattices split the ground-state doublets of all Kramers ions (e.g. $NdFeO_3$, $ErFeO_3$, and $YbFeO_3$), generating low-energy excitations around 1 meV. In non-Kramers systems, low-energy excitations arise only when the ground state forms an accidental pseudo-doublet, as observed for Ho$^{3+}$ in $HoFeO_3$; such pseudo-doublets exhibit field-induced splitting analogous to Kramers ions. In contrast, true singlet ground states, exemplified by Pr$^{3+}$, show no zero-field splitting. Strong anisotropies are found in both internal- and external-field responses of the CF excitations in these $REFeO_3$. These results provide a unified explanation for the anomalous Zeeman splitting of CF ground states in $REFeO_3$.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.04976v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们使用优化的 CF 模拟，系统地研究了 $REFeO_3$ 中 Kramers (Nd$^{3+}$、Er$^{3+}$、Yb$^{3+}$) 和非 Kramers (Pr$^{3+}$、Ho$^{3+}$) 稀土离子的晶体场 (CF) 激发。 Fe$^{3+}$ 和 RE$^{3+}$ 亚晶格的内部磁场分裂所有克莱默离子（例如 $NdFeO_3$、$ErFeO_3$ 和 $YbFeO_3$）的基态双峰，产生约 1 meV 的低能激发。在非克莱默系统中，只有当基态形成偶然的伪双峰时才会出现低能激发，正如在 $HoFeO_3$ 中对 Ho$^{3+}$ 观察到的那样；这种赝双峰表现出类似于克莱默离子的场诱导分裂。相反，真正的单重态基态（以 Pr$^{3+}$ 为例）没有表现出零场分裂。在这些 $REFeO_3$ 中 CF 激励的内部和外部场响应中都发现了强各向异性。这些结果为 $REFeO_3$ 中 CF 基态的异常塞曼分裂提供了统一的解释。
