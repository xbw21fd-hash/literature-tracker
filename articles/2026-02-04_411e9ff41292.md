## Abstract
arXiv:2507.08418v4 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Neural quantum states are a promising framework for simulating many-body quantum dynamics, as they can represent states with volume-law entanglement. As time evolves, the neural network parameters are typically optimized at discrete time steps to approximate the wave function at each point in time. Given the differentiability of the wave function stemming from the Schr\"odinger equation, here we impose a time-continuous and differentiable parameterization of the neural network by expressing its parameters as linear combinations of temporal basis functions with trainable, time-independent coefficients. We test this ansatz, referred to as the smooth neural quantum state (\textit{s}-NQS) with a loss function defined over an extended time interval, under a sudden quench of a non-integrable many-body quantum spin chain. We demonstrate accurate time evolution using a restricted Boltzmann machine as the instantaneous neural network architecture. We show that the parameterization enables accurate simulations with fewer variational parameters, independent of time-step resolution. Furthermore, the smooth neural quantum state also allows us to initialize and evaluate the wave function at times not included in the training set, both within and beyond the training interval.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.08418v4 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：神经量子态是模拟多体量子动力学的一个有前途的框架，因为它们可以表示具有体积定律纠缠的状态。随着时间的推移，神经网络参数通常在离散时间步长上进行优化，以逼近每个时间点的波函数。考虑到源于薛定格方程的波函数的可微性，这里我们通过将其参数表示为时间基函数与可训练的、时间无关系数的线性组合来对神经网络进行时间连续且可微的参数化。我们在不可积多体量子自旋链的突然猝灭下测试了这个 ansatz，称为平滑神经量子态（\textit{s}-NQS），其损失函数在延长的时间间隔内定义。我们使用受限玻尔兹曼机作为瞬时神经网络架构演示了精确的时间演化，我们表明参数化可以使用更少的变分参数进行精确模拟，而与时间步分辨率无关。此外，平滑的神经量子状态还允许我们有时初始化和评估不包含在训练集中的波函数，无论是在训练间隔内还是在训练间隔外。
