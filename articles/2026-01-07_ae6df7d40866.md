## Abstract
arXiv:2506.20871v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Photonic flatbands offer promising light-matter interaction due to their unique slow-light nature. In recent years, flatbands have also attracted significant interest in optical engineering because of their angle-insensitive resonant characteristics. However, to date, no studies have reported the dispersionless behavior of flatbands under arbitrary two-dimensional incident angles. Here, we present a two-dimensional photonic flatband created using a silicon metasurface with a Lieb lattice-inspired structure which demonstrates a locally flat photonic band for both transverse electric (TE) and transverse magnetic (TM) polarized light. Employing Fourier imaging, we analyze the energy-momentum dispersion of the flatband metasurface under arbitrary two-dimensional incident angles, demonstrating dispersionless flatbands with a change in resonance within $\pm2 nm$ up to $\pm24\^o$ or $\pm5\^o$, depending on the polarization state and incident angle. This geometry can be adapted for various applications in local field enhancement, enhanced photodetection, and augmented reality displays.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.20871v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：光子平带由于其独特的慢光性质而提供了有前景的光与物质相互作用。近年来，平带由于其对角度不敏感的谐振特性也引起了光学工程的极大兴趣。然而，迄今为止，还没有研究报道任意二维入射角下平带的无色散行为。在这里，我们提出了使用具有利布晶格启发结构的硅超表面创建的二维光子平带，该二维光子平带展示了横向电（TE）和横向磁（TM）偏振光的局部平坦光子带。采用傅里叶成像，我们分析了任意二维入射角下平带超表面的能量-动量色散，证明了无色散平带的谐振变化在 $\pm2 nm$ 至 $\pm24\^o$ 或 $\pm5\^o$ 范围内，具体取决于偏振态和入射角。这种几何形状可适用于局部场增强、增强光电探测和增强现实显示等各种应用。
