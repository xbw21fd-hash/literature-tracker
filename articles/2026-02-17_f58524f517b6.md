## Abstract
We investigate the microscopic origin of the ultralow lattice thermal conductivity in Rb 2 ZnTe by combining anharmonic lattice dynamics based on first-principles density functional theory with the Boltzmann transport equation (BTE). Full cubic and quartic anharmonicity is included, accounting for phonon renormalization and both three-phonon and four-phonon scattering processes. In addition, due to the unusually strong coherence effects in Rb 2 ZnTe, we consider off-diagonal contributions beyond the conventional BTE framework. The calculated phonon transport approaches the Ioffe–Regel limit, indicating the breakdown of the quasiparticle picture. To obtain an accurate thermal conductivity, we employ a machine-learning potential and perform Green–Kubo molecular dynamics simulations using GPUMD. At 300 K, the computed thermal conductivity increases by 38% compared to previous estimates, yet remains close to the amorphous limit, highlighting Rb 2 ZnTe as a promising thermoelectric material. The methodology presented here provides a robust framework for understanding lattice thermal transport in strongly anharmonic crystals and guiding the rational design of low-thermal-conductivity materials.

## 摘要（中文）
我们通过将基于第一性原理密度泛函理论的非谐晶格动力学与玻尔兹曼输运方程（BTE）相结合，研究了 Rb 2 ZnTe 中超低晶格热导率的微观起源。包括完整的三次和四次非谐性，解释声子重整化以及三声子和四声子散射过程。此外，由于 Rb 2 ZnTe 中异常强的相干效应，我们考虑了传统 BTE 框架之外的非对角线贡献。计算出的声子输运接近 Ioffe-Regel 极限，表明准粒子图像被破坏。为了获得准确的热导率，我们利用机器学习潜力并使用 GPUMD 执行 Green-Kubo 分子动力学模拟。在 300 K 时，计算出的热导率比之前的估计增加了 38%，但仍接近非晶态极限，凸显了 Rb 2 ZnTe 作为一种有前途的热电材料。这里提出的方法为理解强非谐晶体中的晶格热传输和指导低导热材料的合理设计提供了一个强大的框架。
