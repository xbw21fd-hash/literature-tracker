## Abstract
Self-driving laboratories (SDLs) are poised to transform materials discovery by integrating automation with machine learning (ML) to accelerate data-driven experimentation. However, most SDL frameworks remain limited by single-feedback optimization and lack the multi-modal diagnostics needed to resolve both optical and structural evolution in complex hybrid systems. Here, we introduce ACCEL, an automated, ML-guided closed-loop platform for identifying and synthesizing target pure phases within quasi-2D halide perovskite systems. Using a ternary 3D:2D compositional space comprising 3D FAPbI3 and two Dion–Jacobson (DJ) spacer–based components, ACCEL optimizes the 3D:2D ratios to identify compositions that converge toward a structurally and optically stable α-FAPbI3-like phase within a quasi-2D design space. Automated in-situ photoluminescence (PL) is used to monitor crystallization kinetics in real time, revealing how the relative fraction of co-spacers governs nucleation rate and phase evolution during film formation. These kinetic insights are integrated with high-throughput synthesis, automated PL analysis, and X-ray diffraction (XRD) similarity metrics within a Gaussian Process–Bayesian Optimization framework to suppress phase heterogeneity and stabilize the target phase. Rather than targeting a predefined DJ layer number, ACCEL enables optimization based on user-defined optical and structural signatures, providing a generalizable route for autonomous phase discovery in complex hybrid materials.

## 摘要（中文）
自动驾驶实验室 (SDL) 准备通过将自动化与机器学习 (ML) 相结合来加速数据驱动的实验，从而改变材料发现过程。然而，大多数 SDL 框架仍然受到单反馈优化的限制，并且缺乏解决复杂混合系统中的光学和结构演化所需的多模式诊断。在这里，我们介绍 ACCEL，这是一种自动化、机器学习引导的闭环平台，用于识别和合成准二维卤化物钙钛矿系统中的目标纯相。 ACCEL 使用由 3D FAPbI3 和两个基于 Dion-Jacobson (DJ) 间隔基的组件组成的三元 3D:2D 成分空间，优化 3D:2D 比率，以识别在准 2D 设计空间内趋向于结构和光学稳定的 α-FAPbI3 类相的成分。自动原位光致发光（PL）用于实时监测结晶动力学，揭示共间隔基的相对分数如何控制成膜过程中的成核速率和相演化。这些动力学见解与高斯过程-贝叶斯优化框架内的高通量合成、自动 PL 分析和 X 射线衍射 (XRD) 相似性度量相集成，以抑制相异质性并稳定目标相。 ACCEL 不是针对预定义的 DJ 层数，而是基于用户定义的光学和结构特征进行优化，为复杂混合材料中的自主相发现提供了一条通用途径。
