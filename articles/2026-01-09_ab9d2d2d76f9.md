## Abstract
arXiv:2512.07220v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Recent progress in open many-body quantum systems has highlighted the importance of the Markov length, the characteristic scale over which conditional correlations decay. It has been proposed that non-equilibrium phases of matter can be defined as equivalence classes of states connected by short-time evolution while maintaining a finite Markov length, a notion called local reversibility. A natural question is whether well-known classical models of non-equilibrium criticality fit within this framework. Here we investigate the Domany-Kinzel model -- which exhibits an active phase and an absorbing phase separated by a 1+1-D directed-percolation transition -- from this information-theoretic perspective. Using tensor network simulations, we provide evidence for local reversibility within the active phase. Notably, the Markov length diverges upon approaching the critical point, unlike classical equilibrium transitions where Markov length is zero due to their Gibbs character. Correspondingly, the conditional mutual information exhibits scaling consistent with directed percolation universality. Further, we analytically study the case of 1+1-D compact directed percolation, where the Markov length diverges throughout the phase diagram due to spontaneous breaking of domain-wall parity symmetry from strong to weak. Nevertheless, the conditional mutual information continues to faithfully detect the corresponding phase transition.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.07220v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：开放多体量子系统的最新进展凸显了马尔可夫长度的重要性，马尔可夫长度是条件相关性衰减的特征尺度。有人提出，物质的非平衡相可以定义为通过短时演化连接的状态的等价类，同时保持有限的马尔可夫长度，这一概念称为局部可逆性。一个自然的问题是著名的非平衡临界性经典模型是否适合这个框架。在这里，我们从信息论的角度研究了 Domany-Kinzel 模型，该模型表现出由 1+1-D 定向渗滤跃迁分隔的活性相和吸收相。使用张量网络模拟，我们为活动阶段内的局部可逆性提供了证据。值得注意的是，马尔可夫长度在接近临界点时发散，这与经典平衡转变不同，经典平衡转变中马尔可夫长度由于其吉布斯特性而为零。相应地，条件互信息表现出与定向渗流普遍性一致的缩放比例。此外，我们分析研究了 1+1-D 紧凑定向渗流的情况，其中由于畴壁宇称对称性从强到弱的自发破缺，马尔可夫长度在整个相图中发散。然而，条件互信息继续忠实地检测相应的相变。
