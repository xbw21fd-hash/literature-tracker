## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03062v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Narrowband perfect absorbers are interesting for spectrum sensing, molecular detection, and infrared imaging. However, their design remains constrained by intuitive, iterative methods that lack flexibility, while also facing challenges in multi-objective optimization. Here, we introduce a deep learning-enabled inverse-design framework that overcomes these limitations through a conditional Wasserstein Generative Adversarial Network (WGAN). The main contribution of this work is a dual-channel image encoding scheme that jointly represents the geometry and thickness of a Si$_3$N$_4$ meta-layer, facilitating the network to learn the distribution of viable structures for a target optical response. This approach naturally solves the inherent ``one-to-many'' design issue, giving a diverse portfolio of functional candidates from a single input spectrum. The designed absorbers achieve exceptional spectral fidelity, with resonance peak errors below 5 nm, a mean squared error (MSE) on the order of $10^{-3}$, and the capacity to produce over 10 distinct, high-performance designs per target. Furthermore, we demonstrate the model's robustness under oblique illumination, showing that it can be efficiently fine-tuned to maintain spectral accuracy across incidence angles from $10^\circ$ to $40^\circ$ by transfer learning, thus extending its practical utility to non-normal operating conditions. Full-wave simulations confirm that the generated geometries support a hybrid plasmonic-dielectric resonance, leading to near-perfect absorption and strong near-field enhancement. Our study provides a robust, physics-aware design paradigm that moves beyond conventional parametric optimization. The introduced framework establishes a versatile platform for the on-demand inverse design of advanced photonic devices for sensing, spectroscopy, and optical signal processing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03062v1 公告类型：新 摘要：窄带完美吸收体对于光谱传感、分子检测和红外成像很有趣。然而，他们的设计仍然受到直观、迭代方法的限制，缺乏灵活性，同时还面临多目标优化的挑战。在这里，我们介绍了一种支持深度学习的逆向设计框架，该框架通过条件 Wasserstein 生成对抗网络（WGAN）克服了这些限制。这项工作的主要贡献是双通道图像编码方案，该方案共同表示 Si$_3$N$_4$ 元层的几何形状和厚度，促进网络学习目标光学响应的​​可行结构的分布。这种方法自然地解决了固有的“一对多”设计问题，从单一输入范围中提供了多样化的功能候选组合。设计的吸收体实现了卓越的光谱保真度，共振峰值误差低于 5 nm，均方误差 (MSE) 约为 10^{-3}$，并且能够为每个目标生成 10 多个不同的高性能设计。此外，我们证明了该模型在倾斜照明下的鲁棒性，表明可以通过迁移学习对其进行有效微调，以在从 $10^\circ$ 到 $40^\circ$ 的入射角范围内保持光谱精度，从而将其实用性扩展到非正常操作条件。全波模拟证实，生成的几何形状支持混合等离子体介电共振，从而实现近乎完美的吸收和强大的近场增强。我们的研究提供了一种强大的、物理感知的设计范式，超越了传统的参数优化。引入的框架为传感、光谱学和光信号处理的先进光子器件的按需逆向设计建立了一个多功能平台。
