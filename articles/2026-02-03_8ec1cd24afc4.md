## Abstract
arXiv:2602.00421v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Infrasound from controlled explosions provide a unique opportunity to isolate atmospheric effects on propagation. We report observations from two campaigns in May and October 2024, each featuring 10-ton TNT-equivalent controlled surface chemical explosions recorded by a dense network of 31 single-sensor stations within 23 km. Despite identical sources, the observed wavefields were very different. October signals followed a near-unimodal period-distance trend, whereas May signals exhibited a pronounced azimuthal bifurcation in both period and celerity. Downwind paths largely preserved the short-period baseline observed in October, while upwind paths showed systematically longer periods caused by wind-driven low-pass filtering. This study provides the first direct observational evidence that tropospheric winds can impose azimuth-dependent low-pass filtering at local ranges, without the influence of measured temperature inversions. Thus, the structure of the atmosphere can modify the spectral characteristics of low-frequency acoustic waves even at a distance of only a few kilometers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.00421v1 公告类型：新 摘要：受控爆炸产生的次声为隔离大气对传播的影响提供了独特的机会。我们报告了 2024 年 5 月和 10 月两次活动的观测结果，每次活动均由 23 公里内 31 个单传感器站组成的密集网络记录到 10 吨 TNT 当量的受控表面化学爆炸。尽管来源相同，但观察到的波场却截然不同。十月信号遵循近乎单峰的周期-距离趋势，而五月信号在周期和速度上都表现出明显的方位角分叉。顺风路径基本上保留了十月份观察到的短期基线，而逆风路径则显示出由风驱动的低通滤波引起的系统较长的周期。这项研究提供了第一个直接观测证据，表明对流层风可以在局部范围内施加与方位角相关的低通滤波，而不受测量的温度反演的影响。因此，即使距离只有几公里，大气结构也可以改变低频声波的频谱特性。
