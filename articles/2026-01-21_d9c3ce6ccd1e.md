## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13074v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Kohn-Luttinger (KL) mechanism of pairing, which describes superconductivity emergent from repulsive interactions, typically yields Cooper pairs at high angular-momentum ($\ell > 0$) and extremely low transition temperatures ($T_c$). Here, we reveal an inter-layer s-wave ($\ell=0$) KL superconductivity with greatly elevated $T_c$ in a multi-layer Hubbard model, which prototypes stacked two-dimensional (2D) electrons in layered van der Waals materials. By employing determinant quantum Monte Carlo and dynamical mean-field theory simulations, we show that a strong pairing attraction $V^{*}$, without the mediation of collective modes, can emerge between inter-layer electrons in the system. As inter-layer repulsion $U$ increases, $V^{*}$ evolves from a conventional KL relation of $V^{*} \propto -U^2$, to a linear strong-coupling scaling of $V^{*} \propto -U$, resulting in enhanced superconductivity at large $U$. This strong-coupling KL pairing is robust against changes in lattice geometries and dimensionalities, and it can persist, in the presence of a large remnant Coulomb repulsion $U^{*}$ between pairing electrons. Using \textit{ab initio} calculations, we propose a few 2D layered van der Waals materials that can potentially realize and control this unconventional superconductivity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13074v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Kohn-Luttinger (KL) 配对机制描述了排斥相互作用产生的超导性，通常在高角动量 ($\ell > 0$) 和极低的转变温度 ($T_c$) 下产生库珀对。在这里，我们揭示了多层哈伯德模型中具有大大提高的 $T_c$ 的层间 s 波 ($\ell=0$) KL 超导性，该模型原型在层状范德华材料中堆叠了二维 (2D) 电子。通过采用行列式量子蒙特卡罗和动态平均场理论模拟，我们表明，在没有集体模式中介的情况下，系统中的层间电子之间可以出现强配对吸引力$V^{*}$。随着层间斥力 $U$ 的增加，$V^{*}$ 从 $V^{*} \propto -U^2$ 的传统 KL 关系演变为 $V^{*} \propto -U$ 的线性强耦合缩放，从而导致大 $U$ 处的超导性增强。这种强耦合 KL 配对对晶格几何形状和维度的变化具有鲁棒性，并且在配对电子之间存在较大残余库仑斥力 $U^{*}$ 的情况下，它可以持续存在。使用 \textit{ab initio} 计算，我们提出了一些可以实现和控制这种非常规超导性的二维层状范德华材料。
