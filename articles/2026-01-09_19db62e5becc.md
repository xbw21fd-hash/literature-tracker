## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04292v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This report builds upon the Categorization of Roles in the Quantum Industry report by providing detailed profiles for 29 distinct roles across the quantum workforce. While the earlier report established a framework of four major role categories (hardware, software, bridging, and public facing and business) and their subcategories, the current report expands on this structural framework by characterizing what professionals in each role actually do, particularly by identifying the tasks, knowledge, skills, abilities (KSAs), and experience typically required for each role. Each role profile follows a standardized structure guided by the Occupational Information Network (O*NET) framework. By presenting a fine-grained view of day-to-day work and qualification expectations, this report serves as a practical resource for educators, students, industry professionals, and policymakers aiming to understand, educate, and support the evolving quantum workforce.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04292v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本报告以《量子行业角色分类》报告为基础，提供了量子劳动力中 29 个不同角色的详细资料。虽然之前的报告建立了四个主要角色类别（硬件、软件、桥接以及面向公众和业务）及其子类别的框架，但当前的报告通过描述每个角色的专业人员实际所做的事情，特别是通过确定每个角色通常所需的任务、知识、技能、能力 (KSA) 和经验，扩展了这个结构框架。每个角色概况都遵循职业信息网络 (O*NET) 框架指导下的标准化结构。通过对日常工作和资格期望进行细致的阐述，本报告为教育工作者、学生、行业专业人士和政策制定者提供了实用资源，旨在了解、教育和支持不断发展的量子劳动力。
