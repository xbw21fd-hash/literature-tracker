## Abstract
arXiv:2512.10856v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Among all excitonic complexes in transition-metal dichalcogenides (TMDs), dark and semi-dark trions are poised to play a stellar role in future quantum technologies due to their long lifetimes, about two orders of magnitude greater than those of their bright counterparts. In monolayer (ML) tungsten disulphide (WS$_2$), accessing these states via a suitable brightening mechanism remains challenging, specially, at elevated temperatures. Here, we demonstrate the brightening of dark trions from ML WS$_2$ over the temperature range, 83 K-115 K, enabled by localized surface plasmon modes in a disordered gold substrate. The resulting photoluminescence (PL) spectrum reveals a distinct spectral doublet with the twin peaks of semi-dark and bright trion states, separated by $\sim$ 45 meV. The origin of the semi-dark trion state lies in intervalley electron-electron scatterings, while its visibility in the PL spectrum is made possible by the enhanced out-of-plane electromagnetic field associated with plasmon localization. We also report on the negative degree of circular polarization in ML WS$_2$ at the energy of the semi-dark trion state. Our results establish a scalable plasmonic route to access valley-polarized semi-dark trions, opening new opportunities for quantum and valleytronic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.10856v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在过渡金属二硫化物 (TMD) 的所有激子配合物中，暗和半暗三离子由于其寿命长（比明亮对应物的寿命长约两个数量级），有望在未来的量子技术中发挥重要作用。在单层（ML）二硫化钨（WS$_2$）中，通过合适的增亮机制进入这些状态仍然具有挑战性，特别是在高温下。在这里，我们展示了 ML WS$_2$ 在 83 K-115 K 的温度范围内通过无序金基底中的局域表面等离激元模式实现的暗 Trion 的增亮。由此产生的光致发光 (PL) 光谱揭示了一个独特的光谱双峰，具有半暗和亮三重子态的双峰，间隔 $\sim$ 45 meV。半暗三重子态的起源在于层间电子-电子散射，而其在 PL 光谱中的可见性是通过与等离子体激元局域化相关的增强的面外电磁场实现的。我们还报告了半暗三重子态能量下 ML WS$_2$ 的负圆偏振度。我们的研究结果建立了一条可扩展的等离子体路径来获取谷偏振半暗三重子，为量子和谷电子应用开辟了新的机会。
