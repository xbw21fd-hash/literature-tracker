## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05306v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Coherent interconnects between gate-defined silicon quantum processing units are essential for scalable quantum computation and long-range entanglement. We argue that one-dimensional electron channels formed in the silicon quantum well of a Si/SiGe heterostructure exhibit strong Coulomb interactions and realize strongly interacting Luttinger liquid physics. At low electron densities, the system enters a Wigner regime characterized by dominant 4kF correlations; increasing the electron density leads to a crossover from the Wigner regime to a Friedel regime with dominant 2kF correlations. We support these results through large-scale density matrix renormalization group (DMRG) simulations of the interacting ground state under both screened and unscreened Coulomb potentials. We propose experimental signatures of the Wigner-Friedel crossover via charge transport and charge sensing in both zero- and high-magnetic field limits. We also analyze the impact of short-range correlated disorder - including random alloy fluctuations and valley splitting variations - and identify that the Wigner-Friedel crossover remains robust until disorder levels of about 400 micro eV. Finally, we show that the Wigner regime enables long-range capacitive coupling between quantum dots across the interconnect, suggesting a route to create long-range entanglement between solid-state qubits. Our results position silicon interconnects as a platform for studying Luttinger liquid physics and for enabling architectures supporting nonlocal quantum error correction and quantum simulation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05306v1 公告类型：新 摘要：门定义的硅量子处理单元之间的相干互连对于可扩展的量子计算和远程纠缠至关重要。我们认为，Si/SiGe 异质结构的硅量子阱中形成的一维电子通道表现出强库仑相互作用，并实现了强相互作用的路廷格液体物理。在低电子密度下，系统进入以 4kF 相关性为主的维格纳状态；增加电子密度会导致从维格纳体系到具有主导 2kF 相关性的弗里德尔体系的交叉。我们通过对屏蔽和非屏蔽库仑势下相互作用的基态进行大规模密度矩阵重正化群（DMRG）模拟来支持这些结果。我们提出了在零磁场和高磁场限制下通过电荷传输和电荷传感实现维格纳-弗里德尔交叉的实验特征。我们还分析了短程相关无序的影响 - 包括随机合金波动和谷分裂变化 - 并确定维格纳-弗里德尔交叉保持稳健，直到无序水平达到约 400 微电子伏特。最后，我们证明维格纳体系能够实现跨互连的量子点之间的长程电容耦合，这提出了在固态量子位之间创建长程纠缠的途径。我们的研究结果将硅互连定位为研究卢廷格液体物理和支持非局域量子纠错和量子模拟的架构的平台。
