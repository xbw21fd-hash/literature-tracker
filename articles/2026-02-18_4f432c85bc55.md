## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15040v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Accurate global Subseasonal-to-Seasonal (S2S) climate forecasting is critical for disaster preparedness and resource management, yet it remains challenging due to chaotic atmospheric dynamics. Existing models predominantly treat atmospheric fields as isotropic images, conflating the distinct physical processes of zonal wave propagation and meridional transport, and leading to suboptimal modeling of anisotropic dynamics. In this paper, we propose the Symmetric Orthogonal Operator Network (SOON) for global S2S climate forecasting. It couples: (1) an Anisotropic Embedding strategy that tokenizes the global grid into latitudinal rings, preserving the integrity of zonal periodic structures; and (2) a stack of SOON Blocks that models the alternating interaction of Zonal and Meridional Operators via a symmetric decomposition, structurally mitigating discretization errors inherent in long-term integration. Extensive experiments on the Earth Reanalysis 5 dataset demonstrate that SOON establishes a new state-of-the-art, significantly outperforming existing methods in both forecasting accuracy and computational efficiency.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15040v1 公告类型：新 摘要：准确的全球次季节到季节 (S2S) 气候预报对于备灾和资源管理至关重要，但由于大气动力学混乱，这仍然具有挑战性。现有模型主要将大气场视为各向同性图像，将纬向波传播和经向传输的不同物理过程混为一谈，并导致各向异性动力学的次优建模。在本文中，我们提出了用于全球 S2S 气候预测的对称正交算子网络（SOON）。它结合了：（1）各向异性嵌入策略，将全局网格标记为纬度环，保持区域周期结构的完整性； (2) 一堆 SOON 块，通过对称分解对纬向和经向算子的交替交互进行建模，从结构上减轻长期积分中固有的离散化误差。对 Earth Reanalysis 5 数据集进行的大量实验表明，SOON 建立了一种新的最先进方法，在预测精度和计算效率方面均显着优于现有方法。
