## Abstract
arXiv:2509.06093v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Materials synthesis procedures are predominantly documented as narrative text in protocols and lab notebooks, rendering them inaccessible to conventional structured data optimization. This language-native nature poses a critical challenge for complex, multistage processes--such as the preparation of boron nitride nanosheet (BNNS)--where outcomes depend on path-dependent choices in exfoliation and functionalization. Here, we recast synthesis planning as a text reasoning task enabled by a lightly structured text database, which preserves the conditional logic and causal contexts essential for expert-like decision-making. Building on a heterogeneous schema that indexes both narrative excerpts and computable entities (e.g., reaction conditions), our system implements a hybrid retrieval engine to combine semantic context with precise parameter filtering. On top of this, the framework operates in two modes, i.e. retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), which grounds recommendations in retrieved evidence modules, and experience-augmented reasoning (EAR), which uses iteratively refined text guides distilled from multi-source narrative data. Instead of suggesting single "optimal" settings, the system produces interpretable guidance aligned with expert reasoning patterns--hypotheses, parameter ranges, and citation-backed standard operating procedures--that support iterative planning and failure diagnosis. We validated this framework on the targeted exfoliation of BNNS, a process highly sensitive to multivariate constraints. The system successfully identified optimal combinations of grinding aids, milling configurations, and separation strategies from a wide range of literature-reported methods, which were experimentally verified to yield high-quality nanosheets, illustrating the potential of language-native reasoning to streamline critical operations in materials processing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.06093v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：材料合成过程主要以叙述性文本形式记录在协议和实验室笔记本中，导致传统的结构化数据优化无法访问它们。这种语言本机性质对复杂的多阶段过程（例如氮化硼纳米片（BNNS）的制备）提出了严峻的挑战，其中结果取决于剥离和功能化的路径依赖选择。在这里，我们将综合规划重新定义为由轻结构化文本数据库支持的文本推理任务，它保留了专家式决策所必需的条件逻辑和因果上下文。我们的系统建立在对叙述摘录和可计算实体（例如反应条件）进行索引的异构模式的基础上，实现了混合检索引擎，将语义上下文与精确的参数过滤相结合。除此之外，该框架以两种模式运行，即检索增强生成（RAG），它以检索到的证据模块中的建议为基础，以及经验增强推理（EAR），它使用从多源叙述数据中提取的迭代完善的文本指南。该系统不是建议单一的“最佳”设置，而是生成与专家推理模式（假设、参数范围和引用支持的标准操作程序）一致的可解释指导，支持迭代规划和故障诊断。我们在 BNNS 的目标剥离上验证了这个框架，这是一个对多变量约束高度敏感的过程。该系统成功地从各种文献报道的方法中识别出研磨助剂、研磨配置和分离策略的最佳组合，这些组合经过实验验证可以产生高质量的纳米片，说明了语言本机推理在简化材料加工中关键操作方面的潜力。
