## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20017v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: A reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS) endows a wireless channel with programmability that can be leveraged to optimize wireless information transfer. While many works study algorithms for optimizing such a programmable channel, relatively little is known about fundamental bounds on the achievable information transfer. In particular, non-trivial bounds that are both electromagnetically consistent (e.g., aware of mutual coupling) and in line with realistic hardware constraints (e.g., few-bit-programmable, potentially lossy loads) are missing. Here, based on a rigorous multiport network model of a single-input single-output (SISO) channel parametrized by 1-bit-programmable RIS elements, we apply a semidefinite relaxation (SDR) to derive a fundamental bound on the achievable SISO channel gain enhancement. A bound on the maximum achievable rate of information transfer at a given noise level follows directly from Shannon's theorem. We apply our bound to several numerical and experimental examples of different RIS-parametrized radio environments. Compared to electromagnetically consistent benchmark bounding strategies (a norm-inequality bound and, where applicable, a relaxation to an idealized beyond-diagonal load network for which a global solution exists), we consistently observe that our SDR-based bound is notably tighter. We reach at least 64 % (but often 100 %) of our SDR-based bound with standard discrete optimization techniques. The applicability of our bound to concrete experimental systems makes it valuable to inform wireless practitioners, e.g., to evaluate RIS hardware design choices and algorithms to optimize the RIS configuration. Our work contributes to the development of an electromagnetic information theory for RIS-parametrized channels as well as other programmable wave systems such as dynamic metasurface antennas or real-life beyond-diagonal RISs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20017v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：可重构智能表面（RIS）赋予无线通道可编程性，可用于优化无线信息传输。虽然许多工作研究用于优化这种可编程通道的算法，但对于可实现的信息传输的基本界限知之甚少。特别是，缺少电磁一致（例如，意识到相互耦合）和符合实际硬件约束（例如，少数位可编程、潜在有损负载）的重要边界。在这里，基于由 1 位可编程 RIS 元件参数化的单输入单输出 (SISO) 通道的严格多端口网络模型，我们应用半定松弛 (SDR) 来导出可实现的 SISO 通道增益增强的基本界限。给定噪声水平下最大可实现的信息传输速率的界限直接来自香农定理。我们将界限应用于不同 RIS 参数化无线电环境的几个数值和实验示例。与电磁一致的基准边界策略（范数不等式边界，以及在适用的情况下，放宽到存在全局解决方案的理想化超对角负载网络）相比，我们始终观察到基于 SDR 的边界明显更紧。通过标准离散优化技术，我们至少达到了基于 SDR 界限的 64%（但通常为 100%）。我们对具体实验系统的适用性使得它对于无线从业者来说很有价值，例如评估 RIS 硬件设计选择和算法以优化 RIS 配置。我们的工作有助于 RIS 参数化通道以及其他可编程波系统（例如动态超表面天线或现实生活中的超对角 RIS）电磁信息理论的发展。
