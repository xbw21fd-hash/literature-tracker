## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04916v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Antiferroelectrics are antipolar materials which possess an electric field-induced phase transition to a polar, ferroelectric phase and offer significant potential for sensing/actuation and energy-storage applications. Known antiferroelectrics are relatively scarce and mainly based on a limited set of perovskite materials and their alloys (e.g., PbZrO$_3$, AgNbO$_3$, NaNbO$_3$). Here, a new family of lead-free, weberite-type antiferroelectrics, identified through a large-scale, first-principles computational search is introduced. The screening methodology, which connects lattice dynamics to antipolar distortions, predicted that La$_3$NbO$_7$ could exhibit antiferroelectricity. We confirm the prediction through the synthesis and characterization of epitaxial La$_3$NbO$_7$ thin films, which display the signature double hysteresis loops of an antiferroelectric material as well as clear evidence of an antipolar ground state structure from transmission electron microscopy. The antiferroelectricity in La$_3$NbO$_7$ is simpler than most known antiferroelectrics and can be explained by a Kittel-type mechanism involving the movement of niobium atoms in an oxygen octahedron through a single phonon mode which results in a smaller change in the volume during the field-induced phase transition. Additionally, it is found that La$_3$NbO$_7$ combines a high threshold field with a high breakdown field ($\approx$ 6MV/cm) - which opens up opportunities for energy-storage applications. This new weberite-type family of materials offers many opportunities to tune electrical and temperature response especially through substitutions on the rare-earth site. Ultimately, this work demonstrates a successful data-driven theory-to-experiment discovery of an entirely new family of antiferroelectrics and provides a blueprint for the future design of ferroic materials.

arXiv:2601.04916v1 公告类型：新 摘要：反铁电材料是反极性材料，具有电场诱导相变到极性铁电相的能力，为传感/驱动和能量存储应用提供了巨大的潜力。已知的反铁电材料相对稀缺，主要基于有限的钙钛矿材料及其合金（例如 PbZrO$_3$、AgNbO$_3$、NaNbO$_3$）。在此，介绍了通过大规模第一原理计算搜索确定的一类新的无铅韦伯莱特型反铁电体。该筛选方法将晶格动力学与反极畸变联系起来，预测 La$_3$NbO$_7$ 可能表现出反铁电性。我们通过外延 La$_3$NbO$_7$ 薄膜的合成和表征证实了这一预测，该薄膜显示了反铁电材料的标志性双磁滞回线，以及来自透射电子显微镜的反极性基态结构的清晰证据。 La$_3$NbO$_7$ 中的反铁电性比大多数已知的反铁电性更简单，并且可以通过基特尔型机制来解释，该机制涉及氧八面体中铌原子通过单声子模式的运动，这导致在场诱导相变期间体积发生较小的变化。此外，我们发现 La$_3$NbO$_7$ 结合了高阈值场和高击穿场（$\approx$ 6MV/cm），这为能量存储应用开辟了机会。这种新的镁铝矿型材料系列提供了许多调整电学和温度响应的机会，特别是通过稀土位点的替代。最终，这项工作展示了一个全新的反铁电体家族的成功的数据驱动的理论到实验发现，并为铁性材料的未来设计提供了蓝图。
