## Abstract
arXiv:2601.11914v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Strong Coulomb interactions in two-dimensional (2D) semiconductors give rise to tightly bound excitons, exciton polarons, and correlated electronic phases such as Wigner crystals (WCs), yet their mutual interplay remains poorly understood. Here we report the observation of multi-branch excitonic Umklapp scattering in both electron and hole WCs realized in ultraclean monolayer WSe$_2$, exhibiting exceptionally high melting temperatures (T$_c$ $\approx$ 20-30 K). Robust Wigner crystallization activates multiple finite-momentum optical resonances, including quasilinearly dispersing, light-like excitons and exciton polarons, extending far beyond the single excitonic Umklapp feature reported previously. Helicity-resolved magneto-optical measurements reveal a pronounced valley dependence of the scattering processes. Combined experiment and theory identify a polaron-induced brightening mechanism in which exciton polarons transfer oscillator strength from bright zero-momentum states to otherwise dark finite-momentum states, explaining the emergence of multiple Umklapp branches where conventional exciton-WC scattering is ineffective. These results establish WC polarons as a new quasiparticle paradigm and introduce polaron-induced Umklapp scattering as a general route to accessing finite-momentum many-body excitations in 2D quantum materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.11914v1 公告类型：新 摘要：二维 (2D) 半导体中的强库仑相互作用会产生紧密束缚的激子、激子极化子和相关电子相，例如维格纳晶体 (WC)，但它们之间的相互作用仍然知之甚少。在这里，我们报告了在超净单层 WSe$_2$ 中实现的电子和空穴 WC 中观察到的多分支激子 Umklapp 散射，表现出极高的熔化温度 (T$_c$ $\approx$ 20-30 K)。鲁棒维格纳结晶激活多个有限动量光学共振，包括准线性色散、类光激子和激子极化子，远远超出了之前报道的单一激子 Umklapp 特征。螺旋度分辨磁光测量揭示了散射过程的明显谷依赖性。结合实验和理论，确定了一种极化子引起的增亮机制，其中激子极化子将振荡器强度从明亮的零动量状态转移到暗的有限动量状态，这解释了传统激子-WC散射无效的多个 Umklapp 分支的出现。这些结果将 WC 极化子确立为一种新的准粒子范式，并引入极化子诱导的 Umklapp 散射作为在 2D 量子材料中获得有限动量多体激发的一般途径。
