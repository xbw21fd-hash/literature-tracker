## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15466v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Consecutive topological phase transitions (TPTs) between strongly correlated electronic phases that differ simultaneously in symmetry breaking and topological order are of fundamental interest in condensed matter physics, yet are rarely realized experimentally. We report two consecutive electric-field-driven TPTs at half filling (nu = 1) in angle-aligned MoTe2/WSe2 moire heterobilayers. With increasing out-of-plane displacement field, a geometrically frustrated Mott insulator evolves into a ferromagnetic quantum anomalous Hall (QAH) Mott insulator, i.e., a spin-polarized topological Mott insulator without an observable charge-gap closure, and subsequently into an antiferromagnetic, valley-coherent Mott insulator (VC-AFM) accompanied by a continuous charge-gap collapse and the emergence of a critical metallic state. Layer-resolved magnetic circular dichroism (MCD), magneto-transport, and compressibility measurements jointly determine the phase diagram. The high-field evolution of the antiferromagnetic state reveals a metamagnetic-like transition at a critical field B*, above which a Chern insulating transport response reappears. Our results establish the MoTe2/WSe2 moire platform as a tunable realization of an extended Kane-Mele-Hubbard model hosting sequential correlation-topology-intertwined transitions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15466v1 公告类型：新 摘要：对称性破缺和拓扑序同时不同的强相关电子相之间的连续拓扑相变（TPT）在凝聚态物理中具有重要意义，但很少通过实验实现。我们报告了在角度对齐的 MoTe2/WSe2 莫尔异质双层中半填充 (nu = 1) 时两个连续电场驱动的 TPT。随着面外位移场的增加，几何挫败莫特绝缘体演变成铁磁量子反常霍尔（QAH）莫特绝缘体，即没有可观察到的电荷间隙闭合的自旋极化拓扑莫特绝缘体，随后演变成反铁磁、谷相干莫特绝缘体（VC-AFM），伴随着连续电荷间隙塌缩和临界金属态的出现。层分辨磁圆二色性 (MCD)、磁输运和压缩性测量共同确定相图。反铁磁态的高场演化揭示了临界场 B* 处的类似变磁的转变，在该转变上方再次出现了陈绝缘输运响应。我们的结果将 MoTe2/WSe2 云纹平台建立为扩展 Kane-Mele-Hubbard 模型的可调实现，该模型托管顺序相关拓扑交织的转变。
