## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18476v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: CeSiI is a newly discovered exfoliable van der Waals (vdW) heavy-fermion metal featured by a long-range antiferromagnetic (AF) order (TN =7.5 K) inside the Kondo coherent state below T* = 50 K. To gain a more profound understanding of the intriguing physics of this material and to uncover novel phenomena driven by quantum criticality, it is imperative to construct the phase diagram of CeSiI detailing the evolutions of T* and TN as a function of external tuning parameters such as pressure (P).In this study, we employ high pressure as an effective tuning knob to investigate this system, thereby generating a comprehensive T-P phase diagram of CeSiI. This diagram is characterized by an unusual V-shaped nonmonotonic evolution of T*(P) and the emergence of a superconducting dome with Tcmax = 240 mK upon suppression of AF order at Pc = 6 GPa, coinciding with the minimum of T*(P).The close proximity of the superconductivity (SC) to the AF instability and an unusually large upper critical field Bc2(0) exceeding 4-7 times the Pauli paramagnetic limit, suggests an unconventional pairing mechanism in CeSiI. Further analyses of normal-state transport properties provide evidence of quantum criticality, i.e., non-Fermi-liquid behavior and divergence of quasiparticle effective mass near Pc = 7 GPa. Our findings not only establish CeSiI as the first vdW heavy-fermion superconductor but also highlight an unconventional nature for the Kondo coherent state at T* at ambient pressure, hence opening a new avenue to study the interplay of strong electron correlation, Kondo hybridization, magnetism, and unconventional SC in the vdW heavy-fermion systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18476v1 公告类型：新 摘要：CeSiI 是一种新发现的可剥离范德华 (vdW) 重费米子金属，其特征在于 T* = 50 K 以下的近藤相干态内具有长程反铁磁 (AF) 级数 (TN =7.5 K)。为了更深入地了解这种材料的有趣物理现象并揭示由量子临界性驱动的新现象，必须构建 CeSiI 相图，详细描述 T* 和 TN 作为压力 (P) 等外部调谐参数的函数的演变。在本研究中，我们采用高压作为有效的调谐旋钮来研究该系统，从而生成 CeSiI 的全面 T-P 相图。该图的特点是 T*(P) 出现不寻常的 V 形非单调演化，并且在 Pc = 6 GPa 抑制 AF 阶数时出现 Tcmax = 240 mK 的超导穹顶，与 T*(P) 的最小值一致。超导性 (SC) 非常接近 AF 不稳定性，并且异常大的上临界场 Bc2(0) 超过泡利顺磁极限的 4-7 倍，表明CeSiI 中的非常规配对机制。对正常态输运特性的进一步分析提供了量子临界性的证据，即非费米液体行为和准粒子有效质量在 Pc = 7 GPa 附近的发散。我们的研究结果不仅确立了CeSiI作为第一个vdW重费米子超导体的地位，而且还强调了环境压力下T*下Kondo相干态的非常规性质，从而为研究vdW重费米子系统中强电子关联、近藤杂化、磁性和非常规SC的相互作用开辟了新途径。
