## Abstract
TLDR: The present experimental results show that the Pnma phase of MnP undergoes a pressure-induced structural phase transition at textasciitilde 15.0,GPa, and suggested that the occurrence of superconductivity in MnP was induced by suppression of the long-range antiferromagnetically ordered state rather than a structural phase Transition.
