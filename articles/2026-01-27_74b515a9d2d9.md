## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18174v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Soft interfaces formed by lipid membranes are fundamental to living cells, synthetic cells, and membrane-based soft materials. However, a quantitative framework linking molecular organization with nonlinear interfacial mechanics remains elusive. Here, we establish an analytical framework that captures the nonlinear elastic response of lipid-membrane-coated synthetic cells under micropipette aspiration. Incorporating both area stretching and curvature bending enables the model to quantitatively reproduce the complete pressure-displacement response within the small-deformation regime. This approach reduces interfacial mechanics to two parameters: the in-plane area-stretching modulus and an out-of-plane bending-related term. Using this unified framework, we experimentally demonstrate that nonlinear interfacial mechanics can be programmed by altering the molecular geometry and effective dimensionality of adsorbed elements. The lipid molecular shape and curvature-dependent packing regulate in-plane stiffness, while DNA nanostructures, the other adsorbed element, introduce an orthogonal control axis via dimensionality: isolated motifs primarily enhance area stretching, whereas three-dimensional network architectures markedly reinforce bending resistance. Together, these results establish a general molecular design principle for programming interfacial mechanics and provide a quantitative foundation for engineering mechanically tunable synthetic cells and soft interfaces.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18174v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由脂质膜形成的软界面是活细胞、合成细胞和膜基软材料的基础。然而，将分子组织与非线性界面力学联系起来的定量框架仍然难以捉摸。在这里，我们建立了一个分析框架，可以捕获脂质膜包被的合成细胞在微移液器抽吸下的非线性弹性响应。结合面积拉伸和曲率弯曲，使模型能够定量地再现小变形范围内的完整压力-位移响应。这种方法将界面力学简化为两个参数：面内区域拉伸模量和面外弯曲相关项。使用这个统一的框架，我们通过实验证明可以通过改变吸附元素的分子几何形状和有效维数来编程非线性界面力学。脂质分子形状和曲率依赖性堆积调节面内刚度，而DNA纳米结构（另一种吸附元素）通过维度引入正交控制轴：孤立的图案主要增强区域拉伸，而三维网络结构则显着增强抗弯曲性。总之，这些结果建立了界面力学编程的通用分子设计原理，并为工程机械可调谐合成细胞和软界面提供了定量基础。
