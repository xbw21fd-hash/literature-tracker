# Altermagnetic phases and phase transitions in Lieb-$5$ Hubbard model

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14200v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The emergence of altermagnetism, the collinear magnetic phase characterized by momentum-dependent spin-split bands but zero net magnetization, has fundamentally reshaped the classification of magnetic order. We propose an altermagnetic (AM) order in a repulsive Hubbard model on the Lieb-$5$ lattice. Considering only nearest-neighbor hoppings within the lattice, we show a phase transition from the nonmagnetic to a unique AM isolated band metal phase (AMIM), allowing clear identification of spin-split states. Additionally, the AM metallic phase (AMM) is also shown to appear as an intermediate phase during the transition from the normal metal to the AMIM in the presence of the diagonal hopping within each unit cell of the Lieb-$5$ lattice. The manifestation of distinct AM phases and the phase transitions, driven by Hubbard interaction and hopping integrals, have been explored in terms of spin-resolved band structure, spectral function, and the behavior of the AM order parameter. The stability of these AM phases against the spin-orbit coupling and temperature is also established.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14200v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交变磁性（以动量相关的自旋分裂带为特征但净磁化强度为零的共线磁相）的出现从根本上重塑了磁序的分类。我们在 Lieb-$5$ 晶格上的排斥哈伯德模型中提出了交变磁 (AM) 阶。仅考虑晶格内的最近邻跳跃，我们展示了从非磁性相到独特的 AM 隔离带金属相 (AMIM) 的相变，从而可以清楚地识别自旋分裂态。此外，在 Lieb-$5$ 晶胞内存在对角线跳跃的情况下，AM 金属相 (AMM) 也显示为从普通金属向 AMIM 转变期间的中间相。由哈伯德相互作用和跳跃积分驱动的不同 AM 相和相变的表现，已经在自旋分辨能带结构、谱函数和 AM 阶参数的行为方面进行了探索。这些 AM 相相对于自旋轨道耦合和温度的稳定性也已确定。
