## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18674v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Ice melting into saline water plays a fundamental role in the dynamics near the ice-ocean interface in polar oceans. The physics of ice melting involves a non-trivial interplay between thermodynamics at the interface, hydrodynamic transport in the bulk and the properties of the ambient ocean. The key control parameters are the density ratio $R_\rho$ proportional to the ambient ocean salinity and the Lewis number $Le = \kappa_T/\kappa_S$, which compares the thermal and salt diffusivities. Increasing the salinity is known to slow down melting, with the melt rate transitioning from subdiffusive to diffusive as $R_\rho$ increases. Here, we ssess the role of turbulence in this transition, using highly-resolved numerical simulations of the two-dimensional Boussinesq equations with a slowly melting upper boundary. We analyse the non-stationary growth of the temperature and meltwater mixing layers, varying the Lewis number and the density ratio. While meltwater is continuously entrained by convection inside the bulk, we identify a transition from convection to diffusion close to the interface. This transition is reflected by the formation of an interfacial boundary layer that regulates the flux of meltwater pouring into the turbulent bulk for $R_\rho \gtrsim 10$. Using mixing-layer diagnostics based on meltwater-concentration thresholds, we observe that the turbulent layer grows super-diffusively $\propto t^{1.33}$, while the interfacial boundary layer expands diffusively $\propto t^{0.5}$ but with a non-universal prefactor. These results indicate that double-diffusive effects are here confined to the interface, and highlight potential limitations of diagnostics based on fixed concentration thresholds in oceanographic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18674v1 公告类型：新 摘要：冰融化成盐水对于极地海洋冰海界面附近的动力学起着重要作用。冰融化的物理学涉及界面热力学、整体流体动力传输和周围海洋特性之间的重要相互作用。关键控制参数是与环境海洋盐度成正比的密度比 $R_\rho$ 和路易斯数 $Le = \kappa_T/\kappa_S$，它比较热扩散率和盐扩散率。众所周知，增加盐度会减慢融化速度，随着 $R_\rho$ 的增加，融化速率会从次扩散转变为扩散。在这里，我们使用具有缓慢熔化上边界的二维 Boussinesq 方程的高分辨率数值模拟来评估湍流在这一转变中的作用。我们分析了温度和融水混合层的非平稳增长，改变了路易斯数和密度比。虽然融水不断地被主体内部的对流夹带，但我们在界面附近发现了从对流到扩散的转变。这种转变通过界面边界层的形成来反映，该界面边界层调节流入湍流体的融水通量 $R_\rho \gtrsim 10$。使用基于融水浓度阈值的混合层诊断，我们观察到湍流层以超扩散的方式增长$\propto t^{1.33}$，而界面边界层以扩散的方式扩展$\propto t^{0.5}$，但具有非通用的前因子。这些结果表明，双扩散效应仅限于界面，并突出了海洋学应用中基于固定浓度阈值的诊断的潜在局限性。
