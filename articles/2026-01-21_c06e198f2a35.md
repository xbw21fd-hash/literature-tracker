## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12914v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Ferroelectric materials such as BaTiO3 exhibit spontaneous polarization that can be reoriented by an external electric field, forming the basis of various memory, actuator, and sensor applications. The polarization switching behavior, however, is strongly influenced by mechanical boundary conditions due to the intrinsic electromechanical coupling in ferroelectrics. In this study, we employ a machine learning interatomic potential to investigate the effect of uniaxial compressive stress on polarization switching and domain wall evolution in the tetragonal phase of BaTiO3. This study revealed a critical stress about 120 MPa which 90 degree polarization switching occurs. Beyond the critical stress, larger supercells exhibit lower activation energies for polarization switching with 180-degree domain wall formation and weaker constraints from periodic boundary conditions, thereby facilitating domain-wall formation. Besides, Increasing compressive stress reduces both the remnant polarization and the coercive field, while a double hysteresis loop emerges at a stress level of 80 MPa. These findings provide atomistic insights into stress-controlled ferroelectric switching and highlight the crucial role of mechanical loading in designing reliable ferroelectric devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12914v1 公告类型：新 摘要：BaTiO3 等铁电材料表现出自发极化，可以通过外部电场重新定向，形成各种存储器、执行器和传感器应用的基础。然而，由于铁电体中固有的机电耦合，极化切换行为受到机械边界条件的强烈影响。在本研究中，我们采用机器学习原子间势来研究单轴压应力对 BaTiO3 四方相中偏振切换和畴壁演化的影响。这项研究揭示了大约 120 MPa 的临界应力，会发生 90 度偏振切换。除了临界应力之外，较大的超级晶胞在形成 180 度畴壁时表现出较低的极化切换激活能，并且周期性边界条件的约束较弱，从而有利于畴壁的形成。此外，增加压应力会降低残余极化和矫顽场，而在80 MPa的应力水平下会出现双磁滞回线。这些发现提供了对应力控制铁电开关的原子学见解，并强调了机械负载在设计可靠的铁电器件中的关键作用。
