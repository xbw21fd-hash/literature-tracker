## Abstract
Lowering the overpotential of oxygen evolution reaction with electrocatalysts is essential for efficient renewable-electricity-driven electrolysis. Active noble-metal catalysts suffer from leaching and scarcity, while non-noble alternatives face limited intrinsic activity. Here we combine computational guidance with experimental validation to identify atomically dispersed tungsten within NiFe oxyhydroxide, namely W1-NiFeOOH, as a promising noble-metal-free oxygen evolution reaction catalyst. An equivariant transformer-based machine-learning interatomic potential accelerates out-of-domain adsorption energy predictions and nominates W1-NiFeOOH from 3,976 single-atom-incorporated metal oxyhydroxide configurations. Cyclic-electrodeposited W1-NiFeOOH achieves a high current density of 13.1 A cm-2at 2.0 V and remains stable for 500 hours in alkaline exchange-membrane water electrolysis with commercial membranes. In situ spectroscopy and density functional theory calculations suggest that subsurface W promoter induces synergistic electron redistribution at neighboring Ni-O-Fe edge active sites, thereby lowering the proton-coupled electron-transfer barrier for the deprotonation step and facilitating transformation into the active γ-phase. This integrated computational-experimental workflow provides a blueprint for cost-effective catalyst design for sustainable energy systems.

## 摘要（中文）
降低电催化剂析氧反应的过电势对于高效可再生电力驱动的电解至关重要。活性贵金属催化剂面临浸出和稀缺的问题，而非贵金属替代品则面临固有活性有限的问题。在这里，我们将计算指导与实验验证相结合，以确定 NiFe 羟基氧化物中原子分散的钨，即 W1-NiFeOOH，作为一种有前途的无贵金属析氧反应催化剂。基于等变变压器的机器学习原子间势加速了域外吸附能预测，并从 3,976 个单原子合并的金属羟基氧化物构型中提名 W1-NiFeOOH。循环电沉积 W1-NiFeOOH 在 2.0 V 下实现了 13.1 A cm-2 的高电流密度，并在使用商业膜的碱性交换膜水电解中保持稳定 500 小时。原位光谱和密度泛函理论计算表明，次表面W促进剂在邻近的Ni-O-Fe边缘活性位点诱导协同电子重新分布，从而降低去质子化步骤的质子耦合电子转移势垒并促进转变为活性γ相。这种集成的计算实验工作流程为可持续能源系统的经济高效的催化剂设计提供了蓝图。
