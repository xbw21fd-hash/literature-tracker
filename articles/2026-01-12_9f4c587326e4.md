## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05621v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Interfacial interactions in two parallel-stacked hexagonal boron-nitride (hBN) layers facilitate sliding ferroelectricity, enabling novel device functionalities. Additionally, when Bernal or twisted bilayer graphene is aligned with an hBN layer, unconventional ferroelectric behavior was observed, though its precise origin remains unclear. Here, we propose an alternative approach to engineering such an unconventional ferroelectricity in graphene-hBN van der Waals (vdW) heterostructures by creating specific types of hBN boundaries and interfaces. We found that the unconventional ferroelectricity can occur--without the alignments at graphene-hBN or hBN-hBN interfaces--when there are hBN edges or interfaces with line defects. By systematically analyzing the gate dependence of mobile and localized charges, we identified key characteristics of localized states that underlie the observed unconventional ferroelectricity, informing future studies. These findings highlight the complexity of the interfacial interactions in graphene/hBN systems, and demonstrate the potential for defect engineering in vdW heterostructures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05621v1 公告类型：新 摘要：两个平行堆叠的六方氮化硼 (hBN) 层中的界面相互作用促进滑动铁电性，从而实现新颖的器件功能。此外，当伯纳尔或扭曲双层石墨烯与六方氮化硼层对齐时，观察到非常规的铁电行为，尽管其确切起源仍不清楚。在这里，我们提出了一种替代方法，通过创建特定类型的六方氮化硼边界和界面，在石墨烯-六方氮化硼范德华（vdW）异质结构中设计这种非常规铁电性。我们发现，当六方氮化硼边缘或界面存在线缺陷时，即使石墨烯-六方氮化硼或六方氮化硼-六方氮化硼界面没有排列，也会出现非常规铁电性。通过系统地分析移动电荷和局域电荷的栅极依赖性，我们确定了所观察到的非常规铁电性背后的局域态的关键特征，为未来的研究提供信息。这些发现凸显了石墨烯/六方氮化硼系统中界面相互作用的复杂性，并证明了 vdW 异质结构中缺陷工程的潜力。
