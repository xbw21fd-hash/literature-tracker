## Abstract
Abstract Using density functional theory, we investigate the in-plane ferroelectricity and piezoelectricity of the w-AlN (10\bar{1}0) surface in ultrathin films composed of 2–5 w-AlN layers. We find that these few-layer structures are stable in both a ferroelectric wurtzite phase—with large positive and negative in-plane polarizations reaching approximately 2.265 C/m2—and a paraelectric hexagonal phase with zero polarization, forming a characteristic triple-well energy landscape with substantial switching barriers. Substituting Sc at the Al sites provides an effective means to tune these barriers, significantly lowering the energy cost of polarization reversal and enabling controllable ferroelectric performance for targeted applications. Furthermore, the ultrathin films exhibit strong in-plane piezoelectric responses, which are further enhanced by Sc alloying. These results highlight Sc-doped w-AlN layers as a promising platform for low-dimensional ferroelectric and piezoelectric devices.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 利用密度泛函理论，我们研究了由 2-5 个 w-AlN 层组成的超薄膜中 w-AlN (10\bar{1}0) 表面的面内铁电性和压电性。我们发现这些几层结构在铁电纤锌矿相（具有大的正负面内极化，达到约 2.265 C/m2）和具有零极化的顺电六方相中都是稳定的，形成具有大量转换势垒的特征三阱能量景观。在 Al 位点取代 Sc 提供了一种有效的方法来调节这些势垒，显着降低极化反转的能量成本，并为目标应用提供可控的铁电性能。此外，超薄膜表现出强烈的面内压电响应，钪合金化进一步增强了这种响应。这些结果凸显了 Sc 掺杂 w-AlN 层作为低维铁电和压电器件的有前景的平台。
