## Abstract
TLDR: It is shown that graph networks trained at scale can reach unprecedented levels of generalization, improving the efficiency of materials discovery by an order of magnitude and leading to the discovery of 2.2 million crystal structures, of which 381,000 are newly discovered~stable materials.
