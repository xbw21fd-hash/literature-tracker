## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16856v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We derive and validate a partition function for low-dimensional systems interacting with a heat bath, addressing the general issue of thermodynamic modeling of nanoscale systems. In contrast to bulk systems in the canonical (NVT) ensemble where the partition function is solely determined by the Hamiltonian of the system and the temperature of the heat bath, our formulation demonstrates that accounting for the interactions with the heat bath is essential for describing the statistical mechanics of low-dimensional materials. To validate our theoretical findings, we develop a molecular dynamics (MD) algorithm for directly modeling the heat bath as a gas reservoir. We first validate our approach using a 1D harmonic oscillator, calculating its length distribution through explicit numerical integration and confirming these results with MD simulations. We then extend our method to investigate the out-of-plane fluctuations of a 2D graphene monolayer immersed in a gas at finite temperature and pressure. Comparisons with conventional NVT ensemble simulations controlled by a thermostat reveal that environmental interactions significantly influence the properties of the 2D material system.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16856v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们推导并验证了与热浴相互作用的低维系统的配分函数，解决了纳米级系统热力学建模的一般问题。与规范（NVT）系综中的体系统相比，其中配分函数仅由系统的哈密顿量和热浴温度决定，我们的公式表明，考虑与热浴的相互作用对于描述低维材料的统计力学至关重要。为了验证我们的理论发现，我们开发了一种分子动力学 (MD) 算法，用于直接将热浴建模为储气层。我们首先使用一维谐振子验证我们的方法，通过显式数值积分计算其长度分布，并通过 MD 模拟确认这些结果。然后，我们扩展我们的方法来研究在有限温度和压力下浸入气体中的二维石墨烯单层的面外波动。与由恒温器控制的传统 NVT 系综模拟的比较表明，环境相互作用显着影响 2D 材料系统的性能。
