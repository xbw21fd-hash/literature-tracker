## Abstract
arXiv:2504.18264v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In recent years, the Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) has emerged as one of the most popular algorithms for solving the electronic structure problem on near-term quantum computers. The utility of VQE is often hindered by the limitations of current quantum hardware, including short qubit coherence times and low gate fidelities. These limitations become particularly pronounced when VQE is used along with deep quantum circuits, such as those required by the "Unitary Coupled Cluster Singles and Doubles" (UCCSD) ansatz, often resulting in significant errors. To address these issues, we propose a low-depth ansatz based on parallelized Givens rotations, which can recover substantial correlation energy while drastically reducing circuit depth and two-qubit gate counts for an arbitrary active space (AS). Also, considering the current hardware architectures with low qubit counts, we introduce a systematic way to select molecular orbitals to define active spaces (ASs) that retain significant electron correlation. We validate our approach by computing bond dissociation profiles of water and strongly correlated systems, such as molecular nitrogen and oxygen, across various ASs. Noiseless simulations using the new ansatz yield ground-state energies comparable to those from the UCCSD ansatz while reducing circuit depth by 50-70%. Moreover, in noisy simulations, our approach achieves energy error rates an order of magnitude lower than that of UCCSD. Considering the efficiency and practical usage of our ansatz, we hope that it becomes a potential choice for performing quantum chemistry calculations on near-term quantum devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.18264v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：近年来，变分量子本征求解器（VQE）已成为解决近期量子计算机上电子结构问题的最流行算法之一。 VQE 的实用性常常受到当前量子硬件的限制的阻碍，包括量子位相干时间短和门保真度低。当 VQE 与深度量子电路一起使用时，例如“酉耦合簇单倍和双倍”(UCCSD) 设计所要求的那些，这些限制变得尤其明显，通常会导致严重错误。为了解决这些问题，我们提出了一种基于并行吉文斯旋转的低深度 ansatz，它可以恢复大量的相关能量，同时大大减少任意活动空间（AS）的电路深度和两个量子位门计数。此外，考虑到当前量子比特数较低的硬件架构，我们引入了一种系统方法来选择分子轨道来定义保留显着电子相关性的活性空间（AS）。我们通过计算水和强相关系统（例如分子氮和氧）在各种 AS 中的键解离谱来验证我们的方法。使用新 ansatz 进行的无噪声模拟产生的基态能量与 UCCSD ansatz 的基态能量相当，同时将电路深度减少 50-70%。此外，在噪声模拟中，我们的方法实现的能量错误率比 UCCSD 低一个数量级。考虑到我们的 ansatz 的效率和实际用途，我们希望它成为在近期量子设备上进行量子化学计算的潜在选择。
