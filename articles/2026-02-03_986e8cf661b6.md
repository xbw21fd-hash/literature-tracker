## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01176v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) have emerged as a powerful paradigm for solving partial differential equations (PDEs) by embedding physical laws directly into neural network training. However, solving high-fidelity PDEs remains computationally prohibitive, particularly for parametric systems requiring multiple evaluations across varying parameter configurations. This paper presents MF-BPINN, a novel multi-fidelity framework that synergistically combines physics-informed neural networks with Bayesian uncertainty quantification and adaptive residual learning. Our approach leverages abundant low-fidelity simulations alongside sparse high-fidelity data through a hierarchical neural architecture that learns nonlinear correlations across fidelity levels. We introduce an adaptive residual network with learnable gating mechanisms that dynamically balances linear and nonlinear fidelity discrepancies. Furthermore, we develop a rigorous Bayesian framework employing Hamiltonian Monte Carlo.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01176v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：物理信息神经网络 (PINN) 已成为通过将物理定律直接嵌入到神经网络训练中来求解偏微分方程 (PDE) 的强大范例。然而，求解高保真偏微分方程在计算上仍然令人望而却步，特别是对于需要跨不同参数配置进行多次评估的参数系统。本文提出了 MF-BPINN，这是一种新颖的多保真度框架，它将物理信息神经网络与贝叶斯不确定性量化和自适应残差学习协同结合起来。我们的方法通过分层神经架构利用丰富的低保真模拟和稀疏的高保真数据，该架构可以学习跨保真度级别的非线性相关性。我们引入了一种具有可学习门控机制的自适应残差网络，可以动态平衡线性和非线性保真度差异。此外，我们利用哈密顿蒙特卡罗开发了严格的贝叶斯框架。
