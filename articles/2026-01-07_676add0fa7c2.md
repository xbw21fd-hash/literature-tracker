## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03210v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin waves (magnons) in 2D materials have received increasing interest due to their unique states and potential for tunability. However, many interesting features of these systems, including Dirac points and topological states, occur at high frequencies, where experimental probes are limited. Here, we study a crystal formed by patterning a hexagonal array of holes in a perpendicularly magnetized thin film. Through simulation, we find that the magnonic band structure imitates that of graphene, but additionally has some kagome-like character and includes a few flat bands. Surprisingly, its nature can be understood using a 9-band tight-binding Hamiltonian. This clear analogy to 2D materials enables band-gap engineering in 2D, topological magnons along 1D phase boundaries, and spectrally isolated modes at 0D point defects. Interestingly, the 1D phase boundaries allow access to the valley degree of freedom through a magnonic analog of the quantum valley-Hall insulator. These approaches can be extended to other magnonic systems, but are potentially more general due to the simplicity of the model, which resembles existing results from electron, phonon, photon, and cold atom systems. This finding brings the physics of spin waves in 2D materials to more experimentally accessible scales, augments it, and outlines a few principles for controlling magnonic states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03210v1 公告类型：新 摘要：二维材料中的自旋波（磁振子）由于其独特的状态和可调性潜力而受到越来越多的关注。然而，这些系统的许多有趣的特征，包括狄拉克点和拓扑状态，都在高频下出现，而实验探针是有限的。在这里，我们研究了通过在垂直磁化薄膜中形成六边形孔阵列图案而形成的晶体。通过模拟，我们发现波能带结构模仿了石墨烯的能带结构，但还具有一些类似 kagome 的特征，并包含一些平带。令人惊讶的是，它的性质可以使用 9 频带紧束缚哈密顿量来理解。这种与 2D 材料的清晰类比使得 2D 中的带隙工程、沿 1D 相边界的拓扑磁振子以及 0D 点缺陷处的光谱隔离模式成为可能。有趣的是，一维相边界允许通过量子谷霍尔绝缘体的波角模拟来获得谷自由度。这些方法可以扩展到其他磁波系统，但由于模型的简单性，它可​​能更通用，类似于电子、声子、光子和冷原子系统的现有结果。这一发现将二维材料中的自旋波物理学带到了更容易通过实验获得的尺度，对其进行了增强，并概述了控制波角态的一些原则。
