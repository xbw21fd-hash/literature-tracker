## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01314v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We examine entropy production in reduced descriptions of collisionless plasmas. Introducing a closure dependent entropy hierarchy, we show that non-conservative moment closures generate a residual entropy associated with irreversible information loss, whereas invariant closures remain reversible. The monotonic growth of this residual entropy imposes a statistical realizability constraint on macroscopic plasma states, excluding regions of phase space independent of dynamical stability. For anisotropic plasmas, we evaluate entropy production within a second-order moment closure, identifying contributions from transport and magnetic field inhomogeneity. The resulting entropy exclusion boundary is broadly consistent with observed anisotropy distributions in space plasmas. Statistical realizability thus emerges as an organizing principle for reduced collisionless plasma descriptions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01314v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了无碰撞等离子体简化描述中的熵产生。引入依赖于闭包的熵层次结构，我们表明非保守矩闭包会生成与不可逆信息丢失相关的残余熵，而不变闭包仍然是可逆的。该残余熵的单调增长对宏观等离子体状态施加了统计可实现性约束，不包括与动态稳定性无关的相空间区域。对于各向异性等离子体，我们评估二阶矩闭合内的熵产生，识别输运和磁场不均匀性的贡献。由此产生的熵排除边界与观测到的空间等离子体中的各向异性分布大致一致。因此，统计可实现性成为减少无碰撞等离子体描述的组织原则。
