## Abstract
arXiv:2511.04659v4 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Reliable nowcasting of extreme precipitation remains difficult because convective systems are strongly nonlinear, multiscale, and nonstationary in 3D. Radar is the backbone of nowcasting, yet existing methods struggle to predict extremes: physics-based extrapolation cannot capture growth and decay, deterministic learning tends to oversmooth and underestimate peaks, and purely generative models often lack physical consistency. Hybrid schemes help but are mostly limited to 2D composite reflectivity, collapsing the atmosphere into one layer and discarding vertical structure critical for height-dependent dynamics. We introduce Nowcast3D, a gray-box, fully 3D framework that works directly on volumetric radar reflectivity. The end-to-end model couples physically constrained neural operators (advection, local diffusion, and microphysics) with a conditional diffusion model to generate ensemble forecasts with quantified uncertainty. Trained on provincial-scale 3D volumes over a $10.24^\circ \times 10.24^\circ$ region and fine-tuned on a $2.56^\circ \times 2.56^\circ$ city region ($0.01^\circ \approx 1$ km), Nowcast3D provides near-real-time forecasts up to 3 h and outperforms competitive baselines in cross-region and temporal out-of-sample tests. It can also infer wind fields without labeled supervision, supporting physically plausible transport. In a nationwide blind evaluation by 160 meteorologists, Nowcast3D ranked first and was preferred in 57% of post-hoc assessments, surpassing the leading baseline (27%). These results highlight its reliability and operational value for extreme precipitation nowcasting.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.04659v4 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：由于对流系统在 3D 中具有强非线性、多尺度和非平稳性，因此可靠的极端降水临近预报仍然很困难。雷达是临近预报的支柱，但现有的方法很难预测极端情况：基于物理的外推法无法捕捉增长和衰减，确定性学习往往会过度平滑和低估峰值，而纯粹的生成模型往往缺乏物理一致性。混合方案有助于但主要限于二维复合反射率，将大气塌缩成一层并放弃对高度相关动力学至关重要的垂直结构。我们推出 Nowcast3D，这是一个灰盒、全 3D 框架，可直接作用于体积雷达反射率。端到端模型将物理约束的神经算子（平流、局部扩散和微物理）与条件扩散模型结合起来，生成具有量化不确定性的集合预测。 Nowcast3D 在 $10.24^\circ \times 10.24^\circ$ 区域的省级 3D 体积上进行训练，并在 $2.56^\circ \times 2.56^\circ$ 城市区域（$0.01^\circ \approx 1$ km）上进行微调，提供长达 3 小时的近实时预测，并在跨区域和时间方面优于竞争基线样本外测试。它还可以在没有标签监督的情况下推断风场，支持物理上合理的运输。在由 160 名气象学家参与的全国盲评估中，Nowcast3D 排名第一，并在 57% 的事后评估中受到青睐，超过了领先的基线 (27%)。这些结果凸显了其对于极端降水临近预报的可靠性和业务价值。
