## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14537v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Scanning tunneling microscopy (STM) and spectroscopy (STS) with $s$-wave superconducting tips has been widely applied to probe exotic superconductors, but its potential for investigating topological superconductors remains unclear. In junctions between an $s$-wave superconductor and a topological superconductor, the dominant tunneling process is Andreev reflection, in which Cooper pairs from the $s$-wave superconductor tunnel as particle--hole excitations into the surface state of the topological superconductor. In this work, we theoretically investigate the fundamental properties of Andreev and shot noise spectroscopy on topological superconductors, focusing on the $dI/dV$ characteristics and current noise. We develop a real-time description of an effective tunneling action incorporating Andreev reflection processes in the Keldysh formalism and derive analytical expressions for the Andreev reflection current and the associated current noise. Furthermore, we perform numerical simulations for representative topological superconductors and provide a catalog of $dI/dV$ spectra and the Fano factor. Our results establish guidelines for probing topological superconductivity using STM with $s$-wave superconducting tips, and provide theoretical benchmarks for future STS experiments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14537v1 公告类型：新 摘要：带有 s$ 波超导尖端的扫描隧道显微镜 (STM) 和光谱学 (STS) 已广泛应用于探测奇异超导体，但其研究拓扑超导体的潜力仍不清楚。在$s$波超导体和拓扑超导体之间的连接处，主要的隧道过程是安德烈夫反射，其中库珀对从$s$波超导体隧道中作为粒子空穴激发进入拓扑超导体的表面状态。在这项工作中，我们从理论上研究了拓扑超导体的 Andreev 和散粒噪声谱的基本特性，重点关注 $dI/dV$ 特性和电流噪声。我们开发了一种有效的隧道效应的实时描述，结合了 Keldysh 形式中的 Andreev 反射过程，并导出了 Andreev 反射电流和相关电流噪声的解析表达式。此外，我们对代表性拓扑超导体进行数值模拟，并提供 $dI/dV$ 谱和 Fano 因子的目录。我们的结果为使用 STM 和 s$ 波超导尖端探测拓扑超导性建立了指导方针，并为未来的 STS 实验提供了理论基准。
