## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15063v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: A central open question in chiral-induced spin selectivity (CISS) is whether weak electron-phonon coupling in a helical molecular junction can generate a sizable spin polarization in two-terminal transport without invoking additional strong symmetry-breaking ingredients. We address this question by implementing a self-consistent nonequilibrium Green's function (NEGF) calculation for a helical tight-binding model with spin-orbit coupling and electron-phonon interactions. The electron-phonon self-energies are evaluated self-consistently, and the transport signal is extracted using the standard magnetization-reversal protocol with a spin-polarized analyzer lead. We benchmark a fully self-consistent NEGF within the self-consistent Born approximation (SCBA) treatment for both global and local electron-phonon couplings against commonly used approximations, including diagonal self-energy schemes. We quantify how the resulting transport regime and spin polarization depend on phonon frequency, coupling strength, bias, temperature, and system size. In contrast to large polarizations and anomalous size trends reported under approximate treatments, the fully self-consistent calculation yields negligible spin polarization, additionally the electron-phonon coupling mainly renormalizes the spectrum, and transport remains quasi-ballistic across the explored parameter range.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.15063v1 公告类型：新 摘要：手性诱导自旋选择性 (CISS) 的一个核心悬而未决的问题是，螺旋分子连接中的弱电子-声子耦合是否可以在两端传输中产生相当大的自旋极化，而无需引入额外的强对称性破坏成分。我们通过对具有自旋轨道耦合和电子声子相互作用的螺旋紧束缚模型实施自洽非平衡格林函数（NEGF）计算来解决这个问题。自洽评估电子声子自能，并使用标准磁化反转协议和自旋极化分析仪引线提取传输信号。我们在自洽玻恩近似 (SCBA) 处理中对全局和局部电子声子耦合的完全自洽 NEGF 与常用近似（包括对角自能方案）进行基准测试。我们量化了最终的传输机制和自旋极化如何取决于声子频率、耦合强度、偏置、温度和系统尺寸。与近似处理下报告的大极化和异常尺寸趋势相比，完全自洽的计算产生的自旋极化可以忽略不计，此外电子声子耦合主要使光谱重新归一化，并且在探索的参数范围内传输保持准弹道。
