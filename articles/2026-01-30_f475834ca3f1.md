## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20907v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Particle-Identification Silicon-Telescope Array (PISTA) is a new detection system designed for high-resolution studies of the fission process induced by multi-nucleon transfer in inverse kinematics. It is specifically optimized for experiments with the VAMOS++ magnetic spectrometer at GANIL (Grand Acc\'el\'erateur National d'Ions Lourds). The array comprises eight trapezoidal $\Delta$E-E silicon telescopes arranged in a corolla configuration. Each telescope integrates two single-sided stripped silicon detectors, enabling target-like recoil identification, energy loss measurements, and trajectory reconstruction. Positioned in close proximity to the target, PISTA's compact geometry achieves high-efficiency tracking of target-like recoils produced in multi-nucleon transfer reactions at Coulomb barrier energies. The spatial segmentation of the array allows precise determination of the mass and charge of the target-like nucleus, and excitation energy of fissioning systems. This work presents the particle identification and excitation energy reconstruction performances for the interactions of $^{238}$U beam with $^{12}$C target. An excitation energy resolution of 870 keV (FWHM) was determined together with mass resolution of 1.1\% (FWHM). The combination of PISTA and VAMOS++ magnetic spectrometer enables unprecedented investigations of the fission process as a function of the excitation energy of the fissioning nucleus, particularly for exotic systems produced in transfer-induced reactions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20907v1 公告类型：新 摘要：粒子识别硅望远镜阵列 (PISTA) 是一种新型检测系统，专为逆运动学中多核子转移引起的裂变过程的高分辨率研究而设计。它专门针对 GANIL (Grand Acc\'el\'erateur National d'Ions Lourds) 的 VAMOS++ 磁谱仪实验进行了优化。该阵列由八个排列成花冠结构的梯形 $\Delta$E-E 硅望远镜组成。每个望远镜都集成了两个单面剥离硅探测器，能够实现类目标反冲识别、能量损失测量和轨迹重建。 PISTA 的位置靠近目标，其紧凑的几何结构可实现对库仑势垒能量下多核子转移反应中产生的类目标反冲的高效跟踪。阵列的空间分割可以精确确定类靶核的质量和电荷以及裂变系统的激发能量。这项工作展示了 $^{238}$U 光束与 $^{12}$C 目标相互作用的粒子识别和激发能量重建性能。确定激发能量分辨率为 870 keV (FWHM)，质量分辨率为 1.1\% (FWHM)。 PISTA 和 VAMOS++ 磁谱仪的结合可以对裂变过程作为裂变核激发能的函数进行前所未有的研究，特别是对于转移诱导反应中产生的奇异系统。
