## Abstract
TLDR: DeepH-hybrid is developed, a deep equivariant neural network method for learning the hybrid-functional Hamiltonian as a function of material structure, which circumvents the time-consuming self-consistent field iterations and enables the study of large-scale materials with hybrid-functional accuracy.
