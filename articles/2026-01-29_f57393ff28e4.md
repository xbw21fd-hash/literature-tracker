## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20636v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin-wave nonreciprocity, manifested as a frequency difference between counterpropagating modes, underpins many proposed magnonic devices. While this effect is commonly attributed to dipolar interactions or interfacial chirality, the microscopic origin of nonreciprocal dispersion in magnetic multilayers remains under debate. Here, we analyze nonreciprocal spin-wave dispersion in planar multilayer heterostructures without Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction. Using a frequency-shift dynamic matrix and an interaction-resolved dynamic energy-density formalism, we show that the frequency asymmetry cannot generally be ascribed to dipolar effects alone. Instead, once counterpropagating modes differ in their geometric structure along the thickness, interlayer exchange dominates the frequency shift. Applied to representative multilayer systems, we find that the interlayer exchange contribution exceeds dipolar and intralayer exchange effects by up to two to three orders of magnitude over a broad wave-vector range. Our results establish interlayer exchange as the primary mechanism controlling nonreciprocal dispersion in multilayer magnonic systems and provide a quantitative framework for engineering large frequency shifts in nonreciprocal magnonic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20636v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自旋波非互易性，表现为反向传播模式之间的频率差，是许多提出的磁振子器件的基础。虽然这种效应通常归因于偶极相互作用或界面手性，但磁性多层中非互易色散的微观起源仍然存在争议。在这里，我们分析了没有 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 相互作用的平面多层异质结构中的非互易自旋波色散。使用频移动态矩阵和相互作用解析的动态能量密度形式，我们表明频率不对称性通常不能单独归因于偶极效应。相反，一旦反向传播模式沿厚度的几何结构不同，层间交换就会主导频移。应用于代表性的多层系统，我们发现在广泛的波矢量范围内，层间交换贡献超过偶极和层内交换效应高达两到三个数量级。我们的结果将层间交换确立为控制多层磁波子系统中不可逆色散的主要机制，并为工程不可逆磁波子器件中的大频移提供了定量框架。
