## Abstract
arXiv:2506.02570v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Microwave quantum memory represents a critical component for quantum radars and resource-efficient approaches to quantum error correction. Superconducting microwave resonators provide highly efficient storage, long coherence times, on-demand reading and even in memory pulse engineering, but it is still challenging to overcome design and materials induced loss channels for on-chip realization. In this work, we present a novel architecture of integrated superconducting quantum memory with a dynamically controlled RF-SQUID coupling element in pulse regime, thus ensuring high efficiency storage and cycling storage time. It demonstrates a memory cycle time of 1.51 $\mu s$ and 57.5% storage fidelity with preservation of the stored pulse shape during the retrieval at single-photon level excitations. We establish that while the proposed active coupler realization introduces no measurable fidelity degradation, the primary limitation arises from impedance matching and materials imperfections. Still the device was used only for storing finite-duration near-single-photon classical microwave pulses, we assert that it operates as a linear device when the photon population in the common resonator remains low so it should be compatible with quantum state storage.The proposed architecture highlights a disruptive potential for on-chip qubit and memory integration for scalable quantum error correction, while identifying specific avenues for near-unity storage fidelity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.02570v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：微波量子存储器是量子雷达和资源高效的量子纠错方法的关键组件。超导微波谐振器提供高效存储、长相干时间、按需读取甚至在存储器脉冲工程中，但克服片上实现的设计和材料引起的损耗通道仍然具有挑战性。在这项工作中，我们提出了一种新型集成超导量子存储器架构，在脉冲状态下具有动态控制的 RF-SQUID 耦合元件，从而确保高效率存储和循环存储时间。它展示了 1.51 $\mu s$ 的存储周期时间和 57.5% 的存储保真度，并且在单光子级激发检索期间保留了存储的脉冲形状。我们确定，虽然所提出的有源耦合器实现不会引入可测量的保真度下降，但主要限制来自阻抗匹配和材料缺陷。尽管该设备仅用于存储有限持续时间的近单光子经典微波脉冲，但我们断言，当公共谐振器中的光子数量保持较低时，它可以作为线性设备运行，因此它应该与量子态存储兼容。所提出的架构强调了片上量子位和存储器集成用于可扩展量子纠错的颠覆性潜力，同时确定了近统一存储保真度的具体途径。
