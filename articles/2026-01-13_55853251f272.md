## Abstract
A novel electronic structure methodology to describe electron transport in Single-Molecule junctions (SMJs) within non-equilibrium Green’s function theory (NEGF), is presented. The approach is based on a formally exact, projection-based quantum embedding (PbE) technique that combines correlated many-electron wavefunction models for the molecular region with a Density Functional Theory (DFT) description of the metallic electrodes. This is achieved by constructing a specialized Hamiltonian for the molecular domain, leveraging Dyson orbitals corresponding to the ionized and electron-attached states of the embedded molecule. The effectiveness of this wavefunction-in-DFT (WFT-in-DFT) embedding scheme is demonstrated through transport calculations for SMJs containing benzene-1,4-diamine (BDA) and its substituted derivatives, employing Hartree–Fock, SOS-ADC(2), and CCSD methods for the molecular subsystem. The results show a marked improvement in the predicted zero-bias conductance compared to conventional DFT-based transport modeling employing the PBE functional. The proposed methodology provides a systematic way to select the most suitable electronic structure methods for the different parts of the system, maintaining a balance between accuracy and computational cost, while ensuring a proper description of electronic correlation within the molecule, which may notably impact electron transport in certain systems.

## 摘要（中文）
提出了一种新颖的电子结构方法，用于描述非平衡格林函数理论 (NEGF) 内单分子结 (SMJ) 中的电子传输。该方法基于形式精确、基于投影的量子嵌入 (PbE) 技术，该技术将分子区域的相关多电子波函数模型与金属电极的密度泛函理论 (DFT) 描述相结合。这是通过为分子域构建专门的哈密顿量来实现的，利用与嵌入分子的电离态和电子附着态相对应的戴森轨道。这种波函数-in-DFT (WFT-in-DFT) 嵌入方案的有效性通过对含有苯-1,4-二胺 (BDA) 及其取代衍生物的 SMJ 的输运计算得到了证明，其中分子子系统采用 Hartree-Fock、SOS-ADC(2) 和 CCSD 方法。结果表明，与采用 PBE 泛函的传统基于 DFT 的输运模型相比，预测的零偏置电导有了显着改善。所提出的方法提供了一种系统的方法来为系统的不同部分选择最合适的电子结构方法，保持准确性和计算成本之间的平衡，同时确保分子内电子相关性的正确描述，这可能会显着影响某些系统中的电子传输。
