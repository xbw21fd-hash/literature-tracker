## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14551v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Thin film BaTiO$_3$ has one of the highest known Pockels coefficients (>1200 pm/V), making it an attractive material for use in electro-optic devices. It is advantageous to integrate BaTiO$_3$ on silicon to enable complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor (CMOS) compatible processing. However, synthesis of high-quality BaTiO$_3$ directly on silicon remains a challenge. Here, we synthesize BaTiO$_3$ using hybrid metal-organic molecular beam epitaxy (hMBE) and demonstrate its transfer onto silicon using thermocompression bonding and chemical lift-off. Hybrid metal-organic MBE enables self-regulated synthesis of highly stoichiometric thin films at high growth rates (>100nm/hr). Our transfer method results in millimeter-scale areas of atomically flat, crack-free BaTiO$_3$ making it a potentially scalable method. Finally, we demonstrate the applicability of our process to device fabrication through characterization of lithographically-patterned and etch-transferred sub-micron features.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14551v1 公告类型：新 摘要：薄膜 BaTiO$_3$ 具有已知最高的普克尔斯系数之一 (>1200 pm/V)，使其成为用于电光器件的有吸引力的材料。将 BaTiO$_3$ 集成在硅上有利于实现互补金属氧化物半导体 (CMOS) 兼容处理。然而，直接在硅上合成高质量 BaTiO$_3$ 仍然是一个挑战。在这里，我们使用混合金属有机分子束外延 (hMBE) 合成了 BaTiO$_3$，并证明了使用热压键合和化学剥离将其转移到硅上。混合金属有机 MBE 能够以高生长速率（>100 nm/hr）自我调节合成高化学计量薄膜。我们的转移方法产生原子级平坦、无裂纹的 BaTiO$_3$ 毫米级区域，使其成为一种潜在的可扩展方法。最后，我们通过光刻图案和蚀刻转移的亚微米特征的表征来证明我们的工艺在器件制造中的适用性。
