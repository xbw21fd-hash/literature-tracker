## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05725v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnon torques, which can operate without involving moving electrons, could circumvent the Joule heating issue. In conventional magnon torque systems, the spin source layer with strong spin-orbit coupling is utilized to inject magnons, and the efficiency is limited by the inherent spin Hall conductivity of the spin source layer. In this work, we observe magnon torques in the Cr/NiO/ferromagnet heterostructure with the effective spin Hall conductivity of 2.45x10^5 hbar/(2e{\Omega}m), twice that of the best conventional magnon torque system. We demonstrate the magnon-torque-driven switching of a perpendicularly magnetized CoFeB layer at room temperature, with a switching power consumption density of 0.136 mW/{\mu}m^2. We find that the magnon torque originates from the orbital hybridization and interfacial inversion symmetry breaking at the Cr/NiO interface. Our findings not only significantly enhance the efficiency of magnon torques, but also provide key insights into the fundamental mechanisms of magnon injections.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05725v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁振子扭矩可以在不涉及移动电子的情况下运行，可以规避焦耳热问题。在传统的磁振子扭矩系统中，利用具有强自旋轨道耦合的自旋源层来注入磁振子，其效率受到自旋源层固有的自旋霍尔电导率的限制。在这项工作中，我们观察了 Cr/NiO/铁磁体异质结构中的磁振子扭矩，其有效自旋霍尔电导率为 2.45x10^5 hbar/(2e{\Omega}m)，是最佳传统磁振子扭矩系统的两倍。我们演示了室温下垂直磁化 CoFeB 层的磁振子扭矩驱动开关，开关功耗密度为 0.136 mW/{\mu}m^2。我们发现磁振子扭矩源于Cr/NiO界面的轨道杂化和界面反演对称性破缺。我们的研究结果不仅显着提高了磁振子扭矩的效率，而且还为磁振子注射的基本机制提供了重要见解。
