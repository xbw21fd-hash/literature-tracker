## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10275v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Characterizing the plasma state in the near-Sun environment is essential to constrain the mechanisms that heat and accelerate the solar wind. In this study, we use Parker Solar Probe (PSP) observations from Encounters 1 through 24 to investigate the radial evolution of solar wind plasma and magnetic field properties in this region. Using intervals with high field-of-view ($>85\%$) coverage, we derive the radial profiles of magnetic field strength ($|B|$), proton density ($N$), bulk speed ($V$), total proton temperature ($T$), parallel ($T_\parallel$) and perpendicular ($T_\perp$) temperatures, temperature anisotropy ($T_\perp/T_\parallel$), plasma beta ($\beta$), Alfv\'{e}n Mach number ($M_A$), and magnetic field fluctuations ($\delta B/B$) for sub and super-Alfv\'{e}nic regions. In super-Alfv\'{e}nic regions, power-law of $|B|$, $N$, $V$, and $T$ as a function of heliocentric distance are broadly consistent with previous \textit{Helios} results at $>0.3$ AU. The radial evolution of the components of the temperature tensor reveals distinct behavior: $T_\perp$ decreases monotonically with distance, whereas $T_\parallel$ exhibits a non-monotonic trend -- decreasing in the sub-Alfv\'{e}nic region, increasing just beyond the Alfv\'{e}n surface. We interpret the increase in $T_\parallel$ as a proxy for proton beam occurrence. We further examine the evolution of magnetic field fluctuations, finding decreasing radial/parallel fluctuations but enhanced tangential/normal/perpendicular fluctuations in sunward direction. These fluctuations may provide free energy for beam generation and particle heating via wave-particle interactions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10275v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：表征近太阳环境中的等离子体状态对于限制加热和加速太阳风的机制至关重要。在这项研究中，我们利用帕克太阳探测器 (PSP) 对遭遇 1 到 24 的观测来研究该区域太阳风等离子体的径向演化和磁场特性。使用高视场覆盖范围 ($>85\%$) 的间隔，我们得出磁场强度 ($|B|$)、质子密度 ($N$)、体速度 ($V$)、总质子温度 ($T$)、平行 ($T_\parallel$) 和垂直 ($T_\perp$) 温度、温度各向异性 ($T_\perp/T_\parallel$)、等离子体 beta 的径向分布($\beta$)、Alfv\'{e}n 马赫数 ($M_A$) 以及亚和超 Alfv\'{e}nic 区域的磁场波动 ($\delta B/B$)。在超 Alfv\'{e}nic 区域，$|B|$、$N$、$V$ 和 $T$ 的幂律作为日心距离的函数，与之前 $>0.3$ AU 的 \textit{Helios} 结果大致一致。温度张量分量的径向演化揭示了不同的行为：$T_\perp$ 随着距离单调减小，而 $T_\parallel$ 表现出非单调趋势——在亚 Alfv\'{e}nic 区域减小，在 Alfv\'{e}n 表面之外增加。我们将 $T_\parallel$ 的增加解释为质子束发生的代理。我们进一步研究了磁场波动的演变，发现向太阳方向的径向/平行波动减少，但切向/法向/垂直波动增强。这些波动可以通过波粒相互作用为光束生成和粒子加热提供自由能。
