## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02555v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Pyrochlore magnets of the form $R_2B_2$O$_7$, in which rare-earth ions on the $R$-site form a three-dimensional network of corner-sharing tetrahedra, provide a canonical setting for geometrical frustration. Ho-based pyrochlores host a dipolar spin-ice ground state, characterized by Ising moments constrained by the ice rules and elementary excitations analogous to magnetic monopoles. Here we examine how controlled chemical disorder influences this state by introducing site mixing on the non-magnetic $B$-site in two compounds. Ho$_2$GaSbO$_7$ contains only Ga$^{3+}$/Sb$^{5+}$ charge disorder, whereas Ho$_2$ScSbO$_7$ exhibits both charge and substantial size disorder arising from the large ionic-radius mismatch between Sc$^{3+}$ and Sb$^{5+}$. Although both materials retain the pyrochlore structure, neutron scattering measurements reveal a reduced correlation length for the $R/B$-site cation ordering and enhanced local structural distortions in Ho$_2$ScSbO$_7$. Despite these structural differences, bulk thermodynamic measurements and magnetic diffuse scattering demonstrate that both systems exhibit the defining signatures of a dipolar spin-ice state. Low-energy inelastic neutron spectroscopy further uncovers broad magnetic excitations that develop within the dipolar spin-ice regime, a feature absent in pristine Ho pyrochlores and indicative of disorder-induced splitting of the non-Kramers ground-state doublet. Together, these results show that controlled disorder generates tunable transverse-field-driven quantum fluctuations in Ho-based pyrochlores, although the dipolar spin-ice state is remarkably robust to this disorder.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02555v1 公告类型：新 摘要：$R_2B_2$O$_7$ 形式的烧绿石磁体，其中 $R$ 位点上的稀土离子形成共享角四面体的三维网络，为几何挫败提供了规范设置。 Ho基烧绿石具有偶极自旋冰基态，其特征是受冰规则约束的伊辛矩和类似于磁单极子的基本激发。在这里，我们通过在两种化合物的非磁性 $B$ 位点上引入位点混合来研究受控化学无序如何影响这种状态。 Ho$_2$GaSbO$_7$ 仅包含 Ga$^{3+}$/Sb$^{5+}$ 电荷无序，而 Ho$_2$ScSbO$_7$ 由于 Sc$^{3+}$ 和 Sb$^{5+}$ 之间的大离子半径不匹配而表现出电荷和大量尺寸无序。尽管两种材料都保留了烧绿石结构，但中子散射测量表明 $R/B$ 位点阳离子排序的相关长度减小，并且 Ho$_2$ScSbO$_7$ 中的局部结构扭曲增强。尽管存在这些结构差异，体热力学测量和磁漫散射表明，这两个系统都表现出偶极自旋冰态的定义特征。低能非弹性中子能谱进一步揭示了偶极自旋冰态内产生的广泛磁激发，这是原始Ho烧绿石中不存在的特征，表明无序诱导的非克莱默基态双峰分裂。总之，这些结果表明，受控无序在 Ho 基烧绿石中产生可调谐横向场驱动的量子涨落，尽管偶极自旋冰态对于这种无序非常稳健。
