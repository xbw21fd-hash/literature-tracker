## Abstract
arXiv:2504.18198v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Spin relaxation via electron-phonon interaction is an important decoherence mechanism for spin qubits. In this work, we study spin relaxation in hexagonal (2H) germanium, a novel direct-gap semiconductor showing great potential to combine highly coherent spin qubits with optical functionality. Focusing on electrostatically defined quantum dots in hexagonal germanium nanowires, we (i) identify geometries where spin qubit experiments are feasible, (ii) compute the nanowire phonon modes, and (iii) describe spin relaxation of hole spin qubits due to phonon-induced lattice reorientation, a direct spin-phonon coupling mechanism that is absent in cubic semiconductors typically used for spin qubits (GaAs, cubic Si, cubic Ge). We obtain the spin relaxation time as a function of nanowire cross section, quantum dot confinement length, and magnetic field. For realistic parameters, we find relaxation times above 10 ms, and reveal that the magnetic field direction maximizing the relaxation time depends on the qubit Larmor frequency. Our results facilitate the design of nanowire quantum dot experiments with long qubit relaxation times.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.18198v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：通过电子-声子相互作用实现的自旋弛豫是自旋量子位的重要退相干机制。在这项工作中，我们研究了六方（2H）锗中的自旋弛豫，这是一种新型的直接带隙半导体，显示出将高度相干自旋量子位与光学功能相结合的巨大潜力。专注于六方锗纳米线中静电定义的量子点，我们（i）确定自旋量子位实验可行的几何形状，（ii）计算纳米线声子模式，以及（iii）描述由于声子引起的晶格重新取向而导致的空穴自旋量子位的自旋弛豫，这是一种直接自旋声子耦合机制，在通常用于自旋量子位（GaAs、立方Si、立方Ge）的立方半导体中不存在。我们获得了自旋弛豫时间作为纳米线横截面、量子点限制长度和磁场的函数。对于实际参数，我们发现弛豫时间高于 10 ms，并揭示使弛豫时间最大化的磁场方向取决于量子位拉莫尔频率。我们的结果促进了具有长量子位弛豫时间的纳米线量子点实验的设计。
