## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13654v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Strontium titanate (SrTiO$_3$), famously described by Nobel laureate K. A. M\"uller as the "drosophila of solid-state physics", has been extensively investigated over the last seventy five years for its intricate coupling of structural, electronic, and dielectric properties and continues to serve as a foundational platform for advancing oxide electronics. In its pristine form, SrTiO$_3$ exhibits quantum paraelectric behavior below 35 K and undergoes an antiferrodistortive phase transition near 105 K. This transition generates ferroelastic twin domains separated by a dense network of domain walls, which function as nanoscale structural defects with far-reaching consequences. While the static influence of ferroelastic domain walls on carrier transport in electron-doped SrTiO$_3$ is well established, recent experimental results show that the emergence of polarity at these walls, combined with strain fields and inherent quantum fluctuations, induces correlated dynamical phenomena such as glass-like relaxations of electrons and memory effects. In this review, we highlight these recent advances, focusing on the subtle interplay between the emergence of nanoscale polar order, quantum fluctuations, and long-range strain fields. We propose that understanding charge carrier dynamics in the background of these complex ferroelastic domain wall landscapes offers a new paradigm for exploring electronic transport in the presence of local polar order and quantum fluctuations, with broad implications for correlated oxides.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13654v1 公告类型：新 摘要：钛酸锶 (SrTiO$_3$) 被诺贝尔奖获得者 K. A. M\"uller 称为“固态物理学的果蝇”，在过去七十五年中因其结构、电子和介电特性的复杂耦合而受到广泛研究，并继续作为推进氧化物电子学的基础平台。以其原始形式， SrTiO$_3$ 在 35 K 以下表现出量子顺电行为，并在接近 105 K 时经历反铁畸变相变。这种转变产生由致密磁畴壁网络分隔的铁弹性孪晶畴，其作为具有深远影响的纳米级结构缺陷，虽然铁弹性畴壁对电子掺杂 SrTiO$_3$ 中载流子传输的静态影响已得到充分证实，但最近的实验结果表明，这些壁上出现的极性，与应变场和固有的量子涨落相结合，在这篇综述中，我们重点介绍了这些最新进展，重点关注纳米级极性有序、量子涨落和长程应变场之间的微妙相互作用。我们建议，了解这些复杂铁弹性畴壁景观背景下的载流子动力学，为探索局部极性有序和量子涨落存在下的电子传输提供了一种新的范式，这对相关氧化物具有广泛的影响。
