## Abstract
arXiv:2512.07974v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Present theoretical predictions for the entanglement entropy through topological defects are violated by numerical simulations. In order to resolve this, we introduce a paradigm shift in the preparation of reduced density matrices in the presence of topological defects, and emphasize the role of defect networks with which they can be dressed. We consider the cases of grouplike and duality defects in detail for the Ising model, and find agreement with all numerically found entanglement entropies. Since our construction functions at the level of reduced density matrices, it accounts for topological defects beyond the entanglement entropy to other entanglement measures.

arXiv:2512.07974v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：数值模拟违反了目前对拓扑缺陷引起的纠缠熵的理论预测。为了解决这个问题，我们在存在拓扑缺陷的情况下引入了制备降密度矩阵的范式转变，并强调了可以修饰它们的缺陷网络的作用。我们详细考虑了伊辛模型的类群和对偶缺陷的情况，并发现与所有数值发现的纠缠熵一致。由于我们的构造函数是在约化密度矩阵的水平上运行的，因此它可以解释除纠缠熵之外的其他纠缠度量的拓扑缺陷。
