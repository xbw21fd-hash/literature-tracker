# Response of hydrogels and microgels to nanoparticle crowding

## Abstract
Internal degrees of freedom of cross-linked polymer networks enable compressible hydrogels and microgels to swell or deswell by absorbing or expelling solvent in response to environmental changes. Macromolecules in solution can influence the swelling response by acting as crowding agents, with practical relevance for drug delivery and biosensing. We extend the Flory–Rehner theory of polymer network swelling to incorporate nanoparticle crowders as another implicit species that can perturb a gel through volume exclusion, including the entropic cost of crowder penetration from polymer field theory. Within the free-energy landscape of asymmetric ternary mixtures, we numerically solve equilibrium relations equating solvent and crowder chemical potentials inside and outside the gel. Independently, we perform Monte Carlo simulations, including trial moves that allow gels to swell and take up crowders. We investigate how crowder size, solution concentration, cross-link density, and solvent quality influence the swelling of polymeric gels, the partitioning of crowders between the inside and outside, and the structural properties of microgel suspensions governed by Hertzian elastic pair interactions. Our results show that nanoparticle crowding promotes deswelling, thereby weakening interparticle correlations, and that, with increasing concentration of crowders, polymeric gels reach a maximum loading capacity that is dependent on crowder size and polymer cross-link density.

## 摘要（中文）
交联聚合物网络的内部自由度使可压缩水凝胶和微凝胶能够通过吸收或排出溶剂来响应环境变化而膨胀或消膨胀。溶液中的大分子可以通过充当拥挤剂来影响肿胀反应，与药物输送和生物传感具有实际相关性。我们扩展了聚合物网络膨胀的 Flory-Rehner 理论，将纳米颗粒拥挤剂纳入作为另一种隐含物种，可以通过体积排除扰动凝胶，包括聚合物场理论中拥挤剂渗透的熵成本。在不对称三元混合物的自由能景观中，我们数值求解了平衡关系，使凝胶内外的溶剂和拥挤化学势相等。我们独立地进行蒙特卡罗模拟，包括允许凝胶膨胀并占据拥挤物的试验移动。我们研究了拥挤器尺寸、溶液浓度、交联密度和溶剂质量如何影响聚合物凝胶的膨胀、拥挤器内部和外部之间的分配，以及由赫兹弹性对相互作用控制的微凝胶悬浮液的结构特性。我们的结果表明，纳米颗粒拥挤促进消溶胀，从而削弱颗粒间相关性，并且随着拥挤剂浓度的增加，聚合物凝胶达到最大负载能力，该最大负载能力取决于拥挤剂尺寸和聚合物交联密度。
