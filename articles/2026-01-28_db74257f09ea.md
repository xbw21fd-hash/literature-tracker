## Abstract
arXiv:2509.08262v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We propose that unconventional Josephson effects can typically emerge in {\it PT}-symmetric antiferromagnetic (AFM) bilayer systems. When proximitized by a conventional superconductor, these heterostructures host dominant interlayer Cooper pairing that features a distinctive spin texture enabled by the strong exchange field. Specifically, we demonstrate a novel mechanism for electrically tunable 0-$\pi$ oscillations in lateral Josephson junctions, controlled by an out-of-plane electric displacement field. This behavior originates from field-induced finite-momentum Cooper pairing, a hallmark of the unique layer-pseudospin structure in {\it PT}-symmetric AFM bilayers. Furthermore, we introduce a Josephson giant magnetoresistor based on these exotic spin-layer-locked Cooper pairs, in which the supercurrent exhibits a strong dependence on the internal N\'{e}el order. Our findings establish {\it PT}-symmetric AFM bilayers as a versatile platform for phase-controllable Josephson junctions and superconducting magnetic random-access memory, with promising applications in superconducting circuits and ultralow-power computing.

arXiv:2509.08262v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出非常规约瑟夫森效应通常会出现在 {\it PT}-对称反铁磁 (AFM) 双层系统中。当接近传统超导体时，这些异质结构具有占主导地位的层间库珀对，其特征是强交换场实现的独特的自旋纹理。具体来说，我们展示了一种在横向约瑟夫森结中电可调 0-$\pi$ 振荡的新颖机制，由平面外电位移场控制。这种行为源于场诱导的有限动量库珀配对，这是 {\it PT} 对称 AFM 双层中独特的层赝自旋结构的标志。此外，我们引入了基于这些奇异的自旋层锁定库珀对的约瑟夫森巨磁电阻器，其中超电流表现出对内部 N\'{e}el 级的强烈依赖性。我们的研究结果确立了 PT 对称 AFM 双层作为相控约瑟夫森结和超导磁性随机存取存储器的多功能平台，在超导电路和超低功耗计算方面具有广阔的应用前景。
