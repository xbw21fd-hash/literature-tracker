## Abstract
TLDR: This work proposes a new criterion to select models with less parameters using a penalty factor instead of the commonly-adopted additional penalty term, and improves the efficiency of variable selection algorithms by estimating the importance of each possible parameter by its relative uncertainty and the error induced in the parameter reduction.
