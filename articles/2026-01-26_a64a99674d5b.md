## Abstract
arXiv:2302.07402v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Various biological and synthetic media out of equilibrium can be viewed as many-ratchet systems that rectify environmental noise through local measurements and information processing, like in Maxwell's prototypical demon. These systems pose a challenge to standard coarse-graining approaches because they are better described in terms of decision-making protocols similar to computer programs rather than force laws. Here, we study a many-body generalization of the Maxwell demon problem: a fluid composed of adaptive particles that achieve collective behavior by biasing noise-driven scattering events subject to measurements. Using a combination of information-theoretic, kinetic, and hydrodynamic tools, we elucidate how microscopic decision-making protocols, rather than microscopic forces, generate macroscopic active states sustained by continuous measurements. These include an informational version of flocking whose order parameter is bounded by the information measured, and the onset of which may be viewed as a measurement-induced phase transition. We find that the signature of such microscopic choices is an `informational activity' that selectively compresses phase space, without work, and causes deviations from equilibrium scaling with the magnitude of environmental noise. We envision applications to noise-induced patterning performed by collections of microrobots guided by reinforcement learning or programmable phoretic colloids in turbulent flows that exploit local measurements and control actions to counteract the scrambling of information by chaos.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2302.07402v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：各种失去平衡的生物和合成介质可以被视为通过局部测量和信息处理来纠正环境噪声的多棘轮系统，就像麦克斯韦的原型恶魔一样。这些系统对标准粗粒度方法提出了挑战，因为它们可以用类似于计算机程序的决策协议而不是强制定律来更好地描述。在这里，我们研究了麦克斯韦妖问题的多体推广：由自适应粒子组成的流体，通过偏置噪声驱动的散射事件进行测量来实现集体行为。通过结合信息论、动力学和流体动力学工具，我们阐明了微观决策协议（而不是微观力）如何产生通过连续测量维持的宏观活性状态。其中包括植绒的信息版本，其顺序参数受测量的信息限制，并且其开始可被视为测量引起的相变。我们发现这种微观选择的特征是一种“信息活动”，它有选择地压缩相空间，而不做功，并导致与环境噪声大小的平衡缩放偏差。我们设想应用到由强化学习或湍流中的可编程泳动胶体引导的微型机器人集合执行的噪声诱导图案，利用局部测量和控制动作来抵消混沌对信息的扰乱。
