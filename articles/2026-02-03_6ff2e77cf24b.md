## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01374v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Triadic percolation turns bond percolation into a dynamical problem governed by an effective one-dimensional unimodal map. We show that the geometry of superstable cycles provides a direct, map-agnostic probe of local nonlinearity: specifically, the distance from the map's maximum to a distinguished next-to-maximum point on the attracting $2^n$-cycle (which coincides with a preimage of the maximum at $2^n$-superstability) scales as $|\Delta p|^{\gamma}$ with $\gamma = 1/z$, where $z$ is the nonflat order of the maximum. This prediction is verified across canonical unimodal families and heterogeneous triadic ensembles, with Lyapunov spectra corroborating the one-dimensional reduction. A derivative condition on the activation kernel fixes the local nonlinearity order $z$ (and thus, under standard unimodal-map hypotheses, the associated $z$-logistic universality class) and gives conditions under which $z>2$ can be realized. The diagnostic operates directly on orbit data under standard regularity assumptions, providing a practical tool to classify universality in higher-order networks.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01374v1 公告类型：新 摘要：三元渗流将键渗流转变为由有效一维单峰映射控制的动态问题。我们证明，超稳定循环的几何结构提供了一种直接的、与地图无关的局部非线性探测：具体来说，从地图的最大值到吸引的 $2^n$ 循环（与 $2^n$ 超稳定处的最大值原像一致）上可区分的下一个最大值点的距离，尺度为 $|\Delta p|^{\gamma}$，其中 $\gamma = 1/z$，其中 $z$ 是非平坦阶数的最大值。这一预测在规范单峰族和异质三元系综中得到了验证，李雅普诺夫谱证实了一维约简。激活核上的导数条件固定了局部非线性阶 $z$（因此，在标准单峰映射假设下，相关联的 $z$-logistic 普遍性类）并给出了可以实现 $z>2$ 的条件。该诊断在标准规律性假设下直接对轨道数据进行操作，提供了对高阶网络中的普遍性进行分类的实用工具。
