## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02794v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study delves into the transformation journey of Zr-based Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs), focusing on enhancing their mechanical properties and hydrogen storage capacities through doping regulation. MOFs, a versatile class of crystalline porous materials, have garnered significant attention due to their unique properties and broad potential applications in gas storage, separation, catalysis, and sensing. Among them, Zr-based MOFs stand out for their exceptional stability and high surface area. This research systematically investigates six key Zr-based MOFs (UIO-66, UIO-67, UIO-68, MOF-801, MOF-802, and MOF-841) using multiscale computational methods, including molecular dynamics (MD) simulations, grand canonical Monte Carlo (GCMC) simulations, and density functional theory (DFT). The study explores the impact of metal ion substitution (Fe, Co, Ni, Cu, Zn) on the mechanical and hydrogen storage properties of these MOFs. Our findings reveal that metal ion substitution significantly influences the mechanical stability and hydrogen adsorption capacity of Zr-based MOFs, providing valuable insights for the design and optimization of high-performance MOF materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02794v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项研究深入研究了锆基金属有机框架（MOF）的转变历程，重点是通过掺杂调节来增强其机械性能和储氢能力。 MOF 是一类多功能的结晶多孔材料，由于其独特的性能和在气体储存、分离、催化和传感方面的广泛潜在应用而引起了广泛的关注。其中，锆基 MOF 因其卓越的稳定性和高表面积而脱颖而出。这项研究使用多尺度计算方法，包括分子动力学（MD）模拟、大正则蒙特卡罗（GCMC）模拟和密度泛函理论（DFT），系统地研究了六种关键的Zr基MOF（UIO-66、UIO-67、UIO-68、MOF-801、MOF-802和MOF-841）。该研究探讨了金属离子替代（Fe、Co、Ni、Cu、Zn）对这些 MOF 的机械和储氢性能的影响。我们的研究结果表明，金属离子取代显着影响Zr基MOF的机械稳定性和氢吸附能力，为高性能MOF材料的设计和优化提供了宝贵的见解。
