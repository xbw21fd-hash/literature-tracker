## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19976v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Fluorescent spin qubits are central building blocks of quantum technologies. Placing these qubits at surfaces maximizes coupling to nearby spins and fields, enabling nanoscale sensing and facilitating integration with photonic and superconducting devices. However, reducing the dimensions or size of established qubit systems without sacrificing the qubit performance or degrading the coherence lifetime remains challenging. Here, we introduce a surface molecular qubit formed by pentacene molecules scaffolded on a two-dimensional (2D) material, hexagonal boron nitride (hBN). The qubit exhibits stable fluorescence and optically detected magnetic resonance (ODMR) from cryogenic to ambient conditions. With fully deuterated pentacene, the Hahn-echo coherence reaches 22 $\mu$s and further extends to 214 $\mu$s under dynamical decoupling, outperforming state-of-the-art shallow NV centers in diamond, despite being positioned directly on the surface. We map the local spin environment, resolving couplings to nearby nuclear and electron spins that can serve as auxiliary quantum resources. This platform combines true surface integration, long qubit coherence, and scalable fabrication, opening routes to quantum sensing, quantum simulation, and hybrid quantum devices. It also paves the way for a broader family of 2D material-supported molecular qubits.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19976v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：荧光自旋量子位是量子技术的核心构建模块。将这些量子位放置在表面可以最大限度地提高与附近自旋和场的耦合，从而实现纳米级传感并促进与光子和超导器件的集成。然而，在不牺牲量子位性能或降低相干寿命的情况下减小已建立的量子位系统的尺寸或尺寸仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们介绍了一种由并五苯分子支架在二维（2D）材料六方氮化硼（hBN）上形成的表面分子量子位。从低温到环境条件，量子位都表现出稳定的荧光和光学检测磁共振 (ODMR)。对于完全氘代并五苯，哈恩回波相干性达到 22 $\mu$s，并在动态解耦下进一步扩展到 214 $\mu$s，尽管直接位于表面，但其性能优于金刚石中最先进的浅 NV 中心。我们映射局部自旋环境，解决与附近核和电子自旋的耦合，这些自旋可以作为辅助量子资源。该平台结合了真正的表面集成、长量子位相干性和可扩展制造，为量子传感、量子模拟和混合量子设备开辟了道路。它还为更广泛的二维材料支持的分子量子位系列铺平了道路。
