## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05197v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Ab initio calculations and angle-resolved photoemission experiments show that the bulk and surface electronic structure of Weyl semimetal candidate MoTe$_2$ changes significantly by tuning the chemical potential by less than 0.4 eV. Calculations show that several Lifshitz transitions can occur among multiple electron and hole Fermi pockets of differing orbital character. Experiments show that 18% Nb-Mo substitution reduces the occupation of bulk and (001) surface bands, effectively producing a chemical potential shift of $\approx 0.3$ eV. Orbital character and dimensionality of the bulk bands is examined by soft X-ray angle resolved photoemission with control of the excitation light polarization. The band filling at the surface is shown to increase upon deposition of alkali atoms. The results indicate that multiple regimes of electronic properties can be easily accessed in this versatile, layered material.

arXiv:2601.05197v1 公告类型：新 摘要：从头计算和角分辨光电发射实验表明，通过将化学势调整小于 0.4 eV，Weyl 半金属候选 MoTe$_2$ 的体相和表面电子结构发生显着变化。计算表明，不同轨道特征的多个电子和空穴费米袋之间可以发生多个利夫席兹跃迁。实验表明，18% Nb-Mo 取代减少了体相和 (001) 表面能带的占据，有效地产生了约 0.3eV 的化学势位移。通过控制激发光偏振的软 X 射线角分辨光电发射来检查体带的轨道特征和维数。表面的能带填充显示随着碱原子的沉积而增加。结果表明，在这种多功能的层状材料中可以轻松获得多种电子特性。
