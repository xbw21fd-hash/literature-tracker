## Abstract
arXiv:2601.04733v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The efficient interfacing of quantum emitters and photons is fundamental to quantum networking. Quantum defects embedded in integrated nanophotonic circuits are promising for such applications due to the deterministic light-matter interactions of high-cooperativity ($C>1$) cavity quantum electrodynamics and potential for scalable integration with active photonic processing. Silicon-vacancy (SiV) centers embedded in diamond nanophotonic cavities are a leading approach due to their excellent optical and spin coherence, however their long-term scalability is limited by the diamond itself, as its suspended geometry and weak nonlinearity necessitates coupling to a second processing chip. Here we realize the first high-cooperativity coupling of quantum defects to hybrid-integrated nanophotonics in a scalable, planar platform. We integrate more than 600 gallium phosphide (GaP) nanophotonic cavities on a diamond substrate with near-surface SiV centers. We examine a particular device with two strongly coupled SiV centers in detail, confirming above-unity cooperativity via multiple independent measurements. Application of an external magnetic field via a permanent magnet enables optical resolution of the SiV spin transitions from which we determine a spin-relaxation time $T_1>0.4$ ms at 4 K. We utilize the high cooperativity coupling to observe spin-dependent transmission switching and the quantum jumps of the SiV spin via single-shot readout. These results, coupled with GaP's strong nonlinear properties, establish GaP-on-diamond as a scalable planar platform for quantum network applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.04733v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：量子发射器和光子的有效连接是量子网络的基础。由于高协同性（$C>1$）腔量子电动力学的确定性光与物质相互作用以及与主动光子处理的可扩展集成的潜力，嵌入集成纳米光子电路中的量子缺陷在此类应用中很有前景。嵌入金刚石纳米光子腔中的硅空位（SiV）中心由于其出色的光学和自旋相干性而成为一种领先的方法，但其长期可扩展性受到金刚石本身的限制，因为其悬浮几何结构和弱非线性需要耦合到第二个处理芯片。在这里，我们在可扩展的平面平台中首次实现了量子缺陷与混合集成纳米光子学的高协同耦合。我们将 600 多个磷化镓 (GaP) 纳米光子腔集成在具有近表面 SiV 中心的金刚石基底上。我们详细检查了具有两个强耦合 SiV 中心的特定设备，通过多个独立测量确认了高于统一的协同性。通过永磁体施加外部磁场可以实现 SiV 自旋跃迁的光学分辨率，由此我们确定 4 K 下的自旋弛豫时间 $T_1>0.4$ ms。我们利用高协同耦合来通过单次读出观察自旋相关的传输切换和 SiV 自旋的量子跃迁。这些结果加上 GaP 强大的非线性特性，将金刚石上的 GaP 确立为量子网络应用的可扩展平面平台。
