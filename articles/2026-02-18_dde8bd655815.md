## Abstract
arXiv:2505.04863v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Spontaneous orientation polarization (SOP) of polar molecules is formed in vacuum-deposited films by tilting their permanent dipole moment against the substrate surface direction. In this study, we developed SOP molecules with high structural asymmetry by introducing multiple fluoroalkyl groups into polar molecules because SOP is driven by asymmetric intermolecular interactions on the film surface during the vacuum deposition. The developed polar molecules exhibited high dipole orientation degrees in vacuum-deposited films and achieved a high surface potential growth rate relative to the film thickness, over -350 mV nm-1, which is a record high for the reported compounds. Furthermore, the dipolar layers introduced at organic thin-film interfaces in hole-only devices and organic photovoltaics to study the impact of dipole interlayers on device performance. The characteristics of the device were observed to be significantly influenced by the SOP polarity, suggesting that the SOP at the organic thin-film interface plays a crucial role in charge transfer and energy level alignment. The findings of this study provide methodologies for the formation of highly anisotropic glassy films, leading to improved performance of organic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.04863v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：真空沉积薄膜中极性分子的自发取向偏振（SOP）是通过将其永久偶极矩倾斜到基板表面方向而形成的。在这项研究中，我们通过在极性分子中引入多个氟烷基基团来开发具有高度结构不对称性的SOP分子，因为SOP是由真空沉积过程中薄膜表面上的不对称分子间相互作用驱动的。所开发的极性分子在真空沉积薄膜中表现出高偶极取向度，并实现了相对于薄膜厚度的高表面电势生长速率，超过-350 mV nm-1，这是已报道化合物的历史新高。此外，在纯空穴器件和有机光伏器件的有机薄膜界面处引入偶极层，以研究偶极夹层对器件性能的影响。观察到器件的特性受到SOP极性的显着影响，这表明有机薄膜界面处的SOP在电荷转移和能级对准中起着至关重要的作用。这项研究的结果提供了形成高度各向异性玻璃薄膜的方法，从而提高了有机器件的性能。
