## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05612v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Whisker-like touch sensors offer unique advantages for short-range perception in environments where visual and long-range sensing are unreliable, such as confined, cluttered, or low-visibility settings. This paper presents a framework for estimating contact points and robot localization in a known planar environment using a single whisker sensor. We develop a family of virtual sensor models. Each model maps robot configurations to sensor observations and enables structured reasoning through the concept of preimages - the set of robot states consistent with a given observation. The notion of virtual sensor models serves as an abstraction to reason about state uncertainty without dependence on physical implementation. By combining sensor observations with a motion model, we estimate the contact point. Iterative estimation then enables reconstruction of obstacle boundaries. Furthermore, intersecting states inferred from current observations with forward-projected states from previous steps allow accurate robot localization without relying on vision or external systems. The framework supports both deterministic and possibilistic formulations and is validated through simulation and physical experiments using a low-cost, 3D printed, Hall-effect-based whisker sensor. Results demonstrate accurate contact estimation and localization with errors under 7 mm, demonstrating the potential of whisker-based sensing as a lightweight, adaptable complement to vision-based navigation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05612v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在视觉和远距离传感不可靠的环境（例如受限、杂乱或能见度低的环境）中，晶须状触摸传感器为短距离感知提供了独特的优势。本文提出了一个使用单个晶须传感器在已知平面环境中估计接触点和机器人定位的框架。我们开发了一系列虚拟传感器模型。每个模型将机器人配置映射到传感器观察，并通过原像的概念（与给定观察一致的机器人状态集）实现结构化推理。虚拟传感器模型的概念作为一种抽象来推理状态不确定性，而不依赖于物理实现。通过将传感器观测结果与运动模型相结合，我们估计接触点。然后，迭代估计能够重建障碍物边界。此外，从当前观察推断出的状态与先前步骤的前向投影状态相交，可以在不依赖视觉或外部系统的情况下实现准确的机器人定位。该框架支持确定性和可能性公式，并使用低成本、3D 打印、基于霍尔效应的晶须传感器通过模拟和物理实验进行验证。结果证明了精确的接触估计和定位，误差低于 7 毫米，展示了基于晶须的传感作为基于视觉的导航的轻量级、适应性强的补充的潜力。
