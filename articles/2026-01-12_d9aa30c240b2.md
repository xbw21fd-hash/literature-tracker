## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05602v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The emergence of altermagnets establishes a new paradigm for multiferroics. Unlike conventional multiferroics relying on direct magnetoelectric coupling, multiferroic altermagnets host a crystal-symmetry-mediated magnetoelectric interaction that is intrinsically more efficient and robust. Among candidate material platforms, layered perovskites are particularly appealing owing to their structural diversity and synthetic versatility. However, magnetoelectric properties at the two-dimensional scale remain largely unexplored, hindering their applicability in miniaturized, highly integrated devices. Here, we systematically investigate the dimensional evolution of ferroelectric polarization and magnetism in perovskite systems through symmetry analysis. We demonstrate that altermagnetism can persist in the two-dimensional limit, yet is strongly constrained by the magnetic configuration-with only C-type antiferromagnetic order supporting it. Based on mode-decomposition calculations, we further reveal that symmetry-restricted multimode couplings simultaneously govern ferroelectric polarization and altermagnetic spin splitting. Finally, combined with first-principles calculations, we propose several strategies to lift the magnetic-configuration constraint, extending the range of viable altermagnetic systems. These results underscore the critical role of dimensionality in symmetry-driven magnetoelectric coupling in perovskite altermagnets and pave the way toward next-generation electrically controlled spintronic and multiferroic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05602v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交流磁体的出现为多铁性建立了新的范例。与依赖直接磁电耦合的传统多铁性材料不同，多铁性交变磁体具有晶体对称介导的磁电相互作用，本质上更有效、更稳健。在候选材料平台中，层状钙钛矿由于其结构多样性和合成多功能性而特别有吸引力。然而，二维尺度的磁电特性在很大程度上仍未得到探索，这阻碍了它们在小型化、高度集成的设备中的应用。在这里，我们通过对称分析系统地研究了钙钛矿系统中铁电极化和磁性的尺寸演化。我们证明交变磁性可以在二维极限内持续存在，但受到磁性构型的强烈约束——只有 C 型反铁磁序支持它。基于模式分解计算，我们进一步揭示了对称限制的多模耦合同时控制铁电极化和交磁自旋分裂。最后，结合第一性原理计算，我们提出了几种解除磁性配置约束的策略，扩大了可行的交变磁系统的范围。这些结果强调了维度在钙钛矿交替磁体中对称驱动磁电耦合中的关键作用，并为下一代电控自旋电子和多铁性器件铺平了道路。
