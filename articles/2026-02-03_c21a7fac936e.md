# Orbital Magnetization of Interacting Electrons

## 中文标题
相互作用电子的轨道磁化

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.02478v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We derive an exact expression for the orbital magnetization of electrons with short-range interactions (such as density-density interactions) in terms of exact zero-frequency response functions of the zero-field system. The result applies to weakly and strongly correlated electrons at zero and finite temperature, provided that the local grand potential density only depends on local thermodynamic parameters. We benchmark the formula for non-interacting and weakly-coupled electrons. To zeroth and first orders in the interaction strength, it agrees with the modern theory of orbital magnetization and its recent generalization to self-consistent Hartree-Fock bands. Our work provides an exact framework of interacting orbital magnetization beyond mean-field treatments, and paves the way for quantitative studies of strongly correlated electrons in external magnetic fields.

arXiv:2602.02478v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们根据零场系统的精确零频率响应函数推导了具有短程相互作用（例如密度-密度相互作用）的电子轨道磁化强度的精确表达式。该结果适用于零温度和有限温度下的弱相关电子和强相关电子，前提是局部大势密度仅取决于局部热力学参数。我们对非相互作用和弱耦合电子的公式进行了基准测试。对于相互作用强度的零阶和一阶，它与现代轨道磁化理论及其最近对自洽 Hartree-Fock 能带的推广一致。我们的工作提供了超越平均场处理的相互作用轨道磁化的精确框架，并为外部磁场中强相关电子的定量研究铺平了道路。
