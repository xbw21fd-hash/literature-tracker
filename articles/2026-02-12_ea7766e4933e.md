## Abstract
arXiv:2407.03293v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The origin of superconductivity in twisted bilayer graphene -- whether phonon-driven or electron-driven -- remains unresolved, in part due to the absence of a quantitative and efficient model for electron-phonon coupling (EPC). In this work, we develop a first-principles-based microscopic theory to calculate EPC in twisted bilayer graphene for arbitrary twist angles without requiring a periodic moir\'e supercell. Our approach combines a momentum-space continuum model for both electronic and phononic structures with a generalized Eliashberg-McMillan theory beyond the adiabatic approximation. Using this framework, we find that the EPC is strongly enhanced near the magic angle. The superconducting transition temperature induced by low-energy phonons peaks at $1.1^\circ$ around 1 K, and remains finite for a range of angles both below and above the magic angles. We predict that superconductivity persists up to $\sim 1.4^\circ$, where superconductivity has been recently observed despite the dispersive electronic bands. Beyond a large density of states, we identify a key condition for strong EPC: resonance between the electronic bandwidth and the dominant phonon frequencies. We also show that the EPC strength of a specific phonon corresponds to the modification of the moir\'e potential. In particular, we identify several $\Gamma$-phonon branches that contribute most significantly to the EPC, which are experimentally detectable via Raman spectroscopy.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2407.03293v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：扭曲双层石墨烯中的超导性起源（无论是声子驱动还是电子驱动）仍未解决，部分原因是缺乏定量且有效的电子声子耦合 (EPC) 模型。在这项工作中，我们开发了一种基于第一原理的微观理论来计算任意扭转角度的扭转双层石墨烯中的 EPC，而不需要周期性莫尔超晶胞。我们的方法将电子和声子结构的动量空间连续介质模型与超越绝热近似的广义 Eliashberg-McMillan 理论相结合。使用这个框架，我们发现 EPC 在魔角附近得到了强烈增强。低能声子引起的超导转变温度在 1 K 左右达到峰值 $1.1^\circ$，并且在魔角以下和以上的角度范围内保持有限。我们预测超导性持续到 $\sim 1.4^\circ$，尽管存在色散电子带，但最近仍观察到超导性。除了大的态密度之外，我们还确定了强 EPC 的一个关键条件：电子带宽和主要声子频率之间的共振。我们还表明，特定声子的 EPC 强度对应于莫尔势的修改。特别是，我们确定了几个对 EPC 贡献最大的 $\Gamma$-声子分支，这些分支可以通过拉曼光谱进行实验检测。
