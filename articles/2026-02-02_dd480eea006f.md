## Abstract
The rational design of efficient bifunctional catalysts for the oxygen evolution reaction (OER) and oxygen reduction reaction (ORR) is crucial for advancing sustainable energy conversion technologies. In this study, spin-polarized density functional theory calculations were performed to systematically investigate palladium (Pd) clusters of different sizes (n = 1–10, 13) supported on the PTI/Li + Cl − (100) surface, which is the oxidation site in experiments. Strong metal–support interactions were found to effectively stabilize Pd clusters on PTI, ensuring robust structural integrity. Free-energy analyses reveal a distinct size-dependent catalytic behavior, with Pd 4 @PTI exhibiting the lowest overpotentials for OER (0.41 V) and ORR (0.95 V). Further examination of scaling relations, free-energy profiles, and electronic descriptors demonstrates that the superior performance of Pd 4 @PTI originates from a balanced adsorption strength of oxygen intermediates, modulated by electronic interactions between Pd clusters and the PTI substrate. These findings provide fundamental insights into the size-dependent activity of Pd clusters on PTI and offer theoretical guidance for the rational design of cost-effective and durable Pd-based catalysts for clean energy applications.

## 摘要（中文）
用于析氧反应（OER）和氧还原反应（ORR）的高效双功能催化剂的合理设计对于推进可持续能源转换技术至关重要。在这项研究中，进行了自旋极化密度泛函理论计算，系统地研究了PTI/Li + Cl - (100)表面上支撑的不同尺寸(n = 1–10, 13)的钯(Pd)簇，该表面是实验中的氧化位点。研究发现，强金属-载体相互作用可以有效稳定 PTI 上的 Pd 簇，确保稳健的结构完整性。自由能分析揭示了独特的尺寸依赖性催化行为，Pd 4 @PTI 表现出最低的 OER (0.41 V) 和 ORR (0.95 V) 过电势。对标度关系、自由能分布和电子描述符的进一步检查表明，Pd 4 @PTI 的优异性能源于氧中间体的平衡吸附强度，并通过 Pd 簇和 PTI 基底之间的电子相互作用进行调节。这些发现为 PTI 上 Pd 簇尺寸依赖性活性提供了基本见解，并为合理设计用于清洁能源应用的经济有效且耐用的 Pd 基催化剂提供了理论指导。
