## Abstract
arXiv:2412.16845v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: The Gas-Kinetic Scheme (GKS), widely used in computational fluid dynamics for simulating hypersonic and other complicated flow phenomena, is extended in this work to electromagnetic problems by solving Maxwell's equations. In contrast to the classical GKS formulation, the proposed scheme employs a discrete rather than a continuous velocity space. By evaluating a time-accurate numerical flux at cell interfaces, the proposed scheme attains second-order accuracy within a single step. Its kinetic formulation provides an inherently multidimensional framework, while the finite-volume formulation ensures straightforward extension to unstructured meshes. Through the incorporation of a collision process, the scheme exhibits lower numerical dissipation than classical flux-vector splitting (FVS) methods. Furthermore, the kinetic decomposition enables direct implementation of non-reflecting boundary conditions. The proposed scheme is validated against several benchmark problems and compared with established methods, including the Finite-Difference Time-Domain (FDTD) method and FVS. A lattice Boltzmann method (LBM) implementation is also included for comparative analysis. Finally, the technique is applied to simulate electromagnetic wave propagation in a realistic aircraft configuration, demonstrating its ability to model complex geometries.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2412.16845v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：气体动力学方案（GKS）广泛应用于计算流体动力学中，用于模拟高超音速和其他复杂的流动现象，在这项工作中通过求解麦克斯韦方程组将其扩展到电磁问题。与经典的 GKS 公式相反，所提出的方案采用离散而不是连续的速度空间。通过评估细胞界面处的时间精确数值通量，所提出的方案在单个步骤内获得了二阶精度。其动力学公式提供了固有的多维框架，而有限体积公式确保了直接扩展到非结构化网格。通过结合碰撞过程，该方案表现出比经典通量矢量分裂 (FVS) 方法更低的数值耗散。此外，动力学分解可以直接实现非反射边界条件。所提出的方案针对多个基准问题进行了验证，并与已建立的方法进行了比较，包括时域有限差分 (FDTD) 方法和 FVS。还包括用于比较分析的格子玻尔兹曼方法 (LBM) 实现。最后，该技术被应用于模拟真实飞机配置中的电磁波传播，展示了其对复杂几何形状进行建模的能力。
