## Abstract
arXiv:2510.18325v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Symbolic regression offers a promising route toward interpretable machine learning, yet existing methods suffer from poor predictability and computational intractability when exploring large expression spaces. I introduce GoodRegressor, a general-purpose C++-based framework that resolves these limitations while preserving full physical interpretability. By combining hierarchical descriptor construction, interaction discovery, nonlinear transformations, statistically rigorous model selection, and stacking ensemble, GoodRegressor efficiently explores symbolic model spaces such as $1.44 \times 10^{457}$, $5.99 \times 10^{124}$, and $4.20 \times 10^{430}$ possible expressions for oxygen-ion conductors, NASICONs, and superconducting oxides, respectively. Across these systems, it produces compact equations that surpass state-of-the-art black-box models and symbolic regressors, improving $R^2$ by $4 \sim 40$ %. The resulting expressions reveal physical insights, for example, into oxygen-ion transport through coordination environment and lattice flexibility. Independent ensemble runs yield nearly identical regressed values and the identical top-ranked candidate, demonstrating high reproducibility. With scalability up to $10^{4392}$ choices without interaction terms, GoodRegressor provides a foundation for general-purpose interpretable machine intelligence.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.18325v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：符号回归为可解释的机器学习提供了一条有前途的途径，但现有方法在探索大型表达空间时存在可预测性差和计算困难性的问题。我介绍 GoodRegressor，这是一个基于 C++ 的通用框架，它解决了这些限制，同时保留了完整的物理可解释性。通过结合分层描述符构造、相互作用发现、非线性变换、统计上严格的模型选择和堆叠集成，GoodRegressor 有效地探索了符号模型空间，例如氧离子导体、NASICON 和超导氧化物的 $1.44 \times 10^{457}$、$5.99 \times 10^{124}$ 和 $4.20 \times 10^{430}$ 可能的表达式，分别。在这些系统中，它产生的紧凑方程超越了最先进的黑盒模型和符号回归器，将 $R^2$ 提高了 $4 \sim 40$ %。由此产生的表达式揭示了物理见解，例如，通过配位环境和晶格柔性来了解氧离子传输。独立的集成运行产生几乎相同的回归值和相同的顶级候选值，表现出高再现性。 GoodRegressor 的可扩展性高达 10^{4392}$ 选项（无需交互项），为通用可解释机器智能奠定了基础。
