## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05860v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Molecular dynamics (MD) simulations are a central tool in science and engineering enabling the study of dynamical behavior and the link between microscopic structure and macroscopic function. Their high computational cost, however, has motivated extensive efforts to develop accelerated alternatives. A promising approach is the use of machine-learning-based simulators that allow for substantially larger time steps than conventional MD. Among these, graph neural network (GNN)-based methods have been found to be especially attractive given that they naturally encode the inductive bias of interacting particle systems, however current architectures remain limited in accuracy and stability. In particular, standard message-passing schemes struggle to efficiently propagate long-range information. Here, we investigate whether spectral-GNN simulators can overcome these limitations by explicitly representing a simulated system in a global eigenmode basis, and therefore better capture long-range and collective behavior. Focusing on disordered elastic networks, model systems for complex materials and biological structures such as proteins, we compare spatial, linear spectral, and nonlinear spectral GNN architectures. We find that while spectral representations alone are not sufficient, nonlinear spectral models substantially outperform alternatives. By learning both the time-dependent dynamics and the mixing of eigenmodes, these models more accurately capture the system's slow, global modes, which dominate macroscopic behavior. This leads to a marked reduction in systematic particle-position error and significantly improved prediction of global physical properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05860v1 公告类型：新 摘要：分子动力学 (MD) 模拟是科学和工程领域的核心工具，可用于研究动力学行为以及微观结构与宏观功能之间的联系。然而，它们的高计算成本促使人们广泛努力开发加速替代方案。一种有前途的方法是使用基于机器学习的模拟器，它允许比传统 MD 更大的时间步长。其中，基于图神经网络（GNN）的方法被发现特别有吸引力，因为它们自然地编码相互作用粒子系统的归纳偏差，但当前的架构在准确性和稳定性方面仍然有限。特别是，标准消息传递方案很难有效地传播远程信息。在这里，我们研究谱 GNN 模拟器是否可以通过在全局本征模基础上明确表示模拟系统来克服这些限制，从而更好地捕获远程和集体行为。我们重点关注无序弹性网络、复杂材料和生物结构（例如蛋白质）的模型系统，比较空间、线性谱和非线性谱 GNN 架构。我们发现，虽然仅靠谱表示还不够，但非线性谱模型的性能远远优于其他模型。通过学习瞬态动力学和特征模态的混合，这些模型可以更准确地捕捉系统的缓慢全局模式，这些模式主导宏观行为。这导致系统粒子位置误差显着减少，并显着改善对全局物理特性的预测。
