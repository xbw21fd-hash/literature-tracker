## Abstract
arXiv:2508.17937v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The small magnitude and long-range character of non-covalent interactions pose a significant challenge for computational quantum chemical and electronic-structure methods alike. State-of-the-art coupled cluster (CC) theory and benchmark-grade diffusion Monte Carlo (DMC) are ideally positioned to tackle these problems, but concerning differences between both methods have been reported in numerous studies of the interaction energy of non-covalently bound dimers. Given that the basic theoretical frameworks underpinning both methods are exact in principle, the error must arise from one or several of the approximations required to make the calculations computationally tractable. Here, we carry out a rigorous and systematic examination of the effect of each of these approximations using the acetic acid dimer and water-peptide systems as convenient testing grounds. Thanks to the use of stringently optimized backflow wave functions we are able to find that the significant discrepancies are dominated by the fixed-node error incurred by the Slater-Jastrow DMC result, while errors in the CC calculations do not significantly alter the result. This finding, likely applicable to other hydrogen-bonded systems, helps establish that CC should be regarded as the benchmark for these systems, and can potentially guide the search for pragmatic solutions to the fixed-node problem in the future.

arXiv:2508.17937v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：非共价相互作用的小量级和长程特征对计算量子化学和电子结构方法提出了重大挑战。最先进的耦合簇 (CC) 理论和基准级扩散蒙特卡罗 (DMC) 非常适合解决这些问题，但在许多非共价结合二聚体相互作用能的研究中报道了两种方法之间的差异。鉴于支撑这两种方法的基本理论框架原则上都是精确的，误差必定是由使计算在计算上易于处理所需的一个或多个近似值产生的。在这里，我们使用乙酸二聚体和水肽系统作为方便的测试场地，对每个近似值的效果进行了严格和系统的检查。由于使用了严格优化的回流波函数，我们能够发现显着的差异主要是由 Slater-Jastrow DMC 结果引起的固定节点误差主导，而 CC 计算中的误差不会显着改变结果。这一发现可能适用于其他氢键系统，有助于确定 CC 应被视为这些系统的基准，并有可能指导未来寻找固定节点问题的实用解决方案。
