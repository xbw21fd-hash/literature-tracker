## Abstract
TLDR: A formalism for rank-efficient equivariant tensor networks (ETNs), i.e., tensor networks that remain invariant under actions of SO(3) upon contraction, and a new class of polynomial-based MLIPs that demonstrate superior performance over existing MLIPs for multicomponent systems.
