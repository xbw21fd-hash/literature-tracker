## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01943v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Establishing quantitative adiabaticity criteria at finite temperature remains substantially less developed than in the pure-state setting, despite the fact that realistic quantum systems are never at absolute zero. Here we derive rigorous bounds on the Hilbert-Schmidt fidelity between mixed states by combining a mixed-state quantum speed limit with mixed-state fidelity susceptibility within the Liouville space formulation of quantum mechanics. Applied to protocols that drive an initial Gibbs state toward a quasi-Gibbs target, these bounds yield an explicit threshold driving rate for the onset of nonadiabaticity. For a broad class of local Hamiltonians in gapped phases, we show that, in the thermodynamic limit, the threshold factorizes into two factors: a system-size contribution that recovers the zero-temperature scaling and a universal temperature-dependent factor. The latter is exponentially close to unity at low temperature, whereas at high temperature it increases linearly with temperature. We verify the predicted scaling in several spin-1/2 chains by obtaining closed-form expressions for the threshold driving rate. Our results provide practical and largely model-independent criteria for finite-temperature adiabaticity in closed many-body systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01943v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：尽管现实的量子系统永远不会处于绝对零，但在有限温度下建立定量绝热性标准仍然远不如纯态设置那么发达。在这里，我们通过将混合态量子速度极限与量子力学刘维尔空间公式中的混合态保真度敏感性相结合，得出混合态之间希尔伯特-施密特保真度的严格界限。应用于将初始吉布斯状态驱动至准吉布斯目标的协议时，这些界限产生了非绝热性开始的明确阈值驱动速率。对于有隙相中的一大类局部哈密顿量，我们表明，在热力学极限下，阈值分解为两个因素：恢复零温标度的系统尺寸贡献和通用的温度相关因素。后者在低温下以指数方式接近于 1，而在高温下则随温度线性增加。我们通过获得阈值驱动速率的封闭式表达式来验证多个自旋 1/2 链中的预测缩放。我们的结果为封闭多体系统中的有限温度绝热性提供了实用且很大程度上与模型无关的标准。
