## Abstract
arXiv:2601.19648v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the frustrated $J_1$-$J_2$-$J_3$ Ising model on the honeycomb lattice, featuring first- and second-neighbor ferromagnetic couplings ($J_1>0$ and $J_2>0$) and third-neighbor antiferromagnetic interactions ($J_3<0$). Using the cluster mean-field method, we analyze the phase transitions in the regime $1/2 < J_2/J_1 \le 1$, where ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic phases compete. Our results reveal that near the strongly frustrated limit $J_3/J_1 = -1$, the system exhibits order-by-disorder state selection, tricritical and bicritical behavior, critical endpoints, and two successive phase transitions. The ferromagnetic-paramagnetic transition remains second order across the entire interaction range, whereas the antiferromagnetic-paramagnetic boundary shows a richer behavior, including both first- and second-order transitions as well as tricriticality. Increasing the second-neighbor coupling $J_2/J_1$ narrows the range of $J_3/J_1$ where first-order antiferromagnetic-paramagnetic transitions occur; beyond a certain threshold, only second-order order-disorder transitions persist. Consequently, the tricritical point shifts toward $J_3/J_1 \approx -1$ as $J_2/J_1$ increases, culminating in a bicritical point where the antiferromagnetic, ferromagnetic, and paramagnetic phases meet.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.19648v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了蜂窝晶格上受挫的 $J_1$-$J_2$-$J_3$ Ising 模型，具有第一和第二邻铁磁耦合（$J_1>0$ 和 $J_2>0$）和第三邻反铁磁相互作用（$J_3<0$）。使用簇平均场方法，我们分析了 $1/2 < J_2/J_1 \le 1$ 区域中的相变，其中铁磁相和反铁磁相相互竞争。我们的结果表明，在强烈受挫极限 $J_3/J_1 = -1$ 附近，系统表现出逐序无序状态选择、三临界和双临界行为、临界端点和两个连续的相变。铁磁-顺磁转变在整个相互作用范围内保持二阶，而反铁磁-顺磁边界表现出更丰富的行为，包括一阶和二阶转变以及三临界性。增加第二邻耦合 $J_2/J_1$ 会缩小发生一阶反铁磁-顺磁转变的 $J_3/J_1$ 范围；超过某个阈值，仅存在二阶有序-无序转变。因此，随着 $J_2/J_1$ 的增加，三临界点向 $J_3/J_1 \approx -1$ 移动，最终达到反铁磁相、铁磁相和顺磁相相遇的双临界点。
