## Abstract
arXiv:2601.15857v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In this work, we report on the study on organic-metal hybrid systems, in particular Co-C60 fullerene thin films. This study mainly focused on the investigation of the morphological and structural evolution of the film surface after various external stimuli designed to provide energy to the system. For film growth, we adopted an innovative approach, combining ion-beam sputtering of a pure metal target with thermal evaporation of C60 in a co-deposition setup. The films underwent a series of treatments to induce modifications. Laser and ion irradiations were performed using a pulsed laser, a continuous Ar beam, and a pulsed C beam. In addition, thermal annealing in vacuum was performed to examine the long-term effects of temperature. The composition of deposited film was investigated using Ion Beam Analysis, the morphology and the structure, and the effects of treatments on the films were studied using SEM and TEM microscopies and Raman spectroscopy. Changes in electrical resistance were also measured to explore potential applications of these films after treatment.

arXiv:2601.15857v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在这项工作中，我们报告了有机金属杂化系统的研究，特别是 Co-C60 富勒烯薄膜。本研究主要集中于研究为系统提供能量的各种外部刺激后薄膜表面的形态和结构演变。对于薄膜生长，我们采用了一种创新方法，将纯金属靶材的离子束溅射与共沉积装置中的 C60 热蒸发相结合。这些薄膜经过一系列处理以引起修改。使用脉冲激光、连续Ar束和脉冲C束进行激光和离子照射。此外，还进行了真空热退火以检查温度的长期影响。使用离子束分析研究了沉积薄膜的成分、形态和结构，并使用 SEM 和 TEM 显微镜以及拉曼光谱研究了处理对薄膜的影响。还测量了电阻的变化，以探索这些薄膜在处理后的潜在应用。
