# Raman response of collective modes in multicomponent superconductors

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05607v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We formulate a microscopic theory of the Raman response of superconducting collective modes in multicomponent superconductors. Starting from a general Bogoliubov--de Gennes (BdG) Hamiltonian with a separable pairing interaction, we derive a gauge-invariant expression for the Raman susceptibility, including a long-range Coulomb interaction. The resulting Raman susceptibility is directly computable for an arbitrary BdG Hamiltonian, which contains single- and multiband systems, spin-singlet and triplet order parameters, and time-reversal-symmetric and time-reversal-symmetry-breaking superconducting states. Based on the microscopic coupling between a Raman source field and collective modes, we derive a symmetry selection rule for Raman-active collective modes and show a group-theoretical classification for all crystalline point groups. This classification provides a unified framework based on the ``higher-order Lifshitz-invariant'' to identify Raman-active collective modes such as Leggett mode, Bardasis-Schrieffer (BS) mode, and clapping mode. As an application, we focus on an effective model of the heavy-fermion superconductor UTe$_2$ with a fully gapped multicomponent odd-parity pairing state. We find sharp in-gap Raman resonances below the quasiparticle continuum, which do not correspond to a conventional Leggett mode but arise from the {\it intraband} relative modes between different pairing components.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05607v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们建立了多组分超导体中超导集体模式拉曼响应的微观理论。从具有可分离配对相互作用的一般 Bogoliubov-de Gennes (BdG) 哈密顿量开始，我们推导出拉曼磁化率的规范不变表达式，包括长程库仑相互作用。由此产生的拉曼磁化率可直接计算任意 BdG 哈密顿量，其中包含单带和多带系统、自旋单重态和三重态阶参数以及时间反演对称和时间反演对称破缺超导态。基于拉曼源场和集体模式之间的微观耦合，我们推导了拉曼活性集体模式的对称选择规则，并展示了所有晶体点群的群论分类。该分类提供了基于“高阶 Lifshitz 不变量”的统一框架，用于识别拉曼活性集体模式，例如 Leggett 模式、Bardasis-Schrieffer (BS) 模式和拍手模式。作为一个应用，我们专注于具有完全间隙多分量奇宇称配对状态的重费米子超导体 UTe$_2$ 的有效模型。我们在准粒子连续体下方发现了尖锐的带隙内拉曼共振，它不对应于传统的莱格特模式，而是由不同配对组件之间的带内相对模式产生。
