## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05212v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Universality classes encompass the analogous thermodynamic behavior of unlike physical systems, at different spatial dimensions $d$, in the vicinity of their critical point. Critical exponents define these classes, with the Ising model being the outstanding prototype that elucidates the differences from the mean-field category, believed to be valid above a critical dimension only. Here, in apparent striking contradiction to the Ising universality class, we demonstrate that the critical behavior of a finite Ising system of $N$ spins in $d = 3$ obeys mean-field Landau theory in the vicinity of its critical point, with classical critical exponents. Yet, when expressed in terms of the linear size $L$ of the system, the free energy unveils its proper finite-size scaling form, from which the thermodynamic limit critical temperature $T_c$ and the Ising critical exponents $\nu$, $\gamma$ and $\beta$ can be identified. We find that the larger the size $L$, the smaller the mean-field region, shrinking to zero in the thermodynamic limit. These conclusions are achieved via the use of an alternative approach to collect data from a Monte Carlo simulation of a three-dimensional Ising model that allows for the evaluation of the free energy per spin $f = f(T,m;L)$ and of the coexistence curve, or spontaneous magnetization at zero magnetic field, $m_{\rm coex} = m(T;L)$ as functions of temperature $T$ and magnetization per spin $m = M/N$. Our results suggest a revision of the role of mean-field theory in the elucidation of critical phenomena.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05212v1 公告类型：新 摘要：普遍性类别包含不同物理系统在不同空间维度 $d$ 的类似热力学行为，在其临界点附近。临界指数定义了这些类别，伊辛模型是阐明与平均场类别之间差异的杰出原型，据信仅在临界尺寸以上才有效。在这里，与伊辛普适性类明显矛盾，我们证明了 $N$ 自旋 $d = 3$ 的有限伊辛系统的临界行为在其临界点附近服从平均场朗道理论，并具有经典临界指数。然而，当用系统的线性尺寸$L$表示时，自由能揭示了其适当的有限尺寸标度形式，从中可以确定热力学极限临界温度$T_c$和伊辛临界指数$\nu$、$\gamma$和$\beta$。我们发现，尺寸$L$越大，平均场区域越小，在热力学极限下缩小到零。这些结论是通过使用另一种方法从三维 Ising 模型的蒙特卡罗模拟收集数据得出的，该模型允许评估每次自旋自由能 $f = f(T,m;L)$ 和共存曲线，或零磁场下的自发磁化强度 $m_{\rm coex} = m(T;L)$ 作为温度 $T$ 和每次自旋磁化强度 $m = M/N$ 的函数。我们的结果建议对平均场理论在阐明关键现象中的作用进行修正。
