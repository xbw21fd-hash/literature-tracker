## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20506v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We report an ambient pressure liquid metal assisted CVD strategy that enables shape programmable growth of micro scale diamond by coupling liquid metl Ga In with ferrocene (Fe(C5H5)2) as an carbon precursor, nanodiamond seeds, and nanosilicon. Building on liquid metal diamond synthesis, this approach pushes liquid metal growth toward a low temperature (900 {\deg}C, 1 atm) while enabling single crystal diamonds to be scaled from ~10 {\mu}m to several tens of micrometers with well developed faceting. Ferrocene decomposition supplies a sustained interfacial carbon flux that is captured and redistributed by the Ga In melt toward seed rich liquid solid interfaces. Defect rich nanodiamond provides the crystallographic template required for reliable sp3 nucleation despite the intrinsically low carbon solubility of Ga In. Nanosilicon plays a distinct, complementary role by tuning interfacial kinetics and facet competition, enabling deliberate control of crystal habit: cubic (~10 {\mu}m), truncated tetrahedral, and fully faceted octahedral diamonds are reproducibly obtained by adjusting the nanosilicon:nanodiamond ratio, with octahedral crystals reaching ~50 {\mu}m. Importantly, crystal size is further scaled by regulating hydrogen flow: lowering the H2 rate increases net carbon retention at the liquid metal interface, raises effective supersaturation, and accelerates diamond deposition. Together, habit control (via nanosilicon: nanodiamond) and size scaling (via H2 flow) establish a practical route silicon driven facet regulation and size under ambient pressure, offering a pathway to tunable micro sized single crystal diamonds under mild conditions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20506v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们报告了一种常压液态金属辅助 CVD 策略，该策略通过将液态金属 Ga In 与作为碳前驱体的二茂铁 (Fe(C5H5)2) 耦合、纳米金刚石种子和纳米硅，实现微型金刚石的形状可编程生长。这种方法以液态金属金刚石合成为基础，将液态金属的生长推向低温（900℃，1atm），同时使单晶金刚石的尺寸从约 10μm 到几十微米，并具有发育良好的刻面。二茂铁分解提供了持续的界面碳通量，该碳通量被 Ga In 熔体捕获并重新分配到富含晶种的液体固体界面。尽管 Ga In 本质上具有低碳溶解度，但富含缺陷的纳米金刚石提供了可靠的 sp3 成核所需的晶体学模板。纳米硅通过调节界面动力学和刻面竞争发挥独特的互补作用，从而能够对晶体习性进行有意控制：通过调整纳米硅：纳米金刚石的比例，可重复获得立方体（~10μm）、截断四面体和全刻面八面体金刚石，八面体晶体达到~50μm。重要的是，通过调节氢气流量可以进一步缩小晶体尺寸：降低氢气速率会增加液态金属界面处的净碳保留量，提高有效过饱和度，并加速金刚石沉积。习惯控制（通过纳米硅：纳米金刚石）和尺寸缩放（通过氢气流）共同建立了一条在环境压力下硅驱动刻面调节和尺寸的实用路线，为在温和条件下可调微米尺寸单晶金刚石提供了途径。
