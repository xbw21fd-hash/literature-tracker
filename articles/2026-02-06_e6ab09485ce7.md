## Abstract
arXiv:2509.03177v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We calculate the crystal-field splitting, ground-state Kramers doublet and intersite exchange interactions within the ground-state doublet manifold using an ab initio Hubbard-I based approach for a representative set of Ce and Yb triangular-lattice compounds. These include the putative quantum spin liquids (QSL) RbCeO$_2$ and YbZn$_2$GaO$_5$ and the antiferromagnets KCeO$_2$ and KCeS$_2$. The calculated nearest-neighbor (NN) couplings are antiferromagnetic and exhibit noticeable anisotropy. The next-nearest-neighbor (NNN) couplings are ferromagnetic in the Ce systems and dominated by classical dipole-dipole interactions in the Yb case. Solving the resulting effective spin-1/2 models by exact diagonalization up to $N=36$ sites, we predict ordered magnetic ground states for all systems, including the two QSL candidates. We explore the phase space of an anisotropic NN + isotropic NNN triangular-lattice model finding that a significant antiferromagnetic NNN coupling is required to stabilize QSL phases, while the NN exchange anisotropy is detrimental to them. Our findings highlight a possibly important role of deviations from the perfect triangular model - like atomic disorder - in real triangular-lattice materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.03177v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们使用基于从头算 Hubbard-I 的方法计算一组代表性 Ce 和 Yb 三角晶格化合物的晶体场分裂、基态 Kramers 双峰和基态双峰流形内的位点交换相互作用。其中包括假定的量子自旋液体 (QSL) RbCeO$_2$ 和 YbZn$_2$GaO$_5$ 以及反铁磁体 KCeO$_2$ 和 KCeS$_2$。计算出的最近邻（NN）耦合是反铁磁性的，并且表现出明显的各向异性。 Ce 系统中的次近邻 (NNN) 耦合是铁磁性的，而 Yb 系统中的耦合则以经典偶极子-偶极子相互作用为主。通过精确对角化高达 $N=36$ 位点来求解所得到的有效自旋 1/2 模型，我们预测了所有系统的有序磁基态，包括两个 QSL 候选系统。我们探索了各向异性 NN + 各向同性 NNN 三角晶格模型的相空间，发现需要显着的反铁磁 NNN 耦合来稳定 QSL 相，而 NN 交换各向异性对其不利。我们的研究结果强调了完美三角形模型的偏差（如原子无序）在真实三角形晶格材料中可能发挥的重要作用。
