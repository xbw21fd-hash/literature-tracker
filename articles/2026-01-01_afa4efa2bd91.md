# Many-electron characterizations of higher-charge superconductors

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23801v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The theoretical understanding of conventional superconductivity as the phonon-assisted formation and condensation of two-electron Cooper pairs is a significant triumph in condensed matter physics. Here, we propose many-electron characterizations of higher-charge superconductivity with Cooper pairs consisting of more than two electrons, by implementing translation symmetrization on parent pair-density-wave-ordered states. In particular, we demonstrate many-electron constructions with vanishing charge-2e sectors, but with sharp signatures in charge-4e or charge-6e expectation values instead. Such characterizations are consistent with previous phenomenology of vestigial order and Ginzburg-Landau theory, yet, instead of point-group-symmetry presumptions, we show that momentum conservation is both vital and sufficient. Our study thus offers a novel, general, and microscopic route to understand and characterize higher-charge superconductivity, including nontrivial experimental signatures such as fractional magnetic flux and period in interferometry, as well as localized Cooper pairs at lattice topological defects.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23801v1 公告类型：新 摘要：对传统超导性的理论理解是声子辅助形成和凝聚双电子库珀对，这是凝聚态物理学的重大胜利。在这里，我们通过在母对密度波有序态上实现平移对称化，提出了由两个以上电子组成的库珀对的高电荷超导性的多电子表征。特别是，我们演示了具有消失的 2e 电荷扇区的多电子结构，但在 4e 电荷或 6e 电荷期望值中具有尖锐的特征。这些特征与先前的残余秩序现象学和金兹堡-朗道理论一致，然而，我们证明动量守恒是至关重要且充分的，而不是点群对称假设。因此，我们的研究提供了一种新颖的、通用的和微观的途径来理解和表征高电荷超导性，包括重要的实验特征，例如干涉测量中的分数磁通量和周期，以及晶格拓扑缺陷处的局域库珀对。
