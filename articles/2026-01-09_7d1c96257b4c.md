## Abstract
arXiv:2510.23852v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Magnetic anisotropy in disordered rare-earth-transition metals (RE-TM) compounds arises from RE atoms occupying asymmetric environments within the TM lattice. However, the underlying mechanism that promotes such environments remains not fully understood. In this study, we investigate amorphous NdCo$_{4.6}$ thin films deposited by magnetron sputtering, where the magnetic anisotropy evolves with thickness from in-plane to out-of-plane orientation above 40 nm. X-ray reflectivity measurements revealed the progressive formation of an additional layer between the 3 nm Si capping layer and the NdCo film with increasing film thickness. To probe the composition and distribution of Co and Nd near the surface, Hard X-ray Photoemission Spectroscopy (HAXPES) was performed on films ranging from 5 nm to 65 nm in thickness using incident photon energies of 7, 19, and 13 keV. These correspond to inelastic electron mean free paths of 7.2-12.3 nm in cobalt. The cobalt atomic concentration, deduced from HAXPES at the Nd 3d and Co 2p excitations, was consistently below the nominal value and varied with both thickness and photon energy. This indicates segregation of RE atoms at the film surface, which becomes more pronounced with increasing thickness. Background analysis of the Co 2p and Nd 3d peaks supports this conclusion. The thickness of the Nd segregated layer is estimated in 2-3 nm. These findings demonstrate that incorporating neodymium into the cobalt lattice incurs an energy cost which is associated with strain due to the volume mismatch between the two elements. Reducing this strain energy promotes anisotropic atomic environments for Nd, thereby explaining the emergence of perpendicular anisotropy and its dependence on film thickness in these RE-TM compounds.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.23852v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：无序稀土过渡金属 (RE-TM) 化合物中的磁各向异性源于稀土原子在 TM 晶格内占据不对称环境。然而，促进这种环境的根本机制仍不完全清楚。在这项研究中，我们研究了通过磁控溅射沉积的非晶 NdCo$_{4.6}$ 薄膜，其中磁各向异性随着厚度从面内取向到面外取向而演变，超过 40 nm。 X 射线反射率测量表明，随着膜厚度的增加，3 nm Si 覆盖层和 NdCo 膜之间逐渐形成附加层。为了探测表面附近 Co 和 Nd 的成分和分布，使用 7、19 和 13 keV 的入射光子能量对厚度范围为 5 nm 至 65 nm 的薄膜进行了硬 X 射线光电发射光谱 (HAXPES)。这些对应于钴中 7.2-12.3 nm 的非弹性电子平均自由程。从 Nd 3d 和 Co 2p 激发下的 HAXPES 推导出来的钴原子浓度始终低于标称值，并且随厚度和光子能量的变化而变化。这表明稀土原子在薄膜表面发生偏析，随着厚度的增加，这种偏析变得更加明显。 Co 2p 和 Nd 3d 峰的背景分析支持了这一结论。 Nd偏析层的厚度估计为2-3nm。这些发现表明，将钕掺入钴晶格会产生能量成本，该能量成本与由于两种元素之间的体积不匹配而产生的应变相关。降低应变能会促进 Nd 的各向异性原子环境，从而解释垂直各向异性的出现及其对这些 RE-TM 化合物中薄膜厚度的依赖性。
