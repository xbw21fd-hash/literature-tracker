## Abstract
In drug discovery, nuisance compounds are chemicals that interfere with biochemical or cell-based assays, often generating misleading signals unrelated to true activity. These molecules operate through diverse, context-dependent mechanisms, ranging from colloidal aggregation to covalent reactivity. They are often referred to as Pan-Assay Interference Compounds (PAINS), signal interferers, or frequent hitters, depending on their mode of action, and they lead to costly false positives and resource misallocation. They are difficult to distinguish from genuine pharmacological activity using rigid substructure filters. Although deep learning has emerged as a powerful alternative, most existing architectures process molecular graphs or SMILES sequences in isolation. We introduce CAGE-Fusion (Co-Attention Graph Embedding Fusion), a multimodal framework that employs a gated co-attention mechanism to enforce bidirectional information exchange between graph-based and sequence-based encoders. This architecture enables the iterative refinement of atom and token embeddings, yielding a chemically coherent representation that captures cross-modal dependencies missed by single-view baselines. CAGE-Fusion demonstrates superior predictive performance across several MoleculeNet benchmarks and achieves a macro-averaged ROC-AUC of 0.94 and MCC of 0.65 on a comprehensive nuisance compound classification task. Beyond prediction the model offers structural transparency by visualizing attention-weighted regions that contribute to a classification. The source code and a web-based interface are freely available for broad scientific reuse.

## 摘要（中文）
在药物发现中，有害化合物是干扰生化或细胞分析的化学物质，通常会产生与真实活性无关的误导性信号。这些分子通过不同的、依赖于环境的机制发挥作用，从胶体聚集到共价反应。它们通常被称为泛测定干扰化合物 (PAINS)、信号干扰物或常见的干扰物，具体取决于其作用模式，并且它们会导致代价高昂的误报和资源错配。使用刚性子结构过滤器很难将它们与真正的药理活性区分开来。尽管深度学习已成为一种强大的替代方案，但大多数现有架构都是孤立地处理分子图或 SMILES 序列。我们引入了 CAGE-Fusion（共同注意图嵌入融合），这是一种多模态框架，它采用门控共同注意机制来强制基于图和基于序列的编码器之间的双向信息交换。这种架构能够迭代细化原子和令牌嵌入，产生化学上一致的表示，捕获单视图基线错过的跨模式依赖关系。 CAGE-Fusion 在多个 MoleculeNet 基准测试中展现了卓越的预测性能，并在综合性有害化合物分类任务中实现了 0.94 的宏观平均 ROC-AUC 和 0.65 的 MCC。除了预测之外，该模型还通过可视化有助于分类的注意力加权区域来提供结构透明度。源代码和基于网络的界面可免费获得，以供广泛的科学重用。
