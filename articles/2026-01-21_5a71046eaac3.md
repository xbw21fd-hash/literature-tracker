## Abstract
arXiv:2508.07712v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Antiferromagnetic ground states, when doped, give rise to rich and complex phenomena, prompting detailed investigations in various spin systems. Here, we study the effect of doping on the one-dimensional $S = 1$ antiferromagnetic Heisenberg model (AFM). Specifically, we investigate how the presence of holes affects the static and dynamic (frequency-dependent) spin-spin correlations of the two-orbital Hubbard-Kanamori chain. The latter, at half-filling and in the strong-interaction limit, maps onto an $S = 1$ Heisenberg model. For moderate interactions, an orbital resonating-valence-bond (orbital-RVB) state emerges up to doping levels of $x \lesssim 0.3$. A detailed analysis of interaction strength $U$ and doping concentration $x$ reveals that this phase inherits the key features of spin excitations found in the half-filled case -- namely, a gapped spin spectrum and ``coherent'' magnon behavior up to a wavevector $q$ determined by the Fermi vector, $2k_\mathrm{F} = \pi(1 - x)$. Furthermore, our results uncover an additional broad, incoherent spectral weight for $q \gtrsim 2k_\mathrm{F}$ at high frequencies. Finally, we show that near the transition to a ferromagnetic phase, a previously unidentified spiral-like state emerges, characterized by spin excitations reminiscent of the $J_1$-$J_2$ Heisenberg model.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.07712v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：反铁磁基态，当掺杂时，会产生丰富而复杂的现象，促进对各种自旋系统的详细研究。在这里，我们研究掺杂对一维 $S = 1$ 反铁磁海森堡模型 (AFM) 的影响。具体来说，我们研究了空穴的存在如何影响双轨道哈伯德-金森链的静态和动态（频率相关）自旋-自旋相关性。后者在半填充和强相互作用极限下映射到 $S = 1$ 海森堡模型。对于中等相互作用，轨道共振价键（轨道 RVB）态会出现高达 $x \lesssim 0.3$ 的掺杂水平。对相互作用强度 $U$ 和掺杂浓度 $x$ 的详细分析表明，该相继承了半填充情况下自旋激发的关键特征，即有间隙的自旋谱和“相干”磁振子行为，直至由费米矢量 $2k_\mathrm{F} = \pi(1 - x)$ 确定的波矢量 $q$。此外，我们的结果揭示了 $q \gtrsim 2k_\mathrm{F}$ 在高频下额外的广泛的、不相干的谱权重。最后，我们表明，在向铁磁相的转变附近，出现了一种先前未识别的螺旋状状态，其特征是自旋激发，让人想起 $J_1$-$J_2$ 海森堡模型。
