## Abstract
arXiv:2504.21535v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We report universal bound states of microwave-shielded ultracold molecules that solely depend on the strengths of long-range dipolar interaction and microwave coupling. Under a highly elliptic microwave field, few-molecule scatterings in three dimension are shown to be governed by effective one-dimensional (1D) models, which well reproduce the tetratomic bound state and the Born-Oppenheimer potential in three-molecule sector. For hexatomic systems comprising three identical molecules, we find much deeper bound state than the tetratomic one, with binding energy exceeding twice of the latter. Strikingly, these bound states display Bose-Fermi duality as facilitated by the effective 1D scattering with a large repulsive core from angular fluctuations. For large molecule ensembles, our results suggest the formation of elongated self-bound droplets with crystalline patterns in both bosonic and fermionic molecules.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.21535v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们报告了微波屏蔽超冷分子的普遍束缚态，该束缚态完全取决于长程偶极相互作用和微波耦合的强度。在高椭圆微波场下，三维少分子散射受有效的一维（1D）模型控制，该模型很好地再现了三分子扇区中的四原子束缚态和玻恩-奥本海默势。对于包含三个相同分子的六原子系统，我们发现比四原子系统更深的结合态，结合能超过后者的两倍。引人注目的是，这些束缚态显示出玻色-费米对偶性，这是由有效的一维散射和来自角度涨落的大排斥核所促进的。对于大分子系综，我们的结果表明在玻色子和费米子分子中形成具有结晶模式的细长自结合液滴。
