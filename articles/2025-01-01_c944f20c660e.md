## Abstract
TLDR: This work introduces the Lasso-GA Hybrid Method (LGHM), a novel approach that combines Lasso regression and genetic algorithms to rapidly construct effective Hamiltonian models that are broadly applicable to both magnetic systems and atomic displacement models.
