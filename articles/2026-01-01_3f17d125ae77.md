## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23820v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We combine for the first time the properties of ordered mesoporous thin films and complex oxide nanoparticles in the design of new heterostructures, taking advantage of the accessible tridimensional pores network. In this work, we demonstrate the feasibility of synthesizing La0.88Sr0.12MnO3 inside the pores of a mesoporous SiO2 thin film, using pulsed laser deposition. In order to understand the filling process, a set of samples were deposited for three different deposition times, on mesoporous and non-mesoporous SiO2 substrates. Their structural, magnetic, magnetocaloric and electrical transport properties were studied. All the results evidence the presence of the manganite compound inside the pores, which was confirmed by cross-section elemental mapping. X-ray reflectometry shows that it is possible to control the filling of the pores, keeping some accessible porosity. The magnetic behavior suggests the presence of weakly interacting ferromagnetic nanoparticles inside the pores. We provide here a successful strategy for the fabrication of complex oxide nanoparticles arrays with highly controlled size and ordering. Their easy incorporation into micro and nanofabrication procedures unveils direct implications in the field of interfaces and nanoparticle devices as diverse as energy conversion systems, solid oxide fuel cells, spintronics and neuromorphic memristor networks.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23820v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们首次将有序介孔薄膜和复合氧化物纳米颗粒的特性结合在新型异质结构的设计中，利用可访问的三维孔隙网络。在这项工作中，我们证明了使用脉冲激光沉积在介孔 SiO2 薄膜的孔内合成 La0.88Sr0.12MnO3 的可行性。为了了解填充过程，在介孔和非介孔 SiO2 基板上沉积了一组样品，并进行了三种不同的沉积时间。研究了它们的结构、磁性、磁热和电传输特性。所有结果都证明孔隙内存在亚锰酸盐化合物，这通过截面元素测绘得到了证实。 X射线反射测量表明可以控制孔隙的填充，保持一些可接近的孔隙率。磁性行为表明孔内存在弱相互作用的铁磁纳米粒子。我们在这里提供了一种成功的策略，用于制造具有高度控制的尺寸和排序的复杂氧化物纳米粒子阵列。它们很容易融入微米和纳米制造过程，这对能量转换系统、固体氧化物燃料电池、自旋电子学和神经形态忆阻器网络等多种界面和纳米粒子设备领域产生了直接影响。
