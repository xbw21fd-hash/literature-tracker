## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01259v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In this manuscript, we study the quench dynamics of a transverse-field XY model starting from coherent Gibbs states. The results reveal that the initial strength of magnetization plays a crucial role in the emergence of dynamical quantum phase transitions. In concrete terms, when quenching within the same phase, through the properties of observables such as Fisher zeros and magnetization, we show that the stronger the initial magnetization, the more difficult the emergence of dynamical quantum phase transitions. The underlying mechanism is that the strong initial magnetization provides a directional effect, which inhibits the spin flipping in the process of quantum quench, making the dynamical quantum phase transition difficult to emerge. Since dynamical quantum phase transitions can be experimentally realized in various artificial systems, we hope that the physics predicted here can be experimentally verified in tabletop platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01259v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在这篇手稿中，我们从相干吉布斯态开始研究横向场 XY 模型的失超动力学。结果表明，磁化强度的初始强度在动态量子相变的出现中起着至关重要的作用。具体来说，当同相内淬灭时，通过费希尔零点和磁化强度等可观测量的性质，我们表明初始磁化强度越强，动态量子相变越难出现。其根本机制是强的初始磁化强度提供了方向性效应，抑制了量子淬灭过程中的自旋翻转，使得动态量子相变难以出现。由于动态量子相变可以在各种人工系统中通过实验实现，因此我们希望这里预测的物理现象可以在桌面平台上通过实验得到验证。
