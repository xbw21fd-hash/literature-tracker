## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21913v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Reconstructing high-resolution sea surface temperatures (SST) from staggered SST measurements is essential for weather forecasting and climate projections. However, when SST measurements are sparse, the resulting inferred SST fields are rather inaccurate. Here, we demonstrate the ability of Sparse Discrete Empirical Interpolation Method (S-DEIM) to reconstruct the high-resolution SST field from sparse in situ observations, without using a model. The S-DEIM estimate consists of two terms, one computed from instantaneous in situ observations using empirical interpolation, and the other learned from the historical time series of observations using recurrent neural networks (RNNs). We train the RNNs using the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's weekly high-resolution SST dataset spanning the years 1989-2021 which constitutes the training data. Subsequently, we examine the performance of S-DEIM on the test data, comprising January 2022 to January 2023. For this test data, S-DEIM infers the high-resolution SST from 100 in situ observations, constituting only 0.2% of the high-resolution spatial grid. We show that the resulting S-DEIM reconstructions are about 40% more accurate than earlier empirical interpolation methods, such as DEIM and Q-DEIM. Furthermore, 91% of S-DEIM estimates fall within $\pm 1^\circ$C of the true SST. We also demonstrate that S-DEIM is robust with respect to sensor placement: even when the sensors are distributed randomly, S-DEIM reconstruction error deteriorates only by 1-2%. S-DEIM is also computationally efficient. Training the RNN, which is performed only once offline, takes approximately one minute. Once trained, the S-DEIM reconstructions are computed in less than a second. As such, S-DEIM can be used for rapid SST reconstruction from sparse streaming observational data in real time.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.21913v1 公告类型：新 摘要：根据交错的海表温度 (SST) 测量结果重建高分辨率海表温度 (SST) 对于天气预报和气候预测至关重要。然而，当海表温度测量稀疏时，所推断的海表温度场相当不准确。在这里，我们展示了稀疏离散经验插值法（S-DEIM）在不使用模型的情况下根据稀疏原位观测重建高分辨率海表温度场的能力。 S-DEIM 估计由两项组成，一项是使用经验插值根据瞬时原位观测计算得出的，另一项是使用循环神经网络 (RNN) 从历史时间序列观测中学习的。我们使用美国国家海洋和大气管理局 1989-2021 年每周高分辨率海表温度数据集（构成训练数据）来训练 RNN。随后，我们检查了S-DEIM在2022年1月至2023年1月的测试数据上的性能。对于该测试数据，S-DEIM从100个原位观测中推断出高分辨率海表温度，仅占高分辨率空间网格的0.2%。我们表明，所得到的 S-DEIM 重建比早期的经验插值方法（例如 DEIM 和 Q-DEIM）准确率高出约 40%。此外，91% 的 S-DEIM 估计值落在真实 SST 的 $\pm 1^\circ$C 范围内。我们还证明了 S-DEIM 在传感器放置方面具有鲁棒性：即使传感器随机分布，S-DEIM 重建误差也仅恶化 1-2%。 S-DEIM 的计算效率也很高。训练 RNN 仅离线执行一次，大约需要一分钟。训练完成后，S-DEIM 重建的计算时间不到一秒。因此，S-DEIM 可用于根据稀疏流观测数据实时快速重建海表温度。
