## Abstract
Vertical van der Waals heterostructures (vdWHs), formed through layer-by-layer stacking of two-dimensional materials, offer appealing opportunities for exploring novel physics and potential applications. However, the conventional approach of mechanical exfoliation followed by manual stacking faces significant challenges in achieving atomically clean interfaces and large size, severely limiting practical applications. Here, we proposed a comprehensive mechanism governing nucleation, orientation and stack control of vertical vdWHs during chemical vapour deposition (CVD) and demonstrated the layer-by-layer epitaxial growth of 1 cm × 1 cm sized single-crystal, rhombohedral-stacked WS2/MoS2films. First-principles calculations reveal that sulphur (S) vacancies in the underlying MoS2layer preferentially form at step edges. These S vacancies serve as nucleation sites for the upper WS2layer and enhance symmetry breaking between rhombohedral- and hexagonal-stacked WS2/MoS2. Leveraging this mechanism, we achieved unidirectionally aligned WS2islands on MoS2substrate that seamlessly coalesce into continuous single-crystal WS2/MoS2films. Systematic characterizations confirmed the single-crystal nature and rhombohedral-stacked configuration, while revealing the ferroelectric properties and self-driven photoelectric response in the resulting heterostructures. Our work establishes a fundamental mechanism for controlled growth of single-crystal vertical heterostructures, providing a robust foundation for scalable manufacturing and future industrial applications.

## 摘要（中文）
通过二维材料逐层堆叠形成的垂直范德华异质结构（vdWH）为探索新颖的物理和潜在应用提供了诱人的机会。然而，机械剥离和手动堆叠的传统方法在实现原子级清洁界面和大尺寸方面面临着重大挑战，严重限制了实际应用。在这里，我们提出了一种在化学气相沉积（CVD）过程中控制垂直VdWHs的成核、取向和堆叠控制的综合机制，并演示了1 cm×1 cm大小的单晶、菱形堆叠的WS2/MoS2薄膜的逐层外延生长。第一原理计算表明，下面的 MoS2 层中的硫 (S) 空位优先在台阶边缘形成。这些 S 空位充当上 WS2 层的成核位点，并增强菱形和六方堆叠 WS2/MoS2 之间的对称性破缺。利用这种机制，我们在 MoS2 基底上实现了单向排列的 WS2 岛，无缝地聚结成连续的单晶 WS2/MoS2 薄膜。系统表征证实了单晶性质和菱形堆叠结构，同时揭示了所得异质结构的铁电特性和自驱动光电响应。我们的工作建立了单晶垂直异质结构受控生长的基本机制，为可扩展的制造和未来的工业应用提供了坚实的基础。
