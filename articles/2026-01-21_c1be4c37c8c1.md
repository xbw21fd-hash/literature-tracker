## Abstract
arXiv:2507.12193v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The one-dimensional (1D) domain wall of 2D $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ topological orders is studied theoretically. The Ising domain wall model is shown to have an emergent SU(2)$_{1}$ conformal symmetry because of a hidden nonsymmorphic octahedral symmetry. While a weak magnetic field is an irrelevant perturbation to the bulk topological orders, it induces a domain wall transition from the Tomonaga-Luttinger liquid to a ferromagnetic order, which spontaneously breaks the anomalous $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ symmetry and the time-reversal symmetry on the domain wall. Moreover, the gapless domain wall state also realizes a 1D topological quantum critical point between a $\mathbb{Z}_{2}^{T}$-symmetry-protected topological phase and a trivial phase, thus demonstrating the holographic construction of topological transitions.

arXiv:2507.12193v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：对二维 $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ 拓扑序的一维（1D）磁畴壁进行了理论研究。由于隐藏的非对称八面体对称性，伊辛畴壁模型显示出具有新兴的 SU(2)$_{1}$ 共角对称性。虽然弱磁场对体拓扑序是无关的扰动，但它会引起磁畴壁从Tomonaga-Luttinger液体到铁磁序的转变，从而自发地打破了磁畴壁上的异常$\mathbb{Z}_{2}$对称性和时间反转对称性。此外，无间隙畴壁态还实现了$\mathbb{Z}_{2}^{T}$对称保护拓扑相和平凡相之间的一维拓扑量子临界点，从而证明了拓扑转变的全息构造。
