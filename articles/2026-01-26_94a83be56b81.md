## Abstract
arXiv:2601.16646v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We develop and numerically analyze an algebraic-geometric framework for spin-adapted coupled-cluster (CC) theory. Since the electronic Hamiltonian is SU(2)-invariant, physically relevant quantum states lie in the spin singlet sector. We give an explicit description of the SU(2)-invariant (spin singlet) many-body space by identifying it with an Artinian commutative ring, called the excitation ring, whose dimension is governed by a Narayana number. We define spin-adapted truncation varieties via embeddings of graded subspaces of this ring, and we identify the CCS truncation variety with the Veronese square of the Grassmannian. Compared to the spin-generalized formulation, this approach yields a substantial reduction in dimension and degree, with direct computational consequences. In particular, the CC degree of the truncation variety -- governing the number of homotopy paths required to compute all CC solutions -- is reduced by orders of magnitude. We present scaling studies demonstrating asymptotic improvements and we exploit this reduction to compute the full solution landscape of spin-adapted CC equations for water and lithium hydride.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.16646v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们开发并数值分析了自旋适应耦合簇（CC）理论的代数几何框架。由于电子哈密顿量是 SU(2) 不变的，物理相关的量子态位于自旋单线态区域。我们通过将 SU(2) 不变（自旋单重态）多体空间与 Artinian 交换环（称为激励环）识别来给出明确的描述，其维度由 Narayana 数控制。我们通过嵌入该环的分级子空间来定义自旋适应截断簇，并且我们将 CCS 截断簇与格拉斯曼的维罗内塞平方一起识别。与自旋广义公式相比，这种方法大大减少了维度和程度，并具有直接的计算结果。特别是，截断簇的 CC 度——控制计算所有 CC 解所需的同伦路径的数量——减少了几个数量级。我们提出了证明渐进改进的标度研究，并利用这种简化来计算水和氢化锂的自旋适应 CC 方程的完整解图。
