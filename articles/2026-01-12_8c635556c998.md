## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05566v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Large and abrupt variations in the electromagnetic properties of materials lead to dramatic effects: even a single step-like change in the refractive index induces striking phenomena, such as time-refraction and time-reflection. When the refractive index varies periodically in time, multiple time-refractions and -reflections interfere, giving rise to photonic time-crystals (PTCs). Importantly, PTCs display momentum bands separated by gaps in which the modes experience exponential amplification, drawing energy from the modulation in a non-resonant fashion. Ordinary nonlinear optics does not operate in this regime: the material response is either very weak or very slow. One of the immediate consequences is that time-reflection of light at optical frequencies has never been observed in experiments. Here, we experimentally realize an order-unity change in the refractive index occurring at sub-cycle rates, and explore the phenomena emerging from it. By varying the duration of the index change from extending over many cycles to being significantly below a single cycle, we observe that the frequency shift of the time-refraction is enhanced as the index variation occurs faster. Our experiment is the gateway for realizing sharp time-interfaces at optical frequencies, which are the key for experimenting with time-reflection, PTCs and new phenomena expected from light-matter interactions in time-varying media.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05566v1 公告类型：新 摘要：材料电磁特性的大而突然的变化会导致戏剧性的影响：即使是折射率的单步变化也会引起惊人的现象，例如时间折射和时间反射。当折射率随时间周期性变化时，多次时间折射和反射会发生干涉，从而产生光子时间晶体（PTC）。重要的是，PTC 显示出由间隙隔开的动量带，其中模式经历指数放大，以非共振方式从调制中汲取能量。普通的非线性光学不能在这种状态下工作：材料响应要么非常弱，要么非常慢。直接后果之一是实验中从未观察到光频率下的光的时间反射。在这里，我们通过实验实现了在子循环速率下发生的折射率的有序统一变化，并探索了由此产生的现象。通过改变折射率变化的持续时间，从延长多个周期到显着低于单个周期，我们观察到，随着折射率变化发生得更快，时间折射的频移增强。我们的实验是在光频率下实现尖锐时间界面的门户，这是实验时间反射、PTC 以及时变介质中光与物质相互作用所预期的新现象的关键。
