## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14003v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Contact electrification give rise to charge accumulation at the interface when two materials are brought into contact with each other. The charge accumulation at the interface will diffuse to the interior of the conducting material if the dimensions of the contacting conducting material is of the order of an unknown critical length scale. This contact electrification induced interlayer charge transfer will modify the fundamental physical properties of both the contacting materials. This review first discusses the reported experimental evidence of flexoelectricity induced contact electrification and interlayer charge transfer in conducting thin film based heterostructures. The interlayer charge transfer creates a gradient of charge carrier in both the thin films constituting the heterostructure and also modifies the electron-electron interactions. Further, the interlayer charge transfer changes the electron-phonon coupling, spin-phonon coupling and magnetoelectronic coupling that give rise to new physical behavior, which did not exist prior to the interlayer charge transfer. The new physical behaviors from interlayer charge transfer and their mechanistic origins are reanalyzed and discussed, which include spin-Hall effect of charge carriers, topological Hall effect of magnetoelectronic electromagnon, inhomogeneous magnetoelectronic multiferroic effect, flexoelectronic proximity effect and topological spin texture. This review article presents a unified picture of current status and future directions that will provide the scientists a stepping stone for research in the field of flexoelectricity mediated contact electrification and interlayer charge transfer mediated behavior in the micro/nanoscale heterostructures of the conducting materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14003v1 公告类型：新 摘要：当两种材料相互接触时，接触起电会在界面处产生电荷积累。如果接触导电材料的尺寸处于未知临界长度尺度的量级，则界面处的电荷积累将扩散到导电材料的内部。这种接触起电引起的层间电荷转移将改变两种接触材料的基本物理特性。本综述首先讨论了在导电薄膜异质结构中弯曲电诱导接触起电和层间电荷转移的实验证据。层间电荷转移在构成异质结构的薄膜中产生载流子梯度，并且还改变了电子-电子相互作用。此外，层间电荷转移改变了电子声子耦合、自旋声子耦合和磁电子耦合，从而产生了层间电荷转移之前不存在的新物理行为。重新分析和讨论了层间电荷转移的新物理行为及其机理起源，包括载流子的自旋霍尔效应、磁电子电磁子的拓扑霍尔效应、非均匀磁电子多铁效应、挠电子邻近效应和拓扑自旋织构。这篇综述文章对当前现状和未来方向进行了统一的描述，这将为科学家们在导电材料的微/纳米级异质结构中弯曲电介导的接触起电和层间电荷转移介导的行为领域的研究提供一个垫脚石。
