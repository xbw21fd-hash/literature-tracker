## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24586v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetoresistance (MR) in magnetic materials arises from spin-exchange coupling between local moments and itinerant electrons, representing a challenging many-body open-quantum problem. Here we develop a comprehensive microscopic theory of MR within an open-quantum-system framework by solving the Liouville-von Neumann equation for a hybrid system of free electrons and local moments using the time-convolutionless projection operator method. Our approach reveals both ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic MR as consequences of temperature- and field-dependent spin decoherence, encompassing spin relaxation and dephasing. In particular, the resistance associated with spin decoherence is governed by the order parameters of magnetic materials, such as the magnetization in ferromagnets and the N\'eel vector in antiferromagnets. This theory deepens the fundamental understanding of MR and offers guidance for interpreting and designing experiments on magnetic materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24586v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁性材料中的磁阻（MR）源自局部矩和流动电子之间的自旋交换耦合，代表了一个具有挑战性的多体开放量子问题。在这里，我们通过使用无时间卷积投影算子方法求解自由电子和局部矩混合系统的刘维尔-冯·诺依曼方程，在开放量子系统框架内发展了全面的磁流变微观理论。我们的方法揭示了铁磁和反铁磁MR是温度和场相关的自旋退相干的结果，包括自旋弛豫和移相。特别是，与自旋退相干相关的电阻受磁性材料的有序参数控制，例如铁磁体中的磁化强度和反铁磁体中的磁力矢量。该理论加深了对磁流变的基本理解，并为磁性材料实验的解释和设计提供了指导。
