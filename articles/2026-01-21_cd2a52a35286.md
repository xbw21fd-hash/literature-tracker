## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12554v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming materials science and engineering, offering powerful tools to navigate complexity, accelerate discovery, and optimize material design in ways previously unattainable. Driven by the accelerating pace of algorithmic advancements and increasing data availability, AI is becoming an essential competency for materials researchers. This review provides a comprehensive and structured overview of the current landscape, synthesizing recent advancements and methodologies for materials scientists seeking to effectively leverage these data-driven techniques. We survey the spectrum of machine learning approaches, from traditional algorithms to advanced deep learning architectures, including CNNs, GNNs, and Transformers, alongside emerging generative AI and probabilistic models such as Gaussian Processes for uncertainty quantification. The review also examines the pivotal role of data in this field, emphasizing how effective representation and featurization strategies, spanning compositional, structural, image-based, and language-inspired approaches, combined with appropriate preprocessing, fundamentally underpin the performance of machine learning models in materials research. Persistent challenges related to data quality, quantity, and standardization, which critically impact model development and application in materials science and engineering, are also addressed.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12554v1 公告类型：新 摘要：人工智能正在迅速改变材料科学和工程，提供强大的工具来驾驭复杂性、加速发现并以以前无法实现的方式优化材料设计。在算法进步加快和数据可用性不断提高的推动下，人工智能正在成为材料研究人员的一项重要能力。这篇综述对当前形势进行了全面、结构化的概述，综合了材料科学家寻求有效利用这些数据驱动技术的最新进展和方法。我们调查了机器学习方法的范围，从传统算法到先进的深度学习架构，包括 CNN、GNN 和 Transformer，以及新兴的生成式人工智能和概率模型，例如用于不确定性量化的高斯过程。该评论还探讨了数据在该领域的关键作用，强调有效的表示和特征化策略（涵盖组合、结构、基于图像和语言启发的方法）如何与适当的预处理相结合，从根本上支撑材料研究中机器学习模型的性能。还解决了与数据质量、数量和标准化相关的持续挑战，这些挑战严重影响材料科学和工程中的模型开发和应用。
