## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03202v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The identification of fractionalized excitations in quantum spin liquids (QSLs) remains a central challenge in condensed matter physics. In dipolar-octupolar (DO) pyrochlores, such as $\text{Ce}_2\text{Zr}_2\text{O}_7$, the candidate $\pi$-flux quantum spin ice (QSI) state is predicted to host both gapless emergent photons and a continuum of spinons. However, resolving these modes at zero field is complicated by their spectral overlap and the presence of nonmagnetic scattering near zero energy. Here, we report neutron scattering experiments on $\text{Ce}_2\text{Zr}_2\text{O}_7$ under a magnetic field along the $[1,1,1]$ direction. In contrast to previous unpolarized studies at zero-field that relied on high-temperature subtraction, we use a same-temperature high-field subtraction protocol to isolate the photon mode. Leveraging the selective coupling of the magnetic field to the dipolar degrees of freedom, we demonstrate the spectroscopic demarcation of these excitations. We observe that weak fields ($\approx 0.15$ T) suppress the low-energy photon weight while leaving the high-energy spinon continuum robust, albeit hardened. Our results, supported by gauge mean-field theory and exact diagonalization calculations, provide strong evidence for the $\pi$-flux QSI state and introduce a powerful field-tuning protocol for investigating DO-QSLs.

arXiv:2601.03202v1 公告类型：新 摘要：量子自旋液体 (QSL) 中分段激发的识别仍然是凝聚态物理中的一个核心挑战。在偶极-八极 (DO) 烧绿石中，例如 $\text{Ce}_2\text{Zr}_2\text{O}_7$，候选 $\pi$-通量量子自旋冰 (QSI) 态预计将容纳无间隙的出射光子和自旋子连续体。然而，在零场下解析这些模式很复杂，因为它们的光谱重叠以及接近零能量的非磁性散射的存在。在这里，我们报告了沿 $[1,1,1]$ 方向的磁场下 $\text{Ce}_2\text{Zr}_2\text{O}_7$ 的中子散射实验。与之前依赖高温减法的零场非偏振研究相比，我们使用相同温度的高场减法协议来隔离光子模式。利用磁场与偶极自由度的选择性耦合，我们演示了这些激发的光谱划分。我们观察到弱场（$\大约 0.15$T）抑制了低能光子权重，同时使高能自旋子连续体保持稳健，尽管是硬化的。我们的结果在规范平均场理论和精确对角化计算的支持下，为 $\pi$-flux QSI 状态提供了强有力的证据，并引入了用于研究 DO-QSL 的强大场调谐协议。
