## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05413v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Exciton dynamics in layered magnetic semiconductors provide a sensitive probe of the interplay between spin order and light-matter interaction. Here, we study thin CrSBr layers using time-resolved photoluminescence spectroscopy in an external magnetic field, revealing a step-like reduction in the exciton lifetime from 11 to 7 ps, during the magnetization flip from the antiferromagnetic to the ferromagnetic phase. The reduction of the exciton lifetime in the ferromagnetic phase persists below the N\'eel temperature, as evidenced by its strong magnetic-field dependence that disappears in the paramagnetic phase. Ab initio calculations reveal a one-dimensional nature of free excitons accompanied by a pronounced change in the oscillator strength across the magnetic phase transition predicting a shorter radiative lifetime of free excitons in the antiferromagnetic phase of CrSBr contradicting the experimental observations. This discrepancy is explained by strong localization of excitons at low tempature. We show both experimentally and theoretically that the observed magnetic switching of the exciton lifetime is attributed to a larger exciton localization volume leading to a larger oscillator strength in the ferromagnetic phase. The results show that disorder-induced localization effects play a key role in exciton dynamics in CrSBr.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05413v1 公告类型：新 摘要：层状磁性半导体中的激子动力学为自旋序和光-物质相互作用之间的相互作用提供了灵敏的探测。在这里，我们在外部磁场中使用时间分辨光致发光光谱研究了薄 CrSBr 层，揭示了在磁化从反铁磁相翻转到铁磁相期间，激子寿命从 11 ps 逐步减少到 7 ps。铁磁相中激子寿命的缩短持续低于镍原子温度，其强磁场依赖性在顺磁相中消失就证明了这一点。从头计算揭示了自由激子的一维性质，伴随着磁相变期间振荡器强度的显着变化，预测 CrSBr 反铁磁相中自由激子的辐射寿命较短，这与实验观察相矛盾。这种差异可以通过激子在低温下的强烈局域化来解释。我们从实验和理论上证明，观察到的激子寿命的磁切换归因于较大的激子局域体积，导致铁磁相中的振荡器强度较大。结果表明，无序引起的局域化效应在 CrSBr 的激子动力学中起着关键作用。
