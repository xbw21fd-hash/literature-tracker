# Current-driven nonlinear skyrmion dynamics in altermagnets

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.13499v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The center of mass and helicity are two dynamic degrees of freedom of skyrmions. In this work, we study the current-driven skyrmion motion in frustrated altermagnets. Contrary to conventional wisdom, we find that the skyrmion helicity is not locked with the skyrmion Hall angle, but unidirectionally rotates with a global angular velocity proportional to the square of the current density. In addition, we find that the helicity rotation velocity is highly anisotropic, depending on the direction of current flows. We also observe helicity oscillation in the terahertz regimes, where the nonlinear mixing between the fast and slow modes generates a magnon frequency comb. Full atomistic spin dynamics simulations verify our theoretical predictions. Our results establish frustrated altermagnets as a promising platform for skyrmionics, THz technology, and frequency comb.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.13499v1 公告类型：新 摘要：质心和螺旋度是斯格明子的两个动态自由度。在这项工作中，我们研究了受挫交流磁体中电流驱动的斯格明子运动。与传统观点相反，我们发现斯格明子螺旋度并不与斯格明子霍尔角锁定，而是以与电流密度平方成正比的全局角速度单向旋转。此外，我们发现螺旋旋转速度具有高度各向异性，具体取决于电流流动的方向。我们还观察到太赫兹范围内的螺旋振荡，其中快模和慢模之间的非线性混合产生磁振子频率梳。完整的原子自旋动力学模拟验证了我们的理论预测。我们的结果表明受挫的交流磁体是斯格明子学、太赫兹技术和频率梳的一个有前途的平台。
