## Abstract
arXiv:2602.05332v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin current offers a promising route toward energy-efficient and high-speed information processing. Developing efficient methods for their generation remains a central challenge in spintronics. Here, we investigate spin current generation via two-dimensional topological spin textures: a skyrmion crystal (SkX) with out-of-plane magnetization, a bimeron crystal (BmX) with in-plane magnetization, and a meron crystal (MX) with zero net magnetization. We show that these distinct spin textures generate spin currents with characteristic spin polarization directions. In the absence of spin--orbit coupling, the SkX and BmX generate spin currents polarized along their magnetization directions, whereas the MX yields no spin current. Upon introducing spin--orbit coupling, while the behavior of the SkX does not qualitatively change, the BmX generates nonzero spin currents in multiple polarization directions. Notably, the MX, despite its zero net magnetization, exhibits a pronounced spin current with out-of-plane spin polarization, driven by an enhanced spin Berry curvature associated with characteristic band degeneracy. We further demonstrate that the electronic and spin transport properties of each texture are governed by their magnetic symmetries. Our results highlight the topological spin textures as efficient sources of spin current even without net magnetization, expanding the design for spintronics devices based on topological magnetic metals.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.05332v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自旋电流为实现节能和高速信息处理提供了一条有前途的途径。开发有效的方法来产生它们仍然是自旋电子学的一个核心挑战。在这里，我们通过二维拓扑自旋纹理研究自旋电流的产生：具有面外磁化的斯格明子晶体（SkX）、具有面内磁化的双聚子晶体（BmX）和具有零净磁化的聚子晶体（MX）。我们表明，这些不同的自旋纹理产生具有特征自旋极化方向的自旋流。在没有自旋轨道耦合的情况下，SkX 和 BmX 产生沿其磁化方向极化的自旋电流，而 MX 不产生自旋电流。引入自旋轨道耦合后，虽然 SkX 的行为没有发生质的变化，但 BmX 在多个极化方向上产生非零自旋电流。值得注意的是，尽管 MX 的净磁化强度为零，但在与特征能带简并相关的自旋贝里曲率增强的驱动下，仍表现出具有面外自旋极化的明显自旋流。我们进一步证明，每种纹理的电子和自旋输运特性都受其磁对称性的控制。我们的结果强调，即使没有净磁化，拓扑自旋纹理也是自旋电流的有效来源，扩展了基于拓扑磁性金属的自旋电子器件的设计。
