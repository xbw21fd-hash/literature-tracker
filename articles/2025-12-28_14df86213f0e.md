# Unlocking Interfacial Catalytic Halogen Atom Transfer at Ag Electrodes with Brønsted Acids

## Abstract
Halogen atom transfer enables mild and programmable access to carbon-centered radicals from accessible C(sp3) organic halides for synthetic chemistry. Unlocking this fundamental mechanistic step with reusable heterogeneous electrocatalysts requires strategies to identify and molecularly tune interfacial halogen atom transfer at complex electrified interfaces. Here, we reveal that Brønsted acids unlock interfacial halogen-atom transfer of unactivated alkyl bromides at catalytic Ag electrodes near the thermodynamic potential. In the absence of Brønsted acids, electrochemical and in-situ spectroscopic data are consistent with the passivation of Ag surfaces by the halogen upon a single turnover, requiring an overpotential of nearly 1.1 V to activate the electrophile that would erode functional group tolerance. The addition of acids promotes halogen atom transfer by 1.1 V, enabling access to primary carbon-centered radicals under mild conditions. This promotion effect is unique to catalytic Ag electrode surfaces and general across structurally diverse acids, provided that their pKa falls below a specific threshold. By demonstrating C(sp3)‒C(sp3) bond formation, we show that Brønsted acids enable interfacial halogen atom transfer at reusable catalytic electrodes for electroorganic synthesis.

## 摘要（中文）
卤素原子转移能够温和且可编程地从可接近的 C(sp3) 有机卤化物中获得以碳为中心的自由基，用于合成化学。使用可重复使用的异质电催化剂解锁这一基本机制步骤需要识别和分子调节复杂带电界面上的界面卤素原子转移的策略。在这里，我们揭示了布朗斯台德酸在接近热力学势的催化银电极上解锁未活化的烷基溴的界面卤素原子转移。在不存在布朗斯台德酸的情况下，电化学和原位光谱数据与单次翻转时卤素对银表面的钝化一致，需要近 1.1 V 的过电势来激活亲电子试剂，从而削弱官能团的耐受性。添加酸可促进卤素原子转移 1.1 V，从而能够在温和条件下获得伯碳中心自由基。这种促进效应是催化银电极表面所独有的，并且在结构不同的酸中普遍存在，前提是它们的 pKa 低于特定阈值。通过证明 C(sp3)−C(sp3) 键的形成，我们表明布朗斯台德酸能够在可重复使用的催化电极上实现界面卤素原子转移，用于有机电合成。
