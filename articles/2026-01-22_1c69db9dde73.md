## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14747v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study investigates the mathematical existence and asymptotic properties of Ulanowicz's structural resilience in complex systems such as supply chain networks. While ecological evidence suggests that sustainable systems gravitate toward an optimal state at $\alpha = 1/\mathrm{e}$, the universality of this configuration in generalized networks remains theoretically unverified. We prove that while optimal resilience is unattainable in two-node networks due to structural over-determinacy, it exists for any directed graph with $N_\mathcal{V} \geq 3$. By constructing a symmetric network model with three types of link weights $(x, y, z)$ and uniform marginal distributions, we derive the governing equations for the optimal resilience configuration. Our analytical and numerical results reveal that as the network size $N_\mathcal{V}$ increases, the link weights required to maintain optimal resilience exhibit a power-law scaling behavior: the adjacent links scale as $O(N_\mathcal{V}^{-1})$, while the non-adjacent links scale as $O(N_\mathcal{V}^{-2})$, both accompanied by specific logarithmic corrections. This work establishes a rigorous mathematical foundation for the optimal resilience framework and provides a unified perspective on how entropy-based principles govern the robustness and evolution of large-scale complex networks, which may offer quantitative guidance for designing large-scale networked systems under robustness constraints.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.14747v1 发布类型：新 摘要：本研究研究了供应链网络等复杂系统中 Ulanowicz 结构弹性的数学存在性和渐近性质。虽然生态证据表明可持续系统倾向于 $\alpha = 1/\mathrm{e}$ 的最佳状态，但这种配置在广义网络中的普遍性在理论上仍未得到验证。我们证明，虽然由于结构超确定性，最佳弹性在两节点网络中无法实现，但它对于任何具有 $N_\mathcal{V} \geq 3$ 的有向图都存在。通过构建具有三种类型的链路权重 $(x, y, z)$ 和均匀边际分布的对称网络模型，我们推导出最佳弹性配置的控制方程。我们的分析和数值结果表明，随着网络规模 $N_\mathcal{V}$ 的增加，保持最佳弹性所需的链接权重表现出幂律缩放行为：相邻链接缩放为 $O(N_\mathcal{V}^{-1})$，而非相邻链接缩放为 $O(N_\mathcal{V}^{-2})$，两者都伴随着特定的对数校正。这项工作为最优弹性框架建立了严格的数学基础，并为基于熵的原理如何控制大规模复杂网络的鲁棒性和演化提供了统一的视角，这可能为设计鲁棒性约束下的大规模网络系统提供定量指导。
