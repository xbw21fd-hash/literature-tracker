## Abstract
arXiv:2601.21704v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This paper examines the connection between photonic band-gap formation in two types of two-dimensional photonic crystals and the emergence of reverse electromagnetic energy flows generated by linearly polarized plane waves incident on a photonic-crystal slab. We show that these reverse energy flows, observed in both transmitted and reflected fields, originate from vortex structures in the Poynting vector. The resulting energy-flow patterns exhibit striking analogies to vortex formation in fluid motion past obstacles. The geometry and dynamics of the Poynting-vector vortices determine whether the incident electromagnetic energy is impeded, leading to the formation of photonic band gaps, or instead guided through the structure, enabling transmission.

arXiv:2601.21704v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本文研究了两种二维光子晶体中的光子带隙形成与入射到光子晶体板的线偏振平面波产生的反向电磁能流之间的联系。我们表明，在透射场和反射场中观察到的这些反向能量流源自坡印廷矢量中的涡旋结构。由此产生的能量流模式与流体穿过障碍物运动中的涡流形成有着惊人的相似之处。坡印廷矢量涡旋的几何形状和动力学决定了入射电磁能是否受到阻碍，从而导致光子带隙的形成，或者是否被引导通过结构，从而实现传输。
