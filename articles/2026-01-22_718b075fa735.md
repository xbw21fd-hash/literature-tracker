## Abstract
arXiv:2601.14292v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: According to the latest provisional statistics released by the UK Department for Transport, Great Britain recorded 1,633 road deaths in 2024, representing a slight increase from 2023 and raising concerns about safety progress, which indicates that preventable fatalities remain a challenge. The deployment of advanced mobility systems, even certified and safety-assessed, is not sufficient to deliver improved safety outcomes, and existing road infrastructure is not sufficiently equipped to prevent severe collisions. Successful application of the ``Safe System'' approach demands systems thinking in an integrated and holistic manner, encompassing all aspects of road safety. This paper argues that road safety must be managed as a complex socio-technical system where risk evolves dynamically and must be continuously monitored. To address these safety gaps, we propose a systems thinking approach that identifies factors contributing to fatal outcomes and mitigates them. The framework consists of four steps: 1) List stakeholders who influence road safety, 2) Model the interactions between these stakeholders, 3) List assumptions that might be identified as factors for fatalities, and 4) Monitor these assumptions throughout the system lifecycle. The approach is applied to the United Kingdom (UK) road network to demonstrate feasibility. The study provides actionable guidance and new KPIs categories for stakeholders to implement road safety monitoring and eliminate any unreasonable road safety risks.

arXiv:2601.14292v1 公告类型：新 摘要：根据英国交通部最新发布的临时统计数据，英国 2024 年道路死亡人数为 1,633 人，较 2023 年略有增加，引发人们对安全进展的担忧，这表明可预防的死亡事故仍然是一个挑战。部署先进的移动系统，即使经过认证和安全评估，也不足以改善安全结果，而且现有的道路基础设施也没有足够的装备来防止严重碰撞。 “安全系统”方法的成功应用需要以综合和整体的方式进行系统思考，涵盖道路安全的各个方面。本文认为，道路安全必须作为一个复杂的社会技术系统进行管理，其中风险动态变化，并且必须持续监控。为了解决这些安全差距，我们提出了一种系统思维方法，可以识别导致致命结果的因素并减轻它们。该框架由四个步骤组成：1) 列出影响道路安全的利益相关者，2) 对这些利益相关者之间的交互进行建模，3) 列出可能被识别为死亡因素的假设，以及 4) 在整个系统生命周期中监控这些假设。该方法应用于英国 (UK) 道路网络以证明可行性。该研究为利益相关者实施道路安全监测并消除任何不合理的道路安全风险提供了可操作的指导和新的关键绩效指标类别。
