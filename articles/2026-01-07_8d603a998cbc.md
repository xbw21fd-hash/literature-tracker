## Abstract
Magnetite nanoparticles (NPs) loaded with antibiotics can be used for targeted delivery of pharmacotherapy directly to the infected organ. In this way, they can overcome the shortcomings of traditional antimicrobial agents, but the amount of research in this area is limited, especially regarding the magnetite NPs synthesized by electron beam technology. The research aim is to study the effect of magnetite ligand-free NPs synthesized by electron beam physical vapor deposition, stabilized with polyvinylpyrrolidone and conjugated with the beta-lactam antibiotic ampicillin, on the development of reference strains of microorganisms. The NPs were synthesized by electron beam technology in vacuum, identified by standard nanotechnology methods and modified with polyvinylpyrrolidone and ampicillin. The nanocomposite’s hydrodynamic diameter, the loading and release of ampicillin, and the effect on the reference strains S. aureus ATCC 25923 and E. coli ATCC 25922 were determined. The synthesis resulted in magnetite NPs (10.7 nm) in a sodium chloride matrix. After coating and conjugation, the particle size was 190 nm. About 2% of ampicillin was loaded into them, which was completely released in 180 minutes with pseudo-second-order kinetics. The minimum inhibitory concentrations against test cultures S. aureus and E. coli were 1.24 μg/ml and 4.93 μg/ml, respectively. The advantage of the nanocomposite was a smaller amount of antibiotic as a part of this nanomaterial mass compared to the pure antibiotic required for a bacteriostatic effect. Therefore, the study reflects one of the options for combining magnetic NPs and an antibiotic that requires following efforts to create a clinically effective nanobiotic.

## 摘要（中文）
负载抗生素的磁铁矿纳米颗粒（NP）可用于将药物治疗直接靶向递送至感染器官。通过这种方式，它们可以克服传统抗菌剂的缺点，但该领域的研究量有限，特别是对于电子束技术合成的磁铁矿纳米颗粒。研究目的是研究通过电子束物理气相沉积合成的无磁铁矿配体的纳米粒子，用聚乙烯吡咯烷酮稳定并与β-内酰胺抗生素氨苄青霉素缀合，对微生物参考菌株的开发的影响。这些纳米颗粒是通过电子束技术在真空中合成的，通过标准纳米技术方法进行鉴定，并用聚乙烯吡咯烷酮和氨苄西林进行修饰。确定了纳米复合材料的流体动力学直径、氨苄青霉素的负载和释放以及对参考菌株金黄色葡萄球菌 ATCC 25923 和大肠杆菌 ATCC 25922 的影响。合成产生了氯化钠基质中的磁铁矿纳米粒子 (10.7 nm)。包被和缀合后，粒径为190 nm。负载约2%的氨苄青霉素，180分钟内完全释放，准二级动力学。对测试培养物金黄色葡萄球菌和大肠杆菌的最低抑制浓度分别为 1.24 μg/ml 和 4.93 μg/ml。与抑菌效果所需的纯抗生素相比，纳米复合材料的优点是作为纳米材料质量一部分的抗生素含量较少。因此，该研究反映了将磁性纳米粒子与抗生素结合的选择之一，需要做出以下努力来创造临床上有效的纳米抗生素。
