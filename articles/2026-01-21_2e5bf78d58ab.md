## Abstract
arXiv:2601.12588v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Indium selenide (In$_2$Se$_3$) has garnered significant attention for its intriguing properties and applications in batteries, solar cells, photodetectors and ferroelectric devices. However, the controlled synthesis of single phase $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ remains challenging owing to its complex phase diagram, presence of multiple polymorphs and the high volatility of selenium that induces non-stoichiometry and unintentional carrier doping. For ferroelectric {\alpha}-In2Se3, minimizing the carrier density is essential because leakage current can obscure polarization switching. Here, we report the growth of $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ single crystals using a unique approach, the Se-flux assisted modified vertical Bridgman technique combined with liquid encapsulation under high pressure. This approach creates a high-pressure, Se-rich environment that effectively minimizes Se-vaporization. Structural and compositional analysis using X-ray diffraction, transmission electron microscopy and energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy confirm the formation of pure $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ single crystals with 3R stacking. Furthermore, the crystals exhibit remarkably low carrier density of 1.5-3.2 $\times$ 10$^{16}$ cm$^{-3}$ at 300K$-$the lowest reported to date, reflecting a significant suppression of Se-vacancies relative to the conventional Bridgman or melt-grown crystals. Through transport and ARPES measurements on different batches of crystals, we also demonstrate that the amount of Se-flux plays a crucial role in controlling Se-vacancies. Our results thus establish this modified Bridgman method as an effective strategy for synthesizing large $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ single crystals with reduced intrinsic defects. This technique can be broadly applied to grow other volatile chalcogenides with reduced defects and controlled stoichiometry.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.12588v1 公告类型：新 摘要：硒化铟 (In$_2$Se$_3$) 因其在电池、太阳能电池、光电探测器和铁电器件中的有趣特性和应用而受到广泛关注。然而，单相 $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ 的受控合成仍然具有挑战性，因为其复杂的相图、多种多晶型物的存在以及硒的高挥发性会导致非化学计量和无意的载流子掺杂。对于铁电 {α}-In2Se3，最小化载流子密度至关重要，因为漏电流会掩盖极化切换。在这里，我们报告了使用独特方法（Se-flux 辅助改良垂直布里奇曼技术与高压液体封装相结合）$\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ 单晶的生长。这种方法创造了一个高压、富含硒的环境，有效地减少了硒的蒸发。使用 X 射线衍射、透射电子显微镜和能量色散 X 射线光谱进行的结构和成分分析证实了具有 3R 堆叠的纯 $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ 单晶的形成。此外，该晶体在 300K$-$ 温度下表现出非常低的载流子密度，为 1.5-3.2 $\times$ 10$^{16}$ cm$^{-3}$，是迄今为止报道的最低值，反映出相对于传统布里奇曼或熔融生长晶体，Se 空位受到显着抑制。通过对不同批次晶体的传输和 ARPES 测量，我们还证明 Se 通量的量在控制 Se 空位方面起着至关重要的作用。因此，我们的结果确立了这种改进的布里奇曼方法作为合成具有减少的内在缺陷的大型 $\alpha$-In$_2$Se$_3$ 单晶的有效策略。该技术可广泛应用于生长其他挥发性硫属化物，减少缺陷并控制化学计量。
