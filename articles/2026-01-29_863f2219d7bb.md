## Abstract
arXiv:2601.20033v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a deep learning framework for modeling and analyzing the small-angle scattering data of polydisperse hard-rod systems, a widely used models for anisotropic colloidal particles. We use a variational autoencoder-based neural network to learn the mapping from the system parameters such as the volume fraction, rod length, and polydispersity, to the scattering function. The dataset for training and testing such neural network model is obtained from Markov chain Monte Carlo simulation of 20,000 hard spherocylinders using the hard particle Monte Carlo package from the HOOMD-blue. Four datasets were generated, each with 5,500 pairs of system parameters and corresponding scattering functions. We use one of the dataset to investigate the feasibility of the learning, and three additional datasets with different polydisperse distribution to demonstrate the generality of our approach. The neural network model transcends the fundamental limitations of the Percus-Yevick approximation by accurately capturing anisotropic interactions and high-concentration effects that analytical models often fail to resolve. This framework achieves significantly higher accuracy in reproducing scattering functions and enables a least-square fitting routine for quantitative data analysis.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.20033v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种深度学习框架，用于建模和分析多分散硬棒系统的小角度散射数据，这是一种广泛使用的各向异性胶体颗粒模型。我们使用基于变分自动编码器的神经网络来学习从系统参数（例如体积分数、杆长度和多分散性）到散射函数的映射。用于训练和测试此类神经网络模型的数据集是使用 HOOMD-blue 的硬粒子蒙特卡罗包对 20,000 个硬球柱进行马尔可夫链蒙特卡罗模拟而获得的。生成了四个数据集，每个数据集都有 5,500 对系统参数和相应的散射函数。我们使用其中一个数据集来研究学习的可行性，并使用三个具有不同多分散分布的附加数据集来证明我们方法的通用性。神经网络模型通过准确捕获分析模型通常无法解决的各向异性相互作用和高浓度效应，超越了 Percus-Yevick 近似的基本局限性。该框架在再现散射函数方面实现了显着更高的精度，并支持用于定量数据分析的最小二乘拟合例程。
