## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06272v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The origin of life poses a problem of combinatorial feasibility: How can temporally supported functional organization arise in exponentially branching assembly spaces when unguided exploration behaves as a memoryless random walk? We show that nonlinear threshold-cascade dynamics in connected interaction networks provide a minimal, substrate-agnostic mechanism that can soften this obstruction. Below a critical connectivity threshold, cascades die out locally and structured input-output response mappings remain sparse and transient-a "functional desert" in which accumulation is dynamically unsupported. Near the critical percolation threshold, system-spanning cascades emerge, enabling discriminative functional responses. We illustrate this transition using a minimal toy model and generalize the argument to arbitrary networked systems. Also near criticality, cascades introduce finite-timescale structural and functional coherence, directional bias, and weak dynamical path-dependence into otherwise memoryless exploration, allowing biased accumulation. This connectivity-driven transition-functional percolation-requires only generic ingredients: interacting units, nonlinear thresholds, influence transmission, and non-zero coherence times. The mechanism does not explain specific biochemical pathways, but it identifies a necessary dynamical regime in which structured functional organization can emerge and be temporarily supported, providing a physical foundation for how combinatorial feasibility barriers can be crossed through network dynamics alone.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06272v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：生命起源提出了组合可行性问题：当无引导的探索表现为无记忆随机游走时，时间支持的功能组织如何在指数分支装配空间中出现？我们表明，连接交互网络中的非线性阈值级联动力学提供了一种最小的、与基底无关的机制，可以软化这种障碍。低于关键的连通性阈值，级联会局部消失，结构化的输入输出响应映射仍然稀疏和短暂——这是一个“功能沙漠”，其中的积累不受动态支持。接近临界渗滤阈值时，出现系统级联反应，从而实现有区别的功能反应。我们使用最小的玩具模型来说明这种转变，并将该论点推广到任意网络系统。同样接近临界点，级联将有限时间尺度的结构和功能一致性、方向偏差和弱动态路径依赖性引入到其他无记忆探索中，从而允许有偏差的积累。这种连接驱动的过渡功能渗透仅需要通用成分：相互作用单元、非线性阈值、影响传输和非零相干时间。该机制不能解释特定的生化途径，但它确定了一种必要的动态机制，其中结构化功能组织可以出现并得到暂时支持，为如何仅通过网络动态跨越组合可行性障碍提供了物理基础。
