## Abstract
arXiv:2601.17525v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Molecular chirality interacting in a non-negligible manner with the spin angular momentum of subatomic particles, mainly electrons or photons, is the cause of a variety of spin-dependent filtering effects in quantum transport. Among them, spin-selective transport at room temperature is clearly one of the most promising properties in the quest for functional spintronic devices. In this context, two main effects have been experimentally investigated in the past 25 years and have attracted significant interest within the community: the so-called Electronic Magnetochiral Anisotropy (eMChA) and Chirality Induced Spin Selectivity (CISS). Despite extensive research, there is still a lack of consensus in the modeling of their microscopic mechanisms. As a consequence, it remains unclear whether the two are truly distinct or if they originate from a common physical cause. With the long-term goal of modelling the main different theories and to test them against the available experimental evidence, we programmed the core of an efficient kinetic Monte Carlo code. The current code models electron transport under an external voltage, distinguishes between $\alpha$ and $\beta$ spin currents, and parametrizes molecules by their intrinsic electron mobility and the effective coupling between electron movement, spin and chirality. The code allows obtaining spin filtering values arising from the effective coupling between these three. We obtain an effect that vanishes at low voltages, with the asymmetry between positive and negative voltages typically found in electrical magnetochiral anisotropy experiments.

arXiv:2601.17525v1 公告类型：新 摘要：分子手性以不可忽略的方式与亚原子粒子（主要是电子或光子）的自旋角动量相互作用，是量子输运中各种自旋相关过滤效应的原因。其中，室温下的自旋选择性输运显然是寻找功能性自旋电子器件中最有前途的特性之一。在此背景下，过去 25 年对两种主要效应进行了实验研究，并引起了业界的极大兴趣：所谓的电子磁手性各向异性 (eMChA) 和手性诱导自旋选择性 (CISS)。尽管进行了广泛的研究，但对其微观机制的建模仍缺乏共识。因此，目前尚不清楚这两者是否真正不同，或者它们是否源于共同的物理原因。出于对主要不同理论进行建模并根据可用实验证据对其进行测试的长期目标，我们编写了高效动力学蒙特卡罗代码的核心。当前的代码模拟外部电压下的电子传输，区分 $\alpha$ 和 $\beta$ 自旋电流，并通过分子的固有电子迁移率以及电子运动、自旋和手性之间的有效耦合来参数化分子。该代码允许获取由这三者之间的有效耦合产生的自旋过滤值。我们获得了一种在低电压下消失的效应，正电压和负电压之间的不对称性通常在电磁手性各向异性实验中发现。
