## Abstract
arXiv:2510.02415v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Machine learning models for the global atmosphere that are capable of producing stable, multi-year simulations of Earth's climate have recently been developed. However, the ability of these ML models to generalize beyond the training distribution remains an open question. In this study, we evaluate the climate response of several state-of-the-art ML models (ACE2-ERA5, NeuralGCM, and cBottle) to a uniform sea surface temperature warming, a widely used benchmark for evaluating climate change. We assess each ML model's performance relative to a physics-based general circulation model (NOAA's Geophysical Fluid Dynamics Laboratory AM4) across key diagnostics, including surface air temperature, precipitation, temperature and wind profiles, and top-of-atmosphere radiation. While the ML models reproduce key aspects of the physical model response, particularly the response of precipitation, some exhibit notable departures from robust physical responses, including radiative responses and land region warming. Our results highlight the promise and current limitations of ML models for climate change applications and suggest that further improvements are needed for robust out-of-sample generalization.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.02415v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：最近开发了全球大气机器学习模型，能够对地球气候进行稳定的多年模拟。然而，这些机器学习模型泛化到训练分布之外的能力仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。在这项研究中，我们评估了几种最先进的机器学习模型（ACE2-ERA5、NeuralGCM 和 cBottle）对均匀海面温度变暖的气候响应，这是评估气候变化的广泛使用的基准。我们评估每个机器学习模型相对于基于物理的大气环流模型（NOAA 地球物理流体动力学实验室 AM4）的关键诊断性能，包括地表气温、降水、温度和风廓线以及大气层顶部辐射。虽然机器学习模型再现了物理模型响应的关键方面，特别是降水的响应，但一些模型表现出与稳健物理响应的显着偏离，包括辐射响应和陆地区域变暖。我们的结果强调了机器学习模型在气候变化应用中的前景和当前的局限性，并表明需要进一步改进以实现稳健的样本外泛化。
