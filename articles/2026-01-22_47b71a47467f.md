## Abstract
arXiv:2512.03609v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Active proteins and membrane-bound motors exert force dipole flows along fluid interfaces and lipid bilayers. We develop a Hamiltonian framework for the interactions of pusher and puller dipoles embedded in an incompressible two-dimensional membrane supported by a shallow viscous subphase. Beginning from the Brinkman-regularized Stokes equations of the membrane-subphase system, we construct the near- and far-field dipolar velocity and associated stream functions. Although generic dipoles reorient under the local membrane vorticity, we show that the far-field dipolar flow is vorticity-free; force-free motors therefore retain fixed orientation and obey a position-based Hamiltonian dynamics in which the positions of N dipoles evolve via an effective Hamiltonian built from the dipolar stream function. In the near field, where the flow possesses finite vorticity, a Hamiltonian formulation is recovered in the quenched-orientation limit. We demonstrate this Hamiltonian structure by simulating ensembles of pusher and puller dipoles and compare the dynamics generated by the near-field and far-field Hamiltonians. For identical dipoles, the far-field Hamiltonian produces rapid clustering from random initial conditions, whereas the near-field Hamiltonian suppresses collapse and yields extended, non-aggregating configurations. Our work thus provides a concrete realization of position-based Hamiltonian descriptions for active particles in incompressible fluid membranes and establishes the important roles of membrane incompressibility and fluid vorticity that leads to the emergence of such Hamiltonian descriptions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.03609v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：活性蛋白和膜结合马达沿着流体界面和脂质双层施加力偶极子流动。我们开发了一个哈密顿框架，用于嵌入由浅粘性亚相支撑的不可压缩二维膜中的推偶极子和拉偶极子的相互作用。从膜-亚相系统的 Brinkman 正则化 Stokes 方程开始，我们构造了近场和远场偶极速度以及相关的流函数。尽管一般偶极子在局部膜涡度下会重新定向，但我们表明远场偶极流是无涡度的；因此，无力电机保持固定方向并服从基于位置的哈密顿动力学，其中 N 偶极子的位置通过根据偶极流函数构建的有效哈密顿量而演变。在近场中，流动具有有限涡度，哈密顿公式在淬火方向极限下恢复。我们通过模拟推偶极子和拉偶极子的系综来演示这种哈密顿结构，并比较近场和远场哈密顿量产生的动力学。对于相同的偶极子，远场哈密顿量从随机初始条件产生快速聚类，而近场哈密顿量抑制崩溃并产生扩展的非聚集配置。因此，我们的工作为不可压缩流体膜中的活性粒子提供了基于位置的哈密顿描述的具体实现，并确立了膜不可压缩性和流体涡度的重要作用，从而导致了这种哈密顿描述的出现。
