## Abstract
arXiv:2502.08702v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Robust phases of matter, which remain stable under small perturbations, are of fundamental importance in statistical physics and quantum information. Recent advances in interactive quantum dynamics have led to renewed interest in out-of-equilibrium dynamical phases and associated phase transitions in both classical and quantum many-body systems. Motivated by these developments, we investigate whether a stable absorbing phase can exist in one-dimensional classical stochastic systems, with local update rules, in the presence of fluctuations. We study models with multiple absorbing states related by discrete symmetries, such as Z2 for two-state systems, and Z3 or S3 for three-state systems. In these models, domain walls perform random walks and coarsen under local rules, which, if perfect, eventually bring the system to an absorbing state in polynomial time. However, imperfect feedback can cause domain walls to branch, potentially leading to an opposing active phase. While two-state models exhibit a well-known transition between absorbing and active phases as the branching rate increases, in three-state models with only local dynamics, branching is a relevant perturbation, ruling out a robust absorbing phase under purely local rules. However, we discover that by incorporating nonlocal information into the feedback, the absorbing phase can be stabilized, with the transition between the active and absorbing phases belonging to a new universality class. Finally, we outline how these classical rules can be implemented using deterministic quantum circuits and discuss their connections to passive error correction.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2502.08702v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在小扰动下保持稳定的物质的鲁棒相在统计物理和量子信息中具有根本重要性。交互式量子动力学的最新进展引起了人们对经典和量子多体系统中的非平衡动力学相和相关相变的新兴趣。受这些发展的推动，我们研究了在存在波动的情况下，具有局部更新规则的一维经典随机系统中是否可以存在稳定的吸收相。我们研究具有由离散对称性相关的多个吸收态的模型，例如二态系统的 Z2 和三态系统的 Z3 或 S3。在这些模型中，畴壁在局部规则下执行随机游走和粗化，如果完美的话，最终使系统在多项式时间内达到吸收状态。然而，不完美的反馈可能会导致磁畴壁分支，从而可能导致相反的活性相。虽然随着分支率的增加，二态模型在吸收相和活性相之间表现出众所周知的转变，但在仅具有局部动力学的三态模型中，分支是相关的扰动，排除了纯粹局部规则下的稳健吸收相。然而，我们发现，通过将非局部信息纳入反馈中，可以稳定吸收阶段，并且活跃阶段和吸收阶段之间的过渡属于新的普遍性类别。最后，我们概述了如何使用确定性量子电路来实现这些经典规则，并讨论它们与被动纠错的联系。
