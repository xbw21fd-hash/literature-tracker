## Abstract
arXiv:2602.01097v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Magnetic fields play a central role in accretion physics around black holes, yet their physical origin within accretion flows remains an open problem. In this work, we investigate the generation and subsequent evolution of magnetic fields triggered by anisotropic radiation fields in black hole accretion discs with compact rotating inner corona. We self-consistently evolve the magnetic field using the generalized field evolution MHD equation, including advection, shear-driven induction, and Hall effects. The radiation field acts as a primary field generator, while azimuthal rotation in the magnetized plasma provides rapid amplification. We find that radiation-generated fields efficiently reach a dominant toroidal component by Keplerian rotation, leading to magnetic field strengths of order $\sim 10^{8}\,\mathrm{G}$ in the vicinity of a 10 solar mass black hole and accretion disc-corona emitting at luminosity equivalent to the Eddington unit. These magnetic fields are achieved within viscous timescales and reach or exceed local equipartition estimates based on gas pressure. When vertical outflows are included, the amplified magnetic fields are advected into the corona, magnetizing disc-launched winds and jet precursors with field strengths of similar order. Our results demonstrate that radiation is not merely a passive component of accretion flows, but provides a robust and unavoidable trigger for the generation of dynamically significant magnetic fields. Our results provide a physically grounded explanation for the origin of large-scale, structured magnetic fields in and around accretion discs. This mechanism offers a pathway for magnetizing accretion discs and their outflows without invoking externally supplied magnetic flux, with broad implications for X-ray binaries, active galactic nuclei and other transients such as gamma-ray bursts (GRBs).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.01097v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：磁场在黑洞周围的吸积物理中发挥着核心作用，但它们在吸积流中的物理起源仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。在这项工作中，我们研究了具有致密旋转内冕的黑洞吸积盘中各向异性辐射场触发的磁场的产生和随后的演化。我们使用广义场演化 MHD 方程自洽地演化磁场，包括平流、剪切驱动感应和霍尔效应。辐射场充当主场发生器，而磁化等离子体中的方位旋转提供快速放大。我们发现辐射产生的场通过开普勒旋转有效地到达主要的环形分量，导致在 10 个太阳质量的黑洞和吸积盘日冕附近产生 $\sim 10^{8}\,\mathrm{G}$ 量级的磁场强度，其发射光度相当于爱丁顿单位。这些磁场是在粘性时间尺度内实现的，并且达到或超过基于气压的局部均分估计。当包括垂直流出时，放大的磁场被平流进入日冕，磁化圆盘发射的风和具有相似数量级场强的射流前兆。我们的结果表明，辐射不仅仅是吸积流的无源组成部分，而且为动态显着磁场的生成提供了强大且不可避免的触发因素。我们的结果为吸积盘内部和周围的大规模结构化磁场的起源提供了物理依据的解释。这种机制提供了一种在不调用外部提供的磁通量的情况下磁化吸积盘及其流出物的途径，对 X 射线双星、活动星系核和其他瞬变如伽马射线爆发 (GRB) 具有广泛的影响。
