# Dissipative corrections to the particle momentum spectrum of a decoupling fluid

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24994v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We present an \emph{ab initio} calculation within quantum statistical field theory and linear response theory, of the dissipative correction to the momentum spectrum of scalar particles emitted at decoupling (freeze-out) from a relativistic fluid assuming the initial state to be in local thermodynamic equilibrium. We obtain an expansion of the Wigner function of the interacting quantum field in terms of the gradients of the classical thermo-hydrodynamic fields - four-temperature vector and reduced chemical potential - evaluated on the initial local-equilibrium hypersurface, rather than on the decoupling (freeze-out) hypersurface as usual in kinetic theory. The gradient expansion includes an unexpected zeroth order term depending on the differences between thermo-hydrodynamic fields at the decoupling and the initial hypersurface. This term encodes a memory of the initial state which is related to the long-distance persistence of the correlation function between Wigner operator and stress-energy tensor and charged current that is discussed in detail. We address the phenomenological implications of these corrections for the momentum spectra measured in relativistic nuclear collisions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24994v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们提出了量子统计场论和线性响应理论中的 \emph{ab initio} 计算，对相对论流体解耦（冻结）时发射的标量粒子的动量谱进行耗散校正，假设初始状态处于局部热力学平衡。我们根据经典热流体力学场的梯度（四温度矢量和约化化学势）获得了相互作用量子场的维格纳函数的展开，该梯度是在初始局部平衡超曲面上评估的，而不是像动力学理论中通常在解耦（冻结）超曲面上评估的。梯度展开包括一个意想不到的零阶项，该零阶项取决于解耦处的热流体动力场与初始超曲面之间的差异。该术语编码初始状态的记忆，该记忆与维格纳算子和应力能量张量以及详细讨论的带电电流之间的相关函数的长距离持久性有关。我们讨论了这些校正对相对论核碰撞中测量的动量谱的现象学影响。
