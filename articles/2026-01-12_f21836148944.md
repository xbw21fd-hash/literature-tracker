## Abstract
arXiv:2601.05717v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Inter-crystal scattering (ICS) in Positron Emission Tomography (PET) is commonly regarded as a degradation effect that might compromise the image spatial resolution. In parallel, the inclusion of ICS events has also been recognized as a potential approach to increase PET sensitivity, which could be especially beneficial in scenarios where the latter is a limiting factor, such as very small animal imaging. Several methods for the recovery of ICS events have been proposed, many of which aim to locate the first interaction, i.e., the Compton scattering site, usually limited by their success rate, computational burden or data and training dependency. Conversely, this work proposes a physics-based model for ICS events, leading to analytical expressions of the sensitivity image and the system matrix (required by statistical reconstruction algorithms), without the need to identify the original line of response. After validating the model, the work shows how ICS events can be integrated into a joint image reconstruction algorithm (based on list-mode MLEM) together with conventional PET events, for which dedicated analytical models are also developed. To assess the performance of the proposed approach, Monte-Carlo simulated and experimental data of an image quality phantom were obtained with the MERMAID small-fish PET scanner prototype. Both simulation and experimental results indicate that, while slightly decreasing the recovery coefficient values, the inclusion of ICS clearly reduces statistical noise and improves uniformity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.05717v1 公告类型：新 摘要：正电子发射断层扫描 (PET) 中的晶间散射 (ICS) 通常被认为是一种可能会损害图像空间分辨率的退化效应。与此同时，纳入 ICS 事件也被认为是提高 PET 敏感性的潜在方法，这在后者是限制因素的情况下尤其有益，例如非常小的动物成像。已经提出了几种恢复 ICS 事件的方法，其中许多方法旨在定位第一次相互作用，即康普顿散射位点，通常受到其成功率、计算负担或数据和训练依赖性的限制。相反，这项工作提出了一种基于物理的 ICS 事件模型，从而得出灵敏度图像和系统矩阵（统计重建算法所需）的解析表达式，而无需识别原始响应线。验证模型后，该工作展示了如何将 ICS 事件与传统 PET 事件一起集成到联合图像重建算法（基于列表模式 MLEM）中，并为此开发了专用分析模型。为了评估所提出方法的性能，使用 MERMAID 小鱼 PET 扫描仪原型获得了图像质量模型的蒙特卡罗模拟和实验数据。模拟和实验结果均表明，在略微降低回收系数值的同时，ICS 的加入明显降低了统计噪声并提高了均匀性。
