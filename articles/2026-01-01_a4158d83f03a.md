# Semiclassical theory for proximity-induced superconducting systems with spin-orbit coupling

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.24241v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We develop a semiclassical theory of superconducting quasiparticles for proximity-induced superconducting systems, where spin-orbit coupling plays a critical role in shaping the quasiparticle dynamics. We reveal the structure of superconducting Berry curvatures in such systems, and derived the superconducting Berry curvature induced thermal Edelstein effect and spin Nernst effect. We calculate these two thermo-spin responses with model systems where Rashba spin-orbit coupling, proximity induced superconductivity, and ferromagnetic order are coexisting.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.24241v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们开发了一种用于邻近感应超导系统的超导准粒子半经典理论，其中自旋轨道耦合在塑造准粒子动力学方面发挥着关键作用。我们揭示了此类系统中超导贝里曲率的结构，并推导了超导贝里曲率引起的热埃德尔斯坦效应和自旋能斯特效应。我们使用 Rashba 自旋轨道耦合、邻近感应超导性和铁磁有序共存的模型系统计算这两种热自旋响应。
