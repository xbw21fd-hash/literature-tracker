## Abstract
arXiv:2407.11498v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Living systems operate out of equilibrium, continuously consuming energy to sustain organised, functional states. Their emergent behaviour usually relies on a set of interconnected chemical reaction networks (CRNs) driven by external fluxes that keep some species at fixed concentrations. Hence, uncovering the principles governing the functioning of these CRNs is crucial to understand how living systems generate and regulate complexity. While kinetics plays a key role in shaping detailed dynamical phenomena, the range of operations of a CRN is fundamentally constrained by thermodynamics. Here, we introduce and analytically derive the "thermodynamic space" of a CRN, i.e., the range of accessible stationary concentrations that can be realized under a given energetic budget. We establish analogous bounds for reaction affinities, shedding light on how global thermodynamic properties, such as the total non-equilibrium driving, can limit local non-equilibrium quantities. We illustrate our results in various paradigmatic examples, demonstrating how the onset of complex behaviors is intimately tangled with the presence of non-equilibrium conditions. By providing a general tool for analysing CRNs, the presented framework constitutes a stepping stone to deepen our ability to predict complex out-of-equilibrium phenomena and design artificial chemical systems, starting from the sole knowledge of the underlying thermodynamic properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2407.11498v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：生命系统的运行失去平衡，不断消耗能量来维持有组织的功能状态。它们的涌现行为通常依赖于一组相互关联的化学反应网络（CRN），这些网络由外部通量驱动，使某些物种保持固定浓度。因此，揭示控制这些 CRN 功能的原理对于理解生命系统如何产生和调节复杂性至关重要。虽然动力学在塑造详细的动力学现象中起着关键作用，但 CRN 的操作范围从根本上受到热力学的限制。在这里，我们引入并分析推导了 CRN 的“热力学空间”，即在给定能量预算下可以实现的可达到的固定浓度范围。我们为反应亲和力建立了类似的界限，揭示了全局热力学性质（例如总非平衡驱动）如何限制局部非平衡量。我们用各种范例来说明我们的结果，展示复杂行为的发生如何与非平衡条件的存在密切相关。通过提供分析 CRN 的通用工具，所提出的框架构成了一个垫脚石，以加深我们预测复杂的非平衡现象和设计人工化学系统的能力，从对潜在热力学性质的唯一了解开始。
