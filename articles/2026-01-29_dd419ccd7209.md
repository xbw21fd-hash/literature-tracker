## Abstract
arXiv:2510.02460v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The Kitaev model belongs to an unconventional class of two-dimensional spin systems characterized by anisotropic, bond-dependent interactions that give rise to Quantum Spin Liquid (QSL) states. These exotic phases, marked by the absence of magnetic ordering even at zero temperature, support fractionalized excitations and emergent gauge fields. A particularly compelling feature of the Kitaev model is its exact solvability, which reveals low-energy excitations in the form of itinerant Majorana fermions-quasiparticles that obey non-Abelian statistics and are of central interest in topological quantum computation due to their inherent robustness against local perturbations and decoherence. Despite extensive theoretical advancements, the experimental identification of QSLs remains challenging, as conventional magnetic probes fail to detect their defining properties. In this work, we present a theoretical investigation of spin current injection from a superconducting metal into a Kitaev quantum spin liquid. By employing a spintronic framework, we derive the dynamics of the injected spin current and demonstrate how its signatures can be traced back to the underlying Majorana excitations in the spin liquid phase. Superconductivity plays a pivotal role in this context, not only as a source of coherent quasiparticles but also as a platform with potential for interfacing with topological quantum devices. Our analysis contrasts the Kitaev-superconductor interface with conventional ferromagnetic junctions, where spin transport is carried by magnons, and highlights distinctive features in the spin current response. These findings open new directions for the detection of QSLs and contribute to the broader effort of integrating topological quantum materials into scalable quantum technologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.02460v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：Kitaev 模型属于一类非常规的二维自旋系统，其特征是各向异性、键依赖性相互作用，可产生量子自旋液体 (QSL) 态。这些奇异相的特点是即使在零温度下也不存在磁序，支持分段激励和涌现规范场。 Kitaev 模型的一个特别引人注目的特征是它的精确可解性，它揭示了以流动马约拉纳费米子准粒子形式存在的低能激发，这些准粒子服从非阿贝尔统计，并且由于其对局部扰动和退相干的固有鲁棒性而成为拓扑量子计算的中心兴趣。尽管理论上取得了广泛的进步，但 QSL 的实验鉴定仍然具有挑战性，因为传统的磁性探针无法检测到它们的定义特性。在这项工作中，我们提出了从超导金属到基塔耶夫量子自旋液体的自旋电流注入的理论研究。通过采用自旋电子框架，我们推导了注入自旋电流的动力学，并演示了如何将其特征追溯到自旋液相中潜在的马约拉纳激发。超导在这方面发挥着关键作用，不仅作为相干准粒子的来源，而且作为具有与拓扑量子器件接口潜力的平台。我们的分析将基塔耶夫-超导体界面与传统铁磁结进行了对比，其中自旋输运由磁振子进行，并突出了自旋电流响应的独特特征。这些发现为 QSL 的检测开辟了新的方向，并有助于将拓扑量子材料集成到可扩展的量子技术中。
