## Abstract
We report the synthesis and characterization of a series of Eu-labeled cyclic peptoids and evaluate their potential as luminescent probes for biological applications. Our results suggest that Eu-peptoid hybrids represent a promising class of luminescent probes for bioimaging, with potential applications in disease diagnosis, drug discovery, and fundamental biological research.
