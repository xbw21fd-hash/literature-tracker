## Abstract
TLDR: 2D ferromagnetic ferroelectricity in layered transition-metal halide systems is discovered and it is revealed that a charged CrBr_textbraceleft 3 monolayer exhibits in-plane multiferroicity, which is ensured by the combination of orbital and charge ordering as realized by the asymmetric Jahn-Teller distortions of octahedral Cr-Br_textbraceleft 6 units.
