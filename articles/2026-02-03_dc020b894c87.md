## Abstract
arXiv:2512.20581v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: RNA function is deeply tied to secondary structure, with most molecules operating through dynamic and heterogeneous structural ensembles. While current analysis tools typically output single static structures or averaged contact maps, chemical probing methods like DMS capture nucleotide-resolution signals that represent the full structural ensemble, which however remain of difficult structural interpretation. To address this, we present MERGE-RNA, a framework that explicitly describes and outputs RNA as a structural ensemble. By modeling the experimental pipeline through a statistical physics framework, MERGE-RNA learns a small set of transferable and interpretable parameters, enabling the seamless integration of measurements across different molecules, probe concentrations, and replicates in a single optimization to improve robustness. Our model employs a maximum-entropy principle to predict thermodynamic populations, introducing only the minimal sequence-specific adjustments necessary to align the ensemble with experimental data. We validate MERGE-RNA on diverse RNAs, showing that it achieves strong structural accuracy and the ability to fill knowledge gaps in single-conformation reference structures. Furthermore, in a designed RNA construct for which we report new DMS data, MERGE-RNA deconvolves mixed states to reveal transient intermediate populations involved in strand displacement, dynamics that remain invisible to traditional analysis methods.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.20581v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：RNA 功能与二级结构密切相关，大多数分子通过动态且异质的结构整体进行操作。虽然当前的分析工具通常输出单个静态结构或平均接触图，但 DMS 等化学探测方法捕获代表完整结构整体的核苷酸分辨率信号，然而，这些信号仍然难以进行结构解释。为了解决这个问题，我们提出了 MERGE-RNA，一个将 RNA 明确描述和输出为结构整体的框架。通过统计物理框架对实验流程进行建模，MERGE-RNA 可以学习一小组可转移和可解释的参数，从而能够在一次优化中无缝集成不同分子、探针浓度和重复的测量结果，从而提高鲁棒性。我们的模型采用最大熵原理来预测热力学总体，仅引入将集合与实验数据对齐所需的最小序列特异性调整。我们在不同的 RNA 上验证了 MERGE-RNA，表明它具有很强的结构准确性，并且能够填补单构象参考结构中的知识空白。此外，在我们报告新 DMS 数据的设计 RNA 构建体中，MERGE-RNA 对混合状态进行解卷积，以揭示涉及链位移的瞬时中间群体，而这对于传统分析方法来说仍然是不可见的。
