## Abstract
TLDR: It is found that the intrinsic electric fields formed at ferroelectric/electrode interfaces determine the nucleation sites and growth rates of ferro electric domains and the orientation and mobility of domain walls, whereas dislocations exert a weak pinning force on domain wall motion.
